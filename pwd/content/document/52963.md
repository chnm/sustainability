Department of War
                                                      Accountants' Office
                                                         February 25th 1796

Nathaniel Gorham Esq-. Supr-. Boston

                            Sir
                               The Treasure of the United States will remit to you 
the sum of five hundred Dollars to be delivered to John Bryant at Springfield - on account of the Armory at that place - for which sum please take his duplicate receipts to be accountable.-

                               I am Sir (indecihperable)
                                William Simmons
