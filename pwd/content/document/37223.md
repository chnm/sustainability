Annapolis April 8. 1785

Dear Sir,

      I wish you would be so kind as to send me a Copy of Col. R. H. Harrisons Account to which was settled and transmitted by me some time since to Philadelphia~ I keep a copy of all Accts I adjust but through mistake this one was Neglected previous to my forwarding it~ 
                       John White

John Betts
