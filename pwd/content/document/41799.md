Col. Saml Hodgdons
26th July 1791
Order to purchase Sundry
Goods for Indians
Pittsburgh [illegible] 26 July 1791
I wish you to purchase the
following articles for the Indians
and deliver them to Adjutant Mor
gan and take his receipt for
them.  List
Two Ruggled Shirts
two pair of leggin
two Breech Clouth
[illegble][lb?] Paint
Yours
Samuel Hodgdon
26 July
Major Craig
