I evacuated to Garfield, Arkansas for about 2 weeks and then we went to Branson, Missouri. There was nothing bad that happened to my house or any of the people I was with, but my grandparents had a tree down on the edge of their house and their back porch so it took a long time for us to get in. The only thing that can be thought as a bad thing is us having to sleep in our car 1 night.
