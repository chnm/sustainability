Philad.  January 2nd 1801
Sir
I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your favor of the 30 Ultimo enclosing the counterprt of Mr. Foxalls contract which shall be delivered to him agreeable to your desire  I forwarded by this day's mail a Package containing Eight Blank commission forms similar to the last.
I am very respectfully
Honble Samuel Dexter Esq			
Secretary of War
