From the Honble
The Secy at War
March Feby 8' 1790
Entre,,
War Office March 8.th 1790
Sir,
The Secretary of War requests you
to examine the the books of your Office relative to the
merits of the enclosed petition, and if possible
to inform him of the result, this day.
I am Sir
Your Most Obedt.
Humble Servant
Wm Knox.
Joseph Howell Esq.
