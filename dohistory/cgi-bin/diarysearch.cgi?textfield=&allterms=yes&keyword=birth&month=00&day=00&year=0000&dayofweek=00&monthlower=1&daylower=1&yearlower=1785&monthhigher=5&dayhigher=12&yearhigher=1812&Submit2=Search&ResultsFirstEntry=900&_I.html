 March 19, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X, Birth Lathrops Son. XX.

rainy morn. mrs Lathrop had her women & was Deld of a Son at 11h 30m morn of a Son which wd 12 lb. I went to Dr Colmans & Procurd 3/ worth of Drugs & returnd home. Sally & Polly Densmore & P. Livermore here.   at Lathr. Birth 13[th]. receivd [ Sugar July 28].  
 

 

 March 21, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard waited on me to mr Twings, his Lady is a little more Comfortable; her Breast has broke twice. we went in to hear them Sing at Evng. Dolly Came home from mr Livermores.   at mr Thwings. Snowd at night.  
 

 

 March 22, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ezekl Pages Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 6h aftern to See mrs Page who was Deld at 10 of a fine Son, her 8th Child. I returnd home at 2h morn, receivd 6/ of mr Page. I had a turn of the Cramp ys morn.   at mr Pages. Birth 14th. 
 

 

 March 25, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to the hook as far as Elisha Preskotts. mrs Barton was Calld to See her Sister who is very Low. I bot at mr Dummers Store 1 yd Durant, 2/4; [1_th] yd Cambric, 2/6; 2 Skeins Silk, /8; 3 knots Thread, [/6]. paid the Cash.   at the hook.  
 

 

 March 26, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth P. Jones Son. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld by Peter Jones to his wife in Labour at 3h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 4 of a Son. I tarried till near night & Left my Patients Cleverly. receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is gone to Survey for Doct Parker. a very rainy night.   at Peter Joness. Birth 15 th. Jobe Springr had a Son born ys night.  
 

 

 March 29, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day. Dolly Spining Cotten. Ephm went to his Brors, Jackys hand is giting better. the freshet is very high. I have had a pain in my head, been at home till 11h Evng when I was Calld to See mrs Parker, ye next house below here.   at mr Parkers.  
 

 

 March 30, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Parkers Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Parker was Safe Deld at 11h foren of a Daugt. I left her and infant Cleverly at 2h pm & returnd home, find Dolly is gone to mr Densmores. mr Ballard returnd at Evng, he Surveyd for Doct Parker Tuesday & Wednsday, Thursday faught fire at Cobesy uper mill. the other 2 days workt at ye Grist mill.   at mr Parkers. Birth 16th. 
 

 

 March 31, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard attended worship at the hook. I was Calld to See mr Parkers infant foren, at Evn to See Asa Masons wife. arivd there at 10h Evn.   at Does & Asa Masons, very bad wriding.  
 

 

 April 2, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Asa Masons son.

Clear. mrs Mason was Safe Deld at the 2nth morn of a Son, her Second Child. Shee was very ill, but I left her & infant Cleverly at 4h 30m, & reacht home at 9. I Calld & helpt little Jack allso. at mr Thwings, his wife is very ill with her Breast & other maledies. Polly Densmore is here to work for Dolly. I went to Bed & Slept after I returnd.   at masons. Birth 17th. I was very much fateagud.  
 

 

 April 3, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wm Pitts Son. XX.

Clear & very pleast. mr Ballard went to Pittstn to work. I was Calld to See mrs Pitt who was in Labour. Shee was Delivrd at 10h Evng of a Dead Son. I tarried all night, mrs Pollard and Voce allso.   at mr Pitts. Birth 18th. 
 

 

 April 5, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr X Buzels Son. XX.

Clear. Dolly washt & Scourd the west roome. mrs Densmore here. mr Ballard returnd from pittstn, Cyrus to work for Jona. I was Calld at Sun Sett to See mrs Buzel who was Safe Deld at 9h Evn of a fine Son. I tarried allnight.   at mr Buzels. Birth 19/th. 
 

 

 April 6, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mrs Buzels & inft Cleverly & arivd at home at 10h morn. I receivd 9/ as fee & hors hire & medisin. I feel not a little falagud as ye rodes are very bad. raind aftern. mr Ballard is gone to his meddow. Dolly is makeing Soap.   at Ditoes. I was much fatagud.  
 

 

 April 8, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dolly washt & Shee & I went to mr Livermores. I was Calld to mr Capins at Evng. mr Ballard been to Colo Howards.   at mr Livermores [mr] Capins.  
 

 

 April 10, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy foren, Clear aftern. I Came from mr Capins to Deacon Coneys, find the old Lady very Sick. Came home, we receivd a Letter from Bror Kingsbury which informs [ ] yt Motr Ballard is very Low, other friends well. I was Calld to See mrs Densmore at 11h.   at Does & Deacon Coneys. receivd a Letter fm Capt Kingsbury of march 30th. 
 

 

 April 11, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Densmores Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Densmore was Deld of a Dagt at 11h morn, is as well as Can be Expected. the inft is troubled much with wind. I gave remedies which releivd & returnd home at 4. Hannah & Parthenia here. mr Ballard been to meeting at ye hook. I went to Danys at Evn.   at mr Densmores. Birth 20th. receivd my fee in Candles.  
 

 

 April 12, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Danys 5th Son.

Clear. I put Mrs Dany to Bed with a fine Son about ye mdl of ye night. Shee & inft are Cleverly. I returnd home at 4h morn, went to bed; had a Severe fit of ye Cramp at 6. Dolly watcht with mrs Densmore. Shee was returnd and was attentive in waiting on me. I went to bed again & Slept till 9. mr Ballard is gone to do Surveying for Esqr Howard. I was Calld to [Epr] Cowens.   at mr Danys. [Birth] 21st. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 April 17, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, warm foren. Sally Cox Came to work for me. mrs Livermore & I went to mr Shubl Hinkleys, the mrs Norcross, mrs Selvester & Ney were there. I Calld to See mrs Parker, her inft is not very well. David & Willm Densmr Sleep here. Ephm is [un ].   at mr Hinklys. Sally Cox here.  
 

 

 April 21, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Cyrus & Jona & little Jack, mr Pitts, his wife, Daugtr & Polly Pollard here. I have been at home. I was Calld at half after 8 Evn to Deacon Coneys, his Lady Expird at 10. mrs Mc Masters & I put her in grave Cloaths. mrs Brooks was present. the former went home after midnight. mrs Brooks & I tarried all night.   at Deacon Conys, his wife Expird at 10 h Evng. 
 

 

 April 26, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Hinkley who is Exercised with a Severe Cough, tarried all night. mrs Hinkley & mrs Ney there. Betsy went home.   at Thos Hinkleys. 
 

 

 April 29, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thoms Hinklys Son, the 9th by XX two wives. XX.

Clear, a Coal morn, fine Day. I was Calld between twelve & one ys morn to See mrs Hinkley who was in Labour & was Deld of a fine Son at 11h. I left her & infant Cleverly & returnd home, find mr Ballard Sett out on a tour of Surveying ys morn. my girls have washt & are Scouring house &C.   at mr Hinkleys. Birth 22 nt, Sixteen of which were Sons. recd 6/.  
 

 

 May 1, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Capins Dagt. XX. 478th Extraction Since I Came to Hollowell.

Clear & warm. I was Calld at 1h morn to See mrs Capen who was in Labour. Shee was Delivd of a fine Dagt at 2. I tarried there till after Dining, went to mr Brookses, mrs Cony & Dagts Came there. I Came to mr Capins again, recd 6/. Cyrus Came to mr Pages Landing & Conducted me over ye river. Ephm heard yt our Hond Mother Ballard is Deceast.   at mr Capans. Birth 23d. we had News of ye Death of motr Ballard.  
 

 

 May 3, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Dolly went to Son Pollard. Bror Moore was here. Ephm Cowen Calld me to See his wife who is unwell.   at mr Cowins.  
 

 

 May 5, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Capt Savage & two Dagts attended worship. mrs Cowen had a turn of faintness, we Calld her women. Shee Seemd better and part of them went home.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 May 6, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Cowin was Lingering and very much Deprest in Spirits. we Calld mrs Fletcher. mrs Coal Calld there. mrs Savage & Fletcher tarried all night.   at Dittoes. Town meeting.  
 

 

 May 7, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Epm Cowens Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Cowens illness Came on about Two ys morn & Shee was Safe Delivd at 3h 30m of a fine Dagt, with 5 pains after my inquiry in to her Case. I tarried, took breakfast and Sett out homewd. Calld at Colo Sewalls, his Child is much better. I arivd at home at 10h morn. I receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is gone to pittstn. Fanny Cox & Hannah Goodin Came here.   at Ditoes. Birth 24th.
