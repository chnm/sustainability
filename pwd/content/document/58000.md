Philadelphia

Lieut. McClellan
19 June 1797
Advises of a [undecipherable]
Rec'd 1st July
Fort Niagara 19 June 1797
Sir
Some days since I purchased a Horse for the use of this Garrison by the direction of Captain Bruff Commanding.
I have (in conformity to your instructions of last fall to Captain Bruff) drawn on you in favor of Messrs Clinch & Van Volgin for 112 Dols 50 Cents at 10 Days sight paye in New York.
I have informed the Gentlemen in whose favor the draft is, to call on the Supervisor of the district of New York, whom you may probably direct to honor it.
With respect I am Sir Your Lieut
John McClellan [undecipherable]
