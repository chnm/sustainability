W.D. Accountants Office

April 1st, 1794

Sir,

You are accountable on my books for 746 dollars 81 cents of the sum you received 26th July 1792 to be delivered to Capt. 

Wm ???ton, which I request you will take immediate measures to exonerate yourself - In consequence of a settlement of one of the officers to whom a part of that sum was to have been paid renders it necessary for your own safety to admit of no delay ~

I am Sir etc
Joseph Howell

LtCharles Hyde

Head Quarters
