War Department July 20th 20th 1791 
Sir 
You will immediately Ship 2000 Thorough gun stocks to McHill of New York, in order to be sent from thence to West Point, each of these Stocks must be paked so that none be transported but what preposect. You will also send all the Spare [undecipherable] Tomrods which are in Stone and 1000 Spare bayonets if there are any; This busness, must be executed as soon as possible 
I am 
Sir 
Your most obedt Servent 
HWK
William Knox
