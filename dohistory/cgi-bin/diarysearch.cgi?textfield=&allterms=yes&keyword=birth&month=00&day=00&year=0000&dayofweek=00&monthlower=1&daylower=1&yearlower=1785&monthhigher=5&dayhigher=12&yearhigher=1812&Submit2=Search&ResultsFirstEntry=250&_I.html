 August 25, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 I went to Isaac Savages ys morn, his wife here afternn, Deby Shaw allso. I was Calld to mr Cowens. Set up all night. our men at ye meddow.    
 

 

 August 26, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 I was Calld to Joseph Fosters from mr Cowens to See Aaron, find him unwell. returnd to mr Cowens, tarried till near night. Left them poorly. Doct Coney been there & to Fosters also.    
 

 

 August 28, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 I did hous work. Howard went from here. mrs Smily here. Hannah Cool came [here]. Parthena [I get on] with [now]. mr Ballard been to ye [school] Surveying. I have been at home. this is Datr Towns Birth day, 29 years old.    
 

 

 August 29, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn, Loury Day. I went to See mrs Weston, Shee is unwell. Calld at [ Colo Nths] & Capt Porter. he is Better. mr Ballard has been at work on the Bridg. Cyrus and mr Gillbreath went to Churches with the spindle of ye mill to be fited.    
 

 

 September 3, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I did not wrest very well. was Calld at 4/O Clock ys morn to see Aaron Foster, find him very unwell. gave him Some Senna and returnd home. Jono gon to Cobesy to attend a Coart Concerning Some uneasiness which hapned between old mr Mc Causlin & Son, Luke Barton & him Self Some time Since. mr Williams & his wife Drank Tea here. Hannah Coole went from here this morn.    
 

 

 September 4, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 at home till aftern, then Calld to Jos Fosters to See Aaron. find him poorly. Calld from there to Mr MC Masters, his wife unwell.    
 

 

 September 7, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 I did hous work. mrs Voce & Daughter here. I ws Calld to See mrs mc Masters in Travil at 7 Evn.    
 

 

 September 8, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr mc Masters Son. X.

mrs mc Masters put to Bed about 2 ys morn with a son, ye infant very weak & Low. I tarid all day. mrs Petingail Came there. Sally Leonerd watcht, the child revivd a little.    
 

 

 September 11, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 returnd from mc Msts. Calld at mr Westons & Williamss Polly Savage [there]. Foster Came to work here yesterday. mr Ballard at mr Pollards. Cyrus is 31 years old this Day.    
 

 

 September 15, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 at home till Sun Set, then Calld to See Andrew Goodwins wife who is unwell.    
 

 

 September 18, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. X Revd mr Fosters son X XX.

I was Calld to Revd mr Fosters Lady in Travl. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 7 Evnn. I watcht. it is her Second Child.    
 

 

 September 19, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 I am at mr Fosters. Shee is Cleverly.    
 

 

 September 22, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Calld to Joseph Williamss to See his wife, very unwell. mr Foster gone to the hook. I Came home at Evinng, find my Famely in helth.    
 

 

 September 23, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 I fited for going to meeting but was Calld to See mrs mc Masters who is unwell. mrs Cowen & I administred remedys that Doct Coney prescribd. her physic began to operate. I left her, returnd as far as mrs Williamss, tarried with her till 10 in Evnng, Shee being Exercisd with much pain, but got easier & I Came home.    
 

 

 September 30, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Pollard Came here. Desird me to Call at his house to See mr Sanford who is Sick there. I Compleid & find him very ill. I attended public worship also. mr Learned here after meeting.    
 

 

 October 6, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Hamlin here till afternn, [is better]. I went to see mrs Porter, She is unwell. has had the Canker & ague in her head, is some better. I Calld to See mr Sanford, find him very Low. mr Ballard Came home from Sincleirs.    
 

 

 October 9, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 I went to williamss to settle with Hanh Cool. Came home, was Calld to See mr Sanford, find him very Low. Revd mr Little maid a prayer with him. mr Ballard gone to Sinclears.    
 

 

 October 10, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I was diging roots forenn. Calld to See mr Sanford afternn. watcht ys night.    
 

 

 October 20, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Peggy Fox Came after me to go and See mrs Foster, Shee has took Cold, her Breast is painful. mr Foster is some better. I attend Funeral of mr Sanford & went back to mr Fosters, helpt her up & applied Some thing to her breast. mrs Voce with me. I went to Bed at ten, was Calld at 11h 30m to See mrs Goodwin in travil. arivd about one.    
 

 

 October 21, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Andrr Goodens Daftr. XX.

Arivd at mr Goodns at 1h noon, find her Safe Deliverd of a fine Dafter. Lady Cox operator. I tarried till ten. Sett out home by water, arivd at noon. attind worship aftern. the Revd mr Fosters Son Baptised, named Nathanl. Genny Cool here. I receivd a handsom reward of mr Goodin. mrs Voce carried it.    
 

 

 October 25, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. John Cumingss Son. XX.

Clear & Pleast. I was Calld to go to John [Cumings] at winthrop at 6h 30m morn. went about 6 miles, had a fall from the hors, did not receive much hurt. was informd the Lady was Safe [Delivd] of a Son. returnd at 7h 30m. Doct Barton and famely here on their way to the westwd. I wish them a Safe pasage. Cyrus went to help them to the vesel. Capt Savages Lady here, made me a prest of 2 hand kerchiefs. Parthena Barton Sleeps here. I went to see mrs Voce ys Evinning. Capt Nichols here.    
 

 

 October 30, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Weston, find her very unwell. was Calld from there to See Revd mr Foster, find him very ill. I watcht with him. raind at Evinng.    
 

 

 October 31, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. I Left mr Foster at the riseing of the Sun. Calld to see mrs Weston, shee is still poorly. I have workt very hard Bucking Cloath & trying Tallow. we have killd thee old Cow this Day. Shee had 40 lb of Tallow, wt in ye whole 430. Doct Coleman Brot his wife home.    
 

 

 November 5, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Wilm Whites Son. XX.

Clear & pleast. I Came from mr Fosters. we made 25 Dozn of Candles. mrs Voce here. Hannah is not So well as usual, I was Calld about mid night to go See Wilm Whites wife. I was very unwell. Seth Williams after me to See his wife also in travil. mrs White Safe Delivrd of a Son by ye asistance of Moses Whites wife before I arivd. I was Exceeding Sick while gone.    
 

 

 November 6, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Seth Williamss Child a Son XXX.

I Came from Whites by water to mrs Westons, Calld to See her. mrs North Calld me in there, gave me Some Brandy, which gave me relief. have been on the Bed the most of ye Day. mr Lee here. Theops bee Laying a flore over ye East room. mr Porter Supt here. mr Learned Sleeps here. I am informed that Seth Williams wife was Safe Delivrd at 7 O Clok ys morn. Shee had a Son.
