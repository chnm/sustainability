D. of War Accountants' Office
June 5th. 1795.
Lieut. John McClallen
West Point
Sir
I received your letters of the 31st ulto. inclosing a Receipt Roll for pay of yourself and recruits for January February & March 1795.  The same has been examined and the sum of 300 Dollrs. 80/100 has been payed to your credit which is the amount of your receipt.
I am Sir Y.C
William Simmons
