 January 25, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Anta Bracketss 2nd Daugt.

Clear [&] Cold. Son Cyrus Came home and went to meeting. I have Bakt bread and Pies, was Calld at 2h pm to go and See the wife of Anthina Bracket who was in Labour. her illness Came on while at the hous of worship. Shee was Safe Deliverd at 6h Evng of her 2nd Daught and 7th Child. I left them as well as Could be Expected, arivd at home at 11h 30m.   at a. Bracketts. Birth 2nd.  
 

 

 January 27, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I was Calld at 12 o Clock by EbenZr Trask to go and See his wife who is unwel. mrs Hammond was brot there before my arival. Shee and lhe women went home and I tarried with her till Feby ye 8 when I Came to Son Pollards. tarry till Monday 9th when I was Calld back.   at home. Calld to mr Trasks in ye night.  
 

 

 February 10, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth EbenZr Trasks Dagt. XX.

mrs Trask was Safe Delived ys morn before day light of her 3 Daught and 9 Child. I left her and inft Cleverly & relurnd home before noon.   at mr Trasks. Birth 3d. receivd 1 Dollar & 5 lb flax.  
 

 

 February 20, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day and Cool. this is ye Anneversary of my Birth. I have Seen 72 years, fue and have ye years of thy hand made been, O God. may I Serve the better the remaining part of my life is my Sinceare [Desear]. Dagt Ballard, her Daugts, Sally & Martha took Tea with us. Jon a & Wm Supt & Sleep here.   at home. mr Heartwel Cutt Some wood for us. I have Seen 72 years this Day.  
 

 

 February 26, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Ketons Daug. XX.

Clear. I have done my washing was was Calld to go to mr Ketons, his wife was Safe Deld at 9h Evng of her 5th Daugt & 10th Child. I tarrie all night. mrs Kingsly went home.   at mr Ketons. Birth 4th. receivd 9 s march [180 ].  
 

 

 February 28, 1807, Saturday

 Clear. James Keton Junr Came and Conducted me home. Becky Cowen Calld here. mr Ballard gone to the Settlement.   at Son Pollards and Came home.  
 

 

 March 4, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren and part of ye Evng. I have done my hous work and Brewd and knitt Some. Jon a [&] Wilm Sleep here.   at home, Brewd. Son Jonas Birth day.  
 

 

 March 21, 1807, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not so Cold. mr Lambard here to Breakfast, he wishes us to keep his Childn till he removs this Side ye rever which he Expects will take Place next weak. I have washt and Brewd, brot 8 pails water. Son Cyrus Came here ys aftern. I have been very unwel this night. Cyrus got up before Day to nurs me.   at home.  
 

 

 March 26, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Fogs Daugt by Rachel Smith. XX.

Clear and Cold. I was Calld at 3h ys morn to See Rachel Smith who was in Labour. Shee was Safe deld of a Daugt which Shee declard James [Fog] was the Falher of. I returnd home at 2h 30m.   at home till 9h ys morn. Birth 5th. June 24th recvd 6/ in part [     ].  
 

 

 March 29, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml Bralys Son.

Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus to Meeting. I was Calld to See Saml Braleys wife by Joel Savage. mountd the mare as Soon as my husband arivd from meeting.   at Saml Braleyss Junr. Birth 6th. at 9h Evng, a fine Son.  
 

 

 March 30, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mr Braleys Early this Morn, Came to Son Jonas. he Sent for his Father, we took Breakfast there. Son Lambard & [Gidyon] Barton allso. I Came home and did Some washing. Georg Heartwel had a fitt and I was Calld there. [Loiza] Gilbd is unwel allso, and Parkers Child.   at mr Braleys, Son Jonas & Heartwels; have done Some washing.  
 

 

 April 6, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Warin Stones Daugt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 2h ys morn to Warrin Stones wife who was in Labour. mr Soal got out of bed when I got to Son Jonas and assisted me. I arivd Safe though the traveting was very bad by reason of ye Snow being Drifted. the Patient was Safe Deld at 1h ys morn of a Daugt, her first Child by ys Mariage. I returnd home at 11h 30m. mr Ballard and Son Cyrus went to Town meeting.   at Warrin Stones. Birth 7th. June 30th receivd 6/ of Warrin Stones wife in Part of my demand. recvd 3/ by John Wall.  
 

 

 April 9, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. it is Fast day. mr Ballard & Cyrus to meeting. I have been at home. mr Soal, who livs with Son Ephm, Calld here.   at home. an infant of Robinson ye Taylor Expired.  
 

 

 April 14, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been feeble ys day but Some Easier. mrs Parker Came here, helpt me get Dinner. mrs Wiman here to Tea, made my Bed and assisted me other ways. Hannah got Breakfast. Ephm been to mill, had _ Bushl rie, Do of indien, ground for us.   at home, feel Some better for which I Desire to bless the author of all Mercies.  
 

 

 April 21, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day. I have washt my Buttery and done my other wk. Ephm & Wilm Sleep here, informd me it was feard yt Daniel Savage was Drownd. I was Calld at 10h Evng to See Son Jonas wife who is unwel.   was Calld at 10h Evng to Son Jonas, mrs Woodwd Came allso. we went to Bed, Slept some.  
 

 

 April 23, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jonas 7th Son.

Cloudy. I was Calld up at 2h 30m. my Daught was ill, her women Calld in and Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30m ys morn of her 7th Son and 10 Child. Shee Seems as wel as we Could Expect and I Came [home] about 10 hours ys morn.   at Son Jonas. Birth 8th. Shee had an ill turn aftern. I went and tarried at night.  
 

 

 April 25, 1807, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld to See my Daught Pollard. mr Trask accompanied me. we reacht there before Dark. they Chose oficers at Son Jonas.   at Son Pollards & Son Jonas.  
 

 

 May 1, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Pollards 5th Daugt.

Cloudy. my Daugt Pollard was taken ill before 3h ys morn and was Deld of her 5th Daugt and 8th Child. at [no entry] ys aftern. I tarrie this night.   at Son Pollards. Birth [8th]. 
 

 

 May 5, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till after 2 pm then Cloudy. I have been very ill with an uncommon pain in my right Side. mr Ballard been down to Judg [Norths] on busines. I have been alone, had to bring in Chips to keep a fire. mrs Nason Calld in to get Some Dock root for ye itch.   at home, very unwel through the day & Night.  
 

 

 May 8, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Part of ys day, Some Shours. mrs Parker Calld in. Betsy Andrus Came, spent aftern; Cookt some food and made my Bed. I feel a little Easier for which I wish to Bless God.   at home, feel some Easier. Hannah & Dolly went away forenn.  
 

 

 May 31, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the Girls went from here after Breakfast. mr Ballard and Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Parker Discoarst foren fm Mattu 25-35 [   ], aft fm 2nd Timy 3d C 5th vers. Son Jona, his wife, Jona, Sally and Martha took Supper with us. mr Badwel Calld in here, mrs Parker allso.   at home. Son Jona, his wife and part of ye Childn here.  
 

 

 June 4, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I have workt in my Gardin, planted bleu potatoes, 2 lb wt, Some at the S. Side ye Gardin and Some where the apple trees were removed from. mr Ballard & Cyrus been to Coart. mrs Parker here, Shee has been unwel, had a Boil on her Breast.   at home, workt in my Gardin. Funeral of Jackson the Barber.  
 

 

 June 14, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, mr Ballard to meeting. Son Jona, his wife, Martha and ye infant here. mrs Wiman Calld in Just at Evng, Son Ephm allso.   at home. Lambe. Part of my Childn here.  
 

 

 June 16, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Cloudy and Some rain aftern. I have transplanted winter Squash and Cucumbers and done my hous work. Son Jona Calld in. ye Blue potatoes I first Planted are Cuming up.   at home, workt in my Gardin.  
 

 

 June 17, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a Cool air. I have transptanted apple trees which Came up ys Spring. I Sett ym between the apple trees which ran N and [S] at ye South of ye hous. I brot Potatoes and other things from ye Seller, was taken So weak in my knees that I Could not get up without my husbands help.   at home, had an ill turn near night.
