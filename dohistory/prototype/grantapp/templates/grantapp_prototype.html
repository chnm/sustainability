Interactive Game
online prototype for a "Doing History" segment



About this prototype:

As part of our application to the National Endowment for the Humanities, we built a prototype for a small portion of the interactive game that lies at the heart of the proposed website.

This prototype has been developed for viewing on high-end computers. A detailed target platform description is available. Ultimately, the website will be accessible for viewers with a wide range of equipment and browsers. However, you will need a high-speed connection and good monitor to fully experience this prototype.

This prototype was developed with extremely limited resources, and is meant to give an indication of the how the "Doing History" pathway of the game might function. Limited resources precluded the involvement of an art director in the development of this prototype. Please keep in mind that is a pale version of our plans for the game and the website.

We have a created a "cheat sheet" for the part of the game implemented in this sample. We are providing it in the event that you do not have enough time to go through the game sample "for real" (which would take several hours). You may want to print it out before you begin so that you can use it while you go through the sample (note: there will be no further links to the cheat sheet).
