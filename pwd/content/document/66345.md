Accountants Office
January 30 1799
I Certify that there is due to Phoebe Shannon Matron to the Hospital at Fort Norfolk the sum of One hundred & twelve Dollars being for her pay up to the 31 December 1798 agreeably to a certificate of Surgy Mate Wm. Barnwell, which sum is to be transmitted to Edward Carrington Supervisor at Richmond to be by him paid to Lieut. Ferdinand L. Claiborne who is to be held accountable for the due application thereof.  W: Simmons
Pay of the Army for 97 = 32 
Pay of the Army for 98 = 80
Dolls 112. 
The Secretary of War
