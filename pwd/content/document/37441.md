Order from the War 
Office for Arms &c. 
20th Sept 1785
War Office Sept. 15th: 1785
Sir
You will be pleased to 
issue on the order of Lieut: Armstrong, 
Arms Accoutrements and Clothing for the 
equipment of Thirty Men. 
I am Sir 
Your Most Obed Servt 
HKnox
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr. 
Philadelphia
