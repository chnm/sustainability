New York. 4 May 1795 
Daniel Tingley 
aic. 6 =
New York May the 4th 1795. 15
Sir 
Enclos'd you have the Return for the month of April agreeable to a form sent forward by the Superintendant of Naval  & Military Stores at Philadelphia together with the Constructors reciept for whatever I have isued to him since I came into office, the which shall^ in future be sent on every month with the Return.                                                                                                                         
I have the honor to be
with due Respect 
Your Very humble Servt.
Danl. Tingley
Timothy Pictering Esquire
