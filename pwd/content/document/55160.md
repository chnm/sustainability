Accountants Office Oct. 5th 1796
Daniel Stevens Esq. Supervisor Charlesto So. Carolina
Sir
The Treasurer of the United States will remit you the sum of Tree hundred seven Dollars & 25/100, being the amount of your account for receiving & paying monies from Department between 13th Feb., & 27th June 1796, and for which you will please to forward your receipt to this Office. I am & c. Wm. Simmons Acct.
