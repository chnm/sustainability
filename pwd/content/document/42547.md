S. Ogden
XXX-124
My dear Sir                                       Newark, March 1st 1792
Capt Williamson, who has lately come from the Genesee River gave me to understand that he feared the Senecas Chiefs would not be down; it consequence of Kirkland's refusing to provide them with Sleighs--I only mean to give you this hint, for your investigation as Williamson i s now in Philadelphia.
I hope you received the letter I sent you a few days ago from Niagaria. Present my love to your household and believe me yours unattesably.
Gen. Knox                                       Saml Ogden
