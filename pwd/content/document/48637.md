War Office-- 
10 November 1794
Lead wanted -- 
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401810.]
Samuel Hodgdon Esq
341  341
Dear Sir.
We are in want, for the security of four rooms of the War Office from accidents of a fire, a sufficiency of Sheet Lead, to lay before each fire place.  Be pleased to furnish the quantity necessary from the public stores.  
Yours sincerely - Jno Stagg Jr
Saml. Hodgdon Esqr 
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401810.
