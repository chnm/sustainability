War Office
May 29th 1797.
Shoes Deliv' Capt' Staats Morris
Company
War-Office, May 29th 1797.
Sir.
Please to deliver the bearer Sergeant Galan, three pairs of shoes,
for the use of a small on command here, from Baltimore, and then
charge the same to Captain Staats Morris's company
By Order of the Secty of War
Jon Stagg [illegible]
Chf Clk.
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
