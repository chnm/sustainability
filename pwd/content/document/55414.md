Dr, The State of South Carolina
In account with The United States Cr. 
By General Account of Militia.  
For Pay and Subsistence of the Militia and pay of the Spies of South Carolina for services performed on the frontiers of said State between the 19th of September 1792 and the 31st of October 1794 pr. abstracts marked.  A 19,224.25.
For Ammunition, transportation of Ammunitions, Express hire &c  Per Abstracts Marked B  3,248.77
For Pay and Subsistence of the Militia and Pay of the Spies of South Carolina for services performed on the frontier of said State between the 3rd of August 1792 and the 31st October 1793 per abstract marked  C 12,394.69
For ditto ditto to ditto for ditto between the 8th of November 1792 and the 7th of October 1795 per abstract marked  D 13,532.54
Dollars 48,400. 25 
Department of War 
Accountants Office
October 31 1786
Wm Simmons Acct
The above form may be paid to the order from the Executive of the State of South Carolina.
