Department of War 

Mrs Sally Greaton. Accountants' Office

Boston                                                 February 19th 1796

Madam

Agreeably to the request of the Secre.y of War you will 
receive from Nath.l Gorham Esq. Super. the sum of ninety Dollars being on account of
the pay of your husband Capt. Rich.,d H. Greaton for which sum you will sign 
duplicate receipts. -- I am (indecipherable)

William Simmons
