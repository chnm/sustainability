Philadelphia 20.th April 1785

Dear George.

I have only my good wishes to send you by this conveyance, except the compliments of our friend Beatty from whom I received a letter a few days since pray deliver or send the enclosed and the money if possible from M. J. ___ and oblige yours

Sincerely [signed] J. Howell George Reid Esq.r
