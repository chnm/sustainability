No 25
Major Craig will please to know whether
10 lbs musket Catridge thread
10 lbs of Sh[eet]thread
200 lbs Brimstone
200 yds Red 
175[?] ditto - White   
110 ditto- Blue [bracket tying Red, White and Blue together] [illegible] 10 inches wide
and A fine Needles [illegible] can be had in Pittsburgh, and at what rates.
I wish to know immediately
Samuel Hodgdon
