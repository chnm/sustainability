New York 30.th April 1791
Errors Excepted William Hill
Sirs,
I wrote you the 27.th Ult.o covering receipt for
four Boxes of Artiellery Clothing, forwarded to your case 
by the Amboy Stage, which I hope arrived in Season, I now
beg leave to Inclose you receipt for Two boxes of Infantry
Shipt by the same conveyances.
General Knox in his directions to me did not
desire Musicis. Clothing to be included in the above
Twenty five Suits, and as I found it envied leave an intire
Company wihtout them. I thought it most advisable
not to send them
I am respectfully
Sir
Your very humb. Serv.t
William Hill
