 November 21, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I have been puting up things. Hepsy washt ye North Bedrooms and Kitchen. mr Ballard been up to ye house, Cyrus for Cart & oxen, and brot a Barril from Parmers. mrsmc Causland and Henry Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 November 22, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and some rain. mr Ballard and Cyrus went up with a load of our Stuff. mrs mc Causland Calld here, Harry went home with her. his behavior has been Such that mr Dingley wd not keep him any longer. what heart aicks his Dear mammy must have in her present Situation. I have been makeing a Shirt for mr Ballard, Hepsy one for Cyrus.   at home.  
 

 

 November 23, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I have been finishing mr Ballards Shirt. he and Cyrus been up to the hous. I went to mr Dingleys aftern. mr Dun Sent me 9/ for assisting his wife. mr Dingly Supt here.   at mr Dingleys.  
 

 

 November 27, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. mr Ballard and Cyrus been down and brot part of the things we left. Jessy Bullin & wife and her Sist Calld here, Old mrss Whealor allso. Ephm and Thomas made a partition between the North room and Entry.   at home.  
 

 

 November 28, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard & I, Cyrus, Jona and wife and mrs Peirce attended worship, it being a day appointed for Thanksgiving. my husband, my Self, Son and Dagt Lambd took Super with mr Shubal Pitts. we Calld at Son Jonas at Evn, reacht home at 30m past [1]h.   at meeting.  
 

 

 November 29, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. mr Ballard been at home. Cyrus went down for Slead and other matters. Ephm & Thomas made our Chamber Stairs. I have been at home, done Some hous work. Hepsy Bakt and part made her loop goun. Some indiens Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 December 3, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant Day, Snowd at 7h Evn. I have been regulateing my things. mr mr [sic] Ballard got Some wood, Cutt up his Beef &C. Cyrus helpt Saml git Some Sheep home. he Bought a new Tea Kettle and brot home. Old mr Pollard Calld to See us.   at home. Old mr Pollard here.  
 

 

 December 6, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Whealors Dagt. XX.

Snowd. I was Calld by mr Whealor at day Break to go and see his wife in Labour. Shee was Safe deld of a Daugt, her first Born, at 10h m. I tarried and Dined, then returnd as far as Son Lambds, tarried and Supt there and Saml Conducted me home at 7h Evn.   at mr Whealors and Son Lambds. Birth 50th. recd 9/.  
 

 

 December 7, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I have been at home, not So well as I Could wish. Son Lambd brot Allin and left him here, then went and Conducted Dagt Pollard to his house. Hepsy and I prepareing to make Some Pies. A Stag of Son Jonas hookt a mare So that Shee Expired Soon.   at home. Peris Hamlins wife Deld by mrss Mosier.  
 

 

 December 15, 1799, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant. mr Ballard and I attended worship all day. I went to warm me at mr Robinsons. Jane Black Calld me in to See her marm and aunt Baxter. mr Warrin has Past thro the operation of being Tapt this fore noon. Sally Tole, Jack Peirce and Son Jonas Childn supt with us. Hepsy Spent the Evng there. Asa Peirce Came home with her.   at meeting.  
 

 

 December 20, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Saunders Son.

Clear. I was Calld at 10h morn to go and See mrs Saunders who was in Labour. I arivd there at 1, find the patient Deld of a son by the help of mrss Edson. I performd what was further necesary. we taried thro the night. I left mother and Child as well as Could be Expected and Came home at 9h [Evng]. Dagt Ballard and Childn Dind. Son and Daugt Lambd Supt with us.   at mr Saunders. Birth 51st. I Since recd 6/. Dagt Ballard & Lambd here.  
 

 

 December 25, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 very Pleast. the Colt Expired and they killd the Stag, mr Waid assisted. I have been at home. Hepsy at Jonas helping his wife.   at home.  
 

 

 January 2, 1800, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

[Birth Davenport Dagt.]

Clear and pleast. I was Calld at 1h 30m morn to See the wife of Thos Davenport who was Safe Deld of her 3d daugt & 5 Child at 12 O Clock in ye day. I returnd home at 4 pm. Sons Lambd and Ephm here, finisht the outside of our house.   at mr Davenports. Birth 1st  
 

 

 January 10, 1800, Friday

view full Diary page

 

[X. Birth Elis Craggs Dagt. X.]

Clear. I was Calld at 1h morn to See a mrss Hrriman, Shee was Deld of a Son before I reacht there, which wd 10 lb 5 oz. I Drest the inft and was then Calld by mr Elias Cragge to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Deld before Day of her 2nd Child and first Dagt. I was there and at mr Herimans till 4h pm when mr Cragge Sett out to Conduct me home. the Sleigh over Sett, the fills broke but we were not hurt. I went to mrs Dutins till another hors and Sleigh were procured and Came home Saffe. mr Crage bestowed 12/ as a reward. this inft 3rd Child.   at mr Herimans. Birth 2nd. mr Craggs. Birth 3 d. recd 12/ as a rewd.  
 

 

 January 12, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I was Calld to Son Lambds to See Thoms, he being very unwell. we Gave him Senna and he Seemd better. I wrode home with mr Ballard at Evng.   at Son Lambds. Thomas is unwell.  
 

 

 January 13, 1800, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at night. I have been at home. Hepsy past an operation for ye itch. mr Ballard and Jona mending Slead. Cyrus went to mill and to Son Lambds. Thomas is much better.   at home.  
 

 

 January 19, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth James Herimans Dagt. XX.

Clear, a pleast Son but windy. mr Ballard, Jon a and his wife attended worship. we went there at Evng. Shubal Pitts & wife there. I was Calld from there at 9h Evn to See the wife of mr Heryman, Shee was Deld very Soon after I arivd, of a Dagt. Shee wrode in a Sleigh 13 miles after 7, her illness was on her when Shee Sett out from Doct Quimbys. I went to See mrss Robinson, Shee is very ill with a Cold.   at Son Jon as & mr Herimans. Birth 4th. recd a Letter of Decr 6th fm Sister Waters with News yt Bror Jonas Dagt Sophia was Dead.  
 

 

 January 25, 1800, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd and windy. mr Ballard went down in Town, our Company Each to their homes. Josiah Parker Calld me and dagt Ballard to See his wife at dusk. Shee was not very ill when we arivd.   at mr Parkers.  
 

 

 January 26, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Josiah Parkers [3d] Son & 7th Child. X.

Clear and Cold. my Patient was Safe Deld at 5h this morn of a fine Son, her husband went and Conducted mrss Stone there and my Dagt & I Came home at 8h morn. I went to bed after I reacht home and Slept. mr Ballard wt to meeting.   at Josiah Parkers. Birth 5th. recd 8/ at Crosbys Store.  
 

 

 January 31, 1800, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Rhoda Pollard & Hanh Ballard here foren. I was Calld at 1h pm to go and See the wife of James moore at Read field, find her very unwell. mrss Gould and Taylor tarried there this night.   at James Moores.  
 

 

 February 1, 1800, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 A Snow Storm. we are at mr Moores, his wivis Sist was Calld at Evng. the patient was very unwell all night.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 2, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth James Moores 3d Son.

Clear and very pleast. mrss Welman was Calld after Day and mrs Moore was Safe Deld of a Son at 7h morn. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 3h pm, receivd 4_ lb Salt pork of mr Moore.   at Ditoes. Birth 6th. Sons Jona & Ephm here at Evng.  
 

 

 February 7, 1800, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to Son Jonas, Hannah is very Lame. went from there to See mrs Parker, find her very ill. put oinions to her feet and gave her a Decoction of mugwort and Feaverfue and Came back to my sons. find him Lame and Hannah very uneasy, tarried till 9h Evn and walkt home.   at Son Jonas and mr Parker.  
 

 

 February 8, 1800, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. I went and tarried with mrss Parker till Evn. Calld at my Sons, find them Some more Comfortable.   at mr Parkers & Son Jonas.  
 

 

 February 9, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was Calld by Wm Chamberlin to go to his Brother Johns wife. I find her unwell but not So ill as Shee might be. tarried there all night, Sufered with ye Cold. mr Pilsbury Expired this aftern at 4 O Clock.   at John Chamberlins. Lambd & wife went past here at Evn.
