Comment card created in response to artist Maxx Sizeler\'s exhibit at 2426 Bradish Place on May 19, 2007. \r\nComplete text:\"She remembers. She remembers what places were before they became what they are now. (Opening line of SPACES IN BETWEEN, \"LAKEVIEWS\" Kathy Randels. Many of my landmarks are gone. Mouton St. lost both its corner houses and beyond.\"\r\n\r\n\"NEIGHBORHOODS: 2426 BRADISH PLACE\" was a multidisciplinary installation, the third in a series, that took place in the gutted ground floor of the home of Michelle Levine and Clay Thomas on May 19th, 2007. Michelle is an artist who is currently rebuilding her house and studio in the Tulane/Gravier neighborhood. Through the use of interviews with neighbors, soundscapes, live music, drawing, photography, and sculpture, this event confronted the range of issues the community is experiencing as they revitalize this neighborhood.\r\n
