Josiah Fox 5th Oct
1796
Rec 12th
Samuel Hodgdon
Superintendent of the military
and naval stores.
Philadelphia
[circle with 304 inside]
Received 
from
State
Dep't.
Nov. 24,1894
R.P.O.401310
Josiah Fox presents his respects to W. Hodgdon
and informs him by the opportunity of this
Conveyance.  the keel of the new frigate is laid
and the stern frame finished. the stern nearly
ready to raise & hope next next week to make a great
progress in the work.
