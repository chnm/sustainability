War Office
Aug 19 [17]97
Cloathing & Arms &c to be Delivd to Saml Hodgdon Esqr for Transportation to Richmond Virgnia to Lt Claiborne
War Office August 19 1797
Sir, 
Be pleased to deliver to Samuel Hodgdon Esqr to be him forwarded to Leiut Claiborne at Richmond in Virginia. Thirty suits of Infantry Cloathing (one thereof to be Serjeants and two Musics) thirty stand of Arms and Accoutrements thirty Knapsacks and One Drum and Fife.
I am Sir your obedt Servant James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr John Harris
