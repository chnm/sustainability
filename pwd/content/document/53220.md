Ebenezer Sproat
Received 18 April 1796
Ans Same - 
Marietta 25
William Simmons Esquire
Accountant War Dpartt
Philadelphia
Recd 15 Apr 96
Marietta 27th March 1796
Dear Sir 
I have not been able to collect all the vouchers to send forward yet one of the Directors of the Ohio Company is been gone the whole winter & not yet returned  I expect soon to have it my power to send forward all the receipts necessary to exonerate me from all the Charges against me at the War Office.  You did not acknowledge the receipt of the letter I writ you from Pitsburgh inclosing Major Craiggs receipts for the money I [took?] to him.  -- I have discharged all the spies in this County by an order from the Secy at War The rolls & power of all I will forward By the next post.  I am Sir with Most Respect your Most Obt Humble Servant E. Sproat 
Wm Simmons [undecipherable]
