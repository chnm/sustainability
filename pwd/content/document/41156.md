7th February 1791O Wolcott Jr Er Auditorwith Majr J. Burnett a/c
Office of late Army AcctsFeby 7th 1791
Sir
Enclosed th is the account of pay and Commutation due the Estate of the late Ichabod Burnett late ADC to Major Genl Greene
If Major Burnets accounts as Contractor for Clothing the So. Army are closed there will be no objection to the passage of the objection formerly existing to the passage if this account ceases--
I am Sir&cJ Howell
