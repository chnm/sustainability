Wm Doughty 
Clerk of the Naval Yard 
Philadelphia [in margin]

                                     W. D. Dec 11, 1794 

Sir,
You will on the Saturday of each week between 12 & 1 oclock report in writing to Messrs Gurney & Smith the number of men who have actually been employed at work at the Naval yard in this city in the course of that week with the amount of their wages when some person will be appointed to pay them.
