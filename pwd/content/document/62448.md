90
Philadelphia, 14 May 1798
Sir,
By order of the Secretary of War I send to your address the Stores and Military Stores inserted in the enclosed Invoice and Bill of Lading - I presume you have his direction for their ultimate destination - you will please to inform me when and the order in which they arrive.
I am
Sir,
your most obedient servant -
Samuel Hodgdon
John Habersham, Esquire.
