 March 16, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. mr Ballard Surveying & makeing a plan for mr Vauhn. I have been Doubling thread and piecing raggs. Hepsy went to mr Hinkleys to twist some thread. Shee went to mrs Gitchels and receivd 6/ of her in part for my assisting her in Child Birth.   at home.  
 

 

 March 19, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came from my Sons. Doct Cony was there to See the Child, who is not any better. I Calld at mrs Titcombs and at Son Pollards, little Sally is unwell, her mouth is Soar and her throat is Sweld some. Dagt Lambart and Childn Came home with me. my son Came for them at Evn. Cyrus at his Brothers.   at Son Jonas and at my Dagts. Genl Sewall Calld to See me this Evn. Dagt & Son Lambart & Childn here. Snowd at Evng.  
 

 

 March 20, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Colo Norths, Conducted me to Son Pollards to See their Babe. I find it better. I Came home at Evn. Cyrus informs that Le faett Seems more Comfortable. I Calld at Son Lambts, they are well.   at Son Pollards & Lambts.  
 

 

 March 21, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Colo North Calld here, he and mr Ballard are gone to woolwidg. I have been at home, mended my winter goun and knit Some. Cyrus Came from Jonas, Le faett is better.   at home. mr Bald to woolwidg. Snowd all night.  
 

 

 March 25, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I was Calld at 4h morn by Genl Sewall to See his Lady who was in Labour. Shee had her pains gradually till the insewing morn. mrss Livermore went with me, we found the wife of Thoms Sewall there. I went to See mrss Comings, find her in a low State, her Complaint is in her throat.   at Genl Sewalls & Solomn Cumings.  
 

 

 March 26, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Genl Henry Sewalls 4th Daugt. XX.

Clear. mrss Sewall was Safe Deld at 3h ys morn of a fine Daugt, her 7th Child. I left her Cleverly and returnd home at 9h 30m. I receivd 6/7 as a reward. mrss Livermore tarries with her till he went to magr FileBrowns for her nurs. mr Ballard been to Son Jonas, all well there. I have had a very bad head aick this aftern & Evn.   at Genl Sewalls. Birth 8th. receivd 6/7 as a reward.  
 

 

 March 27, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth [Nathn] Shaws 3d Dagt. XX.

Cloudy the most of the day, Some Snow. I do not feel very well but attended at the hous of the widdow Sewall on her husbands funeral. Son Jona brot his two oldest Sons here. Cyrus went up there, Carrid Le faett home. Sons Jona and Lambart are gone to Conduct Wm and Levy Parmer, John and Freeman Hinkley before EbenZ Farewell for Tryal for their asaulting John Johnson and Rufus Ballard on the high road. I was Calld at 7h Evn by mr Shaw to See his wife, Shee was Safe Deld at 8 of a Dagt, her 5th Child and is Cleverly. I tarried all night. I had a fit of the night mair.   at the funeral mr Moses Sewall, and at mr Shaws, hook. Birth 9th. recd 7/6 as a reward.  
 

 

 March 28, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. I returnd home at 7h this morn, left my patient Cleverly. mr Ballard is gone up to the uper Parrish on business, he was hearing Coart aftern.   at mr Shaws. mr Johnson & Ballard Sleep here.  
 

 

 April 1, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinkleys 2nd Son & 3 Child. XX.

Cloudy. mr Ballard and Cyrus are gone to meeting. Cyrus Came home at noon, it raind. James Hinkley Came at 1h pm for me to go and See his wife who is in Labour. we arivd, find his mother and Ben Churches wife there. we Calld the mc Causland wives after Sun Sett & Patient was Deld at 7h Evn of a fine Son, her 3d Child, and is Cleverly. I tarried all night.   at James Hinkleys, New France. Birth 10th. recd 1_ bushl Corn the 4 inst. 
 

 

 April 2, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, raind Some. I left my patient as well as Could be Expected and returnd home at 10h m. mr Ballard & Cyrus are gone to Town meeting, it was Put by by reason of the warning being illegal. I went to bed and Slept Some. Snowd at Evng.   at James Hinkleys.  
 

 

 April 4, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard gone up Streat. I feel very unwell. James Hinkley brot me 1_ bushels Corn for my assisting his wife.   at home. mr James Hinkly brot me 1_ bushel Corn.  
 

 

 April 5, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. this day is Sett aPart as a day of Fasting and Prayer. mr Ballard, Cyrus and my Self attended worship at Augusta. the Revd mr Stone Discoarsd from Provs 14th Ch 34th vers. I went to mrss Pattins at intermition.   at meeting, wrode in a Sleigh. Calld at Son Lamb ts. 
 

 

 April 23, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kennys Dagt. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. I have been very unwell but being Calld by mr James Kenny at 4h pm, I went to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30m of her Second Dagt & 4th Child. I left her and inft very Comfortable and arivd at home at 10h 30m Evn.   at mr James Kenneys. Birth 11th. receivd 6/ for my Service.  
 

 

 April 24, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Town Sleeps here. I have been very unwell but was Calld at Evn to go to See the wife of Thos Kenny. I wrode to Esqr Smiths and went by water from there, found mrs Kenny very unwell and her women with her.   at Thos Kenneys.  
 

 

 April 25, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was with my Patient but Shee is more Comfortable. the women went home Except her Sist Blanchard.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 26, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I tarried with my Patient this Day. Shee washt and Bakt.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 27, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home at 10h morn, find my hous alone and Every thing in arms, did not find time to Set down till 2 pm. mr Shubal Hinkleys wife here to See me. Elisha Noles here, paid me in Cloath for assisting his wife.   at Ditoes this morn. mrs Hinkley here afternoon.  
 

 

 April 28, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard makeing plan and went to Hallowell. I have done my house work. Watson diging the gardin. Shubal Pittss wife Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 May 2, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ephm Burges 2nd Son. XX. Augst 27th 1799 receivd 3/7.

Cloudy and Cool. I was Calld this morn to See the wife of Ephm Burges who was in Labour. Shee was Deld about 8h morn of her Second Son and 3d Child. I lefe mother and infant Cleverly and returnd home between ten and Eleven. mr Burges paid me 6/ in part for my Service. I have Brewd a medisinal beere. mr Ballard is gone to Riedfield to Survey. Jonas hired man here helping Plough.   at Ephm Burges. Birth 12th. receivd 6/. I was Cald at Evng to See genl Sewalls wife whos Breast is Soar. I was there all night.  
 

 

 May 7, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Town meeting. mr James was Chosen to represent the Town of Augusta in ye Genl Coart this year. I have been Some more Comfortable ys day, have done part of my washing. mrss Densmore & Livermore Calld in to See me. Watson to work at Son Jon as. Cyrus Sleeps here. Henry mc Causland wounded his hand.   at home. feel my Self a little better. mrss Densmore & Livermore here.  
 

 

 May 10, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard & Son Pollard went to take their Sheep out of pound at Hallowell. Old mr Lambard Calld to See me, his famely are well. Watsons Brother dind here. Polly Livermore Came in and helpt me. Polly Barbarick Came to work here.   at home. Son Town Dind here. mrs Dingley Calld [here].  
 

 

 May 11, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thoms Kenneys Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrss Baxter here. I have been kniting. I was Calld at 8h 30m Evng to See the wife of Thoms Kenney who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 10h 30m of a Dagt. I tarried all night, my Patient as well as Could be Expected. this is her 6th Child.   at Thoms Kenneys. Birth 13th. receivd 9/ July 11th by Son Cyrus.  
 

 

 May 12, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home by water, arivd at 6h 30m morn. mr Abrm Devenport assisted to Conduct me there by water and home again. I do feel really fatagud with my Tour, went to bed at 10h and rose at 2h pm, feel much refreshed. Isaac Hardins wife here.   at Ditoes. Isaac Hardins wife here.  
 

 

 May 15, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Capt Stackpole Calld here this morn, informs yt Dagt Town is very unwell. mrss Dingley & Childn here. Polly washt ye west room and ironed her Gown.   at home. mrss Dingley here.  
 

 

 May 17, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld by Saml Cony at 3h morn to go and assist to put Grave Cloaths on the remains of Solmn Cumings wife who Expired ys ["morn" - implied]. mrs Mc Master & Fletcher assisted me.   at mr Cumingss, his wife Expired at 3 hours morn.
