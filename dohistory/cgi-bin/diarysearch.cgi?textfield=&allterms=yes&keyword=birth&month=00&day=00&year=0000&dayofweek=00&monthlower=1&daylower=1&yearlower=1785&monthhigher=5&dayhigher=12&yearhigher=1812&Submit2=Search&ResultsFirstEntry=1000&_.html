 December 7, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Benn Whites XX. And X. Parkers Dagts. XX.

at Whites, his wife was Deld at 12 O Clok of a Dagt, and I was Calld back to mr Parkers. his Lady was Dild at 9h 30m of a Dagt. I am Some fatagud. Son Town here.   at Whites & Parkers. Birth 50. Birth 51.  
 

 

 December 8, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snow, hail & rain. mr Parker went for his Nurs. I left his Lady at 4 pm, as well as Could be Expected & walkt over ye river; wrode mr Ballards hors home. I had a wrestless night by fataug & weting my feet.   at mr Parkers. returnd home.  
 

 

 December 9, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Surveying for mr Pollard & Page. I went to mr Finnys & Benjamins, brot my wollen web goun home & went to mr Peter Clearks. they ingagd to weav it. I Calld at Capt Meloys Store, Bot a Shall @ 5/6. he made me a present of a muslin apron. I bot at Capt F Browns 5-2/1 pints Brandy 2/9, 3 puter poringers 4/6, paper pins /10; Total 8/1.   at Finnys & the hook.  
 

 

 December 15, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. our Childn all here yt are in ys Town, mr Pitts & his wife & mrs S. Williams. I was Calld at Evn to See mr Deckmans Child who has a Swelling on ye Throat, mrss Pollard, kimbal & Capin there.   at mr Dickmans to See a Sick Child. Dolly went home with Jona. 
 

 

 December 17, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Hamlins Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 6h morn to See mrss Hamlin, Shee was Deld of a Dagt at 7h & 20m. I Spent the most of ye Day with her. I went to See mrs Dickmans Child, find it very Sick; the Lady had a fitt. mrs Pollard was there. I left mrs Hamlin & infant very Comfortable & returnd home at Evn. mr Livermors Laid a floor in our kitchen.   at mr Hamlins. Birth 52nt. receivd 12/ Feby 5th 1794 of mr Hamln. 
 

 

 December 24, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth John Badcks Dagt.

Clear. I mrs Childs a little Easier. I was Calld to John Badcoks to See his wife at 1h pm. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 9. I tarried all night, mr [sic] patient as well as Could be Expected; the Negro Doctres there.   at Ditoes & Badcoks. Birth 53d. mrs Childs had Doct Cony with her this night.  
 

 

 December 25, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, a Snow Storm aftern. I returnd to mr Childs, his wife very ill. I was Calld at Evn to mr Pollards to See mr Blak & [Davids] Childrn, they are ver [torn page].   at mr Childs & Pollards. mrs Dickmans Child [Expird] yester[night].  
 

 

 January 12, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. mr Ballard attended worship all Day, my Self aftern. I was Calld to See mrs Benjamin at 7h Evng. mrs Voce & Stevens there all night. it was Severe Cold. my Patient at her fathers.   at meetg & mr Savages. Sally Bakt mins Pies at Evng.  
 

 

 January 13, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benjamins Son. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy, Cold Day. Snowd at night. my Patients illness Came on, her women Calld again & Shee was Safe Deld at 7 Evn of a Son. I tarried all night. mr Benjamin is absent.   At mr Savages. Birth 1st. receivd 6/ July 10th.  
 

 

 January 21, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, rain at night. mr Ballard returnd. mr Town Sleeps here. I have been at home. Margarett Foot here, Desird me to go & See mrs Norcross whos Breast is Soar. Ephm at his Brothers, Jacky is Sick.   at home. Son Town here.  
 

 

 January 22, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld to Son Jonas to See his oldest Son who has ye Canker. there all night, ye Child very sick.   at Son Jonas, his Child Sick.  
 

 

 January 24, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Herseys Dagt. XX.

Snowd ye morn. Capt Hersey Calld me to See his wife about Day. we got Safe over the river, the freshet rose & it Broke up. his wife Safe Deld at 7h Evng of a Dagt, all like to do well.   at Capt Herseys. Birth 2 nt. 
 

 

 January 25, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cold. at Capt Herseys, he was Seeking a nurs & transporting women home. I tarried all night, my patients Cleverly.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 26, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. Capt Hersey went & brot mrs Conrey to nurs his wife. I went to mr Parkers, Judg Lithgow & Colo Howard there.   at Detoes & mr Parkers.  
 

 

 January 27, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld from mr Parkers to mr Williamss to See his youngest Child who is very Sick, there all night. mr Parker gave me 18/ for attending his Lady in her illness with her Last Child.   at mr Parkers & Williamss. 
 

 

 January 28, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld from mr Wilmss to mr Parkers to See his infant who is unwell, returnd to mr Williamss. mrss Fletcher & Ingerham watcht.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 31, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Seth Williamss Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Williams in travil & was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 7h 40m Evng. I tarried all night. Betsy is Some more Comfortable. mrs Williams & infant are Cleverly.   at Ditoes. Birth 3d. 
 

 

 February 1, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd from mr Williamss, left my Patients as well as Could be Expectd. Calld to See mrs Hersey, Shee is Comforlable. I have receivd 9/ of Capt Hersey as reward for my Servec & 15/ of mr Williams. I Calld at mr Parkers, his Lady made me a prest of 1_ yds ribbin, her infant is better.   at Ditos, Capt Herseys & mr Parkers.  
 

 

 February 7, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Eliab Shaws Dagt.

 Snowd. I was Calld Early ys morn to Eliab Shaws, his wife was Safe Dild of a daught at 1h pm & I returnd home. wrode there, & home, on Saml Braleys Slead. I Calld at my Sons, his Child is Better. Dolly is at mr Hamlins. I began a Cottne Stockin.   at Eliab Shaws. Birth 4th. April 29, 1795, receivd of Son Jona on Shaws acount, 3/.  
 

 

 February 8, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jasson Butlers Dagt. XX.

Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard went to Survey Land for mr Gilley. I was Calld at 5h Evn to See thee wife of Jason Butler at ye hook. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 5h 50m, and I tarried all night & nurst her. receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Butlers. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 February 9, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home & went to meeting, we all went but Cyrus. I went to mr Seth Williamss after Exercises, his Sick Dagt is very Low. I Calld to Sam Pollards, they are well. find Dolly very unwell, Balht her feet & gave her herb Tea.   at Butlers, Hamlins, Williamss, Son Pollards and at meeting. Doct Ward & Patty Bullins Cryd the first time.  
 

 

 February 10, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, raind a little at Evn. Ephm went to Mathew meddow & returnd with his load at 11h morn. I went to Doct Colmans & Bot medesin for which I paid 7/6. Calld at mr Childs, Pollards, Pitses & Son Pollards. Jonas wife & Childn Came home with me. Dolly is very unwell.   at Doct Colmans & other Neighbs. 
 

 

 February 16, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I attended worship at the hook aftern. Ephm Burges & Joanny Jewell Cryd 1st time. I Calld at Capt me loys after meeting.   at meeting & at Capt Meloys.  
 

 

 February 18, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard at mr Noress Surveying. I was Calld at 7 Evn to Moses Sewals wife in Labour. mrs Cleark, Greely & 2 mrss Duttuns assistants.   at mr Moses Sewalls.  
 

 

 February 19, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Sewals Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn. mrs Sewall was Deld of a fine Daugt at 12m past midnight & is Cleverly. I tarried thro ye night. mr Sewall gave me as a reward 1_ yd mode, 2 Skeins Silk, 1 oz thread, a paper & Card of pins. Dolly with her Brors & Sisters Sett out for winslow.   At Detoes. Birth 6th. Benn Porter Sleeps here. Sally goes to her Brors Weding.
