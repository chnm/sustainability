 April 17, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld from Capt Moloys to Benn Branches wife in Labour. I had a fall in the mire by the Bridg between here and Densmores.   at Ditoes & Benn Branches.  
 

 

 April 18, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Branches Son. XX.

Clear the most of the Day. mrs Branch was Deld of a Son at 9h 30m morn. I left Cleverly at 11, Calld at Nath Runels and mr Dyers and Esqr Runels. returnd as far as Son Pollards and went to See mrs Capin, find her not So well as Could be wisht. was at mr Gills, they are well. mrs Abbot is unwell. I Calld to See mrs Parker who is very unwell. Dolly was at mr Pollards, Capins, Gills and mr Burtuns. my Girls have Made me 2 Barrils of Soap this weak.   at [mr Branches]. Birth 18th. receivd 9/ at Deckmans Store April 27th by Branches order.  
 

 

 April 22, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. I Combd flax and wint to the hook for Corn but got none. I bot 28 lb rice and an ounce turnip Seed of Capt Fillabrown, cost 8/3. I Calld at the most of ye Stores, walkt there and home again. Calld to See Capt Welts who is Confind by a wound in his thigh.   at the hook. I was unwell in the night.  
 

 

 April 24, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am more Comfortable. mr Isaac Savage Seconds & wife here on a visit. mr John Abbot Calld me at 6h pm to See his wife who is unwell. I was there this night. Shee and I Slept Some. mr Town took Breakfast here. Dolly went to Son Pollards. mrss Cashin here foren.   at mr Abbots. mr Savage and wife here, Son Town allso.  
 

 

 April 25, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Abbots Dagt. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. mrs Abbot was very unwell all day, her women Calld at 1h pm and Shee was Deld at 3 of a Dagt, her first born. I went in at mr Blacks, his wife and mrss Smith Calld to See mrss Abbot. I went to See mrs Capin, returnd and took Care of my patients all night.   at mr Abbots. Birth 19th. receivd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 April 26, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt John Moloys Dagt. XX.

rainy. I was Calld by Capt Molloy about Day, find his wife very unwell. Shee had Some women Calld at 10hM, and Some Came in at intermition. Shee was Deld at 1h pm of a Dagt, her Second Child, both dagts. I tarried till near night when I left mother and Child Cleverly, and returnd. find my famely well. mr Densmores and Livrmrs Dagts here at Evng. mrss Moloy was unwell all Last night alltho Shee Did not Call me till morn.   at Ditoes and Capt Molloys. Birth 20th. receivd 18/ as A reward.  
 

 

 April 27, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night. I went to mr Capins, find her mending; to mr Abbots, his wife is Cleverly; to mr Hamlins, his wife is unwell; at mr Dickmans, receivd 9/ on Benn Branches acount for assisting his wife in Child Birth. I Calld at mr Smiths and took Tea, went and Slept at Son Pollards. I was in great Danger by a fall from the hors but Escapt unhurt thro Gods preserving mercy.   at Son Pollards and other Neigrs. a rainy night. preservd when in Danger by a fall from a hors.  
 

 

 April 28, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Baxter Came to Son Pollards for me to go and Attend his wife in the operation of an Emetic. it had a favourable opperation and I Came to mr Blacks [&] took Tea with her, went to Son Pollards and Slept.   at mr [Baxters] and others. Dolly bo t a Barril Cyder in a vessel, Price [21/].  
 

 

 May 1, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Claps Son. XX

Clear. mr Hodges I was Calld to See mrss Clap who was in Labour. I left home at 4h pm, walkt as far as mr Hodges, wrode with Edwd Savage Junr the rest of ye way. my Patient was Deld at 5 of a Son and is Cleverly. I tarried all night, Slept Some.   at mr Clapss. Birth 21st. I saw mrss Jones there.  
 

 

 May 2, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrss Parker, Shee is not So well as I Could wish. I Calld to See mrss Kimbal, Shee is unwell; at mr Crages and Dind; at mr Thwings, they are as well as usual; at mr Capins, Shee is mending; at mr Abbots, Shee is feeble; at Son Pollards, they are well as usual. I wrode his hors home.   at Ditoes and other Neighs. Sally gone to her Dads. Cyrus Came home.  
 

 

 May 5, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleasant. mr Ballard, my Self, Selva & Ephm went to meeting at the Acadima. a Discourse Deld by Revd mr Bradford of Pownalboro adapted to the ocation, viz, the opening the Semenary of inslruction. Deacon Coney and Lady Dind with us and I went to mr Livermrs with them. Jonas wife & Children Came here. the house he built for Bizer Benjamin to liv in was Consumd by fire last Evng.   at meeting at ye hook and at mr Livermores.  
 

 

 May 8, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard at Esqr Conys. Dagt Ballard and her Childn went home. Dolly and Sally washt & went to mr Gills. mr Princ Baker here. I was Calld to See Capt Molloys infant, it is unwell. we gave it Rhubarb. I tarried all night.   at Capt Molloys. Dagt Ballard went home, Son Town Slept here.  
 

 

 May 12, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have workt in my Gardin. Capt F Browns Company were Calld to Exercise this Day. Phillip Bullin and Wm Parmer & mr Lambart, Anna & Fanny took Tea here, and the Girls Sett out for home at Dusk. I planted Beens & Cucumbrs.   at home. miss Cocks & Bullin went home.  
 

 

 May 15, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Lambart and wife went to Dine at Son Pollards, and Spend aftern at mr Capins. mr Capin has been writing on Plans for mr Ballard, he bestowd 6/ as a reward for my assisting his wife.   at home.  
 

 

 May 19, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Blacks Daugt. XX.

Clear foren. I went to the hook, bot a Brass kettle wt 5 lb at 4/ pr lb of Capt FilleBrown. Cloudy aftern. I went to mr Pollards, was at mr Smiths, Pittss, Gills and Son Pollards. Came home at 5, Sett Down to write to Doct Barton and was Calld off by mr Hodges to See mrs Black. Shee was Deld at 7 of a Daugt, her 4th Child; number of Sex Equal. I tarried all night, my Patient was Exercisd with Some pain.   at ye hook & fort, at mr Blacks. Birth 22nt. raind at night. fee receivd June 4th.  
 

 

 May 20, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jessy Kimbals Dagt, first Born. XX.

rainy. I was Calld from mr Blacks to Capt Cockss to See his Dagt Kimbal at 4h morn. Shee was very ill but was Safe Deld at 4h pm of a fine Daught. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and returnd home by water; was Some fatagud. our famely were in Bed wn I Came [home].   at Capt Cockss. Birth 23 d. receivd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 May 21, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. Sally Hamlin here, Came yesterday. I was Calld to See Edmond Getchels wife who is unwell, tarried all night.   at mr Getchells.  
 

 

 May 22, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld from mr Gitchels to See Briant Fletchers infant which is very Sick; returnd at Evn.   at Ditoes & mr Fletchers.  
 

 

 May 25, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth X Edmond Getchels Son. XX.

Clear and pleasant Except Some what Cool. mrss Getchell had her women Calld aftern and was Safe Deld of a Son at 4h pm. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected at 7. Calld to See mr Fletchers Child, find it a little more Comforlable.   at Ditoes. Birth 24th. receivd 6/7.  
 

 

 May 26, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jery Badcks Son. XX.

Clear and pleast. I was Calld by Benn Petingal to go to Jerimy Badcoks. went to ye fort by water, was very wet when I arivd there. was taken on hors back there, wrode more than a mile then orderd to return, ye patient being Deld of a Son. I Crost the river in ye Boat after receiving 6/ of mr Petngll for my troubl. I was invited to take Breakfast with mr Smith. I Dind there allso. took Tea at mr Gills. Calld to See mrs Capin, was Calld from there by Brestow Grefin, a man of Coulor whos wife is white. Came home on our hors, went from there by water and was much fatagud by Climbing ye assent. find ye patient Some what ill but I had opt to take rest by Sleep.   Calld to Jery Badcoks. Birth 25th. 
 

 

 May 27, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Grefins Son. XX.

Clear and pleast. I was at the hous of Brestow Grifins. this wife was Safe Deld of a Son at 6 hours pm. I receivd 6/ as a reward, returnd home by water.   at Grifins. Birth 26th.  
 

 

 June 3, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Ezra Smiths Dagt. XX.

Cloudy part of ye Day. I was Calld at 7h morn by mr Ezra Smith to See his wife who was Deld at 9h 30m of a fine Dagt. I tarried with her the Day and night. Shee is as well as Could be Expected. Shee has got ye rash.   at mr Ezra Smiths. Birth 27th. Capt Dancy Left ye fort.  
 

 

 June 4, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, wind to ye Northerd. mr Kilridg Conducted his wife to Capt Deans Vessel at ye hook Bound to the South Shore. I Came from mr Smiths after assisting to git his wife up and to Bed again. Calld at mr Gills. Saw Son Town, his famely are well as usual. I was at Esq Haywoods and Son Pollards. my Dagt was here helping Dolly about her Bed quillt.   at mr Smiths. receivd 7/6 as a reward. Polly [sic].  
 

 

 June 8, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. mr Ballard Sett out to asist in Laying out a road to new Sharon on Sandy river. Ephm went and wrode the hors home. I have been at home, tending Cloath to whiten and Doing Divers other matters.   at home. mr Ballard Sett out to Lay out a road.  
 

 

 June 15, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dolly put in a Bed quillt all tho Shee has been very unwell. mr Capin, Gill, Dagts Pollard and Ballard and mrss P. Pollard Came and assisted her. the Gentmn Came to Conduct them home.   at home. we were quillting on Dollys Bed quillt.
