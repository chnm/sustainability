War Office 
Aug 16 1797.
Inf Cloathing to be forwd to Ensign Wm. R. Boote at Brunswick New Jersey
War-Office, August 16th 1797. - 
Please to deliver to Wm. Hodgdon, to be sent to New Brunswick New Jersey, for Ensign William R. Boote, One suit of (Infantry) Serjeants clothing and two suits for Music - also a fife - 
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr John Harris
