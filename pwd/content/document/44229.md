War Department
16 of February 1793
Sir/
I have the honor respectfully to submit to your consideration certain general ideas, which may be proper for the heads of the Instructions to the Commissioners for treating with the hostile Indians. After you shall have decided upon the general principles of the Instructions, the details may be formed + submitted to your judgment. 
With the highest respect
I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your most Obedt.
hble Servant
Signed, H Knox
