327
May 7th 1798.
Thomas Evans on the
subject of Doctr.  Rey-
nolds' application_
James McHenry Esquire
Secty of War.
No 327-
Recd. June 7th
NN
Dear Sir
I enclose you some letters which have come to me, soliciting my
agency in procuring appointmt. as Surgeons. _ Mr. Reynolds & Mr. Purdie are
personally unknown to me. _ The Gentlemen who recommend them are known
& I have noted upon their letters respectively in a brief manner my estimation
of them.  I am with perfect respect
Your most obed. Servant
T. Evans
June 7, 1798
