Six             West Point July 24th 1789
I have forwarded by the Beaver Capt Jefferson the Returns of Ordinance and Quarter Masters Sores for the month of June 1789-
I have given W Isaac Garrison a Certificate for the Forty Carts of Wood which he furnished the Garrison last Winter and Carting Mr Jame -
The Honorable Major Genl Knox
Secretary of War-
