From Genl Knox--5th April 1791
133 private
Philadelphia, April 5. 1791
Dear Sir
Captain Sayles, having declined accepting the company to be raised in Rhode Island, I ask your recommendation in confidence of some person to fill his place who will do honor the same.  A Captain Holden has been mentioned, but I do not know his character.  A captain of the late war of excellent character would be preferred.  Pray tell me the precise character of Lieutenant Sherman
Your brother having declined to Supply the company I beg you to recommend some person to fill his place.  Major Franks, the inspector, has been sent on with money to be advanced to the contractor.
I have taken the liberty of ordering some clothing to your address, the expences of which will be paid by the person furnishing the supplies.
I amDear Sir,Your obedt, humble ServtHKnox
Col. Jereh Olney
