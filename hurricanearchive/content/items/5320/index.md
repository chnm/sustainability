It was the second night of the hurricane, it was very hot.  No water, No electricity and no generator. My kids where both sick, they where crying. I tried sleeping on the ground outside. Mosquitos where literally eating me alive.  My body felt as if it was on fire.  I prayed for mercy.  I prayed for a breeze.  I asked the good Lord if it could get any worse or is it going to get better?  It was then that the Lord sent my neighbor at 1:00 a.m. with a generator.  I was so blessed and I blessed my neighbor over and over again.  Wesley was my Hero!  We all need wonderful neighbors such as he.
