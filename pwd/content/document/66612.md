Accountants' Office
February 28 1799
Agreeably to an account admitted and allowed by the
Secretary of War there is due to Henry Glen the sum of Thirteen
dollars +nineteen Cents being for the amount of his account for Storing Shells,
Cannon Ball [undecipherable] belonging to the United States in the Town of Schennectady
New York from 1 January to 31 December 1798 ~
[undecipherable] 13. 90.                                           

W. Simmons

Acct [undecipherable]

The Secretary of War
