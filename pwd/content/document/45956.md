Attention Genl Knox

Dated 3 Feby 1794
General Knox's compliments to Mr. Humphreys and will be happy to have a conference with him at his War-Office any time before three o'clock this day, if it will be convenient to Mr. Humphreys
Thursday
Feb. 3d 1794
