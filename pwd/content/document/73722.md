5. February 1800.
James McHenry.
War Department
5 February 1800
Sir
The annexed papers have been received since my letter of the 30 November last, which inclosed all the claims that had then been presented, under the "act for the relief of the refugees from the British Provinces of Canada and Nova Scotia."

I have the honor to be
Sir
Your Obedient Servant
James McHenry
The Secretary of the Treasury
