War Office
March 20th 1797
1 Copy Baron Steuben's Army 
Regulations for the Secy of War.
War Office, March 20 1797
Sir.
Please to send me one of Baron Steuben's military regulations.  
Your Ob' Serv'  
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. Harris.
