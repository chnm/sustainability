Department of War
Accountants Office
April 24 1795
The Treasurer has remitted to you to be transmitted to Lt. John McClallen at Albany for the pay of himself and his recruits, for [portion of page missing] and March 1795, the Sum of Three hundred ten dollars and Ninety Cents, for which sum you will please take his receipt to be accountable to this office for the due application thereof
I am Sir Your obedt. Ser.  Wm. Simmons
Nicholas Fish Esq.
Supervisor
New York
