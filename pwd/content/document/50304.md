Department of War Accountant's Office
May 27 1795
I certify that there is due to John Cadwallader, Brigade Inspection of Huntingdon County State of Pennsyla the sum of fifty Dollars and ninety nine Cents, being so much paid by him for the Services of Capt. Thomas Prather, and four Spies employed on the Frontiers of Huntingdon County, State of Pennsyla in June 1794 in consequence of the Hostile appearance of Indians at that time.
Wm. Simmons Acct.
Dolls 50.99/100
The Secy of War.
