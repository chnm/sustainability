Martha Ballard's Diary, March 2 - 21, 1811

2 7
Clear. I went from Son Towns to Son Lambds, Patty Town with me. Son & Dt L. went home with her. from Son [Towns] to Lambds.  
3 1
Clear and pleast. Son & Dagt L. to Fairfield. at Son Lambds.  
4 2
Clear. Son Town Conducted my husband from his hous to Son Lambds. at ditoes, husband Came.  
5 3
Clear. we were at. Ditoes. 
6 4
Snowd. we are at. Ditoes. 
7 5
X. Birth mrs Newalls Son. XX. 		Clear. I was Calld to Winslow to see mrs Newall. Shee was Deld at 4h am of a Son which wd 11 & 2/1 lb. I tarried with her till after Tea, receivd 9/. at mrs Newals. Birth 2nt. recd 9.  
8 6
Clear. my husband, Self, Son & Dt Lambd & mrs Gill to vasit John Emery. at mr Emerys.  
9 7
Clear part of ye day. we are at Son Lamds. mrs Gill & Childn to Fairfield. Son Jona dind here & Sett out for home. at Son Lambds.  
10 1
Clear. we were at meeting at Clinton. A mr Crosby Spake from Acts 11 Chapt. at meeting.  
11 2
Clear. w went fm Son Lambds to Son Towns. at Son Towns.  
12 3
Clear. at Ditoes. at Ditoes.  
13 4
from Son Towns to Son Lambds. at Lambds.  
14 5
Son L. sent for my husband. Jona Came. at Ditoes.  
15 6
Clear & very pleast. my husband Sett out for home, Allin & wm B. with him in a Carriage of mr Bealses. mrs Gill, Dagt Lambd & I at Colo Mowerss on a vasit this aftern. Son L. & L. Barton there. a Ditoes. my husband went home.  
16 7
very pleast. we went to Esqr Jonstons. at Esq Jonston.  
17 1
Clear & very pleast. Son Jona Sleps at Son Lambds. at Son Lambds.  
18 2
Clear & pleast. mrs Gill & I to mr Philbrooks.   
19 3
Clear part of the day. Jona B. Junr Came to Son Lambds very lambe by a Cutt on his foot. he Sleeps here. at Son Lambds.  
20 4
Clear. Jona Sett out for Augusta. at Ditoes.  
21 5
Clear part of ye day. I have Carded Cottne. Lucy Lambard Spun 2 Skeins. at Ditoes.
