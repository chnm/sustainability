Many lives have been shattered\r\nMany are alive by the grace of God and to Him they are thankful\r\nAnd to them material things don\'t matter\r\n\r\nGod\'s hand will provide them food, shelter, clothing and shoes to wear\r\nAnd all their heartaches and sorrows He will bare\r\n\r\nThere is hope when you give God complete control\r\nHe will take care of all your needs and save your soul\r\n\r\nMany hearts have been broken and many have been reunited with the ones they love\r\nDuring and after the hurricane many only had hope through prayer to God above\r\n\r\nGod is there when all other hope is gone\r\nGod will take care of you - He won\'t make you stand alone\r\n\r\nWhen everything else in life fails\r\nPut God in charge - His power will prevail\r\n\r\nIn good times and bad, in pain and sorrow\r\nGod will take care of you, today and tomorrow\r\n\r\nI pray that you have accepted Jesus into your heart\r\nAnd that in every day of your life He plays a part\r\n\r\nGod will help you to find a place to live\r\nHe has many blessings he will give\r\n\r\nJust pray God will carry through the darkest storm\r\nMany miracles He has already performed\r\n\r\nSo in God place all your trust\r\nGive it all to Him - that is a must\r\n
