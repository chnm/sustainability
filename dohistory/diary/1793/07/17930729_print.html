Martha Ballard's Diary, July 29 - August 4, 1793

29 2
Clear. mr Ballard went to Do business with mr Vauhn, Cyrus to work for him Self up abov here. I have been at home Doing hous work. Sally Spoold a warp. at home.  
30 3
Cloudy, a fine rain at night. Dolly & Ephm relurnd from Son Towns, left them all Comfortable. I have been at home. at home.  
31 4
rain foren, Clear aftern. I went to See Son Pollard & mrs Child, the former is Bitter, ye latter is much as Shee has been. mr Patridge, his Lady & Ruth Rockwood here while I was absent. at Son Pollards & mr Childs.  
1 5
Clear. mr Ballard Doing Business at the hook. my Girls washt foren & went to Quillt for mrs Pollard. I went to See Polly Livermore who is Siesd with St Vites Daunce. was Calld at 11h to See Black Hitty, went by water. at mr Livermrs & Griffins.  
2 6
X. Birth Black hitties Son. XX. 		Clear. I put my Patient to Bed with a Son at 1h, morn & returnd home about Sunrise; took a nap. mrs Livermr Sent for me to help her Dress a blister on Pattys neck. mrs Benjamin here to have a goun made. mrs Porter, Pitts & Dagt Pollard here. mr Ballard been at the hook part of this Day. at Grifins & mr Livermors. Birth35th. receivd 6/ may 17th 1795.  
3 7
Clear. mr Ballard & Son giting in hay. I went to See Patty Livermore, find her a little more Composd, mrs Densmore there. Cyrus informs me yt Jonas Son Jona is unwell. at mr Livrmrs. 
4 F
Clear & hott, my famely all, Except my Self, attended worship forenn. I went aftern, was informd that little Jona was very ill; went to See him. Patty Town Sleeps here. at meeting. my Son & mr [Child hi we wors ].
