Armoury Department
Springfield July 30: 1796
Received from the War Office in a letter from Mr. Samuel Lewis Two Thousand four hundred & Seventy four dollars &
thirty-five cents for the Use of the United States Armoury at this post, & for which I am Accountable for the due application.
David Ames Superintendant
