My Katrina time was a bit difficult.  I went all over the south moving from place to place not really staying in one perticular place for a long period of time.  However when I was in Lake Charles in a state park out there.  This church group set up out there in the park and was very helpful to the people that were affected by Hurrican Katrina.  The church group would supply us with meals, ice, and water several times a day they made sure that we would never run out.  They even offered to wash our clthes for us something we just couldn\'t take advantage of.  This church group would always make sure that we were very well taken care of.  For that I was eternaly thankful for these people coming to our aid in a time of need.  I am originally from Gretna, Louisiana
