NMAH Story: Story

On the day of September 11th, I didn't know about the news until I was in my English class period 5. We were about to watch a movie when we caught a sight of what had happen on news. I was tired that day so i didn't understand what had happend when some of my friends told me about the bombing and the other stuff. I was like I haven't heard about it yet. So in my english class i saw the news and saw the bombing of the twin towers. I was scared, not scared of bombing here at cali, but afraid that more people are dying. I didn't know what to do. Should I cried now? or should i pretend to look away and don't think about it until i get home?"When I thought about this, i am worried about their safety and think about how the people who died will cause their families to be in ruined. When i went back home my mom told me about the story too when she got off to work. She was in shocked because someone over there was living there,too. But they were okay. I've never known that this day would happend. I think that this day was the most terrifying day ever. Hope the world will be in peace.
