M.r Joshua Humphreys
Letter from Gen.l Knox
dated 12 Ap.r 1794
Letter from Gen.l Knox
to prepare Models & Drafts
for the Frigates, April 1794
War Department
April 12, 1794
Sir
I request that you would please immedi-diately to prepare the models for the frame of the frigates proposed by you in your letter of this date and also that you would please to prepare an accurate draft, and models of the same, the latter to have the frame accurately described.--
I am
Sir 
Your obedt Servant
[signed, Knox]
M.r Joshua Humphreys
