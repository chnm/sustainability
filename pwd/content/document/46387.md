Capt Nicholas Hannah
Capt 3d Sub-Legion
April 14 1794
30 
Col. Joseph Howell
Accountant 
War Department
Alexandria April 14 1794
Sir
Inclosed you have the Muster Roll.  Qualified to, It should have been done before, but that I was confined to my bed & the Magistrate's neglecting to call on me which he promised to do before the Post left Town.  
I am Sir with respect Your Humble Servant
N Hannah Capt 
US. Legion
Col. Joseph Howell
