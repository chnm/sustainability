134 Philadelphia 26th June 1798

Sir:

I must again trespass on your goodness by requesting you to send four of the twenty four pounders remaining at Providence to Portland in the district of Main to the address of Lemuel Weeks Merchant in that place. By a letter received from that Gentleman we are informed he has a Brig at Providence returning to Portland which will take them in, should she be there at the receipt of this letter, you will have no more trouble than barely shipping the, should she have sailed you will please to find some other Vessel bound to the same place and put them on board her.

I am Sir

With Sentiments of esteem Your most Obedient Servant

Samuel Hodgdon

Captn Joseph Elliot

Officer Commanding at Fort Wolcott New Port Rode Island
