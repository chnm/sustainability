Phila.  March 17. 1798
Inclosed you have an Account of the Metal, and tools sold you __  Also those receipted for to be paid or returned __ As I am bringing my matters to a close I must insist upon an immediate Settlement of both of these accounts __  The Tools you may return, the money you will bring, for what are not, together with the Metal __ the length of time I have already waited, and the disposition I have sown to serve you, leaves me room to doubt you will immediately Comply with this request.  
S.H
Tho. Cater
