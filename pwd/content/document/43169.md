Letter from the War Office
June 30 1792
to deliver 100 Powder to Colonel Procter
[Encircled 148]
Mr. Wm. Knox
Sir,
The Secretary of War directs that you furnish to the order of Col Thomas Procter, one hundred weight of musket powder for the service of the militia infantry on the 4th of July next - 
Yours Jno Stagg Junr, Chf Clk
War Office
June 30th 1792
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894. R.P.O. 401310.]
