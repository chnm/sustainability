 January 5, 1808, Tuesday

 Clear and Cold. Cyrus was Calld to tend mill for Saml Cuming, he is Sick. I have done a fortuits wash. Hanh B. & Sally Pollard & Wm Bald Sleep here.   at home.

 Clear and more moderate. Sally wenl home with Hannah. mrs Emry took Breakfast and Ephm Came and Conducted [her] home in a Sleigh. EbenZ Moore and his Sister Betsy Calld here, Dind and went on for home. mr John Austin took 6 Sheep of my husband ys Day.   at home. EbenZr & Betsy Moore here. mrs Emry left here.  
 

 

 January 29, 1808, Friday

 Clear. I have been very ill. Hannah did my Bakeing & other work about hous and attended to me.   at home, very Sick.  
 

 

 February 20, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Cold. I have Brewd, knit, finisht a paer of mits for my husband and knit on his Stockin. this is the anneversary of my Birth, 73 years have I Seen, past thro many Sceins. may it pleas ye Great Parent of ye universe to Enable me to liv devoted to his Service the little time I have to Sojourn in this world. Allin Lamb brot us 23 lb Beef.   at home, have Seen 73 years ys Day. Allin, [Barny] & Ephm B. Dind here. Funeral mr Edward Springer.  
 

 

 February 21, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. Cyrus Came home. we did not attend public worship this day. Son Lambd, his wife and Babe here at Evning.   at home. Son & Dagt Lambd Calld in at Evng.  
 

 

 February 28, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and moderate. mr Ballard and Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Stone Discoard from Galations 6th C 8 v. Son Jona and wife Calld in after meeting.   at home.  
 

 

 March 10, 1808, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I had a very ill turn this morn but through ye Goodness of ye Great Parent I feel more Comfortable ys aflern.   at home.  
 

 

 March 15, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. Cyrus went to Mill with _ lb wheat. Dagt Lambard washt her Cloaths & mine. mr Stilson Calld in to See me. this is the aniversary of Charls Gills killing him Self. mr Ballard very Sick all night. I gave him Andersons Pills. I was up to take Care of him 3 times, he had ye Cramp.   at home. my husband very ill all night with [Cramp] & Disintery.  
 

 

 March 24, 1808, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I have wound & Doubled yarn. Son Jona Calld here aftern, informd us that Old mr Cowen Departed this life ys day, aged 89 years.   at home. Death of Old mr Cowen.  
 

 

 March 26, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard attended Funeral of Old mr Cowen. A mr Fogg Performd, delivered a discoars from Revelations 14 C 13 vers, right Blessed &C. Sons Lambard & Cyrus Came home at Evng. I have finisht doubling Daugt Lambards Cottne yn, there were 15 Sceins.   at home. Funeral Old mr Cowen. Death Edsons infant.  
 

 

 March 30, 1808, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, finisht a Stockin mrs Wiman & Dagt Ballard here. Son Ephm has Seen 39 years this day.   at home. Son Ephms Birth day.  
 

 

 April 5, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, raind Some. I heard that Sally Ballard was Sick and went to my Sons, find here relievd and Cleverly. her Complaint was the Collic. I tarried and quilted on my Dagts Bed quilt till Sun Sett. we did not finish it.   at Son Jonas, Sally has been unwel.  
 

 

 April 9, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been kniting. mr Ballard Clearing a piece North of ye hous. Cyrus assisted him fore n, went to the Settlement aftern on Business for us. he brot 2 qts molases & 2 lb Butter.   at home, a trying Scein hapend  
 

 

 April 11, 1808, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been very Sick with pain in my head &C. mrss Cleark Calld here. Jona and wife Came ys aftern. I have got Easier thro Gods Goodness.   at home, very unwell. Son Jona and wife here to Tea.  
 

 

 April 19, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Still remain very ill. mrs Farewel left here for home. my pain Some abated.   at home, very ill yet.  
 

 

 April 29, 1808, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleasant. I went to Lecture, Calld at mr Thwings, Slept at Son Ephms. Sopha been to meeting. I have not been to meeting Since the Sabbath after Thanksgiving till now.   at mr Thwings to Lecture & Son Ephms.

Luke Sawd limbs off Balm giliad trees and Cutt one down.   at home. mr Stedman here. Daugt Ballard & mrs Gilbart here.

 July 3, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, my Self and Cyrus to meeting, it was Communion. Revd mr Stone discoarst foren fm 1st Corinthng 11 C 26 vers, aftern hebrews 12th C 25 vers.  July 10, 1808, Sunday

 Clear part of ye day, raind at Evng. Son Jonas wife & mrs Gill Calld in. mr Ballard & Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Cushman performd, his Text foren Mat 12 C last vers, aftern Prov 22nt 9th vers.   at home, feel [bet ] feeble. Son Jona Sent us Chees, [Bu ] & fish.  
 

 

 July 16, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and hott. I have houghd part of my purple Corn and Cabbages. mr Nathl & Saml Cumings wives Calld here. Daugt Pollard & her Sons here, Saml Sleeps here. Shee & Thoms at Son Jonas, his wife is very unwel. Dagt Lambd been to See her. mr Lambd had a Barl flour brot home. I have mended a pair Cottne hoes for mr Ballard.   at home. 2 mrs Cumingss & Dagt Pollard here, gave us 4_ lb pork.  
 

 

 July 29, 1808, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Ephms 3d Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 3h m to Son Ephms, his wife in Labour. Son Jonas wife went with me. my Dagt Deld at 7h 30m of her 3d Dagt. her Case was Some Alarming but Shee revivd and Seems Comfortable. Dagt B went to Esq Titcombs and Ephms wife had a fainting fit. Old Lady Hamlin and I attended her [this day ].   [at Son Ephms]. Birth 1st. receivd 18 S as reward.  
 

 

 August 4, 1808, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm Except A lite Shower near night. I have gathered Beens & Pees, Sent Some to Son Ephms, his wife is Cleverly. mr Ballard been to Hallooell Surveying. Saml Gill Came Came [sic] here for his Sister.   at home. Old mrs Parker here.
