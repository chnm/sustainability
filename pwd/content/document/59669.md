JA to AA

[JA to AA] XXIX 166 [1797]

Oct.25.

My Dearest Friend
       
I sent you the letters _ I could not keep my hands off of Mabby's. I beg her pardon.
They write me flattering accounts from Phil<sup>a</sup>.
M<sup>r</sup> Anthony writes most confidently. No danger
No fever - alls well.

When [Brister?] goes he should throw [undecipherable] into the [undecipherable] vault [undecipherable]. 

I think we ought to have been together to day. But tomorrow will do.

I am glad Malcolm came out.

We must prepare to go to Phila the first week in Nov<sup>r</sup> if no bad news.

Caroline & all are well

The President of the U.S.
25 October 1797
28 Dec Recd
3 Jany 1798 ansd
