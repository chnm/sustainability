The Paymaster Genl has left in my hands
Two Final Settlement notes belonging to
James Certain amounting to 104 dollrs & 9/90
which he can have by calling for them
at my office, & if he is not able to come
himself he may send a proper Power or
order on me to whom I must deliver them
Dec 6 1786
P T Curtenius
