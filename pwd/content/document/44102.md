War department,
19:th January. 1793.
Sir
Your letter of the 10:th has been received. I have supposed that some hasty omission has been highly blazoned. As far as my view of your conduct extends I consider your experience, your industry and your integrity as highly valuable and useful to the public service -
I am
Sir
Your humble servant
Knox
Major Craig
