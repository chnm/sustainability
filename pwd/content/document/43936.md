Philadelphia 8th December 1792
Enclosed you have the requested invoice relative to Boxes numbers Seven and Eight – your receipt in my hand mentions that they came to hand in good Order, and the Waggoner has been Settled with accordingly

I am Sir

your most obedient servant

Samuel Hodgdon

Major Isaac Craig
