 September 2, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have felt unwel, was so ill aftern that I Sent for Hannat Ballard to Bake for me, her marm Came and got me to bed & nurst. H. & Linda Sleep here, Shubl WickSon allso.   at home, very unwel. this day Dagt Lambard is 37 years old.  
 

 

 September 3, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am more Comfortable for which I wish to thank ye author. mr Ballard to meeting, Text [no entry]. mr Livermore & wife Calld in here, mr Hall [&] wife allso. Lon & Dagt Lambard took Tea here.   at home, am better.  
 

 

 September 6, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of the day. I was Calld to See James Ballard who is unwel. we administred a Clister which Seemd to relieve him. I returnd at 11h am. mrs Farewel Came in, Shee and Jona Junr & Allin Lambard Dind. Allin left Son Towns yesterday, all well there.   at Son Jonas to See James.  
 

 

 September 7, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home till 9h pm when I was Calld by a mr Smith to go and See his wife who is unwel. I left Patty Town here.   at James Smiths.  
 

 

 September 8, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Smiths Son, 7th Child. XX.

Clear and pleasant. I have been with mrs Smith, Shee has been unwel all day. her Neighbour women were Calld in at 1h pm. Shee was Safe deld before 2 of her 4th Son & 7th Child. I receivd 1 lb Tea 6[_] do Sugar and 1 qt of Ginn & _ peck Salt as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 11th. receivd 11S as a reward. Child wd 10lb _ lb.  
 

 

 September 10, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. mr Ballard to meeting. my Childn went to Son Jonas. I went and Dind, was Calld to mr Livermores, tarried all night.   at Son Jonas & mr [Livermores].  
 

 

 September 12, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd to my Sons, was at mr Childs, Westons & mr Hamlins. hear that Elijah Barton has gone away being acused of Shooting mr Chadock which hapned 9th inst when he was asisting a Surveyer in runing out land in [no entry]. there were three guns fired. the men who Cummitted the Crime were in disguise, a woful afair. I went with others to See the Jail.   at Livermors and other Places. Saw Joseph Bowman & Lady.  
 

 

 September 16, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Wm Stones 4th Dagt and 10th Child.

raind part of the day. I was Calld at 5h 30m ys morn to go and See the wife of Wm Stone who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld about 10 of her 4th Daught and 10 Child. I tarried to See her made Comfortable. Calld to See mrs Cleark, find her not able to Sett up. arivd home at 1h pm, did various kinds of work &C. mr Ballard to Son Ephms. I am informd that Elijah Barton & 7 others are in Jail on acount of Chaddocks Death. O my Dear Sister, how I feel for her.   at Wm Slones and Saml Clearks. Birth 12th. receivd a Chees, wd 7 lb octr 7 lb 11 oz.  
 

 

 September 17, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard to meeting. Text foren John 20th, aftern Revd mr Buckminster of Boston performd Text 42 psalm 2nd vers. the Wido Pierce & Dagt Ballard, Sally, James & Elisabeth Dind here. mrs Hall Calld in ys aftern. Luke Barton Came in allso.   at home, do not feel very well. mrs Pierce here.  
 

 

 September 18, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 3h morn to See John Wals wife who is unwel.   at mrs Wals all day, feel feeble.  
 

 

 September 19, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Wals 3d Child & 1st Son. XX.

Clear and warm. mrs Wals illness Came on Early ys morn. Shee was after A hard Labour Safe Delivd about 12 of her first Son and 3d Child. I left her Comfortable and reacht at 2h pm. the inft wd 10_ lb.   at Ditoes. Birth 13th. receivd 9 qts Molases Oct 18th as reward.  
 

 

 September 23, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy the most of the day. I have done Some washing. Rhoda Pollarr Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 September 24, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear the most of the day. Dagt Ballard, Marta, James & [E abh] here. Son Ephm Calld Dolly, Wm, Henry & Barny here. Dolly and Sally Ballard Sleep here. mr Balld been watching his field to keep the Cattle out.   at home. Childn & grand Childn here.  
 

 

 September 25, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren. Dolly & Sally went away after Breakfast. I have done hous work & harvested Some Beens. mr Ballard mending fence by ye Corn.   at home. mrs Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 September 27, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till Evng, began to rain at [3]. mrs Emry Took Tea with us. we pickt hops of the vines of 5 Poles ys Evng. Son Jonas wife Calld in at dusk, Says mrss Cleark is better.   at home. mrs Emry here.  
 

 

 October 1, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 [Clear the most] of the day. mr Ballard to meeting. Son Jona & wife, Martha, James & Elisabeth Supt. Son Pollard and John Bolton Calld here.   at home. Gidion Barton here and others.  
 

 

 October 4, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wm Saunders 1st Son, & 3d Child. XX.

Clear and warm. I was Calld at 1h ys morn to See the wife of Wilm Saunders who was in Labour. her illness Came on at 3h pm and I took her into my Care. Shee was Safe Delivd about 5 of her first Son & 3d Child. I left her & inft Comfortable and reacht home at dusk in ye Evng. Wm Ballard & Wilm Barny Lambd Sleep here this night. the Town was in an up roar by those who are Calld indiens makeing their apearence.   at Wm Saunders. Birth 14th. receivd 9s at mgr Howards Store.

 October 7, 1809, Saturday

 Clear. I went from mr Livermores to Son Ephms. Sist Barton, B. Porter, his wife & Son in law there, they went to the Jail to See Elijah. Sist & I were Calld to Liverms again.   at mr Livermores and Son Ephms.  
 

 

 October 8, 1809, Sunday

[ X. Birth Saml Livermores Son & Dagt. XX.]

Clear. I was at mr Livermores, Shee was Deld of a Son at 5h 30m ys morn and of a Daught at 6h 20. her illness Severe but thro Gods mercie [there] Cleverly. I returnd home before night.   at mr Livermors. Birth 15th and 16.  
 

 

 October 20, 1809, Friday

X. Birth mr Yettons 4th Son. X.

Clear. I was Calld by mr Yetton to See his wife at 1h ys morn from Son Lambards. patient Delivd at 5h Pm of her 4th son and 7th Child. I tarried all night, Patient Cleverly.   at mr Yettns, Hollwell. Birth 17th. recvd 10/6.

Cleard before day. I was at Wm Brooks, his wife deld of a Dagt at 9h morn. I returnd home at 1h pm, receivd 2/1 busl Corn, price 50 Cents.   at Wm Brookss. Birth 18th.

 November 3, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Epms Son, 4th Child. XX.

raind Some. mrs Hamlin and Titcomb were assistants at the birth of Son Ephmss Son & 4th Child which took place at ye 1st ys morn. they left there at 3 & went home. my Dagt Comfortable. I returnd home at 1h Pm, Sophia with me. Patty Town went to Nurs her aunt. mrs Farewel & Sophi Sleep here.   at Son Ephms. Birth 19th. Patty went to Nurs. receivd 3 dols.
