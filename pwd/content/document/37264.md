Sir Philada 10th May 1785
Agreeably to your request fo the 30th April last I have sent to Mr Mott Treas.r of N. Jersey a return of the two Certificates issued to you for pay & subsistence as A L Camp the Obsticle I flatter myself is now removed that has deprived you of the Interest due on them.
Cap. Jon Dayton Jos. Howell Junr
