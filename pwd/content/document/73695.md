January 30th 1800
I Certify, that there is due to Daniel Davis late a Private in Captn Street's Co. Collo Carlisle's Regiment of Marylands militia employed on the Western Expedition in 1794, the sum of Eighteen dollars, & 9/100 , for his pay in full to the 10 December 1794, the expiration of his services which sum is payable to Saml Smith per order -
W. S.
Militia on Western Expedition Drs. 18.9
The Secretary of War
