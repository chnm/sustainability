War Dept.
James Willkinson and
Benjamin Hawkins Esquires
Commissioners Recd.
War Department
16th July 1801
Gentlemen
By a letter received this morning from Mr.
Davis that gentleman announcs that he is under
the necessity of [undecipherable] to act under the appointment
of joint Commissioner with you to hold treaties with 
certain Indian Nations South of the Ohio. Andrew
Pickens Esquire has been appointed in [undecipherable]
and request to join you as soon as possible in 
the mean while you will proceed on the business
of your mission conformably to the commissions
and instruction herewith transmitted.
Mr. Thomas Mounger Contracts for
the state of Georgia is directed to hold himself
in readiness to furnish supplies of rations at
Fort Wilkinson [undecipherable] your requisitions
I have the honor to be,
with respectful consideration
Gentlemen
Your very obd. Servt.
H. Dearborn



James Wilkinson and
Benjn. Hawkins, Esquires
