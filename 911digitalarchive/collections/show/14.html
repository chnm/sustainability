Middle East and Middle Eastern American Center Interviews

Title

Middle East and Middle Eastern American Center Interviews

Description

This project was conducted under the auspices of the Middle East and Middle Eastern American Center (MEMEAC), at the Graduate Center, City University of New York (CUNY). Between June 2002 and March 2003, 72 interviews were conducted with Arab and/or Muslim Americans. This collection contains 68 of those interviews and represents an extensive and in-depth examination of the impact of the backlash on the Middle Eastern diaspora communities.
