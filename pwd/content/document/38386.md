General Office
Dec 6 1786
Will you see to good onto examine the rolls of the German Reg and see if JOhn Whitman war of one of the Penn Company therein and if he mustered died on Feb 1770 as certified in the     which please remit to enclosed in your I am    your
Sincerely 
W Nicholson
