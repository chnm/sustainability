September 11, 2002. Memorial celebration the year after the September 11, 2001, attacks on New York and Washington.\r\n\r\nNunez Community College is located in Chalmette, in St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana. The school was formed by the 1992 merger of Elaine P. Nunez Technical Institute and St. Bernard Parish Community College. As a result, it offers both academic and vocational classes, a situation which is unusual within Louisiana. In 2005 it was heavily damaged by flooding\r\ndue to Hurricane Katrina.\r\n
