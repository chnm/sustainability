Throughout the course the emphasis is on taking the areas of existing student interest and using these as windows to elaborate upon American history to broaden students' perspectives. This is necessary as they are in no way required to do any American history and have hundreds of courses to chose from. In alternative semesters, a course on modern American is offered.

Students can also study American history transnationally in thematic courses on Environmental History; Women in the Modern World; and the Politics and Practice of History, a pre-honours seminar.
