D. of War Accountants' Office
                                                May 9th. 1795               

Thomas Overton. Esq.r Insp.r Fayetteville

  NoCarolina                                                              
                                 Sir                                                  
                                   Your letter of the 20.tj ult.o to the late Accountant I have this day received 

covering the following receipts viz.t

      Capt.t Griffith I. M. Rees' for two hundred dollars, Lieu.t Robert Roward for one hundred thirty Six dollars and 

93 Cents and Lieu.t Jonathan Robeson for one hundred and twenty seven dollars and 40 cts

                                I am Sir J. C.
                                  William Simmons
