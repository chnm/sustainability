9th June 1790from M. McConnellEntd
Philada 9th June 1790
Dear Sir
Inclosed are Lieuts Jno Barclays and Andrew Johnstons orders for their months pay for the use of the society of the Cincinnatis
The certificates I will thank you to take the trouble to inclose by post as soon as convenient.  I hope the business of adjournment will come right in the Senate and am [undecipherable] with much esteem 
Your Obt ServtMat. McConnell
