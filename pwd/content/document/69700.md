July 22d 1799
I Certify that there is due to Tobias Lear Secretary to George Washington, General of the Armies of The United States, the sum of Three hundred fifty one Dollars & 9/100, being for his pay forage & subsistence for April, May, June 1799.
W. S.
Pay of the Army 225.00
Forage of Officers 36.00
Subsistance of the Army 90.[0]9
Dolls 351.[0]9
The Secy of War
