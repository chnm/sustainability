Martha Ballard's Diary, June 29 - July 3, 1792

29 6
Some Showers foren. Son Town, his wife & Dagts Sett out from here at ye 11th h morn Bound for Pittstn. I have wed Gardin Some. Hannah is not So well as usual. at home.  
30 7
Clear & hott. I have been at home, wed my Beets & Sett Cabbages . Son Town, his wife & Dagts returnd from Pittstn; his Dads famely are well, his Sister Mc Causlin is more Comfortable than Shee has been, her inft is very Low. it is not Expected it will long Survive. Son Town left here & went as far as my Sons. at home. have not been So well as usual. Son & Dagt Town & Childn Sett out for home.  
1 G
Clear. my Dagt Hannah had a tooth Extracted this morn by mr Livermore. mr Ballard & my Self & Son Ephm attended public Worship. mr Smith Discourst in ye morn from John 7th C 37:V in ye aftn fm 1 Corinth 4th 20th. mr Livermr here after Exercise. Cyrus Came home at Evn. Hannah is not So well as I Could wish. I attended worship. mr Joseph Slackpl had a Son Born, it was not alive.  
2 2
Clear. I workt in my Gardin Some foren. was Calld to See Georg Bollons wife who was in Labour, at 1h pm. Shee remaind in a Linguring Situation thro ye night. at Georg Boltons.  
3 3
Birth Georg Boltons 4th Son & 5th Child. 		Clear. my Patients illness increast at ye 11th h morn & Shee was Safe Deld at 30m pm of a Son, both mother & inft are Cleverly. I left them at 3 & arivd at home before night, Considerably fatagud. Hannah & Patty at mr Densmores. at Does. Birth 25th. I Bot a pair Shoes for Hannah, Cost 4/8.
