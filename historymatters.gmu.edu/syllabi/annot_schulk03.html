To illustrate differing approaches to the study and teaching of history, I play two popular songs for my students. First, I play and we discuss Billy Joel's "We Didn't Start the Fire." Then I play Bob Dylan's "With God on Our Side" and we compare the two songwriters' differing approaches to the study of history. I emphasize to students that I teach "Bob Dylan History," history as interpretation, rather than "Bill Joel History," as one so-called "fact" after another.
