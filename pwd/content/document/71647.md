War Department 
Trenton 7th October 1799
James McHenry
recd 10th
Ansd 22d Novr 
requesting an Estimate of the Clerks necessary to be employed in this office, and their salaries also including the Accountants & Messenger with the Contingencies.
War Department, Trenton, Octor: 7, 1799
Sir,
To enable me to complete my Estimate for the ensuing year, I request you will please to furnish me with an Estimate of the number of Clerks necessary to be employed in your Office, and their Salaries; also including your own, and that of a Messenger, with the Contingent Expences, for the year 1800.
I am Sir, Your Obedt: Servt: 
James McHenry
The Accountant of the War Department.
