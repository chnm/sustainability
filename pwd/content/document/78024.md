Saml. Hodgdon Esqr.                        Accountant's Office
Philada.                                   March 3 1801
Sir
Nathaniel Smith has received a
credit at the office for 3000 Cartouche Boxes, delivered in
the Public Stores, at Philada., agreeably to your receipt
dated 6 Nov. 1800 at 1 14/100 drs. amounting to $ 3420.
o which you will be pleased to make the necessary
entries on the Books of your Office.  I am de
W.S.
