War Office
February 15th 1798
Priv Woolen Overalls to be Dld Lt McRea for the Marine Service
AF
War Office 15 February 1798
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to Lieutt McRea for the Marines on board the Frigate United States, eight pair of Woollen overalls
and am Sir 
your Obd Servt
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr: John Harris
