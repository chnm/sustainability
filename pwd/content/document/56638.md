George Clendinen Esq.
Late Lieut. Col. of Kenhawa County
Virginia__care Col. G. Jackson. Congress
Department of War Accountants Office
Febuary 21th. 1797
Sir
You stand charges on the Books of this
Office with a considerable sum of Money received in the year
1791 for pay of the Militia sc. of Kenhawa County, Virginia
you are therefore requested to forward without delay the ne-
cessary Vouchers, that the different sums paid by you /if 
admintable/ may be taxed to your Credit and the Balance
if any remains in your hands, be paid to the Treasurer of the 
United States to close your account, the length of time
elapsed since you received the money, makes it highly necessary
a settlement should immediately take place ___
I am Yg,
Wm Simmons
Acct.
