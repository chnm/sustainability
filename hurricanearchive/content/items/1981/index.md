For the hurricane I went to Texas. It was boring there but it was clean and there was a lot of things to see. I didn`t like the school  I went to but I liked the teachers.I got lost in the school I went to in Texas.\r\n\r\n\r\nWhen I arrived in Louisiana  there was a lot of houses that were damaged. I went in my room and there was a hole in the ceiling. We could not  sleep at my house so we had to sleep at my grandmother`s house. I hated sleeping there because there were roaches there and when you go to the restroom the door locks by itself when you\'re in there.\r\n\r\nWe stayed at my grandmother`s house for three days, then we went home. We found that the ceiling still had a hole in it because no one fixed it, but we were able to sleep there.
