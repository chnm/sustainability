Navy Department
Secretary of War
Ben Stoddart
29 May 1800
Secy of the Navy 
[illegible] proposes I should pay 200 to Mr French at Public expense.
Navy Dept. 29 May 1800
Dr Sir
I must have my office in the City - I have indeed taken steps for that purpose.  I should suppose Genl [& Mrs?] Lee would be glad to take the House of Mr. French - perhaps not to pay so high a rent.  The best way [to set?] clear of this business will probably be, to pay 200 Drs at Public expense & to offer the House to Mrs. Lee for the remainder.  He will probably be at the City, soon after the 1st Jun. 
I am [illegible] Ben Stoddert 
Jas McHenry.
