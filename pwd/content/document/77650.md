War Dept
Thee Accountant of the War Department
War department
29 December 1800.
Samuel Dexter
Recd. same day 
Ansd. 31st 
requiring a statement of 
Monies offered for the 
Fortification of Port & Harbor --.
Statement Furnished
31 Dec 1800
War Department
29 Dec 1800.
Sir
You will be pleased to furnish me as
soon as possible with a Statement of the
Expenditures on account of the appropriations
for the Fortification of Ports & Harbors, designating 
the amount of expenditure of each port.
I am Sir
Your obed Servt.
Saml. Dexter
Thee Accountant of the War Dept
