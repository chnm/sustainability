Middletown Augt 31st 1796
Sir
I have received your letter of 3d Inst. with the inclosed invoice on the 25th.  I will thank you to forward Music Cloathin as soon as posable.  I transmit you a Receipt for the Cloathing agreable to the Invoice
I am you Obedient Humble Servt.
Edward Miller Capt. 
Colo. Samuel Hodgdon
NS Complements to friends Harris & Coalesworthy
