 October 1, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I tarried at ditoes till aft diner. Sist Barton Came there. I Came home, Calld at son Lambds, Thoms is unwell, Barney allso. I workt in my Gardin afer I Came home.   at Ditoes & son Lambds.  
 

 

 October 5, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to Son Lambds, his Son Barny is Sick with the measels. I Calld at mr Towns, his Child has ye Disentary. mrs Andrus Spake to me as I Came home, her famely are unwell, mrs Dullun allso. I Sent her Safron & meregoulds.   at [Son] Lambards & mr Towns.  
 

 

 October 16, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I have been very unwell. Polly Farewell did my hous work. I went aftern to see Barna Lambd, find him very low indead; tarried there all night. I Calld at mr Andrews, find 5 of his famely Sick with ye measels.   at Son Lambds & mr Andrewss. 
 

 

 October 18, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Monasah Duttuns Son. XX.

Clear. I Came home, did Some washing, wido Savage Came here near night. I was Soon after Calld to go and See mrs Duttun who is in Labour and was safe Deld at 7h Ev of a Son, her Second Child. Shee is as well as Could be Expected as Shee has the measels. I Sett with her till aft mid night, mrs Springer afterwards.   at mr Andruss. Birth 8th. Novr 15 th recd 6/7 of mr Andrus.  
 

 

 October 21, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Duttun, her milk is Coming and Shee Seemd very much distresed till 10h Evn when Shee got Easier and I went to bed at 11, got Some Sleep.   at mr Andruss. 
 

 

 October 24, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been unwell. mr Ballard to meeting. I was Calld at 10h Evn to mr Andruss, mrs Duttun very Sick. I spent ye night there.   at mr Andruss at 10h Evng. 
 

 

 October 25, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Duttun very ill yet. I Came home. Sally Church washt for me, mr Ballard to mr Breads.   at Detoes  
 

 

 October 26, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I am informd that mrs Duttun is very Sick. I walkt there, tarried all night. her aunts Pain & Springer and Doct Howard there, he put a Blister on her Breast, gave her Squells, &C, &C.   [at Detoes.] [Doct] Howard there.  
 

 

 October 27, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & rain. mrs Duttun Some more Comfortable but very weak. mrs Edson was Calld and I Came home.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 October 28, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I was Calld at 2h pm to see mrs Duttun whome was thot to be Expireing. I sett with her all night. mrs Edson went home, mrs Arnald Came and Sett with me. mrs Ands is allmost Confined with a pain in her Back.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 October 29, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a great frost. I left mrs Duttun at 7h m, her Babe is unwell. I have been unwell, Sally Church helpt me. I have paid her 4/ for what Shee has done for me this weak. I was Calld about Sun Sett to go and assist to put on the grave Cloaths for mrs Duttun who is now Expired. her husband is Come to see her, arivd before Shee was diad. mrs Arnald & Cypher & Betsy Church assisted me to perform what was Done, the Babe is very unwell.   at Detoes. mrs Duttun Expird.  
 

 

 October 31, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & some rain. we all went to the funeral of mrs Duttun, Jonas famely allso. mr Wilboro Prayed [&] Deld a discoars on ye ocation.   at didoes.  
 

 

 November 2, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my friends left here. I have not been well. Sally Church washt for me. Calvin Ballard Calld here, Son Jona allso; he informd that mr Duttuns inft Expird is buried ys day.   at home. Calvin Ballard here.  
 

 

 November 3, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. Sally workt for me, went home at Evn. I was Calld at 7h Evng by mr Evlith to go and See his wife. the wrideing was very bad, but we arivd Safe from harm.   at mr Evliths.  
 

 

 November 4, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Evliths Daugt. XX.

Cloudy. mrs Evlith was Safe Deld at 2h ys morn of a daughtr, her first Born. I lefe her afler he brot mrs Conry as Nurs. Shee was very ill but I left her as well as Could be Expected. mrs Eads Deld by Doct Cony, her inft is dead.   at Ditoes. Birth 9th. recd 10/6 as a reward.  
 

 

 November 20, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, done my hous work, ironed and Bakt an oven full of pumkin Pies. A mr Steward, his wife and five Childn Calld here on their way (from Hallifax in mount vernon) to Canaan. they have been 16 days on their joirney. I gave them Some refreshment. mr Ballard been down below, informs yt Benn Page fell from Timy Pages phraim and was much hurt.   at home. a famely Calld here bound to Canaan by the name of Steward.  
 

 

 November 28, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. Sons Jona, Lambard and wives and 5 of Jon as Childn Supt with us, Hannah was not here. I am informd at Evng of the Death of Colo Duttuns wife and that her Funeral was attended from her Son SamlS. may the visitation of providence be Sanctified to all nearly Concerned and the Call be heard by us all to be allso ready, as it is apointed to all men once to die and after death the Judgment. may we have our loins girt our lamps Burning that we may not be [Surprised] when the Bride groom Shall Come.   at home, am informd of ye Death of Col o Duttons wife.  
 

 

 November 29, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jonas [3d Daugt & 7th Child].

Clear and pleast. I rose at 4h ys morn. mr Ballard Sett out to Survey for Judg Robbins. I have been Seting my hous in Some degree of order. Son Ephm made me a present of a Chest. W. Cypher brot it from ye Shop and I filld it with lining. was Calld about 2h pm to go and See Son Jon as wife. Shee had her women Calld at 9 and was Safe Deld at 11 of her 7th Child and 3d Daught & is Cleverly.   at Son Jonas. Birth 10th. I Came home at 2 and got Some Sleep.  
 

 

 November 30, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy ye most of the day. Sally Church Came and helpt m wash. I went to my Sons, find them as well as Could be Expected. Cyrus went to Son Pollards, Carried the wool we had Carded, and after a Girl, but Shee was not ready to Come and he returnd without her. Nabby Fall is Come to nurs my Sons wife. mrs Farewell Calld here on her return home.   at my Sons. Sally Church helpt me, I pd her 2/1_. mrs Farewell Calld.  
 

 

 December 2, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, Son Jon a and his Son Jon a, went to Survey Land for mr Whipple. I was at my Sons, assisted her some and dresed the infant. Came home and Bakt bread, Beef and pork for Super. Cutt raggs for flocks & Coverled at Evng. Cyrus & Bill got wood.   at my Sons.  
 

 

 December 3, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. Son Jona Came for me to go and Dress his infant. mrs Hodges there. Shee Calld here, had ye youngest of her Dagt infts with her. I did my aironing aftern and Cutt raggs at Evng.   at [Ditoes]. mrs Hodges here.  
 

 

 December 5, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, raind at Evng. I went to Son Jonas about noon. mr Ballard and Cyrus Calld in after meeting and took Supper with them. mrs Gillbart and Saunders Came there at Evng.   [at they are Comfortable].  
 

 

 December 7, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work. mrs Farewell Calld here. I went to my sons, find mrs Gill and Dagt Lambd there; they Came home with me. Ephm & Allin Came and Slept here. Jason Peirce & wife at my Sons. Son Cyrus been to Reedfield for Rachel Allin, but did not obtain her. mr Gill, Sons Lambd & Jona here at Evng.   at Ditoes. Dagt Lambd & mrs Gill here.  
 

 

 December 10, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I have done my hous wk and mended hoes. mr Ballard gone to mr Lambds. mrs Cypher brot me 10 Egg, 1/10d. I let her have 1/6. I was Calld to mr Pittss at 9h Evn.   at mr Shubal Pittss at Evng. mrs Cypher here.  
 

 

 December 11, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. [Birth Shubal Pittss 2nd Son. XX.]

Cloudy and Some Snow. my Patient was Safe Deld at 2h 5m ys morn of her Second Son and 4th Child. I tarried with her till afternn, left her & inft Cleverly and Came home, thro some falague, had my hous work all to do.   at Ditoes. Birth 11th. recd 9/4d as a reward.
