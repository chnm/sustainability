mail-

Fort Feyett[sic]
2.d Jany. 1801
Gen.l Wilkinson
Fort Fayette
2d Jan.ry 1801
Sir
It always gives me satisfaction to afford any Indulgence to a meritorious officer compatible with the discharge of his Duty. Your application for a Furlough must come through your Colonel. I am Sir
With consideration & respect
Your ms. obd.t-
Jas Wilkinson
I am obliged by your good wishes, & shall reciprocate them, so long as you continue the good officer I knew you} JW
Lieut. John Wallington
4th Regt Infy
Fort Nash
Via Nashville Cumberland
