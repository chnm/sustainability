 June 8, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I left my patient after Breakfast, arivd at Son Lambarts at 11. mrs Capin there. I Dind there and Came home. Did up my work, feel Some fatagud. Dagt Lambart Cookt a Loin of veal for me on which we dind. Son Jona Dind there.   at Ditoes, Ebn and Son Lambarts. mrs Capin was there.  
 

 

 June 11, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Daniel Fosters Dagt. XX.

rainy. I Bakt. Son Lambart Calld me to See his wife who is afflicted with the tooth aik. I was Calld from there at 3h pm to See the wife of Daniel Fosterss. who was in Labour, and was Deld of a fine Dagt, her third Child, at 8h pm. I left them Cleverly and returnd to Son Lambarts at 11h Evn. find my Dagt has had her tooth Extracted by Doct Colman and Shee is Easy. I tarried there this night, Slept with my Dagt.   at Son Lambarts. my Dagt unwell, went from there to Daniel Fosters. Birth 29th. receivd 6/ as reward. augst 24 recd 3/.  
 

 

 June 15, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Abial Heringtons 3d Dagt.

 Clear. I have workt very hard till after Six pm whin I was Calld in hast to go to See the wife of Abiah Herinton who was in Labour, and was Deld at 7h 30m by the help of mrs Smith. I found her and her infant Dagt Cleverly on my arival. I tarried all night, there were 22 in number Slept under that roof ys night.   at mr Braleys. Birth 30th.  
 

 

 June 16, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Some rain aftern. I returnd from mr Braleys. I Calld to See the wido Jones, Shee is not very well; at mr John Joness, Ruthe Lee is very unwell. I Calld to See mrs rice, Shee is unwell. Dind at Son Pollards. Calld at Lambarts. Came home, Skimd my milk and was Calld Directly to See mrs Rice who is in Labour, her women were Calld but Shee had an unnatural travel.   at mr Bralys and other friends.  
 

 

 June 17, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Rices Dagt. XX.

rainy morn. mrs Rice was Deld at 9h 30m, mrs Rice was Dild of a fine Dagt, her third Child. I returnd home at 2, mr Lambart with me. he Butchered a Swine for us. I Sett up very late to Do the work which is necessary and do feel very much fatagud.   at mr Rices. Birth 31st. fee receivd.  
 

 

 June 18, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Son Lambart Came and Cutt my pork and I Salted it. I feel very unwell but have worried thro Doing my work. mrssmc Master & her Dagt Bell Calld here. Cyrus had of Saml Cony 1 galn Molases and 1/2 galn W. I. rhum, price of both was 9/. we have it on mr Gills acount.   at home. mrs mc Masters & Bell here.  
 

 

 June 21, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I have been at home, am unwell yet. mrs Dingly here aftern, it Seems Cleverly to See her So kind.   at home.  
 

 

 June 23, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Rolandss Son & 4th Child. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld at 1h morn to See the wife of mr Rollands of the South Parrish. Shee was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 6h 30m, and I returnd home at 1h pm. left my patients Cleverly. I receivd as a reward 9/. Cyrus and Ebenz brot Poles for our beens and Sett them. Cyrus went to mill.   at mr Rolandss. Birth 32nd. receivd 9/.  
 

 

 June 26, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I was at home all Day, had a very ill turn at Evn. mrs Ficky here, eat Dinner.   at home. feel very unwell.  
 

 

 June 29, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye Day, some Showers. I have Sett muskmellon plants and Done my hous work. mrs Heartford Calld here. Betsy Andrews Came here at noon to work for me.   at home.  
 

 

 July 1, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Dingly this morn, find her very unwell. her Throat is Soar and Shee pukt blood. I Sent her Some honey. I attended Lecter after noon. was to See David Pollards Child, it has been very Sick. Dagt Pollards infant is very unwell. Dagt Lambt was there. I am informd that Ruth Lee Expird this morn at 10h.   at mr Dingleys and at meeting. Revd mr Farrer performd. mr Gillit made the first prayer. Death mrs Lee.  
 

 

 July 3, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ephm Burges Son. XX.

Clear and Comfortable. I was Calld at 8h morn to See the wife of Ephm Burges who was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 10. I left her Cleverly.   at Ephm Burgess foren, at meeting aftern and at mr Crags. Birth 33d. I Came home from mr Burgess at 3h pm, then walkt to meeting. the remains of Rhuth Lee were Deposeted in the house of worship and after Service were Conducted to the Burying ground of the Honb Daneil Cony, and there interd. mr Crage Calld me to See his little Son who is unwell. I Calld to See Dagt Lambart, Shee is some unwell. at mr Crages & Son Lambarts.  
 

 

 July 4, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Sett out aftern to go and See Dagt Lambart, was mett by mr Kitrick who wisht me to go and See his wife. I went and tarried all night, my Patient Some unwell. I went to bed and Slept very well.   at mr Kitridges.  
 

 

 July 5, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. Son Lambart Calld me from mr Kitridges to See my Dagt. I tarried there till aftern, then went home. returnd and Came home again. left my Dagt more Comfortable. mrss Burtun and her Son James Slept here.   at Ditoes & Son Lambarts. mrss Hamlin here and mrss Burtun Sleeps here.  
 

 

 July 6, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinkly Junr Dagt. XX.

Clear and warm. I rose and hoed Some before my Door, was Calld by James Hinkley Junr to go to new france. I went. his wife was Deld of a fine Dagt between 12 and 1 O Clok aftern, and I returnd. then went to Son Lambarts. Son Jon a and wife & Dagt Pollard there. mrs Capin & Sally Webber were here while I was absent.   at James Hinkleys at new france. Birth 34th. receivd [8/7]. mr Burtuns Vesil was launchd 20 m before I came home.  
 

 

 July 8, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Kitridges Son. XX.

Clear and warm. I wed gardin in ye morn. went to See Dagt Lambart, Shee is unwell but wrode my hors to Son Pollards. I went there, was Calld by mr Kitrich to See his wife who is in Labour. her women were Calld at 8h Evn, Shee was Deld at 10 of a Son, her first born. mr Kimbal was there after me. I Could not leave my Patient and he returnd with out me.   at Sons Lambarts & Pollards & mr Kitridges. Birth 35th. 
 

 

 July 9, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came from mr Kitridge at two hours morn to mr Lambarts, find my Dagt more Comfortable. Shee has been very Smart all Day. mr Ballard and Bullin returnd from their Tour of Surveying in good health. I Came home at near Sun Sett.   at Son Lambarts. Birth 
 

 

 July 10, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, my Self and Cyrus attended public worship. I Came to Son Lambarts at noon. we Supt there after meeting. Shee wrode home with me and Cyrus went home with her. Sally Cocks went to See mrs Kimbal, Shee was Deld of a Dead dagt on the morn of ye 9th inst. the operation performd by Ben Page. the infants Limbs were much dislocated, as I am informd.   at Son Lambarts and at meeting.  
 

 

 July 12, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. I Came home and went into the gardin, Sett Beets. Betsy went to try her goun on. mr Moses White applied to me to assist his wife Some future day. mr Ballard and Cyrus are gone to Coart, Ebinz haying.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 July 22, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Lambts Son & first Born. XX.

Clear and very warm. Dagt Lambt had her women Calld this foren, and was Deld of a Son at 6h pm. Shee was very ill but thro the great goodness of God, Shee and inft are alive and as Comfortable as Can be Expected. I am with her this night, Dagt Ballard allso.   at Son Lambarts. Birth 36th. ["Uncle Allen, July 22/96" written here; does not appear to be in Mrs. Ballard's writing.]  
 

 

 July 23, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am with my Dagt Lambart. Shee has no other Nurs yet but is as well as Can be Expected. Dagt Ballard went home. I watcht. Debby Law Came and washt and Slept there.   at Son Lambarts.  
 

 

 July 24, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ansel Neys Son. XX.

Clear morn, a heavy thunder and rain aftern. I was Calld from Son Lambarts at 10h m to AnSel Neys wife who was Deld of a son, her first Born, at 2h pm. heavy thunder while her illness was on her. I left mother and infant Cleverly, Came home, put Clean Linning on and went to Son Lambarts. find her without any help Except Dagt Pollard there with both her Childn, and Jon as lit Dagt which was brot to take the milk from Dollys Breast.   at Ditoes and at Ansil Neys. Birth 37th. I receivd 9/3_ of mr Ney.  
 

 

 July 25, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been with my Dagt. Polly Butterfield Came to be her Nurs at Eve. I tarried this night, mother and inft as will as Can be Expected.   at Son Lambts, her Nurs Came at Evn.  
 

 

 July 27, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Whites Dagt. XX.

Clear and very warm. Son Jonas wife Came here. I houghd Cabages, gatherd beens and went to mr Moses Whites wife who was in Labour, and was Dild of a Dagt at 10h Evn. I tarried all night.   at mr Moses Whites. Birth 38th. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 July 28, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I returnd home. mrs Dingley Came here. I Cut herbs to Dry, houghd french turnips, then went to See my Dagt Lambart, find her very Comfortable. Dagt Pollard & Childn & mrs Carter there to Tea. mrs Carter bestowd 18/3_ for my assisting her in travil June 5th.   at Ditoes and Son Lambarts. Shubal Pitts & Sally Cocks were Joind in Marriage ys Day.
