City of WashingtonWar Department,21 June 1800.
Sir,
Since my letter to you of the 16th inst. I have reqeived your's of the 19th by M.r Perkin, and approve of what you have done with regard to M.rSephard.  The enclosed Letter, which I have left open for your perusal, will inform him of my having confirmed your Determination.
The Sum you requested ^2600 Dollars I remit by M.rPerkin agreeably to your Order and Estimate.
I am, Sir, Your ObedServ.tSam.l Dexter
M.r Samuel Annin
