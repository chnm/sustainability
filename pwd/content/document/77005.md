Intendant of Stores OfficePhiladelphia 15th October 1800
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to John Wilkins Esq. Quarter Master General or order for transportation to Colo Ebenezer Stevens Agent for the Department of War New York, the following clothing, arms &c for the use of the after mentioned companies, of the 2d Regiment of Artillerists and Engineers_to complete the requisition of Major Decus Wadsworth dated the 5th inst Vizt
For Captain Samuel Eddins Company, Fifty seven artillery Hats, four Sergeants Coats, Four sergeants vests, seven sergeants linen overalls, eight sergeants woolen overalls, six sergeants Shirts, one music Coat, thirty nine privates Coats, eighty privates woolen Overalls, two Musiciansswords & two sword Belts and fifty six bayonet Scabbards.
For Capt. Francis K. Hugers Company Thirty one artillery Hats, two sergeants Vests, eight sergeants woolen overalls, two Sergeants Shirts, thirty-seven privates Coats, one hundred privates woolenoveralls,
overalls, twelve shoulder knots, two Swords and Belts for non commissioned officers, two Swords and Belts for Musicians, & sixty four bayonet Scabbards.
For Captain John Bishops Company sixty nine artillery Hats, four sergeants Coats, four sergeants vests, eight sergeants woolen overalls, eight sergeants Shirts, two music Coats, sixty three privates Coats, ten shoulder knots, four Swords and belts for non commissioned officers.
Your most obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John HarrisPublic StorekeeperPhiladelphia
