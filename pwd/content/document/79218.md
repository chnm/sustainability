War Office
July 19. 1798
Musk. Bayonets, Ramrods
& Flints to be Dld John Nichols


War department
July 19. 1798
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to John Nicholson four
hundred and fifty steel Ramrods, four hundred and fifty Bayonets
and two hundred flints, to fix to the Brass mounted Muskets in Store
James W Henry
Samuel Hodgdon



Mr. John Harris.
