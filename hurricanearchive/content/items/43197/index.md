Now, and even shortly after Katrina, I try not to think about it. And, actually, besides the annoying news specials that come on TV every August, I never do.  Sitting around thinking about Katrina and what happened and how we lost everything is not going to help anyone accomplish anything. Yes, the government failed us, and couldn\'t have cared less, but nothing much has changed- nor will it ever.  At this point, if you haven\'t gotten yourself together after that storm, I think that is to no one\'s fault but yours.
