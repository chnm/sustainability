Sir
Having just received your letter, accepting the office of Secretary of the Treasury it is [undeciperable] that I should authorise & grant you, as I as by this letter, to continue to execute all the functions of Secretary of War, until further arrangements are made in that office. 
Samuel Dexter Esqr. Sec of the Treasury
