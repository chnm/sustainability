We\'re in Lakeview now.  I went to grammar school not far from here.  Lakeview is another neighborhood that was devastated by wind and water damage.  Notice how the flood lines mark the homes.  You can tell how fast or slowly the water receded.  Notice the large trees in the center of the photo.  The trees that are standing are stripped badly of their leaves.  The floodwater that stood her for several weeks was mostly saltwater.  How badly will these grand trees be damaged by the salt?  The foreground of the photo is the large neutral ground that used to be green.  There are glimpses of green trying to grow above the muck.  I\'ve been using the term \"neutral ground\".  For those of you who aren\'t familiar with the term it\'s what the rest of the world refers to as the median.
