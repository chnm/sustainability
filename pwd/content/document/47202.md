War Office 10th July
1794 - Drums to
be delivered to Lieut.
Grayson
Mr. Hodgdon
71 N. Water Street
119
War Department July 10. 1794
Sir
Please to issue to Lieut William Smallwood Grayson, for Captain Richard Scott Blackburn's company of Artillerists and Engineers, one new Drum - with carriage and case and one pair drum sticks, two fifes, and fife cases.
I am Sir,
with respect,
Yr. obt. Serv.t
Jno Stagg Jnr
Cty Clk
Sam'l Hodgdon Esq.
