We've designed this site around the process of using primary sources for delving into the past. You'll find lots of examples, guidance, and rare primary sources.
  • Our centerpiece is Martha Ballard's diary, all 27 years of it, handwritten and transcribed. Explore randomly to your heart's content or use the search engine to locate specific entries.
  • Our Archive of Primary Sources about early America and Martha Ballard is a unique collection you can't find in any one physical place.
  • The History Toolkit is full of research tips and forms that will show you what you need to know to use specific kinds of documents and sources.
  • Two in-depth interactive examples of how to work with primary source documents, Martha and a Man-Midwife and One Rape. Two Stories., will help prepare you to use primary sources and go do your own local research.
