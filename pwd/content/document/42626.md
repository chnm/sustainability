Order from the War Office
March 22 1792
To deliver Cap Kerrey 20 Suits of Clothing
[Marginalia: 60 circled in pencil]
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nove 24, 1894, R.P.O. 401810
War department, March 22d 1792
Mr Knox 
Please to furnish Captain Kerrey with twenty suits of uniform clothing complete - he having previously inspected the Same - You will forward it to Brunswick by the Stage.  
Jno Stagg Junr. Chf Clk
