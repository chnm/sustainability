 February 28, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I left mrs Tylor as well as Could be Expected. Calld at son Pollards, her Babe is not well. I arivd at home about noon.   at Ditoes & Son Pollards. Hannat Bolton remains were interd.  
 

 

 March 12, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & some Snow. I have been very unwell. mrs Farewell Calld here. I had a very Sick night, was Put in bed by my husband & Cyrus and Lay in my Cloaths.   at home, very Sick at night.  
 

 

 March 15, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, very unwell forenn. Betsy Andrus Came to help me, began my wash. Jona & famely returnd. Ephm, B. Cypher, Robd Fletcher got 6 loads wood here and 1 to Jonas.   at home, Betsy Andrus Came to assist me.  
 

 

 March 22, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleast. Son Cyrus Sett out for wiscassett, Son Ephm went to Conduct him part of the way in a Sleigh. he is gone to tend a mill for mr Silas Lee. may God guide and Preserve him, and return him in Safety. mr Ballard is gone to the Settlement. I feel gloomy. Saml Freeman is Cuting wood at our door. Ephm returnd at Evng, informd me his Mare, with the Sleigh, fell thro ye ice opisite mr Smiths. two men Came to their asistance and, thro Gods goodness, the lives of my Sons and hors were Saved. the harness was Some damaged.   at home. Son Cyrus Sett out for mr Lees at ye point. they fell thro ye ice against mr Smiths, were assisted by two men and Escaped with their lives. Dagt Ballad here.  
 

 

 April 5, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard and Ephm to Town meeting. I have done Some Sewing and part of the hous work, but have not been able to Sett up this aftern but very little. Dolly washt.   at home. mrss Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 April 7, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day, a thunder Shower aftern. Dagt Lambard and 2 Childn, Son Jon a, his wife and all his Childn took Tea with us. Son Pollard Calld at our door, his famely are as well as usual. Ephm put the lower Casements in to 3 windows & a light in the out hous. Dolly went to mr Parkers.   at home. Childn to visit me. Ruth Fairfield of winslow Expired.  
 

 

 April 8, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard and Dolly went to meeting, it is ye yearly fast. I have been at home. Son Lambd & mr Gill Calld here. I am informed yt the wife of Church Williams was Deld of a Son, her 2nd Child, ys morn.   at home. mr Ballard to meeting, fast day.  
 

 

 April 21, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day and more moderate. I have done part of my hous work, Sorted Potatoes, Cleard my Seller of 3 pails ful of dirt, Sett 30 Cabbage Stumps and removd part of the banking from the hous. Dagt Ballard & 3 of her Childn & Sally Farewell took Tea here. Son Pollard Calld, his Famely are well. I have had a pain in my Stomach.   at home. Dagt Ballard here.  
 

 

 April 25, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard to meeting. Sons Lambd, Jona & Ephm to Son Pollards. Capt Stackpool Came down to inform us of the death of Lucy Town, may God pleas to Sanctify the dispensation to us all, and may we hear the Call, be ye allso ready.   at home. was informd of ye death of Lucy Town.  
 

 

 April 27, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold & windy. Sons Lambard & Ephm Sett out ys morn to go to Son Towns. the remains of Lucy Town are to be interd at 2h ys aftern. may the famely, and Connections with the Neighbours, have the presence of the Great Parent of the universe and may ye Call, be ye allso ready, be attended to by us all. Son & Dagt Ballard and part of yr Childn Supt with us. mr Ballard been giting Hay fm Motts.   at home, feel not well. Funeral of Lucy Town y s aftern. Dolly Kimbal went home.  
 

 

 April 30, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Abm Devenports Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at ye 5th h morn to go and See the wife of mr Abm Devenport who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 9 of her 1st Dagt and 4th Child. I returnd and attended Lecter. Calld at mr Blacks, Saunders and moodys, & at Several Stores. Son & Dagt Ballard Supt here. Son Cyrus Came home from ye Point, arivd at 9h Evng; he is Some fatagued.   at mr A. Devenports. Birth 5th. recd 12/ as a rewd. I was at Lecture aftern & at mr Moodys.  
 

 

 May 4, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Sett Curent Bushes, Sowd Letues and pepper grass, finisht removing the Banking from ye hous; feel fatagued. Georg Rheeds wife Deld of a Daught this day. Ephm and Saml gone to Training.   at home. workt in my Gardin. Ephm Gone to Traning.  
 

 

 May 5, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold for ye Season. I Sett out french Turnips, 2 of another kind [&] 1 Beat. I went to Eliab Shaws with a web to be wove, 25 Skeins wp, 2do Lining filling, & 47 Do Cottne. I was gone 2h fm home.   at mr Eliab Shaws & Calld at mr Parkers.  
 

 

 May 8, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the fire drove mrs Welch and famely out, they Sleep at our hous. I have been out the most of ye day watching ye fire, was Calld by James Keton to go & See his wife who is unwell.   at mr Ketons at night.

 May 16, 1802, Sunday

 at Ketons, his wife got better and her women went home.   at Ketons.  
 

 

 May 17, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Showery. I went to See Old mrs Tytor. mrs Keton had her women Calld again.   at Ditoes & at mr Tylors.  
 

 

 May 18, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Bth Ketons 5th Dagt.

rainy. my Patient was Safe Deld at 3h ys morn of her 5th daught and 8th Child. I left her aftern and Came to Son Pollards but Could not proseed on acount of ye rain.   at Ketons and Son Pollards. Birth 6th.  
 

 

 May 21, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been very unwell. we Cookt the head and harslit of the Veal. Jona, his wife [&] siStr, wm & Sally Dind with us. Dagt Ballard had an ill turn at Evng, Pukt up 6 larg worms.   at home. my Childn here.  
 

 

 May 30, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my famely Except my Self went to meeting. Jona Ballard waited on mrs Savage to Sidniy, Shee Calld here. Dagt Ballard and part of her Childn ["Calld here." - implied] I made Tea for them. mrs Farewell Calld here after meeting.   at home. mrs Savage and mrs Farewell Calld here.  
 

 

 June 12, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I ptanted W mellons. had pain aftern, but went to mr Parkers to See his wife who was in Labour; her women Calld at Evng.   at mr Parkers. Birth 7 th. 
 

 

 June 13, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X.[Birth mr Parkers Dagt]. X.

Clear. I returnd from mr Parkers, left her Cleverly. Shee was Deld of a daugt at 2h ys morn, her 4th Dagt [&] 8th Child. I had a Severe Pain in my Stomach and made use of Senna. heavy thunder at Evn.   at Ditoes. Birth 7th. recd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 June 22, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day, thunder & a Shower adtern. I have helpt do my hous work. Son Pollard here, made me a prest of A head and harslet of a Calf. he is not very well. Son Jona & wife & Jack Dind with us. I have had a very ill turn.   at home. Clarrisa to her Fathers.  
 

 

 June 23, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I have been at home, did Some work in my gardin altho I had a Pain in my Stomach, was very ill with it all night.   at home, was Sick all night.  
 

 

 June 24, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day. I have had a very Sick day. Dagt Ballard Came to See me Several times. I got Ease and had a better night. Dagt Bald to mrs Farewels aftern.   at home, very ill indead, all day.
