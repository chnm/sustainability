The Paymaster General for Ante above
War Department 15th Novr 1800

Sir
In answer to your Letter relative to Colo Tobias Lear's account that I am of opinion, that his pay ought to be allowed after the Death of the Commander in Chief, for as long a time as was necessary for the completion of the business mentioned in your Letter.
