Martha Ballard's Diary, January 30 - February 6, 1788

30 4
Clear & very pleast Day. mr Ballard Sett out at 10h this morn for Winslow, was Disapointed of a hors & returnd. Snowd this Eving. I have been at home. Jono Changd his oxen for the hors he ond the first inst. at home.  
31 5
Clear. mr Ballard Sett our for winslow. I was Calld to See mr Weston who has an ulser gathering in his throat, tarried & watcht. am informd of ye Death of Wilm Kenadys wife at Sandy river. Capt Norcross maried to mrs Nancy Hussey. mariag Capt Norcros. I was at mr Westons.  
1 6
Clear. I returnd from mr Westons, he is not better; has Sent for Doct Williams. Henry Badcoks wife Delivrd of a Dat 17 of Januy. Birth. I was at mr Westons.  
2 7
Clear. mr Ballard returnd from Son Towns, informs that Patty is Scolt very Bad & their infant remains very Low. the rest of ye famely are well. I was at home.  
3 F
Clear & warm. mr Ballard, Theops, Jono, & the Girls went to meeting. we hear that mr Weston is Better. it raind all night. at home.  
4 2
X. Birth Capt Neys Son. X. 		I was Calld at 1Hm to Capt Neys wife in Travil. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 4. I returnd home at 1 H PM. receivd 9 lb Sugar & 1 lb Candles. Left the Patient Cleverly. this is her Second Son & Seventh Child. at Capt Neys.  
5 3
Snowd & Blowd, & is very Cold. I have been at home. Foster Left this for the westward. Jono attemptd to go to the meddow but did not get there. We are informd that Andw Goodins wife is very Sick; Sent for Doct Williams. Foster left here.  
6 4
Clear & Cold. Capt Savage & wife, mr Bullin & Deacn Emerson Dind here. mr Ballard & he are gone to See Jabiz Cowen. Dolly is unwell with a Cold.
