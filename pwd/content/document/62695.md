James Bayard
16th May 1790
Pistols to repair
197 1/2
Dear Sir
I have left my pistols at your house  I shall be much obliged if you will allow them to be put in complete order by one of your workmen.  The stock of one is broke and would thank you to have the breach examined if no defect in it.
With much regard
I am your obt. Servt. 
J A Bayard
26 May 1790
