194
Philadelphia 23d, August 1798
Sir,
By order of the Secretary of War, I have purchased and herewith send to your address the Stationary inserted in the enclosed Invoice. I have been very particular in making the purchase, & I hope the whole will answer your purpose, and meet your acceptance.
With respectful consideration I am Sir your most obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
Intendent of Stores of the U S.
George Washington Esqr
Lt General &c
Mount Vernon
Invoice of Stationary
2 reams extra large quarto gilt Paper
1 ream ditto patent copying Do.
21 Papers Coping Ink Powder
Intendants Office 23d August 1798.
Samuel Hodgdon
Geo: Washington Esqr.
