War Office
Feb 19 1799
A flag to be [undecipherable] for the use of the Cherokee Nation

Indian Dept
the
War department 15 February 1799
Sir
The Purveyor has been directed to purchase and deposit with you a flag of the United States for the use of the Cherokee as soon as you shall receive the same deliver it to Samuel Hodgdon Esq. for transportation.

James W Henry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
