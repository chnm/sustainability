Department of War
                                                  Accountants' Office
                                                    February 27th 1796

Ens. Charles Martin.

 Fort LeBeauf
                          
                          Sir
                              I have to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 

9 Inst enclosing sundry muster, Pay, and Receipt Rolls which after examination will be passed to your Credit .- your Recruiting Account - when it arrives shall be attended to without delay - it is a desirable thing to have all those accounts adjusted as soon as possible-

                              I am Sir (indecipherable)
                                 William Simmons
