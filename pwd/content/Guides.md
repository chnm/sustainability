The following guides are divided into three sections: *How to use the Papers of the War Department* has information on how to navigate, search, and use documents in our collection; *Transcribing the Papers of the War Department* contains a user manual for our trainscription system; and *Historical Context* offers useful information about reading eighteenth-century handwriting, commonly used abbreviations, and resources to learn more about this period in American history.
