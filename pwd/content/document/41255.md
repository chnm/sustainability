(No. 19)
[Letter from the Secretary of War to the Governor of Pennsylvania]
War DepartmentMarch 14th 1791
Sir/
I have the honor to inclose for your Excellency's satisfaction, a copy of the Letter written to the Lieutenants of the Counties of Washington, Allegheny and Westmoreland, on the 10th instant, and which was transmitted by Express the same day--
I am Sir &c(sign'd) H. Knox, Secy of War
