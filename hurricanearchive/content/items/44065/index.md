My Aunt and her family at first went to the Hyatt Hotel instead of evacuating. While they were there they took care of this old couple whose family left them there. When they did evacuate they ended up in Batesville, Mississippi. They stayed there until my cousins graduated. When they took one of there first visits back to look at there house there was this huge boat that ended up in there backyard. They are still in the process of rebuilding there house. One of my memories is that eventhough she had her own family to worry about she took care of the old couple. That inspires me to do the same if I am every in that situation.
