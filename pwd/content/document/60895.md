War Office
Jany 25 1798
Horse to be ded Colo Francis Mentges
xo
War Office January 25.1798
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to Col. Mentges on order
a sorrel horse for Wiandot Chief, (Crane) now in this city
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
Mr. Harris will please to sent the Horse to Mr.
Dunwoody.  Mr. Steel will give a Receipt for the
Horse.
J. Mentges
