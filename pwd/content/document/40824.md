description: "Burnet confirms that he received a message from Knox informing him of the suggestions that General Greene was conducting an improper business relationship with John Banks and Company, and\
  \ that the evidence was in Burnet\x92s possession by way of his son\x92s papers.  The allegations were also that Greene compelled Burnet to relinquish this evidence to him.  Burnet addresses these allegations\
  \ and declares them to be patently false.  There was no evidence of a corrupt relationship between Greene and John Banks, and it certainly was not in the hands of Burnet or his son.  Burnet states that\
  \ he has never seen anything to give the slightest suggestion that such impropriety ever occurred."
