West Point 10 March 1780
Sir, 
Aggreeable to Contract made with Mr. James ManonI certify that the son James Manon has cleaned Five - Hundred Stands of Arms for the United States at eighteen Ninety Sixths of a Dollar Each - and is entitled to receive thirty five in pounds Ten shillings for the same at the War Office in New York ten days after the Certificate is presented - which makes up the number of fifteen hundred he has cleaned - I am Sir - 
The Honorable Majar Genl Knox
Secretary at War - New York -
