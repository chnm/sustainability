Samuel Hodgdon Esq

 Philadelphia

James Manning

 & Co-23d December

Recd 25th 1795
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr.   New York Dec.23d. 1795

                Dear Sir,

                     We received yours of the 22d.

Dec., we shall make enquiry concerning Capt. 
L. Hammerieus Vefsel and the Goods-But you omitted 
to inclose the Invoice of the Goods- It will be necefsary
we should be furnished with this without delay-
We shall attend to the Invoife

                               We are
                                     Sir
                                     Your Obed. Sert.
                                     James Manning & Co

Dec. 24th. Since writing the above we have
enquired after the things you mention-We find
Col. Rochfontaines Boxes of Claret are in Town, from
what we can learn the other articles are at Long
Island, but we cannot tell what the articles are
until we get the Invoice.  We are as above

                                      James Manning & Co
