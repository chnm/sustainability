New York, Jannuary 4th 1787
Sir
In the account extracted from my books
omitted to enter against Captain James Goorions the sum of
145 04/90 Dollars Specie which tgeLt. W. Gen. charges to him for
the purchase of a Waggon and earn when tge army was disbanded.
I am therefore to request that you will retain his order in our
hands until he produces a certificate from Csbl. Carrington or
myself that he has accounted for this sum.
Pierce
Jn. Hopkins Esquire
