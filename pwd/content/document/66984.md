War Office
April 1st 1799
Medicines & Hospital Stores 
to be Del the Qr M G
War department April 1. 1799
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to the order of John Wilkins Esqr. Quarter Master General all the articles of Medicine Instruments and Hospital Stores mentioned in the enclosed return.  
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon

Mr John Harris
