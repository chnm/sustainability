Primary Source

The strike has been commemorated further afield than the Pilbara, especially by the left. For example, many unions have commemorated the strike in recent years. In 2010 the singer and song-writer Shane Howard recorded a song Clancy & Dooley & Don McLeod that emphasises the support unions lent the strike.
