 February 20, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Some Snow & a very Chilly air. Sally returnd from weding at Evng. Bror Ebenz Dind & mr Livermore Supt here. it is a Cold, Slormy night. I am 59 years old this Day. I was Calld at 11h Evn to Joseph Savages wife, her women with her.   a Josph Savages.  
 

 

 February 21, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at mr Savages, her women all tarried all night.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 February 22, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & windy. I was at Ditoes. the women all went home. my Patient Seemd to be Some better.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 24, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, Clear aftern. my Son Came for me to See his wife but I Could not liav my Patient. I Sent a remedy which relievd my Dagt. thee women were Calld to mrs Savage & Shee was ill thro the most of ye night.   at Ditoes. Slept none ys night.  
 

 

 February 25, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Josep Savages Dagt.

 Clear. mrs Savage was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 2h morn. I left her as Comfortable as Could be Expected at 6, and Came to my Sons. find his wife a little better. I tarried there this Day & night.   at [Ditoes & my Sons]. Birth 7th. 
 

 

 February 27, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wilm Swantons Son. XX.

Clear till Evn when it Snowd. I was Calld at 1h pm to See mrss Swanton who Deld at 7 of a Son, her Second Child. I went to See Capt Savage who is unwell. Calld allso to See mr Williamss Dagt who remains very weak. Came home about 11h Evng. I receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Swantons. Birth 8th.  
 

 

 February 28, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleast. Ephm workt for mr Kennady. mr Cyrus went to Capt Filly Browns, Bot me 14 lb flower, gave 2/5, G Bd /2. mr Patridg & wife Calld here at Evng. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 March 4, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear but very Cold for march. Eph got 1 Load wood to mr Burtun, his Oxen pesterd him So he quitt work aflern. this is ye anniversary of Son Jonas Birth.   at home. Son Jon a is 31 years old this Day.  
 

 

 March 6, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Bror Town & others went home. I was Calld at 11h to See the wife of mr Roland Smith who was in Labour. I was there all night. Bror Ebenz Moore Slept here on his way to Oxford. Snowd at night.   at mr Smiths, his wife was ill.  
 

 

 March 7, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Roland Smiths Son. XX.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. mrs Smith was Safe Deld at 6h pm of a fine Son & is as well as Could be Expected; her illness was very Severe. mr Smith Conducted me home at 9h Evng. he gave me 18/7 as a reward for my Service. mrs Cleark, Felps & Robinson were assistants at the Birth.   at Ditoes. Birth 9th. a first Born. Bror Moore Sett out for Oxford. I Sent a Letter to Sister Waters.  
 

 

 March 9, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Hardin Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard, my Self & Ephm attended worship foren. I Dind at Capt Meloys, was Calld out of meeting, in ye first prayer, to mrs Hardin. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 5. I was Conducted home by mr James Page, arivd at 10.   at Isaac Hardins. Birth 10th. mr James Page Sleeps here. fee recd may 20.  
 

 

 March 12, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Some rain afternn. I Sett out to go to mr Burtuns, it began to rain before I got as far as Son Pollards and I Calld there, tarried a little while & returnd home. Cyrus went for Dolly, brot Her home in mr Hamlins Slay.   at Son Pollards. I am informd yt mrs Hardin wove 1_ yds this Day, & 3 y next day.  
 

 

 March 18, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. we are makeing Soap. mr Thads Snell was here, Gave me 7/4 as a reward for attending his wife in Child Birth Jany 14th, 1793. my son Jona was here, informs me his wife is unwell. I sent her Some medisin. mr Freeman here, had 3 Sheets, Dead Blanks, to Carry to mr Ballard. informs me he was well last first Day. I have been at home.   at home. heard fm mr Ballard. funeral John Badcks wife.  
 

 

 March 20, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Savages Son. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy aflern. I was Calld at 8h morn to See the wife of James Savage, Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 1h pm. I left her Comfortable & returnd homewd as far as my Son Jonas, arivd there at 7h Evn, tarried all night. my Dagt is a little more Comforlable than Shee was.   at James Savages. Birth 11th. mr Savg Sent me 2 Bushl Corn to my Sons.  
 

 

 March 23, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to meeting at ye hook. Colo Sewall Calld me to his house, his Lady is not very well.   at Colo Sewalls  
 

 

 March 25, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Morgins Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from Colo Sewalls to See mrs Morgin. Shee was Deld of a Daugtr at 4h Pm. I returnd to mr Pollards, tarried there ys night.   at Does & mr Morgins. Birth 12th. receivd 6/. first Born.  
 

 

 March 26, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinklys Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to mr Patridges to See Patty Mc neere who has ye Collic & fitts. Calld from there to Colo Sewalls, from there to James Hinkleys Junr, his wife was Deld at 11h Evn of a Son, her first Child. I tarrid all night. the wrideing is very Bad. the fresht over the Bridges at ye hook.   at Ditoes Colo Sewalls & others. Birth 13th. receivd 6/. her first Born.  
 

 

 March 27, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came from mr Hinkleys, Calld at home & returnd to Colo Sewalls.   at Hinkleys & Colo Sewalls.  
 

 

 March 31, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Snowd before night. I went to mr Parkers this morn, was Calld to Jason Pierses; his wife is unwell.   at Ditoes. mr Parkers & Pierces.  
 

 

 April 1, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I was at mr Pierces, his wife So ill that we Calld mrs Pollard & Savage. Jonas hors fell thro the ice on ye mill Pond.   at mr Pierces.  
 

 

 April 2, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jason Pierces Dagt. XX.

Clear & pleast. mrs Pierce was Deld at 10h morn of a Dagt, her first Child. I left her Comfortable & returnd to Colo Sewalls. I receivd 6/ by my Dt Dolly June 6th of mr Pierce.   at Pierces & Colo Sewalls. Birth 14th. her first Born.  
 

 

 April 5, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Colo Sewalls Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Sew Calld us up at 1h ys morn. mrs Pollard & Voce Calld at 6h morn. mrs Sewall was Dilivd of a Dagt before they got inn & is Cleverly. mr Ballard Calld there & Dind. I Came from there at 2h pm. Calld at Son Pollards & Spent ye rest of ye aftern. arivd at home at 7, feel fatagud. Dolly tarries at Son Pollards ys night.   at Ditoes. Birth 15th. recd 8/3. her fifth Child.  
 

 

 April 11, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. Doct Coney Calld here. mr Ballard went to ye hook. I had a Severe pain in my head at Evng & greatly opprest with wind in my Stomach. had got So much Ease as to Sleep when I was Calld by moses Springer to See his Lady.   at mr Moses Springers.  
 

 

 April 12, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Springers Son. XX.

Clear. I was at mr Springers, his Lady was Deld at 5h 27m morn of a Son. I lefe her & inft very Comfortable & returnd home at 11h. I went on to ye bed & Slept. mr Ballard been to ye hook. Betsy Voce was here.   at Ditoes. Birth 16th. recd 7/6. a first Born.  
 

 

 April 24, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Natn Burgss Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard went to Varsalboro. I was Calld to See the wife of Nathan Burges who was Safe Deld (tho very ill indeed) at 11h Evn of a fine Son. I tarried all night. my patients are as well as Can be Expected, her husband is at Sea, if living.   at Nalhn Burgess. Birth 17th. a first Born. receivd 6/.
