July 6, 1790 (Tuesday)

6 3

 

 Clear. I Left home Early Bound for Pounalboro; mr Ballard allso. we went on Board Leut Pollards Boat. Stopt at Pitts town. got to mr Hatches where we took Lodgings During the Coarts Setting. went into Coart aftn at Coart.

July 7, 1790 (Wednesday)

7 4

 

 at Pounalboro; attended Coart at Dito.

July 8, 1790 (Thursday)

8 5

 

 at Ditto; attended &C, &C at Do.

July 9, 1790 (Friday)

9 6

 

 at Ditto; attended &C, &C at Do.

July 10, 1790 (Saturday)

10 7

 

 at Ditto; attended the Cause between this Common welth & Jo North Esq; was tried & given to the jury at Do.
