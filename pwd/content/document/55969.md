Pittsburg 23d December 1796
Sir
Your letter of the 16th instant enclosing one addressed to Red
Pole a Shawnee Chief is received & shall be delivered to him on his arrival
The letter accompanying this OI resume will infrom you of the Daeth of
Major General Anthony Wayne at Presqu'isle on the 15th instant at 2 O'Clock
Actt Major DeButts one of the Generals Aids De Camp is expected
in town this evening.
I am, Sir, Yr Mo Ob St. I.C.


Hon'ble
James McHenry
Secr'y of War
Philadelphia
