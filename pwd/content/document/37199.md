New Ark March 22.85
  Sir
            I am told by the treasurer of the state of New Jersey
  that in your late returns to him of the Jersey line no mem=
  -tion is made of the Detached individuals in the horse, Artille=
  =ry, Artificers, [undecipherable] and altho' the treasurer has returns of
  them in his own Office, yet he thinks nothing valid unless
  it comes from you, I am therefore induced to Apply to you
  in a particular manner in behalf of Major Jeremiah Brown
  of Colonel Baldwin's Artificers and myself late a Lieu.t
  in the 2nd Regiment of Artillery and will be particular=
  =ly indebted to you to furnish him with returns of us si=
  =milar to those of the Jersey Reg.ts - Both of us have been
  credited to Jersey and received our Depreciation from
  that state and are residents in it. Should you
  find this request consistant with your duty, I would
  thank you to send the return asked for as soon as you
  can make it convenient to the Treasurers Office at
  Trenton.                             
                                      Wm. Pennington
 Jos. Howell Jun.r
