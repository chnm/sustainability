The objects that appear in this collection describe the work of the Student Hurricane Network (SHN).  In the fall of 2005, law students from across the country formed the SHN as a national association dedicated to providing long-term assistance to communities and individuals affected by Hurricane Katrina. The documents and photographs in this collection are related to the work of law students in creating and coordinating volunteer opportunities in the Gulf Coast.
