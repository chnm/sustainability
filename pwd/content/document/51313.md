Alexander Gibson
Capt. 4 Sub-Legion
recd 12. Sept
Arvd 14. 1795
William Simmons
accountant
war department
Staunton Sept.2 1795
Sir
You will please to forward me three hundred twenty seven dollars 48cents for pay and subsistence due me up to the 31st ultimo which sum you will enter to my Debit and when my Acct. is stated the necessary cre= dits shall be given.
A muster & pay roll of my recruits is enclosed to the secretary of war
I am sir your Hum' Sert
Alex Gibson Capt.
4th Sub Legion
William Simmonsacct.
