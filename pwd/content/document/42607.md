Major Nicholl's Certificate favor of Charles Croxall
I recollect perfectly that the greatest part of Captain Eichelbergers Company in Col Hartleys Regiment were recruited by Mr. Charles Croxell
William Nichols
March 16th 1792
delivered the Account of Capt Eichelberger to Richd P Mercer the 7th of March 1792
Richard Harrison Esquire
Auditing the Treasury

Left by the Auditor of the Treasury for Settlement March 2d 1792

Capt Croxall asserts he inlisted 52 Men & marched to Camp in their Company afterwards inlisted 27 men
