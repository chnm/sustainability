To the Secretary at War29th Octr 1790.
Providence 29th Octr 1790
Sir
The Bearrer Capt Forest has taken another Load of Millitary Stores per Enclosed Return weighing Twenty Eight Tons, for the Freight of which & Delivering the same at West Point he is to Receive Forty Nine Dollars
I am with must EsteemSirYour Most Obed. Hum. StJereh Olney
Henry Knox EsqrSecretary at War
