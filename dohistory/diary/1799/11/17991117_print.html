Martha Ballard's Diary, November 17 - 23, 1799

17 1
Cloudy and not very Cold. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to meeting. I have been at home. mr Balld at mr Dingleys at Evn, his youngest Child is 1 year old this Day. Ephm and Samuel Came to help Cyrus git our Ducks home. they got ym to ye Landing and they lost Sight of them it being dark. at home.  
18 2
Cloudy. mr Ballard up to our hous, Cyrus went aftern. I went to Hallowell to Call for Some arearages but did not receive anything but promises. mrss Molloy removd from her habitation by the Academy to mr Martins. mrss Clap and Daugt took Tea with me. Hepsy Brown returnd to liv with me. David Robbins Sleeps here, he and my husband reaconed. at Hallowel.  
19 3
Clear part of the Day and pleast, Cold at Evn. mr Ballard and Cyrus went up to ye house with a Load of our things. I have Doubled all my hoes yarn and began to make Shirts for Cyrus. Hepsy Done my hous work & Twisted 14 knotts yarn and Sewed with me at Evn. Lydia went to Son Jonas. mr Amos Partridg Calld and gave me 12/ as a rewd for Assisting his wife ye 15th August tast. at home. mr Partridg Calld here.  
20 4
Clear and Cold. mr Ballard been Surveying for the mr Ingarhams. Cyrus been to the hook and Cuting wood and bringing water for Brewing. Hepsy done house work and we workt on the Shirts. I felt unwell aftern. at home.  
21 5
Clear and Cool. I have been puting up things. Hepsy washt ye North Bedrooms and Kitchen. mr Ballard been up to ye house, Cyrus for Cart & oxen, and brot a Barril from Parmers. mrsmc Causland and Henry Calld here. at home.  
22 6
Cloudy and some rain. mr Ballard and Cyrus went up with a load of our Stuff. mrs mc Causland Calld here, Harry went home with her. his behavior has been Such that mr Dingley wd not keep him any longer. what heart aicks his Dear mammy must have in her present Situation. I have been makeing a Shirt for mr Ballard, Hepsy one for Cyrus. at home.  
23 7
Clear part of the day. I have been finishing mr Ballards Shirt. he and Cyrus been up to the hous. I went to mr Dingleys aftern. mr Dun Sent me 9/ for assisting his wife. mr Dingly Supt here. at mr Dingleys.
