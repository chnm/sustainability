<p>The Jay Treaty with Great Britain was very controversial at the time. In this<a href="/document.php?id=17167"> letter</a> to Baltimore merchant Robert Oliver, Secretary of War James McHenry warns that if the House doesn’t appropriate funding for the Treaty, the British will not withdraw their troops from the Western posts.</p>
