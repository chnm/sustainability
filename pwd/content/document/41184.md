18 Feby 1791
To the Secy of War
Mass
H Jackson Reg
P.O. Feby 18, 1791
Sir
It appears that Capt T. H. Condy has produced Payrolls of Col. H. Jacksons Regt to an amot covering the sums rec'd advanced by the US on accot per the regt & leaving a balnc due the regt of Dols 819 80/90 which was paid him but no evidence of the disposition of this balnce as also the whole monies previously advanced has not been produced which I apprehend ought to be done by Capt Condy previously to an allowance being made him for an extension of the to service--
I have the Honor &cJ. Howell
The HonbleThe Secrety of War
