- through what channel the complaint has been entered is unknown to me
- assert the Oneidas have expressed their satisfaction in their present blacksmith
- have not heard a word of complaint which is a thing they are not backward to offer for the smallest failure
- make immediate enquiry and report to you the true situation
- suppose it would not be displeasing to the Quakers to introduce one of their own people in the place of the present blacksmith
- Quakers reside at Oneida
- your letter

- procure one for that part of the Six Nations as his situation is remote from any settlement of white people and is truly a sacrifice of society and
- principal objection stated by the Secretary of State appears to be for his keeping a trading shop or selling liquor to the Indians which is a thing I
- told me he never did anything of the kind unless they extorted it of him
