Feby 1791
To the Dep of Treasury
for 5,000 Dollars
Pay Office, Feby 8 1791
Sir

Being entirely out of Cash induces me to request you will be pleased to grant me the sum of 5000 dollars on Account of Arrearages of pay due the Troops to the 31 Dec 1789
I have the Honor to be Dr Sir
J -
The Honorable 
The Secy of The Tre
