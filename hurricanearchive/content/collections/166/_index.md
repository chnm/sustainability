Post-Katrina photographs by Lorraine Monseu. Monseu is a visual artist who also worked as a circus sign painter,  actress, clown, and stiltwalker in New Orleans from 1995 to 2006. She was forced to relocate because of high post-Katrina housing costs. She hopes to return to the city soon.
