Saml Hodgdon22 July 1791PittsburgStraw wanted
Pittsburg 22 July 1791
Straw is wanting for the Troops already on this ground, and more will be called for immediately for those coming in. 
You will be pleased to see that both are supplied
Your Most Obedient Servant 
Samuel Hodgdon 
Major Craig 
By [undecipherable] this day to [undecipherable] express
