War Department 4thDecember 1800
Isreal Whelan
above
_______

  15


Sir
You will be pleased to receive from the SW 
superintendent of Military Stores, a quantity of soldiers
clothing cut out, and have it made up on the best terms
you can as soon as may be.
I have also to request that you will see Mr. Lewis
15
on the subject of the Map of Tennessee mentioned and described in
the enclosed note and agree with him on behalf of the Department 
for the original or a well executed copy.
I am Sir [undecipherable]
