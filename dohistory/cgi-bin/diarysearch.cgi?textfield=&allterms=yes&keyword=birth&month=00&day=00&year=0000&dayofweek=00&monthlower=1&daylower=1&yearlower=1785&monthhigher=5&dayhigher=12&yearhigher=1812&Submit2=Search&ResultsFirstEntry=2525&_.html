 February 13, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd till 10h morn, Clear the rest of ye day. I have been mending Cloaths, doing hous wk & kniting. Le faett & William Sleep here. the School Master was Calld home to See A Sick Sister, the School dismist.   at home. a young Squagh here. Funeral Saml Ward of Sidney.  
 

 

 February 20, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dagts Pollard & Ballard & Polly Town Dind here. Son Pold Came and Conducted his wife & Babe home. I have Seen 74 years this day, may ye remainder of my life be Spent as becometh a Christian and Close in [peice].   at home. Dagls Balld & Polld here. aniversary of my Birth, 74th. 
 

 

 March 2, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day. I have done Some mending, knit and done my hous wk. Son Jona Calld in, Le faett brot my Tea kettle fm Brookss. he & Wm Sleep here. Dagt Lambd Sent us piggs harslet.   at home, Snowd this night.  
 

 

 March 9, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & windy. Son Town & his Childn left here after Breakfast to go to Son Ephms. Judg Cony and Capt Seth Williams Calld in to See us. Sally, M and Lucy L here, brot me 3 lb _ of drie fish. I have Bakt Some Pumpkin Pies and knit, Finisht footing mr Ballards Stockins.   at home. Judg Cony & Capt Williams here. Le faett & Saml Getchel Sleep here.  
 

 

 March 11, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I have done hous wk & Some Mending. Pally Town & Rhoda Pollard Calld here. John Arnold after ashes for Son Ephm. Lefaett & ranus Pierce Sleep here.   at home, heard that Esqr Tods Buildings were Burnt.  
 

 

 March 17, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear day, Cloudy at Evng. mr Benjamin Porter & wife & Babe here, Dind and took Tea. Dagts Ballard & Lambard to Tea. mr Porter Conducted Dagts home, Expect to tarrie at Dt Lambds. Ephm & William Sleep here. Elisabth ye indn here, I let her have potatoes. A Son of John Babcocks here for a record of his Birth which was Sept 11, 1791.   at home. Benn Porter, Son [Jona], their [wives] & Dagt Lambd here.  
 

 

 March 22, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd ys morn, Clear part of ye day. Dagt Lambard, Son Ephms wife and Allin & Sopha here. La faett & Wm Sleep here. mrs Smith Calld in here. Son Epms wife brot us 5 lb Cod fish, 1 do Butter & 5 do flower.   at home. Dagts Lambd & Ballard here.  
 

 

 April 1, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

[Birth Son Jonas 11th Child, th Daugt].

Clear and pleast. I have done hs wk and knit. Barny Lambd here, mr Ballard to ye Settlement. brot home 2 lb Sugar, _ do Tea, 1 qt Spirit. I was Calld at 6h pm to Son Jonas, his wife being in Labour. we Calld mrs Bennet & Smith and Shee was Deld at 10h 30m of a Daught that wd 9_ lb. I tarried all night. Son Jona has been in Jail, Came home about 9h Evng.   at Sone Jonas. Birth 1st.  
 

 

 April 3, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I was Calld to Son Jonas between 3 & 4 morn to See James who was in a fitt. we administd a Clister which gave Some relief. Doct Ellis was Calld, approvd of whad we had done. he left Senna & manna & drops. I left him at dusk much revived. mrs Baudin there.   at Ditoes. James has had a fitt. mrss Wiman is very low. Jona Junr Sleeps here.  
 

 

 April 4, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I went ys aftern to See James, he is very unwel yet. Calld to See mrss Wiman, find her very low.   at Diloes & at mr Wimans. S. & W. Sleep here.  
 

 

 April 13, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 raind all most all day & Evng. I returnd home about 9h Am after removing ye patient & Changing her Lining, left her Sleeping and a little Easier. I found Sally Farewel & Hannah Ballard here, they Slept with Pally Town here Last night. Sally here ys night allso.   at Ditoes, his wife is very Law. Sally Farewel here.  
 

 

 April 15, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have felt unwel but have Bakt and done part of my hous wk. Pally did my washing. Shee was Calld to watch with mrss Wiman, Shee is no better. Left & Wm Sleep here.   at home, Bakt. Pally washt & went to watch wh mrss Wiman.  
 

 

 April 19, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to mr Wimans at 8h am, his wife is near ye Close of Life. I returnd home at 11, have removd ye Banking fm S end & west Side of our house. went back and watcht with mrss Wiman, mrss Smith with me.   at mr Wimans, watcht. Dolly, Sally & Martha Slept here.  
 

 

 April 20, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. mrss Wiman Expired at 3h ys morn, aged 51. mrss Smith & I put her grave Cloaths on. Son Jona was Calld in to assist us in removeing the Corps into the west room. the Famely filld with Sorrow on the berievment. we Shall all feel the loss. may it be Sanctifyed to us all. I left them, returnd home by Son Jonas. they are as wel as Could be Expected. I Slept 2 hours, rose and performd my wk. mr Ballard to Son Pollards. they Sent me 3 lb fine pork. Dolly Lambd went home, Cyrus to mr Coneys.   at home. at mr Wimans, his wife Expired at 3h ys morn. Shee [told] us Shee felt resigned to leav the [world]. Death.  
 

 

 April 22, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Cloudy aftn, raind at night. I did Some washing forn, attended at mr Wimans hous, his wives Remains were interd. A Discrs Deliverd by mr Low, last prayer by mr Smith. words ot ye Text were, I must work while ye day lasts &C. Saml Wiman has Seen 14 years this day. Dagt Lambd, Lucy, Wm & Patty Town took Tea. my dt and Childn Sleep here.   at mr Wimans, the remains of his wife were intered. Funeral. Saml Wima is 14 years ys day.  
 

 

 April 28, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the ground frose hard ys morn. I have finisht Dagt Lambds Soap, there is a Larg Barril full & 2 Pails full. Set up a new Leach and put my own Soape to boiling. mrs Farewel Calld here. Dagt Ballard Sent me 9 oz Buller, 1 lb 2 oz pork and Some milk.   at [home]. mrs [Farewel] here.  
 

 

 May 1, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

[Birth John Shaws 2nd Daught.]

Clear. I was Calld about mid night to John Shaws wife who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 8h ys morn of her 2nd Child and Daght. I returnd home about noone. mr Ballard went to Town meeting. Magr Saml Howard was Chosen to represent ys Town in general Coart.   at John Shaws. Birth 2nd. June 16 th receivd 7S and 6d of mr Shaw.  
 

 

 May 3, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I have helpt do my hous work. Patty washt and Cleand bed roome. we removd Cyruss Bed and Chest up Chamber. mrs Smith [&] Brooks Calld here, inform me mrs Mosier is very Sick.   at home. Dagt Ballard Sent us 4_ lb Chees.  
 

 

 May 4, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and some rain. I went to Dagt Lambards. Calld to See mrss Mosier, find her very Sick.   at Dagt Lambds & mr Mosiers.  
 

 

 May 7, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Pipers Son & 5th Child. XX.

Clear part of the day. I was Calld from Son Ephms to mr Pipers Early ys morn, his wife in Labour. She was Deld of a Son, her 5th Child at 12 O Clock. we took dinner & I left them, went to Daugt Lambds; tarri all night. Henry had a very Sick night. I did not undres but Slept Some. Sophia Gill Slept there. my dagt been to See mrss Mosier, finds her very Sick.   at Son Ephms. mr Pipers & dagt Lambards. Birth 3d. receivd 9s at Babcock 24 inst.  
 

 

 May 14, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day, raind Some. mr Ballard to meeting. Revd mr Stone discoarst fm Romans 1st C 28th vers. Hannahs Ballard & Getchel Calld here. Epm & Wm Sleep here.   at home. Rhoda left here afler dinner.  
 

 

 May 25, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. At James Catons. Birth his 11 Child & 5 Son. XXX.

Clear. I was Calld by mr Caton at 2h 30m ys morn to go and See his wife. I arivd there at Sun rise, found her delivard of a Son. her 11th Child, but not Safe. I performd what was necessary and left her as Comfortable as Could be Expected. arivd home before ten and Sent herbs and other matters.   at James Calons. Birth 4th. fell fm the hors on my way home. receivd 9s of Wilm Babcock.  
 

 

 May 27, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and windy. I have transplanted Cucumbers and done other work in my gardin. mr Ballard to Hallowell. Calld at Dagt Lambards, brot 6 lb _ veal fm her. Le faett ploughd ye S end of our field.   at home, Brewd & workt in gardn. 
 

 

 May 28, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Pollards 9 Child, Daugt ye 6th.

Clear part of the day. I was Calld by Son Pollard at 2h 30m ys morn to go and See his wife. we reacht there before 5 and my Dagt was Deld at 6 of a fine Daught, her ninth Child, and is Comfortable. the Babe wd 11 pounds. Sally Cleark & Preuda Snow Came at Evng, there all night.   at Son Pollards. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 May 29, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 raind. my Daugt & inft Comfortable. Pruda helpt wash, Sally assisted. Shee tarried all night, all Cleverly. Son Pollard went to Watervill.   at [Ditoes].
