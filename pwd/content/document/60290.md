Thomas Davis Esq.r
in Congress
Accountants Office
December 8th 1797
Sir.
Enclosed you will be pleased to receive a copy of the Account Currnent of Mr. Joshua Barbie with duplicate of a letter wrote to him, which I presume, never got to Hand from the nature of Mr. Barbee's letter to you. I request you will transmit him the enclosures to him as early as you conveniently can to enable him to furnish the necessary Vouchers.
I am &
W. Simmons
Actt
