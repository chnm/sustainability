War department 19 October 1799
Sir,
A Mr. Bullet in a letter dated the 30 August ulto received today states that he sent on about a year ago a couple of pay rolls for  the spies employed in  Jefferson County for the years 1790 and 1794 that he the informed only partial payments had been made upon them and requested information from the Office that would enable him to let the claimants know the reason of such extraordinary delay adding that he was apprehensive money had been drawn out of the Office by some means and perhaps misapplied."
I request you will give me such information respecting the state of this business as you possess. 
I am Sir
Your obedient servant
James McHenry
William Simmons Esqr
Accountant
