Ensign Wallington
6.th July 1795

John Mills, Major & Adj.t Gen.l
GreenVille
Jefferson July 6.th 1795
Sir
A Number of Men in this Garrison is Destitute of Clothing, your advice, In what manner I can procure them / will much oblige your Ob.t & very Hb.l Serv.t
John Wallington Ensign
Commanding

Major John Mills
GreenVille
