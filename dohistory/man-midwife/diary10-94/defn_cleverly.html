Q: What did left her Cleverly mean?

A: " In some English dialects to leave a person 'cleverly' meant to leave them in health."

Evidence: Ulrich, Laurel Thatcher.
A Midwife's Tale. 1990

"That was certainly the intended meaning of [a similar entry]. Yet leaving a mother and a child 'cleverly' also meant applying one's own adroitness and dexterity. A midwife's skill was mirrored in her patient's well-being." --Laurel Thatcher Ulrich, A Midwife's Tale, p. 181"
