                   Order_____

               Subsistence Money
               to be paid to Genl. Knox
XXVI-29
                              NYork 12 April 1790

Sir,

    Please to pay to General Knox or order
    all my subsistence as it shall become
    due commencing from the first of Janur
    1790-&charge to account of
                    Your humble servt.

Joseph Howell Esq.            W Moor Lieut. Artly
