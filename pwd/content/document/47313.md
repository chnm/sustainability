Treasury Department
Revenue Office 16 July 1794
Sir,
The Superintendent of the Establishments on the Delaware for the protection of Shipping informs me, that a report has been made by a large Majority of the Gentlemen appointed by you to examine into the prospects of injury to the winter Harbour you
you will oblige me by a copy of that report. 
with great respect,
I have the honor to be Sir, Yr mo: obt Servt. 
Signed T. C. 
Commissr of the Reve
The Secy at War
