Aug 17th 1794
From Alex Hamilton


My Dr Sir


Inclosed are some papers, which were sent me shortly after my return to this City from Philadelphia but which from Mr. Hamilton's situation [undecipherable]

of business & have been forgotten -- If there is anything to be said to my correspondent you will enable me as speedily as possible to say it.  I send you the 100 Dollars you lent me and a further sum to reimburse some money paid for me by Lewis

Yrs Affecly
A Hamilton
Aug 17 . 1797
