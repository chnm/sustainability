Captain John Macpherson
1348  III-11
W. Jackson
New-York, October 9th. 1789.
Sir,
The President of the United States directs me to inform you that he will be much engaged in business to-day, and he purposes going into the country early to-morrow morning: But any communications of the nature mentioned in your letter may be made with equal propriety to General Knox as to himself.
I am Sir,
Your most obedient Servant
W Jackson.
