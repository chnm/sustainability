342. 
                     " Mr David C. Claypoole            
                                                                                   Accountants" Office
                      Philadelphia
                                                                                     December 31 1800
                                Sir

                                            I have received your letter dated the 18" Inst
                    with the annexed account for printing done for this Department,
                    I return it to you & request as the Records of the Office are 
                    consumed by which I could be furnished with the necessary infor-
                   - mation that you will attest to the account. The oath to set
                   forth that you were directed to publish the advertisements therein
                   charged, & that the rates so charged are agreeably to established 
                   allowances for like services - The charge for your paper furnished
                   the War Office must be made in a separate account - and
                   forwarded to the Secretary - who will direct it to be paid out
                   of a special fund appropriated for his Office
                                                                                                  I am &E
                                                                                                  
                                                                                           WS.
