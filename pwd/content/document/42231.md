Fort Pitt 1st Decemb 1791

Sir

    An Doctor Carmichaels Arrival here, he found it necessary to Establish, an Hospital for Several Such that were here of Capt Hashels Company.  Share therefore on  May or Respects order. Reneted a House & furnished it with sundry articles that appear necessary. on the Requisition of the Doctor but least a demand should be made in future, that may Exseed the Stated Allowance,  I have to Request Instructions, Respecting the Hospital & Barrack Arrangement in General__

    I have herewith Inclosed a Return of Stores up to this date, a Considerable part of which I intend to [undeciperable] by Aloy or Trescott Detachement, who non waits for orders [undeciperable] Aloy or Trescott has agreed to leave a Subaltern & a party to wait for the Gueralls that are coming forward__

    I have Recieved your favor of the 24* [undeciperable] her morresnt I shall pay particular attention to your Instruction therein.  

          Iam Sir

             Your Obedt

                 Humbl Servt
            
                         Isaac Craig__

Honbl

   Majr Genl H Knox
