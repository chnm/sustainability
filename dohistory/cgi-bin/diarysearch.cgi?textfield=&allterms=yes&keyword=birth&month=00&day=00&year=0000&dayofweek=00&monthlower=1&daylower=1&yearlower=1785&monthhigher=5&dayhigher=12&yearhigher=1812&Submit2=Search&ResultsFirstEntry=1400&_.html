 July 30, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld at 10h morn to See mrss Baly who is in Labour. her women were Calld near night. I have been Exercisd with a gripeing pain.   at mr Balys in the North Parrish.  
 

 

 July 31, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Balys Son. XX.

Clear and warm Day. mrs Baly was Deld of a Son (her Second Child) at 2h morn. I took a nap of Sleep. Sett out for home at 5. Calld at Son Jonas, all well there; at Son Lambarts, they are Cleverly. mrs Cowen Calld here, my little Grand Sons Sleep with us.   at Ditoes. Birth 39th. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 August 2, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I helpt Do the house work foren, went to Son Lambarts aftern. her Dear little infant has got the thrush. I went to mr Baxters and Voces. Calld to See Dagt Pollard, Shee & Childn are well. I returnd home, went to mr Beemans, find her not So well as Could be wisht. mr Capin has been writing for mr Ballard on plans.   At Son Lambarts and others.  
 

 

 August 4, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. mr Dingley and his wife, with others, Sett out for Boston this morn. I went to Son Lambarts, find her Cleverly and the infant as well as we Could Expect. Revd mr Stone there to visit, mrs Hamlin, Savage & Benjamin allso. Ephm Came home with me.   at Son Lambarts.  
 

 

 August 5, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Judsons Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Judsun at the hook at 7h morn. Shee was Deld at 3h pm of a Dagt, her 3d Child, all Daugts. I left her & infant Cleverly and returnd home a little before Sun Sett. mrs Pickford was here.   at mr Judsons. Birth 40. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 August 6, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to Son Lambarts this foren, Dind there, mrs Capin there. my Dagt is remarkable well, her Dear little Babe is recovering of the Soar mouth. I was Calld from there to See mrss Burns, find her very unwell. applied remidies which gave Some relief. I tarried all night, Shee had Some rest.   at Son Lambarts & at mr Burnses.  
 

 

 August 7, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am at mr Burnses, his wife is unwell yet. I was Calld from her to See mrss Robbins who feels her Self unwell, but Seems more Comfortable aftern. I Dind & took Tea and returnd home at Evn, find mrss Pikford, her Sister Nabby and mrs Dingleys Dear little Dagt here. mrs Welch & Childn Came here to tarrie ys night.   at mr Burnses & mr Robbinss. mrs Welch & Childn Sleep here.  
 

 

 August 8, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. Patty washt. I helpt about house. Jason Pierce & wife, mrs Heartford & Pickfors and Nancy Dingly here to Tea. mr Saml Pierce Sleeps here. Revd mr Stone Calld on us ys fornn.   at home. had Compy. mr Stone here foren.  
 

 

 August 10, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard been Surveying for mr Bridg. I have been at home, Done my house work. Patty Livirmore here with Nancy Dingly. Ebenz puling Flax. Son Ephm Came here, brot 13_ lb Veal from Son Lambart. my Daugt is Cleverly.   at home.  
 

 

 August 14, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See Capt Littlefields infant who is afflicted with a rupture. I recommended the application of Brandy to ye part affected. they inform me that [Jo] Page Says it must be opined, which I Should think improper from present appearance. Son Ephm Sleeps here.   at Capt Littlefields to See his infant. Ebenz went to Monmoth. Cyrus Came home.  
 

 

 August 17, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Binjamin here for a Lamb of Son Jon as ys morn. I went to Son Lambarts & mr Pilsburys, he is very Sick. I Calld at Son Pollards, they are as well as usual. John Braly brot Patty here.   an mr Pillsburys & my Sons Pollards & Lambarts.  
 

 

 August 19, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. the widdo Savage Dind here, Son Pollard and Jona allso. they Conducted their wives & Childn home. Jessy Bullin here inpertuning Patty Easty to go and work for his Marm. may Divine goodness direct me to Some one Sutable to assist me in my business.   at home.  
 

 

 August 21, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I attended worship at the fort. Peter Parkers youngest Child Bapiesed by the name of John Ruggles. I Calld at Son Lambarts, they are as well as they have been. the Babe is relaxt yett. I was Calld to mr Robbins at 10.   at meeting, Son Lambts [&] mr Robbinses.  
 

 

 August 22, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Chandler Robbinss Son. XX.

Clear and very warm. mrss Robbins was Deld at 3h morn of her Second Son and is as well as Can be Expected. I tarried till afternoon, receivd 13/6 as a rewd. mr Ballard returnd from his Journey, Son Town with him. mrss Savage, Trask and Phillip Bullin Dind here.   at mr Robbinss. Birth 41st. mr Ballard returnd.  
 

 

 August 24, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Waids Dagt.

Clear. mr Trask Came for his wife. Patty went to the hook in ye Boat with her. Old Lady Cony Calld here. I was Calld at 5h pm to See mrss Waid who is in labour. Shee was Deld of a Dagt at 7h Evn. I returnd home at 10. mr Waid was gone to trooping. we had thunder & rain in ye night.   at mr Waids. Birth 42nt. 
 

 

 August 25, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren. I went to See my Dagt Pollards Dear little Babe, it has a Sweling on the throat. it broak and discharged some this aftern, and the Child Seems more Comfortable. I Calld at Shubal Pittss door, his wife is unwell; at mr Pillsburies, find him very low, but I think not So Sick as when I Saw him last. my Dagt Lambart went to visit mrss Kitridg this aftern.   at Son Pollards & Carters Store & mr Pillsburies.  
 

 

 August 26, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Surveying for mr Robbins. I went to the hook, bot of mrses Sewall 3_ yds mode @ 6/6, 2 Skiens Silk /5, three _ yds ribbond @ 1/ 3/6, at mr Robbens, one hand kercf 2/5, 3_ yd ribbond @ /6, 3:10:_. I Calld to See mrs Greely who is all most blind.   at the hook. mr Ballard Surveying for mr Robbins.  
 

 

 August 28, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XX

 Clear. I attended worship at ye fort. Son Lambart had his infant Baptised by the name of Allin. I Calld to See Son Pollards infant, find it a little more Comfortable. mrss Hodges there. Phillip Bullin Supt and Sleeps here. Sons Cyrus & Ephm here.   at meeting, Sons Pollards & Lambarts. Bullin Sleeps here. my Dagt Town is 38 years old this Day.  
 

 

 September 2, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X

 Cloudy. I have been at home mr Philip Norcross, his wife unwell. I was Calld at 1h morn. we had a very rainy night.   at Philip Norcross. Dagt Lambt 24 years old ys day.  
 

 

 September 9, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Robirt Abbots Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. mrss Paten Dind here. I went to Lecter aftern, A mr Bowers performd. I Calld at mr Pollards and Son Lambarts. mrss Duttun wrode my hors home. I wrode home afterwards. mrs Abbot Deld at 3h morn of a Dagt.   at Lecter, mr Bowers Performd. Birth 43d. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 September 12, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Phillip Norcross Son. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld at 2h morn to See the wife of mr Phillip Norcross, found her very ill. Shee was Safe Deld at 3h of her Second Son and 7th Child. I receivd 9/ as a reward and returnd home, went to bed and Slept. mr Dingly Calld here. Ebenz went to Pittstn aftern. Cyrus went to mr Brookss, bot 2 Stone potts for to put Butter in; they cost 3/6 each - 7/6.   at [Phillip Norcross]. Birth 44th. receivd 9/ as a [reward].  
 

 

 September 14, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Wests Son & 2nd Child. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld at 11h morn to See the wife of mr West, who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 1 pm of a fine Son. I left her at 3, receivd 9/. mother & inft were Cleverly. I was Calld to Tim Pages. very [rainy].   at mr Wests. Birth 45th. receivd 9/  
 

 

 September 15, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jimy Pages Son. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain till noon. mrs Page was Deld about 5h morn of a Son, her first Born. I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd at home at noon, acompanied by Old mr Page. Son Jona here. Old Lady Hamlin, mrss Burtun, mrss Dingly, Ruth Rockwood & two of her Brothers took Tea with me.   at [mr Pages. receivd 12/. Birth 46th].  
 

 

 September 18, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn, Clear middle of the day, Showers aftern. Polly has been to her Fathers, Shee informs me they had haill there. Capt Abrm Pages wife Calld here. I have been at home.   at home. Polly Wall went to her Dadys.  
 

 

 September 23, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and windy. EbenZ workt with ye OXen for mr Child. I have been washing. Polly went up as far as mr Carters Store. I was Calld to See mr Waids infant, it has the hooping Cough, is very ill.   at mr Waids to See their infant. Cyrus informd me yt Dagt Town fell of their hors.
