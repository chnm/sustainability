War-department 
January 5" 1792 
Sir 
I enclose duplicate of my letters to the lieutenants of the counties of Allegany, Westmoreland, and Washington counties, in Pennsylvania, and to the lieutenant of Ohio County in Virginia-The originals were transmitted to you on the 29th ultimo past, and you were requested to forward the three latter by express- If you are satisfied that the dispatches will certainly reach the respective lieutenants you need not find the enclosed by an express you will however forward them by the first direct conveyance.
I am Sir
Your very humble se
HKnox
Major Craig
