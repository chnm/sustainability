 June 19, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Waters went from here to go to Bror Ebenzr Moors & Doct Bartons, Jona gone with him. Sally Farewel Calld here. Shee had been to ye Stores for Cloathing for Son Ephms Babe. Hannah Ballard went to draw her Aunts Breasts, returnd at Evn. Says her aunt is quite unwel this aftern. I have Cut grass, houghd the polatoes west Side ye hous. feel fatagued.   at home.  
 

 

 June 20, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Cutt grass, Sage and feverfue [&] Burnit. went aftern to See Son Ephms wife, find her more Comfortable. her Babe Seems Cleverly. Cyrus been down to the Settlement. informs me yt Ebenz Towns youngest Child Expired ys day.   at mrs Farewels, find them more Comfortable. A Death.  
 

 

 June 22, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have workt in my Gardin, Sett 24 plants, transplantd W Squash & Cucumbers. mrs Stone Calld to See me. mr Lambard and Waters went to Son Pollards, Jona & wife allso. my Nepheu Sleeps here.   at home. mr Waters Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 23, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work and attended to my plants. Covered and uncoverd them. done Some washing for my Nephew but what pain I have endurd in my left knee & legg. mr Waters Calld Son Jona & wife to See me. they went home [again].   at home, very ill at Evng. 
 

 

 June 24, 1804, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cloudy alternately. I have been Exercised with much pain but have had my work to do. Dagt Lambd Came in to See me, Shee was going to the quarterly meeting of the methodist Society. mr Waters attended at that meeting. Heman Burr Calld and took Tea here.   at home, very unwell. Dagt Lambd Calld to See me.  
 

 

 June 25, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been Exercised with pain in my limbs but have helpt do my work. Betsy Church Came this morn to help me. we Bakt Some Pies and Bread. mrs Farewel Calld here, Says that Son Ephms wife is feeble yet. mr Waters took Breakfast, dind & Supt with me. I had roast Lamb & Green Peas.   at home. we had green Peas. I wrote to Sister Walers.  
 

 

 June 28, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 raind Some. I have workt in my Gardin. Betsy washt the rooms. Cyrus went and Carried Greens to mrss Pitts. I was very Severly Exercised with my pain, went to bed Early.   at home, very ill at Evng. 
 

 

 July 2, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have workt in my Gardin. Sally Farewel Calld here, Says Polly and the Babe are more Comfortable. Cyrus been to Carry Greens & Sallod to Ephm.   at home. Sally Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 July 4, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. the annaversary of indePendance. there was an Oration deliverd at Augusta meeting hous by a mr Fuller, and another at the Coart hous by mr Thustan Whiten. I have workt in my Gardin and knit Some. Cyrus down to hear and See. Betsy pickt wool and went to her Fathers. Dagt Ballard went to her Bror Badcocks.   at home. indipendance was Cellibrated. an Oration Deld by mr Fuller of Augusta.  
 

 

 July 5, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I workt in my Gardin foren, Gathered String Beens and green [Peas]. got them ready to Eat, and was Calld in hast to James Boltons wife who is not very wel. was there all night, very much aflicted by flees.   at James Bolton. I had Peas & Beens from my Gardin. Death [Cpt Jones inft.]  
 

 

 July 6, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dagt Pollard Sent a hors to Boltons for me. I went there, and to mr Woodwds to see his Child who is not wel. was Calld back at Evng.   at Ditos & at Son Pollards. Death [Edmd] Warins inft.  
 

 

 July 7, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home, left the patient better. was informd that ye infants of Capt Jones and Edmond Warrin were dead. [Ba ] Smith washt for Self here and [dind] with me. I am informd of the death of mrss Robinson at the 4 Corners. I have Sett Cucumbers & Cabbages. Cyrus gone to the Settlement. we had a heavy Shour. the wind was very Severe at the fort, did Some Damage to rafts &C. Joan Smith Sleeps here. Son Ephm & Lemuel Slee supt here.   at Boltns. Funeral mrs Robinsons, Capt Joness & E. Warrins infants.  
 

 

 July 10, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Betsy washt. I houghd in my Gardin and pikt wool. mrs Stone, Wickson and Chace here to Tea. Hannah & Sally sleep here.   at home, had Compy. mrs Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 July 18, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I have felt very unwel. mrs Farewel Calld here. I Scolt the Bed room and Bed Stead. Betsy washt ye floor and Buttery. Hannah and Sally Sleep here. Lemuel Supt here.   at home. Dagt Balld [& mrss] Farewel been here.  
 

 

 July 20, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. the old Lady here all day, shee & Hannah and Sally Sleep here. mrss Bunker and Saunders Calld here. I finisht picking my Black wool.   at home, not very well.  
 

 

 July 21, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done various kinds of work. Old Lady Arnold left here forenn. Sally & Belsy Church Calld in here. Nancy Titcomb & Parthena Pitts here to Eat Curents.   at home. Lathrop & Burr here. Ephm took Supper here. X  
 

 

 July 25, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work and some in my Gardin. Son Ephm Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 August 2, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I have Done my hous work and Some in my Gardin. Joanna Smith workt here, spun 2 Skeins of [Cotten] yarn and did Some Chors. Sally Farewel Calld and took Tea with me, informs that Son Ephms infant has a Soar Mouth.   at home. Sally Farewel informd yt Ephms Babe has a Soar mouth. it is 7 months Since my husband went to Jail.  
 

 

 August 3, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day, Some Showers. Joanna washt. I workt in my Gardin and did hous wk. Shee went home some time before night. Luke Barton Came and Conducted Sally Pollard from here. Cyrus been to his Bror Ephms, the infant is not well.   at home. mr Puringtn Calld here. Sally went home.  
 

 

 August 8, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, Done my hous work but am feeble. mrs Farewel Calld and informd me the Babe was very Sick yet. mr Purington here for Camomile for his Son James. he is very unwell. Lemuel Sleeps here.   at home. mrs Farewel here.  
 

 

 August 28, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mr Gills after Breakfast, Calld at mr Craggs & Pittss. reacht my Sons at 12 [o] Clock. at _ past was Calld from there to Belgrade to Sherebiah Clearks. reacht there at 3h pm, find his wife in Labour. Shee Linguered thro the night.   at Belgrade.  
 

 

 August 29, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Sherebiah Clearks 6th Child & 4th Daugt. XX.

Clear. mrs Cleark Deld at 8h morn of her 6th Child & 4th Dagt. a fine Child and is as wel as Could be Expected. I went to mr Bisbys, Hankseys and Sett out home. Calld at S. Boltons, Dind at Son Pollards, S. Boltons and Brackets. reacht home before sun Sett. Lemuel Sleeps here, Says the babe is no better.   at mr Clearks. Birth 6th. recvd 9/ as a Compensation.  
 

 

 September 13, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I went to Son Jonas to See Patty, find her much as Shee has been. Dagt Ballard informd me [yt] Betsy Andrews was Deld of a fine Son this morn 3m after mid night. I have been unwell, was much relaxt last night. Sally Farewel is in ye Same Situation.   at Son Jonas. Pally is much as Shee was. Funeral of Chases infant.  
 

 

 September 22, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work and ironed. mrs Coal Came here foren and dind here. mrs Cypher Calld aftern. Cyrus been to take his Fathers Cloaths to him.   at home. mrs Coal Dind here.  
 

 

 October 3, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and moderate. Nabby Hatch here on a visit. Dagt Ballard to Bake. I gave ye former _ Bs Apples for her marm. I went after Sun Sett to See Jack Peirce, find him very weak. he has had Doct Howard to See him who Bled and gave an Emettic. he Seems very feeble. Heman Burr Supt here and went to help take Care of him. Cyrus is gone to husk at mr [Andrus]. I was Calld in the night to EbenZ Trasks wife who was in Labour. his Oldest Son is Sick. my hors thrue me as I went up.   at Son Jonas. Nabby Hatch here & Dind.
