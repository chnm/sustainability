War Department
Oct 7 1794
Sir
I have the honour to transmit your Excellency the extract of a letter from Mr Jay dated London 12 of July 1794, with a request in behalf of the President of the United States that the agreement which it specifies should be duly observed as it respects the frontiers of Vermont.
The status quo as it existed immediately after the peace of 1783 is to be inviolably observed. All encroachments since that period are to be abandoned.
I have the honor to be 
with great respect
Your Excellency's Obedient Servant
H Knox
Secy of war 

His Excellency
the Governor of the State
of Vermont
