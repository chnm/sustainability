We left the thursday before the hurricane hit , we left about 5:30 in the morning to get a head start before the major rush came . In mauricville we got into traffiic for like two hours but the rest of the way it was pretty smooth sailing except for when we tried to stop for gas or for food then we got held there for like an hour just waiting in line to get gas. So we stayed there for like three weeks  and we came back twice to hook up the generator to the house so my mom could stay there because she was at the hospitial to help. While the huricane was going through  we were celebrating my cousins birthday .
