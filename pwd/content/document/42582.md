description: Kirkland reports the arrival of the Five Nations delegation to Bethlehem Pennsylvania. Expresses gratitude for assignment of Colonel Procter and Captain Sedam and their help in getting the
  delegation through the settlements along the Susquehanna River. Goes on to describe the requested lodging requirements of the different nations; Seneka, Buffaloe, Ononadagos, Cayogas, Genesee, Oneidas,
  Tuscaroras, for himself and for French Peter.  Recommends that Colonel Proctor go forward to assist in arrangements. Discusses efforts to arrange visit of Captain Brant; has asked for direction from General
  Israel Chapin. Recommends a military parade for the Chiefs upon their arrival.
