Captain Callender Irvine 
7th August 1798
Recd 10th - 
Army
Carlisle August 7th 1798. 
Sir
I inclose you a list of Public Clothing & arms forwarded by Wm. Cook Waggoner to this place which i have recd. in good order - among the clothing I have recd. I find there are no Coats suitable for my Music, by forwarding them by the first opportunity you will oblige Sir, 
Your most obdt. Servt  
Callender Irvine
Captn. 2d Regmt. 
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr. 
[Stamped: The National Archives of the United States 1924]
Paid 12 1/2
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
Philadelphia
