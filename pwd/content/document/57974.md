Abstract of Capt. Brant letter
West Niagara 16 june 1797
We have no news but that the French are endeavoring to insinuate themselves among the Indians to revail on them to act both against the Brigith & Americans.
I am
Dear sir
your friend and o[undecipherable]
signed Jos Brant
