War office
June 5 " 1800
Cloaths for the 1st Reg Outf to be fowd  to Lt Dransy at Fort Wollcott
Dir
War Department June 5. 1800
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to John Wilkins Jun[ior] Esq. for 
transportation to Fort Wollcot near Newport Rhode Island to the 
address of Lieut Dransy of the first Regiment of Artillerists and 
Engineers, Six Hats, One Sergeants Coat four privates coats two Sergt:
Vests four private vests, three Sergeants Shirts Sixteen privates Shirts
twenty pair of Shoes, two Sergeants Linen Overalls eight privates linen 
Overalls, two Sergeants Woollen Overalls Seven privates Woollen Overalls
five Blankets four Stocks and Clasps.  
Ben Stoddard
charged with the duty 
of Serg of Re War
Depart. 
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
