 July 16, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Coutches 3d Son. XX.

Cloudy morn, raind aftern. I was Calld at Sun rise to See John Coutches wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd of a fine Son at 7h 30m morn. I receivd 6/ as a reward & left my Patients very Comfortable, & returnd home at 10. mr Pollard, Pitts, Son Jona, their wives, mrs Burton & Cyrus Dind here. we had a fine Loin of Veal & String Beens. mr Ballard was Calld to mr Kennadies at Evn.   at John Coutches. Birth 34th. my Childn here. Jonas wife & Childn tarry all night.  
 

 

 July 21, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, my Self & two Sons attended public worship. I went to my Sons in ye intermition, their infant is unwell. I Calld to See Son Pollard, he is lame. mr Pitts is very unwell with a Cold, Dolly went there after meeting.   al meeting & at my Sons.  
 

 

 July 27, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Ephm & Dolly went to winslow. mr Ballard to Survey hous Lotts for mr Vauhn. I went to See Phebe Hinkley, find her Sore is giting better. Calld to See mrs Parker, Shee is more Comfortable.   at mr Hinkleys & mr Parkers.  
 

 

 August 1, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Doing Business at the hook. my Girls washt foren & went to Quillt for mrs Pollard. I went to See Polly Livermore who is Siesd with St Vites Daunce. was Calld at 11h to See Black Hitty, went by water.   at mr Livermrs & Griffins.  
 

 

 August 2, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Black hitties Son. XX.

Clear. I put my Patient to Bed with a Son at 1h, morn & returnd home about Sunrise; took a nap. mrs Livermr Sent for me to help her Dress a blister on Pattys neck. mrs Benjamin here to have a goun made. mrs Porter, Pitts & Dagt Pollard here. mr Ballard been at the hook part of this Day.   at Grifins & mr Livermors. Birth 35th. receivd 6/ may 17th 1795.  
 

 

 August 4, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott, my famely all, Except my Self, attended worship forenn. I went aftern, was informd that little Jona was very ill; went to See him. Patty Town Sleeps here.   at meeting. my Son & mr [Child hi we wors ].  
 

 

 August 5, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a light Shower. mr Ballard went aftern to Do Surveying for mr Vauhn. I went as far as mr Sheperds, Bot 2 lb Sugar @ /10, 1 Chip hat @ /9; to Doct Colmans, bt 1 oz Senna, Do manna @ 1/ pr oz, 1 oz annis Seed /4, 1/2 oz pink root /4_; at mr Livermores, bt 1 Skein Sewing Silk /4.   at the Stores below here. Calld to See the mrs s Dumers & mrs Norcross.

 Clear & very warm. I was Calld to See Wilm Hewins wife    
 

 

 August 21, 1793, Wednesday

 Clear, mrs O. Hamlin Came here last Evng, is makeing her Self a Bunnit. Dolly had an ill turn.   at home.  
 

 

 September 1, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I was at meeting. mrs Ingerham informd me that mrss Hewin was Delivd of a Son the 26 ult. I was at mr Childs at intermition, find his wife as Shee has been. Son Pollard, mr Pitts, their wives & Polly Pollard here at Evn.   at meeting. my famely all there.  
 

 

 September 3, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jery Dumers Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 9h morn to See mr Jere Dumers Lady who was in Labour, Shee was Deld at 5h Evng of a fine Son, their first Born. I tarried & Nurst my Patients thro the night; they are Comfortable.   at mr Dumers. Birth 36th. 
 

 

 September 6, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth MC Neels Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 6h morn to See mrs mc Neele who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 12 of a Son which was inspird with life but Expird in a Short time after the Birth. I left her as well as Could be Expected at 6h pm & Came home. Dolly & Sally Came home from Son Pollards.   at mr mc Neeles. Birth 37th. Dagt Ballard & her [Childn] Came ys Evng.  
 

 

 September 8, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & a Cool air. mr Ballard, Cyrus, & Ephm & I attended Divine Service. the revd mr Turners Discourse was Concerning the Great Day of Judgment. I went to See mrs Dummer, find her weak but tollerable Comfortable, her Babe is a fine Child. Son Jona, Pollard, his wife & mrs Pitts here after meeting. my Childn all went home. mr Savage here, informs that a boy who Servd at mr Sheppards was Drowned at, or near, his wharf ys afternoon. I was Calld to See mrs Lashnees at 11h Evng.   at meeting & at mr Dumers. my Childn here. Jonas wife went home. An indian Boy was Drowned.  
 

 

 September 9, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. John Lashnees Son. XX.

Clear till near night when it raind. mrs Lashnees was Safe Deld at 5h morn of a fine Son which wd 11_ lb. I tarried with her till 6h pm, left her Cleverly as Could be Expected. I receivd [7/4d] for my Service. Calld to See mrs Dummer, find her Cleverly. mr Ballard Sett out on his Tour of Surveying. Ephm went with him to take the hors home.   at mr Lashnees. Birth 38th. mr Balld left home to Survey 4 Townships.  
 

 

 September 11, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A very rainy aftern. Ephm returnd about 1h pm, Son Towns famely well. Dolly went to work at mr Blaks. I have been at home, Sally Spining. Son Cyrus is 37 years Old this Day.   at home. Cyruss Birth Day 37 years.  
 

 

 September 14, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Did house work till near night & workt in my Gardin. Sally & I made Some Pies. I was Siesd with a Severe Pain in my Back which lasted an hour when I Seemd freed there from. mr Buzell Calld me to go & See his Sister Judy who was in Labr. I arivd at his house about Dusk. I felt the Collick before I reacht there, was Severely Exercised with it thro the night.   at mr Buzells, New France.  
 

 

 September 15, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Slepn Hinklys Son by Judy Buzell, out of Wedlock. XX.

Clear. I was Still Labouring under the triall of ye Collick. mrs Hinkley took great Care of me, I was Easier towards night when my Patients illness Came on more regular, and Shee was Safe Deld at 5h pm of a fine Son, which Shee Declard Stephen Hinkly is thee true father off. Shee is as well as can be Expected. I am Exceeding feeble thro the night.   at Dittoes. Birth 39th. I receivd 6/. there is 2/ Due for hors & medisin.

[we] Calld on mrs Capin.   at Ditoes, mr Wessons & Childs. I tarried at Son Pollards the night.  
 

 

 September 23, 1793, Monday

 Clear. I was Calld at 1h morn to mr Daws at Capt Cars.    
 

 

 September 25, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home from mr Daws, left her more Comfortable. Calld at mr Dumers, his inft has ye thrush. Wilm Hurd, his wife & Sist Betsy, & mr Meriam Sleep here, brot me a letter from Doct Barton which informs Capt Learneds wife is Dead. mrs Voce & Betsy Dind. mr Ballard returnd from Surveying.   at Ditoes. I receivd a Letter from Doc t Barton of augst 15, '93.  
 

 

 September 30, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. Son Town went to Pittstn, returnd & went to Son Jonas. Dolly went to mr Pollards. James More here, paid me for assisting his wife in may 14, 1791. mr Ballard [Lauing] out a Landing at the hook by martins.   at home. Son Town went from here.  
 

 

 October 3, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Towns Son & 9th Child.

Clear. mrs Child here till aftern. Son Town Came for me at 2h pm. I went forward as Soon as posable. Crost the ferry at 3, arivd at Son Towns at 7. Shee my Dagt was Safe Deld of a Son at 7h 30m. mrs Stackpl, Stratn & mrs [sic].   at Son Towns. Birth 40th, of which 27 are Sons.  
 

 

 October 4, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 a little rain this morn. Dagt Town is Cleverly. I tarried there this Day. the two mrs Fullers at Son Towns. mrs [Cragen] & Strattne there. Harnon Barton unwell at night.   at Son Towns.  
 

 

 October 8, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Magr Goodin Dind here. I was Calld to See mrs Daw, her Husband returnd from Boston.   at mr Daws.  
 

 

 October 10, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sally Came home, washt the west room. I was Calld to See mrs Benjamin, Shee has a pain in her Stomach. I walkt there & home, feel much fatagud. Dolly is at Son Pollards, he Came home [yesterday].   at Bizer Benjamins.
