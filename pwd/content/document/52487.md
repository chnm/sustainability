The Secretary of War
Department of War
Accountants Office
January 5th 1796
Sir
In my last letter to Constant Freeman
Agent of the War Departmt. in Georgia Informed him that the
claim of Milita of that State would be decided on wihtout
delay_ This information I was led to give him from the
conversation I had with you a few days ago on that 
Subject_ As the Post will set out for that Country on
Friday next I have thought proper Sir to remind you thereof
and that I may be enabled to give the information promised.
I am Sir &c.
William Simmons
