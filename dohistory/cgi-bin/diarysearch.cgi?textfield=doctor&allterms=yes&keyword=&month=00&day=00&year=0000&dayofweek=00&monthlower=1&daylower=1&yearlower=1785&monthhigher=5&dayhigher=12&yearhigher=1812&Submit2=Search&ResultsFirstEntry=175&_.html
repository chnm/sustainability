 July 1, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mr Savage, find him Easier. returnd [&] wed Garden all day, Except writing to Doct Barton & Bror Collins Moore.   at mr Savages. wrote to Doct Barton & Bror Collins.  
 

 

 July 19, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, rain aftn. I wed gardin till aftern, then went to see Polly Savages Child, it is Burnt. mr Ballard is gone to the hook Doct Coneys. William Haywood Sleeps here.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 August 9, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I returnd home, Left my Patient much more Comfortable. find Son Town here on my return, his famely are well. Doct Williams and Son & Collins Moore Sleep here.   at Dittoes. receivd 1/2_ for ye medesin & time Spent.  
 

 

 August 10, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Rainy foren, Clear part aftn. Doct Wilms & Son, mr Moore took Breakfast & Dind here. I went out after 3 h pm, hilld my Cabbages before ye Door then gatherd 2 pails full of Pees & helpt Shell them.   at home.  
 

 

 September 14, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I have been at home takeing Care of gardin Seeds. mrs Benjamin here. Doct Whiteaker Sleeps here. mr Tappin & mr Savage took Tea here.   at home.  
 

 

 September 15, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Hainses Dagt. XX.

Clear. the Doct left here before I rose. I was Calld to See mrs Hains Early this morn. Shee was Safe Deld at ye 10 hour Evn of a fine Dagt. I tarried all night. my Patients Cleverly. this is her 11th Child, 6th Dagt. mrs Cumings went home with mrs Harris. mrs Page & Briggs tarried at mr Hainses.   at mr Hainses. Birth 23d. I receivd 6/ of mr Hains. receivd Letters from friends.  
 

 

 September 16, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd from mr Hainses by the way of ye hook; dismist the Lad which acompanied me at mr Wades. Calld to See her Child. lift it Some tinchture. Calld at mr Hodges, Jose unwell; at mr Westons; all well at mr Savages, Polly Better. Cyrus Delivrd me Letters, one from Broth Elijah of Sept 4th / 90, 1 from Sist Walers of ye 5th and 1 from Doct Barton, same date, informing my friends ar well Except Colo [Jewns] wife.   at mr Hainses, Wades, Hodges, Westons & Savages.  
 

 

 October 13, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Doct Coney Came to See the Child, orderd Blisters & left Senna and some Drops. I Came from there at noon, left the Child very ill. Cyrus there near night. the Neighbours there, it was thot to be Expireing.   at Dittoes. Dolly & Hannah Rockwood here.  
 

 

 November 7, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I attended public worship. Deacon Coney made ye 1st prayer, Esqr Petingil ye Last forenn. Colo Sewal ye 1st aft & red Doct Dodridges Discoarses, mr Livermn Last prayer, but few attended. Polly Bisbe Came here to help us.   at meeting. Richardson went from here with his rafft.  
 

 

 November 13, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Colo Sewalls Son. XX.

A Clear & Butifull Day. mrs Sewall was ill till 3 h pm when Shee was thro Divine asistance made the Living Mother of a Living Son, her 3rd Child. mrs Brooks, Belcher, Colman, Pollard & Voce assisted us. I went to Colo Howard at Evng to ask assistance for Polly Taytor. Colo Sewall gave me 6/8 as a reward, Conducted me over ye river. I Calld at mr Craggs, was informd that Hannah North was thot to be Expireing this morn but is revived. mrs Colman informd me that the Doct, her Husband, gave Hannah a Dose of Callom Calomel for worms which gave relief. She went next Day to See mrs Goodin and took Cold, which has brot her into the Sittuation Shee now is. when I arivd home found Capt Silva Learned here from oxford. Letters.   at Dittoes. Capt Silva Learned of Oxford Sleeps here, brot a letter fm Broth Collins, 1 fm. Sister Barton of Oct. 31. Jon a killd his Oxen. Birth 28th. 
 

 

 November 26, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Ezra Hodges 3d dagt & 5th Child; the first was 5 ys old ye 10 of Sept last. XX

10 of Sept last. XX Clear part of the Day & very Cold. I am informd that Jona & others who were attempting to go Down with a rafft were inclosd by the ice at Bumber hook Point. thee ice makes very fast. Doct Williams & his Son here at Evn. Could not pass with yr Horses, left ym here and went to mr Pollards. I was Calld at 8 h Evn to See mrs Hodges in Labour. her women were Calld and Shee was Safe Delivrd at 10 h and 30 m Evn. mrs Pollard being unwell Did not arive till the moment the Child was Born. the Ladies who assisted took Supper after all our matters were Completed, & retend home about 12. mrs Pollard and my Self tarried all night & Sett up. mrs Hodges rested finely, ye inft allso.   at mr Savages and mr Hodges. thee ice Stopt at Fort Westorn. Birth 29th. Saml Dawin fell thro ye ice as he was attempting to go of ye rafft to ye Shore, but was Savd by their geting to him with a Cannoe & takeing him in.  
 

 

 December 10, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy ye wind to ye S. I have been mending hoes. mrs Wheeler left here after Breakfast. Doct Whitaker and his Son Sleep here. mr Ballard been makeing Plann. I was informd that Andw Goodins youngest Child Expird ye 6th inst.   I was at home. Dr Whitaker here.  
 

 

 December 18, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Benn Browns Dagt. XX. receivd my fee Novr 22nt 1791 in Turkeys.

Clear and very Cold. Dolly is unwell, took Soap pills, had Several faint turns. I went to See Polly mc kethney, find her very low in Strength and Spirrits. Doct Colman let blood in both her feet. I was Calld at ye 8th h Evn in hast to See mrs Brown. Shee was Safe Deld at 9 h 30 m of a fine Dagt, her 7th Child. mr Brown was absent. mrs Woodwd and my self tarried all night.   at mr Craggs & Browns. Birth 32nt. Death Polly Taylor.  
 

 

 December 23, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. at mr Craggs, tarried all night. mrs Jones & I Sett up. Pally rested better. Doct Williams there thee last & this night.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 December 31, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. I went to mr Craggs. Dolly remains mutch as Shee was. Doct Williams went to See Capt Page and returnd, tarried all night. I Close ye year there.   at mr Craggs & watcht.  
 

 

 January 4, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, the weather moderate. Doct Coney here; took acount of Births & Deaths ye year past from my minnits. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 January 12, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Titcom took Breakfast. Capt Slackpool, Bullin, Goodin & Town, and yr wives Dind & took Tea here, mr Benn Porter to Tea, I have been at home. Dolly Seems not So well. Doct Coney here, left her Some Bitters.   at home. a number of friends here on a visit.  
 

 

 January 25, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & not very Cold. I was unwell, Did not rise till mr Pinkam Calld me to go to mr Bisbes, his Dagt Polly being Siesd with a Complaint in her Nervs. I made use of pipe Phisic which gave relief & Shee rested much Bitter than Shee had for Some nights past. Capt Haywoods Lady & I watcht.   at mr Bisbes. mrs Haywood there & Doct Williams at Evng.  
 

 

 February 19, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd some. I left my Patients as well as Could be Expected in ye Care of mrs Walker and arivd at home before noon. I receivd 3/ of mr Greely in part for my performance. mr Bullin here, informs that he asisted Doct Williams to open the Soar on Willm Densmores thigh.   at mr Gralys & Colo Sewalls.  
 

 

 February 22, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. mr Ballard Came to inform me yt Capt Nicholss Lady was at our house. I returnd after we had took Breakfast, leaving my Patients remarkably Comfortable. after Chatting a little while with my friends, took a walk with them as far as mr Pollards. Calld at mr Blaks & Savages on my return. Fredrick Foot, his wife & Sister here on their way to mr Bullins. mr Pitt here.   at Ditoes. mr Thomas gave me 6/ as a reward. I receivd a Letter from Doct Barton of Freby 6th. 
 

 

 February 25, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. Jona & Ephm went to Pitts Town for Hay, mr Ballard to mr Carrs. I went to mr Savages to See about my Saddle irons. mr Thomas gave me a Letter from Doct Barton of Jany 26th. Snows this Evng.   at mr Savages. receivd a Letter from Doct Barton of Jany 26th. 
 

 

 February 27, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, wind S, Cloudy aftn & Evn. I went to See Willm Densmore, helpt Dress his Soar, it Smells very disagreably. I Coalld at mr Pollards, find Moses very unwel with a pain in his Ear and Side of his head. my girls went to mr Hamlins. the hors got away and Dolly tarried all night. mr Savage and wife here, inform me yt Doct Colman has opend ye Soar in William Densmores groin.   at mr Densmores & mr Pollards.  
 

 

 March 1, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I have been at home, have wrote to Doct Barton and Brother Collins & Sist Waters. Capt Getchel was here, informs me his Brother, ye magr, is at ye point of Death with a Cancer which has broak out in as much as a Douzen places on his boddy.   at home. wrote to my Friends at Oxford.  
 

 

 March 26, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Baker Left here Early. Doct Coney here to See Dolly. I have been at home. mr Ballard went to ye hook. Joseph Foster here to work.   at home. Esq Coney here.  
 

 

 April 23, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & a Brisk South wind. Son Town left here. mr Ballard went to mr Joness, had his mare Dockt. I have been at home. Cyrus Came here at Evng. Capt Lathrop went up with a Sloop ys aftn. Magr Getchel Expird this Day.   at home. mr Town left here. I wrote to Doct Barton. a Death.
