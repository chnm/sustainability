No 8
War Department
May 10th 1794
Sir,
You are to afford hands for the boat for the Officer of the Revenue, stationed at your post, and you must occasionally afford him proper assistance in the execution of his duty - 
I am Sir, Your humble Servant. 
signed 
H. Knox 
Secy of War 
Copy
The Officer 
Commanding at Wheeling
