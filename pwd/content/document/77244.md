From Saml. Hodgson
requesting the purchase of  [100 Â ?] hats

Israel Whelen
Purveyor,
Present
Intendant of Stores Office
Philadelphia,17.November 1800.
Sir,  
I find by the Store Keepers Report that
fiftythree Hats for Artillerists are wanting to complete
the orders which he has already issued, and Returns
from the Corps are daily received as of which em-
brace that articles.  I wish you to purchase or procure, 
and turn into the public store as as early as possible
One hundreds, answwering to the Pattern that I will fur.
nish.
I am,
Sir,
Your most obedient Servant,
Samuel Hodgdon
Israel Whelen Esq.
Purveyor
