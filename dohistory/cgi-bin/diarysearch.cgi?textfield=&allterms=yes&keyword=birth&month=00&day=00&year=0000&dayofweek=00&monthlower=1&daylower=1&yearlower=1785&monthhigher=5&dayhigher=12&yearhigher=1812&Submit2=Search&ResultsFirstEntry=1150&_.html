 November 24, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to mr Childs, Burtuns. he gave me 6/7 for assisting his wife in Labour. I Calld at old mr Pollards, mrs Baxter & Dagt Pollard Dind there. Roseanna Green was Sick with a pain in her head. I put her to Bed & Swet her. mrs Baxter had a turn of hysterics.   at mr Baxters & Son Pollards Burtins & old mr Pollards.  
 

 

 November 25, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I was at mr Baxters till aftern. Calld at Son Pollards, Son Jona there has movd there with his famely. Sally Cox is gone to her Dads, returnd with Dolly, went to Livrmr s.    
 

 

 November 27, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Cleard of at noone. Dolly went to mr Pollards to work, Shee Seems to be more unwell than Shee has lately been. I have been at home. mrs Smith Calld here, Son Jona here at Evng.   at home. Dolly is gone to mr Pollards.  
 

 

 November 29, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Phinneys Dagt. XX.

Snowd. mr Ballard finisht fixing the Barn to house his Cattle. I was Calld at Evn to See mrs Phinney, found her very ill. Shee was Deld of a Dagt, her first Child by her present husband, at 9h Evn. I tarried all night, mrs Desnmr allso. my Palients Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as a [reward].   at mr Phinneys. Birth 47th. his first Born, her first 6th Child.  
 

 

 December 6, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard is gone to mr Follets. mr Densmore Butched a Swine for us. Dolly is mending Capps for me. mr Baxter & wife Calld here, Shee Seems more Comfortable. I finisht a Stockin and began an other. mr Ballard bot 6 lb Butter of mr Cumings.   at home. mrs Baxter here.  
 

 

 December 7, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. we had Son Jon as Sleigh. Dolly & I wrode to ye hook to meeting. I was Calld at 9h Evn by Asa Williams to See his wife. I gave her medesin and used means which gave relief, tarried all night.   at hook meeting & at Asa Williamss.  
 

 

 December 8, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I left mrs Williams Comfortable as we Could Expect and Came home. Calld at mr Craigs, all well there; at Son Pollards, Doore. they and Son Jonas famely are Cleverly. mr Ballard gone up to fort, Cyrus to hook, Ephm to meddow. mrs Baxter been here to Day.   at mr Williams & Crages.  
 

 

 December 19, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charlss Cockss Son. XX.

misty. I was Calld out of Bed by Charls Cocks to So his wife who was in Labour. I Crost on the ice at Martins. Shee was made the Living mother of a Son, her first Born, at 10 & 20m morn. I tarried with her all night. the ice moovd by there.   at Charls Cockss. Birth 48th. receivd 6/7d. 
 

 

 December 20, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I tarried with mrs Cocks, Shee & infant are Cleverly. rain & thunder ys night.   at [Charls] Cockss. 
 

 

 December 21, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came from mr Cocks by water, left all well as Could be Expected. I Saw a Schooner go up by mr Goodins and a boat allso. I find a pair of new Shoes & put them on, they fitt me very well. Selvstr G. Moore Sleep here. we were alarmd by a noise in the night, the Girls & I rose.   at Ditoes. left thm Cleverly.  
 

 

 December 30, 1794, Tuesday

 Clear fore n, Cloudy aftern. I went to See Danl Livirmore, he is very ill with the mumps.   at mr Livermores to See his Son Daniel.  
 

 

 January 2, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Joshua Learned Dagt. XX.

Clear & very pleasant. I was Calld at 2 h morn to See the wife of Joshua Learned who was in Labour and was made the living mother of a fine Dagt at 10h Evng. Capt Neys Lady and mrs Dany assisted, tarryd all night.   at mr Learnds. Birth 1st for the pres t year.  
 

 

 January 3, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & remarkable pleast. I left my patients Cleverly and returnd home at noone, receivd 7/6 as a reward. find my famely well. Dagt Pollard, mrs Barton and Polly Pollard were here yesterday.   at Ditoes. Dolly had Company yesterday.  
 

 

 January 7, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A Severe Snow Storm. I was Calld at 6h Evn to go & See Capt Saml Huseys wife, went to mr Sheppards where they left me all night.   at mr Sheppards all night.  
 

 

 January 14, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Craiges Son, Rheubin Branards allso Son allso. XX.

Clear. I Came home and was Calld by mr Craige to See his wife who was in labour, & was Deld of her first Child, a Son, at 10h Evn and is as Cleverly as Could be Expected. I was Calld from there to See the wife of Rheubin Branard who was Delivd of her first Son & 4th Child before my arival.   at Capt Huseys, Conys & Branards. Births 2nt and 3d. Asa Cumingss remains interd this aftern.

 February 1, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jon as Dagt.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. Dagt Ballard was Delivd of a Dagt at 3h pm. mrs Woodwd & I tarried all night. I went to Bed & Slept Some.   at Jonas. Birth 6th.  
 

 

 February 2, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Black Hitties Son.

Snowd. I was Calld from Jonas to See Black Hitty. Shee was Deld of a Son before I arivd, found her Exercisd with pain, administred Something for her relief. returnd home by way of mr Husseys, all as well there as usual. I went to See my Dagt Ballard after I Came home, find her & infant Cleverly. Dolly is gone to work at Old mr Pollards. Sally Cocks gave me a Snuff Box.   at my Sons and Nicholass. Birth 7th. this man is a Portugues who was Brot here by mrs s Hussey from Nantucket.  
 

 

 February 5, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I arose this morn, found the ice was gone by our hous and that ye freshett higher than I Ever have Seene it. I Sent Cyrus to See what Circumstance Jon a and famely were in, he returnd with news that his house was Covered Several feet from the bottom with water and that his wife and Childn were Carried in at mr Burtuns. I went Directly there, find my Dagt Comfortable to admiration. I tarrid with her all night, do not find as she has taken Cold, notwithStanding Shee was out three or four hours on her bed in a Sleigh. they did but Escape before their house was Startd from the foundation and the Chimney throun Down. they have Since found more of their affects than Could have been Expected.   at my Son Jonas. his famely Drove out by thee freshet as ice being Jambd. his wife been Deld but 4 Days. the grait preserving mercy of God has wonderfully apeared for her and famely in their preservation.  
 

 

 February 8, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. my famely, Except Sally & my Self, attended Devine Service foren, my Self & Shee aftern. Tilton went from here to mr Livermores afternon. I Calld to See Son Jon as wife, find her Comfortable even to admiration. may the benevalent authur of all our mercies Still Continue his Goodness to her and her famely. mr Gill and White here at Evng.   at meeting, and at Son Pollards. Augustus Ballard and S. G. More Sleep here this night.  
 

 

 February 9, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of the Day. mr Ballard, Silva Moore & Ephm been Surveying the river. Dolly wrode to Son Pollards, finds Jonas wife as well as Can be Expected. mrs Barton & Sally Cocks washt, I assisted to Do kitchen work.   at home. Dolly made a Gown for mrss Abbott.  
 

 

 February 11, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Husseys 3d Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to Capt Husseys, walkt to the Loading place from there, Crosst the mountains of ice. arivd all most fatagud to Death, found his wife Deld of a Son. I returnd home by fort Weston, wrode our hors from mr Pollards. Dagt Ballard [Cleverly].   at Capt Husseys. Birth 8 th to which I have been Calld.
