W.D. Accountants Office
                                 April 5th 1794

Sirs

                The Treasurer is directed to remit

to you the following sums viz.

           To be paid Michael G. Houdin [?] at Albany

For Salary from 1st July 1793 to 31st March 1794 inclusive _ _ _ 180.00 To be paid to Capt Ro. Ledam [?] For pay of his recruits for Decembr 1793 _ _ _ 31.87 To be paid do for pay of himself and his recruits for Janry & Feby 1794 _176.80

  To be paid Capt Bezl Howe

For pay of himself and his recruits

  For January & February 1794    _    _   _                   250.70
                                  78
           For which you will be pleased to take 

duplicate receipts accordingly.

           As Capt Ledam [?] is ordered to your 

city I suppose it would be best for you to retain his Money until he arrives~

                                    I am Sirs etc
                                     Joseph Howell

Nicholas Fish Esqr Supervisor - New York
