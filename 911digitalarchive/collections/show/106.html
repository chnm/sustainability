Danita Bishundat

Title

Danita Bishundat

Description

I met Danita Bishundat at the Women’s Memorial in May 2002, during an outreach effort. Danita’s brother Romeo Bishundat was killed in the Pentagon attack on September 11. The family has agreed to submit a family narrative of their experiences in addition to a photo essay of their son’s life and death.

-Chrissie Brodigan
