War Offfice
24th May 
1796
War department
Samuel Hodgdon Esq [circled: 155] 
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894. R.P.O. 401310] 
War Office May 24. 1796
Sir,
I have handed your letter of this date to the Secretary.  He says it will answer if the Vessel that takes the forge will sail on Thursday or Friday.  
I am Sir
your obed Servt
Nn Jones
Samuel Hodgdon Esq
