War Office 9th Octo 1794 Clothing wanted at Lancaster 
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. No. 24. 1894. R. P. O. 401310.]
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr No. 71  North Water Street
279
War department
October 9 1794
Sir,
Please to have forwarded to Lancaster addressed to Captain James Gamble of the Corps of Artillerists and Engineers the following articles of clothing .. viz'- 
+ One Serjeants Coat √
+ One .. ditto .. Vest √
+ Two ... ditto Overalls (Woollen) √
+ Three .. ditto Shirts - √
+ Nineteen privates Coats √
+ Nineteen .. do .. Vests √
+ Thirty eight . do. Woolen Overalls√
+ Fifty seven do.. Shirts√
+ Forty pairs of Shoes √
+ Forty pairs of Socks √
+ Twenty stocks √
+ Twenty stock Clasps √
+ Twenty blankets 
I am Sir, 
With great respect
Your obed. Servant
Jno Stagg Junr 
[& order]
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
