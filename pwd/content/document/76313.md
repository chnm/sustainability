I. 176War Department, 19 July 1800
Sir
I was honored this morning with your Letter of the 10th inst.t the enclosed recommendations shall be duly attended to.
The List of proper characters to fill the existing military vacancies is maturing, and shall soon be submitted to you.
I transmit for your signature a warrant for the Execution of the Sentence of the Court Martial on Samuel Ewing.
I have the honor to bewith profound respecyour very obed.t servtSam.l Dexter
The President of the United States
