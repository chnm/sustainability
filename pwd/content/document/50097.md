To fix the carriage of the Chickasaw good from Pittsburg to Nashville, I want to know precisely the weight of the whole. This I wish to be furnished with to-day, that I may settle with the gentleman who will con-tract for such carriage & sits in waiting.
Yr.obt.servt.
T. Pickering [signature]
War Office May 12. 1795
Samuel Hodgdon Esq
