The Secretary
XXXVI-143
Dear Sir

  May I invite the three char-ge's des affaires to attend the cere-mony?  may they be permitted to bring respectable strangers of their nation with [out or in??] Strike-through text[indecipherable] limitation of num-bers? do ladies go?  if they do, must mrs Otts. must be named in the invitation to mr Otts.  I will beg the favor of your answer to queries & govern myself accordingly. only be so good as have reserved for them a seat in a respectable position. 

                          yours affectionately & respectfully

                                 T. H. Jefferson

Thursday even.
