folio 178 (October 30, 1789 meeting)

  

  178
Voted  to accept the Letter Reported by the Committee to be sent to the Ministers of Boston.
Voted the Town Clerk signe said Letter.
Voted to adjurn this meeting to 30th Day of October Instant at 10 o'Clock fore noon & then to meet at this House met agreeable to adjurnment.
Voted to adjurn to 2 o'Clock this after noon--
then this meeting being opened according to adjurnment.-
Voted

Will.m. Howard a committe to settle with Mr. Isaac Foster agreeable to the report of the Refferees & to take a Discharge & acquitance from said Foster for this Town.--

Then this meeting was Dissolved

  attest Joseph North Town Clerk
