Order in favor of
Capt. John Harris
for 10 knapsacks
Intendant of Stores Office
Philadelphia 29th, Dec. 1800
Sir,
Be pleased to issue to the order of Capt. John Harris of the third Regt. of Infantry Ten painted Knapsacks, for the use of Recruits at the rendezvous in this city under his command, agreeably to his return dated this day.
Your most obedient Servant,
Samuel Hodgson
Mr. John Harris
