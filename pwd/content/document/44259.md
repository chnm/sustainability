War Department
February 23 1793
Sir
I have received your letter of the 15th, instant with its enclosures, enclosing the returns of the stores on the hand 20th, July last, and since received and also the articles forwarded to the several ports and issues at Pittsburg to the instant.
I am
Sir
Your humble servant
HKnox
Secy of War

Major Craig
