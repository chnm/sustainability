aligned right]New York June 27.th 1786

Sir

Inclosed is a Particular Acco.t of the Persons to whom I paid the New Emission money received by me from Peter Boyen for the German Regiments taken from my Vouchers. I suppose it unnecessary that you have the Vouchers unless to prosecute any Person who has failed orders on me for it

I am Sir

PJ

John Nicholson Esq.
