Internationally celebrated artist Rolland Golden resides in Folsom, Louisiana and Natchez, Mississippi.  A New Orleans native, Golden has had over 100 one-man shows in galleries, cultural centers and museums in the US, as well as one-man touring invitational exhibitions, notably, in the former USSR in 1976-77, and in France 1993-95. Golden's Katrina exhibit at the New Orleans Museum of Art (opening November 2007) represents some of the most stunning visual interpretations of the devastation; it also marks his 50th year as a professional artist. <br/>
