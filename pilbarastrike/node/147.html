Primary Source

Kathleen Brown, one of the non-Aboriginal people who joined the group in 1953, taught school at Yandeyarra while the group waited for the Western Australian Department of Education to provide trained teachers. This is an account of her experiences.

A Teacher's Experiences in Yandeyarra
Citation

Kathleen Brown, untitled article on her experiences at Yandeyarra in 1953, Joan Williams Papers, State Library of Western Australia, MS 5425.
