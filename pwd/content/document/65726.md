Tobias Lear 
13th December Rec'd 14 1798
Recommendation &c Mr. Delaney to work on the Canal 
L L  1798 
Saml Hodgdon
Superintendent of Military Stores
227
Sir,
This will be handed to you by Mr. Morris Delaney, who has been employed in the Canal & lock work in the Susquehanna & on the Potomac - he is a steady faithful man, and gave satisfaction in the Service of the Potomac Company.  Should such a person be wanting at Harper's Ferry  I can venture to recommend Mr. Delaney, for fidelity sobriety and attention to his business as a lock master &c. 
I am respectfully &c Sir 
Your most Obt S[ervant]
Tobias Lear
Decr 13 98
Saml Hodgson Esqr
