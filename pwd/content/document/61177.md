War Officer March 2d 1798
Mr. John Harris  
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to Lieutenant McRea for the Marines on board the Frigate "United States" Two Coats, three Vests, one Linnen Overalls, six Woolen Overalls, three shirts, three pair of Shoes, one pair of Socks, two stocks and clasps, six blankets and two Knapsacks. 
James McHenry
