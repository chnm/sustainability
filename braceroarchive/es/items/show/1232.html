Title

Newspaper Article

Description

This article clipping is in regards to a meeting that the Mexican government had with ex-braceros and their supporters. The point of the meeting was to find a conclusion to the back wages owed to the ex-braceros, but the meeting was inconclusive. The Mexican government stated that it did not owe anything to the ex-braceros. This article was published February 13, 2004.
