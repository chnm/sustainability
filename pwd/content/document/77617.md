26
Hon.ble
Mr.
Griswold
War Department 23 December 1800
Sir 
I am honored with your favor of yesterday. 
The amount thought necessary for fortifying ports &
harbors has not heretofore been inserted in the annual 
estimate of expenditures; it was therefore omitted in the es
timate for 1801. 
I presume that the deficiency of past appropria
tions is not equal to 100,000 Dollars, but the precise amount
cannot be known. I think that to appropriate for that 
object Two Hundred Thousand Dollars in the whole would be sufficient 
for the ensuing years. 
I have the Honor [illegible]
