[Written along right edge at the top of the page:]
Jeremiah Condy
19th September
1800
Recd 22.


Paia 25
Colonel Samuel Hodgdon
Intendant of Stores
Philadelphia
Dear Sir
You will oblige me exceedingly by forwarding the enclosed Letter for The 8undecipherable] Pickering to him as soon as you conveniently can - as also to torward the Enclosed Letter for amsterdam by any Vessell for that Place or any Port in the Dutch Territory or Hamburgh.
I shall be happy to render you any Sercive in my power in return
I am obt. Sert
Jeremiah Condy
Gerogetown Sep. 19.1800
Col. Hodgdon
