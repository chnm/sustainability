West Point January 15th 1790 - 43

Sir/

Lieut Pierce has made application to me for Camp Kettles and Straw for the men in this Garrison-- Their is but two Camp Kettles in store that is fit for service-- if you should think proper that straw should be provided for the men it can be got at about 3d or 4 pence per Bungle-- I wish to be in formd how much will be allowed to each man, and how often

I am sir &c

The Honorable
Major Genl Knox
Secretary at War
