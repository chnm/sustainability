Major J Miller 
Adjt Genl 
HeadQuarters
Captn Marschalk
8 June 1795
Fort Jefferson
Fort Jefferson 8 June
Sir 
I have taken into the public store at this post one barrel of whiskey, the property of Joe Gard
I have also permitted thirty-seven barrels to pass the property of the public, agreeable to the enclosed certificate from the waggon master.  
With respect Your mo Ob
A. Marschalk Capt
Major J Miller
