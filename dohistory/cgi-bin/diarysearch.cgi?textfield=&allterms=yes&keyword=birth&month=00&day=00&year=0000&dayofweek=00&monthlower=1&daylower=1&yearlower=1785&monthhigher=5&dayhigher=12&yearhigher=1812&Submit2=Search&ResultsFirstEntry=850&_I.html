 September 1, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cool. I gatherd up Corn the Swine had broak Down in our field. I was Calld aftrn at 6h to See mr Childs inft. I walkt there, tarried all night. we gave the patient a portion off rhubarb, it is much relievd.   at mr Childs.  
 

 

 September 3, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my Girls washt at ye Brook. I was Calld at 2h pm to See mrs Savage who was in Labour. her women Calld at Evng.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 September 4, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Isaac Savages Dagt.

 Clear. mrs Savag was Deld at 2h morn off a Dagt, her 11th Child & is Cleverly. I was Calld Early to See mr Andrewss infant who is Sick with the Chin Cough. went to my Sons, his Son is unwell. I returnd home at Evn. I Saw mr Benn Brown, his wife & her Sister on their way to Canaan. mrs Brown is recoverd off her Late indisposition.   at Does. Birth 31st. mr Andrus paid me a fee off 6/ for allendance. Isaiah Manley Expird.  
 

 

 September 5, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charls Clearks Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld this night to See Charls Clearks wife who was Deld at 3h morn & is of a Son, her 4th Child, her 3d is Destilule of reason by reason fitts. I receivd 6/ of mr Cleark, gave him a former acount off 6/. Calld to See mr Uriah Clearks Dagts who are Confind with weakness. I Calld at mr Pollards & at mr Hodges, his Childn unwell.   at Charl Clearks. Birth 32nt. my Girls went to mr Pollards at Evng. 
 

 

 September 16, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Allins Dagt. XX.

Clear part of ye Day. I was Calld by mr Allin at 7h morn to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld at 9 off a Dagt. I left her Cleverly at 12. mr Allin gave me 6/ as a reward. he Conducted me to ye ferry & I attended worship in Public afternn, walkt home. Ephm has the Chicken Pox.   at mr Allins. Birth 33d. Cyrus was at home. a rainy night.  
 

 

 September 19, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard & the rest off my famely went to See ye muster. mrs Mc Causland & others Dind & took Tea here. Jonas wife tarries here. I receivd [9]/ off mrs Powel for asisting her.   at home. a General Muster of ye regament at Shubal Hinkleys. Andrus and Son Jona fout. 
 

 

 September 28, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a Cold N W wind. mrss Hinkley Calld here. I have been at home, mr working on thee Bridg.   at home.  
 

 

 September 29, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mr Densmore Butcherd a Swine for us which wd 30[  ]. mr Ballard went to ye hook. Esq Jewet off [Dover] Sleeps here. I have been at home. mrs Colman Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 September 30, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard & Parthenia & Dolly & Ephm attended Public worship. mr Smith Deliverd a farewell Sermon, his text foren Corinth 13:13, aftn, Isah 17:17: Wilm Haywood Dind & my son Jona Supt here. I receivd a Letter from Doct Barton informing his famely are well & that Aunt Moore was Deceast, & her remains were interd 15 inst.   at home. receivd a Letter from Doct Barton which informs me off ye Death of Aunt Moore.  
 

 

 October 12, 1792, Friday

 Cloudy foren, Clear aftern. I went to mr Wicksoms, Calld at mr Hamlins & Baxters & mr Dinsmores. I finisht Card ing hannahs Cottne ys Evng.   at mr Wicksons & others.  
 

 

 October 22, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I Did work of Divers kinds till about midnight, was then Calld to See mrs Kenny.   at mr Gillmans  
 

 

 October 23, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kenneys Son. XX.

Clear & not So Cold. mrs Kenney Deld of a Son at 10h morn. I left her & inft Cleverly at 3 pm & returnd home, receivd 6/.   at mr Kenneys. Birth 34th. 
 

 

 October 24, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Densmore & Dolly here to work makeing Gouns for my Self & all three of the Girls. mr Ballard at mr Dummers, Bot 6 Bowls, Cost 3/. mr Densmr Supt here. I was Calld to Thos Kenneys at 11 Evn.   at Thos Kenneys. raind at 10h Evng. 
 

 

 October 25, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth J. Kenney Son. X. X. Birth James Burtun Dagt. XX.

A rainy morn. mrs Kenny Deld off a Son at 1h morn, and mr Kenney gave me 6/ as a reward. mr Burtun Came after me at 2 o Clok. very hard rain. I left mrs Kenny her & inft Cleverly. I walkt to ye Landing thro great fatague, passt by water to mr Wessons, walkt from there to mr Burtuns. [ ] arivd at 4h morn, find his wife Deld off a Dagt but in Danger for want off assistance, but thro providence I Delivd her Safe at 4h 30m. I was as muddy as I Could well be. I tarried till near night. mr Burtun made me a prest of 6/ & a Looking Glass, price 7/6, which I beStowed on my Dagt Hannah.   al Does & mr Burluns. B, 35. Birth 36th. mr Ballad at Esqr Conys, recvd his pay of him. Cyrus was here.  
 

 

 October 26, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleasant. mr Isaac Savage & his wife Dind here. mrs Black, her Sist Pollard & Polly here aftern. I was Calld to See mrs Kenney, made use off means & medesin which gave relief, & returnd home at 10h Evng. mr Savage & wife left here at 12.   at mr Whites. we had Company.  
 

 

 October 27, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Elisha Preskts Son. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld at at [sic] 4h morn to See the wife off Elisha Preskott who was in Labour, & was Safe Deld off a Son at 5h Evn. Shee was very ill, her Husband was absent. I was attended by an English young Lady who Lodges at mrs Sherburns. I left my Patients Cleverly & arive at home at 9h Evn; find my Son, his wife & Son here, all well.   at Preskotts. Birth 37th. Son Jona & wife here. I Bot a Chees, wt 11 lb @ /7 pr lb.  
 

 

 November 1, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ezra Hodges Son. XX.

Cloudy & Some rain. mr Town left here after breakfast. the Girls had the Ladies to help them quillt. I was Calld to See mrss Hodges at 4h pm, Shee was Safe Deld at 11h Evn off a very fine Son, her Sixth Child. mr Ballard Came home.   at mr Hodges. Birth 38th. Son Town went home.  
 

 

 November 2, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Stickneys Dagt. XX.

Clear foren, Cloudy aftn, rain at Evng. I receivd 6/ of mr Hodges & returnd home at noon, left my patient Cleverly. Calld at mr Burtuns, his Lady is Cleverly. I Bot off him 2 iron kettles which Cost 7/, 1 Spider @ 3/6, 2 pepper boxes & 2 Dippirs @ /6 Each, 2/; 1 yd binding /1, Ginn 2/6; total 15/1. I went to magr Stickneys at 9h Evn, his wife Deld at 11h 5m off a Dagt. I tarried all night.   at Does & mr Burtuns & magr Stickneys. Birth 39. Bizer Benjamins wife Deld of 2 Sons, Both Dead.  
 

 

 November 3, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy foren. I tarried at the Magrs till after Dineing, receivd 6/ as a reward. mrs Conry Came with me to mr Devenports where we Spent ye remainder of ye aftern, returnd home at Dusk. I left my Patients Cleverly.   at mr Stickneys & mr Devenports. 
 

 

 November 6, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Burtun who has had an ill turn & is Better. Dolly Sleeps here.   at mr Burtuns.  
 

 

 December 2, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear but Cold. we were informd that Old Lady Coney was not like to Survive but little while as her Symptoms are more Dangerous. mr Livermore, his wife & Son & my Self went over to See her. Crost ye river in a Canoe by breaking thro ye ice in Several places. we found the old Lady in A very helples & allmost Sencles Situation. her Dagt Cumings is her Nurs, her tryals are great. God grant her Strength & patients to perform her arduous task. Moses Pollard was very ill this night. I was Calld up to help Hannah Nurs him.   I went to See Old Lady Coney who has had a late Shock of ye Palsey, her left Side is useless.  
 

 

 December 3, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Clear. mr Ballard Sett out to Survey mr Pittss Land North from him. mrs Pollard Came here to See her Son who is very ill indeed. we gave him Sage Tea & a decoction off Snak root & Cammomile, [Steamd] his feet &C. the Girls washt, Jona here.   at home. mrs Pollard here.  
 

 

 December 7, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I was Calld to See mrs Child, Shee is very unwell. I recommended to her a portion of Senna. I Calld at mr Wessons & Pollards. I Bot 1 mugg a 1/, 2 tumblers [2]/, 2 Cruitts 1/2.   at mr Childs. Ellenr Taylor Expird  
 

 

 December 16, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Cold. I went to my Sons to See his Child, it is a little more Comfortable. mrs Pitts wrode with me as far as Son Pollards. I went in & warmd, they are well there. Cyrus returnd from Sidney. John Chamberlin was marid ye 10 inst, removed his wife to Sidney ye 11th. Shee was Deld of a Dagt ye 12th & it Expird before night.   at my Sons, the Child is better.
