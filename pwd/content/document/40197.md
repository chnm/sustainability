Gen Knox's Letter
Filed January 6th 1790
1789
Dec 17th
[large X]
War Office December 17th 1789
Sir
In addition to my request of the 14th instant I am constrained to ask the patronage of your Excellency, to the enclosed public letters for Kentucky, as we have no established mode of Conveyance to that district. -
I should be happy of an opportunity of performing any similar services for your Excellency.
I have the honor
to be Sir
your most obedient
Humble Servant
KNOX
His Excellency
Beverly Randolph
