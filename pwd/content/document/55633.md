War Office 22d November 1796
Samuel Hodgdon Esq
[encircled: 370]
[stamped:  Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894. R.P.O. 401810.]
War Office November 22d 1796
Sir,
I will thank you to inform me whether the Waggons now returning to Knoxville will be able to take any and what number of Suits of Clothing.  The Secretary would if there is room  in them  send a small supply to that Quarter.  
Your obed Serv
Nn Jones
