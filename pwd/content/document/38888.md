West Point August 13th 1787 Sirs

I have enclosed Ensign Luses Receipt for the One hundred and fifty Eight Dollars, which I gave you mine for 

Please to transmit me my Receipt by Mr Luse when he returns to this place 

I have stated Wallace's affairs, and you have them enclosed, which you will deliver to Capt Mercer, as the Commanding Officer as you may think Proper  I am sir Capt Beatty 
