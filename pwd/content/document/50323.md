Department of War Acct's Office May 29, 1795
Estimate of Cloathing of a non Commissioned Officer Music or Private for one year
Price Amount
1 Hat	.67	.67
1 Coat	4.00	4.00
1 Vest	1.00	1.00
2 Pair woolen Overalls	1.40	2.80
2 Pair Linnen Overalls	1.20	2.40
4 Pair Shoes	.81	3.24
4 Shirts	1.00	4.00
2 Pair Socks	.09	.18
1 Blanket	1.50	1.50
1 Stock	.08	.08
1 Clasp	.04	.04
D19.91
Estimate of one Year's Cloathing due to those Men of Lieut. William Rickard's Company whose times of Service expire during the year 1795 per his return 40 non Commissioned Officers, Music & Privates at 19 Dollars 91 Cents each...Dls 796.40/100
Wm. Simmons Acct.
Dolls. 796.40/100
The Secy of War.
