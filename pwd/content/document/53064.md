Agent's Office Department of War
Charleston So. Carolina 8th March 1796
Sir/
agreeably to your instructions
contained in your letter of 31. December 1795
and 14th January 1796. I have made the
several payments as therein dettailed, to Constant
Freeman Esquire Agent of the War department 
in Georgia, and herewith enclose you his anual
Receipts therefor_
I am Sir
Your
Most obt. Servt.
Dan. Stevens (Agent Wardpt.
