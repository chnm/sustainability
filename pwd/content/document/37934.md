Comptrolrs. Office

June 14, 1786

Dr. Sir,

In the return you were good enough to enclose me of the Officers of the 8th. Regt. deranged I find not Cap. John Hughes  the man now a member of Assembly, through his own Affidavit, and the Certificates of some other Officers of the regiment, has obtained the depreciation from me  I had before I received yours got a hint of the mans character &c  Will you be so kind as to serch the musters from June 1778 until the time of the derangement (I suppose they are in Bankson's accounts) and see & certify if mustered resigned and when 

Jno. Nicholson

Cap. Howell

P. S.

P. S. Cap: McHenry was of the 5 Penna. Reg. I enclose a rect. for Cap. Ramsay's Certificate
