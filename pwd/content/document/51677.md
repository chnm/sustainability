- Six Nations remain very quiet
- Senekas in council at Fort Erie
- Oneidas have there sold to the commissioners of New York one hundred thousand acres of their reservation
- sum they sold for I have not been able to learn
- Capt. John, the Oneida chief died of sickness while at Albany
- presumed to draw on you for the same
- rec'd a further supply
- make me accountable for the sum and for the expenditures of the whole
- your letter
- inclosed one thousand dollars
- inclosed a receipt to you for the same
- mentioned to you in my last I was under necessity for money for the use of the six nations
