York.dec 15
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
Q. M. Genl
post Philadelphia
Letter from Wm. Hill 
enclosing a Receipt for 
2 Boxes Cloathing 
Dated 15 Decr 1791
New york 15th Dec 1791
Sir
By desire of the Secretary of War I forward you two boxes contg the following articles of clothing for the Troops in the service of the United States, by the way of Amboy. The receipt for the delivery shall be transmitted you by Post wishing them safe and speedily to hand.  [illegible] with much respect 
Sir 
Your very hum[ble] 
William Hill 
(Duplicate)
SH
1 } A box Contg. 160 pair Woollen overals
7 } A box Contg 153 pair - Do. 
499 pair Woolen Socks
Received New York 15th December 1791 from William Hill on board of the Amboy Stage Boat Two Boxes #1 & 7 and mark'd SH containing clothing for the Troops in the service of the United States which I promise to forward to Samuel Hodgdon Esqr. Q. M. General in Phila. the danger of the Navigation excepted he paying the Customary charge of Transportation having Sign'd Duplicate Receipts - 

1 Contains 160 pair Woollen overals

7 Contains 153 pair __ Do [and] 499 pair Woollen Socks
Robt Patterson
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894 R.P.O. 401310.]
