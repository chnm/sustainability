Estimate of pay & forage due the detachments of Fort Fayette & Big beaver Block House, Fort Franklin & Wheeling founded on the authority of my Estimate of the 13th Inst - Viz
Fort Fayette for the months of January, February, March & April 1793   Dolls. 944
Big beaver Block House for Do Do  224
Fort Franklin for the months January February & March 1793  471
Wheeling for the Months Jany Feby March & April 1793 444
Dolls 2033. 
War department
Accountants Office
March 25 1794
Joseph Howell Acct. 
Major Isaac Craig
