Philadelphia 12th December
Sir,
By a letter just received from McPerkins I am informed the men that went from hence were
all arrived and that three forges were already at work. The Vessel that took the Goods and Materials
was safely arrived at George Town and Mr. Mackey was gone to receive them and send them to his
post. Has Mr. Brindley received his instructions? The Artist for the Mill Work and the undertaker of
the digging of the Canal are very deserious of having his report that they may make arrangements
for going forward. Both are willing to undertake by Measurements as you proposed.
I am
Sir
Your Most Obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
James McHenry Esq
