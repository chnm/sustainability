description: From Flint River Georgia, Bernard report to Seagrove that he has been to Cussetah Towns with David Cornell, head warrior of Tuchabachees. Conclusion was to give satisfaction for robbery and
  murders at Traders Hill. Men were sent by Cussetahs to kill the perpetrators. Daniel Dourouzeaux, villain who turned Spaniard, at behest of Panton, interfered with the business. Nevertheless, both upper
  and lower Creeks have determined it should be done. Discusses intrigues by Spanish agent to undermine United States through Indian tribes. Cornell pledges loyalty to United States and General Washington.
  Cornell wishes Seagrove to visit to counteract influence of Spanish agents.
