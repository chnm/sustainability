War Office
January 31, 1800
Cloathes to be fwd to Charleston, SC for the use of Capt. Watterson's Comp.
Orders for 1800
War Department 1 January 1800
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to John Wilkins Junior Esquire Quarter Master General for transportation to Charleston South Carolina the following articles for the use of Captain Kalteisens Company of artillerists & Engineers



 Sixteen Hats
 Seventy three Socks


 Eleven boots
 Fourteen Stocks and Clasps


 Thirteen Vests
 Sixteen Blankets


 Twenty two Shirts
 Sixty six Canteens


Twenty seven pairs of Shoes
 Fifteen Epaulets


 One pair Serg Woollen Overalls
 Two musicians boots


 Forty mens pair ater. W. Overalls blue


 Twenty pair Linen Overalls

James WHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
M. John Harris
