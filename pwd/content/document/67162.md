                                                                    Accountant Office
                                                                         April 5 1799


I certify that there is due to Adam Hoops, Major of the 2nd Regiment of Artillary & Eng7 the sum of Three hundred and forty seven Dollars and eighty cents being for his pay, forage and subsistence from 1 December 1798 to 31 March 1799.

W Simmons
