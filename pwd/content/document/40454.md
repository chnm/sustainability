Springfield March 12 1790
Sir
When I think of the attention that has been paid to me in my necessitous circumstances, and of my sittuation in life, I cannot but with gratitude think on the author of it, that his name may ever be remember'd in my famerly I have named a son Henry Knox - he was born on the first of March, he is a fine large boy.
Your Honor will please to excuse me for takeing the liberty of addressing you on such a subject
I am Sir with much esteem
Your Honors Very obeient Servant
John Bryant
Honle General Knox
Secretary at War
