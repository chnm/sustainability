We begin the second semester of the American Civilization course with the assignment of the research paper. With the increased availability of high quality sources on-line, we now require students to use at least one electronic source for the research paper. To avoid excessive reliance on electronic sources, we also insist that students utilize at least two print sources. In an effort to help students find suitable Web sites, we provide them with an initial list linked to the syllabus with active links to a number of sites rich in primary sources.
