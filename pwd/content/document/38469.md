New York Jannuary 11th 1787

Dear Sir

Colonel Baldions regiment now paid for 1776 on an abstract referrinf to the Pay Rolls which are not to be found. I cannot therefore ascertain the Officers Non Commissioned Officers or Privates, the numbers of men in the regiment or those who had deserted from it

Pierce

Oliver Wolcott Jun. Esq.
