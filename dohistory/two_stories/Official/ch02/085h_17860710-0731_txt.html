July 10 through July 31, 1786

  

  July, 1786.         Hallowel.
Monday 10.  
Tuesday 11.  
Wedsday 12.  
Thursday 13.  
Friday 14.  
Saturday 15.  
Sunday 16. Mr. Foster preached in the meetghouse.
Monday 17.  
Tuesday 18.  
Wedsday 19.  
Thursday 20.  
Friday 21.  
Saturday 22.  
Sunday 23. Mr. Foster preached -- [poor Doctrine]
Monday 24. Mr. Emerson of Georgetown called & lodged here.
Tuesday 25.  
Wedsday 26. Mr. I. Foster took my Horse from Mr. Emerson's pasture.
Thursday 27. My brother Jotham arrived from Bath.
Friday 28.  
Saturday 29. Moses Sewall went down river.
Sunday 30. Mr. Moor of Pownalborough Wiscassit preached in exchanges with Mr. Foster.
