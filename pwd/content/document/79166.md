de Jaudenes + de Viar                
Philadelphia 12th, Octt. r 1791
Sir
In reply to your favor of this morning we have the honor to transmitt to you the inclosed certificate, which being upon the same terms that the one you mention, will, no doubt be agreable to your wishes.
We feel extreamely happy in assureing you that our private mali-tion will always lead us to render you and your friends any services consistant with our public duty,private gacity; and [undecipherable]
request in question attended with bad circumstances we have very willingly complied with.
We have the honor to be 
with great respect
Sir
Your most bedt
hum. servants
Joseph de Jaudenes  Jsef Ignacio de Viar
J. I. Jw
