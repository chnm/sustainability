7 January 1800
                           William Bennett
Dear Sir.


         I received your letter of the 

4th of Dcr. on the 30th. 


         You will find in this mail a letter from the Secretary of the Navy for your son Edward, and a warrant of Midshipmen.  I hope he will choose himself by his conduct to the authority of the confidence because in him and live to be an honour to his country.
                                               

                                               I am To Sir
                                               Yours truly
                                               James McHenry


William Bernnitt Sqr.
