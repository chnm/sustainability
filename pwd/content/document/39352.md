Resolve of Congress
apptt of Indian Agents
reed July 2. 88
1788
June 23rd –
War Office, June 23rd, 1788.
Sir,
Being unacquainted with the residence of Colonel Joseph Martin I have taken the liberty to enclose a letter to your Excellency for him. 
Colonel Martin having been chosen by Congress, agent for the Cherokee nation of indians, agreeably to the within [undecipherable] it is of forme importance that he should receive his appointment as soon as possible, which I request your Excellency to have the goodness to forward.
I have the honor to be,
With the highest respect,
Your Excellency’s
Most obed. & most humble
Servant
Knox
His Excellency
Governor Randolph
