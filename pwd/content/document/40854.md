Secretary of War

  filed Aug. 24. 90
   August 9th      1790



                War Department



               His Excellency
     
           The Governor of Virginia

                     Richmond
War  department
Aug. 9th 1790

Sir
I take the liberty to select your
protection of the enclosed packet of letters
for Judge Innes of Kentucky, and request
you would be pleased to forward them in
the safest and most expeditious manner
I have the honor to be 
Sir
your most obedient 
Humble Servant
                           
H Knox

His Excellency
The Governor of Virginia
