Return for Winter Clothing for one Sergt. & one private of the First U. S. Regt. of Infantry for the ensuing year. 
[11 Column Table Headers: For Whom. Hats, Coats. Vests. Shirts. Stocks & Clasps. Pairs of Shoes. Pairs of Socks. Pairs of Woolen Overalls. Shoulder Knots. Blanket.]
Sergeans. 1 [hat]. 1 [coat]. 1 [vest]. 2 [shirts]. 1 [stock & clasp]. 2 [pr shoes]. 2 [pr socks]. 2 [woolen overalls]. 2 [shoulder knots]. 1 [blanket]. 
Privates. 1 [hat]. 1 [coat]. 1 [vest]. 2 [shirts]. 1 [stock & clasp]. 2 [pr shoes]. 2 [pr socks]. 2 [woolen overalls]. 2 [shoulder knots]. 1 [blanket]. 
Total 2 [hat]. 2 [coat]. 2 [vest].  4 [shirts]. 2 [Hook & clasp]. 4 [pr shoes]. 4 [pr socks]. 4 [woolen overalls]. 2 [shoulder knots]. 2 [blanket]. 
Charles Hyde Captain
First U.S. Regt. Infy. 
Sir, you will be pleased to issue two Hats, two coats, two Vests, four shirts, two stocks & clasps, four pair shoes, four pair socks, four pair woolen overalls, two Epaulets, & two Blankets agreeably to the above Return.  
Philadelphia 13th Novr. 1799.  
Chas.Hyde Captain
First Regt.
