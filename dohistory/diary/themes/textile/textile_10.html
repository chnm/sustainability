 November 2, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page
2 5

 

 

 Clear. we brot in 40 Cabages. I have been at home, knit Some, finisht my mits and began to foot my Stockins. at home. Hepsy Spun 1 Skein woolin yarn.
 

 

 November 8, 1797, Wednesday

view full Diary page
[8] 4

 

 

 Clear but Cool. mr Ballard Came to Son Lambards and Conducted me home about noon. I have been Seeing to my pickels, apples and other matters. Hepsy is at work on my wool which is for Stockins yet. at Son Lambards.
 

 

 December 9, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page
9 7

 

 

 Clear part of the Day, a little Snow after. Son Jona Came and Conducted his Sons and Dorkis and Allin Lambart from here. mr Ballard been to his hous to work. I have been at home, finisht my Self a Cottne Shift. I footed a pair hoes for Jack, knitt hands to a pair mitts for my husband and nurst the Childn. at home. Dorkis and the little Boys went home.
 

 

 September 14, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page
14 6

 

 

 Clear. I have been overhauling my wool and Sorting it. mrss Dingly Sent for me aftern, Shee is unwell. mrs Densmore Came there. I Sent for Jane to tarry with her and Came home my self. at mr Dingleys, his wife is unwell.
 

 

 September 29, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page
29 7

 

 

 Clear. my Son Jon a & I left Son Towns and reacht home before Sun Sett. it has been very Cold ys day. I left my Dear Dagt a little more Comfortable than Shee was when I went there. Watson got in the last of our flax ys Evng. Jane returnd here this Morn. at Son Towns. my Dagt Seems a little revivd. mrss Hynes watcht with her last night.

 October 18, 1798, Thursday

18 5

 

 

 Clear and pleast Except Some windy. I have Done my hous work. the Girls Broke wool. I went to See mrs Dingley aftern. Shee is as well as the times will admit of. at mr Dingleys. Jane had a tooth Extracted, and one last Evng.
