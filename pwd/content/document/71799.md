107 Accountants Office October 5 1799
I certify that there is due to Francis H. Huger, Captain 2nd Regt. of Artl. & Engrs. the sum of, Sixty four Dollars & 91/100, being balance on settlement of his account for Recruiting, & Contingent expences including pay from 1 May to 30 Sept 1799, which sum is to be transmitted to Danl. Stevens Supervisor Charleston and to be by him paid to the said Capt. Huger.
Pay of the Army..Doll. 64.91 W.S.
The Sec. r of War
