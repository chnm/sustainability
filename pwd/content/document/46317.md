W.D. Accountants Office

April 5th 1794

Sir

I received your letter of 20th Ult covering your receipts for payment to your re= cruits which are passed to your credit - The Trea= surer has remitted to Nicholas Fish Esqr. Supervisor at New York the sum of 250 dollars &0 Cts being for pay of Yourself and recruits for January and February 1794 which you will discharge a= 

76 greeably to the inclosed pay roll ~

I am Sirs etc

Joseph Howell Capt Bezaleel Howe Westpoint via Peekskill State of New York
