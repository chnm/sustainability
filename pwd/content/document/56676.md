362
Accountants Office
February 28 1797~
I certify that there is due to John Seth Captain in the 3d
United States Regiment the sum of [underlined] ninety-six dollars and
[underlined] twenty cents [end of underlining] being for his Pay from the 1st January to the 28th
February '97 inclusive and for his Subsistence from 24 January
1797 until the same period.
Dollars 96 20 100	William Simmons
Acct.
The Secy of War
