No. 9
Knoxville
February 7th 1797
Recd of David
Henley, Agent of
war forty five dol-
lars in full of the
within acct.

      Geo. Farragut
The United States
To George Farragut [undecipherable], 
for his services as an assistan Store 
Keeper at Knoxville at fivteen Dollars 
per month from the 1st day of October 
to 31st day of December 1796 being
one quarter at 180 Dollars per Annum

               Dollars 45 - 0
               Geo. Farragut

I certify that I have Examined the above Account
and find due to George Farragut the sum 
of Forty five Dollars which please pay
Col David Henley          Stephen Hillisaper

      AWD                    Jany 28, 1797
