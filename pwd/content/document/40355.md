Department of War
Accountants Office
February 6th 1790

John Matthews Esq.
Supr Savannah
Sir
The Treasurer of the United States
will transmit you the sum of Two hundred and forty four Dollars
being for the Compensation of Isidore Stouf temporary Engin[undecipherable]
at Savannah from the 1st September to 31st December 1790
inclusive which sum you will please to pay the said
Isidore Stouf on his application therefore, taking his du=
=plicate Receipt for the same.
I am Sir & e
William Simons
