                                      Pay Office New York June 15th 1786

Sir

            By Colonel Blount I send you orders drawn by

the Treasury on the Receiver of the continental taxes for your state
in favor of the Officers of the Line agreeable to the List inclosed to
you in my letter of the 10th Instant amounting to Thirteen thousand
three hundred & sixty seven dollars 34/90, the receipts for which on
your payment I wish you to transmit me to this Office.

                                                 JP

Major Robert Fenner
