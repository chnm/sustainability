Caleb Swan Esqr
P.M.G. Detroit 
Accountants Office
September 29th 1797
Sir
Herewith you will receive an extract taken [from] the Warrants Book of this Office from 1st September to the [present] day for your government.  
The date of my last letter to you was the 18th enclosing a letter for Captain Barnard Gaines and for Ensign Yelverton Peyton.  
I am &c. Wm Simmons Acct
