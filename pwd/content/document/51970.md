299
D. of War. Accountant's Office.
Nov. 7, 1795.
Presley Nevill Esqr.
Pittsburgh.
Sir,
I have in absence of the Account and to acknowledge the Receipt of your letter of the 30th[undecipherable] together with your receipt for 2500 Dollars, which sum is carried to the Debit of your Account as pay Masters to the Militia Army, on the Books of this office. ~
I am [undecipherable]
Peter Hagner Clke.
D of War. Accountant's Office
