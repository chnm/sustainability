War-department, March 10th.1790
Sir
I am directed by the Secretary of War to
request, you will, with all possible dispatch, for
ward the letter addressed to the lieutenant of
the County of Wasington in the State of Penn
sylvaia, which will be delivered to you by
the bearer.
I am
Sir,
with great respect,
Your very humble Servt.
Jon Stagg Junr.
Chf Clk
