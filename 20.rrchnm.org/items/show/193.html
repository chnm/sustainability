Title

Papers of the War Department 1784-1800

Description

Grant proposes to continue the work started at by Theodore Crackel and his team at East Stroudsburg State University in 1994. The purpose and goal is to make available some 50,000 War Department documents that have been recovered through a multi-year search of hundreds of repositories and to make this archive accessible online and free.

Deliverables

Deliverables include; transition and building of infrastructure for the project, Papers of the War Department 1784-1800 (Series 1) website with 12,500 documents, including full content search for one quarter of the collection.

(1) Transfer the project to a new home at George Mason University;

(2) Develop the technical infrastructure for delivering the papers online;

(3) Complete the process of locating documents in additional archives and libraries;

(4) Complete the digitization of approximately 7,500 remaining documents;

(5) Enter basic metadata for approximately 11,000 remaining documents and link individual image files to these new records as well as 4,000 others that have not yet been linked;

(6) Provide detailed finding information (including document abstracts) for 6,500 additional documents;

(7) Launch a public, beta test bed for the online papers that would include one-quarter of the entire collection.
