 June 25, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page
25 2

 

 

 rainy. mr Ballard Sett plants. I quilld for hannah Cool. Peggy Came from Springers, Enforms yt mrs Lumber is Dangers ly Sick lately, put to bed with a Son. Foster went from here ys day. mr Hovey Brot me 3 lb Butr.  
 

 

 June 26, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page
26 3

 

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Vasalboro. Leut Browns wife, old mrs Gardy & mrs Frich, Capt Savage & wife here. the girls went to mr Crages. mrs Loes & Rhoda Came home wh ym.  
 

 

 June 27, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page
27 4

 

 

 A Shower this morn. Isaac Hardin Came for me to go and See his oldist Son, Sick with the rash; it is Sweled on ye Neck. mrs Harris, Cowen & Foster there. I returnd home at 5 PM. mr Ballard at work on the high way. mr Porter and N. Howard here ys night.  
 

 

 June 29, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page
29 6

 

 

 Clear. Sherebiah Town went to Pitts town. Nathan Howard went on Bord ye vesel for Boston. Theophelus Came in from his farm. Polly Higins had 2 oz Salv, her mother Scalt her feet. I went to ThoS Sewals, Calld at Wilms, mr Pollards & Westons, watcht with Becky. Shee is very Sick.  
 

 

 June 30, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page
30 7

 

 

 at mr Sewalls all Day. Came home at Eving.  
 

 

 July 1, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page
1 G

 

 

 Clear. we had ice an intch thik in our yard South Side of ye hous this morn. I attended public worship; a Sermon preacht on ye Death of Lady Howards Daughter. Pamela out publisht. mr Porter here. Isaac Leighton Came here to work fors us the ensueing week. this is the Day apointed for Brother Collins Moores Joining in wedlok with mrs Betsy Chamberlin of winchester. may their Connections be for their happyness.  
 

 

 July 2, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page
2 2

 

 

 rainy morn. Sally Peirce Bakt here. I went to See mrs Savage, Shees unwell. Hannah Coole gone to mrs Sewall.  
 

 

 July 3, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page
3 3

 

 

 A Shower. mr Ballard went to a meeting at the Church. mrs Gardy came here. I have had a pain in my Stomach. Ephm went to the meeting hous to Catechizeing. mr Hardin here, Says his Child has had the Soar opend on its throat & is much Better.  
 

 

 July 6, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page
6 6

 

 

 Clear. mrs Tylor Brot the Black Child here. mr Ballard went to mr Cars. they procurd her 1 Bushel of wheat. mr Porter, Joseph Burton & the Girlls went to mr Faughts. James Stackpool & Betsy Town came here. Polly Savage, Sally Peirce here. Hannah Cool unwell, her throat sore. I have been at home.  
 

 

 July 7, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page
7 7

 

 

 Clear. mr Ballard gone to Church meeting. Pamela & Hannah went to mr Densmors. Betsy Cut out Pollys gown. hannah Cool remains unwell. I Sett up wh her till 11 O Clok.
