I dreamed of the big oak tree in front of the green shudders on the brick house that I spent my life growing up in.  I thought of walking on the pathway toward the glass front door.  I thought of what a small walk it was from the driveway to the pathway to the door.  Then, barely able to walk, I felt what a long walk it was to the door from the bed that I laid in so far away.   \r\n\r\n
