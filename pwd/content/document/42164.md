War department,
Nov 10 1791
Sir,
I have received your letter of the [undecipherable] was the enclosures.
Thro some mistake the overalls were not forwarded as soon as was expected, however these are now on the way to Pittsburg, three boxes of woolen Overalls, and a medicine chest_ The latter is addressed to Dr. Carmichael, who is to remain at Pittsburg for the present.
I am
Sir,
Your humble Servt
HKnox
Secry. of War
Major Craig
