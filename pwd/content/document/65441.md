Colonel David Henley
16 November 1798
Recd 2 Jany 1799
Samuel Hodgdon Esquire 
Philadelphia
Major Colbert
189
Sir
Colbert a chickasaw being undetermined yesterday to set off with the other Chiefs has determined this evening to do it and I have purchased him a Horse for the purpose, inclose you a receipt for the Sums - he cost forty five dollars.  
I am Sir 
Your Hum Servt
David Henley
Knox 16th Novemr 1798
Samuel Hodgdon esqr
