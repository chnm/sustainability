Office do Army Accounts. NYork

July 7 1786

Sir

I have recd. your Letter advisind me that the papers of your late Office are forwarded to Philadelphia and I shall take the Earliest Opp.o to get them to this Place.

You pay as Judge Advocate has not been settled for the paid in two Capacities and that you have already been paid for the Same as Auditer__Alex Fowler Esq. ........ JP.
