Title

Musée Galliera

Name

Musée Galliera

Address in Paris

10, Avenue Pierre-1er-de-Serbie

Years Open

1894 - present

Gallery Description

The museum was originally conceived by Marie Brignole-Sale de Ferrari, the Duchesse de Galliera to house temporary art exhibitions. As of 1977 it is a museum of fashion.

Latin American Artists Exhibited


1923: Carlos Alberto Castellanos in the "Exposición de Cartons de Tapisseries pour Aubusson"
