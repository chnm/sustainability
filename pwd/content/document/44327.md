War department
Samuel Hodgdon Esq.
Letter from the War office dated 7th March 1793
Received from State Dep't Nov.24.1984 B.P.O. 401810

War Department 

March 7th 1793

Sir

The Secretary of War requests that you will receive from on board teh Vessel Commerce lately arrived from Charleston South Carolina a quantity of damaged gun-barrels &c Public property and have the same deposited in the store under your care.

I enclose you a copy of A. A. Mullers letter of the 23d Ulto on the Subject.

I am 

Sir with Esteem

Your humble servant

Jno Staff


Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
