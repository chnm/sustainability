War Office
Colonel Jeremiah Olney
Providence
Rhode Island
free Knox
Letter from the Secretary of War Octr 20th 1789
Sir

  I sincerely hope you may be

able to find the villains who stole the
public powder - I approve of the reward
you have offered, and of the measures
you have taken to secure the Magazine
in future.

   I am e'vr
      Your most Obedient Servant



               Knox
