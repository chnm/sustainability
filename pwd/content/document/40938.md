War Department Sepr 24th 1790
Sir
In the Absence of the Secretary of War, who is gone to the Eastward, I have the honor to transmit the inclosed Copy of his letter to your Excellency of the 15th instant
I have the honor to be SirWith the highest respectYour Excellency'sMost obedient & Veryhumble ServantJno Stagg Junr Chf Clk
His ExcellencyThe Governor ofVirginia
