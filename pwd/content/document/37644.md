Mr Ingles Valuation of the Public building at Campington - 14th Jany 1786
The subscriber by Request having measured &
Valued the Materals of an Old Store in
Campinton the property of the United States
are of Opinion they are worth twenty
pounds




G W Ingels

Philada Jany 13th 1786
