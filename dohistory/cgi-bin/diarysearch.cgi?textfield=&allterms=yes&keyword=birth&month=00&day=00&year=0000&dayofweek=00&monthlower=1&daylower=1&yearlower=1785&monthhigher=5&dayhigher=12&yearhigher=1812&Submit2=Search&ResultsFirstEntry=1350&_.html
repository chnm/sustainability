 April 12, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Catons Son & Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Caton has been ill and was Deld of a Son at 2h 30m pm, and of a Dagt at 3h pm. I tarried all night, have had but little Sleep this three nights. mr Simeon Clearks wife watcht. I Could not Sleep for flees.   at mr Catons. Birth 17th & 18th. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 April 13, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home attended by John Chamberlin. Calld at Son Jonas, his wife is very unwell; at Peter Joness and Son Lambarts, they are well. find my famely well on my return. Nabby has made my Soap Since I left home. I found 80 flees on my Cloaths after I Came home, which I brot from mr Catons.   at Ditoes and others.  
 

 

 April 21, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard been Surveying. Son Lambart Butcherd a Swine for us. the mrs Pages and mrs Cumings Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 April 27, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Browns Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld about Day Break to See mr Benn Browns wife. I walkt to the ferry, wrode from there, arivd at 6 h morn. my Patients illness Came on Severe at 9. Shee was Deld at 12 of a very fine Dagt. her illness was Severe and Shee was very faint after. Shee was put in Bed, but revivd and Seemd as Comfortable as Could be Expected. I left her about 3h pm and walkt Down to the ferry, had Son Pollards hors to wride home. mr Brown gave me 7/6 as a reward. I Calld at mr James Cowens; mr Pierces, find him better than he has been; at wido Savages, Shee is unwell. Crost at the ferry, was in at Old mr Pollards.   at mr Benn Browns. Birth 19th. receivd 7/6.  
 

 

 April 29, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thos Kennys Dagt. XX.

rainy. I was Calld aftern to go to Ephn Cowens. I prepared to go and Thoms Kenny Came, Exprest a wish to have me go and See his wife. mr Cowen Consented. I went, find her very ill. Shee was Deld of a Dagt about Sun Sett. I left them Comfortable and went to Cowens at Evn, find her not very Sick. I lost my Shoes.   at Thos Kenneys. Birth 20th. at Ephm Cowens allso, lost my Shoes & fell from my hors.  
 

 

 April 30, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. at mr Cowens. he went Early to Seek my Shoes, found them, informs me Mrs Kenny is Cleverly. mrs Cowen is not very Sick. I tarried all night.   at Ephm Cowens.  
 

 

 May 3, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Nabby is So well that Shee washt. I have mended Cloaths for mr Ballard, he went to Son Jonas. Ephm here this morn for Some Sause and Eggs. Cyrus & Ebenz ploughing North part of ye field & Door yard. mrs Mc Master, her Dagt Bell and mrs Hardford Calld here. I was unwell at Evn, but was Calld at 11 O Clok to Petr Clearks Junr, his wife ill.   I was at Peter Clearks.  
 

 

 May 4, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Peter Cleark Junrs Second Sone. XX.

A foggy morn. mrs Cleark was Deld of a son at 3h morn. I left her and inft Cleverly and returnd home at 5. Old Lady Cony Calld here going to the hook. mr Ballard, Ebenz & Nabby went to the Exhibition. I feel not So well as I Could wish.   at Peter Clearks. Birth 21st. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 May 7, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy the most of ye Day. Cyrus went to Pitts tn. mrs Whealor went from here. I have workt in my Gardin and door yd, removd my gardin roots. Ebenz made fence on the Entervail. I was Calld by magr Stickney to go to Capt Littlefields, his Lady unwell. her women were Calld, we were there ys night.   at Capt Lilllefields. mrs Whealor went from here. raind at night.  
 

 

 May 8, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Littlefields Son. XX.

rainy. I was with mrs Littlefield who is very unwell, her illness increast and her women Calld at 10h Evn. Shee was Safe Deld at 11 and 3/4 of a fine son and is Cleverly. we all tarried thro the night.   at Ditoes. Birth 22nt.  
 

 

 May 9, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn. I left my Patients Cleverly and returnd home at 9h m. Ebenz went to training at Capt Smiths. Cyrus went to mill &C, &C. we Sett Seed Beets & Carrots in the Door yard. I laid down and took Some Sleep aftern. Nabby went to See Mary Densmore, finds her very Poorly. Ebenz returnd this Evn with the Mallancoly news of Peter Jones being Drowned. may God grant his Supporting mercie to the Afflicted widdow, and Other Connections, and enable us all to make a wise improvmint.   at Ditoes. I was informd this Evng that Peter Jones was Drownd this aftern in the mill Stream.  
 

 

 May 10, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. Cyrus went to his Brothers, informs on his return that mr Peter Jones was taken out of the water. I was Determind to go and See the Distrest widdow, but was Calld to See the wife of Ephm Cowen, who is very unwill.   at Ephm Cowens, his wife is very unwell.  
 

 

 May 12, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld from mr Cowens to See mrs Allin, find her very unwell, returnd at 1h pm, find my Patient not So well as I left her; there I remain.   at Ditoes and at mr Allins.  
 

 

 May 14, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ephm Cowins 3d Dagt and 4th Child. XX.

Cloudy. I was at mr Cowens, his wife was in Labour all Day. mrs Savage and Swanton went to See the Ship Monlezumaum Launcht. we had 11 Children there thro the Day. Shee Calld the Day Calld [sic] her women at Evn and was Safe Dild at 11h Evn of a fine Dagt. I tarried thro the night, my Patients Cleverly.   at Ditoes. Birth 23d. April 1799 Ephm Cowen Credt by Sping 19 Skeins of Lining yarn what was my Due was 9/.  
 

 

 May 15, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I left mrs Cowen and her infant Cleverly at 7h morn and arivd about nine at my own habitation, find my family all well. Son Ephm here and Son Jonas Sons here. Cyrus and Ebenz went to meeting. Cyrus Conducted my Grand Sons home afterwards.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 May 16, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. I went to See mrs Waid and Foster. Calld to See Mary Densmore, Shee is very low. I went to mrs Leaches and paid Sarah Neal 6/ which was 1/7_ more than I owd her for Spining. Sarah went to Capt Blunts to liv.   at mr Waids and other Neighbours.  
 

 

 May 21, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the Day, Showers of rain & haill. I planted Cucumbers and workt in my gardin. went to See mrs Dingly, Shee is not So well as Shee Could wish. I Calld at mr Livermores, all well there. I took my Candles of the Stiks and put them in to the Box, have it full and 1 DoZn more.   at mr Dinglys and mr Livermores.  
 

 

 May 27, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy the most of the Day. Nabby returnd at noon. I was calld to See the wife of Josiah Norcross at 6h pm, find her very unwell. I tarried ys night, Slept Some.   at Josiah Norcross. 
 

 

 May 28, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Josiah Norcross Dagt. XX.

rainy foren. I was at mr Norcross, his wife Deld at 3h pm of a Dagt. I receivd 8/1_ and returnd home at SunSett. Nabby Jewel left my house and business at the middle of the Day bound for Monmoth, accompanied by mr Allin. I paid her 1/1_ as Shee past mr Norcross.   at mr Norcross. Birth 24th. Nabby Jewel left here.  
 

 

 May 30, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld out of bed this morn to go and See mrs Waid who has the Collic. I gave her Some red peppers, Steept, and Shee got ease Soon and was able to Sitt up and work aftern. mr Wade Conducted me home at Evn, he let me have a Salmon fish, wt 21 lb.   at mr Waids, his wife has the Collic.  
 

 

 June 1, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Cyrus and Ebenz made a pin for the Sheep. Son Town here, he informs me his wife is not So well as usual. Dear Child, I wish to See her. I have been makeing Soap, am informd ys Evn that Old mr Smith at ye fort Expird ys aftern. may we all hear the Call and be prepared to follow him, may God Enable the Surviveing Connections to make a wise improvment of this visitation.   at home. Death of Old mr Smith.  
 

 

 June 3, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I attended funeral of Old mr Smith this aflern. I Calld at Son Lambarts as I went, my Dagt went with me, Dagt Pollard went allso. my Son Cyrus attended.   at the house of Capt Ezra Smith. the remains of his Father interd.  
 

 

 June 4, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Joseph Youngs Son.

Clear. I have been Doing my house work. we had a Calf Butcherd by mr Lambart. I Cleand ye head and feet and Bakt. mrs Savage Came here to See me. I was Calld to See ye wife of Joseph Young at 6h Evn. Shee was Deld at 10, her infant Expird at 11, it was a Son.   at Joseph Youngs. Birth 25th. ye Child Expired an hour old. 5th [    ].  
 

 

 June 5, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Carters Dagt. [first Born] XX. Birth John Stratins Son, first Born. XX.

Cloudy part of the Day. I Came home from mr Youngs at 7h morn, got Breakfast and part Did my work, and was Calld to See mrs Carter. Shee was Deld of a Dagt at 11h 30m, it Expird an hour after ye Birth. Old Lady Hamlin is Come here from Oxford, Shee brot m a Letter of may 11th from Doct Barton. I was Calld at 3h pm from mrs Carter to mrs Straton. Shee was Deld at 4h 30m of a Son who is likely to liv. I Came home at Evn and Do feel much fatagud, but was oblidged to Sett up and Cook the orful of my Veal.   at Ditoes, and mr Carters & Gillmans. Births X 26th and 27th. X I receivd a Letter from Doct Barton of may 11, 96 by mrs Hamlin who is Come to reside in this Place for a time.  
 

 

 June 7, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Elisha Noless Dagt. XX.

Clear. Son Town left here Early, going to Coart. I have Done my work, Sett out at 3h pm to See Dagt Lambart, was met by mr Noles and Disered to go and See his Brother Elishas wife who is in Labour. I arivd at 6 and Shee was Deld of a Dagt, her third Child, at 9h Evn. I tarried till morn.   at Elisha Noless. Birth 28th. April 27, 1798 receivd 3_ yds Cottne Cloath @ 3/.
