[James McHenry]  <br>
Sec<sup>y</sup> of War

N<sup>o</sup> 4


War Office May 14<sup>th</sup> 1795

Sir

The Bearer hereof is M<sup>r</sup> Josiah Fox who for near a year
past has been employed in the Navy Department in this City assisting
M<sup>r</sup> Humphreys the Constructor in making the Draughts & Moulds
for the Frigates. He is handsomely Spoken of by M<sup>r</sup>. Humphreys who
thinks there are few Men in this Country equally qualifyed in this
line  Mr Fox's good Character & professional skill render
him a Valuable Acquisition to the United States & beg leave to
to recommend him to your Attention

The absence of M<sup>r</sup> Morgan, who was
destined to Construct the Frigate to be built in Virginia has induced me
to send M<sup>r</sup> Fox there as an Assistant Constructor, & who will be able
to direct with Certainty the application of the Draughts and Moulds 
in fashioning all the Timbers and Building the ship

I am Sir respectfully <br>
Y<sup>r</sup> most Ob<sup>t</sup> Serv<sup>t</sup>. <br>
Timothy Pickering


M<sup>r</sup>. W<sup>m</sup> Pennock
