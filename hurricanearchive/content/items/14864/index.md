I am a representative of the US Coast Guard who volunteered to go to LA from FL after Katrina hit.  I felt helpless alone but with the help of all my shipmates, I strongly believe we made a difference.  People had different attitudes.  Some were greatful for being saved, some were angry at God for losing everything.  I, personally, think that life is irreplaceable, period. Nobody can take away from you your own character, personality or education but God!  Everything else can be rebuild on any other meridian of this globe.  God Bless all of you!  Nice Mardi Gras, BTW!
