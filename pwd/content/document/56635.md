Major John Stagg
21st February 1797
Want to know what Cannon are at Pittsburgh & Knoxville

Samuel Hodgdon Esq
Feb 21 1797
Dear Sir
Will you be pleased to inform the Secretary of War, the number of Cannon, Brass or Iron, mounted or dismounted at Pittsburg).  Also if there are any at Knoxville.
Yours respectfully, 

Jno Stagg Junr
S. Hodgdon Esqr.
