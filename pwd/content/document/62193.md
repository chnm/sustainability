War Office
April 30 1798
Travelling forge complt.
to be Dld for the Garrison at
Fort Mifflin
War department
April 30th 1798
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to the order of Major Tousard for the use of the Garrison at Fort Mifflin One Travelling forge with all its tolls and implements complete.  
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
