These photos come from the Hurricane Katrina &amp; Rita Clearinghouse, and were contributed by Jay Edwards, John Pine, Patrick Hesp &amp; Barrett Kennedy.

This clearinghouse has been established at the Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge to 
facilitate the collection, dissemination, and archiving of data related to Hurricane Katrina and Rita.
 Katrina made landfall in Louisiana on Monday, August 29, 2005 and Rita made landfall on September 24, 2005.
