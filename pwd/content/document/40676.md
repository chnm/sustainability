May 19 - 1790
The old Emissions charged to Major Richard Call for which the specie pay due him was by a Letter from the late John Pierce Esqr PMGl of the 15 January 1787 directed to be retained on your hands being now accounted for by him there rem now remains no Objections to you paying the same
I am Sir Yr &c J. H
Jno Hopkins Esquire
