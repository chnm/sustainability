Letter from Secry of War.
A 13. November .17799
War Department 30 Novem, 1799

Sir

Pursuant to the "Act for the relief of the Refugees from the British provinces of Canada and Nova Scotia," I have the honor to transmit herewith, the claims and evidences of persons of that description, which have been received at this office.
I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
James McHenry
The Secretary of the Treasury
