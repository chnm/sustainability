Department of War
                                             Accountant's Office
                                                February 24th 1796

Nehemiah Freeman, Esqr.

Supply Pay Master

               West Point

                              Sir

                                    I have to acknowledge the receipt of your letter
of the 18th - Instant - together with the Receipt Roll of the Corps of A. & E. for the month of November, and Sundry other receipts which will be examined and the amount proper will be passed to your Credit, of which you will be informed. -

                            I am Sir (indecipherable) 
                                  William Simmons
