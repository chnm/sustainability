Accountants Office

January 6 1800


Caleb Swan Esqr.

P.M.G

Sir

I hand you herewith an Abstract of expenses incurred

by Captn. Amos Stoddard of the 2d. Reg.t of A & I for pursuing &

apprehending sundry Deserters, amounting to the sum of one

hundred thirty nine Dollars & 65/100, you will of course make

te necessary stoppages from their pay. I AM [ILLEGIBLE] w.s.
