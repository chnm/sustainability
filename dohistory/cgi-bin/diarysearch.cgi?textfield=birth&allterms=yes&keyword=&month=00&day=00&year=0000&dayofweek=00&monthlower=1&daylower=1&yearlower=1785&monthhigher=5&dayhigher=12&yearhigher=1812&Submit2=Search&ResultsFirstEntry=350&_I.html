 January 13, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benjamins Son. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy, Cold Day. Snowd at night. my Patients illness Came on, her women Calld again & Shee was Safe Deld at 7 Evn of a Son. I tarried all night. mr Benjamin is absent.   At mr Savages. Birth 1st. receivd 6/ July 10th.  
 

 

 January 24, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Herseys Dagt. XX.

Snowd ye morn. Capt Hersey Calld me to See his wife about Day. we got Safe over the river, the freshet rose & it Broke up. his wife Safe Deld at 7h Evng of a Dagt, all like to do well.   at Capt Herseys. Birth 2 nt. 
 

 

 January 31, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Seth Williamss Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Williams in travil & was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 7h 40m Evng. I tarried all night. Betsy is Some more Comfortable. mrs Williams & infant are Cleverly.   at Ditoes. Birth 3d. 
 

 

 February 7, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Eliab Shaws Dagt.

 Snowd. I was Calld Early ys morn to Eliab Shaws, his wife was Safe Dild of a daught at 1h pm & I returnd home. wrode there, & home, on Saml Braleys Slead. I Calld at my Sons, his Child is Better. Dolly is at mr Hamlins. I began a Cottne Stockin.   at Eliab Shaws. Birth 4th. April 29, 1795, receivd of Son Jona on Shaws acount, 3/.  
 

 

 February 8, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jasson Butlers Dagt. XX.

Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard went to Survey Land for mr Gilley. I was Calld at 5h Evn to See thee wife of Jason Butler at ye hook. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 5h 50m, and I tarried all night & nurst her. receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Butlers. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 February 19, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Sewals Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn. mrs Sewall was Deld of a fine Daugt at 12m past midnight & is Cleverly. I tarried thro ye night. mr Sewall gave me as a reward 1_ yd mode, 2 Skeins Silk, 1 oz thread, a paper & Card of pins. Dolly with her Brors & Sisters Sett out for winslow.   At Detoes. Birth 6th. Benn Porter Sleeps here. Sally goes to her Brors Weding.  
 

 

 February 25, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Josep Savages Dagt.

 Clear. mrs Savage was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 2h morn. I left her as Comfortable as Could be Expected at 6, and Came to my Sons. find his wife a little better. I tarried there this Day & night.   at [Ditoes & my Sons]. Birth 7th. 
 

 

 February 27, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wilm Swantons Son. XX.

Clear till Evn when it Snowd. I was Calld at 1h pm to See mrss Swanton who Deld at 7 of a Son, her Second Child. I went to See Capt Savage who is unwell. Calld allso to See mr Williamss Dagt who remains very weak. Came home about 11h Evng. I receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Swantons. Birth 8th.  
 

 

 March 4, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear but very Cold for march. Eph got 1 Load wood to mr Burtun, his Oxen pesterd him So he quitt work aflern. this is ye anniversary of Son Jonas Birth.   at home. Son Jon a is 31 years old this Day.  
 

 

 March 7, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Roland Smiths Son. XX.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. mrs Smith was Safe Deld at 6h pm of a fine Son & is as well as Could be Expected; her illness was very Severe. mr Smith Conducted me home at 9h Evng. he gave me 18/7 as a reward for my Service. mrs Cleark, Felps & Robinson were assistants at the Birth.   at Ditoes. Birth 9th. a first Born. Bror Moore Sett out for Oxford. I Sent a Letter to Sister Waters.  
 

 

 March 9, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Hardin Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard, my Self & Ephm attended worship foren. I Dind at Capt Meloys, was Calld out of meeting, in ye first prayer, to mrs Hardin. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 5. I was Conducted home by mr James Page, arivd at 10.   at Isaac Hardins. Birth 10th. mr James Page Sleeps here. fee recd may 20.  
 

 

 March 18, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. we are makeing Soap. mr Thads Snell was here, Gave me 7/4 as a reward for attending his wife in Child Birth Jany 14th, 1793. my son Jona was here, informs me his wife is unwell. I sent her Some medisin. mr Freeman here, had 3 Sheets, Dead Blanks, to Carry to mr Ballard. informs me he was well last first Day. I have been at home.   at home. heard fm mr Ballard. funeral John Badcks wife.  
 

 

 March 20, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Savages Son. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy aflern. I was Calld at 8h morn to See the wife of James Savage, Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 1h pm. I left her Comfortable & returnd homewd as far as my Son Jonas, arivd there at 7h Evn, tarried all night. my Dagt is a little more Comforlable than Shee was.   at James Savages. Birth 11th. mr Savg Sent me 2 Bushl Corn to my Sons.

 March 26, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinklys Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to mr Patridges to See Patty Mc neere who has ye Collic & fitts.  April 5, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Colo Sewalls Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Sew Calld us up at 1h ys morn. mrs Pollard & Voce Calld at 6h morn. mrs Sewall was Dilivd of a Dagt before they got inn & is Cleverly. mr Ballard Calld there & Dind. I Came from there at 2h pm. Calld at Son Pollards & Spent ye rest of ye aftern. arivd at home at 7, feel fatagud. Dolly tarries at Son Pollards ys night.   at Ditoes.  April 24, 1794, Thursday

X. Birth Natn Burgss Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard went to Varsalboro. I was Calld to See the wife of Nathan Burges who was Safe Deld (tho very ill indeed) at 11h Evn of a fine Son. I tarried all night. my patients are as well as Can be Expected, her husband is at Sea, if living.   at Nalhn Burgess. Birth 17th. a first Born. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 April 26, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kenneys Dagt. XX.

rainy. Doct Williams Dind here. mr Ballard is gone to mr Thomass, a Law Seut there between mr Peter Parker & Colo Sewall. I was Calld at 2h Evn to See James Kenneys wife who was in Labour, & was Delivd at 11h of a Dagt; her husband was absent. I tarried all night, had no Sleep.   at James Kenneys. Birth 18th. 
 

 

 May 5, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Waids Dagt.

 Cloudy. mr Beaman was here, Desird me to Go & See his Lady; left his hors for me to wride. I went & Dind with her. went to See mrs Carr, find her much More Comfortable; her husband is Some Better. I Calld to See mrs Fillebrown, Shee is Cleverly. I was at mr Fesandans Store, bot 4 lb Cottne @ 2/4, 1 pr Shoes 4/. at Jery Dmrs bot 2 yds Durant @ 2/9. Came back to mrs Beamans, was Calld by mr Waid to See his wife who was in Labour, & was Safe Deld at at [sic] 10h Evn of a Dagt. I tarried all night.   at mr Beamans & others at hook, from there to mr Waids. Birth 19th. we had a Colt from our mare ys Day.
