 November 10, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cool. Benn Dexter & his Sister Came here, Son Town also. mr Smyle Came to work. I was Calld to Thoms Sewalls to See his Lady.   at mr Sewalls. Son Town here.  
 

 

 November 12, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thos Sewalls Son. XX.

Clear. I was at mr Sewalls. his Lady Calld me up at 4 ys morn & Sent for her women. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son at half after Six Eving & I returnd home about 8. Left mother & Child Cleverly. find wido Williams & Polly Savage here. the wind Blod part of our Barn of yesterdy & Damaged many other Buildings in this Town. Killed some Swine.   at mr Sewalls. Sally Dexter went from here to winthrop. I receivd 6/ of mr Sewalls.  
 

 

 November 16, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Mary [Lord] Savages Dafter. XX.

Clear. I attended worship aftern & was Calld about the 6th hour Evn to See polly Savage in Travil. Shee was Delivrd at 8. mrs Williams & I Came back to our house 12 & 40m. William Haywood here & Supt.   at meeting. & at mr Savages.  
 

 

 November 20, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & rain. I went to mr Pollards to heare what was Laid before the Revd. Counsil Examine Eveidnces against Revd mr Foster. I was Calld to See Polly Savage who had a Pain in her Stomach & Side. w [sic] gave her Some Feaver fue Tea & Shee was Easier very Soone.   I was at mr Pollards & Savages.  
 

 

 November 21, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I attended at Counsil to hear the Evidences Examind. Margarett Fox gave a very Contirary Evidencd Concerning her working on ye Sabath from what Shee Did when Calld in the Cause of Capt Sewalls Defameing the Revd mr Foster.   I was at mr Pollard & at mr Westons. mr Benn Dexter & his Sister Sleep here.  
 

 

 November 23, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of Magr Sticknys Son & first Child. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy aft. I attended worship. Revd mr Brow Williams preacht foren from the first of John 2nt 6 verse [  ]. Revd mr mc Lain aft frm Jobe 34 & 31.32. the result of ye Counsel was red. I went home with mr Jackson, his Dagt Stickney was Delivd at ye 11h Evn of a Son.   at meeting & at mr Jacksons. Put mrs Stickney to Bed. receivd 12/ of ye Magr. Snowd.  
 

 

 November 24, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 I Came home from mr Jacksons. Left mgr Stickneys Lady & Son Cleverly. I went to mr Davises & Bougt a Cloak of mrs Anne, paid X 12/ X. there is 3/ Due X.   I Came from mr Jacksons. wido Wms Sleeps here.  
 

 

 November 27, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Asa Wilmss Child, a Dagt. XX.

Snow & haill & rain. Thanksgiving Day. I Did not attend worship. mrs Williams here. Parthena & Dolly went to mr Foots aftr we Dind. I was Calld to go to Asa Williamss wife in Travil at 9th hour Eving. I went as far as the turn of the road beyond the Bridg this Side mr Browns, mett a mesage Desireing I would return. mr Pitts acompanied me home, the rideing very bad. the girls went no farther than mr Bisbes.   at home till Eving then Calld by Asa Wilms.  
 

 

 December 2, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to Capt Savages to See mrs Cowen. a very windy Day.   I was at Capt Savages, went by water.  
 

 

 December 4, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth of Epm Cowens Dagtr. XX.

at Dt os. mrs Cowen ill, Calld her women. was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 8th h pm & is Cleverly. I tarried all night.   at Ditoes. Capt Nichols took Letters for Sist B & Hanh. 
 

 

 December 5, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I Came home, Crost on the ice. Calld at mr Westons, Colo Norths & mr Savages. mr Ballard is Gone to mr Jacksons.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 December 7, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth of William Wings junr Dagt & firs t Child. XX.

Clear, Cloudy & moderate. I was Calld to See the wife of William Wing at Day Break; found her very ill. Shee was Safe Deld about 10th h AM. Jessy Bullin attended me there & home as far as the meeting house. I find Polly Whealor very unwell.   I was at mr Wings. Calld to See wido Williams. I wrote to my Dat Hannah.  
 

 

 December 9, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd & raind. we were boiling Soap. mr Ballard went to meet Select Gent men. David Learned Sleeps here. Polly Whealer is very unwell. Doct Coney Came to see her, Left her Some remidies. wodwd Allin here to work, he Came yester Day. I receivd 4 lb Chocolat of Ephm Cowen for assisting his wife ys 4th inst.   I have [been] at home. Snowd.  
 

 

 December 15, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy foren, Clear aft. mr Ballard is unwell. Polly is not So well as Shee has been. Doct Coney Calld to See her. mrs Hamlin here to warp a web. the Girls been to wido Willimss. Charlotte here at Evng. I have been at home.   I was at home.  
 

 

 December 24, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. mr Ballard went to magr Goodins. Calld to See Polly Whealor. Shee is no Better, the infant is. I have been at home. Danl Bolton & his wife Dind here. we made Some mins Pies. there was a Sleigh over Sett a Cuming down the hill abov our house.   I have been at home. Stephen Dudly of Winthrp frz his hands this night.  
 

 

 December 29, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I have been at home. mr Ballard went to mr Pollards with Letters to Send by Doct Coney. Parthena is Some Easier. I have had a pain in my head. Birth Jos Browns Dagt.   at home.  
 

 

 December 31, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I went to mr Livermores to See Polly Whealor; find her very Low. Sett up with her. there I Closed ye year in asisting the Distresed. I was at Mr Livermores. Births in Hollowell in 1788 - 17 Sons & 25 Dagts. Deaths - males 7; Feemales 5.    
 

 

 January 6, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dolly & Clarrissa washt. mr Ballard went to Collect Rates. I was at Revd mr Fosters & mr Westons. Jona is unwell. Parthena is Better, Ephm also. I Calld at mr Daviss & left 3/ for mrss Anne Davis, it being all that was Due to her for a Cloak I had of her.   at Revd Fosters & other Neighbrs. 
 

 

 January 10, 1789, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. mr Ballard at Colo Norths Setling with the meeting hous Committee. I have been at home part of ye day. was Calld to See Nancy Savage aftn, Left her Some tinct of Alloes, 1-1/2 oz, prs 1/X.   Hannah Fletcher Deld of a Son 8 ins t. 
 

 

 January 11, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I was Calld to See Nancy Savage. She Continues to Puke & Purge. Shee is Cutting her Eye teeth. I was informd yt a Dagtr of Saml Goodins has Lately had a Child. Shee is unmarried.   I went to mr Savages.  
 

 

 February 1, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I have been at home. Saml Babcock had a Son born.   at home. Birth.  
 

 

 February 6, 1789, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Coutches Son. XX.

A bitter Cold morn. I rose at ye 4th H, was Calld at ye 6th to See mrs Coutch who was in travil & was Delivrd at ye 8th of a fine Son. her Second Child, both Sons. I returnd at ye 6th h Evn, left all well.   at mr Coutchs. 
 

 

 February 18, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Rubin Branards Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to mr Branards wife in Travil at ye 9th h morn. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 5 PM. I returnd at 8 Evn. Son & Dagt Town been to his Dadys.   at mr Branards, Winthp.  
 

 

 February 21, 1789, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Ezekl Pages Daftr. XX.

Clear & pleast. mr Hadg workt here half ye Day. mr Ballard returnd. Capt Belcher & Esqr Coney from Boston. I receivd a letter from Broth Edwd Davis informing of his wifes Death. I was Calld to See mrs Page in travil at 10h & 30mEvn. Shee was Deld of a Dagt at about 11, all Cleaverly. I tarried till morn.   at mr Pages. receivd 6/ for my Servis.  
 

 

 February 26, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of mr Jacob Chandlers Dagt, Born Dead. XX.

Clear. I am very unwell with a pain in my Neck & Shoulder. Jacob Chandler here, informd his wife has fitts & he wisht for me to go & See her. the young people Left here, went to Capt Danl Smiths. mr Pollard Calld me to his Dagt, Chandler, at 5h 30M Evng. we arivd at 7h 30m. She was Deld at ye 9th hour of a Dagt, Still Born, & Seems more Comfortable. I Sett up & tended her with her Marm. Shee Seemd revivd in ye morn.   at mr Chandlers.
