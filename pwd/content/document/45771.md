December 28th 1793
Major Isaac Craig 
Pittsburg
War department
No 14d
December 28 1793
Sir
I have received your letter of the 20 instant Mr. Hodgdon forwards you by this post the Sum of five thousand dollars for the purposes mentioned in your letter of the 13
I will write to the Secretary of the Treasury respecting supplying the Troops at Fort Franklin with Whiskey and transmit you his decision thereon
I am Sir your humble servt
H Knox Secy of War
Major Isaac Craig
