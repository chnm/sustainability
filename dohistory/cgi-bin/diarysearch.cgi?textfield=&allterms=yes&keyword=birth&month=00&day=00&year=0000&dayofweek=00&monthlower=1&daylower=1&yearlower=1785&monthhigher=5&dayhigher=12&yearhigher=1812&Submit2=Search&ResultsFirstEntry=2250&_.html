 January 10, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Heman Burr Conducted Rachel Town from mr Gills in a Sleigh. Shee has not been at home Since Shee went to meeting last Sabath day. I lold her I thought Shee used me very ill, Shee was very much ofended.   at home, had to remove my bed into the kitchen without any help.  
 

 

 January 13, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ys Day. I have been very unwell. Lydia Nudd Calld here. Son & Dagt Lambd Came in to See me, made me a prest of 9_ lb Beef and 1_ lb Butter. Son Town Came at Evng, made me a prest of a fine Goos. Doct Barton Came, he Sleeps here allso.   at home. friends here to See me. Cyrus Saw his fathir ys day, he is well.  
 

 

 January 14, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ys day. Doct Barton and Son Town left here after Break fast. I have done my hous work but feel very unable. Dagt Ballard Calld in to See me. I Sent mrs Getchel 5/3d on Rachel Towns acount which was all that was due from me to her.   at home.  
 

 

 January 21, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Death of Old mrs Fairfield.

Clear and very Cold. I have been at home. Cyrus went and Carried his Fathers Clean Cloaths, was informd of the Death of Jona Runels & the wife of John Bolton. may we all attend to ye Call, be ye allso ready.   at home. funeral of Jona Runels of Sydney.  
 

 

 January 28, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day, Snowd before night. Cyrus went to Carrie his Fathers Clean Cloaths. mrs Farewel Calld to See me. Heman Burr Sleeps here, informs me that Son Pollards Babe is unwell.   at home. mrs Farewel Calld to See me.  
 

 

 March 8, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear but windy. I have been at home, feel very unwell. Ephms wife assisterd me to do my hous work, her Marm & Sister Calld here. I roasted a Goos for Supper which Son Town gave me.   at home. Cookt A Goos which Son Town Gave me 13 th Jany.  
 

 

 March 17, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Part of the day. Son & Dagt Pollard and part of thier Childn here. Shee went on to See her Father. I went aftn Conducted by Lemuel Witham, a lad who is Com to work with Son Ephm. I returnd at Evn very unwell. Lemuel went to take Son Lambds hors and Sleigh to Joness for Jona and him to Come up in but not finding them ready to Come returnd with it. they Came up on foot and Jona Came here with out his hat. took him from his Supper, pusht him out adors, Drove him home to his hous Damning and pushing him down and Struck him. Shaw and Burr went on after to prevent his being deprived of life. I followed on falling as I went till meeting Dagt Lambard was assisted by her. I reacht his hous, find him Cursing and Swearing he would go and giv him a Hard whiping. my Dagt Lambd desired Heman to go and Conducted him to Ephm. he went and tarried all night. Son Lambd brot me home in his Sleigh. O that the God of all Mercy would forgiv him this and all other misconduct.   at Shubl Pittss, feel very unwel and what a Scean had I to go at Evng. may a Good God Support me. funeral mr James Hinkley of Hallowel, his Death was very Suden.  
 

 

 March 21, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

[Birth Wm Stones 6th son.]

Clear. I was Calld before morn to Wm Stones wife in Labour. Shee was Deld at 9h morn of her 6th son and 8th Child. I returnd aftern.   at Wm Stones. Birth 1st recvd 6/ may 10th. 
 

 

 March 27, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth David Edwardss & Lucy Shaws Dagt. XX.

Clear. Pheebe Church workt here. I paid her /9d. I was Calld at noon to go and See Lucy Shaw (who was at mr A. Brackets) in Labour and was Safe Deld about 2h pm of a Daught. Shee Declared that David Edwards was the True Father of the Child. I left her Comfortable and reacht home before SunSett, find Joanna Babcock here. Shee is Come to do our work.   at mr Anthonys Brackets to See Lucy Shaw. Birth 2nd. out of wedlock. mrs Trask Sleeps [here]. fee recd Decr. 4th.  
 

 

 March 30, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast part of the day, Snowd and raind at night. Son Jona, his wife [&] 3 Childn took Tea here. Son Ephm has Seen 25 years this day.   at home. Jona, his wife and Part of the Childn here. Son Ephms Birth day.  
 

 

 April 3, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jonas 5th Sone.

Clear. mrs Farewel Calld here, Shee was going to the Settlement. Dagt Ballard Sent for me aftern Shee being in Labour, and was Deld of a dead Son before Sun Sett; untimely birth. I tarried all night, mrss Farewel & Smith also.   at Son Jona. Birth 3d. premature.  
 

 

 April 8, 1804, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Lemuel and Jack Ballard went to meeting to ye uper parsh. Called to See Joseph Woodward, find him insane. I have been at home, Joanna went to Son Jonas.   at home.  
 

 

 April 9, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I have felt not So well as I Could wish. mrs Farewel Calld and took Tea with me. Son Ephm been down to ye Settlement, he Sleeps here this night.   at home. mrs Farewel here.  
 

 

 April 20, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. I workt in my Gardin part of ye day Cuting the old fence. Dagt Ballard Came here, mrs Smith Calld in on business with her. we Dind on a loin of roast Veal. mrss Andrews spint aftern here.   at home. Dagt Ballard, mrss Smith & mrss Ands here.  
 

 

 April 21, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, workt in my Gardin the most of the day. mrs Farewel Calld here, we [have] Brewd.   at home. mrs Farewl Calld here.  
 

 

 April 28, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, a rain Bow in ye west. Cloudy aftern, raind Some at Evn. I have finisht a pair hoes for Cyrus, began one for my husband, workt Some in my Gardin. Sally Farewel, mrss Badcock & Pierce Calld in to See me. Leml is gone to Sydni for Ephms Mare and Jonas OXen. mercy here to spin thread, Joanna to my Sons to twist yarn.   at home. the remains of the wife of John Pinkham interd this day.  
 

 

 May 3, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Cloudy aftern. I have Bakt flour Bread, Joa Brown and Brewd and went to the settlement, and got her a pair Shoes at Hamlins. Lathrop had a Call to the Settlement, Ephm went down. Lydia Hallowell took Tea here. Allin & Thomas & William Supt and Sleep here.   at home. allin and Thoms Lambd Sleep here.  
 

 

 May 7, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Clear aftern. my Dear husband attended me to mr Gills after Breakfast. he Came and took Tea. I tarried all night. mrss Gill was taken in Labour about the middle of ye night and assistanc was Calld in.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 May 8, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charls Gills 7 Child, 3d Dag t. XX.

Clear and very warm. my patient was Safely Deld of her 9th Child & 3d Dagt at 3h 30m ys morn, and is Cleverly. I tarried till mrs Conr Came to Nurs and Came home attended [by] Harnon Barton. Daugt Ballard Came and took Tea. Son Ephm & Prentiss to wk at the school hous, my husband allso.   at Ditoe. Dagt Ballard here to Tea. Birth 4th. recvd 1 Gln Gin [and] 1 Bushl Corn.  
 

 

 May 15, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 raind ys morn, Cloudy most of ye day. I have been Cuting Coverled raggs. Joanna Sewing them and doing hous work. Charlotte Brown Calld here. Son Ephm finisht his jobb of wk at the School house.   at home.  
 

 

 June 1, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I have been doing my Soap. Dagt Lambd and Rhoda Pollard took Tea here. Cyrus been to Hallowell for 3 Sheep. Joanna Did hous work and Pickt wool.   at home. Dagt Lambd Calld here.  
 

 

 June 2, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been Cleaning the Buttery, finisht my Soap. Sowd Some peas South of ye hous, dug Chips in the door yd &C. mrs Farewel Calld here. Lemuel is 15 years old ys day. Joanna Cleand hous. Cyrus & Nicholas Shaw Planted Potatoes & Yoaked the hoggs.   at home. mrs Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 June 9, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I workt in the door yd forenn, did my ironing aftern. Sally Farewel Calld here. I have felt very feeble ys day.   at home.  
 

 

 June 14, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Bakt white & Bn Bread and workt in my Gardin. [Isaac] Waters, Son of my Sister, Dind here. I was Calld at 10h Evn to see Son Ephms wife who was in Labour. I walkt there. Cyrus wend to Call Ephm to attend to his wives Sickness. he reacht mrs Farewels at 11, went and Conducted Jonas wife there.   at mrs Farewels at night. my Nephue Isaac Waters Dind here.  
 

 

 June 15, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Ephms Daugt. first Born. XX.

Clear and warm. Ephms wife was Safe Deld at 4h and 40m y morn of a Daugt, her first Child. it wd 6_ lb. Shee was very faint after delivery. I tarried with her till near 2h pm when I left her and inft Comfortable. was fatagued watking home, went on to the Bed and rested the remainder of ye aftern.   at mrs Farewels. Birth 5th. at 4h 40 m ys morn.
