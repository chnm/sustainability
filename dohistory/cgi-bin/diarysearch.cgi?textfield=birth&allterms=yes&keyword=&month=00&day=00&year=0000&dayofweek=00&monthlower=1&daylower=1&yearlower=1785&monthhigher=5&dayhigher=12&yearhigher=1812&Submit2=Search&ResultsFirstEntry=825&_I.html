 March 25, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Pierces Son, first born. XX.

Clear part of ye day. I was Calld after 1h Pm to See the wife of John Pierce who was in Labour. Shee was delivd about 2 of a Son, her first Born; her husband absent. Sister Barton & Son Jonas wife there but Shee was Delivd before they arivd. Sist & I got home at dusk.   at Capt Smilh. Birth 4th. Jany 19 recvd the [last] of my Due.  
 

 

 March 30, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth B. Shaws 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard Came to Son Jonas, took Breakfast and we Came home. left ye Childn better. I was Calld at 3h 30m pm to See B. Shaws wife East of ye Bridg. Shee was Deld of a Dagt, her Second Child, about 11 Evng. I tarried till morn.   at Ditoes & B. Shaws. Birth 5th. recd 9/ Cash as reward.  
 

 

 April 11, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Danl Woodwards 4th Son.

Clear and Spring like weather. I was Calld at 11h Am to go and See the wife of Daniel Woodward who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 1h Pm of 4th Son & 5th Child. I left her and infant Comfortable and reacht home about 3. I receivd 1s 6d in part of fee. Dagt Ballard and James here to Tea.   at Daniel Woodward. Birth 6th. recd 1/6d May 1st 1 Dolr & 20 cents.

X. Birth Lues Hamlins [3d] Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 7h morn to See Luis Hamlins wife, found her Deliverd of a fine Son by the help of mrs Pratt and Shubal Pitses wife [  ]. I attended public worship aftern. mr Hamlin Conducted me home, gave me 6S.   at mr Hamlns. Case 8th performd before I reacht there.  
 

 

 May 28, 1810, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Joshua Heaths 3d Son. XX.

Clear foren, Showers aftern. I was Calld at 2h ys morn to See Joshua Heaths Lady who was in Labour, Shee was Safe Deld at 3 of her 3d Son and 5th Child. Shee & infant are Cleverly. I was Conduted hom wd as far as Capt Smiths by mr Allby. I Calld in to See mrs Pierce, find her Breast is not well yet. I sorted Beens for Planting and did my other wk.   at mr Heaths. Birth 9th. receivd 6/ June 2nd 3/ S. 
 

 

 June 29, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Orocks Dagt. XX.

Clear and warm. I have washt Some wool, my Buttery and other matters. was Calld at 3h pm by mr Orock to See his Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 4 of a fine Dagt, her first Child by her present Husband. I left them as wel as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 6, did my work.   at mr Orrocks. Birth 10th. receivd 2 Dols as A reward.  
 

 

 July 5, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Enoch Rowels 3d Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn and Some rain. mr Rowel Calld on me at 6h 30m to to and See his wife in Labour. I found the Child born when I arivd. I finisht the performance. it is hir 3d Dagt and 4th Child. I returnd to Son Jonas, Dind there; mr Ballard allso. I came home, removd Beet Plants and did other matters.   at mr Rowel. Birth 11th. recd 9/ as a reward.

Birth Asa Pierces Son.

Clear. I was Calld by Asa Pierce at 8h morn to go and See his wife who was Safe Delivd at 5h pm of a Son. I left them Comforlable and reacht home after 9h Evng, felt fatagud.   at Asa Pierces. Birth 12th. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 August 15, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Martins 5th Dagt, 6th Child. XX.

Clear. I was Calld 30m after mid night by mr Martin of Hallowell to go and See his wife who is in Labour. I went, Shee was linguring litt 11h 30m Am when Shee was Safe Deld of her 6 Child & 5th Daugt. I went from her to See her Mother who apears to be near ye Close of life. God grant her Change may be happy. I returnd home at 10h Evng. I left my Patients very Comfortable.   at mr Martin Hallowell & at Isaac Clearks. Birth 13. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 September 8, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Howard [2nd] Dagt. XX.

Cloudy & some rain. mrs Howard was Safe Deld at 2h am of her 2nd Dagt and 5th Child. I returnd home at 10h 30m am, receivd 1 Dolr. left mother and inft Cleverly. performd a Considerable work. I Sent by Ephm Bond, got 2 qts N E Spirit and 3_ lb Sugar. Dagt Ballard in here aftn.   at Ditoes. Birth 14th. receivd 6s, 28 recd 3 & a gift of 10 Candles.  
 

 

 October 16, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. [Birth Hanh Getchels Son, & first born. XX.]

Clear. I was Calld to Son Jonas to See Hannah Getchel who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son which had life but the Smallist I ever Saw alive. Shee Says Wm Sands is the father. I Sett up all night, Polly Lankester with me. Sist Barton went to Bed, got some Sleep.   at Son Jonas. Birth 15th. Dagt Pollard Some Easier. receivd [9/] as rewd.  
 

 

 November 14, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ebenz Hovys 3d Child & 2nd Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld by Ebenz Hovy Some time before day to go and See his wife in Labour. I went on beyond Son Jonas, mett Joel Savage on the Same arand. my Patient was Deld before noon & Isaac Savages wife Came fm mr Savage who was Deld by mrs Carter and Comfortable. I left my patient Cleverly and [relurnd] home at 3h 30m pm. I [Calld] to See mrs Bracket who is very feeble.   at Ebenz Hovys. Birth 16th. receivd 9/ as a reward. Joel Savages Dagt Born at Son Jonas near night.

XXX. Birth Abm Devenports 2nd dagt & 5th Child. XXX.

Clear & Cold. mrs D. had her women Calld in ys morn and was Deld of a Daugt, her Second, & 5th Child at 5h 20m and is as Comfortable as Can be Expected. Clear & Cool. I was Calld to mr Rowels, his wife in Labour. I reacht there at 11h am. the Patient Deld of a dagt, her first born at 1h pm.  June 6, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Winsl Burdins Dagt by Fanny Hodges out of Wedlock. XX.

Clear morn, raind & Thundered before night. I was Calld Early ys morn to mr Hodges to see his Dagt Fanny who was in Labour & was Safe Delivered of a Dagt, her first born, which Shee in the Severety of her illness Dectared Winslow Burden was the Falher of. I tarried till 4h pm then left them Comfortable. Came to Capt Pittss, went in at mr Thwings, Came back to ye Capts & tarried all night.   at Ezra Hodges. Birth 7th. recvd 6/, 12-2/1 lb Tea.

 June 13, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Oroks 2nd Dagt by this wife. XX.

Clear & pleast. I was Calld at the riseing of the Sun by mr Orok to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 8h 30m of a Daught, her Second by him. I tarried till 5h pm & he Conducted me to Son Jonas. I walkt fm there home, find mr Ballard not So wel as I Could wish. I took Care of my piggs, fouls & Sett Squash plants.   at mr Oroks. Birth 9th. recd two Dollars reward.
