332

Joseph Howell Esq

Board of Treasury Jan 25th 1788

Sir

I am directed by the Board to transit to you a memorial of P P De Saas son of the late General De Haas, and to request the favour of you to report on the circumstance stated therein, and particularly in the nature and extent of the accounts referred to in the memorial.

I am with esteem your obed humble servt

Will Dun Sec y
