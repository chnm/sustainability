oseph Howell Esq

Comptroller Genl Officers Philadelphia December 10th 1787

Dr Sir

Inclosed are some more papers relative to the accts of Col Wood - he has a valuable woman his wife and his family are deserving they are in distress and whatsoever pension may be coming to him would relieve them - you have now all the papers you will ever get I wish you would make the gen statement you can and close his acct - I am often supplied to by them and would thank you for a line on this subject.

I am Dr Sir

M ob serv

Jno Nicholson
