Ella Gertrude Clanton Thomas (1834-1907) was born and lived her entire life near Augusta, Georgia. Her 13-volume diary is in the William Perkins Library, Duke University, Durham, N.C., and available on microfilm in Anne Firor Scott, ed., Southern Women and their Families in the 19th Century (Bethesda, Md.: University Publications of America), Series H, reels 27-29. Selections from her diary are in Virginia Ingraham Burr, ed., The Secret Eye: the Journal of Ella Gertrude Clanton Thomas, 1848-1889 (Chapel Hill: University of North Carolina Press, 1990).
