Elizabeth Town 16 jany 1785
Sir
I have to acknowledge the receipt of your Letter of the 23d of Decembr accompanying a part of the Jersey Line from No. 22.636 to 25,331 and from 556 is to 56.178 inclusive, and also yourLetter of the 6th instant with a coppy of the Regester of the said line from No. 8,109 to 9.702 inclusive, also a copy of the registers of Settlement's made by individual officers [undescripable] of the Jersey line Tos Howell Junr. Wm. Livingston
