If you have any questions or comments about DoHistory.org, would like permission to use anything on the site, or have any technical questions (such as broken links or pages not working), please send an e-mail to chnm@gmu.edu.

Please be aware that we may not be able to respond to your email, but it will be read. As a service to our funders and to know which audiences find this site useful, please provide a little information about yourself (whether you are a teacher, a student, or other professional). Your name and email address will not be sold or redistributed, and you will not receive unsolicited email. If you wish to remain anonymous, all fields are optional, and you can fill them in at your discretion.
