10 april 1800
Secry at War
War Department
10 april 1800
Sir
In pursuance of the Act for the relief of the refugees from the British provinces of Canada and Nova Scotia, I transmit the papers annexed; being the residue of all those received within the two years appropriated for the reception of claims under the said act.
I have the honor to be hdt servt
Sir
your most 
James McHenry
The Secretary of the Treasury
