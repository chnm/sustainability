Summary Information

Title: Congress of Racial Equality Records

Inclusive Dates: circa 1964-1970

Creator:

  • Congress of Racial Equality
  • Val Coleman
  • Antoine Perot
  • Ruth Turner


Quantity: 

1 archive box

Repository:

  • Nishani Frazier
  • Harambee City

Abstract:

The records of the Congress of Racial Equality primarily covers the black power era.  It includes multiple chapter information along with national office records.  The majority of the records date circa 1964-1970. Most images were obtained from Cleveland State University Archives Collection on CORE and School Segregation in Cleveland and from the special collections of the Cleveland Public Library.  Images are also available via Cleveland Memory Project at http://www.clevelandmemory.org/

 

HARAMBEE CITY digital archives and website is a creation of Dr. Nishani Frazier, Elias Tzoc, and Markus Wust. The website is currently hosted by the Roy Rosenzweig Center for History and New Media at George Mason University. This website and its content therein are copyrighted 2016.
