Richmond May 2d 1787
Thereby [undecipherable] I [undecipherable] Joseph Howell Esqr. to receive the Pay due my late Husband John Jones from the United States as a  [undecipherable] in the 4th Regt. of Artillery as [undecipherable] Return - also additional Pay as Waggoner Certificate of Qr. Mr General -
