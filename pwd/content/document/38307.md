New York October 26, 1786
to Oliver Wollcott, Esq

New York October 25th, 1786                                                                                                                                         

Sir, 
Agreeably to your request of the 19 th Inf. I have inclosed you a return of the names of the Men recruited by Capt.Samuel Granger and his Officers specifying the date of their several Inlistments excepting Thos Watson which does (where time of Inlistment) not appear on the original return.

    It is not in my power to inform you of the names of the Men enlisted by Lieut. Trowbridge as they are not specified in the return by the Account Current it appears that forty one of the the Men has been carried to his credit.

                                        I am with respect etc
                                         Yours

                                                  Jw. Pierce


Oliver Wollcott Jr. Esq
