Jackson, Thomas Tredwell
Col. Sheldon's Reg't &
A.D.C. to Maj. Gen. Lord Stirling
Rev. War 

William Simmons Esqr
Accountant
War department
Philadelphia
New York 13 January 
John Stagg Jr. 
recd 18th
New York, January 13th. 1798. 
Dear Sir,
The bearer is Thomas Tredwell Jackson; an officer of reputation in Sheldon's regiment of light dragoons, in the late war, and an Aid-de-Camp to Major General Lord Stirling.  He has some business to transact in the offices of the War department. Your official aid, if required, will render him essential service, and in so doing confer an obligation, on 
Dear Sir, 
Your sincere Friend. 
Jno Stagg junr. 
William Simmons Esqr
Accountant War department. 
P. S. Remember me to my friend Hagner, and the other gentlemen in your office.  J
