 September 18, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at Evng to Savage Boltons. I got some Sleep.   at Savage Boltons.  
 

 

 September 19, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Savage Boltons Dagt.

Clear. mrs Bolton Safe Deld at 3h Pm of her 5 Dagt and 11th Child. I left them Comfortable, Came to Son Pollards and Sleep there.   at Detoes. Birth 12th.  
 

 

 September 27, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Clear aftern. Sister Williams took Breakfast here. mr Ballard returnd from his Tour of Surveying at Davis Town. he went to attend Coart directly. mrs Farewel Calld here, I went to my Sons with her. Son Town Sleeps here, informs me Polly is better. the rest of his Famely are wel.   at Son Jonas. Son Town Sleeps here. mr Ballard returnd.  
 

 

 October 2, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the men to meeting. I sett out aftern, met Augustus Ballard, his wife and 2 Childn going to our hous. I returnd home. Son Jona, his wife and part of their Childn here to Tea. my dagt and I went to mr Danys at Evng. I Deld her 6/ which Genl Town Sent her as a present.   at Mr Danys. Augusts Ballard & wife here.  
 

 

 October 16, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Badcoks Son. XX.

Clear. Son Lambd Brot his wife & Babe here. I was Calld at Sun Sett to go and See the wife of Saml Badcock ye 3d. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son at 8h Evng. I tarried all night.   at Saml Badcoks. Birth 13th. recd 12/.  
 

 

 October 17, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I tarried with my Patient till aftern. Calld at Son Lambds. I reacht home at Sun Sett. my Colt is Lamed.   at Detoes.  
 

 

 October 25, 1803, Tuesday

X. Birth Pres Hamlins 3d Child. .

Clear. I went to Peres Hamlins, Put his wife to Bed with her 3d Child and first Dagt, left them Comfortable and returnd home at 3h Pm. recd [9/] as a rewd.   at Peres Hamlins. Birth 14th. recd 9/.  
 

 

 October 31, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Cottles 16th Child. X.

Clear and very windy. mr Cottle Calld me Early ys morn to See his wife. Shee was Safe deld at 4h pm of her 10th Daugt and 16th Child. I tarrid there all night. I receivd 9/ for a reward.   at mr Cottles. Birth 15th, a 16th Child.  
 

 

 November 1, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. I left My Patients as well as Could be Expected. Calld at Son Pollards and reacht home at 10h morn, did my hous work up.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 November 11, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I got Breakfast, was then Calld to See the wife of Jery Towl who is unwell; was there this day and night.   at Jery Towls.  
 

 

 November 12, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jery Towls 11th Child, a Dagt. XX.

Clear part of the day. my Patient was So ill as to have women Calld in aftr 5h morn. Shee Lingured till 45m pm when Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt, her 11th Child. it wd 9 lb _. I left her at about 2 and returnd home, find Some work to do; finisht it at 10h 40m Evng.   at Jery Towls. Birth 15th. Decr 10 th, Crdt by a pr Shoes.  
 

 

 November 22, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. mr Ballard over the river to Call for Parish taxes. I went to help mrs Dany quilt. Dagt Ballard & Sist, Betsy Ands, Joe & Betsy Smith were there. I fold Cloaths and ironed part of them at Evng.   at mr Danys. Son Jona Sent us a Goose.  
 

 

 December 2, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Part of the day and Colder. Son & Dagt Ballard, part of their Childn, mrs Getchel and Bhillip Bullin Dind here. Rachel & Joana Smith to Tea. mr Mathews here at Evng for pills for Lynda Gitchel, Shee is very ill, he says.   at home. P. Bullin here, he was Joind in wedlock yesterd with mrs Thustin.  
 

 

 December 5, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard workt at ye Shop till aftern when he Came in; is not very well. Rachel washt, I did ye hous work & knit Some. Polly Town is 22 years [old] ys day.   at home. Son Lambd Calld here. Polly Towns Birth day.  
 

 

 December 7, 1803, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast for ye Season. I have been at home, did hous work. we Bakt Broun bread and Pumkin pies. mrs Farewel Calld here. mr Ballard workt at ye Shop, he is not very well.   at home. mrs Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 December 17, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cloudy by turns. I have Done my hous work. Sally Farwel Calld here as Shee was going to her Marms. Son Ephm took Supper here then went to mrss Farewels.   at home.  
 

 

 December 20, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Whitons Dagt.

Clear and very Cool. Dagt Ballard here for Stay pieces. Polly & Sally Farewel Calld here as they were going down to ye Settlement ys Evng.   at home, did my work [          ] and knit 40 purls.  
 

 

 December 21, 1803, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 11h last night to go and See A mrs Whitton at Hallowel. I arivd about 1 this morn, find the patient Deld of her Child, a daugt. her 5 Child and 2nt by her Prest husband. Shee was not fully delivd. I performd what was remaining to be done and put her to bed Safe. I tarried all night. Mrss Freeman, Benn Whites and T. Stickneys wives were there had dresed the infant &C,&C. I Sett out for home the wrideing very bad. I Calld at the Revd mr Stones and warmd, and from there I reacht home at noon. Capt Toby Dind with us. I was very much fatagued indead.   at home till 11h Evn when Calld by A mr Whinton to Hallowell. Birth 16th.  
 

 

 December 24, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Francis Touls Son & first [Child. Birth 17 th. XX.]

Snowd last night and Some this day. Son Lambd brot his Sister Capin here foren. Shee and my Dagt Set out for home after 3h pm. I then washt mr Ballard & Cyruss Shirts. have Bakt ys day. was Calld by Francis Toul to go and See his wife at dusk, arivd at Son Pollards where they reside, at 6 Evn. Shee was Deld at 8 of a Son, her first born. I tarrie all night, Slept Some.   at home till Evn, then to F. Towls. mrs Capin been here. I made a pres of 3 pulits to Dolly Lambd.  
 

 

 December 25, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and misty. I left my Patient at 2h Pm, reacht home at 3, find I had been Calld for to go to Hallowell. Son Ephm Supt. Lathrop & Burr Came at Evng. Bur Sleeps here, they have finished their work for mr Voce.   at Son Pollards. Ephm finishd work for Voce las t night.  
 

 

 December 27, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I am very unwell. mr Waid here, Butchered two Swine for us and 1 for Jona. I have been very unwel and Old Lady Cypher Came and assisted us to do the work.   at home. we had our Swine Butcherd. I am very Sick. mrs Cypher helpt me.  
 

 

 December 29, 1803, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Cypher washt. I am So wel as to help her. mrs Farewel Calld here.   at home. mrs Cypher helpt me.  
 

 

 January 2, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard is gone to ye meddow. I feel very unwel. my husband Came home at 4h pm, took a little food. Complains of feeling the pain in his Stomach, but was Calld by John Sewall to answer an Execution of 800 Dollars. was by him Conducted to the Jail in augusta and Commited. our two Sons, Jona and Ephm were bound for the liberty of the yard for him. I pray the great Parent of the universe to Protect him and giv him Comfort in his present tryal.   at home, very unwel. my Husband was this Evng Committd to Jail on acount of the tax Collection.  
 

 

 January 3, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. Son Lambard Conducted his wife and Babe here, left them with me. they tarrie all night, Slept with me. I had a very ill turn in the night but, Blessed be the Great author of all my mercies, I got Easier. Son Ephm Came here ys Evng very ill with a Cold. I gave him herb Tea.   at home. Dagt Lambd So kind as to Com and See me. we made Sausages. Jonas wife Calld in to See me.  
 

 

 January 7, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I have felt So wel as to help do my work. Rachel washt the floors. mrs Farewel Calld here. I have had a Severe turn of Coughing ys Evng. Cyrus went to Carrie Clean Cloaths to his Father, found him well; he Dind with Judg Cony this day.   at home. mrs Farewel Calld to See me.
