July 
1786
War Office July 13th 1786
Sir.
The two companies were ordered
to the rapids of the Ohio by Major North
the first oppertunity occurring after the 
resolve of the 22nd of June, he left this city
on the 28th of June and is probably by this 
time at Fort Pitt, and perhaps at the 
Muskingham.
Colonel Harmar with nami the 
officer who shall command the detachment.
I requested to know at the office of 
the Secretary of Congress whether it was
expected that I should transmit the
resolve of Congress of the 30th of June to 
the Governor of Virginia and was
informed that it had been already
trans-
mitted from that Office. 
I have the honor to be
Sir
Your most obed Servant
HKnox
The Honble
Colonel Monroe
