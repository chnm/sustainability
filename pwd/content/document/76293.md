July 12 1800
I Certify that there is due to Barnard Hudson, Store Keeper at Fort Renslaer & it dependencies the sum of one hundred & seventy two Dollars being for his services from 1 Jany to 31 Decr 1799, which sum is to be paid to Abram. Bradley Attorney for said Barnard Hudson. 
W S 
Drs. 172.
The Secy of War
