June 21st 1791
General Birgoiyne
Lt Montgomary
of the 4th [Undecipherable]
Sir
I hav the honour to enclose a letter I have received from Lt Montgomery of the Kingston regiment.
What Mr Montgomery states of his having been employed upon the recruiting service is perfectly correct.
I have the honour to be with quick + resend
Sir
Your most Obedient + most Humble Sert:
J: Burgoyne

Hertford Shire
June 21: 1791
Rt Honble Sec.y of War +c +c +c
