Martha Ballard's Diary, December 31, 1795

31 5
X. Birth Saml Daviss Dagt. XX. 		Snowd. I was at Samuel Davises, his wife was Deld of [a] Dagt after a Linguring illness at 5h 30m morn. mrs Davis, his Mother, had fitts after her her Delivery. I allended in Each rooms and left all Cleverly. I receivd 7/6 of mr Davis, made a presint of 1/6 to the infant. Cyrus went and procured a Sleigh and Conducted Dagt Pollard and her Babes home. Birth 608th Since I Came to this Town on Oct 14th, 1777; all Since July 1778. at mr Davis s. Birth 51 st. old mrs s Davis had fitts. now this year is Closd and are we mended? if not, God grant we may be.
