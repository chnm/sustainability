Memorandum for Major Sutherland
Received 
from
State Dep't.
Nov. 24, 1894
R.P.O.O. 401310
Memorandum for Major Sutherland
To Inform Colonel Hodgsden
That the botes are not sufficient to carey the horses.  that we 
shall want three botes more and that I will endevour to 
Get Captain Shaylers botes or other botes at Muskingdum and
any others I can to make up, what will be enough to carey
the horses  if the can be spared until Colonel Hogden sends
Down other botes to replace them, Major Sutherland will
please inform Colonel Hodgsden how we are provided
with botes, and the Number Necessary for the Reception
of the horses, and the way I intend to take them
