Thomas Smith Esqr
Feby 27. 1787
Copied
New York Feby 26 1787

Sir
Inclosed you will received further continuance of my Register of certificates issued. I wrote you last week that the certificates offered for sale by Willson ^ who is was in your Goal is counterfeit. I presume that Mr Nowells deposition on the subject undecipherable line as he wants to go to [undecipherable: Hil?] & I shall wish for that reason his residence it may be accepted on the Loyal of this fellow.
Thos. Smith Esquire
