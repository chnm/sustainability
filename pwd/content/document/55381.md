Philadelphia 27. October 1796.
Sir
The above is an accurate statement for the equipment of a Dragoon – It far exceeds your Estimate, to account for this you will observe that several essential Articles of equipment is added –
[signed] Saml. Hodgdon
[to] Saml. Lewis

---
Dolls. 13, 551. –
Philadelphia 29 Nov. 1796.
Sir
The above imbraces all the articles inserted in your list; with the addition of Gun Carriages – perhaps Grape, Cannister and Bar Shot in suitable proportiens should be added, if so, on notice it shall be done – Sundry small articles as appendages to the above will be necessary, but their cost being inconsiderable, I thought it unnecessary to insert them.
your most obedient Servant,
[signed]Saml. Hodgdon
[to] Mr. Fox[?]
[With regards to the weight abbreviations transcribed on the 12lb shot, note that tons were at this time made up of 20 centals, or American hundredweight - hence the abbreviation of "cent".]
