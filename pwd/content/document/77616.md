War Department 23 Dec 1800
Sir
I am honored with your favor of yesterday.
The amount thought necessary for fortifying ports &
harbors has not heretofore been inserted in the annual
estimate of expenditures; it was therefore omitted in the
estimate for 1801.
I presume that the deficiency of past appropriations
is not equal to 100,000 Dollars, but the precise amount
cannot be known.  I think that to appropriate for that
object Two Hundred Thousand Dollars in the whole
would be sufficient for the ensuing years.
I have the Honor to be
Sir, Your obdt Serv.
Sam l Dexter
Hon. Mr. Griswold
