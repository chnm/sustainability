July 6 1799
I certify that there is due to David C. Claypoole,the sume of sixty
seven dollars & 3/100 being the amount of his account and that of the House
of David C R Septimus Claypoole for advertising deserters between 23 Jan 1798
and 16 May 1799
UR6 exp DR. 67.33          4288     W.S.
