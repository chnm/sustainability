 April 1, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinkleys 2nd Son & 3 Child. XX.

Cloudy. mr Ballard and Cyrus are gone to meeting. Cyrus Came home at noon, it raind. James Hinkley Came at 1h pm for me to go and See his wife who is in Labour. we arivd, find his mother and Ben Churches wife there. we Calld the mc Causland wives after Sun Sett & Patient was Deld at 7h Evn of a fine Son, her 3d Child, and is Cleverly. I tarried all night.   at James Hinkleys, New France. Birth 10th. recd 1_ bushl Corn the 4 inst. 
 

 

 April 23, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kennys Dagt. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. I have been very unwell but being Calld by mr James Kenny at 4h pm, I went to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld at 5h 30m of her Second Dagt & 4th Child. I left her and inft very Comfortable and arivd at home at 10h 30m Evn.   at mr James Kenneys. Birth 11th. receivd 6/ for my Service.  
 

 

 May 2, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ephm Burges 2nd Son. XX. Augst 27th 1799 receivd 3/7.

Cloudy and Cool. I was Calld this morn to See the wife of Ephm Burges who was in Labour. Shee was Deld about 8h morn of her Second Son and 3d Child. I lefe mother and infant Cleverly and returnd home between ten and Eleven. mr Burges paid me 6/ in part for my Service. I have Brewd a medisinal beere. mr Ballard is gone to Riedfield to Survey. Jonas hired man here helping Plough.   at Ephm Burges. Birth 12th. receivd 6/. I was Cald at Evng to See genl Sewalls wife whos Breast is Soar. I was there all night.  
 

 

 May 11, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thoms Kenneys Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrss Baxter here. I have been kniting. I was Calld at 8h 30m Evng to See the wife of Thoms Kenney who was in Labour and was Safe Deld at 10h 30m of a Dagt. I tarried all night, my Patient as well as Could be Expected. this is her 6th Child.   at Thoms Kenneys. Birth 13th. receivd 9/ July 11th by Son Cyrus.  
 

 

 June 3, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I attended public worship at the fort. Nabby Andrews at meeting. old mr Pollard Calld to See us and took Tea here. mr Pollard informd me of a woman up where he livs who has lately been Deld of two Childn, and there were 8 weaks between the Births.   at Meeting. mr Bond had prayrs on the Death of his mother. remarkable Birth of Twins.  
 

 

 June 12, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Rachl Savages Dagt. X.

Clear. mr Ballard went to Son Jon as. I was Calld to See Rachel Savage who was in Labour and was Deld at 6h pm of a Dead Child. I left her Cleverly and returnd home at 9h Evn.   At wido Savages. Birth 14, recd 6/.  
 

 

 June 14, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Lewis Hamlins Dagt & first Child. X.

Clear. I was Calld at 5h 30m to See the wife of Lewis Hamlin who was in Labour. She was Safe Deld at 4h 20m pm of a Dagt, which wd 8 lb 2/1. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected, at Sun Sett, and returnd home. found Polly Dingley and Polly Livermore here. mrs Dickmans Sister at George town interd this day. the wife of James Bridg was Deld this morn at 1h of a Son, it was Born dead and is to be interd ys Evn. Doct Page was Operator, poor unfortunate man in the practice. Capt Zimry Haywood Departed this life as he was walking from his Barn to his house, instantly by an Apoplecktic fit. may it teach us the uncertainty of Deaths aproach and inable to be allways ready. may God Sanctify the Dealings of his providence to us all, but more Especially to the Connections of the Decesed.   at Lewis Hamlins. Birth 15th. recd 12/ July 5th '99. Birth James Bridges Son, it was Born Dead. their first Born and intered this Evng. Capt Zimry Haywood Expired this Evng.  
 

 

 June 18, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wm Bells Son. XX.

Clear and very warm. I was Calld at [ ]th h morn by Wilm Bell to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 8h 30m Evng of a Son, her first born, and is very Comfortable. mrss Mc Master and Haywood were my assistants. I tarried all night.   at mr Bells. Birth 16th. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 June 20, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and hott fore noon. I went to mr Bells, find her more Comfortable. I was at Lewis Hamlins, his wife is Cleverly; at mr Crags, Pegy very low. I Calld at mr Heartfords, his wife Deld of two Dead Babes by Esqr Cony this morn, one of Each Sex.   at mr Bells and others. Birth. mrs Heartford twins, a Son & Dagt.  
 

 

 June 21, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of a Son of John V. Davis, born by Mehitable Peirc, her first Born. XX.

a rainy Day. I was Calld at the first hour to See mrs Mehettable Peirce who was in Labour, and was Safe Deld at 7h morn of a very fine Son which Shee Declared was begoton by John Varsal Davis Esqr. this Shee positively affirmd in the Extremity of her illness, in presenc of the wife of Jon a Ballard who with Hosea Houland were my assistants in the Case. the latter was Stept into an other room when I Examined my Patient. mrss Tetcomb was Calld but did not arive till after the Birth of the Child. mrss Burtun and the mr Father of the Child Came to See them aftern. I receivd 24/ as a reward and returnd home. mr Ballard went up and acompanied me home.   at Son Jonas. Birth 17th. I receivd 24/ as a reward.  
 

 

 June 28, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I have been to Hallowell at Divers Stores, was at Moses Parmers, receivd 9/ of his wife for assisting her in Child birth. I went to See mrss Bell aftern, find her and infant more Comfortable, mrs Loes Harris Nursing her. I Calld at Sons Pollards & Lambards. mr Pollard has been to his Fathers, they are well Except his Dady. he was at Son Towns, my Dagt is better.   at Hallowell and at mr Bells. at Son Pollards & Lambts. 
 

 

 July 5, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. my Dagt Seems a little Easier than Shee was Last night. I was Calld at 9h this morn to See mrss Lowell who was in Labour. Shee had been attended by Doct Page last night; he was Calld home at Sunrise to his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 11h foren of a Son. the Doct was Calld to my Patient and I returnd home at 4h pm. I receivd 9/ of mr Lowell. my Dagt Town has had a better day this day. Shee had but little feaver. mr Gill & Lambd took Tea with us.   at mr Lowells at Hallowell. find mrss Gill and Dagt Lambt & her Childn here. Birth Doct Pages Son.  
 

 

 July 7, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Kitridgs Dagt. XX.

Clear and warm. my Dagt Town Seemd Comfortable thro the day, had a feavour fitt at night. I was Calld at 9h morn to See mrss Kitridg who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 10h 30m of a Daugt, her Second Child. I left her and inft Cleverly and returnd home at 2h pm. I receivd 7/6 as a reward.   at mr Kitredges. Birth 18th. recd 7/6. Dagt Town had a feavor fitt at nigt. 
 

 

 July 15, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ansel Neys 2nd Son. XX.

Clear. I Did not feel very well this morn. mr Ballard, Cyrus & Watson went to meeting. Dagt Pollard wen Came here to See her Sister. Nabby went up to See her marm aftern. I was Calld to Ansel Neys, walkt as far as Saml Norcross, found the Patient in the hands of Doct Page. he gave the Case up to me and Shee was (after I removd obstructions) Safe Deld at 4h pm of a fine son. I left mother & Child Cleverly and walkt home, arivd at 8h Evn. I receivd 9/ as a reward. Doct Cony, mrss Haywood, Straten, Toby and Dagt Lambd & mrs Pitts here while I was absent. Dagt Lambard Sleeps with mrss Town ys night.   at Ansel Neys. Birth 19th. recd 9/4_ as a reward. my Dagt Town is [ ] as Comfortable as Shee has been for Some Days Past.  
 

 

 July 20, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Abel Chases Son and first Born.

Clear Except Showers. I was Calld at 1h 30m to go and See the wife of Abel Chase who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 5h 30m of A fine Son, her first Born. I left her and inft as well as Could be Expected and returnd home at 9h foren. my Dagt Town had a Severe fitt of Ague and feaver. mrs Dingley Came in and helpt me. mrss Livermore & Densmore here aftern. my Dagt had a faint turn at Evn. I gave her Caster and Shee revivd.   at Abel Chases. returnd at 9h m. my Dagt Town had a Shakeing fit, began at 10, and a Severe feaver fit after it. Birth 20th. 
 

 

 July 25, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Printer Robinsons Son & Second Child.

Clear. I was Calld at 4h ys morn to See mrss Robinson, wife to the printer. Shee was Deld of a fine Son before I arivd. Shee was alone at the Birth, her Neighbour women had got her to bed. I Drest the infant, took Breakfast and returnd. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Jonas. the wives of James & Thos Hinkley Spent the aftern here. we are informd that Son & Dagt Town went Past Levy Robinsons this morn.   at mr Robinsons. Birth 21st. two mrsss Hinkleys here.  
 

 

 August 6, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. our men went to work at Son Jonas. Meriah Grifin Came and washt for me. I Did my house work and washt the Bed rooms & Kitchen. this day is the Anniversary of my Daght Pollards Birth.   at home. Meriah Grifin washt for me. I paid her 2/ for her Service. we are informd Dagt Town was very Low las t tuesday.  
 

 

 August 19, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth mr Woods Son & first Born. XX.

Clear and very warm. mrss Wood was Safe Deld at 6h morn of a fine Son. I left her and inft Comfortable, and reacht home at 10. I went onto the Bed and Slept till aftern. Son and Dagt Lambard and Childn, Becky Sewall, mrss Dingley and mrs Eunice Stackpole here after meeting. I receivd 9/ of mrss Wood Sept 12, 1798 for my Service there this day.   at mr Woods. Birth 22nt. mrs Dingley informs me that Dagt Town remains much as Shee was.  
 

 

 August 21, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Browns Dagt & 11 Child. XX.

Clear Except a Shower at Evn. mrss Brown was Deld of a Dagt, her 11th Child, at 11h 30m. I left her and inft as well as Could be Expected and reacht home before Sun Sett. mr Brown Gave me 6/ Cash and a Chees, wd 7 lb, for my Service. his Son Natl Came for the hors, he Sleeps here this night.   at mr Browns. Birth 23d. I heard, at mr Thoms, that Dagt Town is no better.  
 

 

 September 2, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we all attended meeting. the Ordinance of the Supper was Administred. I went to mr Dingleys, Nancy is better. they informd me that Dagt Town has been more Comfortable ys Two Days, for which favr I would Bless the Great Author of all our mercies. Son Ephm and Jery Heartford took Tea with us. I was Calld about Sun sett to See the wife of Zyba Petingail who was in Labour, but not very ill. I tarried all night. had no Sleep.   at meeting. the Lords Spr administrd. I was at mr Dingleys & informd yt Dagt Town is revivd a little. was at mr Petingals. Son Ephm here. this is the aneversarey of the Birth of Dagt Lambard, 26 years Since.  
 

 

 September 4, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Zeba Petingls Dagt & first Born.

Cloudy morn. mrss Petingail was Deliverd of A fine Dagt at 1h 40m this morn. I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd at home at 4h and 20m, went to bed and Slept till 8. have been Since Doing matters about house.   at mr Petingls. Birth 24th. I receivd 12/ as a reward for my Service.  
 

 

 September 12, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Richd Fosters 2nd Son and 3d Child. XX.

Clear. mrss Foster was Safe Deld at the 12h of a fine Son her 3d Child, which wd 14 lb. I left her Cleverly at 3 and arivd at home at 7h Evng. I find that I have had 2 Calls Sinc I have ben gone, one by mr Robbins and one by mr Cleark. God Grant them Safe Deliverance.   at mr Fosters. Birth 25th. receivd 6/ Oct 21st of mr Fostr at Son Lambards.  
 

 

 October 19, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Carlows Dagt, his 11th Child. XX.

Clear and Pleasant. I was Calld at 9h morn by John Carlo to go and See his wife, Shee was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a Dagt, her 11th Child, at 8h 30m Evn. I receivd _ a kentle of Cod fish for my Service, and returnd home at 11 / OClok. left mother and infant as well as Could be Expected.   at John Carlows. Birth 26th. receivd half kentle Cod fish.  
 

 

 November 8, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Thwings Dagt.

 Cloudy. I have Brewd & Bakt, and was Calld after Sun Sett to mr Thwings to See his Lady who was in Labour, and was Deld of a Dagt at 8h Evn. I walkt to mr Thwings. he Saw mr Ballard returning from Son Towns, who informd him that our Dear Dagt Departed this life this Day. I returnd home about 10h Evn, when mr Ballard infmd me Shee Expired at 10h this morn. may God grand us all hearts to Submit to his will and to hear the Call, be allso ready.   at mr Thwings. Birth 27th. recd 6/ as rewd. Death of my Dagt Town, aged 40 years, 2 months and 11 days.
