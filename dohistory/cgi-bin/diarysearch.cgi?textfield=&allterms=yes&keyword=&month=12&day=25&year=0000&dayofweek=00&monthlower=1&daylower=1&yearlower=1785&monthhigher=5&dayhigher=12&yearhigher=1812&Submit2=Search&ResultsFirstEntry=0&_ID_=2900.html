 Clear & very pleasant for Christmas. the Ground Covered with Snow about two inches deep. I have been at home mending Ephms trousers & cuting Raggs for a Coverled.   I was at home.  
 

 

 December 25, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Cold. mr Cragg Calld me to See Polly mc kethney. Shee is no Better. mrs Voce Came and we Changd her linning. I find Dolly very ill at my return, her Blister very painfull. mr Putnam of 7 mile Brook took Break fast here.   at Does. Snowd all night.

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Esqr Lithgows Son. XX.

Snowd & was Cold. mrs Lithgow was very unwell all Day. her women were Calld in towards Evng & Shee was Safe Delivrd of a fine Son at 10 h Evn, and is Cleverly. mrs Pettee, Stackpool, Thomas & Collar taried all night.   at Does. Birth [36]th. I Sett up with my Patient, her first Born.

 December 25, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & remarkable pleast for Christmas. I went to Son Pollards, little Jona Ballard is unwell, the rest of their famelies are well. Dolly & Sally have washt, Scourd my puter & washt the Kitchen. I am informd that the wife of Richard Foster is very Sick of a feavour.   at Son Pollards. Jona Ballard is unwell. I began a mittn for Cyrus.

X. Birth [  ] Thwings Son. XX.

Clear and Cold. mr Thwing Sett out for mrs Eastman to take Care of his wife who Cept Chamber all day and was Deld at 5h pm of her Third Son. he arivd at 7 with her Nurs. I tarried all night, my patient Cleverly.   At mr Thwings. Birth 59th. Saml Norcss inft interd.
