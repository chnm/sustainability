Oct 5.99
J McHenry
Trenton 5 October. 1799
Sir.
Mr. Geo. Salmon of Baltimore, has
informed me in a letter the 2d inst.
that he remitted you fifteen hundred
dollars on account of my last install-
ment.  I inclose a check in your favour
for 387 50/100 which I believe will complete
the payment, and request you to
take the trouble to do what is necessary
in the case.
I am with regard Sir
Your most obdt
James McHenry
