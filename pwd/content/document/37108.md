Sirs                                Pay Office
                                    Philada 6th Jany 1785
I do myself the honor to transmit you a copy of the registers of the late line of New Jersey from No 8109 to 9,702 inclusive, also a copy of the Registers of Settlements made by Individual Officers of the Jersey line --
There  a number of men that belong'd to Col Spencer's Col. Lee Genl Hazins of the Invalid Regimt's that were Citizens of Jersey, whose ac-
counts have been liquidated, but for the want of the proper returns, I am not able for the present to ascertain who they are if this can be done you may rest assured the residue shall be forwarded those - now sent with the former include the whole line as they stood when the army was discharged: You will exceedingly oblige me by a line acknowledging the receipt of the registers. 

His Excellency 
Wm. Livingston
Esq.
