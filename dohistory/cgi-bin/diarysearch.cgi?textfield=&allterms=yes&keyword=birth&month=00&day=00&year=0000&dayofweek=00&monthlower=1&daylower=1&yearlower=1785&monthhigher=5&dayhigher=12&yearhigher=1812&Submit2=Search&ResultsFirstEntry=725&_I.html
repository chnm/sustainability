 October 18, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mr Ballard & Hodges went to Pitts town for ye Boat, got her up as far as here. William Haywood & Moses Pollard Dind here. Hannah washt, Dolly weaving. I have been at home. mr Weston Calld here. mrs Sherburns Infant was interd.   at home. funeral of mrs Sherburns inft.  
 

 

 October 23, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd & raind. I was Calld to See Sally Peirce at 9 h morn, the rideing very Bad. Doct Colman Calld me from there aft noon to See his Lady. I was Calld from there to Sally before morn.   at mr Peirces. A Snow Storm.  
 

 

 October 24, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Sally Peirces Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from ye Docts to Sally, Shee was Safe Deld at 1 h pm of a fine Son. her illness very Severe but I left her Cleverly & returnd to ye Docts about Sun Sett. Sally Declard that my Son Jona was the father of her Child.   at Doct Colmans & mr Peirces. Birth 27th. 
 

 

 October 25, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. I was Calld from the Docts to See mrs Tubs, shee removd to Thoms Hinkleys to be Sick, was very unwell thro ye Day. I was Calld at 11 h pm to See mrs Wesson.   at mr Tubs & Westons.  
 

 

 October 26, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Tubss Dagt.

 Clear. I was Calld from mrs Weston to mrs Tubs (at 2h 30m Morn) who was Safe Deld at 10h 03m of a Dagt. her Labour Severe but I left her Cleverly at 12h & returnd to mr Westons.   at Dito. Birth 28th. 
 

 

 October 27, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Pleast. I was Calld from mr Westons to See mrs Childs, find her very unwell. I Calld at mr Hamlins & returnd to mr Wessons. mrs Johnson went to Capt P. Browns.   at mr Wessons & Childs. 
 

 

 October 30, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Doct Colmans Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn, Snowd before noon. I was Calld from mr Wessons to Doct Colmans at 3h. morn, his Lady Safe Deld of a fine Dagt at 11h. 40m. mrs Dummer and Sewall attended her with me.   at Ditoes & Doct Colmns. Birth 29th. receivd 9/8.  
 

 

 October 31, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rain. I returnd home by Water, am informd that mrs Goodin Departed this life 29th inst. Shee has left an Husband & 7 Childn to bewail the loss, Parents, Brors & Sisters allso. may God giv thim the Sanctyfying Crace to mourn aright. I was Calld to mr Westons at 5h pm.   at the Docts & at home & mr Wessons.  
 

 

 November 2, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & a Chilly air. mr Wesson Calld me from the hook last Evng. I went to see [his] Child.   at mr Wessons.  
 

 

 November 9, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Wessons Son. XX.

Clear & Cool. I was Calld at 3 h morn to See mrs Wesson, Shee was Safe Deld at 4h. 30m. of a fine Son. mrs Pollard, Duttun & Hamlin assisted. Shee and Child are Cleverly. I recievd 12/ as reward. mr Ballard Sett out for to Explore the Country Back of fort Hallifax, inployd by this Common wealth. I tarried at mr Westons, mrs Fillebn & Sally Floid allso.   at mr Wessons. Birth 30th. at [mr Wesss]. a rainy night.  
 

 

 November 12, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day. mrs Densmore Sent for me to See her Infant, it has a Cough & Soar mouth. her Childn have ye Cough. I Calld to See mr Livermr, he is better. I receivd a line from Dagt Town of 8th which informs She & famely are well.   at Ditoes & mrs Livermors. I receivd a Letter from Dagt Town.  
 

 

 November 14, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & moderate. mr Dany Calld me Early this morn to See his youngest Child who is Burnt on his face. I went & made an ointment & Dresst it. I went to See mrs Selvester, find her Cleverly. I Calld at [mr] Parmers, Bot a pail, paid 1/6 off John Badcok, Thos Hinkley & Benn White. I receivd of each 6/ as a reward for assisting their wives in Child [Birth]. I was at mr Densmores, his Childn unwell.   at mr Danys & other Neigr s. I Spent Evng at mr Densmors.  
 

 

 November 16, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I began to make Candles & was Calld to See mrs Wesson, I find her as well as Could be Expected, but of the mind Shee Can not take Care of her inft at home. a Stupud afair, I think, but She must Do as Shee pleases.   at mr Wessons. walkt home, was much fatgd. 
 

 

 November 20, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mr Brdwl Livermore Calld on me this morn, favrs me with Carrying a Letter to Doct Waters. I went to See mr Densmores famely, the inft is very ill; the other Childn much as they have been. I Came home, Supt & went back & watcht. Elephelet Robbins Came there for me to go to mr Pollards, Betsy Heuston being very Sick, but I Could not go.   at mr [Densmors].  
 

 

 November 24, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Towns [6]th Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. my Dagt was Seisd with her Labour, her women were Calld and Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 10 h Evn. I Sett up with her thro ye night. Shee was faint when we put her in Bed, but revivd & is Cleverly. this is her 8th Child. a rainy night.   at Son Towns. Birth 31st. 
 

 

 November 25, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn, Cleard off aftn. the Ladies went away. my Dagt is Cleverly.   at Does.  
 

 

 November 26, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mrs Haywood and her Dagt Stratten to See my Dagt this morn. Shee is Cleverly.   at Does. death of mr Hewens infant.  
 

 

 November 27, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. my Son & I Sett out to Come Down, Calld at Capt Grants. Shee Came home from Charls Jacksons, informd me that his Dagt Jane was Deld of a Son at 10h ys morn. we Came to mr Pollards ferry. I was Conducted over, my Son turnd back for home. I tarred at mr Pollards & watcht with Betsy Heuston, who is near the Close of life. mr Voce Sett with me.   I Came from Winslo to mr Pollard, watcht with Betsy Heustn. Funeral of mr Hewens infant.  
 

 

 November 28, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Webbers Dagt. XX.

Clear & Cold morn. I was Calld by mr Webber of Pittstn to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld at 1h pm of a Dagt & is as well as Can be Expected. her Childn have the Cough. my Son Towns Childn have it allso.   at mr Webbers, pitts ton. Birth 32nt. Betsy Hueston Expird at 6h Evn.  
 

 

 November 29, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. I Sett out for home, accompanied by mr Webber & Dore, the wrideing very bad. I Calld to See mrs Colman, Shee is Cleverly. I arivd at home a 1h pm. mrs Barril gave me 6/ Capt Norcross Lady Sent me for assisting her in travil April 9th 1791. mrs Welch here ys day to warp a webb. Jona was out on a rafft last thurs Day night in a very Severe Storm. we are informd that mr David Jackson of this Town was unfortunately Drowned between here & Boston on ye 18th inst, his Vessel & Cargo lost.   at Ditoes. mr Webber bestowd 8/8 for my Service.  
 

 

 November 30, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear, Serene morn, over Cast day. mr Ballard, my Self & Jona attended Funeral of Betsy Heuston who Expird at her Uncle Pollards. Shee was interd at 4h pm. I Calld to See mr Densmores Childn, find them a little more Comfortable. my Dagt Hannah is very unwell ys Evn. I gave her Some Cammomile & Camphr. Shee Batht her feet & went into a warm Bed. I hope it will relieve her.   I attended funeral of Betsy Heuston.  
 

 

 December 6, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Bradfords Son. XX. receivd 6/ by my Son Cyrus, Sept 30 1792 of mr Bradfd.

Snowd all day. I was Calld by mr Bradford of Pitts ton at 7h morn. I wrode our hors as far as the hook Point. Ephm went to take her home. I then prosceeded by water, arivd at 9h, found the Lady Safe Deld (by Doct Parker) of a very fine Son, her Second Child; both Sons. I was Conducted by Nath Norcross (of Littleboro) by water as far as mr Selvesters. Calld there and warmd, and walkt from there home. I was wet & much fatagud indeed. I arivd at home at 7h Evn.   at mr Bradfords of Pitts ton. Snowd. 33nd Birth.  
 

 

 December 8, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd Some. mr Ballard went to help Cyrus Dress his mill. Clear at Evn. I was Calld at 9h Evn to see mrs Walker. the ice run but we got Safe over. I wrode from Mr Devenports Landing.   at mr Walkers.  
 

 

 December 9, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Walkers Son. XX.

Clear morn, Snowd before noon, Clear aftn. mrs Walkers was Safe Deld of a Son whichwd 10-1/4 lb. I Came from there at 11h & arivd Safe at home at 1 h pm, find that mrs Pitts had Sent for me. I laid Down & Slept, had a Severe fit of Cramp.   at Does. Birth 34th. fee receivd Feby [25] 1792.  
 

 

 December 12, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I left mr Joness at noon. Calld at mr Wessons, Dind there, find her weak. Shee has not walkt her room any Since Shee was put to bed. her infant is at mr Hewens. I Calld at mr Densmores, their Children are giting better. Esqr Lithgo here, requested of me to go & See his Lady next weak. we had a very rainy night.   at Ditoes & mr Wessons & Densmors.
