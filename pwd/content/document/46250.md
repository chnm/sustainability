W.D. Accountants Office
March 31, 1794


Sirs

It is the desire of the Secretary

for the Department of War, that it should be promulgated to the Citizens of Kentucky the promptitude of the Government to re- ward them for their services.

I therefore request you to insert in your paper the inclosed adver= tisement to be continued reqularly for the term of Eight Weeks _ the expense I will discharge on your draft ~

I am Sir etc
Joseph Howell

Mr. John Bradford Printer - Lexington

Kentucky
