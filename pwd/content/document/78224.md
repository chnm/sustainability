No. 22
Voucher
Wood for Major Toussard
26 25/100 Drs
Sundry Persons
We the subscribers do each of us acknowledge to have
received from John Wilkins/ Quarter Master General /
James Miller Agt the sums opposite our names for the 
several loads of Wood bought by order of the Secretary
of War for the use of Major Lewis Tousard appointed
to superintend the formation of a Code of Exercises
manoeuvres and Regulations for the Artillery Corps
of the United States  Philada Feby 7. 1800
One cord hickory wood Eight Dollars   William Harlan his mark
One cord oak wood Six Dollars  Enoch Abrams
One cord oak wood Six Dollars  Wm Litzenberg
One cord oak wood Six 25/100 Dollars  Phineas Roberts his mark
__________
26 25/100 Drs
Witness  Edgar Patterson [illegible] [illegible] 6 Feby
Major L. Toussards
Receipts for Four Cords
Wood  Feby 7, 1800
Received Philda February 7th 1800 from
John Wilkins Jr. Quarter Master General James
Miller Agt  Four Cords of Wood agreeably
to the Secretary at War Letter dated 6t Inst
Having signed duplicates
a Subordinate Receipt for the receipts  Lewis Toussard Major
contained in Voucher 615 of Art &
Engineers
