Accountants Office
January 2nd 1796.
I certify that there is due to Francis Mentges Inspector of the Troops and Garrisons of the United States the sum of Sixty two dollars for his pay from the 1st to the a 31st of December 1795. inc- William Simmons Dr 02-
The Secry of War-
