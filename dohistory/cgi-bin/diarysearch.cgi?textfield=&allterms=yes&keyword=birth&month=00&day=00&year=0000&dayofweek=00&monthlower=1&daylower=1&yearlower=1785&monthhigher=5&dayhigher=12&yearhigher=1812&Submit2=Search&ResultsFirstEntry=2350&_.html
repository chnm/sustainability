 May 20, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I am still very ill. Son Ephm here, mrs Pierce & mrs Cleark allso. Dagt Ballard went to mr Pierces aftern. Polly made me some grewel.   at home, very ill indead all day.  
 

 

 May 23, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. I have been very unwel, my throat is very Soar. Doct Howard was Calld to see Sally. Shee Seems more Comfortable. we Dind on roast Veal.   at home, feel very unwel.  
 

 

 May 29, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

my Husband Liberated ys Day.

Clear. this is Election Day. my Husband was Sett at Liberty this day. he went to Son Lambards, finds William very Sick. he came for me, we both went and tarry there all night. ye Babe very ill.   at Ditoes, mr Patridges & Son Lambds. 
 

 

 June 1, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Came and took Breakfast at Son Lambds. I Set out for home, went to Son Ephms, look my Cloaths. Calld to See mr Craggs Child. at C. Voces, Bot 1yd black Durant. Calld to See mrs Thwing, Shee is better.   at Ditoes and others.  
 

 

 June 12, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Josiah Thomsons 4th Dagt.

Clear. mr Thomson Calld [m] to See his wife who was in Labour. I reacht there about 8h m, Shee was Safe Deld at noon of her 4 Dagt & 5 Child. I left them Comfortable & returnd home at 3h Pm.   at mr Thomsons. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 June 23, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have felt unwell, did not attend worship, my husband did. mrs Getchel Calld here on her way home.   at home. Marriage mr Stackp to M. Babcock.  
 

 

 July 4, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. mr Ballard been to thee Crotch of ye roads to hear an Oration Deliverd. returnd to Jonses, walkt in procescion with ye Federals. he returnd home at 10h Evn. Dagt Ballard & I took Tea at mr Pierces.   at mr Pierces.  
 

 

 July 5, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Son Ephms wife Dind here. mr Ballard and I went to Lecture. I went home with Dagt Lambd, her Babe is very much aflicted with a humer. I Calld at Capt Williams.   at Lectur & Son Lambds.  
 

 

 July 18, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. Son Lambd Calld me at 3h Pm to go and See his Daugt Lucy who is very Sick.   at Son Lambd, mr Ballard Sleeps there.  
 

 

 July 20, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we Sett out from Son L., my husband and I on hors Back. Son & Dagt, Allin, Barny, Dolly and Sally Pollard walkt. the hors I was on Started a little below Zibe Petingails & threw me, Son Lambd ran to my assistance. I mounted the other hors and we went to mr Gills to attend ye Funeral of his Child. revd mr Stone made a fine Prayer on the ocation. I Could not prosceed to the grave but tarried till they returnd and then went to Son Lambds. Lucy is unwell yet. I had a Distrest night. Old mrs Savage took Care of Lucy.   at Son Lambds & at the Funeral of mr Gills Dag t Betsy.  
 

 

 July 25, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard & I gatherd Cherrys. mr B. went down to the settlement with 15 qts. Sold 7 qts & gave the others to mrs Thwing & Pitts. mrss Conry & Gill here ys aftern. Sally Farewel, 2 of mr Eads Daughts and 3 of his Sons Calld in.   at home. mrs Conry & Gill here.  
 

 

 August 2, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Showers. Sist Barton and I went to Son Ephms. I left her there and returnd home at Evng. we Calld at Peris, Hantons & Amos Patridges. I Calld at Shubl Pittss, bot Candles and Snuff.   at Son Ephms. Sist Barton with me.  
 

 

 August 12, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I have Done my washing. Was Calld at 5h 30m Pm to go and See the wife of mr James Caton who was in Labour. we went to Bed. I Slept a little.   at mr J. Catons. June 9th receivd 1 Bushel Wheat.  
 

 

 August 13, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Catons 9th Child 4th Son. XX.

Clear. my Patient was Deld of her 4th Son and 9th Child at 4h morn. I left them as wel as Could be Expected and Came to Son Pollards at 9. he and wife are not So wel as we Could wish. I reacht home at 11, Old mrs Dunpha with me. I rensed and hung out my Cloaths, gathered mustard &C.   at mr Catons [&] Son Pollard. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 August 19, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Bakt and done other work, was Calld in hast to mr Cyphers to See Old mrs Pollard who fell from her hors and was Carried in there. Doct Winship Came and lett blood. Shee was put into a Shais, Conducted to mr Blacks. I went & tarried all night.   at mr Cyphers & Blacks. mrs Pollard fell from her hors, hurt her much.  
 

 

 September 1, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard and I attended Divine Service, it is Communion day. Revd mr Stone Discoarst forenn from Thesns V C 7 & 8 Vs. mr Grey Sleeps here. he maid a Prayer in my Sons famely. Nabby been to Son Pollards.   at meeting. Communion. this is the aneversary of Son Lambards Birth.  
 

 

 September 4, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I finisht my wash, was Calld to See Wilm Lambd who is very Sick. he has ye Chickin Pox Rash & Canker. mr Ballard Came there & tarried all night.   at Son Lambds. William Sick.  
 

 

 September 7, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am with my Childn. the Child is much better. my Dagt quite fatagued. Allin returnd from Brunsick, arivd at home between 3 & 4h Pm. mr Lambd Conducted me home. Parson Grey Calld here.   at Ditoes. Allin returnd from Brunswick. Wm is Better. I Came home.  
 

 

 September 11, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Capt Nichols here.

Clear and warm. Jonas wife Calld me A liar &C. may God forgiv her. it would make a heathen Blush to read the whole Shee Said if I Should write it.   at home. Cyrus is 49 years old ys day.  
 

 

 September 20, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Cleand my Stares & entry and Sellar and done my hous work. my husband workt for Son Jona. mr Perrum Calld to See him.   at home. mr Crosby of Belgrade Drowned ys day.  
 

 

 October 1, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Coart. I have Done various Sorts of work, feel Some fatagued. mrs Wiman, Nason & Pierce have Calld in here.   at home. Cyrus brol me Oinions fm Sn Lambds.  
 

 

 October 8, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day. mr Ballard bn to Coart. the Caus between James Howard and the magr Saml H. was Calld this day. I have Done Some washing. mrs Wiman here to Bake.   at home. mrs Wiman Bakt here.  
 

 

 October 21, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sally Pierce and mrs Wiman here to Bake. two of mr Puringtons Dagts Calld here at Evng. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 November 3, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Ephms 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld between 12 & 1 O Clock ys morn by my Son Ephm to go to See his wife. Shee was Deld of her Second Child & Daugt belween 4 & 5 and is Cleverly. I went to meeting foren, it is Communion. the babe wd 9lbs _. to Dagt Lambd tarried there foren.   at Son Ephms and at meeting, the Communion. Birth 6th. recvd as a Present a Gound Patrn. 
 

 

 November 10, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd, raind & haild. mr Ballard to meeting. Cyrus & I have been at home. Ephms wife Cleverly.   at home.
