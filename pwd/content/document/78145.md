Memorandum for Doct. Johnston
Call upon Capt. Howell in New York for
a certified copy of three recei0pts give by Capt
Abraham Smith for Maj. Dunlaps pay in the
6 Pennsylvania regiment 1776. Vz. one rec.t for
2 months pay and one rect for one months pay
received from Colo. Trubull- also one other
rec.t to Mase & Colwell for Two Thousand doll.
in which it is presumed the remendor of
Dunlaps pay, was included. I would wish
Capt. Howal to mention particularly as he did
to me that I cannot receive any pay from
the public until the last mentioned rect is
settled by Abram Smith, then Capt of the
Regiment - and who had the settling of
the acct
Ja. Dunlap
