[aligned right] Office of Army of Accounts June 28.th 1786

Sir

Your Letter of the 22.d is arrived. I am much Obliged for the information Contained in it and hope when you have the Opportunity that you may be able to furnish me with with more the advances by Meace & Caldwell to the Officers of the Delaware Regiment as far as I am furnished [aligned right] is 
63

I am Sir PJ
