Elizabeth Town March 16th. 1785

 Sir
       I have only time to acquaint you by request of
 my father Gen.l Dayton that in the settlement he made
 with you in Philadelphia you made a mistake in the
 delivery of the notes.  You will find on consulting the Con=
 =tinental & New Jersey scales of Depreciation that there
 was no Depreciation allowed at the time you dated
 his notes by those scales when you have actually compu-
 =ted it at about 2 for one. You would have been inform
         /\
 =ed of it before this, had not General Dayton expected
 for a long past that Col. Ogden would go to Philad.a
 and among other things settle this.

 The settlement was had}      Jon.a Dayton
     Septem.r 17.1784
     Jo.s Howell Jun.r
