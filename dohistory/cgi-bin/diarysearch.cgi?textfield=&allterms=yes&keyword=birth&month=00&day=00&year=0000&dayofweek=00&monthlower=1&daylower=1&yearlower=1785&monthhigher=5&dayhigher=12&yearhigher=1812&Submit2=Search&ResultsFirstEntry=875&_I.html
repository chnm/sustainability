 December 29, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Porters Dagt. XX.

Clear & pleasant. mrs Porter was Safe Deld at 1h morn of a Dagt. I left her at 9, as well as Could be Expected, & returnd home without attendance or Company. Calld at mr Pages & Son Pollards, Son Jona and his wife & Son there. Patty at her uncles. I lost a Larg hand kercf as I was on my way to washington.   at Dtoes. Birth 40th. 
 

 

 January 13, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Thwing was here, Desird me to go & visit his Lady. I wrode up aftern, find her not very well. I Came from there a Sun Sett. Calld at Son Pollards & took Tea, arivd at home at 8h Evn.   at mr Thwings.  
 

 

 January 14, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Thwings Son. XX. Birth mr Suells Son. X.

Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to Town meeting. I Bakt & Brewd & was Calld to See mrs Thwing at 6h Evn. Shee was Deld at 10 off her first Born, a Son. I was waited for by mr Brown to attend mrs Suell who we arivd there at 11 30m. Shee was Deld off a Son at 12. I tarried there thro the night. my Patient is Cleverly. receivd 7/4 march 18 1794.   at mr Thwings & Suells. Birth 1 st. Birth 2nd. receivd 6/ of mr Thwing.  
 

 

 January 15, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I left mrs Suell Cleverly at 8h morn. went to mr Thwings, find her & inft Cleverly, he went after her marm. I tarried till 3 pm when my Son Cyrus Came & Conducted me home, find Dagt Ballard & her Son & Lucy Town here; her Daddy & mrs Barton Came here yester Evn, are [doing] Business with Judg Boman. Son Jona Came at Evn.   at mr Suells & Thwings.  
 

 

 January 16, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn. Jona & his wife went home. Georg Brown Came here at Evn. I was Calld to Shubal Henkleys, Jona Came there for me. returnd home without me. I was with my Patient all night.   at mr Hinkleys.  
 

 

 January 17, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Shubl Hinkleys Son. XX.

Snowd. I was at mr Hinkleys. Son Jon a Came again at Evn, they Could not Dismiss me. mrs Hinkley was Deld of a fine Son at half after 11h Evn, and I was Conducted to my Sons. receivd 6/ as a reward from mr Hinkley.   at Does. Birth 3d. this Birth was on ye 18 at 1 or 2 in the morn.  
 

 

 January 21, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at my Sons. Isaac Hardin Calld me to See his wife at 9h Evn.   at Does.  
 

 

 January 22, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Bth Hardins Son. XX.

at Hardins, his wife Deld of a Son at 3h morn & I returnd to my Sons at 8h morn, find Sally tolerable. mr Hardin paid me 6/ at mr Blaks.   at [Hardns]. Birth 4th. 
 

 

 January 23, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Blaks Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from my Sons to See mrs Blak who was Deld at 5h Evn of a Son & I returnd. mr Blak gave me 9/5d as a reward.   at mr Blaks. Birth 5th.  
 

 

 January 26, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Savage Botlns Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from my Sons to Savage Boltons at 8h Evn, his wife was Deld at 11h 50m of a Dagt, her 6th Child.   at Does & Boltons. Birth 6th.  
 

 

 February 4, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jonas Son. XX.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. I feel Some more Comfortable, went to washing; mr Ballard to mr Sheppards. my Son Jona Came for me and Cyrus went & Brot our hors back. we went in hast, find my Dagt ill. her women were Calld & Shee Safe Deld at 8h Evn of a Son, and is very Comfortable.   at my Sons. Birth 7th. 
 

 

 February 10, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to ye hook aftn to hear mr Smith. Son Jona was here, his wife is Cleverly. I havi been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 February 12, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cold morn, Snowd aftern. mr Mc Causland Came here for mrs Barton, his wife is failing fast. he informs me that mr Suells inft Expird ye 12th Day of its life. mr Livermr Dind here, Son Jona allso. Jacob Bradbury Calld me to See his wife who was in Labour with her first Child.   at Jacob Bradburys. mr Ballard & Ephm Came home from Winthrop.  
 

 

 February 13, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Bradburys Son. XX.

Storm of Snow. mrs Bradbury was Delivd at 9h 30m in morn of a Son & I returnd home at 1 pm, find that my little Grand Son has Done Cleverly while I was absent.   at Ditoes. Birth 8th. receivd my fee Oct the 11.  
 

 

 February 14, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. Son Jona Came here, I went home with him; Carried his little Son home. mr Ballard went up & brot me home. we Calld at mr Pollards. Leut Pollard brot his wife home ys Day. Son & Dagt Town & Dagt Dolly Came here at Evng.   at my Sons, his wife is as well as Can be Expected; the infant is Some unwell  
 

 

 February 15, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. my Childn went to Son Pollards & I was Calld to mr Catons. mrs Livermr Came & Bakt me Bread.   at mr Catons.  
 

 

 February 16, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Catons Dagt. XX.

Clear & Cold. mrs Caton was Safe Deld at 3h morn of a Dagt and I returnd home at 10h, receivd 6/ as a reward. I Calld to see mrs Jackson, find her very low. mr Stratton & Lady, Son Town, his wife & Dolly Came here at Evn. mr James Page took Tea with us. Cyrus been [to] mill.   at Catons. Birth 9th. 
 

 

 February 21, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. mr Ballard went to Varsalboro to Survey. I Combd Some flax. was Calld to See mrs Childs & infant, tarried all night.   at mr Childs.  
 

 

 February 22, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. mrs Child is unwell with a Cold, the infant is teething. I left them after Dining. Calld at mr Hamlins, Sally is unwell; at mr Pitts, his Lady is not So well as Shee Could wish; at mr Wessons, all well there; at Son Pollards. Came from there in a Sleigh. mr Keth attended me.   at Dittoes & other Neigrs. 
 

 

 February 24, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Cars Dagt. XX.

Snowd morn. I was Calld at 3h to Moses Cars Lady. Shee was Delivd at 5 of a fine Dagt, her first Born. I arivd at home at 8. mr Car gave me 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard attended worship at ye academy.   at Moses Cars. Birth 10th. 
 

 

 February 28, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. mr Waid workt here. the widdow, Dolly wrode with her. mr Thwing here, his wife has a Sore Breast. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 March 1, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. I went to See mrs Thwing, Shee has a Sweld Breast, & I was at mr Childs, his wife is unwell; the infant allso. mr Ballard has been Surveying for Esq Matthew Howard, & went to mrs Husseys.   at mr Thwings & mr Childs.  
 

 

 March 11, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A rainy Day. Capt Stackpole here. mr Ballard went to Winslow. I have been at home till 11h Evng when I was Calld to See Leut D. Pollards wife.   at home mr Pollards.  
 

 

 March 12, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth David Pollards Son. XX.

Clear & Pleast. I was at mr Pollards, his wife was Deld at 11h Evn of a Son. mrss Voce, Williams, Hamlin & Black assisted. mrs Gordin had fitts. mr Ballard returnd from winslow.   at Ditoes. Birth 11th. receivd 6 as a reward. 
 

 

 March 18, 1793, Monday

X. Birth Hinklys Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. I went from Latps to James hinkleys at 3h morn, his wife Deld at 7 Evng of a Dagt at 7 Evn & I returnd at 10 to mr Lathrops. find her ill. I receivd 6/ of mr Hinkly.   at mr Hinkleys. Birth [12th].
