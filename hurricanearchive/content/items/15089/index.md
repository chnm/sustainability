Evacuee testimonial from Acadiana Outreach Center:\r\n\r\nAvon employees from Iowa and Missouri make individual care packages for evacuees.  One Avon employee, who experienced homelessness herself first-hand, was moved by the plight of the evacuees and motivated to gather personal care kits across several states and personally deliver them to our Hurricane Relief Center here.  The packages include shampoos, deodorants, bug guard, lotions, lip balm, tooth brushes, tooth paste etc - basically daily essentials.     \r\n\r\nOriginally posted: http://www.acadianaoutreach.org/site59.php
