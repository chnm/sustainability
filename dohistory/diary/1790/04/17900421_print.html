Martha Ballard's Diary, April 21 - 26, 1790

21 4
A very rainy foren. Cleard of pleast at ye 3 h pm. mr Ballard Bot two Bushels of Corn of meloy; gave 10/ in Cash. I have been at home. Foster went from here; he has not Eat anything here ys Day. Samuel Cumings Junrs Son Drowned. at home. Saml Smith here for Sustenance for his father who is Sick. Death.  
22 5
Cloudy morn. Clear ye middle of ye Day. Revd mr Foster here. David Pollard & mr Sprague brot Loggs to our mill. mr Ballard & Georg Bolton Setled acounts. mr Richardson receivd money of mr Ballard for Servic Done in the business of Surveying last fall. mrs Chamberlin & Sally Bolton here; Drank Tea with me. I have [been] at home.  
23 6
Clear & pleasant. I have been at home. mr Dexter here; informs that Biah Town is wasting fast, that Nathan Haywood is mending & that a woman in their new Plantation Expired in Child bed last Lords Day, & Son Towns famely are well. mr Shuball Pitts here this night. Elisha Mathews had a Son Drowned at his Logg Landing as they were raffting Loggs. at home. a Death; Mathewss Son Drownd.  
24 7
Cloudy morn. Sun Shine before noon. mr Ballard Gone to Settle with Capt Hersey. Hannah Putt Soup on to Boil this morn. I am informd that a mrs Cragg of winthrop Departed this life the weak past. mr Andrews gave me a Lemmon ys Day & mrs Hamlin gave me a piece of Veal. at home. news of a Death. there was a Drowned Lad taken up at ye Chopps in this river.  
25 C
Clear & Pleasant. mr Learned was here; took Tea & went to Varsalboro. I have been at home. Jobe Sleeps here. Ephms tooth aicht. at home; unwell.  
26 2
Clear & Pleasant. we were informd that mr Pollard had broke his Legg this morn. I am Calld to See mrs Burton who is unwell. Son Town is Coming with Loggs, our men gone to meet him, it is 12 OClok. mrs Mc Cathney, mrs Black, Judy Huston, Polly Pollard, Nr Savage & wife took Tea here. mr Stratten, Son Town & Robsn, Dexter Dind here. my Dagt & famely well. Biah Declineing, Nalhan Haywood mending. at home. mr Pollard Broke his Legg.
