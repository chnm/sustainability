Welcome Teachers!

Eagle Eye Citizen is a freely available resource designed to help middle and high school students think critically about civic participation, Congress, and American history using primary sources from the Library of Congress. Discover ways to integrate Eagle Eye Citizen into the classroom — from helpful videos to lesson planning ideas and tips on differentiation.
