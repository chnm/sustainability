Letter from the War Office
No Date
John Gish's Receipt for Cannon wanted
[stamped: Received in State Dep't Nov. 24 1894 R.P.O. 401310]
Dear Sir.
The General wishes you to come to the office immediately, and to bring the receipts of the waggoner who took the brass cannon to Pittsburg__It seems they are left on the road__
When did the Sheet Iron go to Pittsburg?
Yr Stagg Friday A.M.
HKnox
