N York 6 June 1786.
Sir
Mr. Rudolph in behalf of his deceased
Brother has made application to sue for his arrears
of pay &c — Major Rudolph Stands Charged
in my office — as will appear by the enclosed acct with
Considerable Sums of money - drawn by him for the
Use of the Legion — which it is necessary should
be accounted for before Major R. Can Receive
his certificates - can be granted —
Mr Rudolf The Brother suggests that his
Brothers vouchers, for the expenditure, and
payment of this money - was were Lost - or
Carried away by Sergt Henderson who deserted
and took along with him Majr. Rudolphs
Baggage — and refers me to you Sir, for
 I wish information the 
such information, as you 
can give possess on the Subject  what I wish you
would [undecipherable] do me the honor to give [undecipherable]
be enabled to form so much depends on the
information you will must oblige me, Pay
recruiting [undecipherable] as early as convenient
The Honble. Mr Lee
