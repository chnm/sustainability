Jany 1. 1796
From F. Sprig
[undecipherable] Jany 1796

William Simmons Esqr.
Accountant of the
War Department
Philadelphia
West Point Jan.y 1st 1796
Sir
I take the liberty to enclose you my account
for Salary, for the months of Octr., Novr., & Decr.
last, you will oblige me by sending the money
through Mr Fish of New York, or in any other
way you deem proper.
I am
Sir
with much Esteem & Respect
Your humble Servant
Frederick Sprig
William Simmons Esqr.
Accountant of the
War Department
