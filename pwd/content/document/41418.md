Major Isaac CraigPittsburg
Honored by Mr. Turnbull
LetterSamuel Hodgdon Esqr25 April 1791particular Instructions Abt Boats purchasing &c
Philadelphia 5 May 1791
Sir
Enclosed you have duplicate receipts for two Loads of stores forwarded to your address the whole of which [shape?] will easily and speedily reach you--On Monday three pieces of Ordnance will leave this for Pittsburg--I wish you to be particular in examining the order in which the Carriages and implements arrive, they are all in the best order when they leave me-- Two more waggons with stores various will leave this for Pittsburg this evening
I was in hopes to have heard that you had disposed of the Peas for I am at a loss whether or not I should foward more--
I amSir Your Most Obedient servantSamuel HodgdonQMG
Enclosed is Windles Rect--the other mentioned is since enclosed separately
Major Craig
