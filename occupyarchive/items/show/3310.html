Title

Be Nice to this man 24-Oct-2011 Liberty Park, NYC Occupy Wall Street

Description

This gentlemen is one of NYCs long-term homeless, a very nice man and incredibly helpful to the protesters many of whom were new to the idea of sleeping outside in the cold.

He helped a lot of people find the little crooks and crannies in the area. If you meet this man, give him you're respect, he deserves it.
