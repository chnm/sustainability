No. 19
Pittsburg 16th July 1791
Sir
You will issue on the enclosed order and you will open a Clothing account taking duplicate receipts for every article of Clothing delivered--A General Order to Morrow will make this business be fully understood--Messages will be made for every article delivered more than was engaged to the Men--I wish you to direct Carl to compleat two horse Boats, and One for forage, the first, they may be called for suddenly--
Your Most Obedient servantSamuel HodgdonQMG
