Board of Treasury - 
                                                          August 6th 1788


Sir
I am directed by the Board of Treasury to transmit to you a 
Petition of General Spencer on the Subject of his Account with
the United States. You will be pleased to state the Circumstan-
=ces set forth in this Memorial, and to Report Specifically your
opinion on them -  

                    I am Sir, by Order, Your obed. Hle. Sert



                          Wm Duer, Secy

                                (pen flourish)

To:
Joseph Howell Esqr
Assist Comm' of

    Army Accounts -
