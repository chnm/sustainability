Major General Cushing
10th December 1800
Recd 13th
[encircled: 212]
[stamped: Received from state Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894, R.P.O. 401310.]
Inspectors Office
City of Washington
Decr 10th 1800
Sir 
I have the honor to enclose a copy of the General order of the thirteenth ultimo for your information - This order was submitted to the Secretary of War & received his sanction previous to its publication.  
The General left this City for PIttsburgh on the fifth instant and I expect he will remain there through the winter.  
I am Sir 
Your Obedt Servt 
T. H. CUSHING
Majr & Inspr 
Colonel Hodgdon
