New Orleanian Joshua Cousin survived Hurricane Katrina &amp; is now blogging at the Houston Astromdome (via Dave Winer). How is it possible? He explains:\r\nThere is an area called ACT Canter (Astrodome Community Technology Center.) the was set up to help people register &amp; Find Missing family, friends &amp; even pets who have been lost or displaced durring the evacuation. I have been using it to tell all my online friends that I\'m alive. Eventually It gave me National Publicity. I Think Im The first known Blogging Survivor.
