Sir NYork June 27.th 1786

I am much Obliged to you for the information Communicated in your Letter of the 23.d which came to hand this Morning__ it is out of my Power untill I have the [aligned right] direction 

62

direction of Congress to Comply with your Wishes. I must therefore take every Consequence that may follow. I am sensible that a Newspaper Controversy must be disagreeable but neither this Consideration nor the Malevolence. the insuety or the threats of any one shall deter me from my duty__

I am with Respect

JP

Col.o Oswald
