Mr Beach
7th May 
1795
[The Following is 90 degrees clock-wise]
Samuel Hodgdon
Philadelphia
forward by
Mr Baldwin
New York May 7th 1795
Sir 
Before I returned from Philadelphia the man who I had ingaged to make the boxes had made what we have  now sent - we were obliged to take them - or I wood not sent them for I see the casks are much better & we shall send the residue in casks - if you can find use for them so that you can pay for them you will much oblige sir 
yours
Nath’ Beach 
Samuel Hodgdon 
Esqr
