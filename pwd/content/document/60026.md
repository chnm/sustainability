William Heth Esq.
Shellelah
Accountants Office
November 17th 1797
Sir,
I received your letter of the 8th. Instant enclosing vouchers of William Taylor & a list of the payments made by Edward Smith.
The Letter addressed to Captain Joseph OBannon is intended for the one who belonged to Col. Eresup's Reg. and as you remark resides in Harding County.
Paul McDermotts name was inserted on the first of Letters which you received by Mistake.
I am
Wm Simmons
