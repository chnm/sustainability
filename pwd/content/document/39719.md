Thompsons Agreement
to repair
the Arms
Samuel Hodgon Esq.
Sir
We will undertake to repair
one thousand stand of the same arms as we
have been at work upon, you allowing us to
pick them out of the fifteen hund that are
on hand & being supplied with the materials
as we have been, at Eight & foure pence for
Musket, and we will finish one hundred
& fifty per Month & shall expect payment
for the repairs of that number on the last
day of each month, untill all are finished
