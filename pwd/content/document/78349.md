Intendants Office
Nov. 11 1800
20 Suits of Inft Cloaths
to be four to Lieut
James Dill at Harrisburgh
Pennsylvania
Intendant of Stores Office
Philadelphia 11th, Nov, 1800
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to John Wilkins Esq. quarter master General, or order, complete suits of Infantry clothing for two Sergeants, & eighteen Privates, addressed to Lieut James Dile and to be transported to the recruiting rendevouz at Harrisburg in Pennsylvania agreeable to a requisition from Capt John Heth, dated this day. 
Your most abt Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
