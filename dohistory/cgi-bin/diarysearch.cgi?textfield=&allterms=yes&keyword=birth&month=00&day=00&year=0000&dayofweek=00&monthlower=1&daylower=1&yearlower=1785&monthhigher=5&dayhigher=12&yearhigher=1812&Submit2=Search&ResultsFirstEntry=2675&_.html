 October 16, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. [Birth Hanh Getchels Son, & first born. XX.]

Clear. I was Calld to Son Jonas to See Hannah Getchel who is in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son which had life but the Smallist I ever Saw alive. Shee Says Wm Sands is the father. I Sett up all night, Polly Lankester with me. Sist Barton went to Bed, got some Sleep.   at Son Jonas. Birth 15th. Dagt Pollard Some Easier. receivd [9/] as rewd.  
 

 

 October 26, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Pleast. I have been ["at home" - implied]. Ephm Calld at L. Hamlins, got me 5 oz Snuff for 1 qt mustard.   at [home]. mr [Lan ] Sent [me] 2 lb [pork].  
 

 

 November 13, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard to the Selllement, Brot home _ lb Salt, _ gln Spirit, _ lb Tea, 2 lb Sugar, 1 lb Candles. the Tea, Sugar & Candles had of Son Ephm. Son Jona Calld here, all wel at Clinton.   at [home]. Funeral of Old mr Bullin of Hallowell.  
 

 

 November 14, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Ebenz Hovys 3d Child & 2nd Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld by Ebenz Hovy Some time before day to go and See his wife in Labour. I went on beyond Son Jonas, mett Joel Savage on the Same arand. my Patient was Deld before noon & Isaac Savages wife Came fm mr Savage who was Deld by mrs Carter and Comfortable. I left my patient Cleverly and [relurnd] home at 3h 30m pm. I [Calld] to See mrs Bracket who is very feeble.   at Ebenz Hovys. Birth 16th. receivd 9/ as a reward. Joel Savages Dagt Born at Son Jonas near night.  
 

 

 November 20, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & rain, Cleard of in ye night. we were alone ys night. I was informd that a mrs Shaw in the Settlement was Delvd of 2 Babs Still born, 3 Docts with her.   at home.  
 

 

 November 21, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have finisht my washing. Hannah Getchel Came in, paid me for attending her in Child Birth.   at home.  
 

 

 November 26, 1810, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day. we had a Swine Butchered. I was Calld Wilm Son of Betsy Andrews who has burnt his hands. Epm & Wm Sleep here.   at mrs Andrewss, Wilm is Burnt.  
 

 

 November 27, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 raind the most of ye day. I have been takeing Care of my meat and doing other matters. Ephm Sleeps here. Son Lambd Calld & took Some apples ys morn, then Sett out for home.   at home.  
 

 

 December 1, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Wm B. Came to Son Ephms to Conduct me home. I Calld at mr Thwings, his wife is Some unwel, Lamb in her right Sholder. Shee let me have 6 Candles.   at Ditos & at mr Thwings.  
 

 

 December 8, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cool. I was Calld by Shubal Wickson Junr to go & See his wife who is in Labour. her women were Calld at Evng. Shee lingured thro the night. we Slept none.   at Wicksons. receivd 1s [ ] [at] mrs Bradford.  
 

 

 December 9, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Shubal Wicksons Dagt. XX.

Clear & Cold. mrs Wickson was Delivd at 9h am of a Daught, her first Child. the women retired and I drest the Babe and left them as wel as Could be Expected. arivd home at 12/OClock, found work to do.   at Ditoes. Birth 17th. receivd 6/25 Cent august 30, 1811.  
 

 

 December 15, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I was Calld at 1h 10m ys morn to go to See the wife of mr Abm Devenport. Shee Lingured thro ye night & her women wint home. it Snowd in ye night.   at mr Devenports.  
 

 

 December 16, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XXX. Birth Abm Devenports 2nd dagt & 5th Child. XXX.

Clear & Cold. mrs D. had her women Calld in ys morn and was Deld of a Daugt, her Second, & 5th Child at 5h 20m and is as Comfortable as Can be Expected. I was Conducted home about noon by her husband. I receivd 5 lb Candles & 1 Dolr.   at Abm Devenports Birth. Birth 18th. recd 12/ for Service. Funeral infant Livermors.  
 

 

 January 3, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was Calld at 10h am to Son Jonus to See Hannah Getchel who is very unwel. Doct Ellis was to See her at Evng, thinks her in danger of having a Seated fever, left Jullop and Salts tarter.   at Son Jonas. Hanh Getchel is unwel there.  
 

 

 January 22, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Sist Barton and I went to See Old mrs Mosier and attended prayers at mr Bonds, his wives remains were interd at Evng. Sist & I Calld to See mrss Young & Thwing. I Came from Capt Ps at dusk, wrode home with Hannahs Gelchel & Ballard.   at mr Mosiers & others. Funeral of mrs Bond.  
 

 

 January 26, 1811, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & not So very Cold. Fineas Pain Sent me 1 blsl wheat in part for what is due to me for assist ing his wife august 11th, 1810. Jona & Wm Ballard Sleep here, inform me Wm Lambd is some better. let us bless God for his mercies.   at home. Jona B. & Wm Sleep here.  
 

 

 February 10, 1811, Sunday

 Cloudy. Jona left here going to Clinton. Son Jona Calld in aftern. Epm brot us a Loade wood.   at home.  
 

 

 February 11, 1811, Monday

X. Birth Capt S. Pitss 3d Sn. XX.

Clear. Capt Shubal Pitts Calld me this morn Early to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Comforlable till 4h pm when her Labour Came on. her women were Calld & She was Safe [ oth] at 7h 30m.   at Capt Pitses. Birth 1st. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 February 16, 1811, Saturday

 Clear part of ye day. Allin Calld on me, wisht me to go home with him tomorrow.   at home.  
 

 

 February 17, 1811, Sunday

 Cloudy morn, Snowd before n. I Sett out with Allin for Clinton at 11h am, reacht Son Lambds about Sun Sett, find Wm very ill.   to Son Lambds.

X. Birth mrs Newalls Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to Winslow to see mrs Newall. Shee was Deld at 4h am of a Son which wd 11 & 2/1 lb. I tarried with her till after Tea, receivd 9/.   at mrs Newals. Birth 2nt. recd 9.

Cloudy, raind at night. I was Calld to See mrs Philbrook who was in labour. Shee was Deld at 5h 30m pm of her 4th Son & 7th Child. I left her very Comfortable.   at mr Philbrooks. Birth 3d. recvd 1/6d.
