Office of Army accounts
New York June 4 1786
D Sir
Inclosed are enclosed the certificates due to Cap Adams
for while his Brother has receipted to me, — I have
not returned them in my hands & inclosed t them to you as I find charges
against the Cols [undecipherable]accounted for, and are what I did not have
with certainty  whether you had [undecipherable] that you was possessed of when you
enclosedforwarded their accounts and whether they had are still to
his debit - I wish you to be so kind as to transmit his
[undecipherable] inform yourself as their business &
to deliver the certificates if you find that the whole
monies charged have been accounted for.
PS he has given me an order to send the certificates
out you, & I wish  therefore that you woud take
his receipt for the same when you deliver them.
Jas. [or Jno] White


The Garden.
verch home
Mr Tracy Post
