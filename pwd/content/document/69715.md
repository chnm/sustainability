77
Accountants Office
July 21 1799
I Certify that there is due to Wm. Lawrence Captain 1 Regt. of Philadelphia militia commanded by
Coll. Francis Gurney ordered into service to suppress an Insurrection in the Western Counties of Penns. in 1794, the sum of Thirteen Dollars & 50/100, being for his retained rations from 22 Septr to 15 Novr. 1794 inclusively  W.S.
Dolls. 13.50
The Secy of War
