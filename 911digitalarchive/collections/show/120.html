Dave McCall

Title

Dave McCall

Description

I met Dave McCall in Somerset County, having been introduced to him through research on Flight 93. Dave was one of the counselors who helped aid families at Seven Springs, and has recently written a book on his experiences. He has contributed more than 500 photographs that were given to him to the Digital Archive.

-Chrissie Brodigan
