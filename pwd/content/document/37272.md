Thomas Overton, Esq.                       D. Of War Accountants' Office
Inspector of the Revenue                       May 15th 1795

 Charleston So. Carolina

Sir

The Treasurer of the United States will remit you the sum of four hundred
Dollars to be by you transmitted to Nicolas  Francis Martinon temporary Engineer at 
Wilmington for which sum you will take his duplicate receipts to be accountable. ----

                I am Sir r. G
                William Simmons
