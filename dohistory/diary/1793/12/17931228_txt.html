28 7

 

 Cloudy. I have been at home, workt about house. Sally washt the west room. Cyrus went to the hook. I Sent 6/ to Susy Cleark for weaving. Ephn been to work for mr Waid. at [h ].
29 F

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, my Sons & Sally attended public worship at ye hook. revd mr Turner performd. mr [sic] Son Jon a here at Evng. I have been at home. at [ho ].
30 2

 

 Clear & very pleast. mr Ballard & Sons have been geting wood for mr Beeman. I have been at home, Bakt & Did house work & knitt Some. Sally wove 3/4 yd, the piece is very tender. at [ho ].
31 3
