War Office 
August 7th 97
Dragoon Priv Cloathing to be delivd to Capt James Taylor
War Office, August 7th 1797
Sir,
Please to deliver Captain James Taylor, two complete suits of dragoon's clothing for privates and two knapsacks.
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
