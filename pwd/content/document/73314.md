January 3rd 1799Strike-through text1800

I certify that there is due to George W. Kirkland

the sum of Eighty dollars being for his pay for Novr. & December

1799 which sum is payable to Joseph Anthony, agreeably to

a power of Relinquishment on file in this Office.

Pay of the Army for 1799

Dollars 80. W.S.

The Secretary of War
