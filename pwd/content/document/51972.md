Sir:
I forgot to remark to you in my last letter that I hold, late, Ensign Remington's order and receipt for his pay for the month of September, being for money paid for him as bail in a special action --
I am, Sir, your Obedient and very humble servant
William Eaton
William Simmons Esquire
