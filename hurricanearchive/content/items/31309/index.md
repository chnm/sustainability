Filmmaker Helen Hill took this photograph of a baby picture of Rene and Michelle Broussard. Helen helped the Broussards when they visited their flooded home for the first time on March 2, 2006. She documented the scene and took closeups of family photos so the images could be preserved. Helen\'s own house was also badly damaged in the flooding after Hurricane Katrina. Helen Hill was murdered on January 4, 2007.
