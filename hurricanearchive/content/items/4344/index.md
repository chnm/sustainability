Life as I remember it.  Totally different.  The beach was completely whipped out and all my younger memories are gone.  Many of my friends and family lost their homes.  It is totally different now.  Condos are becoming a big thing on the coast, and there is nothing to really do on the coast anymore.  Yes, bits and pieces of our beach is being cleaned, but everything has been damaged and Biloxi does not look the same.  I miss it, but I believe Biloxi will be built back up and even better than before!
