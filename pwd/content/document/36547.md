New York Feby 10th 1784

Sir,
I am favored with your
two letters upon a subject that I am
very sorry it is not in my powers
to give satisfactory answers--
any money will come into the Treasury.
I am not able to determine, but hope 
it takes place soon, after making good
on engagement to the Governor for
monies he provided when Genl. Washington
was here for his use to the amount
of three thousand dollars, you may
rest assured the Army shall have
the supplies if that will be in
time to prevent their having them
to a discount,  I am Sir,
with great [illegible]
[illegible]
J Pierce Esq
