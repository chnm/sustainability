Tyonna, Red Car - Tyonna, oh boy there was something going on with Tyonna.  Never quite figured it out.  Sweet girl, though. She was a huge perfectionist and in definite need of attention and validation.  She tore up probably twenty drawings that she “just wasn’t happy with”.  If I recall, many were of houses and buildings.  Finally in the end, she drew the car driving off in the sunset.  When I asked her if that was her car she shrugged, “It’s a secret.”
