Letter from the War Office informing of Saddles no. date 1792
Samuel Hodgdon Esq.1
Monday Morn:g 
Dear Sir 
The enclosed will give you the necessary information respecting the Saddles arrived from New York-We have no invoice of the Shoes-a bill of lading was transmitted to the War-Office, and I gave it to Capt. Freeman-
Yours Sincerely. 
Sir Stagg Jim
Sam: Hodgdon Esq.
