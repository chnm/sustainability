Lieut. Peter Shoemaker                                   Accountants Office

    Reading                                               June 22nd 1797

Sir
I received your letter of the 20th Instant enclosing a receipt of bounty paid to Thomas Brown which will be placed with those already sent.
Enclosed is a letter for Captain Lyman enclosing one hundred Dollars on account of his pay, which i have directed him to deliver his receipts soon to you, you will be pleased on your delivering him the money to foward his Receipts to this office by different conveyances, I enclose you the receipts to be signed.
Signed-
I am vc.
Wm. simmons 
acct.
