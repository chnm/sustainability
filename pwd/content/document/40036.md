Fort Harmar, September 12, 1789
Dear Sir:  Major Doughty informs me in his letter dated of the 
21st ultimo, that he arrived at Little Miami on the 
16th, and after reconnoitering for three days from 
thence to the Big Miami, for an eligible situation 
whereon to erect the works for head quarters, he had 
at length determined to fix upon a spot opposite 
Licking river, which he represents as high and healthy, 
abounding with never failing springs, +c., and the 
most proper position he could find for the purpose.
I am, +c.
Jos. Harmar
The Honorable General Knox
Secretary at War.---
