W.D. Accountants Office
                                  April 9th 1794 

Sirs

                     The Treasurer will remit to you

to be paid to Daniel Bissell at Middle= town the sum of ninety dollars and 50 Cts. being 

                              80

for pay due to his recruits for Jany & Feby 1794 for which he is to receipt to be accountable.

                              I am Sirs etc
                                Joseph Howell

John Chester Esqr Supervisor - Weathersfield

  Connecticut
