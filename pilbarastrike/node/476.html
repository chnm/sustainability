Primary Source

The Council for Aboriginal Rights championed the cooperative and sought to tell the story of this venture in bulletin after bulletin and in letters to the editors of newspapers. In an attempt to publicise the example of the cooperative, the Council planned a film and a pamphlet but it only had the resources to issue a special bulletin and mount a lecture tour by Don McLeod in 1955.

Council for Aboriginal Rights Special Bulletin
Citation

Council for Aboriginal Rights, Bulletin, no. 6, 26 August 1955.
