64) Office of Army Accts. N, York August 8th.1786

Dr. Sir,
Having a good conveyance by my friend Mr. Samuel Clark of your city, I have sent the accounts of Genl Deh[illegible ends in s, Dehases?] as requested in yours of the 3d. instant __
The accounts of Colonel Henry Becker are now before me -- as he cannot inform me what monies he has received, and being doubtful of the charges which appears on our books being proper I request you will once more examine your books, and transmit as soon as possible those he stands charged with by your state -- You will further please to send the charges against Major Saml. Benezet
I oblige your HbleServ
J Howell
John Nicholson Esqr.
