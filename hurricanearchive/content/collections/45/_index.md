This collection contains four groupings of photographs taken in October, November, and December 2005 and January 2006 that trace the destruction of Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans, Metairie, and Gentilly, Louisiana; and in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi.

Photographer Mark Rayner lives in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and originally posted these images to his website: www.WildDogDigital.com
