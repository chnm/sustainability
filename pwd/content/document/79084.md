Knoxville August 29<sup>th</sup> 1797.

Contingent Expence D<sup>r</sup> To Cash
Paid Hugh Dunlap for Services performed for Col. Butler on Nolachucky   15.--

Commissary's Department D<sup>r</sup> To Cash
Paid Jonah Walters his Services as departing Commissary & Q. Master from 1<sup>st</sup> July to 1 Aug.
@ 400 Dollars p. Annum  33.69

Commissary Department D<sup>r</sup> To Cash
Advance Lt. Saml. R. Davidson for acting as spring commissary to the troops at South west point  46.7

30<sup>th</sup>
Stephen Hillis D<sup>r</sup> To Cash
Paid Robert Wyley atto. to Sundry persons for Seviers Expedition   287.--
Paid William Dunwoody atto. to Sundry persons for Seviers Expedition   69.--
William Dunwoody atto. Sundry services for 1793  22.80
---------------------------------------------------------
                                                  378.80

Quarter Master Department D<sup>r</sup> To Cash
For sundries & Bills paid for Lt. Saml. R. Davidsons House at Tellico   91.21

Contingent Expence D<sup>r</sup> To the Treasurer
For warrant N<sup>o</sup> 2216 in favour of Lt. Saml. Davidson for Expences from Philadelphia to Knoxville as express to order on Clothing.   100.--

Cash D<sup>r</sup> To Contingent Expence
Being the balance of Lt. Davidsons traveling expences   5.14
