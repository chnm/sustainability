 April 16, 1810, Monday

X. Birth mr Bradfords 3 Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 7h Evng by mr Bradford to go and See his wife who is in Labour. Shee was Delivd at 10h 30m of her 3d Son and 6th Child. I tarrie all night. Novr recd 3 [ October 5].   at mr Bradfords. Birth 7th. reced 3[ ] in [ ] June & July.  
 

 

 April 17, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mrs Bradford & infant Comfortable after Breakfast & reacht home at 7. Calld at Son Jonas, his Son Sett out for Clinton, mr Allby with him, he took gardin Seeds & potatoes for Son Lambd. Sister Barton has been quite Sick with her Eyes being Soar.   at Ditoes & at Son Jonas. walkt from my Sons, the roads very bad.  
 

 

 April 20, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sist So much better yt Shee has Spun 3 Skeins. I was Calld at noon by mr Bradford to go and See his wife who has an ill turn. I administred some remediy which gave her Some relief.   at mr Bradfords, his wife has an ill turn.

 Cloudy, raind in the night. I have been makeing Soap. Dagt Ballard here of an arrand. Betsy Wiman, H. Ballard, Linda Getchel & Betsy Pierce Calld in here.   at home makeing Soap.  
 

 

 April 29, 1810, Sunday

 Clear day, raind at night. Son Jona Calld in here.   at home.

 Clear. Son Ephm here, had Currint Bushes, Rheubarb and other roots. Warrin Stones wife [&] Shubal WickSons Calld, had gardin Seeds. I have done hous work, dugg parsnips &C. Daniel Woodward paid me 1 Dol and 20 Cents.   at home.

 Clear part of ye day. I went to Son Jonas to See mrs Peirc but Shee was gone home, her Breast Soare.   at Son Jonas.  
 

 

 May 11, 1810, Friday

 Clear. mrs Farewel left here. mr Ballard and I went to Lecter. mr Stone deliverd A discoars from Hebrews 13 C 1st vers. we went to Son Ephms after meeting.   at Lecter and at Son Ephms.  
 

 

 May 15, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 raind in the night, Clear foren, Showers aftern with Thunder. mr Bullin Calld here, his famely are wel. he informd of a Death at Hollowell this morn.   at home. mr Bullin here.  
 

 

 May 16, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Showers. I have been at home, am informd that the Remains of wido Lamb were interd ys aftern. Shee was 75 years Old lacking about 1 month. may we all hear ye Call and be allso ready.   at [home]. Funeral wido Lamb.  
 

 

 May 20, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant. Son Jona Calld in here. Allin Sett out for home but Slept at Son Jonas. Wm to meeting. mr Cogswel Discoarst from Jeremiah 17 C 9th vers. Elijah Barton Sleeps here, informs me his Mother is much better than Shee has been.   at [home]. Elijah Barton here.

X. Birth Lues Hamlins [3d] Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 7h morn to See Luis Hamlins wife, found her Deliverd of a fine Son by the help of mrs Pratt and Shubal Pitses wife [  ]. I attended public worship aftern. mr Hamlin Conducted me home, gave me 6S.   at mr Hamlns. Case 8th performd before I reacht there.  
 

 

 May 28, 1810, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Joshua Heaths 3d Son. XX.

Clear foren, Showers aftern. I was Calld at 2h ys morn to See Joshua Heaths Lady who was in Labour, Shee was Safe Deld at 3 of her 3d Son and 5th Child. Shee & infant are Cleverly. I was Conduted hom wd as far as Capt Smiths by mr Allby. I Calld in to See mrs Pierce, find her Breast is not well yet. I sorted Beens for Planting and did my other wk.   at mr Heaths. Birth 9th. receivd 6/ June 2nd 3/ S.

 Clear. I was Calld by mr Ranlet to go and See his wife who is in Danger of an Aborsion. Shee got more Comfortable. I tarried all night.   at mr Ranlets.  
 

 

 June 6, 1810, Wednesday

 Clear. I left the Patient after breakfast free from pain but weak. I Calld at mr Thwings, receivd 1 Dollar in part of my Due; at mr Lues Hamlins, find his wife very Comfortable. did Some washing and went to Son Jonas. my Dagt Came home with me.   at Ditoes & others. Churnd and washt Some at home. mr B. plantd potaloes.

 Clear foren, A very heavy Shower aftern. Son Ephms wife Came to See me. I have with great pains mended a Shirt for my husband.   at [home]. Son Epms wife here.  
 

 

 June 20, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I have planted Beens, Corn of ye purple kind & potatoes of Lukes kind in my gardin foren. my husband and I attend at the Meeting hous at 4h pm where ye remains of Genl Henry Sewal [wive] were lodgd till Revd mr Gillit [Delivd] a discoarse fm Hebs 12 Chap 11 vers. mr Cogswel made the Closeing prayer and the Funeral proceeded to the grave, and we returnd home at Sun Sett.   at mr Thwings and at the Funeral of mrss Sewal. Calld at Son Jonas.  
 

 

 June 29, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Orocks Dagt. XX.

Clear and warm. I have washt Some wool, my Buttery and other matters. was Calld at 3h pm by mr Orock to See his Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 4 of a fine Dagt, her first Child by her present Husband. I left them as wel as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 6, did my work.   at mr Orrocks. Birth 10th. receivd 2 Dols as A reward.  
 

 

 July 5, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Enoch Rowels 3d Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn and Some rain. mr Rowel Calld on me at 6h 30m to to and See his wife in Labour. I found the Child born when I arivd. I finisht the performance. it is hir 3d Dagt and 4th Child. I returnd to Son Jonas, Dind there; mr Ballard allso. I came home, removd Beet Plants and did other matters.   at mr Rowel. Birth 11th. recd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 July 6, 1810, Friday

Birth Asa Pierces Son.

Clear. I was Calld by Asa Pierce at 8h morn to go and See his wife who was Safe Delivd at 5h pm of a Son. I left them Comforlable and reacht home after 9h Evng, felt fatagud.   at Asa Pierces. Birth 12th. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 July 7, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear the most of ys day. Jason Pierce and wife Calld in here. I have done hous work, my ironing &C. mr Ballard went to mill. Brot 2/1 bushl rie meal to Son Ephms, got 2/1 bsl potatoes; to a Store, got 2/1 lb Tea, 2 lb Sugar & Some Spirit. all Cost /5 shilings & 3d.   at home. Jason Pierce Calld [here].
