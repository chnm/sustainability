H
May 19 - 1790
Sey of War
Copied
Pay Office of the United StatesNew York May 19th 1790
Sir
Application is has been made to me for the Subsistence of by Cap. Jonathan Pearsee of Colo Wlletts Regt for the year 1783 Amounting to Thirty dollars & no cts also & Lt. Duncan Campbell of Colo Willetts Rt for subsis due them in 1782, fee Vizt
Cap. J. Pearsee Subsis from 7 April to 31 Aug 1782 ---- 30.40Lt. D. Campbell from do to 30 June ------- 8.86
for which sums you will please to issue yr Warrants
