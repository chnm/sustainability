316 Joseph Howell Esq

Dr Sir

I received your favour of the 13th inst covering a certificate no 911822 for twenty two dollars which shall be delivered. Leonard (undecipherable) on his application - also the certificate no 94825 for six hundred and fifteen and (undecipherable) dollars for Col James Cunningham which I have sent to him.

I am Dear Sir

W most obed serv

Jno Nicholson

Comptroller Genl Office

Nov 3 1787
