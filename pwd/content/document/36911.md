To the Commissioners for treating with Indian Nations
Philadelphia July 16. 1784
Gentlemen
In obedience to the order of the Committee of the States, I have the honor to enclose you a copy of a letter from general Clark dated "The falls of Ohio 26 April 1784 and the answer of the Committee thereto
With great respect I have the honor to be Gentlemen
your most obedient humble Serv  C. T.
[Unreadable] Howard Postmaster genl
Philada July 16. 1784
