27    

Accountants' Office
  June 4th 1799

 I certify that there is due to Captn Richard R. Cusack of the 2nd Company of Grenadiers, 1st Reg.l Philadelphia Militia 
commanded by Coll Francis Gurney called unto service to suppress an
Insurrection in the Western Counties of Pennsylv.a in 1794, the sum
of Twenty one Dollars and 80/100, being for his retained rations from 14th
Sept.r to 24 Dec.r 1794 inclusively.
Doll.r 21.80                              WmSimmons
The Sec.y of War                                             (indeciperable

                       W.l 4137
