- hope the one enclosed will answer your purpose
- copied from the several inspection returns
- copy
- ascertain the sum advanced by the state and the balance that may be due them
- agreeably to our instructions
- statement of the whole amount of the invalid pensions advanced & paid to this state
- honor to inform you that we were appointed at the last session of the honorable general assembly a committee for the purpose of settling the accounts
- make a return of to you of those now paid by this state
- have not been able to settle the accounts as yet
- having completed the return we now inclose it containing 59 invalids that have been inspected agreeably to an ordinance of congress
- removed from this state
- noted in the said return against their respective names
- continued to pay their pensions
- others are since dead
- pensions of consequence cease
- invalids who have not yet passed the inspection required
- whom we are authorized to call upon to appear in person
- able to examine their pretensions
- your letter to his excellency the governor on the subject of the invalids being in his possession
