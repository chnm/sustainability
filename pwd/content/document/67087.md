Accountant's Office
April 3rd 1799
I Certify that there is due to Tobias Lear Secretary to George Washington General of the armies of the United States, the sum of Four hundred seventy Dollars, and fifty eight Cents, being for his pay subsistence & Forage from 1 December 1798 to the 31 March 1799.
W. Simmons, Accnt
