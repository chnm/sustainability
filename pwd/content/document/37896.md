32 West Point 1 June 1786
Sir
I have forward [undecipherable] [undecipherable} Mr. {undecipherable} the returns of ordinance and quarter {undecipherable} for the month of May 1786
Agreeable to your Letter I engaged Mr. Manon upon the sum pay and rations he had last season, which I believe is a reasonable as any man I can get considering the importance of the affair as it will require a man of judgement to sort all the arms, and he will be often left in the store alone
The commissary informed me that the contract for the provisions yesterday, and no person appearing to issue upon a new one, I have drawn upon him for six days provisions for the men ~
I am sir The Honorable Major Genl Knox Secretary at War --
