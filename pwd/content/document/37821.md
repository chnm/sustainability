New York 6th May 1786

Dr. Sir,

Agreeable to your request of the 24th ulto. I have examined the rolls of the late 12 Regiment wherein James Wilson appears the 1 February 1777, and is mustered in 3d Reg, deserted Jany. 20, 1780.  how long he continued as a Deserter I am unable to inform you but have reason to believe he did not join the regiment in the year 1780; as he is not on the returns for 5 months pay  the pay from -


the first of January to the day of the death is made up and a Certificate issued to the Agents 

Inclosed is a return of the Supernumerary Officers of the late 8th. Regiment of Penna. altho there are no charges in the books of the Pay Office against those Gentlemen, yet I am led to believe that they received money from Col. McCay for the recruiting service, as that Gentleman is charges with the monies for that purpose which are unaccounted for 
I am &c
J. Howell Jur.

Jno. Nicholson Esqr.
