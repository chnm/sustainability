 October 19, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Carlows Dagt, his 11th Child. XX.

Clear and Pleasant. I was Calld at 9h morn by John Carlo to go and See his wife, Shee was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a Dagt, her 11th Child, at 8h 30m Evn. I receivd _ a kentle of Cod fish for my Service, and returnd home at 11 / OClok. left mother and infant as well as Could be Expected.   at John Carlows. Birth 26th. receivd half kentle Cod fish.  
 

 

 October 23, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, raind after noone and night. I went to Son Lambards and Pollards, I find them all about, heard that my Dagt Town was very ill first day night.   at my Sons Polld & Lambts. 
 

 

 October 24, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, Cleard of aftern, a very Pleast Evng. mr Densmore & wife, mrs Thomas, Mary and the Widdow Forbes took Tea with me. Esqr Wesson Calld here.   at home, had Compy. 
 

 

 October 25, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant. Jane Welch left me. I paid her for her Labour when Shee gets her Shoes fm Childs. Eunice went to Doct Colmans and promised to get here on Thurdsday next. I went to mr Dingleys at Evn. Joseph White Expired.   at mr Dingleys. Death of Joseph White.  
 

 

 November 2, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, Clear aftern. mr Ballard and I attended Lecture. we Calld at Son Lambards, they have a Boy come to liv with them by the name of Trask. I Saw son Pollard, he informd me they got to Sidney Safe.   at Lectur. hear that John Braly Expird last night. Funeral mr Pages Son.  
 

 

 November 4, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Some Snow fel in the past night. mr Ballard and I attended worship all day. the Ordinance of the Supper was Administred. mrss Lamb partook with us. I was Calld at Sun Sett to go to Esqr Haywoods and See his Sick Children, the Dagt was very ill all night.   at meeting and at Esqr Haywoods, watcht with his Childn.  
 

 

 November 8, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Thwings Dagt.

 Cloudy. I have Brewd & Bakt, and was Calld after Sun Sett to mr Thwings to See his Lady who was in Labour, and was Deld of a Dagt at 8h Evn. I walkt to mr Thwings. he Saw mr Ballard returning from Son Towns, who informd him that our Dear Dagt Departed this life this Day. I returnd home about 10h Evn, when mr Ballard infmd me Shee Expired at 10h this morn. may God grand us all hearts to Submit to his will and to hear the Call, be allso ready.   at mr Thwings. Birth 27th. recd 6/ as rewd. Death of my Dagt Town, aged 40 years, 2 months and 11 days.  
 

 

 November 11, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. we Tarried at the forenon Service and returnd home aftern. mr Cushman Delivd a find Discoarse on the Ocation from Psalm 88 & 18th vers.   at meeting at Winslow forn, Came home aftern.  
 

 

 November 14, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleast. Pally Livermore washt for me. Polly Goodin Calld here going to mr Dingleys. Pally Sleeps here this night. I have been at home. mr Finny had our OXen to plough this day.   at home. Pally Livermr washt for me.  
 

 

 November 15, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. mr Town Calld here. I have been at home. mrss Livermore Came to See me this aftern. Bradwil Sleeps here.   at home. mrss Livermr made me a visit.  
 

 

 November 17, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth [    ] Dinglys Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to See mrss Dingley at 9h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 10h and 30m of a fine Dagt. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd home at 2 pm. mr Ezekl Pages Lady here aftern. Patty Livermore workt for me. Sally Fletcher [Came here].   at mr [Dinglys]. Birth 28th.  
 

 

 November 18, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Suky Kenadys Son. XX.

Cloudy, Snowd Some and was very Cold. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to meeting. I was Calld at 9h 30m Evng to mr Kenadas to See his Dagt Suky, who was in Labour. her Child was Born a few muinets before I arivd. I Performd all that was necessary and we put her to Bed Comfortable. I tarried all night. they Say that Wilm Ellis is the father of ye Child.   at mr Kenadys. Birth 29th. May 14 th 99 recd my fee in Spining.  
 

 

 November 19, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. mr Ballard went to the hook, bot _ Gn Spirt, 2 lb Sugar, 2do Candles. I Came home from mr Kenadys, left the Patients Cleverly. I have been all most Sick all day. Sally has done my work and mended.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 November 23, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Son Jonas. I killd 3 fowls and drest them. I was Calld at 10h Evn by Lemuel Devenport to go and See his wife who is in Labour. old Lady Devenport, mrss Sewall & Sole attended.   at Lemuel Devenports.  
 

 

 November 24, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Lemuel Devenports Dagt. XX.

Clear. my Patient was Safe Deld at 4h morn of a fine Daught, her first Born. I tarried till 9h m and Sett out for home. arivd at 10. I receivd 9/ as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 30th. recd 9/ as a reward. this is the 750 Case I have [asd].  
 

 

 November 27, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant. I was Calld at 10h Evn to See the wife of mr Philip Norcross who was in Labour. mrss Randal & Hankerson there.   at mr Phillip Norcross.  
 

 

 November 28, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Phillip Norcrosses Dagt.

 Cloudy, raind at Evng. mrss NorCros was Safe Deld at 2h morn of her Sixth Daughter & 8th Child. I left mother and infant Cleverly and returnd home at 10 forn. Sally and I have been Bakeing Pies.   at Ditoes. Birth 31st.  
 

 

 December 2, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Snowd before night. mr Ballard only of my famely at meeting. mr Dingley Calld me to See his wife who has a very Soar throat. we bathd and put on Mullin. Cyrus Came home to [Towns].   at mr Dingleys.  
 

 

 December 3, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at Evn. mr Ballard went to winthrop, Carried 25 yds of white woolen Cloath to be Coulered and prest. I was Calld to See mrss Dingley again, find her very ill. I tarrid all night, Sett with her. Shee Seemd Easier before morn, but her throat is very much Sweld inwardly.   at mr Dingleys, Shee is very Sick. mr Heartford & wife allso  
 

 

 December 4, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came home at 10h morn, find that mr Ballard has had a fit of the Collic, but he is got Easier and went up to Town this aftern. mr Waid Butcherd a Beef Cow for us.   at Ditoes, the patient a little Easier. mr Ballard had the Collic.  
 

 

 December 5, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Waid Choping wood for us. mr Dingley Calld me to See his wife who is very ill with the rash. I went and Spent the day and night with her. Doct Cony was Calld, he approvd of what I had done. he left her Some Cm tart, Niter & Senna.   at Ditoes. Doct Cony was Calld. Polly Town is 17 years old ys Day.  
 

 

 December 6, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mrss Livermore Came to mr Dingleys for me to go and See her Sick Dagt. I find Patty very Sick. I put Black wool, wet with Brandy & pepper, in her Ears which gave her present relief. I returnd and helpt mrss Dingly up. Shee Seems as well as Can be Expected. Doct Cony Came to See her. Nancy Complains. Son Town Came here. I was Calld to mr Dingleys in hast by reason of her Bleeding at her mouth. mrss Densmore there. mrs Mc Causland Calld from there to Rheubin Moores, I to mr Lakemans.   at Ditoes and at mr Livermores. mr Dingley has our hors to go for mrss Mc Causland.  
 

 

 December 7, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Lakemans Son.

Clear. I put mrss Lakeman to Bed with her 4th Son and 5th Child at 2h morn, returnd to mr Dingleys at 6, find his wife more Comfortable. I receivd 6/ as a reward of Lakeman.   at Ditoes & mr Lakemans. Birth 32. receivd 6/. July '99 rec 3/.  
 

 

 December 14, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Anty Braketts 3d Son. XX.

Clear. I did my house work. Chopt meet for Pies &C. Sally Spun and pikt wool. I was Calld at 7h 30m to go and See the wife of Antony Brakett, Shee was Deld at 9h 30m of a Son. I tarried all night.   at mr Anty Braketts. Birth 33d. receivd 8/4 as a reward.  
 

 

 December 15, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left my Patient as well as Could be Expected, and Came on as far as Son Lambards at 10h morn. Son Cyrus Conducted me from there after we Dind. mr Dingley here, Says his wife and Childn are better. he Seems not So well as he Could wish.   at Anty Brakets and Son Lambards.
