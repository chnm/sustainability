43
Department of War Accountant's Office
June 10 1795
I Certify that there is due to Mary Weed the Sum of thirty five Dollars and sixty seven Cents being the amount of her Account for subsisting sundry Deserters from the Army of the United States confined in the jail of the City and County of Philadelphia from 2nd April to 6 June 1795 inclusive, which sum is payable to P. B. Wood Clerk for the jail. 
Wm Simmons
Acct
Dolls 35 and 67/100
The Secy of War
