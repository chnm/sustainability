Register's Office 30, Jany. 1786.

Dr. Sir,

The Board of Treasury having required from this Office an estimate of the Public debt up to the 31 Decemr. last renders it necessary for me to give you some trouble  you will therefore be pleased to furnish an Estimate of all the Certificates issued by the Commissioner for settling the army accounts up to that period
I am &c
Jos. Stretch

Mr J. Howell
