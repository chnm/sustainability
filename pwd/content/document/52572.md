388

Department of War-

Accountants Office

January 14 1796

Mr. James Hackett

Portsmouth

New Hampshire

Sir

Your letter of the 5<sup>th</sup> Instant I have received enclosing your account for salary from 1<sup>st</sup> of October to the 31<sup>st</sup> of December 1795 inc <s>[indecipherable</s> accounting to 500 Dollars which sum you will receive from Joshua Wentworth Esq/. Sups. of New Hamps hire and for whicks sum you will give him duplicate receipts.-

I am Sir j.c-

William Simmons
