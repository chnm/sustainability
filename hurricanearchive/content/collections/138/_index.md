Rosalie O'Connor is a photographer based in New York City whose best known work centers upon the dance world.
A member of American Ballet Theater (ABT) in the 1980s and 1990s, Ms. O'Connor started photographing the ABT and other dance companies.

Raised in New Orleans, Ms. O'Connor returns often to visit her family. In April 2006, she took a number of photographs of New Orleans post-Katrina. More information about her work can be found at: www.rosalieoconnor.com
