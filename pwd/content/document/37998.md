[aligned right]New York June 29. 1786

Sir

Since my Answer to yours in which you were so friendly as to inform me of W. Sullicans intended Attack on my Character in the Public Prints. I have been honored by Congress

with an Approbation of my Conduct. Should Mr Sullivan State persist in his unwarrantable purpose. I write you

[aligned right] to 

64.

to publish this Act (wch I have Inclosed) army Answer to his aspensions

I am Sir

PJ

Col.o Oswald
