Department of War
                                                      Accountants' Office
                                                        February 25 th 1796

Edward Carrington Esq r. Sup.re

Richmond

                                    Sir
                                      I received your letter under date of the 14thInstant - enclosing William Graves's Receipt for one hundred and fourteen Dollars, which will be passed to your credit on the Books of this Office.-

                               I am Sir (indecipherable)
                                   William Simmons
