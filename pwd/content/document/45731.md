Southwark, Decem 16 1793

Dear Sir

     I think it necessary to inform you in what manner the Tonage is calculated that I have made
 the Estimate from.

     In the first place to find the length of draught rabbit forward you take 3/5 of the Beam as
 usual; from that point to the after part of the Stern post allowing its width measurement not to
 exceed 1/12 of the Beam. That length being determined, you then multiply it by the length of the
 Beam, & that product by the height of the gundeck Beams amidships on the top of the Beams, added
 to half of her waste amidships which <s>last</s> product divide by 95 which gives the number of
 Tons Required.

                                                       I am Respectfully yours Sir
                                                                       Joshua Humphreys
