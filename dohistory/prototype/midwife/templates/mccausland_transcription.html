Cloudy. at mr Sewalls, they were
intimidated & Calld Dr Page who
gave my Patient [20] drops of
Laudenum which put her into
Such a Stupor her pains (which
were regular & promising) in a
manner [Stopt] till near night
when Shee pukt & they returned
& Shee was Delivd at 7h Evn
of a Son, her first Born. I left
her Cleverly at 10 & walkt home.
I receivd 12/ as a reward.
