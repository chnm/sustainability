 July 3, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Showers & thunder. Gillbh Left her. I watch at Fosters. Calld to See Willms.   watcht, was at Wmss. 
 

 

 July 4, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came from Fosters; left her very Low. Calld at Wmss; he is Low also; then went to mr Rheds, bt 5 lb Butter & 10-1/2 lb Chees; helpt do hous work the rest of the day. Jabish Cowin here; helpt us to water & wood. we had Six men yesterd & ys Day also.   was at Wmss. 
 

 

 July 9, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came home from Wilmss. Nathan Howard Calld here. my Self & Dagts attended funeral of mrs Foster. I was at Colo Nhs, Capt Porters, mr Westons & mr Crages & Wilmss. mr Thwing very much Distrest with an Austma. mr Gille & Patrige Sleep here.   at Ditoes, mrs Foster interd.  
 

 

 July 15, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 I was Calld up by Theops Hamlin at 2h morn, his wife & James Burton with him. I was informd that Nr Wilms was wors. I went there & tarried till aftern. Left him some Easier. Dolly & Parthena went to Josh Fosters; there were 7 Young Women Came home with them. Hamlin [&] wife & Burton Sleep here.   I was at Nr Wilmss. 
 

 

 July 16, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Theops Hamlin went to hous keeping. Hannah went up with his wife. I Combd flax foren. was Calld to mr Densmoores to See their Children who are unwell. Came back to mr Williamss; tarried all night; he did not rest much. Sally Burton & Martha Kenada here.   at Dens[mors] & Wilms. watcht.  
 

 

 July 18, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Combd flax for Cyrus & was Calld to See Saml Weston who is Sick; his Complaints are Pukeing & Purging; he has Voided 8 worms. we gave him Some Senna & Rhubarb. I Calld to See mr Williams; he Seems more Composed. Polly Cool there. Parthena been to Revd mr Fosters. his Lady gave her a yd ribbon & Sent one to Dolly.   at mr Westons & Williamss. mr Ballard to ye hook.  
 

 

 July 19, 1788, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Broth Barton here; took Breakfast. old mrs Cowen Dind. Jabizh workt here. I went to Wilmss at 3h pm; he had a very ill Turn. we were aprehensive he was Expireing, but revivd. I Came home at 10h Evn. Doct Barton Came here at 10h Evn, tarries here.   I was at Nr Wilmss. 
 

 

 July 20, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Hardins Son. XX.

Clear. Doct Barton & others took Breakfast here. I was Calld to Isaac Hardins wife in travil. Calld to mrs Williams to hear from her Husband, he is near Expireing; found mrs Hardn poorly. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 11h & 1/2 Evn. I tarried all night.   at mr Hardins. Smith & [Cloe] Cleark Published. Spoke with mrs Wilms.  
 

 

 July 21, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Left mrs Hardin at 5h morn as Cleverly as Could be Expected. Calld at mrs Williamses; find her Husband Expired. mr Flint Barton & mr Hardin Breakfasted here. I went to mrs Williamss & tarried till Eving, but feel very much fatagud.   at mrs Williams, her Husband Expird at 3h morn.  
 

 

 July 22, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Revd mr Foster here, Desird me to go & See his Lady. my Datr, Dolly, is there. mr Hovey brot me 5-1/2 lb of Buller which was Due to me for attending his wife the 3d of March last. I went to See mrs Foster; her Breast is soar and Shee is unwell other ways. I attended Funeral of mr Williams. There were a Larg Concoarse of people; the widdow fainted. I gave her Some Drops & Shee revivd. Gille & True Sleep here.   I attended Funeral of Joseph Williams & was at Revd mr Fosters.  
 

 

 July 24, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard Surveying for Thos Hinkly. I have been at home. Hannah went to See mrs Williams. mrs Woodward Calld here.   Capt [Benn] Stickny Married to Nabby Jackson.  
 

 

 August 1, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld Early this morn to See thee Revd mr Fosters Lady who is very unwell; her Breast is Likely to Break. I aplyed a Poltis of Sorril & returnd home. mrs Williams & Polly Cool & mr Savage here after nn. Daniel Robbins Left here Bound for the westward. Dolly Slept with mrs Wilms.   I was at mr Fosters & wido Williams s. my Girls washt thee floars, Parthena wrote Letters to her friends westward.  
 

 

 August 3, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Shower. mr Balad only of our famely went to meeting. I went to See mrs Foster; find her very much Distrest with her breast. Danl Robbins here.   I was at Revd mr Fosters.  
 

 

 August 4, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I helpt Spread the yarn to whiten then went to the revd mr Fosters; tarried till near night. I opend her Breast; it discharged a Larg quantity. I Left her much more Comfortable. I Calld at mr Westons. Sammy is about at Colo Norths, also his Lady is very ill. at mrs Williamss, Shee is Comfortable; her Child also.   at mrs Fosters. her Breast is Easier. at Wido Williamss also.  
 

 

 August 5, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Showers. I went to See mrs Foster; her Breast is Easier. mrs North is Better; mr Savage, his wife & Polly, Drank Tea; hannah Woodward also. I rode to Theops Hamlins. Calld at Doct Colmans.   I was at mr Fosters. Calld at Wdo Wilmss. 
 

 

 August 6, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I gatherd herbs & was Calld to mr Fosters. Calld at widow Williamss; her Child is very unwell. mr Ballards hand is much Better. Capt Porters Lady has been here. Parthena gone to Stay with mrs Williams. mr Gilbe here this night.   at mr Fosters & wido Williamss. 
 

 

 August 7, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth of Leut John Shaws XX Daugt Daugt. [sic].

Clear Part of the Day; some Showers. I went to See mrs Williams; her Child is very unwell. mrs Pollard there, mr Vahn also. Polly Bisbe tarried with her this night. I was Calld to Leut John Shaws Lady who is in travil; left home at 9h 30m. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt about 11h Evn. I returned home at two.   at mrs Williamss. Dolly finisht Strikeing Harnis to a Forty.  
 

 

 August 8, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Weston Calld here. I went to See mrs Foster. Shee has been not So well. Came to mrs Williamss; her Child very Sick; it had fitts. mrs Porter there the aftern. mrs Pollard & Polly Hinkly & mr Saml Peirce Stay there. he attended me home.   I was at Revd mr Fosters & the wido Williams. Patty had fitts.  
 

 

 August 10, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 fitts at [8]h Evn. it had 9 by 1/2 after 1 morn, then was Easy till 9, then the fitts Came on, it had 4 in the 11hr, then another which Continued 1h & 30m. I Came home & Changd my Linning. Ephm Cowen here in intermition. mr Goold, a Gentleman from N. Brantery, Preacht here. I went to mrs Wilmss, tarried all night. Patty Exceeding ill. Polly Bisbe & my Dat Hanh watcht.   at wido Wmss. mrs Voce & mrs Harris watcht. at Detoes. mrs Foot & Clarrisa Barton here.  
 

 

 August 11, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn; Showers aftern. I was at wido Wilmss all Day. we thought the Child was Expireing. the Neighbours Calld but it revivd. we administred Clysters prepard with English mallows & Camamile, which operated & relaxed the Bowels. I lift it Easy and returnd home at Eving. Hannah been to mrs Westons, Parthena & Dolly there Last Satterday to help her quillt a Bed quilt. [Guild] Partrage Came here.   at Ditos. I hear that old mrs Hutchens of winthrop Departed this Life Sudenly this Day.  
 

 

 August 13, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth of mr Fullers Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from mrs Williamss at 2h 30m to mr Fullers Lady in Travil. Shee was Safe Deld at 1h pm of a Son. I returnd home at 4 & find mrs Polly White here. Shee Slept here last night. mr Fuller paid me 6/ as a fee, gave me 5 Bowls. Extraidinary for medisin. Patty Williamss fitts on her.   at Ditos. Polly White left here. Parthena is 17 years old this Day.  
 

 

 August 14, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to wido Williams, tarried all night. her Child very ill. old mrs Fletcher & Sally & Polly tillton there.   at Detos. Set up.  
 

 

 August 15, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. I Came home at the 5thh, went to Sleep at the 8th & to wdos at 1 pm, found mrs Cumings there. Patty Expird at 5h pm. I put on her Grave Cloaths with the asistance of mrs Cumings & mrs Hanh mc Kathny. mrs Bullin & mrs Lois Harris are there.   at Ditos. Pally Expird at 5h pm. Aged 8 months & 17 days & 10 hours old.  
 

 

 August 18, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Revd mr Foster & Lady and wido Williams & mrs Lois Harris Dind with us. mrs Rheed & mrs Blakman Calld here. mrs Dorkis Pollard here to warp a piece. mrs Savage here; says James is unwell. Jona been to Pitts Town for a pair of Shoes.   I have been at home.  
 

 

 August 21, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard gone to mr Carrs. I went to mr Savages; he Came home his face very much brused & Sweld. I Came home, had News that his Coal Pitt was like to burn up. I rode to the Neighbors below to Call asistance to putt it out. Thomas Smyley Sleeps here. Hannah is at wido Williams.   at mr Savages & wido Williamss.
