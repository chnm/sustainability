James Manning
                             & Co_ 18th October
                             Recd 20th_1796

Samuel Hodgdon Esq.

           Merchant
         Philadelphia
Samuel Hodgdon.                             New York October. 18th. 1796
Sir,

    We are favoured with yours

of the 15th. Instant_ The last draft on Latting &
Deal was paid some days ago_ We should
have remitted it before now, but the indisposition
of our G.M. prevented our obtaining a small
account for freight of goods to the Indians_
We annex an account at foot. by which it
appears there is a balance in your favor of 239 &
23/100 Dollars for which we have drawn in
Your favour on John Barnes at three Days sight.

                    We are
                          Sir,
                          Your Ob.Servants
                            James Manning & Co.

D. Saml. Hodgdon Esqr. in acct. with Jas. Manning & Co.
