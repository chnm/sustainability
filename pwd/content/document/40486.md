Cumberland County SS I do certify that Ephraim Steel before whom the wether power of Attorney was acknowledged was at the time of said acknowledgement and now is a Justice of the peace and of the Court of Common pleas for the said County and that due faith and credit is and ought to be given as well in Court of Justice as thereout
In Testimony whereof I have hereunt set my Hand and the peablse seal of said County the 24th day of March [undecipherable] 1790
