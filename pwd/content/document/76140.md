War Department, 26 June 1800.
Sir
I enclose a Copy of a Letter received this morning from Capt.t William MacRea of the Second Regiment of Artillerists and Engineers.
I am of opinion that his request of an advance of pay and subsistence is reasonable and ought to be granted: you will therefore be pleased to cause the porper measures to be taken to that effect, as soon as possible.  Capt.t MacRea states that he has forwarded his accounts for Settlement.

 I am SirYour most obedt Serv.tSaml Dexter
The Paymaster General
Copy City of Washington25th June, 1800.
