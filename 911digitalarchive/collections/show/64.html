EMT pictures at Ground Zero

Title

EMT pictures at Ground Zero

Description

This collection of photos were taken by an EMT who worked at Ground Zero in the days following the attacks on the World Trade Center Towers. The images show the collapsed buildings and various EMT workers in their efforts to help.
