Putting the clips in the sequence of D-B-C-A creates the illusion of people going on a train trip to see a river gorge. But if you look closely, you can see that the clips are largely unrelated, and that, for example, clips D & B are actually from a New York City subway (a favorite early film subject). Since the New York City subways did not include scenic river overlooks in their routes, the edited film presents a fiction that seems very real.
