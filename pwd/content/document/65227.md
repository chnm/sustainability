  \ country for the winter (apparently for private business) and will return in the spring.  In the meantime, Colonel John Morrow will be in command.  Langham states that the establishment at Harper\x92\
  s Ferry seems to be prospering and that he would like to remain as superintendent after the plan is ready for completion of public stores.  Langham has no objection to quitting his western pursuits, but\
  \ until then he must continue his pursuit of private business out of necessity, though ensuring the public business is not neglected.  Langham has made this known to the Secretary of War."
