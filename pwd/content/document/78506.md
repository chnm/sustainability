General Thomas Clarke

             

                     Point Repose



                            nc.
Wilmington August 8.th 1780

  Sir

            P dy your letter of the 6th July I find
  the Pay Master General has charged your with 18000 Dollars
  delivered Mr. Slade by your order in December 1779
  as Pay due the First Regiment North Carolina Continental
  Troops,  I then acted as Pay Master to the said Regiment
  in the absence of Capt. Read. and as acknowledge to have 
  Received at Philadelphia of Mr. Slade in December 1779
  the said sum of 18000 Continental Dollars, which he
  received from the Pay Master General by your order for
  the use of the Officers and Soldiers of the said first
  Regiment

                              I am Sir with Esteem
                                 Your Ob.t Serv
 General Tho.s Clark                Tho.s Callender
