War Office
February 1 [17]98
Cloathing to be forwd to Lt Pope at the Natchez
War Office February 1, 1798
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to Samuel Hodgdon Esqr for the detachment under Lieut Pope at the Natchez - one Sergeants and one privates suits of Artillerists Clothing, also Infantry clothing complete for two Sergeants two Music and thirty four privates
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
