Tim.o Dwight
July 25. 1786
No 18 No 69


of no Signification M


Mr Pierce of A.G.A Accts
Mr Whitmore is earnestly
requested to forward this
immediately by his
very good Friend T Dwight


New York


from Timothy Dwight
recd 29 July 1786
J Howell Jr
The United States of No America in an Acct with Timothy Dwight
Dr To Depreciation of my pay as Chaplain By Ballance
to a Brigade in the Federal Army during the
whole time of my Service from Sept 1 1777
to Match 1 1779.
Greconfd in Connecticut July 15 1786

To Mr Pierce Paymaster & Auditor General of Army Acompts 
Timothy Dwight
