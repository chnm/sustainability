John Kunell
affidavit 13th
May 1788
On Tuesday the thirteenth day of May 1788
personally appeared before me, one of the Alderman
of the city of New York John Kemble who
being duly sworn on the holdy Evangelists
deposeth and saith that on or about the 
night of the thirteenth of July 1787 a certain 
brown bay horse marked with a saddle mark
on the rear side both his hind feet white
the property of John Story Esquire was
stolen from the pasture of the said deponens
by a person or persons unknown and further
this deponent saith not.




 Sworn on the above
Date before me
