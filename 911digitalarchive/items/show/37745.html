September 11 Email: Body


9/11/01 11:49
J - I am trying to get in touch with a # of ppl. Obviously there are no phone lines...please email me back. I just landed in St Louis from Newark and am in shock...as I am sure all of you are. Prayers...

12:35
C - You have no idea what chaos is going on here right now. My uncle is OK and so is one of my cousins. My other cousin is still unaccounted for...work has just closed down. The building is under lock down. I am about to kneel down and pray for my friends that were in the WTC...and we have heard no word yet. I have never seen anything like this in my life and people are pretty freaked...

12:47
J - just nauseous and loss. It is truly unbelievable. My prayers are with your family and friends. There will not be a person in the world not effected by this. If anything significant happens or occurs, let me know. I landed about 20 minutes after it happened. I am still in shock. Talk to you in a little while.

13:18
C - My two friends are OK...one in St Vincents with burns [which was later found out to be a rumor. This friend perished on the 105th floor] and that's all the info we have so far. Still one cousin missing. Trying to figure out where to stay tonight...dude, you gotta listen to the news and hear the shit/commentary that's going on. Hey - I hope your friends et al are all OK as well. Don't know where I'll be tonight but be careful traveling...I tried your cell phone and to be honest, there aren't a ton of things working here right now - phones, TV, subways, etc.

14:12
J - Hey - Call J if you need a place to stay. They have tons of room and you can walk there. My cell is on, but hard to get through. Let me know if I can do anything from here but I am a little freaked and by myself and can't reach most people.
