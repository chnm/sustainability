Letter
from Rob Bentham
Recd Nov. 12 1791
[stamp] Received from State Dep't. 
Nov. 24 1894
R.P.O.401310
To Coln Hodgden QMG
Dear Sir,
I understand that the publick horsis Lays in my Corn feald avery night and that must bee attended with Consedarbel Loss to me - I make no dout but the peopel ho hes the Charge of them Don't Cair aney thing about it or thay would have tolde me of it -If I was abel to get out of the hous It should not bee the Cais - So I have nobody but you to apply to ho gesheel will not alow of aney such on justice - 
from your friend and all ways Ready to serve - 
Robert Ben
To Coln Hodgdon LMG
