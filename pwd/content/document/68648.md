War Office
June 12 1799.
Cloathing for 16th Regt
War department June. 12. 1799
Sir,
	Be pleased to deliver to John Wilkins Jun. Esquire Quarter Master General to be transported to New York to the address of Colonel Ebenezer Stevens forty four Sergeants twenty two Musicians and – six hundred and - forty suits of Clothing for the Sixteenth Regiment of Infantry –
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr John Harris
