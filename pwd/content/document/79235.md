No. 7 
James S Deblois Purser of
Constitution frigate
At Sea 18 August 1798
Recd from Office of the
Secy of the Navy
29 Sept. 1798
Frigate Constitution 87
at Sea Augt 18.1798

Honble Secy of the Navy.
Sir
I presume by this Conveyanace the recruiting Officers for this Ship will forward you their Accounts for the expenditure of moneys they have reced from me for the recruiting service and presuming you might have occasion for my Acct. with the US, I take the liberty of inclosing it. You will please observe I have some time since forwarded to the Honble Secy of War duplicate rects tp the amo of 2850 Dollars if you wish the other duplicate rects. please inform me. I request you will please direct a list of prices to the Shops I recd from H. Jackson Esqr. Boston to be forwarded me as I may thereby be enabled to Charge those who take any up. I solicit the favour of you to forward me a few Copies of the Rules & regulations of our Navy. There is but one Copy on board, and that belongs to the Captain who will not suffer it to go out of his hands. I never but once have had an oppy of reading them which is the Case of most of the Officers on board.
I have the Honor to be
Yr Obedt Servt
Jas S Deblois
