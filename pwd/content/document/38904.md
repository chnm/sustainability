New York Sep. 4. 1787
Sir
Your letter of 17. ulto is Recd and I am to acquaint you that you have been made up in the Invalid Corps for your pay to 22 June 1781 when on which day you are returned as discharged from the Regt the Balance which was due to you as an invalid was 99.15/90 dollars and has been paid by Colonel Nicola, to your order in favor of Richard Murthwaite. -- you are returned also as having joined the invalids from the 10 Penna Regt
[Stamped "National Archives of the United States"]
Francis Mulls
Philadephia
