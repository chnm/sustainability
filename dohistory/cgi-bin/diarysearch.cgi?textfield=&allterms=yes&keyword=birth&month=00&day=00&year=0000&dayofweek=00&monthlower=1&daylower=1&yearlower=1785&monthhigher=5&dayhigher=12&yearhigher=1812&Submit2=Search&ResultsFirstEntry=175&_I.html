 January 25, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I Came home on the Slead, fited Some things for Lidia Savage; Sent Dolly with them. mr Ballard is gone to See Thoms Sewall to Converse with him Concerning ills he is acused of Spreading of the Revd mr Foster, he returnd with out any Satisfaction.    
 

 

 January 26, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth. Nathan Swettland Son. X.

I was Calld to Nathan Swettlands wife at 1/O Clok this morn, found her Safe Deliverd of a Son about 2. James Hinkleys wife operator. I returnd home at ten before noon. it Snows. I receivd 6/ of mr Swettland for Trouble & medicin. ys is her third Child. I was Calld at Dusk to mr Savages to See Lidia who is very Sick. Set up with her all night, mrs Chamberlin also.    
 

 

 January 30, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleast. I was Calld to see mr Savages Dafter, find her very Low. Set up with her all night. Shee pukt up a considerable quantity of phlegm and Seems Some revivd.    
 

 

 February 1, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. Son Town went home. I was Calld to mr Savages, gave Lidia some Senna. it Snowd at Evinng. I Set up all night.    
 

 

 February 2, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Benn Blaks Dafter. X.

Clear. I Came home & Dolly went up. mr Ballard unwell. Jono & Dicky gone to the meddow. I went to Bed at 7 & was Calld at 10 to go to mrs Blake in travil. Shee was Deliverd of a Dafter half after 11 [in] Evnn. I tarried all night. its very cold.    
 

 

 February 8, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX Birth. James Savages Son. XX.

I was Calld half after Six this morn to James Savages wife in Travil. Shee was Safely Deliverd at Six Eving of a fine Son, her first Child. it has been Severe Cold, Snowd at night. I tarried all night.    
 

 

 February 9, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home, Rode on a Slead. was Calld to see mr Porters Infant, it being unwell, gave it some Senna.    
 

 

 February 10, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See Seth Williamss youngest Child & Georg Browns Children. Calld at Porters, his Child is Better.    
 

 

 February 14, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd before Sun Sett. I was unwell, my throat Sore, went to Bed at nine. was Calld at half after ten By mr Read to see his wife in Travil. it raind fast as I went & almost all night. old mrs Savage & mrs Stone were attendants.    
 

 

 February 15, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Reads Son. X.

mrs Read was Deliverd of a Son at four this morn. it Cleard of & is very pleasant. I Calld to See mrs Savage, find her poorly. mrs Shaw, Shee remains Low. I returnd home at half after Ten ys morn, receivd 6/ of mr Read as fee. 2/ remains X Due for medicin.    
 

 

 February 18, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to meeting aftern. mr Floid Brot a note for thanks for his wives Delivry. I was Calld in to See mr Childs. mrs Becky Wiman here for Some tincture for him. mr Howard here from Sebastacuk.    
 

 

 February 20, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 A Snow Storm. I was Calld to See mrs Reed, am unfitt to go out, But went. find her Exercised with Some pain & Sore throat. returnd at Dusk, feel unwell. I have Seen 52 years this Day.    
 

 

 February 24, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Elias Taylors Daughter. XX I recieved nothing.

Clear & pleast. I was Calld about Sun rise to Go to See Elias Taylors wife in travil. met a mesage informing Shee was Safely Delivd. returnd home & feel more Comfortable than I have Some Days past. Pamela & Dolly went to See mrs Foster, Shee is unwell. mr Hersey here this Eving.    
 

 

 February 25, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some Snow. I was Calld to See Joseph Fosters wife & Daughter, find them poorly. returnd & found Ephm Lambd.    
 

 

 February 26, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & warm. Revd mr Foster here, Says his Lady is unwell. Deacon Emerson & mr Woodward here also. mr Ballard been to gett mr Savage to asist makeing a Kirb for mr Fosters well. I have been at home.    
 

 

 March 3, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. I finisht mrs Fosters [dress], helpt Brue & went to See mrs Williams aftern. Shee paid me 6/ for attending her at the Birth of her Daughter. it Snowd before morng & was windy. 36th Snow.    
 

 

 March 6, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. widow Mary Shepherds Dafter. X.

A Cold morn. I was Calld to See the widow Mary Shepherd in Travil. Left home at five, put her Safe to Bed with a Daughter about 4 PM. returnd home at 8 Evinng. mr Ballard been to mr Stephens and to the Hook. it Snowd Some. mr Winslow moovd this Day passt by mr Hainses.    
 

 

 March 8, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Jono & ye Girls Sett out for winslow at 9h & 30m this morn. Revd mr Foster & Lady Dind here Esqr Petingale & Asa Williams also. informs his wife was Delivrd of a Dafter the 1st inst. Rufus Ballard came here.    
 

 

 March 22, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Swetts Dafter. X.

observd as a day of humiliation & prayer. I went to the house of worship. was Calld out by mr Swett at ye Conclution of ye first prayer. went to the Hook on the ice. put his wife to Bed safe with a Daughter & Slept at mr Duttens.    
 

 

 March 26, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Ephm had a fit.

Clear & Cool. Ephm had a Fitt. Doct Coney Calld to See him. mr Ballard been at mr Pollards with ye other Select gent men.    
 

 

 April 19, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Very Cold. Sherebiah Town Came here for ma to go to Son Towns, Their youngest Child being sick. I had mr Wimans hors, we arivd there at Dark. the Child very ill.    
 

 

 April 22, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Welmans Son. X.

I was Calld to mr Welmans at 9 ys morn, his wife Safe Delivd at 7 Evn of a Son. I tarried all night, watcht. Capt Savages wife there. It raind ys Evinng.    
 

 

 April 26, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Nathan Tylors Dafter. XX. Receivd nothing.

I was Calld at 2 O Clok to Nathan Taylors wife in Travil, found her Dilivrd of a Dafter. Doct Williams operator. very rainy. I tarried till morn then Sett out on foot, walkt to the Bridg Below mr Kenidays when Tylor Came up with a hors. I rode home, arivd at 7 much fatagud. Jono & Saml Dicky had a quarrel; what a Shame it is. CapT Nichols & his Son Jonathan here this morn going to CobessiContee.    
 

 

 May 3, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX Birth. Charls Cleark, Second Dafter. XX. fee receivd Oct 18th, 1788.

Clear norn. I was Calld at 6/O Clok this morn to Charls Clearks wife who is in Travil & Shee was Safe Deliverd of a Dafter at one PM. I returnd home at 5. the girls have washt & Scoured. mr Stephens Brot Pamela home. We had Pork of Jonathan.
