description: General Elijah Clarke has illegally formed a military corps, under the banner of the United States, to set upon an expedition against the Spanish Floridas, a nation whom are at peace with the
  United States. Governor George Mathews of Georgia has already issued a proclamation against the action, but Knox believes it may be necessary to punish the actors. President Washington wants Mathews to
  take "the most energetic and decisive measures within your power, for suppressing the said design." Assures him that the United States will fund any action taken by the Georgia militia for accomplishing
  this.
