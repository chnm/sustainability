Thomas Proctor's Receipt
June 19th, 1792
[Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894. R.P.O. 401310.]
Rec'd June 19th 1792 from Mr. Wm. Knox, Clerk of Military stores, forty seven 10 pds Sixty Tubes - and six portfires - or also Standard and Eighty Eight pounds of Cannon powder: by order of the secretary at War for the Celebration of the 4th of July ensuing.  
Thomas Procter
