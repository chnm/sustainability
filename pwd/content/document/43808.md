Novem 10 
10/9th
Recd
Savannah, November 10th, 1792

Sir,

                                                        I have to acknowledge the receipt

of your letter dated the 29th of September last, inclosing Invoice
and paid bill of lading for one box of Shoes for the Troops in this
State, which was received in good order.

                                                        I am, Sir,
                                                        Your obedient Servant
                                                         John Habersham.
                                                          Agent for the supply of the Troops
                                                           of the U. States in Georgia.


Samuel Hodgon, Esq
