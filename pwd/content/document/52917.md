Department of War.

Richard Harrison Esq-. Accountants' Office Auditor of the Treasury - February 19th 1796-

                          Sir
                                 Major Isaac Craig has rendered an amount to 

this office for Compensation as Agent to this Department at Pittsburg. - I have there fore to request you will please to furnish me with information of the sum he receives for Compensation in the Quarter Masters Department - which will govern in the allowance to be made him here.-

                                            I am Sir (indecipherable)
                                                 William Simmons
