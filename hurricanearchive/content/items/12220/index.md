Where am I now?\r\n	I am emotionally drained from hearing nothing but things about Katrina.  It\'s always Katrina this Katrina that. All people talk about is what happened to them, it\'s not that I really don\'t care; it\'s just that I don\'t want to hear about it anymore.  Katrina passed through a year ago and I still hear stuff about it, I just want it to stop.  Now I know people who live in New Orleans still haven\'t got all there money, but if it\'s taking this long to get your money why don\'t they get some neighbors to come and help do some of the gutting or painting of the walls or something.  My house, which is in Slidell, has been repaired and I think that it might be hard for people, but I also think that it\'s time for people to move on with their lives. \r\n
