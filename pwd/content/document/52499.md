War department
Absalom Baird Esq.


[Note along right margin:] War Dept. Jan. 1796 Discharge sent
War Office January 6. 1796
Sir
The peace made with the Indians North West
of the Ohio, and the universal quiet on our frontiers, renders
it unnecessary to keep up any militia Guards or Scouts;
you will therefore be pleased to discharge immediately
those employed under your direction, and transmit to
this office the necessary documents for a final settlement
of their compensations__
I am Sir
Your obedt. Servant
Timothy Pickering


Absalom Baird Esq
Brigade Inspector
Washington County
