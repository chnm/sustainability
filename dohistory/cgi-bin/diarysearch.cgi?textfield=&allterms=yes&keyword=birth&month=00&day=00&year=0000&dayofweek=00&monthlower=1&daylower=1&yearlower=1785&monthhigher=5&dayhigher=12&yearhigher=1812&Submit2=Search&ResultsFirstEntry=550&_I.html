 February 18, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Benn Prescotts Son. XX.

a remarcable Pleasant Day. I put mrs Prescott to Bed at 5h 30 m Evn with a fine Son. Shee has been very ill indead, but I left her in hopefull Sircumstance & returnd home at half afler 10. Shuball Pitts here.   at Dittoes. Birth 8th. first Born.  
 

 

 February 19, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleasant. I was Calld to See mrs Chamberlin who is Exercisd with pain in her Back & Limbs. I went to See mrs Savage allso. mrs Bolton was there. my girls washt.   I was at mr Chamberlins & Savages.  
 

 

 February 20, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X

 Clear & very Pleasant. I was again Calld to See mrs Chamberlin. Shee remains very much Exercised with pain. I adminestred a Clyster which gave great relief. I went to See mrs Bolton; dind there. I finisht footing a pair of Stockins for Polly Town. I have livd to See 55 years this day.   I was at mr Chamlns. this is my Bir thDay; 55 years old.  
 

 

 February 23, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Capt Belchers Son. XX

Clear & very pleasant. I was Calld by Harshorn Coney at 1 h morn to See Capt Belchers Lady in travil. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 2 hours & 55 m of a very fine Son which Shee Calls Hyrum. the Capt was absent; left home Bound for Sandy river yester morn. I relurnd at ye 11 Hour attended by Saml Coney. this is mrs Belchers 4th Son & 8th Child. we receivd a Letter from Doct Barton of Jany 15/90 which informs our friends are well. I Slept a nap ys aftern.   at Capt Belchers. we recd a letter from Doct Barton of Jany 15, 1790. Birth 9th. 
 

 

 February 26, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleasant. Hannah was unwell this forenn. I attended a Lecter at the meeting house aftn. Revd mr Eaton & Parson Emerson performd. I Calld at mr Pollards; was informd that Sherebiah Town was Declineing very fast; he thinks he Shall not Continue Long in this world. I allso hear that Luke Barton was thought to be Expireing this morn.   I went to Lecter & to mr Hamlins.  
 

 

 February 27, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 I was Calld at 1 hour morn to See the wife of Henry Badcok. mrs Tolman & I Spent the day with her. mrs Smith & her marm Came at Dusk. we had not much Sleep.   at mr Badcoks.  
 

 

 February 28, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Henry Badcoks Dagt. XX.

Clear & pleasant. mrs Badcok Calld mrs Bisbee at 1h pm & was Safe Delivrd at 4 of a dagt, her fifth Child; the first a Son. I Left her & infant Cleverly at ye 6th hour. arivd at ye 7th. my Dagts gone to mr Hamlins. Jon a is gone to See Luke Bartons; returnd at Evn. informs that he is no better.   at Dittoes. Birth 10th.  
 

 

 March 4, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Savage Boltons 3d Son & 4th Child. XX

Clear & very Cold & windy. Suse went from here. I feel very unwell all Day; was Calld after Sun Sett to See mrs Bolton. Shee was Safe Delivrd at midnight of a fine Son. I left her Cleverly & returnd home at 4 O Clok. Jona is 27 years old ys Day.   at Savage Boltons. Jona is 27 years old ys Day. Birth 11th. 
 

 

 March 6, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd fore n. I was Calld to See Nancy Savage. Shee had the Collic. we administred an Oil Clister which Gave relief. I returnd at 1h pm, then went to Colo Sewalls to get his Lady to make me a Bonnet; to mr Crosbys Store. Bot 2-1/4 yds Gause @ 1/10, 1 paper of pins @ /9, 2 yds Ribbin @ 2/5. was informd by mr Redington that Luke Bartons life was not Expected. I Calld at mr Pollards. Shee informs Son Towns famely were well last Wedensday. Biah was very Low indeed.   at mr Savages to See Nancy. it Snowd. 
 

 

 March 7, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some Snow. I was Calld by Savage Bolton to See his wife who was Exercised with pain. I went & administerd Some remedies which gave relief; returnd at 2h pm. Cyrus went after Parthena; Brot her home at Dusk.   at mr Boltons. Death mrs Hart last Evn g. 
 

 

 March 11, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Walkers Son. XX.

I was Called at ye 6th hour ys morn to See mrs Walker at ye hook. Shee was sprigh about house till 11; was Safe Delivrd at 12 h & 15m of a fine Son. I returnd at 5 h pm. We had a very Sever Snow Storm. Doct Williams Sleeps here. mr Ballard was informd that Brothr Town has lost his Cow Since he left home.   I was at mr Wakers. Birth 12th. Death of Luke Barton; he Expird at 11 Evng. 
 

 

 March 12, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. Doct Williams was Calld to See Phile Petengail. he returnd & Sleeps here; informs his patient has got the Shingles.   I have been at home.  
 

 

 March 22, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Diah Prescotts Son & [ ] Child. XX

Snow. mr Bullin & his Dagt Pally here. I was Calld to see the wife of Jedediah Prescott of Winthrop at 1 h pm by Stephen Pullin; he was 2 & 1/2 hours from home till his return with me. the good Lady was Safe Delivrd at 10 of a fine Son. I tarried all night.   at mr diah Prescotts, Winthrop. Birth 13th.  
 

 

 March 23, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I Came from mr Prescotts after Breakfast. mett mr Ballard on his way to Unity to Survey ye river. I arivd at home at noon. mr Prescott Dind here. he let me have 5 pecks of Rie for my service asisting his Lady. mr Livermore & Jesse Bullin took Tea here. I have been to see Neigr Savages folks; find them poorly with a Cold. mrs Patty left here. I am informd that mrs Mason Expird the 21st inst & her remains were Carried to Varsalboro for interment this Day.   at mr Prescotts & Savages.  
 

 

 March 28, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Burjes who has the Collic. mrs Cragg was in Travil. I was Detaind there all night. mrs Braly Sent for me. they Could not Consint I [Shd] go.   at mr Craggs.  
 

 

 March 29, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. at mr Craggs. Shee lingers. mrs Braly Deld of a Dagt.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 March 30, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Cragg had her women; her travil Comes on gradually. I was Calld to Andw Goodwins, his wife in Labour. was Calld to Craggs again at mid night.   at Detoes & mr Goodwins.  
 

 

 March 31, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Craggs Dagt. X

mrs Craggs illness increast and Shee was Safe Delivrd of a very fine Dagt at 2h 30 m pm. I left her as well as Could be Expected & returnd to mr Goodwins at Evng. receivd 6/8 of mr Cragg. I was very much fatagud but had time to rest.   at Ditoes. Birth 14th. first Born.  
 

 

 April 1, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 at mr Goodwins; am So much overcome with my illness & falague, I am but just able to walk the room. mrs Goodn is very unwell.   at Goodns. 
 

 

 April 4, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Andw Goodwins Son. X

Cloudy. mrs Goodin Calld her women & was Safe Delivrd of a fine Son at 12 O Clok & is Cleverly. I watch with her & feel much better than I have Done.   at Dittoes. Birth 15. my Son Jona has Seen 27 years ys Day.  
 

 

 April 5, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 a little rain. I left mrs Goodwin & Child Cleverly. Calld to See mrs Cragg. Shee is not So well as I Could wish. arivd at home at ye 10 h morn, find Foster is returnd. I went to mr Craggs, administred a Clister of milk,water & Salt, applyd ointmint & a Bath of Tansey, mugwort, Cammomile & Hysop, which gave mrs Cragg great relief. Calld at mr Savages, gave Nancy a Dose of Salt & water which Causd her to puke. Shee threw up two worms and seemd Easier & I Came home.   at Ditoes & mr Craggs. receivd 6/ & 1 yd of gaus of Goodin. at mr Craggs & Savages. Capt Sewall, Carr & Goodin Chose into the ofis of Select men.  
 

 

 April 9, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to Saml Cumingss & paid her 18/ which was her Due for Cloath I had of her, & returnd a yard that was left. I Calld at mr Hamlins, Burgess & mr Craggs. Dorcas Pollard & Alice Burges here on a visit.   at Saml Cumings & mr Craggs.  
 

 

 April 11, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was Calld to see mrs Cragg who is Exceeding ill. Shee has taken 2 potions of rhubarb & thee bark allso Since friday noon. the phisic opperated till Shee Expird. I tarried & walcht. Sett up alone. Shee began to Puke at 4 h morn.   at mr Craggs.  
 

 

 April 12, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I took a little nap ys morn. Doct Coney was Calld; plainly told the famely mrs Cragg must Die. Shee acordingly did Expire at 10 h Evn with out the lest Struggle. mrs Pollard & my Self & mrs Voce put on her Grave Cloaths. mr Pollard & his Lady tarried all night. mrs Voce & my Self allso.   at Ditoes, his wife Expird. Sally While Came to giv the infant Suck.  
 

 

 April 13, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleasant. I tarried at mr Craggs & asisted what I was able. my Dagt Dolly washt there, Judy Huston allso. mr Black & mr Bent Sett there this night. the Corps were Coffind & Sett in the west room. purge & Smell very ofensive.   at Dittoes. tarried all night.
