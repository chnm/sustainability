Charlott July 27th1781 --

67,600

 Recd from John Pierce ["Paymaster General" written above name "John Pierce"] asquire by the hands of John White Escort from the Board of war, sixty-seven thousand and six hundred dollars of the New Emition which I promis to deliver to Joseph Clay Esq. or General Green or either of their orders with three duplicates which I promise to deliver at the same time having tow recets of the same Tenor & Date A.D. Q. M.
                                            Wm S. Alexander

turn over [these words are encircled at the bottom of the page.] 
                Office of Army Accounts November 28th.. 1786

Testify that the above is a true Copy taken from the original

                                     signed J. Pierce --- Ecd
