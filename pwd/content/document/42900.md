Letter from the War Off
May 7th 1792
________________________
to purchase two horses &
two Saddles & Bridles
________________________
Mr. Wm. Knox
War-department,
May 7th 1792.
Sir.
The Secretary of War requests that you will immediately purchase for the Service of the United States, two
Strong, able bodied, cheap horses-(not of the first rate)-and
two common Saddles + bridles__ Before you contract
for them, it will be well for you to call at this office;
and let us know the price____
I am
Sir
Your humble Servt.
[undecipherable] Stagg [undecipherable]  

Mr. Wm. Knox__
