The National Museum of American History has a long history of collecting materials associated with elections and voting. The contested 2000 presidential election peaked the national interest in questions associated with voting technologies. Explore these materials to discover how the 1898 Standard Voting Machine entered the museum's collection and the ways that the curators have considered displaying the object.

Place your mouse over an individual item to get the title of the item. Click on the icon to access the item and brief annotation.
