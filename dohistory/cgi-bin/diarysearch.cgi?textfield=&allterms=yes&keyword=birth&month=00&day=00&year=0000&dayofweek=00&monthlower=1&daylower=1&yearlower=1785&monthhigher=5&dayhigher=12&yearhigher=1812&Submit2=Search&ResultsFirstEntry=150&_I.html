 November 5, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XXX Birth. Capt Springers Daft.

A Chilly air. I attinded worship. was Calld about Sun Sett to Capt Springers wife in Travil. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Dafter at 10 O Clok ys Evenng. I Sett up with her all night.    
 

 

 November 6, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear morn, Cloudy afternn. I Came home from mr Springers. Left them Cleverly arivd at home at 12 O Clok. was Calld at 2 PM to mr Woodwards to See mr Blakman who is unwell. Came home at 6. the girls went to mr Westons. mr Ballard went to ye hook foren, to Revd mr Fosters aftern. Clarisa Barton here.    
 

 

 November 8, 1786, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 I have been at home. ye girls washt. mrs Savage & Kitty & mrs Woodward here. I was Seisd with the Collic in the night, had a Severe Shock. Hannah Calld up to tend me.    
 

 

 November 13, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Isaac Hardins Son. X

Clear. I was Calld to See Isaac Hardings wife in Travil. Shee was Safe Delivrd a 3 / OClok PM of a Son. the remains of Obediah Harris were inter[d] this aftern. I Stay at Hardins ys night.    
 

 

 November 14, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. I Came home from Hardins at 1 OClok, was iminediately Calld to See mrs Weston, Shee being very unwell. I aplyd a Blister, Batht her feet, put on a Back Plaster, returnd home at Evinng. Left her a Little Easyer. left mrs Hardin Comfortable. Shee was helpt up & her Bed made. my girls helpt mrs Pollard quilt Bed quilt. Lidia Bisbe was here after noone.    
 

 

 November 23, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Williams, her Breast is Soar. Bunker Ferwell there. I Saw a hors fall in to the river three times by ye fort wharf. the 4th attempt they got him over the river. a Snow fell this Night.    
 

 

 November 26, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. mr Blakmans Son. X

A bitter Cold morn. I rose at day break, had a Shaking fitt & pain in my head. did not attend public worship. Pamele Staid at home. I was Calld to See mrs Blakman in Travil. left home at Sun Sett, got there after much fatagud. found her put Safe to Bed by mrs Ellis, tarried all night. mr Blakman gave me 6/8.    
 

 

 November 27, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Came home at 11/ O Clok. Left all Cleaverly. I went to Capt Sualls and Thomas Sualls & Revd mr Fosters spent Evinng at Esqr Howards. mr Ballard Came yr to accompany me home.    
 

 

 November 28, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Exceeding Cold & windy. mr Georg Brown here, has froos Both his Ears. I put some Salve on them. Pamela is 18 years old this Day. we have all Been mending old garments. Welches wife Delivrd of a Dafter the 19th inst. mrs Fletcher operator.    
 

 

 December 14, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Thanksgiving Day. I went to meeting & was Calld to Colon Howards to ascist mrs Pollard Lay out his Son James. Mrs Betsy, Polly & Jack are very Sick. I watcht. Doct Colman unwel. Death of James Howard.    
 

 

 December 16, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleasant till towards Evinng. I have been very unwell, attended with a Collick pain, not able to perform any Business. mr Ballard Gone to the meddow after Hay. Ephm Been over ye pond for wood. my husband got a Load of hay, Came home much fatagud. brusd his Shins with the Crust So that they Blistered. I hear that Esqr Howard had an ill turn Last night.    
 

 

 December 18, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Ephm had a Severe fitt.

Clear & pleast. I am free from the Collic pain. we are informd of Jacky Howards Death. I went to attend funeral, but by reason of Esqr Howards being taken very ill, I was Calld in there & tarried with them till about 9 O Clok when Cyrus came there; informd yt Ephm was in a fitt. I returnd home in great hast Led by Cyrus & York. found my Little Son revivd. Left ye Esqr more Comfortable, Betsy also.    
 

 

 December 21, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I was Calld to See Moses Pollard, he being unwell. Saw Sherebh Town, Sons family well. Revd mr Foster & Lady Dind there. I Drank Tea with mrs Weston. mr Ballard been to mr Fosters ys Evinng.    
 

 

 December 28, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. mrs X Bents Son. X

Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Weston, She Being unwell. was Calld from there to mr Jacksons, to mrs Bent in Travil. her Child Born before I arivd. I put her Safe to Bed. Set with her this night.    
 

 

 December 30, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Snowd before night. I went to mr Porters & Pollards. David just arivd. mr Ballard gone to Cutt wood for mr Foster. Williams & True Dresing Flax here. I was Calld to Capt Sualls wife in Travil, got there at 7 O Clock Evinng.    
 

 

 December 31, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Capt X Harry Sewalls Son. fee Recvd. X.

a fogy Day. mrs Suall Delivard at 1 / ys morn of a Son & is Cleaverly. I returnd home at 8 ys morn. Did not atlend worship. Son & Dafter Town, Sherebiah, Ezra & Lucy here. mr Faught & Clarisa Barton supt here. Saml Badcock wife to Bed with a Son the 29th of January. mrs Fletcher operator.    
 

 

 January 6, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cold. I was Calld to see [Billy] James Savage who is Sick. Lucy & Hannah went to mr Pollards.    
 

 

 January 8, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cold. Capt Savages wife here. I was Calld to See mrs Savage & Jamey, mr Bullin Came there & brought me & Lucy home; Carried her home with him. I Sett up with her Child till 12 O Clok.    
 

 

 January 12, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 a snow Storm. I was Calld to mr Savages to See his Childn that were Sick. Doct Coney there. mr Bullin & wife went from here. I stayd at Savgs & watcht. Son Town Came here, his Childn Been unwell. at home.    
 

 

 January 14, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Daughter Town & I tard at home. mr Town & Howard & Sisters Came here. I was Calld to Savages, mr Balld went with me. Neighr Willms & wife, & Betsy Thomas here.    
 

 

 January 15, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Son Town Left here. it raind. the young folks rode out, I was Calld to Savages. Set up all night, the Childn very [peavish].    
 

 

 January 18, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. we Began to make Soap. Revd mr Foster here, going to attend funeral of Nathanl Runels Child. Desired me to go and See his Lady & Child who were unwell; I went. I was Calld to see mr Hodges [      ] Ballard Came home with me.[      ] we found James & Eunice Stackpool here, [Echabod Pitts] also.
