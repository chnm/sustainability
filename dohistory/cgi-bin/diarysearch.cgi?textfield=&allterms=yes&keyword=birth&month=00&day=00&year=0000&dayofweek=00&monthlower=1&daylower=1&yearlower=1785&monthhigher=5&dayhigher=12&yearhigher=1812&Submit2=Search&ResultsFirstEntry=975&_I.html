 October 11, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, fine Day till near night when a Sea turn Came on. Esqr Ebenz Haild & Lady, & mrs Strattne, Dagt Pollard & mrs Pitts Dind here. Dolly & Sally went to mr Pollards with them. I have been at home. mr Bradbury Sent me 6/ for assisting his wife Febry 13th.   at home. Esqr Haild & Lady here, & others.  
 

 

 October 12, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Dows 3d Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn, Clear aftn. I was Calld to See mrs Dow who is in Labour at 8h morn. Shee was Delivd of a fine Dagt at 11. I left her Cleverly at 6h Evn, receivd 6/ as a reward. Sally Densmore Sleeps here. Jacky Ballard here.   at mr Dows. Birth 41st. 
 

 

 October 16, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to James Moores, his wife unwell. mrs Tylar and mrs Welman there all night.   at James Moores.  
 

 

 October 17, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth [Alphs] XX Lyons Dagt XX.

Clear. mrs Moore is unwell but Consented yt I Should go to See mrs Lyon who was in labour & was Safe Deld at 8h Evn of a Dagt & I returnd to mr Moores, find her more unwell. mrs Tylor & Welman were Calld.   at Moores & Lyons. Birth 42nt. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 October 18, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Moores Dagt. X.

Clear. mrs Moore was Deld of a Dagt at 6h 30m. I left her and infant Comfortable at noon & returnd home, find my famely well; was much fatagud.   at moores. Birth 43d. receivd 6/9.  
 

 

 October 20, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd & raind ys foren, Clear aftern. mr Lee Preacht at the Hook but my famely Did not attend. mr Parker was here aftern, Exprest his Desire that I Should make his Lady a visit Soon. I have read 2 of Doct Prices Discourses ys Day.   at home. mr Parker here. Snowd ys morn.  
 

 

 October 21, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been picking wool. John Jones Calld here. Dolly gone to mr Wessons. my sons are Planking the Seller where it fell in yesterday.   at home. J. Jones here. I have made a Cheese.  
 

 

 October 27, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Nathl Shaws Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard & Sons went to meeting, mr Turner Performd. Sally went aftern. Augustus Ballard was brot here ys aftern. I was Calld at 8h Evn to See Nathl Shaws wife, Shee was Delivd at 11h 5m of a Son. I tarried all night.   at mr Shaws. Birth 44th. I receivd 6/.  
 

 

 October 28, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Timy Pages Son. XX.

Clear. I returnd from mr Shaws, left them Cleverly. find Agsts much as he was. Dolly is gone to Son Pollards. mr Bullin here ys Day. I was Calld aftern to mr Timy Pages wife who was Safe Deld at 7h Evn. I tarried all night, her Child was a Son which wd 11lb 1/4.   at Shaws & Timy Pages. Birth 45th, for ys year & 500th Since I Came to this Country.  
 

 

 October 29, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 A very Severe Snow Storm with a heavy wind from ye Noth. I tarried at mr Pages ys Day & night, mrs Coks allso. my patients Cleverly.   at mr Pages. Feby 94 receivd 3/7_ off mr Page.  
 

 

 November 7, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X.[Birth Reads Dagt]. XX.

Clear & very pleast. I was Calld at five O Clok morn to See mrs Read who was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 9. I returnd home at noone, left my Patients Cleverly; receivd 6/ as a reward. Son Jona, his wife & Childn all Supt with us. Pally & Jack Sleep here. mr Thomas waited on Dolly to mr Pollards.   at mr Reeds. Birth 46th.  
 

 

 November 8, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night when there was a light Shower. Cyrus Conducted Patty & Jacky home. the Ship Grounded near our Landing, morn, & Fleeted & went Down after Sun Sett. I have been at home. Rufus Ballard here this Day, informs me yt his Sister Lucy was Delivd of a Son last weak & is Comfortable. Augustus Seems Dull all Day.   at home.  
 

 

 November 10, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard, my Self & Childn attend worship at the hook. Revd mr Turner Discoarst from those words. God is a Spirrit. I Calld at Capt Meloys after Exercise. Dolly wrode home & Sent the hors back for me. Dagt Pollard & mrs Pitts & Capt Nichols here.   at Meeting.  
 

 

 November 11, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleasant. Dolly went to work at mr Hamlins. Georg Brown here. Andw mc Causlin Calld me to See his wife at Evn.   at Andw mc Causlands.  
 

 

 November 12, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Pleast. I was at Ditoes, & James Mc Causlands, my Patient, unwell with a Disentary.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 November 14, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & misty. I returnd home, left my patient more Comfortable, find my husband Confind with an ague in his face. Augustus is better. Dolly went to Benjamins, Sally to Shubal Hinkleys. I Calld to See mrs Daw & mrs Beamans Child.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 November 15, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Cold. mrs Holdman here to have a goun made. mrs Benjamin to have a Cloak Cut, Polly Rust after work. I was Calld to mr Parkers aftern. mr Ballard is better.   at mr Parkers. mrs Holdman here.  
 

 

 November 17, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Poores Dagt. XX.

rainy. I was Calld from mr Parkers at 2h morn to mr Poores. Doct Page was Calld before my arival. I Extracted the Child, a Dagt. he Chose to Close the Loin. I returnd home at 8 am, receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard & Ephm attend worship, Dolly & Sally aftern. Charls & John Coks Supt here. I was Calld to Capt meloys at 11h Evn. raind.   at Ditoes & mr Poores. Birth 47 th. A Daughtr at Capt Meloys allso.  
 

 

 November 18, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Meloys Dagt. XX.

at Capt Meloys, his Lady in Labour, her women Calld. (it was a Severe Storm of rain. Cleard of with Snow). my patient Deld at 8h 5m Evn of a fine Dagt, her attendants, mrss Cleark & Duttun, Sewall & my Self. we had an Elligant Supper and I tarried all night.   at Capt Meloys. Birth 48th. I receivd 24/.  
 

 

 November 19, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home after Dineing. Revd mr Turner and Esq Cony Supt here. I was Calld to mr Parkers at 11 Evn.   at Ditoes & mr Parkers. mr Turner here.  
 

 

 November 24, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 A very heavy rain foren, Sun Shone at 12. mr Ballard went to Meeting. Dolly is unwell Pukeing in ye night. Esqr Coney Calld here, he has been to Join mr Smith & mrs Nancy Cleark in Marriage.   at home. Doct Cony here.  
 

 

 November 25, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Esqr Howards Dagt. XX.

Clear & Pleast. Dolly went to her Brothers. Polly Pollard Came home with her to have a Goun made. Esqr M. Howard Calld me to See his Lady who was Delivd of a Dagt at 8h 10m Evn. I tarried all night.   at Esqr Mathew Howard. Birth 49th. 
 

 

 November 27, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Pollard brot my Dagt here to warp a web, Polly went home with him. mr Poor Calld me to See his wife who is not so well as Could be wisht, find mr Livermores Sons Laying a hearth in our kitchen. the Bridg acrost the Gully was Coverd ys Day.   at mr Poors, mrs Greely there.
