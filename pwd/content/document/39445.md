Richmond Virginia July 25th 1788
Sir
The inclosed will account for the appearant mystery in mine of yesterday  please to do your best forme
Msr A Dunscomb
To Joseph Howell Esqr.
B. Lieut Scotts unsettled account on file with the Letters
