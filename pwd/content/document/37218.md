New York 4 April 1785
 Sir
         I enclose a coppy of an Order drawn by Gen.l
   Patterson on Colo. Trumbull, in favor of Capt. Ebenezer
   Sullivan informs me his Company refused to receive the
   money, unless he would make up the depreciation, & 
   that this circumstance prevented him from drawing
   the same.
                                                I

I have forwarded a copy of the Order to Col. Trumbull,
  and have received his answer, Copy of which I enclose
  for your aid in searching into affair - If you will be so
  obliging as to investigate the subject, and transmit to
  me the result, it will enable me to give Mr. Sullivan
  the information he wishes.
                             Abiel Foster
Jos. Howell Jun.r
