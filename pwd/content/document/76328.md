199.
Accountants Office July 21, 1800
Saml Hodgdon Esq. Superintendent of M.S.
Sir
On settlement of the account of Jon.n Jackson Esq. as Agent of Fortifications at Boston for expenditures in the month of June last he has received a credit on the books of this office for fourteen wheelbarrows with Iron Work complete delivered at Castle Island at 4 21/100 dolls. each amounting to fifty eight dollars, & 94/100 for which you will be pleased to make the necessary entries on the Books of the Superintendant of Military Stores.
I am &c W.S.
