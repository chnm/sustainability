Accountants Office
February 28th 1797
I certify that there is due to Campbell Smith the sum of
Seventy six Dollars and Eighty Cents being for his pay and
subsistence as Lieutenant in the 4th US Regiment and
Pay, Forage, and Subsistence as Brigade Major to Brigadier
General Wilkinson from the [illegible] February 1797 inclus.
Doll. 76 80 100	Wm Simmons
Acct.
The Secy of War
