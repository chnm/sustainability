 January 10, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not very Cold. mr Wiesom here ys morn for advise, his wife being in Dangerous Saircumstance. I Sent her a Stiptic powder which he gave her; it gave relief. I went my Self, left her Some things to take. Calld at mr Hamlins, his wife not so well as I Could wish; at mr Burjess, Shee is more Comfortable than Shee was; at mr Westons, Shee not So well as usual; at mr Craggs, Polly apears to be mending. they removd her into the Chamber ys Day. I Came home at Dusk. Son & Dagt Town and their Little Daft Came here, Left their famely well. mrs Pollard paid me 1/6 for work I Did for her.   aT mr Wiesoms & other Neigrs. Phinehas Pane, a Dagt Born.  
 

 

 January 14, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Esqr Weston, mr Pitts & Foster, Son & Dagt Town took Breakfast. I was Calld to See Polly Bisbe, tarried all night. She was Delirious in her feavor fitt.   at Bisbes.  
 

 

 January 15, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some Snow. Polly a little Easier. I left her near night. Calld at mr Taylors, his youngest Child has the meaSels. I arivd at home aft Sun Sett.   at Ditoes. Son Town went to Winthrop.  
 

 

 January 16, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. Son & Dagt Town & Parthena returnd at Evng. I was Calld to Saml Cumingss to his wife in Labour. Shee was Delivd of a Dagt at 1h 30m, about 5 m before my arival. his parents with her & no one Else. mrs Briggs and hamlin there after her Delivery; tarried thro ye night, my patient Exercised with much pain.   at Saml Cumings. J. Kenny here for me to go & See his wife.  
 

 

 January 17, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Cumings Dagt. XX.

a Severe Snow Storm. I left mrs Cumings at 2 h pm a little Easier. I got home thro much Diffuculty, find yt mr Kenny was here after me Soon after I left home last Evng. hear all so yt Esqr Coneys Lady was Delivrd of a Dagt this morn at 10 h.   at Ditoes. Birth Saml Cumings Dgt at 1 h. Esqr Coneys Dagt at 10 h morn.  
 

 

 January 21, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. mr Ballard went from home bound for Sandy river. I was Calld to See Wm Densmore, he was Siesd with the Collic, got Ease before I arivd. I returnd home & went Directly to mr Bisbes, tarried & watcht. Pollys feaver very high the most of ye night. Wm Howard Came there at Evn. the wind Shifted to ye S & blew very hard. mrs Bisbe is very anxious about Polly.   at mr Bisbes. mrs Rockwood went home.  
 

 

 January 25, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & not very Cold. I was unwell, Did not rise till mr Pinkam Calld me to go to mr Bisbes, his Dagt Polly being Siesd with a Complaint in her Nervs. I made use of pipe Phisic which gave relief & Shee rested much Bitter than Shee had for Some nights past. Capt Haywoods Lady & I watcht.   at mr Bisbes. mrs Haywood there & Doct Williams at Evng.  
 

 

 January 26, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Blaks Son. XX.

Cloudy morn. I was Calld from mr Bisbes at Day Dawn by mr James Blak to See his wife. Shee was Deld at 7h 13m morn of a fine Son. I tarried thro the Day & night; was awake y most of ye night.   at Dos & mr Blaks. Birth 1st of my asisting ys year.  
 

 

 January 28, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Catons Dagt and 1st Child. XX. fee recd July 21.

Cloudy. I was Calld by mr Field to go to See mrs Caton at 1 h morn, walkt there, was allmost over com when I arivd. I put her Safe to bed with a Dagt at 3, and returnd home at 7. her Husband not at home. I was Calld at 8 to See Capt Burjess little Son who was Scalt on his left arm yester morn. I Drest ye arm. Calld to See mrs Hamlin & Burtun, receivd 6/ of mr Burtun as a reward for assisting his wife in travil Sept 23d. Calld to See mrs Blak, find her Cleverly. Calld at mr Savages to rest my Self, find mr Benn Leighton here when I Came home. my Girls gone to See Polly mc keckney. Hannah informs me Polly Bisbe is Better.   at mr Chamberlins, Burjess, and other Neigrs. Birth 2nt which I have assisted in ys year. I have not rested in my Bed more yn 2 or 3 hours Sinc Tuesday morn.  
 

 

 January 30, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd all Day. mr Ballard & Jona attended worship at ye Coart hous. mr Smith of winthrop performd. Wilm Haywood Sleeps here. Clear Evnn. Thoms Kenny Calld me at 11 h to See his wife. we got there at ye 12th.   at Kennys.  
 

 

 January 31, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth T. Kennys Dagt. XX.

Began to Snow Latter part of last night and a Severe Storme. the wind very high. mrs Kenny Deld at 9 h morn of a Dagt. I Left her & inft as well as Could be Expicted & returnd at 3 pm.   at Kennys. Birth 3d. June 24th receivd 6/ of mr Kenny.  
 

 

 February 2, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. I had a Severe pain in my head ys morn, but was Calld to See mrs Caton. Shee has taken Some Cold and has a pain in her hip. I feel much better after I returnd. Dolly is very unwell thro the Day, but Seemd revivd in ye Evng. mrs Benjamin Spent ye Evng here.   at mr Chamberlins.  
 

 

 February 3, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mrs Welchs Dagt.

Clear. I was Calld at 9 ys morn to See mrs Welch who is in Labour. Shee was Delivd at 12 of a Dagt, Dead Born, and was very ill after Delivery. I tarried with her till about 9 Evn when Shee Seemed a little more Comfortable. I was then Calld to Thomas Hinklys.   at mrs Welches. Birth 4th. mr Ballard gone to wiscasset.  
 

 

 February 4, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thoms Hinklys Son. XX.

Clear. mrs Hinkley was Delivd about 1 h morn of a fine Son. I left her Comfortable at 8, and returnd home. Calld to See mrs Welch, find her as well as Could be Expected, her Sister Cyphers with her. I was Calld immedialely after my return to See Willm Densmore find him very low, his thigh Sweld as much as it was when I saw him before. Jona & Ephm gone to the meddow   at Thos Hinkleys. Birth 5th. 
 

 

 February 5, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & moderate. I went to See Esqr Conys Lady, find her a little more Comfortable than Shee has been; Shee is troubled with Biles and Soar Nipples. I Calld and helpt git mrs Welch out of Bed, Shee is as well as Could be Expected. I Calld to See mrs Blak, Shee had an ill turn yesterday but is Better. Dolly Seems some better this Evng. mr Ballard returnd from wiscasset.   at Esqr Coneys, mrs Welchs & mr Blaks.  
 

 

 February 6, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleast. I went to See mrs Welch in ye forenn, find her a little Easier. I Calld at mr Savages. Dolly Rode out and is more Comfortable. mr Hamlin and Burtun & yr wives, mr Patridg and mrs H. Rockwood Spent Evn here. mr Blackman Calld me to See his wives Sister. I had a fall out of ye Sleigh on my way. I receivd no hurt, tarried all night. mrs Thompson watcht. I went to bed ye latter part of ye night, gave ye Lady Some Senna & manna with Seeds and left her an ounce of Elixer propts.   at mrs Welches and mr Blackmns. 
 

 

 February 7, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some Snow. I left my Patient a little Easier & returnd home. Twisted 7 knotts of yn then I went to See mrs Welch, find her as well as Could be Expected. Son Town here, his famely well.   at mr Blackmns. 
 

 

 February 12, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld by mr Crane to go to See mrs Pond of winthrop, went as far as mr Saml Cumingss, was there informd the Lady was safe Delivrd by mrs Whiteing, of a Dagt. I returnd home at ye 11 h morn attended by mr Eastman. I went to mr Pollards & mr Westons aftern. Leut Hains Learned Sleeps here. I bot 1lb Tamerins of mr Baxter & a pinte of wine of mr Weston.   at mr Cumingss, Pollards & mr Westons. Leut Learned here.  
 

 

 February 18, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Greelys Dagt. XX.

Clear & Cold. I went to See Wilm Densmore, find him much as he has been of late. I was Calld by mr Greely (from there) to See his Lady who was in Labour & was Safe Deld at 10h 30m Evn of a fine Dagt, her 7th Child of which 4 are Dagts. I tarried all night, Slept a little.   at mr Densmores & mr Greelys. Birth 6th. receivd 3/ august 16th 1794 of mr Greely.  
 

 

 February 19, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd some. I left my Patients as well as Could be Expected in ye Care of mrs Walker and arivd at home before noon. I receivd 3/ of mr Greely in part for my performance. mr Bullin here, informs that he asisted Doct Williams to open the Soar on Willm Densmores thigh.   at mr Gralys & Colo Sewalls.  
 

 

 February 20, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day, Cloudy aftn. I attended Public worship, mr Smith performd. mrs Pierce Sent a Bill for prayers. I was Calld at 9 h Evn to See Leut Pollards wife who was in Labour. mrs Savage, Williams & Cowen there all night.   at meeting and mr Thomass. I am 56 years old ys Day.  
 

 

 February 21, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Leut David Pollards Dagt. X.

Clear & Cold. mrs Pollard was very ill thro ye Day. Old Lady Fletcher, mrs Pollard & Ephm Cowens wife aded to our Company, and my patient was Safe Delivrd at 9 h Evn of a fine Dagt and is Cleverly, Considering how ill Shee has been. I tarried all night. Sett up with mrs Pollard.   at mr Thomass. Birth 7th. 
 

 

 February 22, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. mr Ballard Came to inform me yt Capt Nicholss Lady was at our house. I returnd after we had took Breakfast, leaving my Patients remarkably Comfortable. after Chatting a little while with my friends, took a walk with them as far as mr Pollards. Calld at mr Blaks & Savages on my return. Fredrick Foot, his wife & Sister here on their way to mr Bullins. mr Pitt here.   at Ditoes. mr Thomas gave me 6/ as a reward. I receivd a Letter from Doct Barton of Freby 6th. 
 

 

 March 10, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snow & rain foren, Clear aftn. I was Calld to See Lidia White who has had fitts this Day, but had left her before I arivd. Shee Complaind of an Opresion at her Stomach and pain in her head. I left her a portion of Senna and manna and returnd home at 11h 20m Eving. my famely all in Bed when I arivd.   at mr Whites to See his Dagt Lidia.  
 

 

 March 15, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Bizer Benjamins Son. X. receivd my fee Sept 4th 1792.

Clear part of ye Day. I went to Dress Pollys finger forenn. mrss mc ithny, Heuston & Whitin & mr Craggs little Dagt here aftn. I was Calld at 6h 1/2 Pm to See mrs Benjamin. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 Evn of a lusty Son. mrs Pollard, Chamln & Caton went home. mrs Welch and I tarried till Day. Ephm Bot 1 lb Chocolat & a Bowl with wood he Carried to markett.   at mr Savages. Birth 8th. Pollymc kethney & others here.
