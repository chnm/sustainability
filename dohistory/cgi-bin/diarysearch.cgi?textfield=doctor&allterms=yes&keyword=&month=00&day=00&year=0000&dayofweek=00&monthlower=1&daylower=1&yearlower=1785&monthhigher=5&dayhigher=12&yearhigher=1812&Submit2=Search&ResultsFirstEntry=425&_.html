 June 19, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Waters went from here to go to Bror Ebenzr Moors & Doct Bartons, Jona gone with him. Sally Farewel Calld here. Shee had been to ye Stores for Cloathing for Son Ephms Babe. Hannah Ballard went to draw her Aunts Breasts, returnd at Evn. Says her aunt is quite unwel this aftern. I have Cut grass, houghd the polatoes west Side ye hous. feel fatagued.   at home.  
 

 

 July 9, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I workt in my Gardin and pikt Some wool. Dagt Pollard Came from Son Lambds here and went home. Shee informd me that mrss Joness Soar had been opened and that Polly Pollard had got her Self into Business. Leml here, informd that Lathrop had a Soar Opened on his face by Dr Mann.   at Son Jonas. Dagt Pollard went home. Hannah & Sally Sleep here.  
 

 

 October 3, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and moderate. Nabby Hatch here on a visit. Dagt Ballard to Bake. I gave ye former _ Bs Apples for her marm. I went after Sun Sett to See Jack Peirce, find him very weak. he has had Doct Howard to See him who Bled and gave an Emettic. he Seems very feeble. Heman Burr Supt here and went to help take Care of him. Cyrus is gone to husk at mr [Andrus]. I was Calld in the night to EbenZ Trasks wife who was in Labour. his Oldest Son is Sick. my hors thrue me as I went up.   at Son Jonas. Nabby Hatch here & Dind.  
 

 

 October 9, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 A rainy day, high wind at night. I had the pain return and all most dispared of life. Doct Man was Calld in the night to See me. mrs Savage Came there in the night by reason of the wind being So high. Shee dare not tarry in her own hous.   at Ditoes, very Sick, the Child is very ill allso.  
 

 

 October 13, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I tarried till ye 11th H and Sett out for home. Calld at Doct Conys to See Lynda Gitchel who is very Sick there. I Calld at Son Jonas to See Jack Pierce, he is thought to be Some better. Dagt Pollard Calld and took Tea and then went on to Son Lambards. Heman Bur went with her.   at Ditoes, Doct Conys & Son Jonas.  
 

 

 October 17, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous wk and other matters. went to Son Jonas at Evng, find Jack Pierce and Le faett Ballard Sick. Doct Howard here to see mrs [Farewel, left her phisec. it operated 10 times. I set with her till 10h Evng.   at son Jonas at Evng.  
 

 

 October 21, 1804, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I have been at home, feel very unwel. mrs Farewel is much as Shee was. Sally been to mr Benets. Lemuel Came here, brot Some Beef & fish to mrs Farewel from Son Ephm. Dagt Ballard Sent me Some roast Veal. Bror Barton Departed this life ys day, and James Snow in ye Coars of the night.   at home, feel unwel. Doct Barton Expird, James Snow also.  
 

 

 October 25, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been geting wood and fatagued much to do it. I broke old loggs with an Old hough and brot in the pieces in a baskett, and O how fatagued I was. Doct Howard Calld to See mrs Farewel. Sally was at Son Jonas on a visit. Shee [informd] me at Evng that my Son was Delermennd to Come in to this hous within a fortnit and that I might tarrie here [or] go and liv in their hous and See how good it was to bring water from this wel. O thou Parent of ye universe, Cutt Short thine aflictions and Sufer me thine unworthy hand maid to See Some Comfort before I go hence.   at home, falagueing very much. I began to wash after 9h Evng and washt my Cloaths in 2 waters.  
 

 

 October 30, 1804, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, sorted apples. Doct Howard here to See mrs Farewel, left her Senna and Bitters. Sally washt her floors.   at home.  
 

 

 November 16, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. Doct Cony was Calld to See Lucy Lambd, gave an Emetic which relievd her.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 November 21, 1804, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Lambards 4th Son.

Clear. I have washt. Sally removd into my North room. Doct Cony to See her. I was Calld at Evn to Dagt Lambard, Shee was Safe Deld at 11h Evn of her 4th son & 6th Child. it wd 12_lb. Shee is Cleverly.   at Son Lambd. Birth 9th. 
 

 

 January 11, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye Day. a mrs Emerson from [Dery] field had fitts here this night. Doct Winship was Calld to her asistance. we were up with her ye most of ye night.   at Dito . 
 

 

 January 12, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and cold. the sick patient was very ill till near night. the Doct tarried till 2h pm. Shee Seems much better this [Evning].   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 19, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. the famely that Slept here Sett out at noon. son Lambd went to Hallowell, brot Dolly from Son Ephms. Sist Moore and mrs Johnston Calld here. I slipt at ye door and lambd my Self, Son Lambd Calld doct Winship to See me.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 20, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the Doct went home, mr Lambd and Allin to meeting. wido Savage, Daniel & wife, mr Dingly & his, here to See me. Son Ephm Came at intermition.   at Ditoes, very Lambe.  
 

 

 January 23, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Doct Wenship Came and Sett a Broken Bone in my foot and I walkt some about house. Son an Dagt Pollard Came here.   at Detoes. had a bone Sett in my foot.  
 

 

 May 23, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. I have been very unwel, my throat is very Soar. Doct Howard was Calld to see Sally. Shee Seems more Comfortable. we Dind on roast Veal.   at home, feel very unwel.  
 

 

 May 30, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am at Son Lambds, the Child is threatoned with fitts. Doct Cony Came to See him, approved of what we had done for ye Child. Left it Elixer Satutus, it Seems Some better.   at Son Lambards, the Babe is very Sick.  
 

 

 August 19, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Bakt and done other work, was Calld in hast to mr Cyphers to See Old mrs Pollard who fell from her hors and was Carried in there. Doct Winship Came and lett blood. Shee was put into a Shais, Conducted to mr Blacks. I went & tarried all night.   at mr Cyphers & Blacks. mrs Pollard fell from her hors, hurt her much.  
 

 

 September 18, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I went to See Son Jonas wife who is unwel, has ye ague in her face & other Complaints. Doct Howard to See her, Extracted a tooth for her. mrs Pierce there, informd me yt Warrin Stone & Sally Church were to be Joind in wedlock this day. mr Ballard Cut fart of his Stalks and got some wood. Allin Lambd here.   at Son Jonas, his wife unwel.  
 

 

 October 23, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a hard frost. I have done my work, went at Evng to See mrs Heartwel. Shee is unwel, Doct Howard to see her.   at mr Heartwels.  
 

 

 September 6, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard, my Self and Son Cyrus attended Bublic worship, it was Communion day. Revd mr Stone Discoarst from Galations III C 1st vers, O [foolish] &C. I went to Son Lambds find his youngest Child Sick, tarried there aftern. Doct Cony Came there after meeting and opened a Soar on the Childs face. I Came to Son Jonas, Sent for Polly Pitts to go there but Shee did not go.   at meeting room. at Son Lambds aftern, their Babe Sick. Doct Cony opend a Soar on its face.  
 

 

 September 4, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Son Ephms 3d Daugt Expired, aged 5 weaks, 2 days & 12 hours.

Clear. I am at my Sons, the Child is no better. Dagt Ballard Came and assisted in takeing Care of it. Doct Cony was Calld, left Sinna [and] Elixir Salutus but it was past the reach of medisin. it Continued till 7h 30m Evng when it gave up ye ghost. a mr Howard tarried all night.   at Ditoes. the Babe Expired at 7h 30 m Evng. mrs Hamlin and Titcomb laid it out.  
 

 

 April 3, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I was Calld to Son Jonas between 3 & 4 morn to See James who was in a fitt. we administd a Clister which gave Some relief. Doct Ellis was Calld, approvd of whad we had done. he left Senna & manna & drops. I left him at dusk much revived. mrs Baudin there.   at Ditoes. James has had a fitt. mrss Wiman is very low. Jona Junr Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 19, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. mr Ballard went to James Boltons, brot A Cow & Calf home. Wm Ballard helpt him. we had a fine Shower [afterorn]. I dug ground South of the hous to Sett Turnip plants. mr Ballard went to Doct Conys, Setled with him. Ballance in our favour 20 Cents for which he had 1 oz Bitters. ye Doct made a present of 1 oz Camphir.   at home, workt Some in my gardin. Doct Cony gave me 1 oz Camphir.
