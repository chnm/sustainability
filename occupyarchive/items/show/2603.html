Title

Occupy Salt Lake City Oral History Interview – Edward Swift

Description

An interview with Edward Swift of the Occupy SLC movement by Jennifer Meredith on November 4, 2011.

The Occupy SLC Oral History Project is being conducted by the University of Utah's American West Center and J. Willard Marriott Library in order to record the thoughts, beliefs and emotions of the people behind this grassroots movement.
