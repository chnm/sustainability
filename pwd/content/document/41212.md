War Department March 5th 1791
Sir,
Your account of expenditures for Colonel McGillivray and the chiefs of the Creek Nation whilst in New York amounting to xxxx 3194 99/100 being mislaid, I will thank you to forward as soon as possible a duplicate thereof properly receipted.
The vouchers of Vandyck & Ten Eyek and from others delivered to this office by Mr. Dalamater are here on file [illegible] the enclosed life [sic] but the others I will be obliged to send with the duplicate of the account. 
I am
Sir 
You most obedient
Servant
H Knox
Colonel Marinus Willett
Newyork
