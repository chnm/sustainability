D of War Accountants' Offices
January 6, 1796
I Certify that there is due to Joshua Humphreys Constructor of the Frigate building in the Port of Philada. The sum of eight hundred thirty three Dollars and thirty three cents being a balance of his account for compensation from the 1st of May 1794 to 31st December 1795 inc.
William Simmons
Drs 833.33
The Secy of War
