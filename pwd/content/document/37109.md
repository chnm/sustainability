New York Jany. 10th 1785

Sir

I enclose a Letter from his Excellency the President of New Hamshire, requesting you to return the same, with the fullest information on the subject of it, which Mr. Pierce's Office will afford: Your compliance, as early as the nature of the business will admit, will much oblige
Yours &c.
Abriel Foster

Capt. Jos Howell Junr
