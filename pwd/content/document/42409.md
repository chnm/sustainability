January 17 1792 from the Auditor of the Treasury
Sir
Auditors Office
Jany 17 1792
I herewith transmit an Account and Vouchers of the late Capt. Barnet Eickleberger for your examination.  
This account I am told has been frequently, and in season, presented at the office, for settlement.  No proof of such presentation exists in this office - possibly there may be some in yours, and if so, I would wish you to favor me, soon as convenient, with a regular Statement & Report thereon.
I am respectfully Sir Yr Obed Servt 
R. Harrison
Joseph Howell Esq
