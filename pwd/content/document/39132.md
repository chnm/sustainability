The Honb

The Commissioners of the treasury.

Office of Army Accounts New York Jan 28th 1788

Gentleman

In obedience to the instructions of your Hon Board of the 25th inst. I di myself the honor to inclose my report on the accounts of the late General De Haas.

I am with great respect gentleman

Yr Ov Servt

J Howell
