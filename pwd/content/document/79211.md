Fort Washington March 28. 1796
SIR,
AT ten Days sight of this first of Exchange the second
of same tenor and date being unpaid, please to pay toSamuel Price & Co.
or Order the Sum of Two Thousand
Dollars, which Sum I have received of him in Cash, and charge the same as per
advice herewith to my Account.
Caleb Swan Paymaster of the
Troops of the United States
TO
The Secretary of War
Philadelphia
