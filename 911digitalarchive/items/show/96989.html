How has your life changed because of what happened on September 11, 2001?

Life has forever been changed due to the terrorist attacks on September 11, 2001. Because of the attacks on America, things such as traveling are more difficult now days. It takes longer to get through security because they must take more precautions now than they ever have before in order to prevent anymore attacks on the country. It’s also more difficult to keep young children entertained while traveling because air ports are so strict with what you are and aren’t allowed to take on your flight with you.

How will you remember the 10th anniversary of the September 11 attacks?

The 9/11 attacks in America will never be forgotten. It has probably been one of the biggest events that has happened in America since I have been born. I would love to New York and go to the location of the attacks that took occurrence on this sad and tragic day in America. Waking up every year on September 11 will never be thought of in the same way again. My mind will always go back to the day that the attacks took place and the quiet and almost empty classroom that I sat in awaiting the arrival for my parents to pick me up and take me home safely. As a child I wasn’t quite sure of what was happening. I just knew that something wasn’t right though because students were being taken out of the class one by one and not returning. September 11, 2001 will be a day in which America will not forget.
