 February 26, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page
26 A

 

 

 Clear part of ye Day. Snowd at Evn. Patty Bullin Dind here. Cyrus & Dolly at home, Jona is not. I have been at home. at home. Patty Bullin here.
 

 

 February 29, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page
29 4

 

 

 Clear, a very Cold morn, Pleast Day. my Son Jonathan Brot his wife & little Son here. mr Ballard returnd from Pittstn. I have been at home, finisht kniting Hannah a pair of hoes. mrs Welch here, Shee left her Dagt Jenny at mr Beemans. at home. Jona Brothis wife here on a visit.
 

 

 March 2, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page
2 6

 

 

 rain. Hannah washt. I have been at home. helpt Sally nurs her Babe, it is not so well as usual. mr Ballard been at home. Ephm brot Hay from mr Densmores. Dolly went there aft Breakfast. at home.
 

 

 April 4, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page
4 4

 

 

 Clear & pleast. Jonn & his wife went to house keeping. Hannah wrode up with Sally, Ephm walkt. mr Manly and he went and Carried Jonns things from here by water in ye forenn. mr Ballard has been to Colo Howards. I went to See mrs Livermore, find her much more Comfortable. mrs Hinkly & White there. Selvenus Hatch Sleeps here. mr Waid & mr Ballard Divided the flax which we raisd on his land. at mr Livermors.
