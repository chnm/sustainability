War Department
April 19th: 1794
Sir,
You will please to furnish Lieutenant Campbell Smith with a Boat and hands to convey him to head Quarters.  Captain Crawford will furnish him with the Escort.
I am Sir,
Your humble Servant
H. Knox
Major Craig
