 October 4, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard has been to ye fort & hook, Cyrus to work up Streat helping remove mr Pitts Store. Selva & James went away after Dinner. Dolly went to mr Shepherds, Bot me 1 yd & 1/4 peirshon @ 7/6, 2 yds ribbond @ /7 1 Skein Silk /4. I have been helping Do house work. Ephm was Siesd with a Severe fitt between 11 & 12h Evn, it was on him near an hour. we Calld mr Livermore & his wife, they tarried till he Seemd to be likely to rest, and went home.   at home. Ephm had fitts this night. mr Livermr & wife were Calld [here turn] before morn.  
 

 

 October 5, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Ephm was taken with an Other fitt about 2h this morn. we Calld mr Livermore again, thinking he was Expireing. he Came to his Sinces in Some Degree & Slept quietly before Day. mr Livermore tarried till morn. I laid down on ye bed with my Child & Slept a little. we were So fatagud we [did] not attend worship.   at home. mr Ballard at meeting. Son & Dagt Pollard [here]. Ephm is Better.  
 

 

 October 6, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been to See mr Hodges Child, they think it is mending. Calld at Son Pollards, they are well, at mr Baxters, his wife is unwell, at Doct Colmans, all well. I had of him 1 oz Galbanum 1/3, Dito alloes 1/, _ oz myrr /7, one nutmeg 1/, 2 oz Clovs. mrs Colman made me a present of a thimble. I Calld at mr Hamlins, they are well. mr Ballard at Doct Coneys & mr Pollards. Cyrus bot m 3 pints Spirrits, Cost 1/20_.   at mr Hodges & others, at Doct Colmans.  
 

 

 October 8, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Felpss Son. XX.

Clear. Ephm had a fitt at day break, has been very sick till after noone. I was Calld at 3h pm to See mrs Felps who was Deld at 6 of a Son, her third Child. I left her & infant Cleverly & returnd home at 9, find Ephm more Comfortable. Cyrus been to Pitts tn to mill.   at mr Felpss. Birth 38th. my Son Ephm had a fitt this morn.  
 

 

 October 9, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Williams Dind here. I was Calld to See David Sewalls wife who is unwell. I was there all night.   at David Sewalls.  
 

 

 October 10, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth David Sewalls Son. XX.

Cloudy. at mr Sewalls, they were intimidated & Calld Dr Page who gave my Patient [50] drops ot Laudenum which put her into Such a Stupor her pains (which were regular & promising) in a manner [Scant] till near night when Shee pukt & they returnd & Shee was Delivd at 7h Evn of a Son, her first Born. I left her Cleverly at 10 & walkt home. I receivd 12/ as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 39th. mr Perham Sleeps here. Dolly is unwell.  
 

 

 October 11, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Nabby Towers Son by Moses Parmer. XX.

Clear, Serene Day. I was Calld to 4h morn to See Nabby Towers who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 11h 30m of a Son which Shee DeClard that Moses Parmer was the father of. I tarried & Dind. Esqr Haywood there. I arivd at home at 3h pm, am fatagud. Dolly is unwell, has a Blister which lames her very much.   at mr Lathrop. Birth 40th. receivd my fee Oct. 2nd by mr Daw at the hook.  
 

 

 October 12, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wilm Whites. 4th Son. XX.

Clear & very pleast. I was Calld belween 12 & 1h to See William Whites wife who was in Labour. I Crost ye river & went Down on hors back. my Patient not very ill. I laid down & took a fine nap. Shee Calld on me about Day Break & was Safe Delivd at 6h 20m of a fine Son which waid 11 lb. I left her & infant Cleverly & arivd at home at noon. Sally Cocks & Patty Levermore ferried me over. mrs Blanchard, Brown, Winstow, Page & Adkins attended. mr Ballard & Sons & mr Perhan are gone to meeting.   at Wilm Whites. Birth 41s t. I receivd 6/as a reward.  
 

 

 October 21, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & moderate. mr Ballard been up to fort, at ye hook. Cyrus to winthrop for Jon as hors. I have helpt Do hous work & knitt. Pally Easty warpt & Beemd a web & went to Jon as for hernes. I was Calld at 10h Evn to See mrs Robbins who is unwell. I went to bed after 2h morn.   at mr Robbins.  
 

 

 October 22, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Chandler Robbinss Son. XX.

Cloudy. mrs Robbins Linguerd till 4h pm when her illness Came on. Doct Parker was Called but Shee Did not wish to See him when he Came & he returnd home. Shee was Safe Deld of a Son, her first Born, at 10h 30m Evn & is as well as Can be Expected. I tarried & watcht. mrs Ney was there and Shee only, Except ye famely.   at mr Robbins. Birth 42nt. 
 

 

 October 23, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Robbins went and brot mrs Voce to Nurs his wife, & Conducted me home in a Boat. he gave me 18/ as a reward. I found mrs Baker at our house on my return.   at Ditoes. receivd 18/.  
 

 

 October 25, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth magr Sticknys 2nt Son. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld by Magr Stickney at 4h morn to See his Lady in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 2h pm of her 4th Child, a Son. her women tarried till near night when we left her & infant Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as reward. find mr Ballard is better & gone to ye hook.   at mgr Stickneys. Birth 43d. mr [Ballard] is more [Comfortable. we ].  
 

 

 October 26, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I was at meeting, the Sacrament administred. I was Calld to See John Hamlin who is unwell. I tarried with mrs Baxter, Shee very unwell. it raind all night.   at meeting. tarried at mr Baxters.  
 

 

 October 28, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. I went to See mr Parkers Childn, find ym better. I was Calld to mrs Baxters, walkt part of ye way, wrode part. the Patient rested very well.   at Ditoes & mr Parkers.  
 

 

 October 30, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Baxter left home Bound to Boston, his wife took rheubarb. I was up with her till near Day, when Calld to See mrs Partradg who is in Labr.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 October 31, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Partridgs Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. I was at mr Partradgs with his wife, Deld of a Dagt at 4h Pm. I tarried all night. She is Cleverly, ye inft allso. mr Partradg bestowd 12/ as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 44th.  
 

 

 November 1, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I left my Patients at noon. Calld to Se mrs Baxter, find her better, but insinsible of the favour. find mr White puting triming on our Desk.   at Ditoes & mr Baxters.  
 

 

 November 3, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, raind at night. I was at the hook. I Saw mrs Mc Causland there. find mrs Robbins Cleverly.   at the hook.  
 

 

 November 5, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Scolt my Pickles & went to See mrs Baxter who, I think, is better tho Shee is not of that mind. I went to See mrs Partradg, find Shee had an ill turn and is better. I was at Son Pollards, their babe has one tooth.   at mr Baxters & others.  
 

 

 November 6, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at Evn to See a Child of magr Stickneys who is very ill. I tarried all night. I have been to mr Parkers, their Child Seems better.   at mgr Stickneys & mr Parkers.  
 

 

 November 14, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Baxters, Shee thinks Shee is no better. John Chamln Calld me from there to Anthony Brackets, his wife is unwell but Seems better, Slept Cleverly. I was there all night. Snowd at night.   at mr Baxters & Brackets.  
 

 

 November 15, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth mr James Burtns Son at XX 11h 5m morn; mr XX Brackets Son at 10h Evn. XX.

Snowd. mr Burtun Came to Bracketts for me about Day to go & See his wife who is in labr. Shee was Deld at 11h 5m foren of a Son. I Dind there, was Calld back to mr Bracketts, his wife in Labour & was Deld at 10h Evn of a Son, her first born. Shee was very ill. I tarried all night. mr Bracket gave me 6/7 as a reward. mrs Boltn Slept there, the other women went home.   at mr Burtuns & Brackets. Births 45th and 46th. recd 6/7 of Bracket.  
 

 

 November 16, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I left mrs Bracket as well as Could be Expected. Calld & found mrs Burtun Cleverly. at mrs Baxters, Shee is much the Same, at Son Pollards & Jonas, all well. little Jona Came here at Evng Conducted by James mc Clewer.   at Bracketts & others.  
 

 

 November 19, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Town took Breakfast here. the Company which Slept here went away. I am Calld on to go & See mrs Baxter, but had no hors & Could not go. Son Jona brot his wife and youngest Child here. mr Ballard returnd at Evn, in health for which favr we Desire to be thankfull.   at hom. Capt Stackpl went fm here. mr Ballard returnd.  
 

 

 November 20, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to meeting, our Childn, mr Pitts, mr Barton & Jo mc Causland Dind with us, the women & Childn Sleep [here].   at home. a high wind [ys] night.
