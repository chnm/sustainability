8327
Philadelphia, August 14th 1792.
My Dear Sir.
Agreeably to your request, of the 3d inst: I now transmit you, enclosed, the petitions and vouchers of Eliphalet Easton, and Salmon Burr, praying to be placed on the pension list of the United States.
I am Dear Sir, with great esteem, Your most obedient servt:
H Knox
