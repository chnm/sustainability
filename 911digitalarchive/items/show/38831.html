September 11 Email: Body

Wow...If you had written 3 weeks earlier I could have seen you...I was actually back in Livingston and NY all summer...I needed surgery on my neck from a car accident 5 years ago...I'm better now...
I must say I'm very impressed with how well you are doing...But I do need to ask what exactly does a "manager
of synergy" do???...
Last week was absolutely terrifying...My Dad works 2 blocks from the WTC and takes the PATH there everyday...It was not untill 4 1/2 hours after the attack began that my Mom was able to reach me to let me know that my Father was ok...They had them in Lock Down in his office with the AC shut off bc they thought it was a biological attack with anthrax or something...My Brother's girlfriend works in that area too, and also takes the Path, she stepped out the doors of the WTC as the 1st plane was hitting...Both of them are ok...I am still close with Pete Liao, he has informed me that everyone in our immidiate group is ok, some people we know or that we have met through the years were on the top floors like 101-106 and are gone, Tell Edgidio I said Hi....
As for me, I went to Hofstra and towards the end of my 1st year got into acting...there went school, amazing how it was the 1st WTC Bombing that molded my decision to leave college and pursue what I wanted for my career...

... I have been on Hiatus with my surgery and recovery, I'm back in LA now and will probably start auditioning soon again, I got back on the 1st of September...
...I got back in touch with Kerin ...I spoke to Santos a few times....I saw JimFoglio once, he has moved up to Albany, were Zamer is now a Doctor, speaking of Doctors, Josh , who we graduated with is an orthopedic surgeon that was at ground zero before the 2nd plane hit...he is okay too, but it was a hell of a day for him...
---WBS--Scott
