 April 25, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came from mrs Burgess at 9h morn. mr Ballard has been to ye hook. Capt Bouland of unity & Reubin Fairfield Dind here. Dolly went to mr Bisbes. Sally & I workt Some in the Gardin. Jason Livermore Junr took Current Sprouts of our Land with out Leav. I Calld him back & he gave ym back. we Sett them & others out in ye Gardin.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 April 26, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kenneys Dagt. XX.

rainy. Doct Williams Dind here. mr Ballard is gone to mr Thomass, a Law Seut there between mr Peter Parker & Colo Sewall. I was Calld at 2h Evn to See James Kenneys wife who was in Labour, & was Delivd at 11h of a Dagt; her husband was absent. I tarried all night, had no Sleep.   at James Kenneys. Birth 18th. 
 

 

 April 27, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast morn. I left my Patients Cleverly at 9h morn & returnd home. I Do feel fatagud. Son Jona & his Son here. mr Ballard left home at 3h pm on a Tour of Surveying for this Commonwealth. may he be Preservd & returnd in Safety is the prayer of me, his Spouse.   at Dittoes. mr Ballard Sett out for Sandy river.  
 

 

 April 28, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Selve Moore & others Sett out to assist mr Ballard in his Surveying. Cyrus went to take the hors home his Dady wrode. I have been at home, workt in my Gardin Seting Currant Bushes &C.   at home. thunder & rain in the night.  
 

 

 May 2, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went from mr Pitss to mr Child, find them Comfortable. mr Dagt Dolly there to work. I went to See mr Wesson who has had ye mumps, he is mending. I Came to Son Pollards took Tea. Calld at mr Densmores, Came home & Sowd Cabbage Seed and Sumer Savory, & Sett Jill run ye ground. the wife of Danl Bullon interd this Day. I am informd of ye Death of mrss Hopkins, mrs Allin & Richd Moores wife & Joell Chandler.   at Ditoes & other Neigrs. the Funeral of mrs Bolton. News of Deaths.  
 

 

 May 5, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Waids Dagt.

 Cloudy. mr Beaman was here, Desird me to Go & See his Lady; left his hors for me to wride. I went & Dind with her. went to See mrs Carr, find her much More Comfortable; her husband is Some Better. I Calld to See mrs Fillebrown, Shee is Cleverly. I was at mr Fesandans Store, bot 4 lb Cottne @ 2/4, 1 pr Shoes 4/. at Jery Dmrs bot 2 yds Durant @ 2/9. Came back to mrs Beamans, was Calld by mr Waid to See his wife who was in Labour, & was Safe Deld at at [sic] 10h Evn of a Dagt. I tarried all night.   at mr Beamans & others at hook, from there to mr Waids. Birth 19th. we had a Colt from our mare ys Day.  
 

 

 May 6, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I returnd from mr Waids, left my Patients Cleverly & took a fine Nap after I Came home. Ephm workt for Shubal Pitts, mr Carlton for me. Dolly & Sally went to mr Shubal Hinklys. Polly Densmr & Lucy Kenada here at Evng.   at mr Waids.  
 

 

 May 7, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, raind at night. mr Wad Calld me to See his wife who had an ill turn. I Sett up with her ys night. mrs Hodgis Dagt & Polly Pollard, Polly Page & Sally Williams were here. my Dagt had two webs warpt.   at mr Waids. Dagt Pollard here.  
 

 

 May 12, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rain ys morn, Clear Part of ye Day. mr Hodges Calld me to See his Dagt Fanny who is Labouring under ye indisposition of a Brokin Limb. I find her very restless. Doct Cony there.   at mr Hodges to See his Child.  
 

 

 May 19, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till aftern. mr Ballard at Doct Coneys. I was Calld to See Rold Smiths wife who is unwell. I Calld at mr Beamans & P. Norcross & T. Kennys. I bot an Earthen Vessel, price 1/.   at [Han] Clearks.  
 

 

 May 20, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleasant. Dolly went to work at mr Hamlins. I was Calld to See Mr Hodgess Child, Shee has been much Exercisd with Pain but is Easier. I am informd that the boddy of a young Girl by ye name of Tilton was found in the woods in Varsalboro. it is Suspected that a Negro by ye name of Edmond Fortis was ye perpetratr. the Coroner was Calld from this Town.   at mr Hodges & Son Pollards. A Girl found Dead, it is thougt was Murderd.  
 

 

 May 21, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I was Calld to See mr Moses Whites wife in Labour, there all day & night. Edmond Fortis, a Black man, was Conducted to Pounalboro Jail for Commiting a Rape on mrs Tilton & afterwds murdering her on ye Evng of ye 18th inst. A most Shocking Sein which he has Confest. he has a wife & 2 Childn.   at mr Whites. Edmond Fortis Conductd to Jaile for murdering mrs Tilton.  
 

 

 May 22, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Whites Dagt. XX.

Cloudy & Some rain. mrss White was Deld at 2h morn of her fifth Daughter & 9th Child. I left mother & Child Cleverly at 7. mr White bestowd 7/6 as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 20th. fee receivd.  
 

 

 May 23, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Beamans Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 7h morn to See the wife of John Beaman in Labour. Shee was Delivd at 10 of a Son; her first Son & 5th Child. I left her a 7h pm Cleverly & Came home. receivd 6/ as a reward. James Sturges Came here to work.   at mr Beamans. Birth 21st. fee Receivd. Funeral of Selvestr, [int]. ys [aft]  
 

 

 May 24, 1794, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. Colo Duttuns Lady Calld to See me. I have Planted Cucumrs & Squash and & mellon Seeds. mr Ballard went to mr Wessons. we have plantd Corn. Dolly returnd from mr Hamlins.   at home.  
 

 

 May 26, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I went to mr Selvesters, bot 6 lb Tamrinds, Cost 6/; 3 lb Cottne @ 3/; & 3 lb Sugar @ 1/. to mr Beamans, Shee is Cleverly. made me a present of 2 qurts of Vinegar. Bot at mr Danys 1 Jugg 1/, one Pott 1/, one Dto /9, 2 pans @ 6d. paid the Cash.   at the hook. mrs Holdman here.  
 

 

 May 30, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I was Calld to mr Densmores Twice ys Day to See their youngest Child who has Swallowed Some Boiling hott Coffee. it Seeme Easier at Evn. Harmon Barton Came here with Ephm & Ezra. Dolly is gone to her Brr Pollards.   at mr Densmores to See his Babe who is Scolt in its mouth & throat.  
 

 

 June 3, 1794, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Ezra went home. Capt Savage & Lady & Georg Brown Dind here. we had a very fine rain aftern. I was Calld by Petl Cleark Junr to See his wife who is unwell. I went to Bed, Slept Some. mrs Coutch there.   at mr Peter Clearks. Capt Savage here.  
 

 

 June 4, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Peter Cleark juners Son, first Child. XX.

Clear. mrs Clearks illness Came on So great that her women were Calld & Shee was Safe Delivd at 8h Evng of a Son. I left her as well as Could be Expected & returnd home at midnight.   at Ditoes. Birth 22nt. I receivd 7/2_.  
 

 

 June 8, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren. mr Ballard and Cyrus & Ephm went to meeting. mr Pitts Dind here. I was Calld to mr Phillip Norcorss wife who is in Labour, her women Calld.   at Phillip Norcross. 
 

 

 June 9, 1794, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Phillip Norcross Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. mrs NorCros was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 1h 30m morn. I left her before noon as well as Could be Expected, receivd 6/. Sally & Dolly washt. Dolly was very Sick all night, had a pain in her Ears.   at Ditoes. Birth 23d. 
 

 

 June 12, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. Augustus Ballard here. I was Calld to See Sally Williams who is not So well as Shee Could wish. I went to See mrs Pitts, mr Capins Child & Dagt Pollard. Dolly is there. mrs Ingerham, Sturges, Crage & Burtun, Sally & P Densmore here.   at Shubal Pitts & other Neigrs. John Houston Expird at Sandy river.  
 

 

 June 18, 1794, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. Dolly went to mr Burtuns to work. I went to See mrs Smith, Shee has taken Cold & is unwell. her Sister Came there ys day. Son Jona Calld me to See his Childn, they are Exercised with a Disentary. I was at Son Pollards. my Dagt is washing.   at mr Smiths, Burtuns, Pollards & Son Jon as. Jamess [Brother] Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 19, 1794, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye Day, rain aftern. I have workt in my Gardin & Doubled hoes yn, & knit Some. Sett Some french Turnips. mr Ballard made plan, the Boys Cutt bushes, James had a brother Calld to See him from ye westwd.   at home. James Sturges Brother here. ye Boys Cut Bushes.  
 

 

 June 20, 1794, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Phillip Norcross Son & 3d Child. XX.

Clear & Cool morn. I was Calld at 5h morn to See Capt Phillip Norcross wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 20m after noon of a fine Son, her 3d Child. I left mother & Child Cleverly at 4h Pm, was Conducted by water to Shepherds wharf, from there walkt home. Calld at Capt Neys, Gows, Parmers, Sewalls. there Bot 2 Skeins Silk, 1/2 Dozn table Spoons, Cost 2/2. Calld at mr Danys, Norcross, & T. Hinkleys, arivd at home at 7h pm. Dolly returnd at Evn. mr Town & Sturges Sleep here. James is unwell.   at Capt Norcross. Birth 24th. receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Town Sleeps here.
