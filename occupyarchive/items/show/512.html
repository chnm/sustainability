Abstract Note

We had to act fast to establish this page, as actions in solidarity with OCCUPY WALL STREET & WWW.OCCUPYTOGETHER.COM are spreading like wildfire. This action will take place JULY 2012 in MONTE RIO, CALIFORNIA. As this action is in the formative stages of planning, more details will be announced in due.

Many people have no idea what BOHEMIAN GROVE is. While rumors fly, we simply urge you to visit wikipedia, as this will present an unbiased view of the subject: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bohemian_grove

OCCUPY BOHEMIAN GROVE is "unofficial" and NOT AFFILIATED with Anonymous, Adbusters or Alex Jones/InfoWars. We are showing solidarity with Occupy Wall Street and this action will be composed of individuals from all backgrounds.
