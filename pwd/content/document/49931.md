April 27. 1795
To Lt. John. McClallen
Dep. of War A Office
April 27.1795
Sir, 
It is intended for the future that each  Officer must make out his own pay rolls, I therefore enclose you some blank rolls one of which together with the Musters you will at the expiration of every two months transmit to this office when an examination will take place and the amount thereof transmitted to you through the usual channel
I am
Sir
your obdsvt.
Wm  Simmons
Lt. John McClallen
in the Corps of A & E
Albany New York
