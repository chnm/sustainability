 August 23, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Hardins Son. XX.

Clear and warm. I was Calld to See mrs Hardin at [  ]h morn, Shee was Deld of a Son before I arivd, assisted by Doct Hubbard. I left her Comfortable, wrode behind mr Saml Cumings to mr Wessons, find her very unwell; went to Son Pollards, hir Child is unwell. I Dind at mr Lambarts, allended meeting aflern & went to mr Burtuns.   at mr Hardins & at meeting. Saml Norcross & his wife here. Birth 35th. 
 

 

 August 24, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Shower. mr Benjamin Calld me to See his Child, it Expird at 4h pm before that I reacht there. mrss Mosier and my Self Put on the grave Cloaths and I Came away. Calld to See mr Burtuns Child, it is mending. I Calld on my Dagts and mr Gill as I went. the Latter is unwell. mr Ballard been to mr Whites and to a Parrish Meeting.   at mr Benjamins, his Child Expird this afternoon.  
 

 

 September 4, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dagt Lambart and Sally Hamlin Dind here. James Moore Calld me to See his wife who is very unwell. mrs Crage, Burtun and others were here after I left home.   at James Moores.  
 

 

 September 6, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mrs Moore Some more Comfortable, walkt to Colo Duttuns farm, wrode mr Moores hors 2 miles then wrode with Eunis Cottle allmost to mr Danas. Calld there, took Tea. her Childn, the Twins, have got the itch or Some other humir. I walkt home from there, find that mr [sic] Sons wife and my own Dagts have been here, Cyrus allso. mr Gows Child and a Child of Daniel Carrs Expird this morn at ye hook.   at Ditoes. Deaths of mr Daniel Carrs and Gows Childn this morn.  
 

 

 September 7, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a light Shower. I went to the Stores at the hook and to See mrss Molloy. Shee has a Soar fingr. I attended prayers at the funerals of mr Carrs and mr Gows Childn. Calld at Capt Fellebrowns, his infant is unwell.   at the hook. attended at the funeral of two Children.  
 

 

 September 8, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Moores 5th Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn. I was Calld out of Bed to go to James Moores. raind before I reacht there, his wife was Deld of a Dagt at 7h 30m. I tarried all night, mrs Tylor and Welman allso.   at James Moores. Birth 36th. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 September 9, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I left my patiet Cleverly after Breakfast. Calld at both my Dagts, find Rhoda Pollard is very feeble. mr Ballard Sett out on a Tour of Surveying yesterday. Son Jona here ys aftern. Georg Andrews Came here to help Ephm, Sleeps here. Sarah Neall Came to work again. Sally Cocks is unwell, her Brother Charls here.   at Ditoes. Sally Cocks is unwell.  
 

 

 September 11, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I tarried at Son Pollards till after Dinner, then went to the Stores and Son Lamberts. was at mr Crages and Calld on Sally Hamlin; and at Old mr Pollards, his Grand Son David put a puter Buttun up his Nose. Doct Coleman took it out. mrss Smith Calld me in to See her, made me a present of 3/4 yd Lawn. little Rhoda Seems a little more Comfortable.   at my Sons and other Neighbrs. Son Cyrus is 39 years old this day.  
 

 

 September 13, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, ye wind Southard. Cyrus Came home, he and Sally & Ephm attended worship all Day, I in ye aftern. Calld at Son Pollards, their Child is much as Shee was, Polly Pollard there. I Calld at Son Lambarts, they went to meeting. my Dagt Came here this morn, Brot me a New Bonnit which Shee made, found Silk and past board and gave me.   at meeting & at my Childrens. my Sons & Sally went to Jonas after meeting.  
 

 

 September 14, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I have been to work in my Gardin, Gatherd Beens & Sheld ym. went to Bed and was in a Drows when wakend by mr GerShom Cocks to go to assist his wife in Labour. I went by water, arivd at at [sic] near 12h.   at mr Gershom Cockss. 
 

 

 September 15, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Gerssm Cockss Dag t. XX.

Clear and very warm. mrss Cocks was Safe Deld at 2h Morn of a fine Dagt, her first Child. I left her and inft Cleverly and arivd home at 10h. I receivd 6/7 for my Service. I left my medesin there thro forGetfullness. Sally went for them. mr Town Came here, his famely are well.   at Ditoes. Birth 37th. receivd 6/7. Sarah Neal went to work at mr Densmors aftern.  
 

 

 September 18, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I have been at home picking wool. Sally Coloured yarn for to make womens Cloaths. [Jamy] Hanskom here.   at home.  
 

 

 September 24, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I helpt break the wool and put the bed quillt into the fraim. mrs Haill Calld and helpt Chalk it.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 September 25, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my Dagt invited her Neighbour Ladies to quillt. we finisht the quillt and had a Sociable meeting. mrss Haywood, widdow Haill and Dagt, Cragen, two mrs Fullers, 2 mrs Stackpols, Wiman, Dingla & Hawl and Collar were the Ladies who assisted my Dagt.   at Ditoes. my Dagt had a Bed Quillt quillted. mr [Putnm] & wife there.  
 

 

 September 27, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Towns 7th Dagt. XX.

rainy part of the Day. my Dagt had her women Calld. Shee was not very ill till after 5h pm, was Delivd at 6 of a Dagt. it had an obstruction of breath at the Nostrils and Expird at 8h Evn. mr Dagt was Exercised with faintness, but revivd and Seemd Confortable after midnight, Slept Some. mrs Spring and Straten tarried all night. I took Sleep after 3h morn.   at Son Towns. Birth 38th. the infant Expird at 8h Evng. mr Danies Dagt Expird aftern.  
 

 

 September 28, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mrs Spring and Strattne went home fore n, mrss Eunice Stakpoole Came to Nurs my Dagt. the infant was interd at 5h pm. my Dagt is as Comfortable as Can be Expected. revd mr Cushman attend at the house, made a prayer well adapted.   at my Sons, the infant was interd at 5 pm.  
 

 

 September 29, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. I left my Dagt at noone. Calld to See mrs Lithgow, Shee has got the mumps. we wrode as far as mr Daws when it began to rain, it abated and we Sett out again, wrode in the rain. I had a fall from my hors by mr Thomases but did not receivd much hurt. we arivd at our house at 6h, am informd of the Death of mr Danies infant Dagt. it Expird on last Lords day.   at Ditoes and returnd home. Calld at Esqr Lethgows. Son Town Sleeps here.  
 

 

 October 4, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren. I went to meeting. mr Gillit Performd. I went to Son Lambarts at intermition, was Calld from there to Colo Sewalls; his Dagt Meriah Expired before I arivd. I tarried there. mrs Harris & I put on her Grave Cloaths. I tarried all night.   at meeting & at Colo Sewalls. his Dagt Expired. Shee was Born May 11, 1792 at 6h pm.  
 

 

 October 5, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came from Colo Sewalls fore n. Calld at mr Parkers & and to See mrs Voce and Old Lady Howard, Crost the river, Calld at mr Burtuns, Son Lambarts, mr Gills. Came to Son Pollards at 1h pm. we quillted a Coat for her before we Slept. mrs Haywood, Hodges, Polly Pollard, Patty Easty and I assisted her.   at Son Pollards & other Neigrs from Colo Sewalls.  
 

 

 October 8, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I was informd this morn that my Dagt Lambart was unwell. I went to See her, find her more Comfortable than Shee was last night. Calld at mr Gills to See her Son who is unwell. Came to Son Pollards, tarried all night. mr Ballard Calld there, he Came home from Surveying this Day.   at my Childns and mr Gills. Son Town Slept here, his folks all well as Can be Expected.  
 

 

 October 11, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, raind aftern. Dagt Lambart Came here. mr Isaac Savage Calld me to See his wife who is unwell. I tarried all night & Son & Dagt Lambt tarried at our house.   at Isaac Savages, his wife unwell.  
 

 

 October 12, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleasant. I returnd home. Spoke with mrs Burtun who informd me her Son James is unwell. Sally Cocks is washing. Shee went to mr Lambarts at Evng. I was Calld to See mrs Savage again. mr Ballard been to town meeting.   at Ditoes. Came home and went there again at Evn.  
 

 

 October 13, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came from Savages to mr Burtuns, Dind. James is unwell. I Calld at mr Lambts & mr Pittss, he is unwell. Slept at Son Pollards. John Abbott moovd there.   at Ditoes & other Neigrs. 
 

 

 October 18, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Pollards Dagt.

Clear. I was Calld at 2h m to See my Dagt Pollard. Shee was Safe Deld at 8h foren of a Dagt. I tarried with her all Day and Sett up with her at night. Shee was Exercised with pain.   at Son Pollards. Birth 39th. 
 

 

 October 20, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of James Page Junrs Son. XX.

rainy. I was Calld at Day break from Son Pollards by James Page Junr to See his wife, find her illness was Coming on. I took a little Sleep there this foren. my Patient was Deld of a Son, her first Born, at 10h Evn. I took a nap by the fire after midnight. her women all tarried thro ye night.   at Ditoes and James Pages. Birth 40th. I receivd 7/9d as reward.
