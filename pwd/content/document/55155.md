Accountants Office Oct. 5th 1796 I certify that there is due to Capt. Edward Miller and the men under his command at Middletown the sum of one hundred five Dollars 46/100, being for his & their pay for the month of September 1796: which sum is to be transmitted to John Chester Supervisor Connecticut the said Capt Miller to be accountable. Dollars 105 46/100 Wm Simmons Acct. The Secy of War
