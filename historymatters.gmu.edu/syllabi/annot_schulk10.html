This assignment is an example of what Professor Randy Bass has labeled "The Novice in the Archive." It utilizes a rich electronic archive of primary source materials, which high school students would not usually have access to, to teach research skills. It builds upon the earlier introductions to search engines and the evaluation of Web sites. The assignment focuses on only a small portion of the enormous "Valley of the Shadow" archive, in part because that portion of the archive best supplements the other materials I have always used in my survey course.

An electronic version of the assignment sheet can be found at http://www.digitalhistory.com/schools/PembrokeHillSchool/sfproj/valleyasc.html
