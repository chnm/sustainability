Treasury Department April 13th 1790
Sir
The Secretary at war informs me that in the Returns of Invalid Pensioners in the State of Massachusetts there is a Sum of Dollars 498 33 100 to be added.
I therefore request that You will pay the Said additional Sum upon the same principles as required by my instructions of the 30th of January last.
I am, Sir
Your Obedt Servt,
Alex Hamilton
Secy of the Treasy
Benj. Lincoln Esqr
Collector for the Port
of Boston
