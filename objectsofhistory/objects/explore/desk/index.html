The Jefferson Desk

In 1776, when Thomas Jefferson came to Philadelphia to join the Second Continental Congress, he hired Benjamin Randolph to build a writing box based on his specifications. This compact design helped him work while away from his home in Virginia, and he drafted the Declaration of Independence upon the desk.
