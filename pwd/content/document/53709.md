Colonel Meigs
19th May 1796
Recd 1st June

Colonel Samuel Hodgdon 
Intendant of Stores
Philadelphia
Greenville 19th May 1796. 
Sir. 
Your letters of the 25th March & of the 1st of April last, are received, containing forms for keeping the Accounts of Army Clothing & of Indian Goods.  I shall endeavor to comply with your request as soon as possible, I did not know till lately Who the proper department for making my Returns of Indian Goods.  
The delivery of the Summer Clothing to the Troops, & the movements of the Army will prevent me from complying with your wishes as soon as I should otherwise do.  
I am sir your Obedient Servant
R: J: Meigs. 
Colonel Hodgedon
