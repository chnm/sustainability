The following object was submitted to the Hurricane Digital Memory Bank a couple of months before Helen Hill\'s January 4, 2007 murder. The theme of the short film (There\'s a Monster in New Orleans) holds new meaning following Helen\'s murder. <br/>\r\nTwo of New Orleans\' talented artists, award-winning filmmaker Helen Hill and musician/vocalist Paul Gailiunas, contribute regularly to the Halloween \"Advent-style\" online calendar known as Gothtober. New Orleans is fortunate that despite losing their Midcity home, they have returned with their son Francis (b. 2004), Rosie the pig, and assorted other beings.\r\n\r\nIn order to view and hear their October 2006 contribution, please select the following link and then click on Oct. 9th to access their Flash movie. Or, you could copy and paste this url into your web browser: <br/>http://www.gothtober.com/\r\n\r\n<br/>To view and hear their first post-Katrina Halloween statement (October 2005), please select the following link, select Archives, 2005, and then click on Oct. 9 to access the flash movie. Or, you could copy and paste this url into your web browser:<br/>http://www.gothtober.com/ARCHIVE/2005/index.html\r\n
