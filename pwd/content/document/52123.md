guern._. November 21 1795
Sir I Should be verry glad if you will perhaps To Send me a bout Twenty Dollers by The Bearrar of I [undecipherable] Pay The Bearrar Ten of it for Halliny & I am in want for a Trifel at [undecipherable] I [Exppacted?] Some Yestherday and Could Not git a Singel Shilling & I Cant Tell Wether or No be for Night my Wife may macke Som Trobl for me or I shold not aske for it a preasent Sir by Complying you wil oblige Sir your humbl Servan
To Mr. Samel Hogdon
Jacob Sorber
Eqr.
