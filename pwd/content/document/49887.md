D. of War Accountants Office
April 24th. 1795
Nicholas Fish.Esq.
New York
Sir
The Treasurer has remitted to you to be transmitted to Lt. John McClallen at Albany being for the pay of himself and is recruits for January, February and March 1795 the sum of three hundreds, ten dollars and Ninety Cents, for which sum you will please take is receipt to be accountable to the Office for the due application thereof.
I am Sir Yc.
William Simmons
