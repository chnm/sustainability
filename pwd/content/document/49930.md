D. of War Accountants Office
April 27th. 1795
Lieut. John McClallen
of Artill. & Engineers
Albany New York
Sir
It is intended for the fu= =ture that each officer must make out his own pay rolls I therefore enclose you some blank rolls, one of which together with the Musters you will at the expiration of every two months transmit to this Office when an examination will take place and the amount there =of transmitted to you through the usual channel.
I am Sir Y.C

William Simmons
