Carolyn Lindsey, 1967-68.\r\n\r\nThe Lindsey family were long-time residents of the Fazendeville community. Prior to Hurricane Katrina, the Lindseys lived in Meraux in St. Bernard Parish. These photographs were collected in November 2006 during an interview with the Lindsey family at their new home in Baton Rouge.
