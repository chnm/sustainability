The New York Committee for Occupational Safety and Health

Title

The New York Committee for Occupational Safety and Health

Description

The New York Committee for Occupational Safety and Health is a non-profit coalition of local unions and individuals dedicated to the rights of workers to safe and healthful jobs.The sections of the NYCOSH web site in this archive have been saved as an overview of the health and safety information related to the aftermath of the World Trade Center attacks which were made available by NYCOSH in the first year following the catastrophe. As an archive, these pages serve as a kind of bibliography of news, research and institutional action about health and safety issues raised by the WTC attacks.
