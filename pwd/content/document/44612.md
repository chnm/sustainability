From
The Secretaries of the
Treasury & War
2d. May 1793

French Treaty
official papers Copy of War
111
Philadelphia
May 2nd, 1793
&#10003; 
Sir,
A conformity of opinion, and upon the same
grounds, enables us to submit to you a joint Answer
to the third of the Questions, which you were pleased
to propose on the 18 th of April to Heads of Departments 
and the Attorney General.
We have concluded that
this mode would be more agreeable to you than
a repetition of the same ideas and arguments in
separate answers.
With perfect respect 
& the truest attachment 
We have the honor to be Sir 
your most obedt & hum servants
The President of the U. States
Alexander Hamilton
H Knox
