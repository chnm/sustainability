158 Accountants Office

                                                       December 9th 1795


I Certify that there is due to Abiathen Alvords, late a private in the 2nd Regiment under the command of Capt Phelon, the Sum of Eight dolles and Sixty three cents being for the balance of his Pay to the 4th of November 1791 the day of his death which sum is payable to Caleb Strong Esqr Attorney to Jonathan. Alvord Father and Heir at Law of the said Abiather Alvord deceased._

____________ Dolls 8 63

        ____

        100

The Secy of War


                                        William Simmons
