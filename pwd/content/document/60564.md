West Point 31 Decr
P: A: Dransey
recd 6 January
Ansd 13th ditto

Willam Simmons Eqr
Accountant of the War Department
Philadelphia
West Point Decembr 21st 1797
Sir
in answer to your letter of the 17th of November which I received only on the 30th of December, (while in my way from Oswego) I refer you to a letter of the secretary of war, to Colonel Rochefontaine of the 15th of november last, which I suppose will remove any Difficulty, 
I am 
Sir
your most obedt Servant
P. A. Dransy
Lieut Arty & Engineers
William Simmons Eqr
