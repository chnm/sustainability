W.D. Accountants Office
                                   April 11th 1794

Sirs

               I return your Muster rolls for

Jany, Feby and March in order that you may insert the date of enlistment of Gersham Moor and request you may in future make your musters complete before they transmitted other= wise it is impossible to form a payroll_ If recruits join from any other Company it should be noted in the Column of remarks _ I also return the certificates of the Magistrates as they are not necessary to be produced untill you settle your recruiting account ~

                         I am Sir etc
                            Joseph Howell

Ensign Garet[?] Voorhees

  Brunswick
    New Jersey
