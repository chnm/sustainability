My hurricane experience for Katrina was full of arguing, fighting, and bickering between all of my family members and it turned out to be hell. During Gustav, I learned my lesson and went where my family wasn\'t going. I ended up in Folsom, LA. Me and about 4 friends. We had 3 generators and a fully stocked fridge and it was surprisingly under the circumstance very fulfilling, I know I will not go anywhere without a generator during a storm again. They make a huge difference. I feel Katrina was a learning experience and taught me how to be patient but take care of yourself and make sure others are taken care of also.
