20
James Smith
Merch.t
Second Street
p.er Post Philadelphia
Pittsburg 2.d Marsch 1796
Easteemed Friend,
The Secretary of War will pay
thee four Hundred Dollars, which 
please place to the A.c of C. Ormsby and
me and forward me a Re.t __
I
fully expected to have been able to have
forwarded you a considerable Sum on My
Own Ac.t before this, but could not in
Consequence of the Army's not beeing paid
the payment has Commenced and I
expect before long to receive Money
from Jones. So Soon Shall Certainly 
remit you all I possibly Can
13 days
Thy Friend
Isaac Gregg


James Smith
