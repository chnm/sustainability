Magor Hodgden Esqr Philadelphia
Ship on barge June ye 5 1794
Dear Sir
as Harvest is cloas at hand I canot go down for Loads at this time.  Beg ye faver of you to send forward 5 or 6 loads to me by Andrew Mclure which he will Recet for and I hope Deliver Safe your Complyance will Much oblage your friend and humbel servant
Robert Culbertson
Magor Hogden Esqr
