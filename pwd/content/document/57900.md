Obtain the letters respecting the burning of the towns and frigates and the plan and when to be executed.  Every paper which may show the projects to be secured.  I am well informed to certain points but want confirmation in others.  You may rely on my protection.  Don't send [?] for money.

Take care and do not be deceived.
