 August 22, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of Jotham Smiths Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 9h morn to See Jotham Smiths wife who was in Labour & was Safe Delivd of a fine Son at ye 12th h. I returnd home at 3h pm then went to See mr Savage. find him Some more Comfortable. the Girls gone to See mrs Hamlin this Eving. Cyrus been to the hook to Day.   at mr Smiths & Nr Savages.  
 

 

 August 24, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Cool. I was unwell before nn and attended public worship aft. Revd mr Foster Spoke from Geneses 2 & 7; made a very fine discorce. wido Williams and mrs Lois Harris here after meeting, Wilm Howard & mr Fillbrook also. Dan'l Robbins returnd from Boston.   At meeting. Calld at wido Williamss. 
 

 

 August 25, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mrs Childs here to warp a web. mr Benn Bisbe paid me 6/ for attending his wife the 28th of March Last in the hour of Travil.   at home.  
 

 

 August 26, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 a rainy Day. I was Calld by Doct Colman to See his Lady in Labour. Shee Calld her women at Evin.   at the 7th hour at Dr Colmans.  
 

 

 August 27, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth Doct Colmans Son. XX.

at Doct Colmans. his Lady was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 3h morn. he went to visit mr Moses Sewalls Lady; found her very Dangerous. brot his nurs & I returnd at ye 9th h morn. Left mother & Child Cleverly. I took a nap of Sleep & went to mrs Williamss & returnd at Evnn. the Girts took a walk with Belsy Chever.   at Dos & at widow Williamss.  
 

 

 August 28, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Pitts Town; informs at his return that mrs Suall is yet alive & that mr Parmers Famely are Sick & much Distrest having no nurs. mr Ballard brot 2 Cheeses which he had of Nathan Swetland, for Katy which wd 13-1/2 lb. this Day my Dat Town is 30 years old. Hannah is gone to wido Williamss.   at home all Day, gatherd Herbs.  
 

 

 August 29, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very hott. I went to the hook; visited the Sick then went to mr Woodwards to See his Children. find his Son in Chains & his Dat very ill with a Swelling on her throat. Shee was Brot on a Slead to mr Browns.   I was at the hook & at mr Wdwds. 
 

 

 August 30, 1788, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth of mr Shubl Hinklys Dagt. XX

Clear & warm. I was Calld to Shuball Hinklys wife in travil at ye 12th hour. Shee was Safe Delivd of a very fine Daut at 2h pm. I returnd home at 7, Left mother & Child Cleverly. found mrs Williams & mrs Hamlin here. Lady Foster has been here also. we Raisd the Saw mill floom. Sherebiah Town Sleeps here.   I was at Suball Hinklys. Sheribiah Town Came here.  
 

 

 September 6, 1788, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I attended funeral of Doct Colmans infant. Calld at mrs Williamss, find her very sick. the Doct gave her a Puke. I tarrd all night.   Funeral of mrs Springer & Doct Colmans infant. I attendid ye latter.  
 

 

 September 7, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home; left mrs Williams very ill. I Slept Some & went there again at Evin. mrs Pollard & others there. Charlotte & Polly Cool there. Parthena watches. the Revd mr Moore preacht.   Hannah is gone to Son Towns with Sherebiah.  
 

 

 September 14, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I attended worship in public. Nethaniel Norcross Desird prayers he being Sick with a feavor. Revd mr Foster Deliverd two Exelent Discoarses from Psalm 90 & 12 vers.   I was at meeting & at wido Wilmss.  
 

 

 September 16, 1788, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I have been at home, am not So well as I could wish. mrs Savage, wido Williams & mrs Harris here at Evin. the girls went to mr Craggs. Jona is not returnd.   at home. I was Calld to Eliab Shaws in the night.  
 

 

 September 17, 1788, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth of Eliab Shaws Daft. receivd my fee Oc tr 22, by Ephm, 1792. XX.

I was Calld between 12 & 1h morn to Eliab Shaws wife in travil. Shee was Safe Delivd at ye 11th of a fine Daught. I left them Cleverly & returnd at 4 pm. mr Hains Learned Came here; he left home Last Wednsday. I receivd a Letter by him from Sister Waters, Dated at Boston. mr Ballard is gone to ye hook. Taylor Came here ys Day.   at Shaws. mr Learned Came here, brogt me a Letter from Sist Waters of ye 12th inst. David Fletchr a Dagt Born.  
 

 

 September 19, 1788, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I was Calld to see mrs Colman; tarried all day. her Sister Left her bound for Nuberg. the Bridg over this Crick was finisht ys Day. Elisabeth Knowland Came to the Doct s to Nurs mrs Colman.   I was at Doct Colmans.  
 

 

 September 21, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I attended public worship. the revd mr Foster Delivd 2 fine Discoarses from Romans 10 & [  ].   I attendd pbc worship & was at wido Wimss. 
 

 

 September 22, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Cloudy aftn. Cyrus went from home Bound for winslow. I have been at home. my Girls washt. Thomas Smyle Came here to work. David Learned Sleeps here. Leut John Shaw paid me 6/ for attending his wife in travil augst ye 7th.   I have been at home.  
 

 

 September 25, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Welches Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard & the other men went to Pitts town to help rais a mill. Shere Town & James Stackpool & mr Learned Dind here. I was Calld to See mrs Welch in travil. Shee was Deld at 9 pm. I Came home at 11.   at mr Welches.  
 

 

 September 29, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mr Childss Son who is unwell. I went as far as mr Edsons. Came home at 2 pm. Broth Moore here. I went afterward to old mr Isaac Savages. Came home at Eving. I Calld at Doct Colmans; his Lady made me a presant of a Cap.   at mr Childs. Dolly finished weaving the Check, 39-2/1 [sic] yds. mr Parmers Son at winthrop Expird of a feavor.  
 

 

 October 2, 1788, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to mr Brookss & mr Welches; find his wife not so well as I could wish. Calld at wido Williams. Hannah gone to Sleep there. My Girls washt the flors. I had Sole Leather for a pair of Shoes of mr Welch, price 1/8.   I was at mr Brookss. 
 

 

 October 4, 1788, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I was Calld to Revd mr Fosters, his Lady unwell. was Calld to Thoms Hinklys wife in travil at Eving. a Boisterus wind & rain as I was on the way there. arivd Safe.   at Revd mr Fosters & Thos Hinkleys.  
 

 

 October 5, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thoms Hinklys Dagt. XX.

at mr Hinklys; her women Calld at 7h Evn. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Daugt at 11 pm. I tarried all night.   at mr Hinklys.  
 

 

 October 6, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Fuller & Child. Calld to see mrs Bates; find her Better. returnd home at 10h m. receivd 7/ of mr Hinkly at magr Stickneys & 7/ on Suball Hinklys acount. wido Williams here. Polly Bisbe workt for me makeing Hannah a goun.   at Hink[lys] & Fullers, mr Batess & mr Westons.  
 

 

 October 12, 1788, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Isaac Clearks Son & Dagt. XX.

Clear & Pleast. I attended public worsp, Revd mr Foster Discoarst from GeneSS 33d & 4th. mr Gille went from here this morn. my Girls went to mr Childs at Eving. Elias Crage & Hannah mc Keckny Cryed. I was Calld in haste at 11h Eving to mr Isaac Clearks Lady in travil. Shee was Safe Delivd of a fine Son & a Daugt before 12; all likely to Do well. I receivd 12/ as a reward.   at meeting & at mr Isaac Clearks.  
 

 

 October 13, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I returnd home from mr Clearks, Left all Cleverly.   at mr Clearks.  
 

 

 October 20, 1788, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Varsalbboro. I went to Colo Norths, receivd 3/ on mr Edsons acount for attending his wife in travil May 12, 1787 & for Salve. it began to rain Evn.   at Colo Norths & wido Williamss.
