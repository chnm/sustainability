Accountants Office,
May 21st 1794
I certify that there is due to Joseph Strong the Sum of
One hundred Sixty eight Dollars for his extra services rendered
as an assistant Hospital Store keeper from the 12 Nov.r 1793 to 24
July 1794 inclusive
William Simmons
