 June 20, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Sprinkling of rain. I went from mr Whites to See mrs LaShance who is Sick, from there to See mrs Daws; find her Cleverly. I Calld at mrs Sherburns, Selvesters, Greelys, Capt Fillibrowns and the mr Norcrosses. arivd at home before night. mr Ballard went up to town. Ephm workt at mr Kenadas. mr Whites Child Seems to be more Comfortable.   at mr Whites & other Neighbours. I receivd 6/ of Saml Cumings Junr for allending his wife.  
 

 

 June 21, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a Coolish air. I have been at home, workt in my Gardin Some. mr Silas Barns here. mr Ballard went to his meddow. Ephm went to Pittstn to See Capt Grants vessel Lancht but was Disapointed, it being Launcht yester day. Jona here, informd me mrs Jones is very unwell ocationed by her Husbands ill usuage, & keeping her in the Seller Bare foot. O the wretch, he Deserves Severe puneshment.   at home. I was informd yt P Jones had abusd his wife.  
 

 

 July 2, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I workt in my Gardin Some foren. was Calld to See Georg Bollons wife who was in Labour, at 1h pm. Shee remaind in a Linguring Situation thro ye night.   at Georg Boltons.  
 

 

 July 3, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Georg Boltons 4th Son & 5 th Child.

Clear. my Patients illness increast at ye 11th h morn & Shee was Safe Deld at 30m pm of a Son, both mother & inft are Cleverly. I left them at 3 & arivd at home before night, Considerably fatagud. Hannah & Patty at mr Densmores.   at Does. Birth 25th. I Bot a pair Shoes for Hannah, Cost 4/8.  
 

 

 July 5, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part off ye Day. Hannah washt. I went to mr Livermrs, his Lady & I went & Spent aftern at mr Densmores, mrs Edson there. Dolly Came home, tarries ys night. mr Ballard been to ye hook, Bot 2/1 Galn N E Rhum & 1 galn molases. Dolly went to mr Wessons, Bot her Self a pair Shoes. I gave her the money.   at mr Densmores. S.J. Foster & Sam l Jackson paid me for asisting their wives.  
 

 

 July 9, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wilm Swantns Dag t. XX.

Cloudy & a moderate rain aftern. I was Calld ys morn to See mrs Swanton who is in Labour with her first Child. Shee was Safe Deld at 9h Evng of a fine Dagt. mother & inft are Cleverly. I tarried thro thee night.   at Capt Savages. Birth 26th. I receivd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 July 14, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Savage Calld me to See his wife, Shee has a pain in her Ear & face. I went to my Sons & mr Burtuns, Bot a Lenten hand krcf @ 4/6, at mr WeSsoms Bot pint & half Brandy, Cost 2/1.   at my Sons & at Neig rs. 
 

 

 July 17, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. S. Jewet Foster here, his inft is mending. he, my Son Cyrus & mrs Rockwd Dind here. mr Daws Sent me 6/ as a reward for assisting his Lady in Labour June 17th. mrs Rockwd went home on hors Back, Cyrus & Hannah in Company. Hannah went to See her other Brother, all well there.   at home.  
 

 

 July 21, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Preskotts 2nd Son. XX.

Clear & fine morn. I was Calld at the Dawn off Day to See Benn Preskotts wife, who was Safe Deld at 1h pm of a Son. I left her Some what Comfortable at 3h 15m. had to wride in a Shower, arivd home at 4. mr Preskott gave me 6/ as a reward. mr Savage was there to See if I Could go & See his wife which Causd my hurrying home. Dolly Complains off a pain in her head.   at Benn Preskotts. Birth 27. his 2nd Child. Cyrus been to Winthrop this Day.  
 

 

 July 23, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went as far as my Sons. Calld to See mrs Andrews and mrs Savage, they are not very well. mr Hamlin had a house phraim raisd this aftern. mr [sic] Dagts are gone there to a Daunce, Ephm allso. Sally was gone to Capt Savages.   at Jonas & other Neig rs. mr Ballard finisht geting his hay.  
 

 

 July 24, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Andrews Son. XX.

Cloudy. I was Calld out Before Day to See thee wife off Saml Dawin, was met at ye foot of ye hill beyond Savage Boltons with News that Shee was got to bed, & returnd home. mr Andrews Calld me at noon, his wife Deld off a Son at 4h PM. I tarried all night, my Patient Cleverly.   at mr Andrwss. Birth 27th. Saml Dawin had a Child Born ys morn.  
 

 

 July 25, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 rain foren, Clear aftern. I returnd from mr Andruss at 9h morn, find Hannah Cox here to work for me. mr Ballard went to Pownalboro ys aftern. mr Vahan Calld here.   at mr Andrewss. 
 

 

 July 28, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Sheppads Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to Seee mrss Sheppard, arivd at 12 O Clok. Shee Calld mrs Ney at Evng and was Deld at 9h of a very fine Son, her 3d Child. mrs Cox & I Sett up with my Patient who is as well as Could be Expected.   at mr Sheppards. Birth 28 th. 
 

 

 July 29, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Warm. I tarried with my Patient thro ye Day, mrs Cox allso. I left her there at 7 Evn. I receivd 24/ 8 as a reward, walkt home. Calld at Capt FilleBrowns, Daws & Norcross.   at Does.  
 

 

 August 1, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Sett out to Survey for mr Vauhan. mr Wing returnd mr Ballards papers. he & my Son Jona Dind here. Cyrus here aftern. my Dagts went to mr Wessons, Shee & her Son Chever Calld here foren.   at home. mr Wing gave mr Ballard 1/2_ for Servis he had Done.  
 

 

 August 2, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Savage who is Exercisd with a Severe pain in her Stomach. I adminestred remeds which gave relief. I went to See Jobe Springer who is very unwell. my Dagts went to quillt for mrs Baxter, Lucy Pettie allso.   at mr Savages & Springers.  
 

 

 August 3, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home, Calld at mr Pollards. Hannah informd me mrs Sheppard wisht to See me & I went, find her Cleverly. he made me a prest off a bottle off wine. I purchast a pair lether Sliprs @ 5/, papr pins /9.   at Savages, Pollards & Sheppards.  
 

 

 August 4, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to pittstn Early ys morn, Jona gone allso. I went on Board Capt Howland, Bot 8_ lb wool @ 1/9; to mr Wessons Bt 2_ lb Loaf Sugar @ 2/4, 2 of Brown @ 1/1 & 1lb raisens @ /9; to mr Childs, paid 2/6 for makeing Patties Shoes. mrs Baxter here on a visit. we had of mr Wesson 1 quart W E R & 3 Lemmons, of mr Livermr 6lb Butter, And of others 7_ lb Lamb, 2 Dzn Pigions. Abrm Page of washington Sent me 3/ which has been my due Since his Twins Birth.   at mr Wessons & other Neigrs, on Board Howland &C.  
 

 

 August 7, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I was Calld to See mr Andrewss inft, who is very Sick with ye thrush. I tarried all night, had not much rest. I was at Jonas, mrs Swanton there.   at mr Andwss. 
 

 

 August 12, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I Did not attend worship. Cyrus Came up about noon, Laid Down & Slept. mr Winget Calld. Page was taken out of the water this morn. mr Medcalf here for me to Go & See mrs Sheppard. I went at Evn, find her Nurs is gone.   at mr Sheppards. David Page was tak n out of the water this morn.  
 

 

 August 17, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Ephm & Polly went to School, mr Ballard to mr Savages. Hannah is makeing Lucy a hatt. I was Calld to See mrs Savage, tarried all night. O the flees.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 August 18, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I tarried at mr Savages till near night, then walkt home. Calld at mr Burtuns & Drank Tea. find mr Pitt & his Bror here. receivd a line from Brr Collins of 7th inst; all well.   at Does. receivd a Letter fm Br Collins of 7th inst. 
 

 

 August 20, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Petengails Dagt. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy aftn. I was Calld by mr Benn Petingail (at 5 ys morn) to See his Lady who is in Labour, & was Safe Delivd at 9h Evn off a fine Dagt, her fifth Child her & 3d Dagt. her last before was 10 years old last march. I tarried all night, my Patient was Exercisd with Faintness for Some time, but revivd & had rest.   at mr Petengails. Birth 29th. James Cowens wife fell from her hors & hurt her much; is at mr Thomass. 
 

 

 August 21, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I left my Patients Cleverly & returnd home at 9h morn. mr Petingl Bestowd 9/ as a reward. mr Seth Williams & Lady made me a prest off Some Coffee, Tea & Sugar. mr Ballard Surveying hous lotts at ye hook. Jery Fairfield, his wife & her Bror Dind here. Ephm went to Jonans.   at Ditoes. Company here.  
 

 

 August 26, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Churchs Son, his 4th Child. XX.

Clear & Cool. mr Ballard & Epm attended worship foren & my Self aftern. Augustus Ballard, Cyrus and mr Pollard Dind here. I went to mr Livermores, his inft unwell. mr Smith Discourst from Luke 6th 4:5:6:7:8 verss. Saml Churches wife Deld 11h 30m Evng.   at meeting. mr mc Causlands Dagt interd at 3 O Clok. Birth 30th.
