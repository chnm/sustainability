NEW YORK CENTRAL SYSTEM

C.R. Strattman
No. 1 Monroe Street
Toledo, Ohio
Zone 3

October 7, 1944

To Whom It May Concern:

The holder of this letter Gatarino Casillas Rodriguez S.S. No. [redacted], Mexican National, who has completed his six months contract as track laborer for the New York Central, has been an excellent worker and given very satisfactory service. We have used him often as an interpreter and he has cooperated in every way and I would strongly recommend him for this kind of work. Any consideration given him will be deserved.

Yours very truly,
[signature]

C. R. Strattman
Division Engineer, N.Y.C.
Toledo, Ohio, U.S.A.
