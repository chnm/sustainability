Philadelphia 21st, July 1798
Enclosed you have an estimate of the supplied wanted at Springfield; and a sketch of what may be proper to import for the clothing of such troops as may be called to the field.
Buttons  I have not inserted because allthose for the artillery are among ourselves.
I am Sir
your most obt. Servant,
Samuel Hodgdon
James McHenry
Secretary of War,
