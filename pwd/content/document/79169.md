Philadelphia
Department of War
Accountants OfficeDecember 5, 1801
Sir
A statement of musket stocks 
delivered at Philadelphia to contractors for supplying muskets 
was furnished by the late Superintendent of Miilitary Stores
commencing on the 22nd November 1798 & ending 31st
May 1800 which on examination is found incorrect
in several instances, not embracing all the stocks 
acknowledged to have been received by the parties.
It is therefore necessary that a more correct statement
should be furnished which shall include all the
stocks delivered up to this time.  You are accordingly
requested to furnish me with such statement as
early as convenient to enable me to check 
the accounts of these Contractors.
I am Sir
Very Respectfully
Your mo obt servt
William Simmons
William Irvine Esquire
