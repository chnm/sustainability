The one thing I remember most about Katrina is Texas. When Katrina was going to hit, my family and I went to stay with my uncle in Houston. Most of the people in the New Orleans Superdome were bused to the Astrodome in Houston. All I would here on the Texas news was how bad New Orleans people were and how much trouble the kids from New Orleans were having adjusting to Texas\' schools because our education sucked. It made me so ashamed.
