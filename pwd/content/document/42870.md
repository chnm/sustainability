War department
May, 3D. 1792.
Sir,
I beg leave to inform you that I rest upon you entirely for a supply of rations at the posts herein after mentioned, and also for articles in the Quarter Master's line, and clean medical assistance, and for such means of of transportation as shall from time to time be directed. 
I am 
Sir
respectfully, 
your humble servt. 
H. Knox
Sec'y. of War
