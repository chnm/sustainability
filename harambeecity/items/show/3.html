CORE Jazz Album

Dublin Core

Title

CORE Jazz Album

Subject

compilation jazz album CORE used as a fundraiser for the organization.

Description

RECORD ONE, SIDE ONE:
Dinah Washington, "The Blues Ain't Nothing But a Woman Cryin For Her Man"; Cannonball Adderly with Machito and his Orchestra, "Cannonology"; Harry Belafante with Machito and his Orchestra, "Lean on Me"; Herbie Mann with Machito and his Orchestra, "Carabunta"; Steve Allen's Roundtable All-Stars, "I Thought About You"; Chris Connor and Maynard Ferguson, "I Feel a Song Coming On".

RECORD ONE, SIDE TWO:
Louis Armstrong and Duke Ellington, "Cotton Tail"; John Coltrane Quartet, "Exotica"; Dizzy Gillespie, "Paris Swing"; Erroll Garner, "Love for Sale"; Woody Herman Sextet with Nat Adderley, "Lullaby of Birdland"; Charlie Parker & Miles Davis, "Klactoveededstene"

RECORD TWO, SIDE ONE:
Stan Getz & Horace Silver, "Strike Up the Band"; Art Tatum, "Dark Eyes"; Dizzy Gillespie & Charlie Parker, " Groovin' High"; J. J. Johnson & Kai Winding, "The Shrike"; Bud Powell, "Off Minor"; Joe Newman & Zoot Sims, "Tater Pie"

RECORD TWO, SIDE TWO:
Joe Williams With Count Basie & His Orchestra, "Everyday"; Sarah Vaughan with Count Basie & His Orchestra, "Until I Met You"; Count Basie & His Orchestra, "Tickle Toe"; Joe Williams with Lambert, Hendricks & Ross with Count Basie & His Orchestra, "Going to Chicago Blues"; Billy Eckstine with Count Basie & his Orchestra, "Jelly, Jelly"; Tony Bennett with count Basie & his Orchestra, "I Guess I'll Have to Change My Plans"

Creator

A1, S1: J. Mayo Williams, A. K. Salim, Green-Waldman, Herbie Mann, Van Heusen-Mercer, Oppenheim-McHugh-Fields

A1, S2: Duke Ellington; John Coltrane; Dizzy Gillespie; Cole Porter; Shearing-Foster, Charlie Parker

A2, S1:
G. & I. Gershwin; Art Tatum (arranged); Dizzy Gillespie; Jose Ferrer; Thelonious Monk; Adriano Acea

A2, S2:
William York; Green-Wolf; Lester Young, Basie-Rushing; Eckstine-Hines; Dietz-Schwartz

Leeds Music Corp.; United Music Co.; Patricia Music Publication Corp.; Burke & Van Heusen; Robins Music Corp; Jowcol Music; Forshay Music Inc.; Harms, Inc.; Duchess Music Co.; New World Music Corp.; Jose Ferrer Music Inc.; Rockaway Music Corp.; Golden State Songs & Arc Music, Co.; Bregman, Vocco & Conn, Inc.; Advanced Music Corp.:

multiple contributors
