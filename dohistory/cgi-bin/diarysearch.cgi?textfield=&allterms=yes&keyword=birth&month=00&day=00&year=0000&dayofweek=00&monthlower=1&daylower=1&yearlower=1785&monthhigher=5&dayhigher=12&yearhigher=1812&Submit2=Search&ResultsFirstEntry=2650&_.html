 July 8, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, raind aftern & all night. Jona Ballard Junr Calld in to See us, they are wel at Clinton. there was a man found dead at Hallowell.   at home. Jona Junr here. A Death.  
 

 

 July 12, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Son Ephm left his hors for me to go and See his Childn. they are not very wel. I Calld to See mrs Young, find her very low. my Sons Childn Some beller. I went to See mrs Ranlet, find her feeble. was at mr Orocks, their infant is very Sick. Calld at mr Livermores, he has Childn Sick allso. I Slept at my Sons.   at Son Ephms and visited others who were Sick. Capt Gartand Cut his throat.  
 

 

 July 13, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I left my Sons after dinner, Calld at Timy Pages, Capt Pitss, Evliths Store, mr Blacks & mr Orocks. his Babe is no better. mrs Hodges has a Child has a Sweling on his head. I reacht home before night. Calld at Son Jonas.    
 

 

 July 15, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we did not go to meeting. mr Benn Petingail & wife Calld here, Hannahs Getchel, Ballard, Linda, Sally and mr Woods Dagt allso. Allin Lambd, Eph & Wm Ballard Sleep here.   at home.  
 

 

 July 22, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Sally Pollard and D. Lambard took their leav of me and Expected to Sett out for Clinton before breakfast. Son Cyrus after for Rome. may they all arive at their homes in safty is my ardent wish. Hannah Pollard Calld here, took her liev of me, [Sett] out for home. may they all be blest and prosper through life.   at [home]. [Hannahs] Ballard, Pollard & Betsy Wiman [      ].  
 

 

 August 1, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day, a Shower aftern. Allin Lambd Sett out for home this morn after breakfast. I have done hous work, Some in ye gardin, and Mended a Shirt for my husband.   at [home]. Son [Jo ] Calld here ys morn. we were alone ys [night].  
 

 

 August 4, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we found a nest with 13 Eggs, one was rottne. I made Cake. was Calld to Son Jonas, Elisa very unwel. we aplyed Turnip poltis to her bowels which gave relief Soon. mr Ballard been to ye meshene with wool, 4-2/1 lb. he was at Son Ephms, their Babe is unwel. he had _ of a hundd flower which he left with Son Jona.   at Son Jonas, the [Babe] very unwel.  
 

 

 August 7, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been doing hous wk and Picking my refues wool. mr Martin of Hallowell here, Exprest a wish to have me attend his wife prety Soone. Jona Sleeps here.   at home. mr Martin of Hallowell here.  
 

 

 August 12, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 raind in ye foren till 10h when the Sun Shone, we had Showers aftern. Son Ephm Calld here, Said there was Preaching. a mr Channing from Boston, Text Matt 11 C 13-14.   at home. Son Ephm Calld here, Sopha has ye Chicn Pox.  
 

 

 August 15, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Martins 5th Dagt, 6th Child. XX.

Clear. I was Calld 30m after mid night by mr Martin of Hallowell to go and See his wife who is in Labour. I went, Shee was linguring litt 11h 30m Am when Shee was Safe Deld of her 6 Child & 5th Daugt. I went from her to See her Mother who apears to be near ye Close of life. God grant her Change may be happy. I returnd home at 10h Evng. I left my Patients very Comfortable.   at mr Martin Hallowell & at Isaac Clearks. Birth 13. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 August 19, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Shours, Clear aftern. Son Lambd Calld here ys morn, he took Breakfast at Son Jonas. my son & Luther Pierce Calld to See me, Luke Barton allso. mr Ballard to meeting and attended as a Pawl holder at ye Funeral of mr Crosby. it was Dark before he Came home. Son Lambd Sleeps here.   at home. Funeral of Old mr Crosby of this town & Isaac Clearks wife of Hallowell.  
 

 

 August 24, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been very ill. Jonas folks Came to take Care of me. mr Ballard to Son Pollards. Linda & Sally Slept here.   at home, Sick.  
 

 

 August 25, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been very ill with pain in my left Side & a Collic. Dagt Ballard to See me. Linda & Sally here all night.   at home, very unwel.  
 

 

 August 27, 1810, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Linda Getchel washt for me, washt Buttery Shelves, floor & kitchen. Sally Farewel took Tea with us. I have done Some work but feel very feeble. I wish to be grateful for all Gods mercies. Linda Sleeps here.   at home, Some Easier. Sally Farewel Calld here.  
 

 

 September 7, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night, raind at Evng. I have had threats of the Collic but have Sheld mustard, Cut up Some & done my hous work. Ephm hauld us wood. I was Calld by Capt Ruel Howard to go and See his wife at 9h 30m Evng.   at Capt Ruel Howard.  
 

 

 September 8, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Howard [2nd] Dagt. XX.

Cloudy & some rain. mrs Howard was Safe Deld at 2h am of her 2nd Dagt and 5th Child. I returnd home at 10h 30m am, receivd 1 Dolr. left mother and inft Cleverly. performd a Considerable work. I Sent by Ephm Bond, got 2 qts N E Spirit and 3_ lb Sugar. Dagt Ballard in here aftn.   at Ditoes. Birth 14th. receivd 6s, 28 recd 3 & a gift of 10 Candles.  
 

 

 September 12, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home till Evn, was Calld to Robd Kenedas at 10h Ev. Dagt Ballard & Lambd Sleep here.    
 

 

 September 15, 1810, Saturday

 Clear and Cool. Son & Dagt Lambd Calld here from Son Pollards, Sett out for Clinton. Rhoda Pollard Calld in with them, Shee was going to Hallowell. I have gatherd Beens, Sheld & Strung them, Cucumbers for pickels, dug potatoes which I Planted June 20th. Sist Barton and Rhoda Pollard Sleep here.   at [home]. Son and Dagt Lambd Sett out for [home].

 Clear. Sist Came here again. Capt Nichols of OXford, his Dagt Moore of Gardiner & 3 Childn Calld here [I] & mr Delenos Dagt.   at [home]. Funeral Flint Barlons Son and Sidney.  
 

 

 September 26, 1810, Wednesday

 Clear. I have gatherd Some apples in my Gardin, Pared, Cut & put out to Drie. Allin Lambd Calld in, he Came to Conduct a Genlleman to Hallowell. he receivd 3 Dols & _ for Shais & Jorney. he informs his Marm & Sarah are unwel. Wm B. Came home from Viany, brot a Chees wd 7 lb, a Box Butter wd 7 lb & _. Allin & Wm Sleep here.   at home, very unwel.
