I Certify that there is due to Samuel Hodgdon
Superintendant of Military Stores the sum of One thousand three hundred
Dollars being for his own salary & that of Mr John Harris Store keeper,
& the clerks employed in their Offices from 1 October to 31 Decbr.
1799 --             W.S.
Drs. 1300~
