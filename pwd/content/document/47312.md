W. D. Accountants Office July 16th 1794 Sir
The Secretary of War has directed a remittance to you of Two hundred and fifty dollars through the hands of Edward Carrington Esqr Supervisor of Virginia, being for the purpose of mounting the Artillery, for which please to sign duplicate receipts to be accountable for the due application thereof ~
I am Sir &c
Joseph Howell
John Fitzgerald - Collector Alexandria - Virginia
