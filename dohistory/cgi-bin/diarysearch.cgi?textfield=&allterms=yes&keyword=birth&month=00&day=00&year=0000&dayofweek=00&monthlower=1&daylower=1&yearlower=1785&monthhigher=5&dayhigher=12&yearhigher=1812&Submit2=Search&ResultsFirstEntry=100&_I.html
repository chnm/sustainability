 May 28, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 it raind ys forenoon. mr Town & Dexter Left here aftern. Polly & Sally, mr Wil[nin] & wife here. mr Savage dind wh us. Lethgo, Davis, Brooks, Theo H., Jesse & Burten all here ys day. I have been at home till 11 Evn when Calld out of Bed to mr Pattins. went on foot to Brookss, then by water.    
 

 

 May 29, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Pattins Dafter.

I put mrs Pattin to Bed with a Dafter at 4 in Morn. Came by water as far as Daviss Store, then walkt home. arivd at :01: o Clok morn.    
 

 

 May 30, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 it raind. Mr Ballard Paid Blanchard all that was his due for his Servis here & Dismist him. he Breakfasted here then went to ye mill & began a quarril with Jono & made Such an outcry as gave my Self & famely great Surprise. I sint for mr Ballard home from Savages. mr Savage & Streeter came with him. I am makeing a pair of trouses for Ephm. Sherebiah Town Came hr ys aftern, Sleeps here. mrs Wodward Calld going home from ye hook.    
 

 

 June 2, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Isaac Cowens Dafter.X

Calld ys morn at 8/O Clok to Isaac Cowens to See his wife, arivd there at 1 / PM. found her Safe Delivd of a Daughter, Born betwixt Six & Seven O Clok. Left them at 3. Returnd home 7, very much fatagud. had 2 falls from my hors which Lamd me Some. Polly & Sally here when I came home.    
 

 

 June 4, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Briant Fletchers Dafter. fee recd. XX

A very warm Day. I was Calld at 8 ys morn to Brian Fletchers. his wife in Travil & was Safe Delivd of a Dafter Betwen ten & 11. I returnd home at 5 aftern. mr Lee here foren. Benn Edwards, mr Wels & David Learned Dind here. I receivd 6/ of mr Fletcher. Beriah Ingerhams wife was Delivrd of a Dafter 30th of may, Extracted by old mrs Fletcher.    
 

 

 June 10, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. X Georg Boltons Son. X

Cool Morn. I was Calld at 4/O Clok to Georg Boltons wife in Travil, put her Safe to Bed with a fine Son at 11 O Clok. Returnd home ys aftern. mrs Gardy Calld at mr Boltons. Jenny Coole here, Drank Tea. mr Ballard Came from Coart.    
 

 

 June 11, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear morn. mr Brown went in hast after Doct Colman for his Eldist Son being sick. I went from home at 12 O Clok to See him, find him very ill. Returnd home at Sun Set, found Sherebiah, Betsy & Polly Town and James Stacpool here. I was informd that mr Hathaway who was Drowned Last week was taken up & is to be interd this day. Ephm Cowin Came here ys Evinng to work for mr Ballard one month.    
 

 

 June 18, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. mr Gordins Dafter. X. receivd not any thing.

Cloudy forenn. I was Calld at 8 O/Clok ys morn to Mr Gordins, his wife in Travil. found her Put to bed by old mrs Fletcher with a Dafter. I calld at mr Willims on my return, found her unwell. tarried there till towards Eving, left her more Comfortable. mr Lee here at Evng and Polly Hamlin.    
 

 

 June 25, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear day. Benn Brown Calld here going to attend worship at Winthrop. mr Williams here; I applid a plaster of Comphry to his ancle. I have red in mr Marshalls Gospel Mistry Mystery of Sanctification.    
 

 

 June 26, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 this is 17 years Since ye Death of my Daft, Triphina, who Deceast Æ 4 yr & 3 months. I was Calld ys day to See mrs Weston, Shee being unwell. mrs North unwell also. Parthena Barton here, Bt 2 gown patren.    
 

 

 June 27, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 a rainy day. mr Ballard went to ye hook. mr Gill breath Came here, is helping Dress ye mill. mr Bullin here; informs yt Ebn Churchs wife was Delivd of a son & Daughter Last weak. Shere Town Sleeps here, informs that John Emry of Sabastacook very much hurt in a Saw mill, his Life not Expetd. Likewise yt Daughter Town has been unwell and is got Comfortable.    
 

 

 June 29, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Town & Jonathan went to Cobese, returnd ys afternn. Brot Betsy up. informd yt a young man had fell into ye streem and was almost drowned, but revived. mrss Brown & Farwel here. we here yt Emrys Leg is Like to ganger. Broth Towns wife was Delivd ye 27 inst of a Dafter & is very ill, in danger of a feaver. I here also yt ye wo Fitch has had a Son & a young womn at Swetlands, Bets Tarr by name, a Daft.    
 

 

 July 1, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear Day. mr Ballard, Son Town, Jono & Ephn Cowen gone to asist Broth Moore Rais his hous. the Girls went to mr Westons. Polly Hamlin Came home with ym, & sleeps hr. Mr Sual here, paid me 6/ for attending his wife in Travil with her Last Child. Capt Springer paid 4/ for my Servis, it being what remaind my Due. mr Hollowy Sleeps here ys night. the anavercery of ye Death of my Dater Dorothy.    
 

 

 July 6, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. X Josept Williamss Dafter & first Child. X

I was Calld at 01/O Clok ys morn to Naighr Williamss wife in Travil. Shee was very ill all day till about ten in ye Evinng when Safely Delivd of a fine Dafter wt 9 lb. I tarid all night. Shee Seemd much fatagud. Sally Peirce Came and tarried with her. Eliab Purkins wife Dilvd of a Son yesterd.    
 

 

 July 7, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home from Williamss ye morn at 01/O Clok. Left her very Comfortable. Polly Fletcher is Nurs. Mrs Cowen & mrs Follett here. Benn Edwards & Wills Dind with us. Hannah & Dolly went to See mrs Wilms. Colo North is returnd from Boston.    
 

 

 July 8, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. mrs Farley here. I went to mr Williamss. Shee has been up & is as well as Can be Expected. I was at mr Porters & mr Westons. Spoke wh mrs Pollard. I hear yt Solomo Browns wife Deceast Last wednsday in Travil, has left a poor famely.    
 

 

 July 14, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 I Came home from Pattings with Neighr Williams, Calld there. we spun Cotten. ye girls gone to mr Crages ys Evinng. it is Cloudy. mr Ballard Came home from Coart after we were all in Bed. mr Porter had 2000 of Clr Boards.    
 

 

 July 17, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the wido Foster & Oliver, Epm Stevens & Pamela Barton Dind here. mr Stevens paid me 6/8 for attending his wife in travil & ye 26 of Last February. I went ys aftern to See mrs Copelin, met her at mr Pollards, spent ye afternn there. mr Ballard & Cowan went Carry a raft of Boards to Capt Huskinss Vesl. Jonathan gone on Board Bound for Boston.    
 

 

 July 27, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I carded Cottne & Cutt Aulders and maid a Sort of a fence part round the yard By the mill Pond, and then was Calld to Eliab Shaws to See his Children, they being Sick with ye Canker rash. Hannah North, Tammy & Sally Cox here. Brot a quarter of Lamb, wt 7-1/2 lb. Polly Bisbe & Adams here to Day.    
 

 

 July 31, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. ye girls washt & Bakt & Browd 1/2 Barril of Beere. mr Ballard went to Church meeting. Mr Leighton & Ezekiel Came to work for us, Epm Cowen also. we Receivd a Great wheal from Nathan Tylor. [Sal] Cowen Delivd of a dafter.    
 

 

 August 1, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. our men went to mow ye Meddow near mr Taylors. mr Ballard & Ephm went to Carry yr Dinner. old mrs Bolton & mrs Savage Drank Coffee here. we spun Some. Polly Taylor Sleeps here. I feele unwell. mrs Duttin Dilivrd of a Dafter, I hear.    
 

 

 August 6, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 I allended worship all day. a Shower. Esqr Lovjoy, David Smily & John Bragg Calld after meeting & Eat with us. this is my Daftr Hannahs Birth day. Aged 17 years. Jono returnd from Boston.    
 

 

 August 8, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Hannah Carried 30 Skeins of Cottne yn to mr Edsons and Brot mrs Densmore here to Make yr gouns. I was Calld to See Isaac Hardins wife, Shee is very unwell. I rode my hors.    
 

 

 August 14, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 I was Calld to See Capt Savages wife who is Sick of a feaver. Shee took phisick, was very Sick after ye operation. I watcht with her this Night.    
 

 

 August 15, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 I have been at Capt Savages till afternn. Shee took phisick, Seemd Releavd. I Calld at mr Thomass, Betsy unwell, a pain in her head. Hannah Savage a pain in ye Stomac. Mr Ballard gone on the Estate of Pitts. I hear yt Rhuben Moores Eldist son is Deceast Last Sabbath Day.
