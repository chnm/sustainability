Department of War
Accountants' Office
January 4th 1795
Forman Cheesman Esq.
New York _
Sir
You will receive from Nicholas Fish Esq. Supervisor The sum of 500 Dollars, being for your Salary from the 1st of October to the 31st Decemb. 1795 Inc _ for which sum please give him Duplicate Receipts
I am Sir &c
William Simmons
