244

Accountant Office October 17 1800

I certify that there is due to Robert Rankin Adjutant of the Marine Corps the sum of four dollars and 49/100 being the expenses incurred by the enlistment of John Morgan alias John Grave a deserter from the detachment of infantry under the command of Major John Cass. WS

D7 4.49

The Sec'y of War
