War Office
		Jany 1 1797
Cordage to be deliv Tench
Francis Esq to fitted for the
Frigate  United States
AF War Office, January 1.st 1797
Sir.
The Cordage intended for the Rigging [(word rigging is underlined) of the Frigate
United States, you will be pleased to deliver to the order of Tench Francis,
the Purveyor, to be fitted under his direction, subject to the further order
of the Secretary of War.
I am, Sir,
Your obedt. Serv:
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
