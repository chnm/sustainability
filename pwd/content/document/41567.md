War department,June 9th 1791
Sir
General Knox is very pressing for the return of the articles just forward to the frontier--he requests that you will lose no time to complete it--Nay he says you must work double tides to accomplish the object--It is of the utmost importance, that he should know this---
YrsJno Stagg Junr
Mr. Wm Knox
