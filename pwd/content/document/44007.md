Letter from the War Office
Decr 29. 1792
80 pairs woolen Overals to be forwarded to Carlisle
Stagg 
275
Samuel Hodgdon Esq
War department, Dec. 29th 1792.
Sir.
You will please to have packed up, and forwarded to Carlisle, for the recruits under Major Thomas Butler, eighty pairs of woolen overalls.  
By Order of the Secretary of War 
Jno Stagg Junr
Chf Clk
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401310.]
