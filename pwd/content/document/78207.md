Annapolis May 13. 1785
Dear Howell
I did not receive your letter until this 
moment and the post goes in less than 10 Minutes, I can
only say therefore I will give a full answer to you
next week, in the meantime the State of Maryland 
has taken no notice of Certificates Issued for debts due
by the United States.  Jno. White
Jos. Howell Junr.
I am sensible you have left nothing 
untried that was in your power respecting Waters
A/c__It is blended somehow in such a manner tat
it must remain doubtful at present but no error can 
take place You know for whether he has, or has not recd.
it; it is not included in he present settlement, or in
other words the United States has credit for it.
Jno. Bells  John White
