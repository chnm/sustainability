 December 29, 1788, Monday

 Cloudy. I have been at home. mr Ballard went to mr Pollards with Letters to Send by Doct Coney. Parthena is Some Easier. I have had a pain in my head. Birth Jos Browns Dagt.   at home.  
 

 

 February 24, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to mr Hamlins & Nr Andruses. Broth Moore & David Learned here. by the Latter we receivd Letters from Broth Collins & Doct Barton.   at mr Hamlins. recd Letters from our friends.  
 

 

 March 5, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, Cold & very windy. mr Town & mrs Eunis here all day. Shee had a Tooth Extracted. I have been at home. Jona brot a load of hay home. there was a young man had his Legg Cutt off at Stirling, by Doct Williams. he brot it to Doct Coneys & descected it.   at home.

view full Diary page

 

 A very rainly day. Doct Williams Came to See my Dagt, says her feaver is at ye [                                ] the infant Distrest with pain at Evng.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 May 22, 1789, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Lucy is a little better, but very Low. Sherebiah went for ye Doct yester day but he did not Come, but Sent Some Castor Niter & Stomach Bitters. mrs Stackpole & mrs Howard watcht this night. I got a nights rest, the first Since I Came here.   at Ditoes. a frost ys morn.  
 

 

 May 27, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Doct Coney & Williams Came to See my Dagt Town, Gave Directions & Left medisins. their opinion is that her Complaints are of a bilious kind which Causes a debilyty and universal weakness, but think Shee will recover with proper atendance & medicin. mr Ebenz Haills Lady Came & Carried the Dear babe home to give it Suck. mrs Sally Stackpool watcht. Lucy Got Some rest.   at Ditoes. a great frost. at Ditoes, a frost ys morn.  
 

 

 May 28, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a frost ys morn. Doct Coney Left this Bound for home. gave Lucy a potion of rhub & Sat wormwd. Shee is very weak and helpless yet, her legg very much Swelld. Shee Complains of a pain in her right Sholder.   at Ditoes. Doct Coleman Calld at mr Towns.  
 

 

 May 30, 1789, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rain in ye night & ys morn. Lucy is a little mending. Parthena Drew her Breast, got a small matter of milk. I have given her of the Bark & Snakrt yest D & ys morn. Shee Complains of a pain in her right Sholder. I put her on a Baise waist Coat & Bathd with Brandy & applid a Blister to her arm. Left her at 1h pm, arivd at home at the 8th. find mr Ballard Lame in his Left knee, the rest of my famely well. also find that Capt Norcroses Lady has Sent me a present of 1 lb Tea & 2 lb Cotten. Doct Whitaker, mr Foster & Esqr Howard here.   at Ditoes. a frost ys morn.  
 

 

 May 31, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cold. Doct Whitaker preacht in the meeting house. mr Ballard attended fore noone, was So Lame he was oblidged to Ly on the Bed aftn. Clarrissa Barton here. Dolly & Shee attended worship aftn. I had pork & Veal & Greens for Dinner. Esqr Howard Dind here. I went to See Nr Savage, he is mending.   I was at Nr Savages. Polly Wale Came here to work for me the weak insuing. a frost ys morn.  
 

 

 June 4, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mrs Hamlin here. I wrote to Sister Waters. Joseph Town Dind here, mr Ezekiel Page also. I receivd a Letter from Parthena which informs that Dagt is gitting better. Cyrus went to Doct Coneys for medicin for her.   at home. wrote to Sist Sally.  
 

 

 June 8, 1789, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Showers aftn with thunder. mr Learned took 4m 400 Boards from here, Sleeps here ys night. I have been to Doct Colmans & mr Pollards. mrs Blacks Breast like to Break.   at mr Pollards & Doct Colmans.  
 

 

 June 9, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. mrs Eunice Hodskins here. mr Learned & Doct Whiteaker Sleep here. I have been at home. Revd mr Foster is removd from mr Thwings to the house where Joseph Williams formerly livd. friend Taber takeing Spruce Boards from this mill on Sherebah Towns acount, ys Eving. I wrote to my Dagt Hannah by mr Learned & Deliverd him 24/ to Carrie her to purchace things at Bostn.   I have been at home, wrote to my Dagt Hannah. Ephraim went to School. Benn Porters wife Been here.  
 

 

 June 26, 1789, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 A Shower ys morn, Clear aftn. Doct Whitaker here. I have been quilling yarn & Combing flax. Dolly weaving. my Dagt Tryphena Expired 20 years this Day since her Death. it was a very hott day & Continued So thro the Sumer.   I have been at home. Anaversry of my Dagt Tryphenas Death.  
 

 

 June 28, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. I attended worship in public, mr Jonston performd. Capt Stackpool Calld Doct Colman out of meeting to visit mr Flint Bartons Son. mrs Foot & Clarrissa here, mr Savage & his wife also. Cyrus had Corn to grind all Day.   I was at meeting.  
 

 

 June 30, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at mr Andrewss to See his Son. Doct Williams opend his Soar ys aftn. mrs Woodward Sleeps here. mr Flint Bartons Son Expired yester day & was interd this aftern.   I was at Dittoes.  
 

 

 July 8, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. I tended my yn till 4 PM then went to See Lidia Bisbe who is Sick of a billious Disorder. a vast Concoarse of people there. I returnd home at midnight, Left Doct Coney there. Clarrissa Barton here.   I was at mr Bisbes.  
 

 

 July 13, 1789, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. Cyrus is gone to Pitts town to asist Hannah in geting home. they arivd with mrs Burton in Company at 5 PM, inform me of ye Death of Doct Conant. our other friends in health. I have been at home. Son Town here, his wife is So well as to walk about Some.   I was at home. Hannah returnd from Oxfd. Son Town here. I had Letters from Collins of June 23, & Sally of June 24th. 
 

 

 July 18, 1789, Saturday

 Clear, Hi wind. I was very unwell. mr Reed & wife here ys morn before I rose. they went to Doct Coneys. Jona killd the Calf. we lent mr Pollard 15-2/1 lb, mr Savage 5 lb, mr Hamlin 3-2/1.   I was at home.

 August 15, 1789, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Allins Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Cld at 1h morn to See mrs Allin, find her not very Sick. was Calld from thence to Doct Colmans Lady in travil. Shee Calld her women & was Safe Deld of a fine Son at ye 11th h. I was Calld from there to See mrs Pond at Winthrop, find her in Dificult Sircumstance. applied Some remedies & gave Directions & Left medisin, Left her at Sun Sett. Came to mr Pollards at ye 10th h Even. very Dark & rainy as I Came down, I had a boy with me from Hardins. was Conducted over ye river by mr mc Farling. by Joseph Foster to mr Allins, find ym all well. his Lady was Siesd with travil a little before day & was Safe Deld at 8/th h morn of a fine Dagt & I arivd Safe at home. I was gone from home 35 hours. I went to Bed & took a fine Knap afternn. Jon a been to winthrop ys day.   at mr Allins, Doct Colmans & mr Ponds, & to mr Allins again. I receivd 9/4 of ye Doctr as a reward for my Service. at mr Allins. recievd my fee Novr 3d 1790 in Chees, at /6 pr pound.  
 

 

 September 23, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleasant. I went over the river, was at Colo Howards, Doct Colmans and Joseph Fosters, find ym all well. mrs Woodwd here, gave me Some pumpkin.   I was at Fort Western.  
 

 

 September 24, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. a mr Voce, at the hook, Cutt his throat ys morn. mrs Woodwd here, gave me two pumpkins & a half. We receivd Letters; mr Ballard one from Doct Barton, I one from Broth Collins, Hannah 1 from Doct Waters, another fm hr Aunt, Parthena 2 fm hr Parents informing they are well.   I was at home. Gillbth hr. Dolly went to mr Hamlins to weav Cyruss Cloath.  
 

 

 November 1, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleasant. mr Hamlin and his wife here, mr Burtun and wife also. my Girls went to Hamlins at Evn, mr Ballard to Doct Coneys. Manly went to Colo Norths to See mr Howard who is Sick there.   I was at home.  
 

 

 November 17, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleasant. I wound 4 Skeins & went to See Capt Sewalls Lady who is unwell. I tarried there all night. Doct Coney was Calld to mr Densmores at Varsalboro, to his wife in travil.   at Capt Sewalls.  
 

 

 November 19, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Cold. I returnd home, Calld at mr Browns, Williamss, Colo Howards, Doct Colmans & Capt Sewalls; his Lady more Comfortable. was at mr Westons, Shee unwell. found Jons unwell. Doct Williams & Coney here, inform that mrs Densmore was Deliverd this morn & Expird at ye Second hour morn. mr Savage & wife here at Evng. Hannah Came home fm Hamlins.   from home. Esqr Lovjoys Dagt Densmore Deceast in Child Bed.
