Philadelphia 5th October 1784.
Sir,
I fear thro' the hurry of business I have admitted sending you the charges against the following named offices of Colonel Spee's Legion, being so much demanded by the State of Pennsylvania & which must be [illegible] to the credit of said State: Captain James Armstrong Two hundred and sevent-five dols -- 275 Lieut. Lawrence Manning On hundred & eighty three 30/90 dols_ _ _ _ 183.30 Doct. Matthew Irvine Three hundred & twenty-four 45/90 Dols_ _ _ _
Yours [illegible character]
J Howell
Duncomb
