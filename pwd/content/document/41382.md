Philadelphia 27th April 1791
Dear Sir--
Enclosed you have Joseph Hunter's receipt for Clothing forwarded for the Levies at Phttsburg-- he has other things in which are mentioned in the receipt but they will probably be left at Carlisle--if so a receipt to demonstrate it takes it out of his obligation to deliver them to you--
I amSir,Your Most Obedient ServantSamuel HodgdonQMG
Major Craig
