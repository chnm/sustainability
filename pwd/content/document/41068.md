Dear Sir 
The President wishes to know if you have heard anything (6011) respecting Mr Ogden who was spoken of for the Atty of New Jersey - As the President is anxious to give in the nomination he will thank you for information on this point.  
I am Dr Sir 
respectfully & sincerely 
Yours,
Tobias Lear 
January 3, 1791
General Knox
