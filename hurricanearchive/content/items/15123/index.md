Victor Minor Jr., father of Clinton Joseph Minor. Victor Minor Jr. was considered the first mayor of Fazendeville. Everyone turned to him when business for the village needed to be conducted.  He was born, raised and lived his entire life in Fazendville.  His mother\'s name was Cora Cook.\r\n\r\nThe ancestors of Evelyn Minor\'s late husband, Clinton Joseph Minor, were long-time residents of Fazendeville. Evelyn Minor now lives with her family in Los Angeles.
