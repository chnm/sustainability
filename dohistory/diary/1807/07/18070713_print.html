Martha Ballard's Diary, July 13 - 18, 1807

13 2
X. Birth John Wals 2nd Dagt. XX. 		Clear. I was Calld by John Wall at 6h ys morn to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 8h morn of her 2nd Daugt & 2nd Child. I left her Cleverly and arivd at home at 12 O Clock [percisly]. I got Dinner and did other matters. Daugts Ballard & Pollard took Tea, Sally, Martha & Ephm allso. at mr John Wals. Birth 11th. receivd 9/ as a reward. 
14 3
Clear. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to hear Prayers on the raising of the meeting hous. they Came home and took Breakfast and went down again to See ye raising. I have been doing matters in my gardin &C. at home. mr Bald & Cyrus Down to raising meeting hous.  
15 4
Clear Except a light Shower. I have done hous work and Some in my gardin. mr Ballard & Cyrus to raising. mrs Parker took Tea with me. at [home]. mr Bald & Cyrus to raising meeting hous.  
16 5
Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard & Cyrus to raising. at home.  
17 6
Clear the most of ye day. I have Cutt Some grass & Tansy. mr Ballard & Cyrus to raising. at home.  
18 7
Clear Except a Light Shower. mr Ballard & Cyrus to raising. I have Brewd and done my other wk tho I have felt very feeble.
