(23)

Annapolis - Feb:y 3d. 1786:

Dear Sir,

Herewith you will receive a receipt for the Certificates you sent me belonging to Caps. Hughes and Gilbert  It should have been sent before but I was in hopes to have delivered them, and had the receipt of the parties themselves to transmit, however I must be accountable 
I am, Sir, your Mo Obedt. Servt.
J. White 

Jos. Howell Esqr
