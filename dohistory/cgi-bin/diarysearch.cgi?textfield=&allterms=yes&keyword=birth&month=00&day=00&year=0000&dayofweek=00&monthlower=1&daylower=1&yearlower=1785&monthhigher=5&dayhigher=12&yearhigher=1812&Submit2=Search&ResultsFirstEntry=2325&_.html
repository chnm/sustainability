 December 8, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

[X. Birth Jery Prebbles Dagt. XX.]

Clear. I have been Piking up wood. was Calld to See the wife of Jery Prebble who was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a daught, her first Born at Sun Sett. I tarried all night. Son Lambd Sent me rum, Butter & Sugar, Tea [&C] ys Day.   at mr Prebbles. Birth 10th. recvd 11/3d as a reward.  
 

 

 December 13, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. mr Thomson brot me wood, _ Cord & Cut it and I brot it into ye hous. mrs Farewel has been to mr Pains, Shee walkt.   at home.  
 

 

 December 14, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Farewel and I washt. mrs Nason Calld here. I Bakt me Indien Cake, the first Bread I have had in my hous this weak.   at home. mrs Nason here.  
 

 

 December 20, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Farewel and I went to son Ephms, my husband met me there. he Exprest a wish for me to keep possision of this hous at present.   at Son Ephms.  
 

 

 December 21, 1804, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Pollards 3d Son.

Clear. I was Calld in hast to Son Pollards, arivd at 1h pm. find his wife Safe Deld by Old mrs Savage of her 3d son & 7th Child and [as] wel as Could be Expected. Child wd 11_ lb.   at Son Pollards. Birth 11th.  
 

 

 December 22, 1804, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at Son Pollards, my Dagt Cleverly. Olliv Fletcher Came to Nurs her.   at [Do].  
 

 

 December 23, 1804, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the Day. Son Cyrus Came to Son Pollards. we Dind on a fine Turkey and he then Conducted me home. I find son Jonas famely had taken possesion of my hous. I was Calld to [se] Georg Boltons infant, it has a very Soar head.   at son Pollards & Georg Boltons.  
 

 

 December 24, 1804, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Snow Storm. Son Pollard Calld here, Says his wife is Cleaverly. Cyrus went to see his father, finds him Better than he has been.   at home.  
 

 

 January 11, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye Day. a mrs Emerson from [Dery] field had fitts here this night. Doct Winship was Calld to her asistance. we were up with her ye most of ye night.   at Dito . 
 

 

 January 12, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and cold. the sick patient was very ill till near night. the Doct tarried till 2h pm. Shee Seems much better this [Evning].   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 17, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. my husband and I at Ditoes. I had a very ill turn ys morn, lay in bed till [noon].   at Ditos.  
 

 

 January 19, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. the famely that Slept here Sett out at noon. son Lambd went to Hallowell, brot Dolly from Son Ephms. Sist Moore and mrs Johnston Calld here. I slipt at ye door and lambd my Self, Son Lambd Calld doct Winship to See me.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 January 26, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye Day. I went from Son Lambds to Ephms and dind with my husband. Dagt Pollard gave me 3/ to git Brandy to bath my foot. Lemuel brot me home, made me a fire. I have been very ill ys night.   at Ditoes, Son Ephms & at home.  
 

 

 January 27, 1805, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowed. I am at home very ill with pain in my head & Back.   at home.  
 

 

 February 5, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have began a Stockin for my Dagt Lambd and done other matters. we are informd that the wife of Geo Hodgskin Departed this life this morn about 5 O Clock after an illness of 4 years.   at home. Death mrs Hodgskens, Varsalboro.  
 

 

 February 16, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Capt Stackpole and wife Calld. mr Hamlin and wife made a visit here.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 March 6, 1805, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Amos Bralys Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at [8]h ys morn to See Amos Bralys wife who was Safe Deld at 11 of a Daugt. I left them Cleverly and went to Son Pollards at 2, returnd home at 8h Evng. mr Hodges Conducted me.   at Amos Bralys & Son Polds. Birth 1st. recd 9/ as a rewrd.  
 

 

 March 14, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Babcocks 3d, Second Son. XX.

Cloudy part of ye Day. I was Calld at 9h ys morn to see the wife of Saml Babcok 3d. Shee was Deld at 11 of her 2nd Son by him. I left them Comfortable and returnd home about sunsett. find Dagt Pollard, her Babe & Dagt Dolly here. the remains of the widdo Hewen were interd ys aftern.   at mr Babcocks. Birth 2nd. Funeral wido Hewin. receivd 9/ June 3d. 
 

 

 March 30, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Farewel & son Ephm Dind here. my Son has Seen 26 years this Day. I have mended my Winter gound.   at home. Son Ephms Birth Day.  
 

 

 April 8, 1805, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Anty Brackets 5th Son.

Cloudy. I was Calld ys aftern by mr A. Bracket to go and See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld 5h pm of her 5th Son and 6th Child. I tarried all night, mrs Springer allso. Polly Bolton there to Nurs her aunt.   at Anty Brackets. Birth 3d. funeral Capt Jasn Conys infant.  
 

 

 April 16, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Oliv went from here to her Fathers. Sally Pierce Came to work for her aunt. I have felt unwel but knit a little. mrs Pierce took Tea here. Old mr Hamlin Calld here.   at home. Sally Pierce Came here.  
 

 

 April 19, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jonas 6th Son.

Clear. I was Calld up at 1h ys morn. Son Jonas wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 2 of her 6th son and 9th Child. I have assisted a little about Nursing and finisht my washing. mrss Andrus, Wiman, Cleark and Pierce were here as assistances. the Patients have been Comfortable thro the Day.   at home. Birth 3d. 
 

 

 May 2, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Farewel and I Came from Lambds. Fanny Bell Murfy left there. Shee receivd all the wages that was her Due. Dolly L. is not very wel, ye Babe is better. I was Calld to Saml Braleys wife in Travil, was much fatagued with my hors on the way.   at Ditoes and at S. Braleys.  
 

 

 May 3, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml Braleys 3d Son & 5th Child.

Clear. mrs Braley was Deld at 3h morn of her 3d Son and 5th Child. I left them Comfortable and Came to Son Pollards at 7, find that he had ye misfortuin to break one of his ribs yesterday. I Calld at mr Bracketts and Towns, find mrs Town a little better. Son Ephms wife here.   at Ditoes. Birth 4th.  
 

 

 May 7, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Oliver Hall workt for Son Jon a. mrs Gilberd & Betsy Andrus with their Babes here to Tea. 2 Ladys Calld in to See mrs Gilbd. mr Patridg here to See my Son. O that he would make A Settlement with his Dear Father with out A Law Sute. the Boys have been to Training.   at home. mr Patridg here on business with Son Jon a.
