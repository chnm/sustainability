Accountants Office

November 30

Mr. William Taylor

Fredericksburg. Virginia

Sir

I received your letter of the 24th Ins[tant]

enclosing a State of expenses, incurred by you in making Payment

to the Virginia Cavalry, which will be considered when you transmit

Account Current, and Vouchers, to this Office for examination.

I am &c

W Sim[mons]
