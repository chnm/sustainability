Letter from the War Office
May 2d 1792
to deliver a gun to Mr Chambers 
Vanderbrock
107
Mr. William Knox 
No. 71 Water Street North 
Stamp: Received from State Dep't Nov. 24, 1894, R.P.O. 401810
War department 
May 2d 1792
Sir 
The Secretary of War request you will deliver to the bearer Joseph Chambers, a gun to his choice, a flint, one pound of best rifle powder and a proportionable quantity of Ball.  If you are not possessed of the rifle powder of excellent quality you will procure the same.
I am Sir Your obedient Servant
R J Vandenbrock
Mr. William Knox
