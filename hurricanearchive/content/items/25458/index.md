hi im frankie, and i was 13 at that time. We lived in the east side of houston when we heard of rita. We decided to stay. So as the storm entered we could see a thick black cloud infront of us. As the storm got in, we could see some of our neighbors shingles fly off. There was a condo infront of us and the wind ripped some of the ceiling off.
