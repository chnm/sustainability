Department of War
Nathaniel Gorham Esq.                                 Accountants' Office
 Sup-r    Boston                                         February 19th 1796
                 Sir
                    The Treasurer of the United States will remit you the sum of 

ninety Dollars which you will please pay to Mrs. Sally Greaton, being on account of

the pay of her husband, Capt Rich.d H Greaton for which sum please take duplicate

receipts; Capt. Greaton to be accountable. -- I am sir (indecipherable)

                                                               William Simmons
