Sundries to be 
charged John Mackey 
&c Abstract from William Simmons 
8th April 1800
Entered
Department of War
Accountants Office
April 8" 1800
Sir, 
On settlement of the accounts of John Mackey Paymaster at the Armory at Harpers Ferry in Virginia he has received a credit on the books of this office for the following articles vizt
100 Match coat blankets _ $223..67
√ 1 Wheel barrow _ 4.
√ 4 Doz large Spoons _ 4.
Drs. 231.67
You will therefore be pleased to make the necessary entries on the books of the Superintendent of Military Stores. –
I am, 
Sir
your Obedt. Servt. 
Wm Simmons
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr.
Superintendt. 
of Military Stores
