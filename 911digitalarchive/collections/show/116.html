Description

The following was written by Louis Brendel, and should accompany his photos.

&quot;Tidal Wave of Tears
The images that I have been allowed to display here are but a flash of the endless picture. The events of September 11, 2001, have touched the world and all generations to come.

Tears will fall not just for our loss or the scar inflected on Liberty, tears will pour to embrace all the kindness and love, which has come forth in our time of need. Let the endless tidal wave of tears flow to wash away &quot;their hate&quot; and &quot;our pain!&quot;
