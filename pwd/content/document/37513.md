War Office 3 Nov. 1785. 
Dear Sir, 
After the march of Capn Zeigler's com- 
pany the recruiting Service will be suspended 
until March.  In the mean time you will 
please to forward your accounts to the comp- 
troller and upon their being passed, you 
shall be paid for the rations you have 
issued. 
I am, dear Sir, 
Your humble Servant 
HKnox 
Clement Biddle Esq.
