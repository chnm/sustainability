District of N Hamps
Supervisors Office

William Simmons Esquire

Accountant Department of War

Philadelphia

Tilton, Daniel Capt 2 Sub Legion 1797

Joshua Wentworth dated 1 decr 1797  read ansd Same time


District of New Hampshire

Supervisor's Office. Dec. 1, 1797

Sir, 

Mr. Langdon a senator to Congress from N. Hampshire was obliging enough to take the care of the 148 4/100 dollars to pay the Treasurer of the United States with has been laying in my had some time that presented of Captain Daniel Tilton Pryor direction and I now enclose Treasurer receipt as received by the hand of W. Langdon.

I am with respect, 

Sir yr most obedient srevt

Josh. Wensworth, supervisor



Williams Simons Esquire 

Accountant Department of War
