War Department. March 28 1794
Sir.
The bearer Major Rivardi is the gentleman
whom the President of the United States has appointed for
the purpose of fortifying Baltimore, Alexandria, and Norfolk.
The circumstances of the latter part being considered as 
the most pressing, the Engineer will at present make but a
short stay at Baltimore, or Alexandria-- He is directed to 
furnish your excellency with a copy of his instructions.
I have the honor to be 
Sir,
with great respect
Your most ___,
Henry Knox

His excellency 
The Governor of Virginia
