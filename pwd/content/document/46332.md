W.D. Accountants Office
                               April 8th 1794

Sirs

           I have this day received your 

muster for Feby and March which I return inclosed the same not being qualified to - agreeably to instruction and which cannot be dispensed with in one instance without it being claimed as a precedent in another - the receipt for December as far as you have paid are right, I will immediately send on the pay when the Muster is returned to me --

                     I am Sir etc
                       Joseph howell

Capt Nicholas Hannah Alexandria _ Virginia
