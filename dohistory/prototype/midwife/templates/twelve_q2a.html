Not all documents are found in predictable places. This account of treatments for the Mathews family, kept by Dr. Page, was stored in the archives with the papers of Benjamin Vaughan, who was a mentor for Benjamin Page.

Using the Mathews account, we can see that Dr. Page did not mix his personal and professional accounts. In contrast, Martha Ballard's diary (which functioned as her accounting system) did mix all aspects of her life. This reflected the interweaving of the personal, the social, and the professional in her life.
