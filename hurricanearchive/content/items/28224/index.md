A New Orleans man writes down his memories of the city\'s lost buildings on a postcard. Viewer comments were incorporated into the artwork of Maxx Sizeler, one of the visual artists who participated in the one-night-only multi-media installation. The event, titled NEIGHBORHOODS: 2426 BRADISH PLACE, was held on May 19, 2007 in the first floor of a gutted house in the Tulane/Gravier neighborhood.
