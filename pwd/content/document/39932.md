New York, 9 July 1789

My dear Sir
I inclose you the statement and <undecipherable> of compsons him__ The release that is required and an echucht of Mr. Webbers letter
I pray you to have all done immediately thus is necessary and <undecipherable> inlike cases, and hanpick the same to me, so that I may inform Mr Webber by the packet which <undecipherable> sail on the 15th or 16th instand.
Mrs Knox and myself have lately had our feelings excited exceedingly by the apprehnsions aplosing our youngest child who has been at Reprouch of death
He was seized on the 28th of June with aviolent dysentry and brought to thelast extremity of life in three days
Thanks be to heaven his disorder seems to be so far mitigated that we have now aprospech of saving him__ We attribute his return to life to having when him fo_ three days to the Nook, and we <undecipherable> again repeat the experiment tomorrow
With the sincerest friendship for you and Mrs Ogden
I am my dear Sir
Your affectionate 
HKnox

Saml Ogden Esqr
