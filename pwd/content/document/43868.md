War-department,
Nov: 24 1792
Sir.
Please to deliver to David Allison Esq., for the use of Captain Joseph Kerr's company of infantry in the service of the United States.
One drum-
One pair drum-sticks.
One drum case - 
One drum carriage
&
One fife, and case.
I am Sir
Your humble Servt:
John Stagg Jnr.
Cty Clk
[margin] Deliver the above
Samuel Hodgdon
