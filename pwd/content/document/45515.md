description: From Flint River, prospects for peace are good it seems. The Warrior King trying to reconcile matters. Met with leadership of Lower Creeks at Cussetah, and with White Lieutenant and upper Creek
  Chiefs at Tuckabatchee. Cowetas promise to lay quiet; talk strongly of killing horse thieves. Seek to get women prisoners back. Ask Seagrove to mediate and bring prisoners with him when he comes to visit.
  One of the women is a relative to White Lieutenant. Indians say ten women and children are missing, but Barnard says only eight were carried off. Suggests the others may have been killed. Barnard has
  been very ill lately.
