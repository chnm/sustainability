From
Capt Russel Bissell
Septemb..30. 1794
Middletown Septer 30th 1794
Sir
I have enclosed a Muster Roll of the present date, a duplicate of which will be forwarded by next mail. The Consideration of Monthly pay, I believe has been an inducement to recruits engaging in Service. I wish it may be continued to the few we have
	With great respect
	I am Sir
	Your most obedent and
	humble Servant 
	Russell Bissell Capt
The Honorable Sect of War
