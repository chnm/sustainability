Department of War
                                                                  Accountants' Office
                                                                     February 25th 1796

Capt. Benj.m Price.

  Fredericktown
       Maryland
                                       Sir
                                         The foregoing is copy of my letter to you of the 16th November 1795- since 

which I have never heard from you - I have therefore to request again that you forward your accounts and vouchers without delay, as the advances to you are of long standing and should have been accounted for long ere this.

                                              I am Sir (indecipherable)
                                                William Simmons

(Editor's Note: written on left of page See Letter under date of the 16. Nov. 1795)
