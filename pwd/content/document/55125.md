Edmund H. Quincey 
Recd 12 Octor 1795-'6
Ansd Same day
Portsmouth New Hampre October 3d, 1796
Sir 
Inclosed I have sent you my acct for salary up to Sept 30 1796, which you will be pleased to have paid.  
I am Sir with Respect your Obed. Sert. 
Edmund H. Quincy

William Simmons Esqr
Accountant
Philadelphia
