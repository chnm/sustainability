Mr Samuel Hodgdon 
Commisary of the Army 
North Water Street No 71
Note from Mr Neal
27 Jany 1795
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401310.]
The Revd Leond Neale presents Compts to Mr. Hodgdon & with great pleasure informs him that the Revd Mr Rivets baggage arrived here last night & that the two gentlemen are ready to obey his call
Tuesday 27 Jan 1795 
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O> 401310.]
