All of these maps portray New York City's East Village neighborhood through different lenses and for different purposes. The bicycle tour map (A) is largely a promotional graphic that shows the race route and also reflects the area's hip youth culture. Elementary school students worked with Green Map System, a community ecology group, to map gardens, parks and other points of interest and created the "Green Map" (D). The Surveillance Camera Players create and publicize maps of surveillance cameras (B) as part of their mission to call attention to violations of privacy imposed by security cameras. The insurance map (C) is produced for the purpose of assessing fire risk and cost of insurance and provides a high degree of detail, showing all buildings.
