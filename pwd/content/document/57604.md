9 oclock
Sir,
Eric have just receive the last evening paper - and the reception of general - p. in France - was by vote no probable- the besonets will go on- 
we try to arm- here a brig, with americain collores. - to war everyone - of our one society - few- j witt propose my self- by and by- 
F X S H
