This was shot on Poplar St. and the cross street was near Ocean.  Again, seeing this much sky in this part of Metairie (actually Bucktown area) was unnatural.  It was around here that Dad and I realized what else was wrong... There was hardly anyone around the area and there was no background noise from traffic, planes and most particularly from birds.  As we went further into our tour of the neighborhoods, we came to more realizationss about many other things that are now not normal about these neighborhoods.  One of the most startling realizations for us came not too far from here.
