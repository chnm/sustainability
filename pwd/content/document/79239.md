Mr. John Harris
Sundries [undecipherable] to Portland, agreeable to the within order July 19 1798
Philadelphia 19th July 1798
Sir,
You will please to put on board the vessel bound for Portland the following Articles to the address of Samuel Weeks ...  Vizt
100 .... 32 pounder Ball
82 -- 400 .... 24  Ditto 
100 .... 18    Ditto
84 -- 300 ... 12    Ditto
(Bracket tot he right side of the above 4 lines) - or such portion of them as may be on hand.
1 Ladle for 32 Pounder.
1 Sponge & worm for ditto.
2 Ladles for ..  24 Pounder
3 Sponges & Worms for .. D:
1 Ladle for .... 18 Pounder
1 Sponge & worm for ... Ditto
2 Ladles for ..... 12 Pounders
3 Sponges & Worms for ... Ditto
2 Gunners Belts with Implements complete.
6 .... Powder Horns
2. Budge Barrels
[undeciperable] 100 Paper Cartridges for 32 Pounders
400 ... d ... ditto .... 24 ... ditto
100 .... d.... ditto .... 18 ... ditto
200-300 ... d ... ditto .... 12 ... ditoo
(bracket to right of above 4 items} or Paper equal to half this number.
6 Lintstocks.
20 lbs Slow Match
10 Setts of Tron Trucks or Garrison Carriage Wheels
Your most obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
W. John Harris
