Cap Erasmus Gill
July 24 1787
Copied
Dr [indecipherable]   Richmond July 24 1787
I intended to have waited on you prior to my leaving Petersburgh, but was disappointed.  I have since my return received a copy of the stating of your account of depreciation and also settled with Mr. Milligan and also of that with the [indecipherable], by which it appears that your [indecipherable] & rations have been twice drawn from Decem 10 [indecipherable] to Jary 1 82 tho' the latter has been at different prices, but on the whole brings you considerably in debt to the United States or the State of Virginia.
I am convinced the error arose from a supposition that the amount settled with Milligan was for your expences, which was not the case as appears by an exact copy of it I have now enclosed you.  A copy also of your depreciation account settled with this State I also inclose you and am with esteem
Dsir
Your obt sevt.
