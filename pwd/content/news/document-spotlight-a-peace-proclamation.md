<p>Transcriber Cpartrid recently ran across and transcribed a document related to treaty between the young United States and the Creek nation. As President of the United States, George Washington was authorized to negotiate treaties with other nations; this treaty, ratified by the Senate as the constitution requires, recognizes the sovereignty of the Creeks, and promises peace and friendship.</p>
<p>With ratified treaty in hand, Washington here asks his military officers to respect the terms of the treaty.</p>
