NOLA Shop Talk\r\nDirector: Todd Voltz/Eric Laws\r\n\r\nNOLA Shop Talk is a brief look at trials and tribulations endured by small business owners in New Orleans following the disasters caused by Hurricanes Katrina and Rita. The owners of five independent businesses offer their stories and suggestions regarding the city of New Orleans in a time of renewal. Created by the crack team of Eric Laws and Todd Voltz, this serious subject is approached in a casual manner allowing the viewer to have a first-hand account of the persistence and the passion with which these people live while working to rebuild a city that they all love. Original music by Eric Laws and Todd Voltz. A Laws of Thunder Production in association with NOVAC.
