Accountant's Office
Jany 6th 1797
I certify that there is due to Joshua Humphrey's the sum of Five hundred Dollars being for his Compensation as Constructor of Frigate building at Philadelphia from Octr 1st to Decr 31st 1796 inclusive.
Wm Simmons
Acct
Drs. 500
The Secy of War
