War Office 26 April 1796
Sir
Captain Thomas Lewis of the 3rd Sub Legion is your most proper officer that I can recommend for the important business you have in contemplation + whose honor and integrity I will be responsible + I am with sincere esteem 

Your most obedient

+ very humble servant
Anthony Wayne
