 April 15, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mr Cragg Conducted Sally White home, her Child is unwell. he went & Calld mrs Hamlin to take his Dear little infant & giv it Suck. I went to See mrs Weston; find her as well as Could be Expected. I Calld at Colo Norths; find mrs North & mrs Cox a little more Comfortable. Came to mr Craggs; fixt my Self to go home. mr Ballard Came in & informd me I could not pass the Brook below mr Savages & I tarried all night. mrs Voce went to mr Hamlins & Slept with the Babe.   at Ditoes & mrs Westons. hear yt mrs Sherburns infant is not like to liv. Betsy Voce here.  
 

 

 April 20, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy till ye 11th h morn when the Sun Shone. mr Ballard & Foster gone to to [sic] a meeting of ye proprietors of the Town Ship Calld unity at the house of mr Carr. Neigr Savage informd me that the Revd Isaac Fosters Lady was Safe Delivd of a Dagt Last Evng & is Cleverly. I have been at home; feel unwell. Cyrus Borrowed a 40 Sleigh of the widdow Coburn for Dolly to weav a pice for Benn Porter.   at home.  
 

 

 April 26, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleasant. we were informd that mr Pollard had broke his Legg this morn. I am Calld to See mrs Burton who is unwell. Son Town is Coming with Loggs, our men gone to meet him, it is 12 OClok. mrs Mc Cathney, mrs Black, Judy Huston, Polly Pollard, Nr Savage & wife took Tea here. mr Stratten, Son Town & Robsn, Dexter Dind here. my Dagt & famely well. Biah Declineing, Nalhan Haywood mending.   at home. mr Pollard Broke his Legg.  
 

 

 April 27, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. I went By water with Son Town as far as mr Pollards Landing; Calld to See him; find him as well as Could be Expected. went to See mrs Burtun; find her much Better than Shee was yesterDy & mrs Burjes, Shee is mending. Came back to mr Pollards; Spent ye aftn. Calld at mr Craggs at Evn. mrs Hamlin & Burtun there. mrs Voce & Becky Hardin, also Polly & Judy Came home with me. Son Town Sleeps here. Cloudy Evng.   at the Neighbors below here.  
 

 

 May 2, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very Pleast morn. I was Desired by mr Cragg to go & See his infant; it has a Soar mouth. I find it is like to have it favourable. I Calld, as I returnd, at mr Craggs. Polly Bisbe & Betsy Chever at mr Hamlins. they with mrs Burtun went to mrs Craggs Grave.   at mr Hamlins & mr Craggs.  
 

 

 May 4, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to mr Goodins for Seeds. Haywood & Savage Bolton went from here to Bring Down Loggs. Cyrus to Georg Boltons for him to Cum & help Jon a who is very unwell. I am atempting to Swett him which Seems to reliev him Some. Georg Brot a Barril of Pork from Bents for us. Jon a is very Sick. I Sett up to nurs him this night.   at home. Jona is unwell.  
 

 

 May 5, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Benn Porters Son. XX.

I was Calld to Benn Porters by mr John Cumings. we arivd at 8h 30 m. he was 3 hours from home. when we arivd, mrs French, Cumings, Prescott & Easty there. it Snowd & raind & haild this Day. mrs Porter was Safe Delivrd at ye 10th h Evn of a fine Son, her Second Child. I Sett up with her till Day. Shee is Cleverly.   I was at mr Benn Porters. Jona very unwell ys day. Birth 16th. 
 

 

 May 6, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I tarried at mr Porters till 2h pm, then Sett out for home; left my patient Cleverly. left mrs French & mr [Keeser] with her. I arivd Safe at home at 6. Capt Betcher here informs his Lady is very unwell indeed.   at mr Porters. Jona went to ye hook for Corn & Coffee.  
 

 

 May 7, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleasant. I have a pain in my head by reason of my fatagues. mr Craggen here; took Breakfast; informs me Sherebiah Town is very Low. I was Calld to see mrs Belcher ys morn; find her a little more Comfortable Except her Breast which is Soar & painfull yet. I was Calld from there at 3 h pm to See mrs Andrews who is unwell; tarried there all night.   at CapT Belchers & mr AndrewsS. Began a Stockin for Foster.  
 

 

 May 9, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Andrewss Son. XX.

Clear & Pleasant. I was Calld between 12 & 1 O clock morn to See mrs Andrews. I walkt; was much fatagud. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 3h 34 m morn of a Son; a Small, weak Babe. I left them at 8, the babe a little revivd. find Dolly very weak indeed. Shee has not been able to Sett up with out fainting ys Day. mrs Pollard being very Sick Sent for me to See her. I find her much as Dolly is, Betsy Chever allso. Doct Coney to See her; informs me Sherebiah is Decaying very fast. I returnd & Gave my Dagt some Lovage Tea which relievd her of faintness. mr James Cowen, his wife & Nephue took breakfast here; mr Stackpool also. Parthena returnd; informs mrs Porter is more Comfortable.   at mr Andws, Pollards, Westons, Craggs & Savages. Birth 17th. Parthena returnd. mrs Mathews Delivrd of a Dagt ys Evng.  
 

 

 May 10, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. mr Ballard went to Winthrop to Survey land for Saml Foster; informs me that mrs Porter is very ill ys Day. Dolly is much Better. the Girls washt. Doct Coney Calld here; Says Broth Town is very ill.   at home.  
 

 

 May 12, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Hannah & Parthena have been Confind to yr Bed, very sick this day. Patty sick allso. Dolly Sett her web to work but feels feeble. I have made out to nurs the Sick. mr Ballard been to Colo Howards & to ye hook. the freshet is up.   at home. Hannah & Parthena Sick.  
 

 

 May 15, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Sowd Some redish seed. mrs Child Sent for me to see her. I find her very unwell. I Calld at mr Pollards. they are Better at mr Craggs, find Polly Bisbe very unwell there. returnd at Evn; find mrs Brown here. Shee is much more Comfortable than Shee has been.   at mr Childss Pollards & Craggs. Hannah is Cleverly this Day.  
 

 

 May 18, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. James Hinkleys Dagt. XX.

Clear & more pleasant. I was at mr Hinkleys, his wife Calld her women & was Safe Delivrd at Sun Sett of a Dagt. her oldest Dagt in her 15th year. Shee has 7 Sons & 2 Dagts. I watcht with her, mrs Cox & Davis tarried.   at Ditoes. Birth 18th. 
 

 

 May 19, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleasant. I returnd home by water as far as mr Westons. Grien, Wilboro & Zilpha are unwell. I Calld at mr Pollards, his wife very unwell. I find my famely well as I left them. Gillbriath helping Cyrus. Capt Howland Came up ys Day   at Ditoes. receivd 6/ of Ben Preskott for assisting his wife Feby 10[]. A Child Lost.  
 

 

 May 27, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Moses Whites 3d Dagt. XX.

Clear. I workt in ye Gardin. Sowd parsnip, Safron &c. was Calld at ye 9 h pm to See Moses Whites wife. went by water as far as the Brick kills, then walkt the other part of ye way. Shee was Safe Delivrd between ye 11th & 12th hours Evn. I tarried all night, my Patient very well.   at Moses Whites. Birth 19th. Letters from my friends. Hannah went to mr Hamlins.  
 

 

 June 6, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Magr Stickneys Dagt. XX.

Clear & hott Day. I was Calld by magr Stickney at half after two ys morn. Shee his Lady was Safe Delivd of a dagt at 5h Evn. I was Calld from there to See mr Ezekiel Pages Lady; find her Sick with ye influenza. administred Some thing for her relief & returnd to mr Jacksons at 11. Tarried all night. Took Care of my patient.   at mr Jacksons. Birth 20th. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 June 7, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 at Dittoes. raind all Day. I tarried ys night; watcht, my patient Exercised with Pain.   at Ditos.  
 

 

 June 8, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld from mr Jacksons to mr Brookss at 4h m; his Lady & 3 Childn Sick with ye measels. I tarried the Day and night, Dolly watcht, the Childns measles turnd.   at mr Brookses. my Sist Debby Expird.  
 

 

 June 9, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at Dittoes. the wind N. I Sett up all night. mrs Brookss disorder at ye height; her infant is Sick. mrs Church has it to nurs.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 June 29, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. Cleard at 8h morn. my Childn left us Bound for home. Dolly went up with them. mr Ballard & mr Kingsbury went to Pitts town. I went to mr Arnals Store, bot 9 yds Callimanco a 2/pr yd. Calld on my return at mr Hamlins. he is returnd from oxford; his wives Sister Came with him. Capt Weston Sleeps hr. we receivd Letters; 1 from Doct Barton, 1 fm Brh Collins , & 1 fm Sister Waters by mr Hamlin.   I went to the hook. we recvd Letters from Oxford & Charlton. I receivd 3/4 of mrs Walker in part of my fee.  
 

 

 July 13, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. we Came to thee Coart house; Saw Melona receive the punishment which the Coart inflicted at 8 hours morn, then returnd to mr Hatches. Paid our reckoning & Sett out for home. I wrode mr Pollards hors; we Dind at magr Smiths. Calld at mr Bullins & mr Jacksons; arivd home near Sun Sett. find Alice Ballard here. Gillbreath helping Cyrus.   at Do & returnd home.  
 

 

 July 18, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Page Calld here, has left her Dagt at mr Shaws. old mr Pitts Drank Tea here.   at home.  
 

 

 July 21, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I wed gardin & went to See Georg Chamberlin; left him Some tinckture of aloes. mrs Savage there. Shee Calld here. mr Ballard been with the Committee on the road. Foster here to work ys Day.   at mr Chamberlins. there was a Shower in ye night.  
 

 

 August 3, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Small Shower. mr Lee here to Settle with mr Ballard and Cyrus. I knit fore n, was ashing my Cabbages when mr Reed Calld me to See his wife. I arivd there about Sun Sett. mrs Woodwd, Ingerham and Williams were asistants.   at mr Reeds. mr Porter Brot Cyrus 16-1/2 lb lamb.
