description: Hamilton complains that the recent appointments in Colonel Bentley's regiment appear to have been made without reference to guiding principles. Hamilton believes hat this practice will lead
  to considerable dissatisfaction among the officers affected. He recommends that Bentley's appointments be rescinded and that future appointments in all regiments be made according to well-established
  rules of promotion.
