War Office
Dec 21 1798
Sundries to be deld. Capt John Bishop
War department December 21. 1798
Sir
Be pleased to deliver to Samuel Hodgdon for transportation to Winchester in Virginia for Captain John Bishop - Four Sergeants and eight privates suits of Artillerists Clothing also Six Sergeants Swords and a Drum and fife. - 
I am Sir Your obed Servant
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr John Harris
Mr Harris will please to have the above articles packed up immediately, as a Waggon going to Winchester will take them in today.
Samuel Hodgdon
December 21 '98
