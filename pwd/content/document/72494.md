Knoxville, November 13th, 1799
Sir
I now forward a Quarterly Return of the
Issues, for the Months of July August and September,
being for Quarter Masters Stores accounted for 
the State of Tennessee.
I am respectfully
Your Humble Servant
David Henley
Agent of War.
Samuel Hodgdon Esq.
