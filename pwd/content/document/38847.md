H. Knox War OfficeJuly 27th1787
War Office, July 27th 1787
Sir
I beg leave to trouble you to obtain the signature
of Ralph Pomeroy Esq. to the within duplicate receipts, and
to deliver him the enclosed order of the board of treasury on
William Imlay Esq.; and also to pay him one dollar
and two thirds on my account.  You will please to transmit
me the receipts by post.
I have the honor to be
Sir,
Your most obed. humb. serv.
HKnox
