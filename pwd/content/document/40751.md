Sexy of War
June 19th
1790
X
War Office June 12th 1790
Sir, 
It is with great diffidence that I take the liberty of requesting your Excellency to forward letters- but the enclosed to Judge Innes containing dispatches of great importance for Brigadier General Harmar, I request the favor of your forwarding them
I have the honor to be 
with great respect 
Your Excellencys most 
Obedt Humble Servant
Knox
Sexy of War
His Excellency
Governor Randolph
