Samuel Lewis
12th September 1798
Recd 13 - 
Encloses a draft for the 1000 Dollars in favour of John Harris on Hooker's Regt. 

Trenton Sep 12th
War department
James W Henry 
Free
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
Superintendant of Military Stores,
Philadelphia
War department, Trenton
Septr: 12th: 1798
Sir,
Agreeably to my promise of yesterday, I now enclose a check on the Bank of the United States, in my favor, or order; which I have endorsed payable to you, for one thousand dollars.  The Sum requested by you, for the Quarter Master department, will be transmitted by a Warrant, in your favor, as usual, tomorrow, and deposited with the Cashier of the Bank U.S: for your receipt for the amount. 
Fenno we hear (to day) is not dead; but the death of Bache confirmed.  
I am, Sir, respectfully 
Your obedient Servant
Samuel Lewis Senr
Samuel Hodgdon Esqr
