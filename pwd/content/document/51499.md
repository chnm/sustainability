Sept. 20, 1795
from Capt. E. Miller 
William Simmons Esq. Acct. 
Philadelphia
Middletown Sept. 20th1795.
Wm. Simmons Esq. 
Philadelphia
Sir
I Receivd. your Letter of the 11th Sept. in which was inclosed an Order of John Chester Esq. for two hundred fifty five dollars & Sixty nine Cents for the pay of myself and men at this Randezvous for the months of July, & August 1795. Also a receipt roll comparing with the above Sum, which will be comple at as soon as posable and forwarded to the Office-
A Final Sum for the Recruiting Service will be Acceptable
To.
Your Humble
Servt. 
Edward Miller Capt.
2nd. Sub Legion
