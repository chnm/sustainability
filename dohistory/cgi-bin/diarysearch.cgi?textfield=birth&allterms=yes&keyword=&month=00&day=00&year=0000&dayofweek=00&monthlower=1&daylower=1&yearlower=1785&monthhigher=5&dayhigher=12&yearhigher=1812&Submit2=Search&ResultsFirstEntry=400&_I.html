 January 14, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Craiges Son, Rheubin Branards allso Son allso. XX.

Clear. I Came home and was Calld by mr Craige to See his wife who was in labour, & was Deld of her first Child, a Son, at 10h Evn and is as Cleverly as Could be Expected. I was Calld from there to See the wife of Rheubin Branard who was Delivd of her first Son & 4th Child before my arival.   at Capt Huseys, Conys & Branards. Births 2nt and 3d. Asa Cumingss remains interd this aftern.  
 

 

 January 17, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thos Stickneys Son. XX.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. I was Calld to See the wife of Thos Stickney who was Dild at 5h Evn of her Second Son, a fine Child. I left my Patients Cleverly and returnd home at 8h. find Dagt Ballard & Childn are gone home. I receivd 6/ as a reward.   at Thos Stickneys. Birth 4.  
 

 

 January 29, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Sheppards Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to See mrss Sheppard at 4h morn. Shee was Deld of a Dagt, her 4th Child, at about 12 hours or mid day. I was Calld from there to Son Jon as wife who is unwell.   at mr Sheppards & Son Jonas. Birth 5th. receivd 24/.  
 

 

 February 1, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jon as Dagt.

Cloudy, Snowd Some. Dagt Ballard was Delivd of a Dagt at 3h pm. mrs Woodwd & I tarried all night. I went to Bed & Slept Some.   at Jonas. Birth 6th.  
 

 

 February 2, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Black Hitties Son.

Snowd. I was Calld from Jonas to See Black Hitty. Shee was Deld of a Son before I arivd, found her Exercisd with pain, administred Something for her relief. returnd home by way of mr Husseys, all as well there as usual. I went to See my Dagt Ballard after I Came home, find her & infant Cleverly. Dolly is gone to work at Old mr Pollards. Sally Cocks gave me a Snuff Box.   at my Sons and Nicholass. Birth 7th. this man is a Portugues who was Brot here by mrs s Hussey from Nantucket.  
 

 

 February 11, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Husseys 3d Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to Capt Husseys, walkt to the Loading place from there, Crosst the mountains of ice. arivd all most fatagud to Death, found his wife Deld of a Son. I returnd home by fort Weston, wrode our hors from mr Pollards. Dagt Ballard [Cleverly].   at Capt Husseys. Birth 8 th to which I have been Calld.  
 

 

 February 14, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth David Pollards 2nd Son. XX.

Clear. I was at home till Evn, 9 O Clock, when I was Calld to See David Pollards wife who was Dild of a fine Son at 10. I tarried all night. mr Stodard is very Sick at that house, Dilarious. Doct Page there to visit him, he was Calld in ye night.   at mr Pollards. Birth 9th. David Pollards wife is 27 years old this day. receivd 6/7 Novr 20 of Old Lady Pollard.  
 

 

 February 20, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and a little Snow. this Day is the annevercyra of my Birth. I have Seen 60 years roall away and but few more must look to See. God grant I may for Death prepared bee!!! I have been at home, finisht a pair of hoes for mr Ballard.   at home.  
 

 

 March 2, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Childss Dagt. XX.

Clear & pleast. mr Gill & wife Sett out for Winslow. I was Calld at 11m to See mrs Child. Shee was Deld at three pm of a Dagt. Benn Porter Came there at Evn for me to go and See his wife. I find Dolly very ill with the mumps when I Came home. Jany 29 = I recd 2 Crowns.   at mr Childs. Birth 10th. Dolly is very ill. Benn Porter Sleeps here. mr Ballard is gone to Sidney.  
 

 

 March 9, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Benn Porters Son.

Clear. mrs Porter in Labour all Day and was Deliverd of a Son at 8 hours Evn. her Case was very Difucult and Shee was very faint after Delivery till about midnight, when Shee revivd and got Some rest. mrs Carr watcht.   at Porters. Birth 11th. A Ship [wreck] mentioned in this Days [herald].  
 

 

 March 17, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Savage [Boltons] Son.

Clear and pleasant. Dolly is quillting a Coat for her Self. I Combd flax foren, was Calld to See mr Partrages Child who is very unwell, has a Cough. I was Calld from there to See Savage Boltons wife who was in Labour and was Deld at 6h pm of a Son. I tarried there all night, my Patient Cleverly.   at mr Partridges & Savage Boltons. Birth 12th. receid 1_ Bushl of apples in the fall, not very good.  
 

 

 March 21, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt James Springers Dagt. XX.

Clear and pleast. mrs Springer was Deld at 7h morn of a Dagt. I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd to mr Gordins, find her about. from there I returnd home, find my famely all well. mr Ballard is gone from home. Sally Cocks is gone to her Dadys. Shee went yesterdy.   at [Capt] [Springers] and [Gordins]. Birth 13[th].  
 

 

 March 27, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I was at mr Gordins, his wife was Deld (at 4h 30m morn) of a Dagt. I left her & inft as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 11h foren. I receivd [3] lb Cottne @ 2/4 pr lb. I Dind and went to See mrs Black, find her better yn my fears. I Calld to See mrs Smith and mrs Capin and at Son Pollards.   at Ditoes. Birth 14th. Calld to Thoms Kenneys at 11h Evng.  
 

 

 April 2, 1795, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. I was Calld at 7h morn to mr Kinneys wife, Shee was Deld at 10h morn of a Son. I left her and inft Cleverly. Crost [part] by drawing ye boat on ye [ice] [thro the] water.   at Ditoes. Birth [15th]. 
 

 

 April 4, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

[Birth Lathrops Dagt].

Cloudy and Clear by turns. mrs Latp was able to work till near SunSet when Shee was more unwell. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 8h Evn and is Comfortable. this is her 5th Child, and one of them is Desceast . I was informd at Evn that a mr Watson was Drouned at Joness mill this aftern.   at Ditoes. Birth 16th. mr Watson was Drownd at Jonsis.  
 

 

 April 13, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Capins Dagt. XX.

Cloudy the most of the Day. I was Calld by mr Capin at 5h morn to See his wife who was in Labour, and was Safe Delivd of a Dagt at 1 pm, and I left her and infant Cleverly and returnd at 3. I was Calld to Capt Molloys.   at mr Capins. Birth 17th. Snowd at night.  
 

 

 April 18, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Branches Son. XX.

Clear the most of the Day. mrs Branch was Deld of a Son at 9h 30m morn. I left Cleverly at 11, Calld at Nath Runels and mr Dyers and Esqr Runels. returnd as far as Son Pollards and went to See mrs Capin, find her not So well as Could be wisht. was at mr Gills, they are well. mrs Abbot is unwell. I Calld to See mrs Parker who is very unwell. Dolly was at mr Pollards, Capins, Gills and mr Burtuns. my Girls have Made me 2 Barrils of Soap this weak.   at [mr Branches]. Birth 18th. receivd 9/ at Deckmans Store April 27th by Branches order.  
 

 

 April 25, 1795, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Abbots Dagt. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. mrs Abbot was very unwell all day, her women Calld at 1h pm and Shee was Deld at 3 of a Dagt, her first born. I went in at mr Blacks, his wife and mrss Smith Calld to See mrss Abbot. I went to See mrs Capin, returnd and took Care of my patients all night.   at mr Abbots. Birth 19th. receivd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 April 26, 1795, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt John Moloys Dagt. XX.

rainy. I was Calld by Capt Molloy about Day, find his wife very unwell. Shee had Some women Calld at 10hM, and Some Came in at intermition. Shee was Deld at 1h pm of a Dagt, her Second Child, both dagts. I tarried till near night when I left mother and Child Cleverly, and returnd. find my famely well. mr Densmores and Livrmrs Dagts here at Evng. mrss Moloy was unwell all Last night alltho Shee Did not Call me till morn.   at Ditoes and Capt Molloys. Birth 20th. receivd 18/ as A reward.  
 

 

 April 27, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night. I went to mr Capins, find her mending; to mr Abbots, his wife is Cleverly; to mr Hamlins, his wife is unwell; at mr Dickmans, receivd 9/ on Benn Branches acount for assisting his wife in Child Birth. I Calld at mr Smiths and took Tea, went and Slept at Son Pollards. I was in great Danger by a fall from the hors but Escapt unhurt thro Gods preserving mercy.   at Son Pollards and other Neigrs. a rainy night. preservd when in Danger by a fall from a hors.  
 

 

 May 1, 1795, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Claps Son. XX

Clear. mr Hodges I was Calld to See mrss Clap who was in Labour. I left home at 4h pm, walkt as far as mr Hodges, wrode with Edwd Savage Junr the rest of ye way. my Patient was Deld at 5 of a Son and is Cleverly. I tarried all night, Slept Some.   at mr Clapss. Birth 21st. I saw mrss Jones there.  
 

 

 May 19, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Blacks Daugt. XX.

Clear foren. I went to the hook, bot a Brass kettle wt 5 lb at 4/ pr lb of Capt FilleBrown. Cloudy aftern. I went to mr Pollards, was at mr Smiths, Pittss, Gills and Son Pollards. Came home at 5, Sett Down to write to Doct Barton and was Calld off by mr Hodges to See mrs Black. Shee was Deld at 7 of a Daugt, her 4th Child; number of Sex Equal. I tarried all night, my Patient was Exercisd with Some pain.   at ye hook & fort, at mr Blacks. Birth 22nt. raind at night. fee receivd June 4th.  
 

 

 May 20, 1795, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jessy Kimbals Dagt, first Born. XX.

rainy. I was Calld from mr Blacks to Capt Cockss to See his Dagt Kimbal at 4h morn. Shee was very ill but was Safe Deld at 4h pm of a fine Daught. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and returnd home by water; was Some fatagud. our famely were in Bed wn I Came [home].   at Capt Cockss. Birth 23 d. receivd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 May 25, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth X Edmond Getchels Son. XX.

Clear and pleasant Except Some what Cool. mrss Getchell had her women Calld aftern and was Safe Deld of a Son at 4h pm. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected at 7. Calld to See mr Fletchers Child, find it a little more Comforlable.   at Ditoes. Birth 24th. receivd 6/7.  
 

 

 May 26, 1795, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jery Badcks Son. XX.

Clear and pleast. I was Calld by Benn Petingal to go to Jerimy Badcoks. went to ye fort by water, was very wet when I arivd there. was taken on hors back there, wrode more than a mile then orderd to return, ye patient being Deld of a Son. I Crost the river in ye Boat after receiving 6/ of mr Petngll for my troubl. I was invited to take Breakfast with mr Smith. I Dind there allso. took Tea at mr Gills. Calld to See mrs Capin, was Calld from there by Brestow Grefin, a man of Coulor whos wife is white. Came home on our hors, went from there by water and was much fatagud by Climbing ye assent. find ye patient Some what ill but I had opt to take rest by Sleep.   Calld to Jery Badcoks. Birth 25th.
