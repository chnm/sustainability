War Department
July 7th 1791
Sir
I have received your favor of the 30th Ultimo containing the ordnance Quarter Master and clothing return up to that date - And when Major Clark's battalion shall be completed I shall expect to receive the muster rolls.
I am
Sir
Your most Obedient Servant
HKnox
Secy of War
Major Isaac Craig
