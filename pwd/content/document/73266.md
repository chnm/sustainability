I certify that there is due to Samuel Park
the sum of Sixty two dollars & 83/100 being for Postage of Letters
addressed to and from Caleb Swan Pay master General on public
Business between 18 Nov. & 31 December 1799 admitted in conformity
to a letter from The Secretary of War of the 8 Nov. 1799 ~
Dollrs 62.83                 W.S.
The Sec of War
