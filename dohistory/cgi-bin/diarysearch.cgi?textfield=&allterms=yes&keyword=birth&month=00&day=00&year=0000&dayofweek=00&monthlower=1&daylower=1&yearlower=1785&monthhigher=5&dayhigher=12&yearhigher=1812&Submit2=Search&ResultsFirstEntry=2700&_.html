 April 4, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Lambds 4th Dagt and 9th Child.

Cloudy. Dagt Lambd [had] her women and Shee was Safe Deld of [her] 4th Dagt & 9th Child at 2h pm. it wd 10/ 2/1 lb. Shee Seems Comfortable as Can be Expected. may we bless [  ] auther. Patty Town Came to Nurs her.   at Ditoes. Birth 4th.  
 

 

 April 7, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast foren. I left Son Lambds at 10h am, Sally Came with me in the Carriage they wrode up in. we Calld at mr Capins to rest the hors, reacht Son Jonas before dark. we took Tea and Came home. I feel fatagued. Allin Came home with me.   I went to See mrs Philbk and Set out for home.  
 

 

 April 8, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 raind foren. Judg Cony Calld in to See us. Dagt Ballard & Childn here aftern. I feel fatagued with my Journey.   at home. Judg Cony here, Dgt Ballard allso.  
 

 

 April 15, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Allin Lambd here, Says his fathers Famely are Cleverly. Harnon Barton with him, his Marm & famely are all wel.   at home, Bakt. Allin here.  
 

 

 April 17, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, Clear aftern. Sister Barton Came here. I was Calld at 10h Evn to mr Pipers, his wife in Labour. Childn Sleep here.   at home till 10h Evng. Sist Barton here.  
 

 

 April 18, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Pipers 3d Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Piper was Safe Deld at 4h am of her 3d dagt. I receivd 1 qt spt, 1 lb Candles, _ lb Sugar. I arivd at home Early. there was a fire raged to ye East of here but fortunately it was Stopt.   at mr Pipers. Birth 5th. fee receivd, 12/.  
 

 

 April 29, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 raind in the morn, Clear the most of ye day. Son Jona, his wife, James & Elisa Dind here. mrs Carter Calld in aflern. A mr Greanlief Calld here, took Breakfast, Says he was Cast away at Sea not long since, his home at 7 mile brook. Sally & Martha Sleep here.   at home. Part of my Childn Dind here.  
 

 

 May 7, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very pleast. I have Sett trees & Current Bushes & done other matters in my Gardin. Esq Titcomb, S. Cumings and Lat Hamlin Calld here. it is training through out ye Common wealth ys day.   at home.  
 

 

 May 19, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard to meeting all day. I went aftern, wrode with mr Church Williams from his house. Calld of mr Carters aft meeting, took Tea. mr Wms Conducted me home in his Shais from his house. Hannah Glidon & Sally Balld Sleep here. I went to Son Jonas for our milk. he and Hanh Getchel Came from Clinton, they are well there.   at meeting, Text John.  
 

 

 May 28, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, raind at night. I left Son Epms after breakfast, Calld at mr Robinsons, mr Orocks, Capt Pittss, mr Pipers, mr Mosiers, mrs Andruss, Smiths, Moodys & Son Jonas; reacht home before night.   at Ditoes & other Neighbors.  
 

 

 May 31, 1811, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Lefaett Rowels Dagt. XX.

Clear & Cool. I was Calld to mr Rowels, his wife in Labour. I reacht there at 11h am. the Patient Deld of a dagt, her first born at 1h pm. I left her & inft as wel as Could be Expected & returnd home about 4. I Bakt after I Came home, recd 1 Dolr.   at mr Rowels. Birth 6th. recd 6/ in part, July 9th paid 6/ in full.  
 

 

 June 6, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Winsl Burdins Dagt by Fanny Hodges out of Wedlock. XX.

Clear morn, raind & Thundered before night. I was Calld Early ys morn to mr Hodges to see his Dagt Fanny who was in Labour & was Safe Delivered of a Dagt, her first born, which Shee in the Severety of her illness Dectared Winslow Burden was the Falher of. I tarried till 4h pm then left them Comfortable. Came to Capt Pittss, went in at mr Thwings, Came back to ye Capts & tarried all night.   at Ezra Hodges. Birth 7th. recvd 6/, 12-2/1 lb Tea.  
 

 

 June 9, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Marshal Edsons 1st Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld about 10h am to See the wife of Marshal Edson who was in Labour. Shee was Safe deld at a little after noon of her first Dagt & 4th Child. I left them very Comfortable at [ ]h pm & returnd home.   at marshal Edsons. Birth 8th. received 12/ as a reward. Wm Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 13, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Oroks 2nd Dagt by this wife. XX.

Clear & pleast. I was Calld at the riseing of the Sun by mr Orok to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 8h 30m of a Daught, her Second by him. I tarried till 5h pm & he Conducted me to Son Jonas. I walkt fm there home, find mr Ballard not So wel as I Could wish. I took Care of my piggs, fouls & Sett Squash plants.   at mr Oroks. Birth 9th. recd two Dollars reward.  
 

 

 June 30, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. Sist Barton, Dagt Ballard, Elisa, mrs Smith & Charlotte took Tea here. Luke Barton Calld in at Evng.   at home, friends here.  
 

 

 July 6, 1811, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I have Brewd & made a Chees. Hannah & Hitty Calld in, they have been gathering rasburies.   at home.  
 

 

 July 17, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. Sist washt my N roome. mrs Farewel went to mr Bennets. I was Calld by mr Mc Fartin at 5h pm to go and See his wife who is near the hour of Labour. I larried at his house till the 25th.   at mc Farlins.  
 

 

 July 25, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mc Farlins 2nd Son, 4th Child by his present wife.

Son Jona & wife went to Clinton 23 inst. sister Barton Set out for Boston in the Packet this morn.   Birth 10th.  
 

 

 August 6, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Moses Patridges 2nd Son & 5th Child.

Clear. I have workt hard in the hous & Gardin. Barny Lambd & Wm Ballard Sleep here. Dagt Lambd Came in the Stage, Slept at mr Dilinghams. Moses Patridg Calld me to See his wife in Labour at 10h Evng. Shee was Safe Deld at 11h 30m Same Evng. I returnd home about 2h morn, her Child was a Son, ye Second male; Shee has had 3 Daugts. mr P. had been after 3 Docters, Could not obtain neither of them before he Calld me.   at Moses Patridgess. Birth 11th. I have recd 3 [p ] Spirit.  
 

 

 August 13, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have washt my Buttery, done other hous wk & wed Gardin. I wen to Son Jonas towards night. mr Ballard been to the Settlement. Dagt Lambard was gone to Son Pollards, Shee Calld at Jonas on return.   to Jonas.  
 

 

 August 26, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Ezra left here after Breakfast to attend Coart, he is Foreman of the Jury. Son Cyrus Calld here after Sun Sett, tarried a fue muniets and went on for home. he informd me that a Child of A mr Bean died with the hooping Cough.   at home. Ezra left here. Son Cyrus Calld here, told of a Death.  
 

 

 August 29, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Some rain aftern. I have workt hard part of ye day but feel very unwel.   at home. aniversary of ye Birth, yesterday, Dagt Lucy, 53 years.  
 

 

 September 1, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warn. I am very Lambe with my toe. Son Jona Sleeps here, his famely at Clinton are wel. this is the anneversary of Son Lambards Birth.   at home, Son Jona Slept here.  
 

 

 September 8, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been So unwel that Hannah B. & Martha Came, got breakfast & took Care of me. H. Gliddon here the rest of ye day & night, Abm Field allso. Son Epm and his Dagt Calld in to See me.   at home, very unwel, there was no preaching at ye meeting hous.  
 

 

 September 14, 1811, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been very Sick. mrs Smith and Stone Came and took Care of me, got my Bed in to the North room. my husband Sent for Doct Cony but they Calld Doct Ellis. he left me medisin. mrss Firbush Came and Set with me till Evn when her Husband Came to accompany her home. Joanna Slept here.   [at home, very Sick. Doct Ellis to See me].
