Copy of a letter to
Cap Butler 30.th March 
1796.
Green Ville March 3oth  1796
Sir
The enclosed orders for L.t Polhemus, I am
directed to transmit to you, as it is supposed he
is at, or will bee soon at your post. and
request you to deliver them to him whenever he
arrives at Laramis - be [undecipherable] to acknowledge
the receipt of this, and inform me if W.
Polhemus has arrived, or you hae heard from 
him.




 by order of the General
Capt. Bulter

 I am Sir your
obedient and Humble Serv
M.
