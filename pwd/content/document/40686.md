Warrant from this Seey
of Ugar
North carolina
Sir
Pay out of the funds furnished you by the Honorable Secnte.
my of the Treatery for it injuries the sum of four hundred
forty three Dollars to the Invalid Penfioners of the united 
States named in the fife trnndmitted to you on the eighth
day of february last for the payment of the first moiety, or to
their legae representatives according to the jurryertions 
specified opposite their names reperstively in the Sisth
column of said list, being for the  seemed inoiety of their 
cunnial herism Ending on the fourth day of march one 
thinef and seven hundred and ninety, for tohieh payment 
this shall be you sufficient warrant.
Give at the War office in the city of
Newyork this 22d day of may 1790.
HKnox
Secry of War
443 Dollars
To john Haywood Eign
