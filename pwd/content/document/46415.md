No 3.
Estimate of Subsistence for the Officers at Fort Fayette 
Big beaver block house Fort Franklin & at Wheeling
for 1793

Captain 365 days @ 3 rations p day equal 1095 [rations] 
Lieutenants [365 days] @ 2 [rations] p day equal 1460
Surgeon Mates [365 days] @ 2 [rations] p day equal 1460 
Total No. Rations 4015
4015 Rations @ 1 Cents each equals 401.50
War Department 
Accountants office
April 18th 1794
Joseph Howell Acct. 
Major Isaac Craig
