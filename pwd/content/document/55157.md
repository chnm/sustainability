Accountants Office Octr. 5th.1796
Jonathan Jackson EsqrBoston
Sir I received your Letter of the 28th ulto. enclosing Capt. George Ingersolls receipt for one hundred & fifty Dollars, which sum will be entered to your Credit on the Books of this Office. I am vc. Wm. Simmons
