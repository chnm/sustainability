Nehemiah Freeman, Esq. Department of War R. P. W. West Point Accountants Office

       New York                                   February 20th 1796
                       Sir
                              I have just time to acknowledge the receipt of your 
letter of the 15 instant together with its accompaniments-- you have enclosed a list
of the retained Bounty due the men enlisted by Capt Richard S. Blackburn which you may
pay out of any unappropriated Monies in your hands-

                          I am sir (indecipherable)
                              William Simmons
