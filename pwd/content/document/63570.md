7-16-1798



                                          undecipherable July 16.th 1798

         By the enclosed note from the Commander in Chief you will see that I have been Directed by him to draw on you for 10'000 Dollars for the use of the Quarter Master Department.  I have only been able to procure undecipherable for 7'000 and am not certain that more will be wanted.- I have advised the Quarter Mater General of my having undecipherable - as you (paper missing) have requested him to give you authority

(paper missing) pay my bills-

                            I am very Respectfully-
                                   This your most
                                         Humble Servt
                                          Wm. H. Harrison

Col Saml Hodgdon                             undecipherable
