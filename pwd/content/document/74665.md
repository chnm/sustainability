Philadelphia March 05 1800
159
		The President of the United States, requests the Secretary of War
to send him without delay a list of the officers of the army who were appointed during
the last recess of the Senate of the United States, that the President may be able 
to make them nominations as the constitution requires.
Secretary of War				Philadelphia March 9th 1800
