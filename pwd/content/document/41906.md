Letter from the War Office
September 23d 1791
Cloathing to be delivered John Schrider in Captain Haskells Company 2d Reg't. 
Mr. Wm. Knox 
at Mr. Hodgdon's in 
Water Street between Arch and Race Street  - No 71
 
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401310]
War-department, Sept: 23d 1791
Sir.
Please to furnish the bearer John Schrider, belonging to Captain Haskell's company ^of regular^, and left sick in hospital, with one pair of woolen overalls - one pair of shoes, and a woolen vest - taking his receipt.  
I am Sir
Your humble servt 
Jno Stagg Junr 
Mr. Wm. Knox 
[stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401310]
