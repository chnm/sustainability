Robert Gilmor Esqr January 2d 1801
Chairman of the Naval Committee
Baltimore
Sir I have received your letter dated 31 Dec last year, with a Bundle of accounts for expenditures on acct of the Fortifications at Baltimore which accounts shall be placed under examination in course and the result communicated to you
I am etc
