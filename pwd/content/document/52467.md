Department of War 377
Accountants' Office
January 4th. 1796
Nicholas Fish Esq.
Supr. New York _
Sir
The Treasurer will remit you the sum of Five hundred Dollars which you will please pay to Forman Cheesman, being for his Salary as Constructor of the Frigate building at New York from the 1st of October to te 31st December 1795 inc. for which sum please take duplicate receipts. __
I am Sir &c
William Simmons
