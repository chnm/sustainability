Letter from the Secretary of War enclosing a report on the claims of certain Canadian Refugees, made in pursuance of the act, entitled "An Act for the relief of the refugees from the British provinces of Canada and Nova Scotia."
30th December, 1800.
Referred to the Committee appointed on the 28th Ultimo, to prepare and bring in a bill for regulating the grants of land appropriated for the refugees from the British provinces of Canada and Nova Scotia.
Com: on Refugees
