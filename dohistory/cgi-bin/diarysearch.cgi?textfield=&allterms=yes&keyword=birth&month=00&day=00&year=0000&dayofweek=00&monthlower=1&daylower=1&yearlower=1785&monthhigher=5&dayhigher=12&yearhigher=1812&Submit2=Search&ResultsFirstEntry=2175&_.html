 December 12, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard to meeting. Ephm & Son Lambd to Son Pollards, they Calld here at Evng. Tommy going home with his dady. Darcos Hodges with Son Pollard going to liv with mr Partrage. mr Bunker was in Company with them.   at [home.] Sons Pollard & Lambd here.  
 

 

 December 20, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Collecting taxes. I have been at home. mrs Farewell Calld here, Sally Church allso.   at home. mrs Farwell here.  
 

 

 January 2, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Waids 2nd Son. XX.

Clear morn, Cloudy part ye Day. I was Calld at 5h ys morn to go and See mrss Waid, Shee was Deld of a son at 10. I left her and infant as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 3 pm. we went to my Sons to dine, or rather Sup, after meeting. Nabby Came at 10h Evn, then went to Jonas.   at mr Waids. Birth 1st. receivd 4:6.  
 

 

 January 4, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Anthony Brackets Dagt. XX.

Clear and very Cold foren, more moderate aftern. I was Calld at 4h morn to See the wife of Anthony Bracket. Shee was, after hard Labour, Safe Deld at 4h 30m Evn of her 4th Child and first Daughtr and I Came home at 7. I spent ye Evng kniting. Hannah, Wilm & Sally Ballard Sleep here, their dady & marm gone to Son Lambds.   at mr Brackets. Birth 2nd. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 January 11, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at Evng. I have been to my Sons to See Patty, Shee fell out of her Brothers arms & hurt her face. I have finished futing mr Ballards hoes and began to fut A pair for my Self. Nabby and I pickt up the Chips at the door. mr Ballard to son Lambds. Docl Barton here, his famely well. Eliab Shaws wife & mrs Braley Calld here, Sarah Cleark allso.   at my Sons. A Suden Death at Hollowell this day, a mr [Johnston].  
 

 

 January 15, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, we Bakt Bread, Pudings, meat & mins Pies. mrs Farewell Calld here at dusk. mr Ballard is gone to run a line for mr Whipple west Side this river.   at home.  
 

 

 January 19, 1803, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night. mr Ballard to Son Pollards, Dagt Ballard & Lambard Came home from there. I have Brewd and done other work.   at home. Dagt Lambd Calld here.  
 

 

 January 21, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

[X. Birth magr Stickneys Dagt. X.]

Clear. I was Calld Early this morn to magr Benn Steckneys wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld of her 3 Daugt & 8th Child, at 5h 30m Evn. I left her Comfortable & went with mr Gill to see his wife.   at mgr Stickneys. Birth 3d. recd [8/] as a reward.  
 

 

 January 28, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been helping Nabby Bake mins Pies. I went into Bed quite fatagued, and was Calld Directly after by mr Young to go and See his wife who was in Labour. arivd there at 11h 30m Evng. her women were Calld.   at [mr Youngs].  
 

 

 January 29, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth John Youngs 2nd Daugt. XX.

Clear and Cold. mrs Young was Safe Deld at 2h morn of her 4th Child & Second Dagt. I tarried with her till after noon and returned home, left her & inft Cleverly. find mr Thwing here Dineing.   at [       ]. Birth 4th. June [7 ] recd 9/.  
 

 

 January 31, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. [Birth of 5th Child, a daugt. Lambd. XX. XX. Birth mrs Bodwels Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 20m after midnight to go and See my Dagt Lambard who was in Labour. She was Safe deld of her 5th Child & 2nd Daught at 1h 20m. I tarried with her till near the middle of the day when I was Calld by mr Edson to go and See mrs Bodwell who was in Labour. Shee was Safe deld at 6h Evng of a Daugt, and I returnd home at 11h 20m. I had a Severe turn of Cramp in the night.   at Son Lambards & mrs Bodwels. Birth Son Lambards Dagt. Birth 5th, fee receivd. Birth 6th, recd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 February 11, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charls Gills 4th Son & [6]th Child. XX.

Clear and Pleast. I was Calld at 3m after mid night to go and See mrs Gill who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10h morn of her 6th Child by her last marriage and 4th son. I left her and infant Cleverly at 1h Pm, the wrideing very bad. I Calld at Son Lambds and took ye milk out of her infts breast, they were very full and hard.   at mr Charls Gills. Birth 7th. March 14th recd of mr Gill, by the hand of Son Ephm, 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 February 13, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Son Pollards 4th Dagt. XX.

rainy. I have been at Ditoes. my Daught was taken in Labour, her women were Calld in before dark and Shee was Safe Deld at 11h 10m Evn of her 4th dagt and 6th Child, and is as well as Can be Expected.   at Ditoes. Birth 8th. recd as a rewd. 
 

 

 February 22, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Pollard returnd from his Mothers, David Pollards wife & Polly Pollard with him. mr Hodges, his wife, Fanny, and his 3 Childn which were Born at a Birth, Sleep there.   at Dito.  
 

 

 February 24, 1803, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I Came home from Son Pollards accompanied by Robd Fletcher. Calld to See mrs Thompson. mrs Farewell Calld at my hous. Shee has been to her hous, finds her hous broken open and part of her Efects gone.   at Ditoes & at home.  
 

 

 February 25, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Capin, Dagt Lambd and Allin & Lucy here. mr Hodges, his wife, his 3 Childn, and Fanny to assist them, they all Slept here Except Fanny & Mehaly; Dagt Ballard been here allso.   at home, had Compy.  
 

 

 March 7, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Thompsons Child.

Clear. I was Calld up about mid night to go to mr Thompsons, wrode beyond mr Edsons when I was met and informd the patient was Delivered. I then returned home attended by mr Edson, arivd at 2h morn, very Cold. I have been mending Cloaths. Lidia washt. a mr Lathrop Came to work for son Ephm.   Set out to go to mr Thompsons.  
 

 

 March 14, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 raind and haild. Son Ephm Deliverd me 12/ which mr Gill Sent to me. I have been at home, felt unwell.   at home.  
 

 

 March 29, 1803, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mr Trask, his wife & Dagt Ballard here to Tea. mr Getchel Calld here. I began to foot a Stockin for Dolly Lambd.   at home, had Company.  
 

 

 April 9, 1803, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, the Snow went of. I was Calld aftern by mr Moody to go and See his wife, found mrs Woodward there. the patient is in Defucalt Circumstances, we both tarried with her all night.   at mr Moodys, his wife unwell.  
 

 

 April 10, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am at mr Moodys. Doct Hubard was Calld to See her, he gave no Medisin but is of opinion that her Complaint wil be thron of by natures Eforts. I tarried this night, a number of Ladies there to visit ye patienl.   at Ditoes. Doct Hubard was Calld there.  
 

 

 April 11, 1803, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I tarried with mrs Moody till near night and returnd home very unwell, find my husband very unwell. I was Exercised with Such a pain in my head that it Seemed as if my life must go. I Calld Lydia up in the night. Shee applyed poultises to my feet and warm Brandy to my head with hot flanell, and I became Some Easier before morning.    
 

 

 April 17, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd and blowd & raind. our men Seeking for the Sheep, found Some of them. mr Ballard to meeting aftern. a funeral discoars was Deld by ye revd mr Stone from PSalm 37th C and 37th vers.   at home.  
 

 

 April 22, 1803, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to my Sons to see his Son Jona who hurt him Self yesterday at his uncle Lambds. we are aprehensive he has a Briech. we Bathd and bandaged him and I Came home. I am informd that mrs Whitwell was Delivd last night, her infant Expired Soon and Shee Expired this morning at 4 O Clock. Dagt Ballard took Coffee here, her Son Wilm Sleeps here. may God Support the bereeved in their affliction and be better to them than what he has taken from them.   at home. hear of the Death of mrs Whitwel & infant. mr Livermore here this aftern.  
 

 

 April 24, 1803, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cloudy by turns. I went to meeting and to the hous of mr Whitwell. his wife and infant were interd, a vast Concoarce of people followed in procecsion after the Corps. we had but one Service at the meeting hous. Revd mr Stone was Calld to attend a funeral at Capt Homans, Varsalborough. mr Gillet Presided at mr Whitwels. I am informd that mrs Cowen is failing very fast.   at meeting and at the funeral of mrss Whitwall & infant. I Calld at mr Pittss, mr Thwings & Moodys.
