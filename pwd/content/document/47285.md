6 Hats

30 Shirts - 4 Serjeants & 26 Privates 
6 W. Overalls  - 1 Serjeants & 5 Privates 
18 L. Overalls - 3 Serjeants & 15 Privates
30 Shoes
6 Stocks
6 Clasps
12 Socks
6 Blankets

6 Canteens
2 Epaulets 
(Signed) Wm Kersey Capt., 1st US. SubLegion
