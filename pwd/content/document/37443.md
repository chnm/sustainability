War Office Septr. 19th. 1785
An order to detach 
Capn Hamtramck's 
company to the Ohio
War Office September 19th: 1785 
Sir 
The Public Service demanding 
that all the Troops which are raised should 
be immediately marched to the Ohio - I desire 
that you would direct the Company under 
Captain Hamtramck to be in readiness to march 
at a days notice - I request you to press 
the recruiting service so as to complete his 
Company. If however this should be impossible 
and that Officers cannot be appointed at 
present to his Company, I may perhaps order 
that some of the recruits which may come 
from Connecticut to join him pro tempe: 
and possibly an Officer or two from that State. 
I am Sir 
Your Hble Servant 
HKnox
Major Nicholas Fish
