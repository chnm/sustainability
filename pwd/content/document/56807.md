Mr. Josiah Fox
War Office
Sir
I shall be obliged by your calling this morning at ten o'clock at this office. I wish for some information relative to prices and facility of ob-taining timber in Virginia, such as masts & spars [& spars inserted with carrot symbol after "masts"] & pine & oak plants.
V. obt Servt
T.Pickering
Department of State
Friday, March 11th 1797 --
