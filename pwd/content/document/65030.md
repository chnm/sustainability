Shepherds Town  Octr 11th 
Major Samuel Hodgdon 
By Post Philadelphia or Trenton
Elias Langham 
11th Octo 1790 
Recd 17
Shepherds Town Octr 11th 1798
Sir,
I have just recd yours of the 25th of Septr ordering certain stores to Staunton to the address of Capt Gibson.
The order shall be completed with [undecipherable] the stores on the way in three days - such Articles as are ordered of which there is now suitable on hand, will be purchased, and the account thereof together with the expense of Transportation rendered at the end of the present quarter.
When I forwarded my returns and accounts, I did not tell you that I had recd some damaged clothing from Capt. Lewis - These articles being entirely unfit for service.  I declined to return them because 'tis unnecessary to keep such articles on hand.
P S
Please review Capt. Bishops receipt for the articles forwarded him at Winchester.  E L
