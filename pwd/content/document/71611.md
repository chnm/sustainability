October 5 1799
Mr. Fred.k Sprigg
West Point
Sir
I have received your letter dated this Instant, enclosing your account for the months of July, Aug. Sept. 1799 amounting to ninety Dollars, which sum you will receive from Nicholas Fish, supervisor New York, and giving him your duplicate receipts.
I am etc W.S.
