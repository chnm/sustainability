38
By this day's post I am honored with your Letter of the 7th Inst. I find myself at full Liberty, and disposed to comply with its contents - shall accordingly without loss of time select the pieces, and proceed in the execution agreeable to the inscription enclosed - and when they are finished, take your advice relative to the delivery. -

You will please to present my best regards to Mrs.. Knox; and believe me to be with all possible respect yours &ca -
S H
Major General Knox
