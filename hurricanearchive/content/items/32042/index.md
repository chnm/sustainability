When Jefferson Parish didn\'t have the pumps on during Katrina, it resulted in 6 inches to 1 foot of water in our house-  this is what the floodwaters did to the old parquet flooring in my parents\' bedroom.  Being soaked in that amount of water and having the house all closed up like that for a month caused the wood flooring to peel off the slab and buckle.   You can blame Aaron Broussard for that!!!
