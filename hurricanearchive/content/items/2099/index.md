Me and my parents had been watching the news for the past few days watching the Hurricane. Then all the sudden the evacuation started I had never seen anything like it, it took us so long just to go a single mile. Our plan was to head to Dallas but seeing that it took us so long to get anywhere and how much gas the trip was taking. We decided insted to just go to the lake where all of our other family was the trip up there took twelve hours. When we got there we saw that some of our family had brought alot of there friends with them. After the Hurricane came the power went off, man staying up there with no power and thirty people and little water that was the worse experience I have ever had.
