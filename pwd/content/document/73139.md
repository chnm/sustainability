W. Buck -
pine street - 
Phil - december 23rd evening
Wm. Buck will please to deliver up to my waiter a horse to convey the quarter
master sergeant to fort Mifflin this evening
to return to morrow morning the hiring
of it to be paid by the quarter master
general's deputy M. Miller North 8th street
No.2. 
Lewis Tousard
[567]
I certify that Wm. Buck
hired a horse for conveying an order to
fort Mifflin in execution of Major general
A Hamilton's order relative to the funeral
honors to be paid to the memory of the
late chief general Washington
The horse was hired in the evening
and returned only at 12o clock the next day
Lewis Tousard
