Knoxville August 31st 1799
Sir
I have drawn on you this day in favor of Joseph Greer, payable to him or order after ten days sight, for which value has been received in [undecipherable] to amount four hundred twenty one Dollars and seventy cents, to be appropriated to the Quarter Master Department and having [passed ] this Bill to the Credit of the Treasurer.
Request your paying the same.
I am very respectfully
Your Humble Servant
David Henley
Agent War
To the Honorable James McHenry Secretary of War Philadelphia
