Genl. Knox
Dear Genl.

                     It is important that you do 
        not omit giving the Governor of Kentucky
        power to protect the road between that
        State & Virginia - Mr. Edwards should
        be the Bearer of the instruction ---

                           Yours etc
                              R. King

Genl. Knox
