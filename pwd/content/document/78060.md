From Letter Book H. Page 89.



                                           Accountants Office
                                               August 27, 1801

Caleb Swan Esq.

   Pay Master Gen.

Sir:

    On settlement of the account Lieu. Meriwether Lewis

1 Regt. Infantry it appears that he has drawn from the con-
tractor 950 Rations as Commanding Officer at Charlottesville,
Virga. from 1 Jan. 1798 to 30 April 1799 amounting to One
hundred forty nine dollars & 96/100, to which he is not
entitled. You will accordingly deduct the same from his
pay a.c. as it becomes due.

                          I am etc.
                          W.L.
