Sir
W.O. May 14th, 1795
The bearer is Mr Josia Fox who for a near a year past has been employed in the Navy department in this city, assisting M.r Humphreys the Constructor in making the draughts of moulds for the frigates. He is handsomely spoken of by M.r Humphreys who thinks there are few men in this Country equally qualified at this time.
M.r
