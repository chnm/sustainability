Secy at War
return of 7 invalids
Certified to him by
Secy Avery -
May 25 Retd 31st
1790
War office may 25th 1790
Sir,
John avery Junior Esq. has transmitted
me Several duplicate certificates of Pensions
of which I have the honor to inclose a list.
But as some explanation is necessary which
I have requested this day, before I could issue
my Warrant. they are not comprehended
in the warrant for the second moiety as
soon I shall be informed of the nature
of the case I shall transmit you a Warrant
for the payment
I am
Sir
Your most obedient
Servant
H Knox
