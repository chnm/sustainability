In a way, I reminded myself of her during this period and began moving on and taking care of business. I didn\'t even know I had that in me. I immediately went back to work (and because of that, got promoted) and bought a house in Algiers for almost nothing because it was the perfect time. I don\'t look back, ever. There are too many people that had it much worse, and I would feel silly feeling sorry for myself. The life lesson that I learned during this time is that a house is a house is a house, but a home is where you make it. \r\n
