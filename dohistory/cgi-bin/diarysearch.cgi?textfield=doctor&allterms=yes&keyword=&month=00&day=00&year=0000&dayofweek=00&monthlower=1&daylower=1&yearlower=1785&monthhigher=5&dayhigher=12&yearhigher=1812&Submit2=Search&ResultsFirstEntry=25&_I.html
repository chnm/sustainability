 April 22, 1786, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Esqr Lovjoy Dind here, Oriah Cleark & his Son also. Son Town Came here. Polly Hamlin & Betsy Chever here. mr Ballard at mr Pollards aftern. X. I Lent Doct Coney six pennywt & a half X of X Rhubarb X. [sic] I have been at home ys Day.    
 

 

 May 1, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Showry Day. Hannah Gone to help Polly H.. it is Town meeting. Doct Coney Chosen to Represent ys Town in ye General Cort; mr Bullin Grandjuren. mrs Ellis was here, informs yt mrs Allin is very Low. ye Northern Ligt appears ys Eving. I sent 32 Skeins of Tow to mrs Chamberlin. mr Butlin & mr Taylor dind here.    
 

 

 May 12, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 I Came home attendid by mr Moor. Receivd my fee & pay for medecin. (arivd at 4/ ys morn). mr Ballard at Doct Coneys makeing Rates yesterd & ys day. Jono found the Loggs & Came home Last Evinng. Sherebiah Town Slept here. Polly Hamlin been here ys day, Eliphlt unwell. the mill Lies still ys night.    
 

 

 May 15, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Savage Boltons Son. X

Clear & Pleast. I was Calld at 11 O/Clok to Savage Boltons, Shee wife being in Travil. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 3 aftern of a fine Son. his oxen attached & Drove away about 7. I walkt home. Jonn has Swapt away our hors with mr Sleeper. mr Gillbreath sleeps here. mr Ballard been at Doctr Coneys makeing rates. Mr Blanchard at work for us. Rachel Faulh here ys Day. ye Girls washt & Bakt & washt floars.    
 

 

 May 19, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 I was at home. Mrs Williams here. thee girls went to mr Craggs. Brot 3 pair of shoes @ 6/ pr pair. mr Ballard Sowd flax & went to Doctr Coneys.    
 

 

 May 24, 1786, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 I spun 14 knots of thread. mr Ballard went to Broth Moores, heard yt Doct Barton has Removd to Winslow. our Mare Returnd. Mr Toby from Sandage here & mr Bisbee, Polly & Sally Drank Tea here.    
 

 

 June 11, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear morn. mr Brown went in hast after Doct Colman for his Eldist Son being sick. I went from home at 12 O Clok to See him, find him very ill. Returnd home at Sun Set, found Sherebiah, Betsy & Polly Town and James Stacpool here. I was informd that mr Hathaway who was Drowned Last week was taken up & is to be interd this day. Ephm Cowin Came here ys Evinng to work for mr Ballard one month.    
 

 

 July 11, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 a hott day. we were Spinng Cotton. mr Ballard Gone to Coart on ye grand Jury. Mrs Coy here, Polly Hamln also. Doctr Coney returnd from Boston, and James Burten. Cyrus Bt 14-1/2 lb Butter of Abram Page. I pickt String Beens.    
 

 

 July 16, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear, hott day. I attended ye public worship aftern, & Bakt Bisquits for Jono to Carry to Sea. Polly Hamlin here, mr Collar also from winslow. he brot his wife & oldest Daughter to varsalboro to see Doct Williams.    
 

 

 July 19, 1786, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. Polly Hamln here. Doctr Barton Breakfasted with us. we Began to hill our Corn.    
 

 

 July 23, 1786, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 a hott day. I attended Divine Servise aftern. mr Foster Delivird a discoarce from Math 25/41ver, a Shour after servis. mrs Woodward & Nbr Savage Supt with us. Isaac Cowen Enformd of ye Death of Susanah Sautbe who Deceast Last weak. Doct Coney returnd ye Rhubarb which he Borrowd of me and 1 penny wt & 20 grains over plus. Lucy Town is one year old this Day.    
 

 

 August 18, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 I have been at home. Polly Taylor spun 6 Skeins of Cottne & Dolly & Hannah did hous work. mr Ballard Came home, he is informd of ye Death of Death of Doct Gardner. Ezckiel Leighton Sleeps here.    
 

 

 August 22, 1786, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Cool. I was at home. mrs Williams here. mr Jones yt Livs at Doct Coneys, hr ys Eving.    
 

 

 August 28, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 this is Daughter Towns Birth Day, aged 28 years. I am not So well as I coold wish. my girls washing. Hannah North here, Says her Mame is Coming to See me. David Fletchers wife was Delivrd of a Son ye 22 inst. Mrs North & ye two mrs Coxs here, mr Brooks wh them. mr Ballard wt to mr Bisbees. Doct Coney here, Says mrs Hardin is unwell again. Polly & Sally, Theophs & [Jemmy] & Elijah Crage here at Evinng.    
 

 

 August 30, 1786, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 a Clear Day. I went to See Sally Brown. She is very unwell. Mr Ballard went to Survay Land for Doct Coney. Mrs Edson Brot me home 24 & 3/4 yds Cottne & Linning Cloath wh Shee wove.    
 

 

 September 15, 1786, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Jono is Better. mr Ballard at Doct Coneys fore noon, making plan aftern. Dolly & Clarisa went to Capt Sualls. Shee went home towards night. Polly Hamlin Sleeps with Dolly. I feel some unwell.    
 

 

 September 18, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Cloudy day. mrs Densmr Came here & workt for me. I feel some unwell, Dolly also. mr Ballard makeing plan. Doct Coney here, Let me have half oz. harts horn & 2 Stiptic Pouds, prs 1/. [Tom Jonsn] Came for me to See mr Willms, he spits Blood.    
 

 

 September 20, 1786, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Came home aftern, found Dolly Better. Polly Taylor & Lucy Porter here. Doct Coney here from mr Browns, Says he is very poorly. mr Ballard Been to ye hook, Carrid thee plann to mr Vaun, receivd his pay. Came home ys Evinng. Jono returnd from Sandy river, Informs yt mrs Eatons father Deseast Last wk & was interd on Sunday Last.
