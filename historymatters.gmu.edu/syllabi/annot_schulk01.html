When parents attend the Open House for the high school at the beginning of the year, each teacher is required to provide a written explanation of the goals and rationale for each course. This year, for the first time, I have posted an electronic version of that explanation for students and parents to consult. High school teachers will recognize the list of required texts as college level readings. Although this class is not an Advanced Placement class, it provides the full equivalent of most college survey courses to an audience of students who are all college bound. From the very beginning, students are taught that their primary task is to learn to identify an author's interpretation and to evaluate interpretation critically on the basis of the evidence.
