March 5th 1801.
I certify that there is due to Doctor James W Wallace sum of Forty dollars **** for Medicine & attendantce on the Recruits stationed at Fauquier Court House under the command of Lt. Jno Williams in February 1800 which is admitted agreeably to the Report of Dr John Bullard to whom his account was required for taxation by the Sec. of War.
W. S.
Dolls 40.
The Sec.y of War
