Knoxville TN 10 July 1799
Sir
I have drawn on you this day
in favor of Joseph Habersham esquire or
order or seventy dollars and thirteen cents
payable at sight, being for value received in
[illegible] of John Crozier Post Master at Knoxville
and which money is to to be appropriated to the
contingent and incidental expences for the
Department of War.  Having passed the
amount of the bill to the credit of the Treasurer
request due honor thereto
I am respectfully
Your Hub. Servt.
David Henley
The honorable
James McHenry Secy War
