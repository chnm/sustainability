Baltimore Ap.l 19.85

Mr. Joseph Howell

Sir

I shall esteem it a particular favor to inform me the of Capt Nich. Ruseton Moore formerly of Moylans House, leaving the Army, I beleive it was the last of 78, or the beginStrike-through texting of 79 - Perhaps he may Appear on the Muster Roll, as a Lieu.t Although he commanded the 4th Troop in Moylan's Reg. at the time he left the Army.

Jo. Howell Jun.r

Tho.s N Bailey
