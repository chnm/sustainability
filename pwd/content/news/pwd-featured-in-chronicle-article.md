<p>This week the hard work of the PWD editors and volunteer transcribers was featured in an article entitled <a href="http://chronicle.com/article/Historians-Ask-the-Public-to/134054/" title="Chronicle Article">“Historians Ask the Public to Help Organize the Past,”</a> in the Chronicle of Higher Education. Reporter Marc Parry offered the story of the archive’s original reconstruction, its innovative online-first format, and our use of community transcription. The piece includes interviews with Editor-in-Chief Christopher Hamner, archive originator Ted Crackel, and the director of the <a href="http://scripto.org/" title="Scripto">Scripto</a> community transcription tool project Sharon Leon.</p>
