Department of War
December 2. 1794
Sir,
You will have the boats in readiness for the detachment which Lieutenant Colonel Butler is directed to sent down the Ohio.
I shall expect to hear from you on the Subject of the Commissary's post by the Return of the post on the 11 instant.
I am
Sir
with esteem
Your humble Servant
H. Knox
Secy of War
Major Isaac Craig
