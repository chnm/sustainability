 June 13, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Henry mc Causlands Son. XX.

Clear & warm. I was at mr mc Causlands, his wifes illness increast about 4h morn. her women were Calld and Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son at 6th h. I relurnd home at noon. recevd 6/ as a reward. I went to See mr Parkers [inft].   at Ditoes. Birth 29th.  
 

 

 June 20, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Greelys Dagt. XX.

raind finely this morn. I was Calld at 4h morn to See mrs Greely. Shee was Safe Delivd at 8 of a fine Dagt, her Eighth Child. I tarried till 8h Evng. Left her & infant very Cleverly. receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Greelys. Birth 30th.  
 

 

 June 23, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinklys Son. XX.

Clear foren. Sally Densmore Brot their infant to have a burn drest. I was Calld to See James Hinkleys ys 3d wife at 12 O Clock. Shee was Delivd at 11h Evn of a Son, her first Born. I tarried all night, my Patients are Cleverly.   at James Hinklys. Birth 31st 
 

 

 June 27, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thos Stikneys Son. XX.

Clear & warm. I was Calld to Thomas Stickneys wife in Labour at 8h morn. Shee was Safe Delivd at 10 of a Son, her first Born. I receivd 6/ as reward, left her Cleverly at 3 pm & returnd. Cyrus Sleeps here. Borrowed 13-3/4 lb veal of mr Densmr.   at Thos Stickneys. Birth 32nt. 
 

 

 July 3, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Neys Son. XX.

Clear & very warm. I was Calld at 8h morn to See Capt Neys Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 of a very fine Son. mrs Cox, Cleark, Hinkly, Pilsbiry & Sherburn were assistants. we tarried till near Sun Sett when we left my Patients Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is returnd. I went to See mrs Densmore, find her Easier.   at Capt Neys & mr Densmrs. Birth 33d. mrs Neys 9th Child.  
 

 

 July 16, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Coutches 3d Son. XX.

Cloudy morn, raind aftern. I was Calld at Sun rise to See John Coutches wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd of a fine Son at 7h 30m morn. I receivd 6/ as a reward & left my Patients very Comfortable, & returnd home at 10. mr Pollard, Pitts, Son Jona, their wives, mrs Burton & Cyrus Dind here. we had a fine Loin of Veal & String Beens. mr Ballard was Calld to mr Kennadies at Evn.   at John Coutches. Birth 34th. my Childn here. Jonas wife & Childn tarry all night.  
 

 

 August 2, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Black hitties Son. XX.

Clear. I put my Patient to Bed with a Son at 1h, morn & returnd home about Sunrise; took a nap. mrs Livermr Sent for me to help her Dress a blister on Pattys neck. mrs Benjamin here to have a goun made. mrs Porter, Pitts & Dagt Pollard here. mr Ballard been at the hook part of this Day.   at Grifins & mr Livermors. Birth 35th. receivd 6/ may 17th 1795.

X. Birth Jery Dumers Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 9h morn to See mr Jere Dumers Lady who was in Labour, Shee was Deld at 5h Evng of a fine Son, their first Born. I tarried & Nurst my Patients thro the night; they are Comfortable.   at mr Dumers. Birth 36th. 
 

 

 September 6, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth MC Neels Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 6h morn to See mrs mc Neele who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 12 of a Son which was inspird with life but Expird in a Short time after the Birth. I left her as well as Could be Expected at 6h pm & Came home. Dolly & Sally Came home from Son Pollards.   at mr mc Neeles. Birth 37th. Dagt Ballard & her [Childn] Came ys Evng.  
 

 

 September 9, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. John Lashnees Son. XX.

Clear till near night when it raind. mrs Lashnees was Safe Deld at 5h morn of a fine Son which wd 11_ lb. I tarried with her till 6h pm, left her Cleverly as Could be Expected. I receivd [7/4d] for my Service. Calld to See mrs Dummer, find her Cleverly. mr Ballard Sett out on his Tour of Surveying. Ephm went with him to take the hors home.   at mr Lashnees. Birth 38th. mr Balld left home to Survey 4 Townships.  
 

 

 September 11, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A very rainy aftern. Ephm returnd about 1h pm, Son Towns famely well. Dolly went to work at mr Blaks. I have been at home, Sally Spining. Son Cyrus is 37 years Old this Day.   at home. Cyruss Birth Day 37 years.  
 

 

 September 15, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Slepn Hinklys Son by Judy Buzell, out of Wedlock. XX.

Clear. I was Still Labouring under the triall of ye Collick. mrs Hinkley took great Care of me, I was Easier towards night when my Patients illness Came on more regular, and Shee was Safe Deld at 5h pm of a fine Son, which Shee Declard Stephen Hinkly is thee true father off. Shee is as well as can be Expected. I am Exceeding feeble thro the night.   at Dittoes. Birth 39th. I receivd 6/. there is 2/ Due for hors & medisin.  
 

 

 October 3, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Towns Son & 9th Child.

Clear. mrs Child here till aftern. Son Town Came for me at 2h pm. I went forward as Soon as posable. Crost the ferry at 3, arivd at Son Towns at 7. Shee my Dagt was Safe Deld of a Son at 7h 30m. mrs Stackpl, Stratn & mrs [sic].   at Son Towns. Birth 40th, of which 27 are Sons.

X. Birth mr Dows 3d Dagt. XX.

Cloudy morn, Clear aftn. I was Calld to See mrs Dow who is in Labour at 8h morn. Shee was Delivd of a fine Dagt at 11. I left her Cleverly at 6h Evn, receivd 6/ as a reward. Sally Densmore Sleeps here. Jacky Ballard here.   at mr Dows. Birth 41st. 
 

 

 October 17, 1793, Thursday

X. Birth [Alphs] XX Lyons Dagt XX.

 October 28, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Timy Pages Son. XX.

Clear. I returnd from mr Shaws, left them Cleverly. find Agsts much as he was. Dolly is gone to Son Pollards. mr Bullin here ys Day. I was Calld aftern to mr Timy Pages wife who was Safe Deld at 7h Evn. I tarried all night, her Child was a Son which wd 11lb 1/4.   at Shaws & Timy Pages.  November 7, 1793, Thursday

X.[Birth Reads Dagt]. XX.

Clear & very pleast. I was Calld at five O Clok morn to See mrs Read who was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 9. I returnd home at noone, left my Patients Cleverly; receivd 6/ as a reward. Son Jona, his wife & Childn all Supt with us. Pally & Jack Sleep here. mr Thomas waited on Dolly to mr Pollards.   at mr Reeds. Birth 46th.  
 

 

 November 17, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Poores Dagt. XX.

rainy. I was Calld from mr Parkers at 2h morn to mr Poores. Doct Page was Calld before my arival. I Extracted the Child, a Dagt. he Chose to Close the Loin. I returnd home at 8 am, receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard & Ephm attend worship, Dolly & Sally aftern. Charls & John Coks Supt here. I was Calld to Capt meloys at 11h Evn. raind.   at Ditoes & mr Poores. Birth 47 th. A Daughtr at Capt Meloys allso.  
 

 

 November 18, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Meloys Dagt. XX.

at Capt Meloys, his Lady in Labour, her women Calld. (it was a Severe Storm of rain. Cleard of with Snow). my patient Deld at 8h 5m Evn of a fine Dagt, her attendants, mrss Cleark & Duttun, Sewall & my Self. we had an Elligant Supper and I tarried all night.   at Capt Meloys. Birth 48th. I receivd 24/.

at Whites, his wife was Deld at 12 O Clok of a Dagt, and I was Calld back to mr Parkers. his Lady was Dild at 9h 30m of a Dagt. I am Some fatagud. Son Town here.   at Whites & Parkers. Birth 50. Birth 51.  
 

 

 December 17, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Hamlins Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld at 6h morn to See mrss Hamlin, Shee was Deld of a Dagt at 7h & 20m. I Spent the most of ye Day with her. I went to See mrs Dickmans Child, find it very Sick; the Lady had a fitt. mrs Pollard was there. I left mrs Hamlin & infant very Comfortable & returnd home at Evn. mr Livermors Laid a floor in our kitchen.   at mr Hamlins. Birth 52nt. receivd 12/ Feby 5th 1794 of mr Hamln.

I tarried all night, mr [sic] patient as well as Could be Expected; the Negro Doctres there.   at Ditoes & Badcoks. Birth 53d. mrs Childs had Doct Cony with her this night.
