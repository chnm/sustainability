  also be brought over if Hodgdon wants. Smith will ask some  his neighbors to board them which they will do for a small reward. He has asked about public strays in the town and if he receives any will
  inform Hodgdon. He needs ropes for a few halters which should be sent at the first opportunity. The envelope leaf includes a list of soldiers, possibly related to the horses.
