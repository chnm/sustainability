  of standing in rank; evidently Barney thinks Captain Talbot is junior to him. Knox explains that Talbot was appointed a Navy Captain from Colonel in 1779, whereas it appears Barney continued as a Lieutenant
  through the end of the war. Knox asks that Barney inform as to whether he will accept or reject his appointment, as soon as it is convenient.
