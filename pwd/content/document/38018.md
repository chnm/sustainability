Discharged September 1778 Nothing Due
J Howell
City Philadelphiae P
Before me Plunket Fleeson one of the
Judges in the Court of Common Pleas for the
County Philadae came Thomas Egan the
Grantor in the within Power of Attorney named
&acknowledged the same to be his act & deed
& desired it being Recorded as such —
Given under my hand & Seal
the 22d July 1786 —
Plun:t Fleeson
