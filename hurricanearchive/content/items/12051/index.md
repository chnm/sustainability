[Comments include \"bad\", \"horrible\", \"a mess\"] All right so it seems like the city still lookin a mess. Now what perspective is ya\'ll using? You using the perspective prior to the storm, or right after the storm, and then you seen it today? You know, what is your base of your comparison?
