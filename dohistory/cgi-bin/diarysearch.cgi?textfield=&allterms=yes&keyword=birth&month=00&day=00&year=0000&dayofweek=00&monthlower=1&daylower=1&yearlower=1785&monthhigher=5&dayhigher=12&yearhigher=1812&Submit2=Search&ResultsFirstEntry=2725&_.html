 September 19, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I had Some rest last night, prais to the Great author. Fanny left here after breakfast, Dagt P. also. I feel very feeble, God grant me patience. Dagt Pollard left me before noon. Hannah Bakt. mrs Bennet Calld here. H. and Mta B. here.   at home, unwel. Dagt Pollard went home. mr Bald gatherd Beens yt were ripe. [20] 6 Clear. I have felt more Comforlable but very weak. M. B. Sleeps here. Fanny at Jonas. these lines are for Yesterday. at home.  
 

 

 September 24, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Farewel Calld to See me. I have done my hous work but feel very feeble. the Boys gathered the Apples in ye west orchard, brot 3 bags full in here. raind at night.   at home.  
 

 

 September 27, 1811, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Hannah Spent part of the day here, mrs Sands Came & washt my Milk vessals, Sleeps here. I had a poore nights rest. Son Jona here at Evng.   at home, feel very feeble. Son Jona Calld to see me.  
 

 

 October 4, 1811, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Cool. Hannah Scolt Pickles & part did my washing. mrs Stone here, brot 12 Cents fm Mrss Carter for Plumbs. mrs Burlun Sent me a present of Tamrinds, Tea and 4 lb Beef. Jona Calld to See me.   at home, felt some Better thro Gods mercie. Son Lambard Sleeps here.  
 

 

 October 11, 1811, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy the most of the day. I feel feeble. Barny Lambd Calld here, gave me a pair. Hannah P. Did some washing.   at home. Barny L. here.  
 

 

 November 3, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Barny Lambard dind here. he & wilm B. got a twich of wood. Dagt Ballard, Marta, James & Elisa here aftern. Epm Came down, Calld in here.   at home. Dagt B.s Childn here.  
 

 

 November 12, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have washt & done Some mending. Abm Field Calld in to See us.   at home.  
 

 

 November 16, 1811, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, raind at night. Son Ephm Calld here at Evng, gave us a quarter Lamb which wd 10 lb. he informd us he was told that Ezra Town was killd by the fall of a Tree last thursday. if So, may God grant us all to take a Seutable notis of his Dealings with us, may he be A Husband to the weddo & Father to ye Childn.   at home.  
 

 

 November 19, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. mr Ballard to the Settlement, got me 2 oz Snuff. I have washt &C. Isaac Farewel Calld at Evng for apples.   at home.  
 

 

 November 20, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Cold. I have ironed. A mrs Donison & another young woman Calld.   at home.  
 

 

 November 25, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Day, Cloudy Evng. I have washt. Le faett Butchered our Spotted pigg. Epm Calld in here, he Came fm Clinton Satter day, all well there. Abner & Wilm Sleep here. Son Jona Came with a raft.   at home, washt.  
 

 

 November 27, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snow & rain. I have been fixing to remove to Son Jonas hous, he Calld here ys Morn, is gone to Jail this aftern. Wm Sleeps here.   at home. Son Jona is gone to Jail for debt.  
 

 

 November 28, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at Evng. Son Epm Calld here, his famely are well. I have kild 5 fowls & done other matters. Le faett did not Come after [ ur go ].   at [home].  
 

 

 December 2, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Shaws first Son, Second Child.

Clear & very Pleasant. I was Calld by Nicholas Shaw at 1h AM to go & See his wife who was in labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 11h and I returnd before night. knit at Evn, I Cleand my piggs head & feet allso.   at [Nicholas] Shaws. Birth 11th.  
 

 

 December 3, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrs Partridges breast, it is very much Sweld but not broke yet. Son Town Came here at Evng, his famely are well, his widdow Dagts allso. he informd that Dagt Lambard had a fainting fitt last night. mrs Burton Sent me 10 oz of Sugar & 2 of Tea by mrs Stone.   at [mr Partridges. Son Town Sleeps here.]  
 

 

 December 4, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld by mr Livermore to See his wife about midnight, reach there at 1h am, find her very unwel. Shee got Esier & [Slept Some].   at [mr Livermores].  
 

 

 December 6, 1811, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 raind part of the day. I returnd at 12 O Clock, left ye patient about hous. I washt & ironed after I Came home.   at [Ditoes].  
 

 

 December 9, 1811, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr [Joseph] Firbushes Son. Birth Wm Spragues 2nd Dgt, 3d Child.

Clear. mr Firbush Calld me at 3h am to go to Capt Smiths to See his wife who w was Deliverd of a Son, her first Born at 5h am. I was Calld from her at 7 by Wilm Sprague to See his wife who was Deld before 12 of a Dagt, her 3 Child. they are all Comfortable. I reacht home at Sun Sett feel some Fatagued. I receivd 2/1 lb Tea of Sprague in part. March 31st recd 1 Bushl Corn at 4/6.   at mr Firbushes. birth 12th. at wm Spragus. birth 13th. Death B. Bodwel.  
 

 

 December 11, 1811, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleasant. I have done hous wk & knit Some. the remains of Betsy Bodwel were interd this aftern. Hannah Ballard attended at the hous. mr Goodhue performd. Betsy Wiman Calld & took Tea with us. Barney Lambd brot me 2 aprons which his Marm Sent me, al wel there.   at home. Funeral of Betey Bodwel.  
 

 

 December 26, 1811, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Epm Calld here. Hanh Pollard with him, going to help his wife who is unwel. mr Ballard Sent 3s by Epm, he got 4/1 Tea, 2-4/1 lb Sugar.   at home.  
 

 

 December 29, 1811, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowed & was Cold & windy. P. T. Voce & inft, Ben Williams Calld. A mr Eddy Sleeps here. I have been at home. wether moderated at 10h Evn.   at home.  
 

 

 December 31, 1811, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant day, Cloudy at Evng. I have mended some Cloaths and wound 4 Skeins hoes yarn. Som Ephm and his wife took Tea with us. Shee looks feeble, their famely are wel. mr Bracket Calld here.   at home. Son Ephm & wife here.  
 

 

 January 4, 1812, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd, Cleard a way at Evng. I was Calld to See mr Firbeshes infant, it has a very bad Cold and is Costive. we administred a Clistar which gave relief. I returnd home about Sun Set. I washt my husband A Shirt after.   at mr Firbishes.  
 

 

 January 17, 1812, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Funeral of Old mrss Shaw. Betsy Wiman Calld here. Revd mr Tappin here, gave mr Ballard 8 Dolars of the money Contributed for the relief of the poore. I was Calld by mr Livermore to go and See his wife who was in Labour, her women were Calld at 9h Evn. old mrss Hamlin, Nichols and 2 mrss Heartfords.   at home till Evng. Revd mr Tappin here. Funeral Old mrss Shaw. I went to mr Livermores at Evng.  
 

 

 January 18, 1812, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Livermores Dagt.

Clear and Cold. mrs Livermore Deld at 10h am of a Dagt after a very Dangerous Labour. I returnd home at 2h pm very much over Come with the Cold. found Dagt Ballard here. mr Field Conducted his Bride home to hous keeping.   at mr Livermores. Birth 1 st. Funeral mrs Baxter.
