From Saml. Hodgdon dtd. 
Decem 27.1800
order for 12 Groce Yellow
Coat Buttons.
Israel Whelen Esquire 
Purveyor
Pres
Intendant of Stores Office
Philadelphia 27th Dec: 1800
Sir, 
Be pleased to purchase and have
deposited in the public store in this city,
Twelve gross of yellow coat Buttons, for
artillery clothing; the same being wanted
to enable the Storekeeper to comply with
sundry orders already issued.
I am, Sir,
Your most obt. Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
Israel Whelen Esq. 
Purveyor
