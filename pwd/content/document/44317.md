Phiadelphia 5th March
Dear Sir
Conrads cash running low, I have paid his Bill of expenses here amounting to 12. dollars and 27 cents – and have also advances him seven dollars and seventy three Cents, to enable him to re- turn — his receipt you have enclosed and the amount is charged to your account, you will settle with him his expenses for returning and cre- dit my account with the advances and all will stand right. —
I am Sir Your Most Obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
Major Isaac Craig
