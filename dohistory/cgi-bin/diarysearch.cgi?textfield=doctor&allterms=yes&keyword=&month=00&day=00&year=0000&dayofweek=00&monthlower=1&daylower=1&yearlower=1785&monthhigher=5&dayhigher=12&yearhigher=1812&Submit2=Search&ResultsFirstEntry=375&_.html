 February 11, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See my Dagt Pollard who is Confined with a Soar Breast. Doct Howard was there, saw it and recommended a wheat breud poltis.   at son Pollards & Lambds.  
 

 

 February 17, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 at Son Pollards. Doct Howard Calld, thought the Breast not fitt to open. my Dagt had an ill night.   at Son Pollards.  
 

 

 February 18, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Pollard Calld Doct Colman to open his wives breast, it had a Copeous discharg and Shee Seems more comfortable. the infant is Cleverly. mrss Black came up with her Brother.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 25, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. at Ditoes. my Dagt very ill, ye Doct sent for.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 26, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. Doct Colman Came and opened my Dagts Breast, Shee is Easier. the Doct Conducted me home. I then went to Son Jonas, his infant very sick. we gave it medisin which gave relief. my son returnd from winslow and I Came home late at Evn. son Towns famely are well. Nabby hail marrid ys Evng.   at Ditoes. my Dagts Breast opend. was at Jonas allso  
 

 

 March 6, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, Betsy to her Grand fathers. Doct Barton Supt, Bror Ebenz here.   at home.  
 

 

 March 22, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Showers. mr Ballard went to meeting. Doct Cony went past here, the traviling bad.   at home.  
 

 

 March 24, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Some Cooler. I returnd from mr Reeds. Doct Cony went there, informs me the Patients are no wors. Dagt Ballard and Childn here aftern.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 16, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not So Cold. mr Ballard gone to Varsalboro to Survey land for Doct Colman. I have workt very hard in my Gardin and feel very much fatagud. Cyrus has helpt me. I Sett out Goosbery Bushes and Currents. Dagt Ballard and Hitty have been to visit mrss Saunders.   at home, workt in my Gardin all day.  
 

 

 April 24, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Dexters Dagt.

 Clear. I have been very unwell but went to See Son Jonas infant, it is unwell with a Cold. I was Calld at Evn to mr Dexters wife, Shee was Deld of a Dagt by Doct Cony before I arivd. I went to mr Shubal Pittss and Slept with Sister Barton.   at my Sons, mr Dexters & Pittss. 11th Case.  
 

 

 May 18, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard Sett out at 11h morn to go and assist Wm Brooks & Epm Town aprise and [Divide] 2000 acrs of tand which lies between this [and] penobscott river to 7 heirs of the late Doct Geneson. Bror & Sister Barton and yr Son Luke left here after Dinner to go to hous keeping at mr Crages Shop. I wish them prosperity. Cyrus and Ephm have been makeing fence. I have been to Son Jonas with my Sister as Shee wint down.   at Son Jonas. Doct Barton, his wife & Luke went from here to hous keeping.  
 

 

 June 13, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, wed 2 Beds Beats and helpt some about hous. Savage Bolton, his wife & youngest Child here. Doct Barton Came ys aftern and tarries ys night.   at home.  
 

 

 July 11, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I houghd in ye Gardn and Shelled peas till 11h morn when I was Sudendly alarmd with news that my Dagt Lambd, in attempting to Come and visit me, was thron from her hors with her dear infant, and much hurt. I imediately Sett out to go and See them, was overtaken by Doct Cony who was Calld to their relief. I found, on my arival, that my Dagt was not So much hurt as was feared; the infts head was very much Sweld. thee Doct Examened it and Bathd it with Camphr. he allso lett Blood and it revivd so that I left them and returnd home near night. find Doct Barton, Gill, Sons Pollard, Jona and their wivis and Childn, who with Son Lambard and Dolly Kymbal, took Tea with us. Blesed by the Great Author of Mercys that the lives of my Childn were Spared.   at Son Lambds. A mercyfull preservation took place when in Emenent Danger.

 Clear. I have done my hous work and Some in my gardin. Dagt Ballard, mrs Trask & Suky Peas & Doct Barton took Tea with us, Ephm & Wm allso.   At home, had Compy.

 Clear Except a Shower at Evn. My Sister and her Childn went from here after Breakfast. the Doct workt here all day, went home at Evn. I have been at home, feel very unfitt to do my work.   at home.

 August 18, 1801, Tuesday

 Clear and very warm, raind at Evng. I have been at home, very unwell. Dagt Ballard Came and got me Some Breakfast & helpt me get Supper. Doct Barton workt here. Son Town Sleeps here, his famely [well].   at home. Son Town Sleeps here.  
 

 

 August 28, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Waid & Pollard here part of ye foren. Capt Stones wife, Dagt Ballard & mrs Saunders here, Doct Barton allso. I have Sufered much this day with my infirmety.   at home. mrs Stone Visited me.  
 

 

 September 14, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am very weak and low but have gathered in part of my Beans. I had a night Swet last night. [I] expect I am fast on a decline.   at home. Doct Barton here.  
 

 

 October 7, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home very unwell. mr Ballard been to the Settlement below. Sally Cypher Came to work here. Jonas wife and her Sister been to mr Motts. Doct Barton here, informs me that my Brother Elijah is Sick with a Cancer on his Sholder.   at home.
