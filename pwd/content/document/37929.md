Comptrolr. Genls. Office

June 13th. 1786.

Dear Sir,

There is a Certificate produced by a friend of mine which he wishes me to endorse of of Penna. it is in the name of John Gray dated Decr. 2d. 1783 between one & two hundred dollars (I think) Quere does he live in this State? did he live therein? what Corps was he in? If you could inform me you would much oblige

Yours &c
Jno. Nicholson

Jos. Howell Esqr
