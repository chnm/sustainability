A W N. E annexe to former Perth Ambroy 2 april 1800
A B C annexe to A W
John Alsted
inclosing 2 papers respecting him as a refugee and one paper respecting John Dyer Morcier as a refugee
fur
The Honble The Secretary of War
Philadelphia
redd 4 April 1800
Perth Amboy April 2nd1800
Sir
I have the honour to enclose the Depositions of Col. Aaron Burr and Matthias Halsted respecting my residence in Canada Qc - And also at the request of Mr John Dyer Mercier my Deposition respecting his residence in Canada Qc. 
I some days since transmitted you an estimate of my hopes & as a refugee from the British Province of to be informed of the receipt of them and the enclosed papers will afford great satisfaction.
I have the honour to be
very respectfully, Sir,
your most obedient servant
John Halsted
The Hon.ble Secretary of War
