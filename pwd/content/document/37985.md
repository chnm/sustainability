June 26 1786
Thomas Tolman Esqr.
Copied
Sir

It is scarcely necessary to enter into an examination
of the vouchers sent me by Colo Safford -as it will be
double the trouble, and not [answer you a valuable]
purpose - the Col. has not therefore lodged your papers
the whole of which I hope you will be able 
to deliver in the course of a short time.
I am Sir
Your Obt Servt.
Office of Army Accounts
New York June 26 1786.

Thos Tolman Esquire
