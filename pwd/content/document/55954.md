David Stodder
Navy Yard Baltimore December 22th 1796
Sir
At your Request of the 19th Inst
I have Sent the Draght you Mentioned
to the war Office if their is to be any
thing further laid down for my government
in the inclosed draught it will be
necessary for it to be Imediately done
and the draught Returned
Am Sir
With Respect
Yor Most Obt. Servt.
David Stodder

Navy Yard Baltimore Decemb 22d 1796
Sir
At your Request of the 9th instant. I have sent the
Draught you mentioned to the War Office. If there is to be any
further laid down for my government in the inclosed Draft
it will be necessary for it to be immediately done and the Dft
Retured.
I am Sir With Respect your most obt. Sert.
David Stodder
