West Point 2d June 1786

Sir

Last night some of Lieut Willcoxes Men deserted from this place, I have sent a Party to Connecticut after them, and in Case they do not take them to inform Mr Willcox concerning it. 

There is now but one sergt and seven men for duty at this place when the party after the deserters return.

I am

The Honorable Major General Knox
Secretary at War
