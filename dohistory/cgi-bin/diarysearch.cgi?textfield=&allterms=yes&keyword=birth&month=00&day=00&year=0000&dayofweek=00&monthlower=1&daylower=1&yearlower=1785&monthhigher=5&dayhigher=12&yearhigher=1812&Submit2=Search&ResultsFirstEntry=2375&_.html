 November 29, 1805, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. we left Son Lambds after Breakfast. my husband & I went to Son Ephms, his wife is troubled with her Breast.   at Ditoes & at Son Ephms.  
 

 

 December 7, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 raind till aftern. I have Bakt Bread and mins Pies and Done some mending and finisht Doubling my Grey yarn. mr Ballard workt for Jona till near night, then went to Son Ephms. finds his wife is poorly with her Breast, it is not Broak yet.   at home.  
 

 

 December 12, 1805, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, part of the day more moderate. mr Ballard & Cyrus Down to Coart. I have washt Some more Cloaths ys day. Sist Farewel Calld here, informs me yt Pollys Breast is very Soar yet.   at home. mrs Farewel here.  
 

 

 December 21, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, Done my hous work and Some mending. mr Ballard and Cyrus been to be Shaved. this is ye aniversary of ye birth [Th] Pollard.   [         ]  
 

 

 December 31, 1805, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Done a Larg wash for me Sinc 12= O Clock and washt my Kitchen. thus I end this year. it is of Gods mercy that I am yet alive and able to perform any business. Joanna Babcock Calld in at Evng to See me. mr Hartwel began a School this day at the hous where he resides, where Son Jon a formerly livd.   at home, washt.  
 

 

 January 1, 1806, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, did Some washing. Joanna Babcock Calld in this Evng. it was last Evng Shee was here.   at home.  
 

 

 February 12, 1806, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Luke B. Conducted Son Ephms wife here foren. my Son Came at Evn, Supt with us and Sett out for home before 9. I have been at home. the Babe Cryed and they Calld at Jonas. Snowd at Evn.   at home. Son Ephm and wife here.  
 

 

 February 17, 1806, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. mr Brown & wife left here at 10h ys morn to go to Farmington. mrs Heartwel made me A Visit, informd me yt Jason Pierces wife was [Deld] of a Son last night or ys morn. I have finisht my Cottne hoes this Evng.   at home. mr & mrs Brown left here. mrs Heartw l here.  
 

 

 February 20, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear aftern, rain at Evn. Allin & Ephm Sleep here. I have been to see mrs Hartwl who has a Soar Breast. mr Ballard down to ye Settlement. I have ys day Seen 71 years. few & [toil] have ye years of thine hand maid been.   at mr Heartw [his ] unwell.  
 

 

 February 21, 1806, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to Son Jonas to See his wife and Babe who are unwel. they are more Comfortable than they were yesterday. mrs Burington Came there before I Came home. Rhoda Pollard and Sally Ballard Came home with me. Allin & Ephm Sleep here.   at Son Jonas, his wife and Babe are unwel.  
 

 

 February 22, 1806, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Rhoda washt some Cloaths for me. I have Bakt Brown & flower Bread. Son Jonas wife Sent me 4_ lb Pork. mrs Cypher Calld here, had my flax Comb. mr Ballard been down to be shaved and to Son Lambds, brot Allins Shirt. Sally went home, Shee was very Sick here last night.   at home. hear [mrs] Gillard has a Son Born this or yesterdy. 
 

 

 March 3, 1806, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I have done Some washing. mr Smith hauld us 2 loads wood. Lefaet 2, Ephm on a hors Slead. Wm Cypher Chopt in the woods. I washt my kitchen after my husband went to Bed. he Calld to See Capt Stone ys aftern, he is Sick.   at home. we had wood hauld. I have washt Some.  
 

 

 March 4, 1806, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day. mr Ballard went to Saml Cumings mill, bot _ bsl rie & 1 qt milk; had _ Busl indien ground. he Calld at Capt Stones, finds him no better. mrs Andrews Sent for herbs. Bracket is Sick.   at home.  
 

 

 March 6, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I have washt Some and done other work, brot 7 pails water. feel not So wel at Evng as I could wish. we hear that mr Wiman & Son Jona were Calld to Capt Stones ys morn about day, it was thought he was Expireing. hear aftern he was alive.   at home, washt Some. mrs Heartwel St me [1 lb Buller].  
 

 

 March 17, 1806, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard to Hallowell. Son Jona & wife Calld on their return from Son Lambards. brot us 45 hering, 1 lb Chees, Do [Butr] and Some oinions, a present from Son Lambd.   at home. Jona & wife Calld here.  
 

 

 April 3, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not So Cold. mr Ballard to meeting, it Fast day. Cyrus Came home and went to meeting. Sons Jona & Lambd Came here at Evng. the Latter Sleeps here, informs that his Famely are wel Except Barny who has had the misfortune to hurt one of his Eyes by a fall.   at home. Son Lambd Sleeps here. mrs Hartwl Calld in ys day.  
 

 

 April 13, 1806, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to meeting. Revd mr Stone Deliverd a Discoarsl forenn on ye Death of mr Childss youngest Child from 2nd Samuel 12 C 23 v. I have been at home. mr Heartwel here, Says his wife is better.   at home.  
 

 

 April 26, 1806, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear forn, Cloudy aftn. I have been So well as to Bake bn and flower bread, Churnd 2 pounds Butter and bt a Cheese, but with great defuculty. Betsy Andrus Came in aftern, Shee kindly assisted me while Shee was here or I Could not have got thro my work. I had 2 lb Butter. Son Jonas youngest Child is Sick. my Dagt has not been to See me. Le faett & Martha here to day.   at home, feel much better thro Gods Goodness. Son Ephm here. Funeral James Savages wife.  
 

 

 May 9, 1806, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Chases Daugt. XX.

rainy part of ye day. I removd a plumb tree from my gardin and Sett it in ye Corn field. Sett Curent Shoots and did Some washing. was Calld at 4h 30m pm to go and See mrs Chace who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld about 6 of a Daugt. I left her and infant Cleverly and reacht home at 8h 30m Evng. was Conducted there and home by Capt Purington.   at mr Chaces. Birth 1st for this year. Decr 17 nth receivd 9/ by Chaces work.  
 

 

 May 21, 1806, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Churnd 3_ lb Butter, Bakt and done other matters. was Calld by Isaac Beals at 5h pm to go and See his wife who was in Labour, tarrie there all night.   at mr Isaac Beals. 26th recd . 
 

 

 May 22, 1806, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Bealss 3d Son & 5th Child. XXX.

Clear, a frost ys morn. the Patient was Safe Deld at 1h ys morn of A Son, her 5th Child and 3d Son. Shee is Cleverly, ye Babe was atact with distrese about 2h after the Birth. we thought it was Dead but it revivd. Mr Beals went to Rhed field and brot a Nurs. I tarried till he returnd and Came home on hors back, had my hous to Sett in order. I have not Slept Sence I left home.   at mr Bealss. Birth 2nt. receivd 6/ in part 26th. recd 3/ by my husband which he receivd of mr Beals.  
 

 

 June 3, 1806, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have workt in my Gardin, done my hous work & Churnd 2_ lb Butter. Old Lady Stone Calld to See me, Shee is unwel. Son Jona & wife here forenn. Soal & Cypher washt their wool 20 fleases. mr Ballard and Cyrus been down to Coart.   at home. mrs Stone here. the remains of mrs Pickford interd ys day.  
 

 

 June 17, 1806, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. mrs Gillbd Came & Calld me to go and See mrs Heartwel who has a pain in her Stomach. I walkt there and home again three times, feel fatagued. Shee Seemd Easier when I was last there. mrs Wiman & Son Jonas wife there. I cookt Pork and beens for Dinner. mr Ballard been houghing Corn and giting Some wood.   at mr Heartwels, his wife is unwel.  
 

 

 June 30, 1806, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warmer than it hase been lately. mrs Wiman here for greens, Hannah Ballard allso. mrs Gilbard paid me for what Soap Shee has had of me. mr Ballard and I went to attend ye funeral of master Saml Titcomb who was Drouned on Saterday last. the wife of Esqr Runels Expired on Satterday night last. her remains were interd this Day. may the frequent instances of mortallity Excite us to be allso ready for our last Change. we were at Son Ephms, all well there. A mr Soal Presided at ye funeral, he Delivered a Descoars adapted to the ocation from Lemantations 3d Chap 39 vers, ye words, Wherefore Doth a living Man Complain &C.   at the funeral of the oldest Son of Saml Titcoms s Esqr. funeral of Old Lady Runels ys Day.  
 

 

 July 1, 1806, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and misty. I have Done my hous work, Sett Some Plants. mr Ballard & I attended meeting aflern. there were an association of Minesters, Revd mr Winship, mr Parker and mr Jenks from Bath. he Delivered a Discoarse from 2nd Timy 1st Ch 7th vrs. I was informd that Jon a How was Drowned last Satterday, his remains not found yet.   at meeting & at mr Thwings, he is very unwell. the mrs Edess were there.
