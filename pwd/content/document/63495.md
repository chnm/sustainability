Samuel Hodgson, Esquire
Philadelphia
[upside down]
John Gorham Coffin
11th July 1798
Rec'd 27 —
Fort Niagara, 11 July 1798
Sir,
I am requested by Major Ricardo to advise you to forward to this place a certified copy of the last Return of Quarter Master's Stores & which Mr. McClellan sent you from this Fort. The return he left here is without Signature and is also incomplete—it follows that it is not in my power to make an accurate Return from the documents in my possession, for I expect to leave this place soon. I hope you will think proper to comply with my request by the next post, as I am desirous of leaving the business of the Quarter Master in a regular manner.
I am Sir respectfully
Your obedient servant
John G. Coffin
Tempy Qr. Master
J. Hodgson Esquire
