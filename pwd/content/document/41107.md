Ens Pryor's Account for doing (illegible) duty at Forts
Recd from Genrl Hamtramck and presented Genl Knox
The United States
To Ensign Abner Prior... Dr.
For his services as Adjutant & Quarter Master
To the garrison of Fort Knox Twenty three months com-
mencing 15th May 1788 and ending 15th April 17--.
(illegible due to fold) per month. 230
For Forage for one horse twenty three months 
commencing 15th May 1788 and ending 21st April
1790 inclusive and 6 dollars per month... 138
Dollars -- 368
Signed A Prior
Signed J F Hamtramck
maj comdt
Neither Genl Hamtramck or Genl. Knox will have anything to do with this account.---
Genl H. referr'ed it to Genl Knox
Jan 18 1791
