Sec at War   Quincy Aug 12th 1799
Sir, 
I will not object to the surgeons & mates selected by Col Price & Col Hunnewell for their regiments.  But I am not fully satisfied with the appointment of Dr. Blake.  He would do very well as a mate but his age does not entitle him I fear to the prominence, if his learning is sufficient.  I wish we had a system digested & Hospital surgeons appointed who might give us better information.
