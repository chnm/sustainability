The Wendsday that we got out of school for the upcomming hurricane my family left for Pensacola, Florida. My whole family left in our car with my mom, dad, sister, and brother it nomally takes us about 7 hours to get there but since the traffic was so bad it took us about 10. When we got there we stayed for about two weeks until it was finally O.K. for us to return. We also experienced winds and wind from Rita during the stay in Florida. I had fun in Florida with all of my friends who I had to leave behind when I moved, we went to the beach and to the movies and just hung out. My mom was going to enroll me into the local high school but decided against it because i would only attend it for a short time and it would be way to much trouble for just to be there for two weeks at the most. When we returned home the only thing was wrong with our house was we had a bunch of trees down in the back and front yard. We had to clean out the freezer with was really gross I wanted to die. I really think that cleaning that thing out was the wrost part of it all.
