August Term at Boston 1790
It appearing to the Justices of the Supreme Judicial Court that the Costs against Joseph North Esqr. who was Indicted for a Trespass -- Tried & Acquitted amount to the sum of £ 18•0•4 agreeable to the Bill taxed above within. Ordered by the Court that the Sheriff of ye County of Lincoln pay said sum out of the fines he may have in his hands unto the persons to whom they are due according to Law in such case provided.

Att. Chas. Cushing Clerk
