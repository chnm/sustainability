Office of Accounts
New York October 26 1786
Sir
Agreeably to your request of the 22nd inst, I have enclosed you a copy of the power of 
Attorney given by James Spencer late of Hazens Regiment to Richard Musthwaite.
It appears by the returns of this office that the said Spencer was discharged as a private soldier.
Yours  J Pierce
W Thomas Sheilds
Goldsmith Philad
