This is to certify that it appears from the Returns of Invalid Pensioners residing in the State of new hampshire that James Reed late a Brigadier General in the Service of the United States is entitled to arrea-ges of pensions previous to the fourth of March one-thousand seven hundred and eighty nine amortizing to two thousand two hundred forty three dollars and eighty nine cents
Given to the War office
this twenty third day 
of May one thousand seven
hundred & ninety one
Knox
Secy of War

2243 Dollars 88 cents

Registered
RJ Vandenbrockch.
