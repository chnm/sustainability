Department of War
Accountants Office
January 29th 1796
Richard Harrison Esqr
Auditor of the Treasury
Sir
I herewith enclose to you the additional Vouchers received from George Fleming for the purchase of Fire Wood for the Corps of Artillerists & Engineers at West Point, which I conceive are proper to be settled at your Office
I am Sir &c
William Simmons
