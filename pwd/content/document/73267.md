January 2nd 1799
I Certify that there is due to John Nicholson Senr. under an appointment made by the Secretary of War, the sum
of Two hundred & ten dollars being for his services Inspecting
& proving sundry Public military Stores from 1 October to the
31 December 1799~
W.S.
Dollars 210.
The Secretary of War
