I decided to stay in my home durning the storm because if the storms would have been as bad as they could have been, I was there to help others in my community. I was fortunated though and the situation didn\'t get as bed as other parts of New Orleans and the other Gulf Coasts regions that were affected by the storms. I lucked out and was safe on the West Bank of New Orleans.
