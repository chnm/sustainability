Wilmington. N. C 24 Seper 1798
Griffith T. M. Ree
Received  October 17th 98
Answered same day

24 January 2014 (EST)
Sir                                          Wilmington NC Sep.t 24th  1798
With this you will receive Muster Rolls and Pay Rolls for the party artillerists and engineers at Fort Johnstone NC for the months of August July 1798.

        The Receipt Rolls for the months of April May & June wants the name of James Williams to be completed, 

this cannot at present be obtained, Williams being on duty at Fayetteville about ninety miles distant

        I am Sir with respect
          Your Most Obedient
            Humble Servant
                G. I. McRee

William Simmons, Esqr
Acctnt War Department
