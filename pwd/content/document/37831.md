New York 8th May 1786

Dear Sir,

Inclosed you will receive one Certificate No. 93,935, for 18264/90 Dols issued in your favor and one do No. 93,936, for 200 Dols. issued in favor of Lt. Percival Butler

Those Certificates were intended to have been forwarded agreeably to your request by Mr Erskine who call'd


in my absence and is now gone to Philadelphia  I have therefore requested Mr McCarthy to trouble himself with them and deliver the same to you  on receivg. them you will be kind enough to inform me by Letter or otherwise.  I request you to call on Mr Benstead; and and also desire him to come on with his accounts as soon as possible 
I am &c
J: Howell Jur.

Mr John Wigton
