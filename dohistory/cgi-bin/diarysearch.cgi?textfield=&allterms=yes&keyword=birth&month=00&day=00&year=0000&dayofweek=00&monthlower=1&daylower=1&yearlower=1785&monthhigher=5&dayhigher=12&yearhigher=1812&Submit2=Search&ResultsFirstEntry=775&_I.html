 February 8, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am very unwell. Hannah is gone to mr Densmores to have an over Coat Cutt out. I was Calld at 2h pm to See Savage Boltons inft, find it a very Destresst Child. I tarried all night, tho very unable. mr Cragg Calld me to See his Dagt who has the Cough & other Complaints. mrs Savage at Boltons.   at mr Boltons, watcht, mrs Brown allso.  
 

 

 February 9, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Snow. mr Bolton went to Docts & Bot Senan & Seeds; I gave the infant Some. they Calld mrs Savage there & I left the Child a little Easier & returnd home, was over Sett once. felt Exceedingly fatagud, went to bed before dark. was Calld out again at ye 11 h pm & went to Joseph Preskotts, his wife in Labour. mrs Hinkly & Preskot wrode part of the way with me.   at Ditoes & mr Preskots. receivd a letter from Sist Waters of Jany 20th 1792. Sent one to Doct Barton.  
 

 

 February 10, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Joseph Preskots Son. XX.

Clear & Cool. my Patient was very unwell thro the night but was Safe Deld at 6h morn of a Lusty Son. I left her & inft Cleverly at 11 morn & arivd at home at 2h pm; receivd 4lb Coffee for my trouble. Jona Came home at Evn, informs me yt Savage Boltons infant Expird at 5 ys morn.   at mr Preskots. Birth 11th. Death mr Boltn infant.  
 

 

 February 12, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Randals Son. XX.

Cloudy morn. I was Calld between midnight & 1h m to See mrs Randal, who was Safe Deld at 1h of a fine Son. Shee Sufferd before my arival but the life of mother & Child were preservd. I tarried all night, receivd my fee in Pork, 18lb, & 4lb Lard for former attendance. I left the Lady & inft as well as Could be Expected. Calld to See mrs F Brown who I find Cleverly.   a mr Randals. Birth 12th.  
 

 

 February 15, 1792, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Waid workt here foren. mr Shubal Pitts, his Sister & Parthenia Barton Calld here, they were going to his Brothers at green lodg. I have been at home.   at home, Parthenia here.  
 

 

 February 17, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some Snow. I was Calld to See mr Densmores inft, it being unwell. mr Pitts, his Sistr Sela, mr Burtun & Lady here at Evn.   at mr Densmors to See his [inft].  
 

 

 February 20, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd ys morn. Cyrus went to Pittstn, Dolly to mr Densmores. Jona has not been here ys Day till night. mr Ballard & Ephm got a load of wood. I have been at home, do not feel So well as I Could wish. I am this Day 57 years old. Hannah has been washing. mr Waid here, Supt.   at home. aneversary of my Birth, 57 years Since.  
 

 

 March 1, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, the wind Southard. I have been at home. my Childn went to mr Burtuns on a visit. Ephm helpt mr Densmores Sons to ye hook with ashes. mrs Black Calld here & went to mr Densmores to try on an overcoat.   at home. my Childn went to mr Burtuns.  
 

 

 March 2, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rain. Hannah washt. I have been at home. helpt Sally nurs her Babe, it is not so well as usual. mr Ballard been at home. Ephm brot Hay from mr Densmores. Dolly went there aft Breakfast.   at home.  
 

 

 March 4, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard is Severly Exercisd with pain in ye Small of his back. there was preaching at our meeting house, we did not attend. mr Warrin of Pittstn performd. Cyrus & Dolly have been at home. mr Pollard, Patridg, mrs Ballard & mrs Dorcs Pollard here at Evn. my Son Jona is 29 years old ys Day.   at home. Company here at Evn. Jonas Birth Day, 29 years old.  
 

 

 March 5, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Severe Snow Storm. Jona went to Pittstn, Brot up 7 Bushels meal. mr Ballard is Some Easier. I have been at home. Hannah washt.   at home. Danl Fosters wife Deld, her inft Expird.  
 

 

 March 6, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Black Edmonds Son.

Snowd ys morn. I was Calld by Edmond at 6h morn to See his wife. had one fall from my hors, was assisted by Moses White in prosceeding the rest of ye way. Lidia was Delivrd at 1h pm of a Son, her first Born. mr Walker Conducted me home in a Sleigh. Hannah has Bakt Pumkin pies.   at Black Edmonds. Birth 13th. 
 

 

 March 12, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Jonns lambs were brot into ye hous, are feeble but revivd. mrs Livermore & I went to See old Lady Coney, Shee has had an ill turn, but is a little better.   at Deaon Coneys.  
 

 

 March 16, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd & raind. mr Haywood & m Pollard Came & Conducted mrs Haill & mrs Bisbe up to Town. mr Densmore & Waid and their wives Calld here on their way to ye hook. mr Ballard, Ephm & David Densmr been to ye meddow, returnd with out giting any hay except a hand Sleigd load, which ye boy halld. I have been at home, am not well yet. mr Black Came & acompanied my Dagts to his house. Jona Came home at Evng informs me his Son was Burnt on one of his feet this day.   at home. had Company. my Dagts gone to mr Blacks. they Spent ye Evng at mr Thomass. 
 

 

 March 25, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & Pleast. Esq Coney Calld to See us. Cyrus & Dolly at home. I have been at home. mr Manley went to his Cousins.   at home.  
 

 

 March 27, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 A foggy misty Day. I was Calld to See mr Nathl Shaws infant, it has the Cough & appeard in Danger of having fitts. mrs Lishance watcht.   at mr Shaws.  
 

 

 March 29, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard & Ephm assisting at ye Sugar works. Jona Sett his Saw mill to work, Sawd 1 log. Hannah & Sally Carding & Sping Cottne.   at home. fasT Day.  
 

 

 March 30, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. mr Ballard & Waid makeing Troughs to Collect Sap in. Jona at ye Saw mill, Ephm with his Dad. he is 13 years old this night at 11 hours Evng. I have been at home nursing Jack, he has not been well. he hurt his Eye with his nale.   at home. Ephms Birth Day.  
 

 

 April 6, 1792, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 foggy morn, Clear middle of ye Day. I Drest Stephens Joness Burn & went to See my Son Jonas wife, returnd to mr Joness. made an ointment of Lint Seed Oil, Bees wax & rosin. Drest the Child & Sett out for home. mr Crage Calld me in to See his little Dagt who is Sick with the Canker. I Drank Tea there, mrs H. Cox was there. I arivd at home at Dusk, the rodes were very muddy & wet the most of the way. mr Ballard has been Laying out house Lotts at ye hook for mr Vauhn.   at Ditoes & at my Sons & mr Craggs.  
 

 

 April 15, 1792, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a frost ys morn. I wrode out as far as mr Shepherds. Calld at mr Shaws, his inft is Better. mr Phillip Norcross gave me 6/ as I was returning home, for my assisting his wife the 20th of Jany.   I was at mr Shepherds & D. Colmans & at [home].  
 

 

 April 17, 1792, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd the most of ye Day. mr Ballard & mr Wade Setled acounts. Manly made a Gate. mr Ball went to the hook and up to Town, he receivd 8/ of Petr Voce which Isaac Hardin left there for my attending his wife in illness. I have been at home.   at home. Isaac Hardin paid me 8/.  
 

 

 April 21, 1792, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard been to Esqr Coneys, receivd 30 Dollars in part for the Surveying he is to Do for this Commonwealth. he allso went to mr Shepherds & ye fort on Business. I have been at home. Hannah made Pumkin Pies. mr Webber here, his wife very ill.   at home. Capt Howard and Howland went up this aftnn with their Vessels.  
 

 

 April 23, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Jacksons Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld by Saml Jackson to See his wife, who was Safe Deld at 1h pm of a Dagt. Shee had a turn of faintness but revivd. I left her and arivd at home about Sun Sett.   at Saml Jacksons. Birth 14th. receivd 6/ fee July 5.  
 

 

 April 30, 1792, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Whites Dagt. fee receivd. XX.

Clear & pleast. I was Calld to See Moses Whites Lady who was in travil. I arivd there at 4h morn. the Lady Safe Deld of a Dagt at 11h 5m. I left her at 2 pm, find on my return that mr Ballard took his Departure on his Tour of Surveying at 12 O Clok. may God prospur & return him [&] his Company in Safety. I receivd 6/ of mr White by mr Roberson. a letter from Bror Collins at 2d inst, one from Doct Barton of 16th; Hannah one fm her aunt Barton of 18, all which inform our friends are well.   at mr Moses Whites. Birth 15th. mr Ballad Sett out on his Tour of Surveyng. I recievd Letters fm oxford.  
 

 

 May 3, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Peter Grants Son. XX.

A Cloudy, Dull Day. I was Calld by Peter Grant to See his wife. they had Calld Doct Parker before I arivd & he Seemd to Chuse to Perform the opperation which took Place at 1h 11m AM. I lift her & infant Cleverly at 2h and arivd Safe at home about 4. Dolly & Sally Densmr here at Evng. I receivd 6/ of mr Grant.   at mr Grants, Pittstn, his first Child was Born. Doct Parker Performd ye Operation.
