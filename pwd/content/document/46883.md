War department June 14 1794
Sir
Lieutant Polhemus complains of a 
want of Axes, Paper and other quartermaster's Stores.
Everything in this line for a garrison of one hundred
men, must be immediately sent to that place, and
also ammunition and provisions for three months.
Capt. Crawford is to command there.
I have ordered one hundred barrells of Powder
twenty tons of Lead, and five hundred arms and
Accoutrements, Flints & to Pittsburg to save there as
a Magazine in case of exigency.
I am
Sir
Your obed. servant.
Knox


Major Isaac Craig
