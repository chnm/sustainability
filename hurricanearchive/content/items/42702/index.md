Everything we owned was gone. I sat on the backporch and cried, and was angry at the same time. We went back to GA, and stayed there until February, until my husband got called back to work at the school. We bought a home in Poplarville, MS., because we wanted to be as far away from the wreckage as possible. It has not been easy starting over, and we miss our families and friends who lived who lived in St.Bernard. Most, like us, did not return, and it is difficult to have 35 years of your life taken away. I lost my job, my home, my friends, my family, and most of all, a way of life that I had always known. I will always feel an emptiness inside for the life we had before. But, I thank God for all He has done for us,and is still doing for us. "
