Henry Glen  [undecipherable] 
Schenectady near Albany
War Office September 10, 1796
Sir
Cadert have issued for a detachment consisting of about ninety men, without artillery, and with provisions only, to carry them to Oswego; to proceed to Albany on their way to that post. 
You will therefore be pleased to prepare the necessary means for their transportation.
I have in consequence of your letter of the 2 instant directed two thousand dollars to be remitted you for which you are to be accountable. 
I am Sir
Your obed Servant
Jance W Henry
Secy of War
Henry Glen Es
