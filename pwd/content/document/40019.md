West Point Sept 3 1789
Sir
Agreeable to your of 22d Ultimo, by Mr. A. Ingells [?].[crossed out words: I have]
I have now delivered him twenty five Tons fifteen hundred of 
Damaged Cannon, Shells and shot, which makes in the whole One
hundred and twenty five tons and fifteen hundred
I now forward the returns of Ordnance and Quarter Master 
Stores for the month of August 1789 --
I wrote you on the 4 Ultimo respecting the wood for
the winter ensuing - please to inform me wether I shall engage
any as the season is approaching fast when it will be difficult
to get it here --
If you should think it proper to have any [ illegible ]
I can have it when [illegible ] until next spring --
I amSir Vc-
Honorable
-Major General Knox
Secretary at War
