War Office 5th April 1790
Sir,
At the request of some members of Congress I am about to transfer to the Government of Virginia and North Carolina the names & [undecipherable] due to the new Commissioned officers and privates belonging to the said States to whom is due the pay in Specie for two months in the year 1782 and four months in the year 1783
I request therefore that you would be so good as to furnish me immediately with Cefts. for that purpose authenticated by you.
I am
Sir
Your most obedient servant
HKnox
Joseph Howell Jr Esqr
