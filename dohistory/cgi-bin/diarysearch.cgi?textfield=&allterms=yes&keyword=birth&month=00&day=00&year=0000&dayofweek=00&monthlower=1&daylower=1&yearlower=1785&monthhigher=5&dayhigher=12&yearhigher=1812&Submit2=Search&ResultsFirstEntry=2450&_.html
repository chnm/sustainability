 June 19, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, feel very unwel. mrs Smith & Parker Calld in here. I have bakt and done my other hous work.   at home. mrs Smith & Parker here.  
 

 

 June 20, 1807, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a light Shower about 3h pm. I have done my hous work and washt, Sett 22 Cabbage Plants. mr Parker Calld in here. mr Ballard has been Surveying a piece of land North of here which Son Jona is about Setting to a mr Haws.   at home. mr Ballad Surveying.  
 

 

 June 21, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. I have been at home. mr Ballard to meeting, Revd mr Stone Discoart fm Luke 10 C 41 & 42 [v]. mrs Farewel Came here, Dind, Son Cyrus allso. he returned to Watervil. Son Jona Calld in.   at home. [mrs Farewel &] Son Cyrus Dind here.  
 

 

 June 23, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I feel more Comfortable for which I wish to bless the great author of mercies. mrs Gill was so kind as to asist ys morn and left here after Breakfast to go to Sidney. I have made 4 under Coats fm my old Cotton Shifts and workt some in my Gardin.    
 

 

 June 24, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Part of ye day. I have workt in my Gardin, over hauled wollin raggs, Cut Some for flock & Some for Carpet filling. mr Ballard been to the Settlement. Rachel Smith Paid [in] 1 Dollar in part of what was Due to me for asisting her in Child [Bed].   [at home. Rachel Smith paid 6/.]  
 

 

 June 29, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except Some Showers. I was Calld between 12 & 1 to go and see mrs Burden who is in Labour, her women Calld before night.   at [mr Burdens].  
 

 

 June 30, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Burdens 5th Son & 11th Child.

Clear. mrs Burdin Deld of her 5th Son & Eleventh Child at 4h 5m ys morn. her illness was very Severe a Short Space but Blessed be God it terminated in Safety and ye inft is numbered among ye living. mrs Saunders [here].   [at mr Burdens. Birth 10th.]  
 

 

 July 4, 1807, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. mr Ballard has been to the meeting hous to hear an Oration Deliverd by Revd mr Cushman. I have Mowd grass round the hous with a knife and Sowd Carrot Seed. Hannah and Sally Pollard & Sally Bald Sleep here. Wm Cypher Came here for water. it raind So he did not go with it. he went out of the hous and I Could not persuad him to Cum in. I went to Bed a little before mid night, my husband rose and and [sic] his Endevour to get him in but Could not, but he Came in afterwards & Slept on thee floor.   at home, Sowd Carrots.  
 

 

 July 9, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous wk and houghd Cucumbers, potatoes, puld up weads in my Gardin. John Town Calld here ys morn. mr Ballard been to ye Settlement, brot home _ gn Spirit, _ lb Tea, 2 lb Sugar.   at home. aneversary of ye Purington Scein.  
 

 

 July 13, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Wals 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld by John Wall at 6h ys morn to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Delivd at 8h morn of her 2nd Daugt & 2nd Child. I left her Cleverly and arivd at home at 12 O Clock [percisly]. I got Dinner and did other matters. Daugts Ballard & Pollard took Tea, Sally, Martha & Ephm allso.   at mr John Wals. Birth 11th. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 July 26, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sons Jona, Ephm & lheir wives and Some of yr Childn here. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to meeting. I was Calld by mr mc farlin to go and See his Sister Bates who is very unwel at his hous.   at mr [McFarlns] to See mrs Bates, receivd 1/6.  
 

 

 August 20, 1807, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy the most of ye day. I have done Some washing, gatherd 4 DZ & 7 Cucumbers. Jona assisted me and took ym to Carrie to markett. mr Ballard, mrs Wiman here to Tea. her husband Calld and Conducted her home.   at home. gatherd Currents, 3 pints.  
 

 

 August 28, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. this is the aniversary of ye Birth of my Daugt Town, 48 years Since. I have done my hous work and workt in my Gardin. mr Ballard went down below, Carried 2 Dzn Cucumrs & 3 Dito of pickele Dito. mrs Carter had 1 dZn & ye pickels. Dagt Lambard 1 DZn Cucumrs.   at home, workt in my gardn. John Davis Esqrs infant Expired.  
 

 

 September 5, 1807, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml BabCocks Son.

Clear. I have been Calld to Saml Babacoks Junrs wife. Shee was Safe deld of her 3d Son by him at ye 2h pm.   at Saml [Babcocks]. Birth 12th.  
 

 

 September 9, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work and went to meeting. it was asoceation. mr Parker only was present who Delivd a good Discoars.   at meeting and Son Lambds. Funeral mr Bonds Child.  
 

 

 September 11, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. there was an oralion Delivd by mr Oldin on the aneversary of the independent Company. Son Cyrus attended. I have done my work tho very unwel.   at home. Son Cyrus 51 years old this day.  
 

 

 September 21, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard been Surveying for mr Weston & Esqr Davis. he Carried 3 DZn Pickeld Cucumbers, Sold them to Ger Norths wife for /9d. I have gathered Cucumbers, Beens, Peas & Squash, Cut herbs and done my hous work. Betsy wiman here for Pickles. her Father here aftn, informs that two of his Sons are very Sick. Dagt Ballard, Martha and James Calld in at [  ].   at [home]. feel [Comfortable] thro [Gods Goodness].  
 

 

 September 22, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jon a Devenports 4th Son. XX.

Clear and pleast. I have done my wk foren. was Calld at 4h pm by Jona Devenport to go and See his wife. Shee was Deld at 8h Evn of her 4th Son and 6th Child. Shee Seems Cleverly. I tarried all night.   at [Jona] [Devenports]. Birth [12th]. receivd 9/ as [reward].  
 

 

 September 23, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. I larried with my Patient till 2h pm. we got her up and made her Bed. Shee is Still Cleverly. I Came by water to Bomans Brook, from there watkt home. Calld at mr Andruss & [wimans].   at [mr] [Devenports], [Andrus] & [Wimans].  
 

 

 October 11, 1807, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. mr Ballard, Cyrus to meeting. Allin & Ephm took Tea here. I was Calld to see Silas Wiman, administred 2 Clisters and returnd home about 9h Evng. we had fresh Cucumbers & green Corn with our Supper.   at mr Wimans to See Silas.  
 

 

 October 13, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I [Cut Pumkin] last night and to day. Gideon Barton took Breakfast here. Jason Pierce and wife Calld in. mr Ballard husking all day.   at home. [mr was un ].  
 

 

 October 14, 1807, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I was Calld to mr Wimans to perform an operation for Silas, from there to See Son Jonas infant; find it Some Better. Came home, got Dinner for my famely and went to mr Wimans again. returnd at Evng. Silas is very feeble.   at mr Wimans & Son Jonas twice this day.  
 

 

 October 27, 1807, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Bakt and did my other work. I attended the funeral of Selas Wiman. mr [Wilborough] Delivd a discoars from St Johns Gospil 8 C 2 vers.   at ye funeral of Silas wiman. mr [Wilbr] performd.  
 

 

 November 2, 1807, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day. I returnd home, left the Patient Some Easier. Came to the meeting hous by water, from mr Thwings on mr Andruss hors home. Cyrus took him home. I find [Luther] Smith here.   at Ditoes & at mr Thwings & Andruss. 
 

 

 December 25, 1807, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. it is Crismas Day. I have done a fortnits wash and was Calld to Son Jonas. he was in great Distress with his arm which he hurt by a fall from his hors last tuesday. I put a poltis on it. Hannah was very ill. I attended to them through the night till after [4] this morn.   at Son Jonas, he and Hannah very unwel.
