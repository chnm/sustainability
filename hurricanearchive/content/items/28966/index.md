Artwork by Shannon Crane hangs on a clothesline as part of "NEIGHBORHOODS: 2426 BRADISH PLACE," a multidisciplinary installation. The Bradish Place show, which was the third in a series,  took place in the gutted ground floor of the home of Michelle Levine and Clay Thomas on May 19th, 2007. Michelle is an artist who is currently rebuilding her house and studio in the Tulane/Gravier neighborhood. Through the use of interviews with neighbors, soundscapes, live music, drawing, photography, and sculpture, this event confronted the range of issues the community is experiencing as they revitalize this neighborhood.
