Accountants' Office
July 25th 1799
Tobias Lear Esqr
Secretary to the Commder in Chief
Sir
I have received your letter dated 17 instt inclosing your account for pay forage & subsistence from 1 April to 30 June 1799 amounting to Drs 351.7/100, which sum you will receive from Mr Samuel Lewis Senr. to whom you will be pleased to transmit your duplicate rects for the same, one of which to be filed in this office.
I am &c W. S.
