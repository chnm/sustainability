Pittsburg 29 September 1797
Sir
Your letter of the 18th + 22nd this moment have come to hand accompanied by a news Paper ..
Your letter of the 15th
222
informs me that you were then getting on Hospital Stores Cloathing &ca for the northern posts via Albany + Schenectady  Quere are Niagara, Detroit + Michilimacinac, or any of these posts to be supplyed by that route? Early information on this point is of importance in making provision for transportation from this post ~
The quantity of Stationary, writing paper in particular received this season has not been adequate to the necessary consumption of the several posts depending on this
I am                   Sir                           Your very obedServ

Samuel Hodgdon esq
Philadelphia
