War department, Nov: 10th 1792.
Sir
I have received your letter of the 2d instant.  I am surprized to hear that a single pair of the woolen overalls, for the troops should have been rejected.  Every article of clothing was reported to me, as having passed a critical examination by the inspectors, who were specially appointed for that purpose.  
I am Sir, 
with esteem,
Your humble Servt
H Knox 
Major Craig
