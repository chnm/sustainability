 August 7, 1808, Sunday

 Clear. Dagt Lambd went to Meeting. I got ready to go aftn when [news] Came that mr Andrus was Dead. mr [Lambard] went & returnd. I went, a Prayer was Deld by Revd mr Stone, Discoarst from. [no entry].   at mrs Andrus, her husband Expird [Sudingly] very [   ].  
 

 

 August 9, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, A Cool morn, warm afterwards. mr Ballard went to Son Ephms, finds them Comfortable. he bot 8 lb pork of C. [Wall]. he, my Self, Son Cyrus, Dagt Lambd, Barny & Lucy attend Funeral of Neighbor Andrus. A mr Kendal Delivd a very long discoars from Revelations 14 C 13th vers. Sons Cyrus, Jona & his wife & Martha took Tea with us. Daugt Lambd had a Severe pain in her head after we Came home.   at the Funeral of mr Andrus.  
 

 

 August 19, 1808, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sister Barton Came here, mr Porter Came down with her. he and Son Pollard Calld in here. I have been at home. mrs Porter Sent us a Chees yt wd 12 lb.   at home. mr Porter here, Sist Barton allso.  
 

 

 August 22, 1808, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Sister Barton & I went to Son Ephms, Calld at Old mr Hamlins, Shee was not at home. we tarried at my sons.   at Son Ephms, Slept there. Funeral mr Robinsn. 
 

 

 August 23, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my Sister & I lefe Son Ephms before 10h morn. Calld at mr Carters then proseeded to Capt Shubal Pittss. Dind & took Tea. we went into Stores for pins but found none. I Sett out for home, walk as far as Rewel Williamss when I met Barny with a hors and I wrode home.   at Son Ephms, mr Carters & Capt Shubal Pitts s.  
 

 

 September 1, 1808, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Joel Savages 2nt Daugt. XX.

I was Calld by Joel Savage at Mid night to go & See his wife who is in Labour. I found her not very ill. I went onto the Bed & rested but did not Sleep. it raind before day break. her illness Came on, her women Calld in and Shee was Safe Deld a Daught her 3d Child at 6h 30m ys morn and is Comfortable. we had a good Breakfast and I receivd 9s as reward. Sett out home, reacht there before 10. was informd that Son Towns youngest Child is dead.   at Joel Savages. Birth 2nd, receivd 9 S as a reward.  
 

 

 September 3, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld by Son Ephm to go and See his infant which I found very Sick. it had a high feaver and much oprest for Breath. we gave it a little Caster Oil and it Seemd Some Easier. I Sett up with it till near 4h morn when I put it in Bed with its Mother and Slept a little. mr Son Calld me up and we thought Expireing but it revived a little. we Sent for Son Jonas wife, Shee Came.   at Son Ephms, his infant very Sick.  
 

 

 September 4, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Son Ephms 3d Daugt Expired, aged 5 weaks, 2 days & 12 hours.

Clear. I am at my Sons, the Child is no better. Dagt Ballard Came and assisted in takeing Care of it. Doct Cony was Calld, left Sinna [and] Elixir Salutus but it was past the reach of medisin. it Continued till 7h 30m Evng when it gave up ye ghost. a mr Howard tarried all night.   at Ditoes. the Babe Expired at 7h 30 m Evng. mrs Hamlin and Titcomb laid it out.  
 

 

 September 5, 1808, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we were at Son Ephms, the Neighbours granted them assistance in makeing preperation for the interment. I Slept in the Chamber alone where ye Corps lay.   at Ditoes, Dagt Ballard went home.  
 

 

 September 13, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, a Sutherly wind. I have washt, feel very much fatagued, allmost Sick. mr Ballard Cuting Stalks. Beniamin Pains hors was killd between mrs Andruss & mr Wimans by going against a Stump & Pain was badly hurt.   at home. mr [Lambd] to Trooping. Pains hors killd, he was very much hurt himSelf.  
 

 

 September 29, 1808, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. I have Cut to drie Squash & pumkins. Old mrs Kenny here for Cabbage. mr Lambard brot me Tea, Sugar & Spirit. 9 S which mr Stinson Sent me for asisting his wife.   at home. I receivd 9S of mr Stinson by Son Lambd.  
 

 

 October 2, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & rain. mr Bald & Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Slone Spake fm [Jeremh] 6th C 16th vers. Son Jona & wife Supt, Son Epm Calld in. mr Lambd to hear mr Rogers.   at home, Childn here.  
 

 

 October 16, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard to meeting, Cyrus Staid with me. Hanh Ballard Came and did my wk. Jona & wife here. I was Calld at 9h 30m to go and See mrs Nason. Text ys day Jerem 13 C 23 vers.   at [home] till 9h 30m Evng, then [to] Nasons.  
 

 

 October 17, 1808, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Nasons 7th Child.

Clear. mrs Nason was Safe Deld at 1h ys morn of a 4th daugt & 7th Child. we all tarried till morn.   at mr Nasons. Birth 3d. 
 

 

 October 24, 1808, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye day, Clear Evng. I have done hous work. dagt Lambd Calld here. Jona Ballard Junr has Seen 17 years ys day.   at home. Dagt Lambd Calld here.  
 

 

 October 29, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. mr Dickman fell from his hors by his Stumbling, was Carried to Son Jonas. Docters Calld, he is very much hurt.   at home. mr Dickman fell from his hors near Son Jonas.  
 

 

 November 1, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. mr Ballard & Cyrus attend funeral of mr Dickman. mr Rogers made a prayer, deliverd a discoars. mr Slone made ye last prayer. Allin Carried 14 fowls home from here.   at home. Funeral mr Dickman.  
 

 

 November 12, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard and I attended Funeral of Old mr Isaac Savages wife. it was attended at mr Wm Stones. mr Com Smith Deld a Discoars from Romans 5th C 21s vers. we did not prosceed to the grave. mr Savage is more Comfortable than he has been.   at the Funeral of mrs Savage.  
 

 

 November 27, 1808, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Pleast Sun but Cool air. mr Ballard and Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Stone discst forn fm Mark, aftn fm Proverbs.   at [home]. Savage Bolton Calld [here].  
 

 

 December 10, 1808, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear the most of ye day. mr mr [sic] Ballard to the Settlement to do business with James Bolton. Capt Osgood Calld here to See him. my husband brot a [new] Sute of Cloaths home which Daugt Lambd and Rhoda made.   at home. I have done my ironing & other [things].  
 

 

 December 27, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Still remain Easier. mr Ballard & Cyrus got wood. Son & Daugt Lambard Calld in to See me at Evng. my Dagt handed me a letter from Hannah waters of Oct 8th informing our friends are wel.    
 

 

 January 8, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear the most of ye day, very Cold night. mr Ballard & Cyrus to meeting, Text Prov 13th 14 vers, aftn Acts 13:15 vers. Augusts Ballard Calld in here. Luke & Allin brot Polly Town up.   at home, friends here.  
 

 

 January 29, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear the most of ye day and not so Cold. I have been at home. mr Ballard & Cyrus to meeting. Revd mr Stone Discst fm Timoy 3 Ch 19 vers. at Evng R. & H. Pollard, H. & M. Ballard, Doly Lambd, Betsy Smith and Jona Porter Calld in to See us.   at home. Grand Childn here at Evng.  
 

 

 January 31, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Part of ye day & not so Cold. mr Ballard and Cyrus attended at Funeral of Georg Crosbys wife. Revd mr Harskal Deliverd A Discoars from Ecleasts 8 C 8 Vers. I have Bakt brown bread & apple pies and knit at Evng. Lefaett Supt here.   at home. Funeral of mrss Crosby. Revd mr Harskal Performd.  
 

 

 February 12, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. mr Ballard to meeting aftern. Revd mr Stone discst foren fm 1 Saml 3 C 18 vers, aftn fm Matt 8th C 11-12 verss. Old mrs Kenny here and Sally & Marta. Jona Junr Sleeps here.   at home. mr Bald to meeting. Luke Bart & D. Savage Calld here. mrs Kenny been here.
