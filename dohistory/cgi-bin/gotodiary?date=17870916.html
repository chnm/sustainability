The Sep. 18-20 entries appear in "One Rape. Two Stories."
11 3

 

 returnd from mc Msts. Calld at mr Westons & Williamss Polly Savage [there]. Foster Came to work here yesterday. mr Ballard at mr Pollards. Cyrus is 31 years old this Day.  
12 4

 

 Cloudy. I have been at home, wound quills for Dolly. She wove 5-1/2 yds.  
13 5

 

 Clear. I feel unwell. mr Ballard gon to ye hook, ye Girls to Capt Porters. Jono brot his Colt from Cobesy.  
14 6

 

 Cloudy & Some rain. mr Ballard gone to Varsalborough to run Lines for mr Carr. Dolly finisht weaving mrs Williamss Cloath. I have been unwell. mrs Savage here ys morn.  
15 7

 

 at home till Sun Set, then Calld to See Andrew Goodwins wife who is unwell.  
16 G

 

 at mr Godws. Shee is Some Better.  
17 2

 

 Came home from mr Goodwins, left her about house. Fanny Cox rode behind me. mr Ballard gon to Survey for mr Pitts.  
18 3

X Birth. X Revd mr Fosters son X XX.

I was Calld to Revd mr Fosters Lady in Travl. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 7 Evnn. I watcht. it is her Second Child.  
19 4

 

 I am at mr Fosters. Shee is Cleverly.  
20 5

 

 at mr Fosters. it raind. Joseph Fosters wife watcht.  
21 6

 

 a friday night.  
22 7

 

 Calld to Joseph Williamss to See his wife, very unwell. mr Foster gone to the hook. I Came home at Evinng, find my Famely in helth.  
23 G

 

 I fited for going to meeting but was Calld to See mrs mc Masters who is unwell. mrs Cowen & I administred remedys that Doct Coney prescribd. her physic began to operate. I left her, returnd as far as mrs Williamss, tarried with her till 10 in Evnng, Shee being Exercisd with much pain, but got easier & I Came home.  
24 2

 

 Clear & pleast. the girls washing. mr Balld makeing Plan of mr Pittss Land. mrs Savage & mrs Brown Drank Tea here.  
25 3

 

 Clear. mr Ballard gone to Capt Pages. he help remove his house. mr Hains Learnd here, Left oxford 2 weaks ago, our Friends all well Except my Mother, who is in a Langued Situation as Brother Collins informs by a Letter Dated Sept 7, 1787.
