No 10
War department
May 10th 1794
Sir, 
I have received your letter of the 2d instant.
The Quarter Master Mr. O'Hara, will leave this tomor for Pittsburg
Please to deliver the enclosed letter to Ensign Levi McClean on his arrival. 
I am Sir
Your humble servant
H Knox 
Major Isaac Craig
My dear sir Be so good as to take special care of and forward
