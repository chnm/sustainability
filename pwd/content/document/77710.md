Sam Hodgdon Esqr Philada
Accountants Office
January 2d 1801
Sir
I have received your letter dated 2q Ulto with the Accounts therein mentioned they have been reported upon & the amount will be remitted in the usual way-The account of disbursements you mention will lay over until those for the last quarter are received~
I am tc
W.S.
