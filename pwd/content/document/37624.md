Pay Office New York January 5 1786
This may certify that Captain William Coxe of the late 10th regiment, is returned in the rolls of sd regiment as a supervisery Officer, retiring from Service 1t July 1778- it further appears on record in this Office that Cap. Coxe has settled his accounts with the U. States
Joseph Howell Jnr
To all Concerned
