War department 10 January 1791
Sir
You will assist Captain John Smith in the recruiting service in the City of New York and its vicinity, and use all possible diligence in the pursuit. 
I am Sir 
your most obedient 
Servant 
H Knox 
Secy of War 
Lt Staats Morris
