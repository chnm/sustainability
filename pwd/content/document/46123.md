Cedar Hall March 10th. of 94
Sir
I have a number of Muster Rolls on hand which I detain for want of a save coveyance Captn Thomas Hickman the Bearer of this goes Through by himself & the Road I am afraid is watched, the first Safe or Large Company that goes from here I will Send to you, the Indiand has done no Mischeif these few days Past tho I think it is owing to bad weather a few warm days will bring them out, Please make my Compliments to his Excell.y & Gen. c [s?] & believe 
[Sir?] to by your.s [&c?]
Robert Hays
Col. David Henley
