Nathl Gorham Esqr Supt Boston
Department of War Accountants Office 21 May 1796
Sir
The Treasurer of the United States will [undecipherable] you the fine of Ninety Dollars which you will [undecipherable] pay to Mrs Sally Greaton being on account of the pay of her Husband Capt Richd H Greaton for which sum please take duplicate [undecipherable] Capt Greaton to be recountable
I am &c William Simmons
