Wm Dearing 2d Lt
Comsn made out -
29th May 1786
May it please your Excellency
I have to request the favour that a Commison may be granted to William Dearing for 2d Liutt. in the first Company of Artillery in the room of Willm. Plastead, who declines serving; inclosed is his Commison.

You will please to order the Commissy
Genl. to furnish two pices of Cannon with their
Apperatus - and to have them repaird and the Comis'rys 
put in good order previous to deli'g them to Capt
Woodwad; who informs me the Company will soon
be Compleated - and ready for disciplining —

I am your Excellencies
obt Hble Sert 
Thos Thompson
To His Excellency John Langdon Esqr
