Elias Boudinot
Received april 19 " 1799
of Wm Simmons accountant
a Warrant N.o 3938. for One
thousand Dollars in full
of the within Bill of Exchange
D.rs 1000.
Nath.l Thomas
No. 330_________ Exchange for 1.000. -- Dollars
Cincinnati March 11.th 1799
Sir,
AT Ten days sight of this first & only bill of Exchange
______________ of the same tenor and date being unpaid,
please to pay to Elias Boudinot or order, the sum of
One Thousand _____
Dollars, which sum I have received of him in Cash :[over the normal text] Pr. Jacob Bunnet Esq.r and charge the
same as per advice herewith to my Account.
C. Swan
To the Sec'ry. of War,
of the United States.
Pay of the army for 1799)

[note in the left marhing:]
presented 18.th April 1799
P. Hagner
