Mr. Wolcott takes the liberty to inclose a Letter for Mr. Howells perusal, with a request that he would be so oblidging as to send a note whether any balances remain due to the Soldiers therein mentioned--possibly the certifs may be in Jno Shermans hands.
Apr 6th 1791
