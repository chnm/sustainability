Copy Letter to Genl. Knox
21st May 1787
Providence 21st May 1787
Sir
Your favr. of 9th Inst. has been Recd. I
shall begin tomorrow the Examination of the full
Stores. the principle part of wich are in this Town. Some
at Warren - Bristol Gtreenwich & at Fort Island in
Newport Harbor as the Complating this business
will Necessarily be attended with some expence for
Labourers. House Live &c &c and it being not of my
power to make the Necessary advances. I must Request
you will be so good as to forward Fifteen Dollars
%the first Conveyance for which I will Stand accountable.
I shall go on with the Examination of the Sores
in this Town, if I can Engage [undecipherable] on the lands, before
the money arrives.
I am Sr.
with Real Esteem
your most Obed. Huml. Ser.
Jereh. Olney


Genl. Knox
Secretary at War
