X. Birth James Savages Dagt. X.

Clear morn, a Shower aftn. I was Calld a 8th hour morn to See James Savages wife. Shee was Safe Deliverd of a fine Dagt at 7 h Pm. I tarried all night, receivd my fee in Spining.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 June 28, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. I attended worship in public, mr Jonston performd. Capt Stackpool Calld Doct Colman out of meeting to visit mr Flint Bartons Son. mrs Foot & Clarrissa here, mr Savage & his wife also. Cyrus had Corn to grind all Day.   I was at meeting.  
 

 

 July 13, 1789, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. Cyrus is gone to Pitts town to asist Hannah in geting home. they arivd with mrs Burton in Company at 5 PM, inform me of ye Death of Doct Conant. our other friends in health. I have been at home. Son Town here, his wife is So well as to walk about Some.   I was at home. Hannah returnd from Oxfd. Son Town here. I had Letters from Collins of June 23, & Sally of June 24th. 
 

 

 July 14, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. Clarrisa went to mr Porters, Cyrus attended her. mr Ballard & Jona & Manly went to asist in puting up a house fraim for mr Ezekiel Page. Son Town went from here. ys Eving is very Cool.   I have been at home.  
 

 

 July 22, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Churches Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld to mrs Church ye 5th h Morn. Shee was Safe Delivrd at 2h 30 PM. I left mother & Dagt Cleverly & returnd at 4. Echabod Pitts Came there for me but I Could not Leave my Patient. William Haywood here, brot Letters from Dagt Town which informs Shee is able to walk & has been to their field yesterdy.   I was at mr Churches. Echabod Pitts s wife Delivrd of a Son ys Evn by mrs Marsh.

Cloudy & Some rain. I was Calld to Stutely Springers wife in travil at ye 4th h morn. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a Dagt at 3 PM. I returnd at ye 7th h, Left my Patient as well as Could be Expected. find mrs Burtun here.   I was at mr Springers.  
 

 

 August 10, 1789, Monday

 Clear Except a sprineling of rain about noon. I went with mrs Emerson as far as mr Crages, Drank Tea there. was Calld by James Moore to See mrs Shearborn who was very unwell. I tarried all night, gave the Lady Some medisin. Shee Seemd mutch more Comfortable. I left her at 2 h pm & returnd home, find I had a Call to go Elswhere.   at mr Crages & mr Sherborns. Calld at Revd mr Fosters. Levy Moore & wife here.

 August 15, 1789, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Allins Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Cld at 1h morn to See mrs Allin, find her not very Sick. was Calld from thence to Doct Colmans Lady in travil. Shee Calld her women & was Safe Deld of a fine Son at ye 11th h. I was Calld from there to See mrs Pond at Winthrop, find her in Dificult Sircumstance. applied Some remedies & gave Directions & Left medisin, Left her at Sun Sett. Came to mr Pollards at ye 10th h Even. very Dark & rainy as I Came down, I had a boy with me from Hardins. was Conducted over ye river by mr mc Farling. by Joseph Foster to mr Allins, find ym all well. his Lady was Siesd with travil a little before day & was Safe Deld at 8/th h morn of a fine Dagt & I arivd Safe at home. I was gone from home 35 hours. I went to Bed & took a fine Knap afternn. Jon a been to winthrop ys day.   at mr Allins, Doct Colmans & mr Ponds, & to mr Allins again. I receivd 9/4 of ye Doctr as a reward for my Service. at mr Allins. recievd my fee Novr 3d 1790 in Chees, at /6 pr pound.  
 

 

 August 18, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

Ezra Hodges Son Born. XX.

Clear & not very hott. I was Calld in ye aftern to See mrs Hodg who was in travil. Shee was Safe Delivrd about ye 10th hour Evn of a fine Son, & 4th Child, which wd 11-1/2 lb. mr Hodg is out at ye meddow. it is his 4th Child . I tarried all night. mrs Burtun here when I left home.   at mr Hodges. I receivd 2 lb Cotten as a reward.

X Birth Hoveys Dagt. XX.

Clear. mrs Hovey was Delivrd of a Dagt at 1h morn & I returnd at ye 9th, left them Cleverly. mrs Cumings gave me a pound of Sheeps wool. I went to Bed & took a knap aftern. Burtun & wife & mrs Cooshin here.   at mr Hoveys.  
 

 

 September 4, 1789, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Shower. mrs Porter & Clarrissa Barton Came here, the latter went with my Girls to help mrs Pollard quilt a Coat. I was Calld to See Polly Savages Child in a fitt. we gave it mullin Syrrup, it revivd & I came home. went aftern to See mrs Hamlin, mrs Porter with me. I have knitt a foot to a Stockin for mr Ballard Since 1 h Evn.   at mr Savages & mr Hamlins. mrs Porter & Clarrissa here. Polly Cool Drowned.  
 

 

 September 10, 1789, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. mrs Pinkams Dagt.

Clear. I was Calld to See Capt Pinkams wife, Shee was at mr Wades. I left home at ye 12 hour, walkt under Shore, had a faint turn after I arivd there. my patient was Delivd of a Dagt at ye 9thh Evn. it was dead Born. I tarried ys night.   I was at mr Wades. a Shower at Evn.  
 

 

 September 11, 1789, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml Churches Child, a Son.

Clear. I lefe mrs Pinkam at ye 8th h morn, Came to mr Hodges. from there to Capt Porters. Left medisin for his Son, price 1/. wint from thence [to] mr Westons, he & mrs Betsy just returnd from the West wd. from there to go to Saml Churches wife in travil. met a mesage which informs Shee is Safe Delivd by mrs Ingerams asistance & they did not wish me to proseed. I returnd, Crost ye river at ye ferry & walkt home. I finisht footing a pair of Stockins for mr Ballard.   at mr Wades, Hodges, Porters, Westons & Pollards.

 Clear & very pleasant. Sherebiah Town & mrs Eunis Stackpool Dine here. Rufus & Lucy Ballard Calld here. my Girls & mrs Eunis went to Capt Porters. Hamlin & wife here. mr Weston raising his Pott ash.   I was at home. Biah [&] mrs Eunice here. Isaac Hard[ins] youngest Child Expired.

 September 22, 1789, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Sherburns Dagt. XX.

Clear & very pleasant. mrs Sherburn was Safe Delivrd at 3 h & 30 M morn of a Dagt. I took a nap of Sleep which was the first since Satterday night. receivd 3/ in Bread & Snuff which, with the flower I had when there before, rewards me for my asisting her. returnd home at ye 11th hour.   at Ditoes. mr Pond let me have 4 lb Butter.  
 

 

 September 27, 1789, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Cloudy aftern. I was Calld to See Genny Coy who is unwell at mr Kennadys. Shee is freequently Exercised with pain of which are ocationed by obstructions. I advised to the use of particellar herbs &C. mr Bartun & wife here at Evn.   I was at mr Kennady.  
 

 

 September 28, 1789, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Shaws Second Dagt. XX.

A very rainy night past & morn. I was Calld up at ye 4th hour to go to Shaws at the hook. walkt as far as mr Wsns, from there by water. was very wet indead. found the woman Safe Delivd by the help of mrs Cleark. I tarried till Evn tide then Came up by water as far as mr Westons, then walkt home ward to the Crick. Could not pass, the water was so much raisd. I went Back and tarried at mr Westons.   I was at Shaws at the hook. mrs Shaw Delivd of a Dagt at ye 3d hour. Decemr 19th, mr Shaw Sent me 1-1/2 lb Chocolat & 5 lb Sugar as a reward for my trouble.  
 

 

 September 30, 1789, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a lite Shower aftern. I went to Capt Herseys, mr Whites & Esqr Husseys, had 6 [lb] of Sheeps wool of the old Lady which is to go towards what they owe mr Ballard. I was Calld to mr Fosters door & askt Some questions.   I was at mrs Husseys. Colo North interagated me Concerning what Conversation mrs Foster had with me Concerning his Conduct.
