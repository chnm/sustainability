The Beaumont Enterprise (Beaumont, Texas) collection comprises individual news stories and entire editions that relate how Hurricanes Katrina and Rita affected Southeast Texas. The collection begins in late August 2005 with stories 
about Katrina evacuees and efforts to assist those dislocated, and ends in June 2006 at the start of the new hurricane season. 

For those interested in tracing how communities rebound from hurricanes, this collection will be most useful because it offers .PDF pages of the Enterprise's printed newspapers
from September 21, 2005 through April 30 2006. Selected stories in text format from September 2005, May and June 2006 are also available. 

The Beaumont Enterprise is a daily newspaper that has served Southeast Texas since 1880.
