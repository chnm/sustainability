War Department 22. Decem 1800
Sir
I have received your favor of the 15th. instant
and enclose a Deposit Warrant for the sum received
by you from Messr. Jesse & Robert on account of
Military Stores.
The Springfield Armory dispute shall be
attended to. 
I am Sir &c
