Detroit 1.st January 1800.

  Sir/

           I have had the honor of receiving your
  letter Dated the 7.th of August '99. a short time
  since, and have to reply, that I shall be able
  to see your in the Spring and make a Settlement
  with you..   I have a Number of Receipts for
  Bounties etc etc to come against that Account -
             Settlement of the
  Receipts for the^ Monies I received from Major
  Hamtramacle & Daniel Britt, I expect to find
  with my Papers at Cincinnati, deposited there
  with my friends. _  It is entirely out of my pow=
  er to make this Settlement untill I can get
  those Papers which is upwards of 300 Miles from
  this place.  however I shall obtain leave of
  Absence in the Spring & shall immediatley proceed
  on to Philadelphia, and then be able to 
                                       close
close the Account. ---

          I am with Great Esteem & Regard,
        
            Sir,

                   Your Obed. Hble Serv.t

                         D Strong Lt. Col.o
                       [undecipherable] 2.d Reg.t



      Will.m Simmons Esqr.
            Acct. Department of War
