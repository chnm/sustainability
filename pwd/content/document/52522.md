Samuel Hodgdon Esqr.
Philadelphia


Lieut. MClellan
January 1796
Recd. 12
Westpoint 7th January 1796
Sir
On the 4th instant, I received two large horses
& One Hogshead of Public Stores, addressed to me as
Tuny.[?] QeMAster & forwarded by Messes James
Manning & Co of New York; but no Invoice
accompanied them.
The Commanding Occicer, ordered me to
open them and ascertain their contents; on examination
(befoer the Assistant Store Keeoer) I found the Articles
& quantity, which are under notice
Four Hundred & Forty six Helmets
Three Hundred & Fifty six Plumes
Four Hundred & Ninetynine Cockades, complete
Eleven - - - - do - - - - incomplete
Forty eight for Sergts Wool: Overalls
Twentyfour Sergts Shirts
Three Silk Epauletts
Seventyfive Buff Swords Belts &
One [undecipherable] Majors Cane, also
some articles of Stationary of which an accurate 
Account cannot beginn undill the Invoice
is received
I am with respect Sir
Your very Obt Servant
Jn McClallen Le
Tuny[?] QrMaster
