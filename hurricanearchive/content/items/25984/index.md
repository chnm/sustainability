This morning, we officially donated my husband\'s Chevy S10 Blazer to the Waveland Animal Control.  They accepted it with open arms.  I took pictures of us handing over the keys.  But actually, we still own the truck until Friday.  We still want to be able to drive around until Friday.\r\n\r\nI can\'t believe I\'m down to the last two full days here.
