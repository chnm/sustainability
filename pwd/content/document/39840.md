United States Loan Office
Boston 6th May 1789
Joseph Howell Jun Esq.
Commissr for Army Accts
Sir
your favours 27th Ulto came safe to
hand with the enclosed Lists of Errors in the printed
Register of Certificates issued by the late John Pierce Esq
P. M. G. many of these errors I have noticed, a thorow
correction will be of great service---
with great respect I am
Sr Your huml Servant N.A. CLO
United States Loan Office
Boston 4th July 1789
Honbl Commissr of the Board of Treasury
Gentlemen
My last was 15th April since which I have received none of your favors.  This serves to transmitt a Quarterly return of business transacted in the Office from 1st April to 30th June last-- Vizt Acct Currt of specie money received & paid with Vouchers balance in my favour 8249 63/90 Dollars-- also Account Current of Indents of Interest recd & paid, balance in favour of the United States 169,671 8/90 Dollars-- also Acct current of Blank Loan Office Certificates received and issued, balance on hand 654 Certificates-- also Account of 40 Specie Loan Office Certificates Vizt No 3607 @ 3646 issued in lieu of depreciated Loan Office Certificates cancelled, amounting to 21,707 24/90 Dollars as per Vouchers No 77 @ 105-- also abstract No 1 of Indents of Interest issued for payment of Interest to 31st Decemr 1787 on Loan Office Certificates amounting to 22972 22/90 [undecipherable page folded] as per Vouchers No 213 @ 296-- also Abstract [undecipherable page folded] Indents of Interest issued for payment of
