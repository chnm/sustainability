September 18, 1787 (Tuesday)

18 3

X Birth. X Revd mr Fosters son X XX.

I was Calld to Revd mr Fosters Lady in Travl. Shee was Safe Deld of a fine Son at 7 Evnn. I watcht. it is her Second Child  

September 19, 1787 (Wednesday)

19 4

 

 I am at mr Fosters. Shee is Cleverly  

September 20, 1787 (Thursday)

20 5

 

 at mr Fosters. it raind. Joseph Fosters wife watcht
