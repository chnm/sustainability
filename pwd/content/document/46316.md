75

W.D. Accountants Office

April 5th, 1794

Sirs

The Treasurer has remitted to Nicholas Fish Esqr. Supervisor at New York to be paid to you the sum of One hundred & eighty dollars being for your Salary from 1st July 1793 to 31st March 1794 inclusive for which you will please to sign duplicate receipts ~

I am Sirs etc

Joseph Howell

Michael G. Houdin [?] Esqr. AsstKeeper of Military Stores at Albany - State of New York
