No 33
Department of War
Jany 3 - 1795
Sir
I have shewn your letter of the 26th Ultimo, which I received yesterday, to General Knox.  He resigned his appointment as Secretary of War at the close of the year.  His successor Coll Timothy Pickering was yesterday appointed.  
I am Sir, with esteem, Your Obt Sert
Jon Stagg, Junr
Major Craig
