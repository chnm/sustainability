- paper of this morning
- prelude to a meeting
- falling yesterday in company with a citizen in trade & an observing man
- asked what appeared to be the prevailing sentiment among the citizens
- those who had the least or no interest immediately depending on the commercial articles of the treaty were dissatisfied
- merchants who were directly & essentially concerned were satisfied
- appointment for negociating
- understood that the President had concurred with the Senate in the ratification of the treaty with Great Britain
- not yet seen any motion for a meeting of the people here for the purpose of condemning it
- republication of the resolutions of the Society of Philadelphia
- enclosed letter
- came to hand last Saturday
- contained two copies of his address to the national convention
- one of which I have taken the liberty to retain
