 January 30, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. mr Chambers Came here with Corn for mr Ballard. I was Calld in hast at 1h pm to go to See my Dagt Lambart who was Exercised with very Severe pains. Dagts Town & Pollard there. we made use of means which gave relief and I returnd home.   at mr Lambarts. my Dagt very unwell.  
 

 

 February 3, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd Some. I have Bakt. Jessy Bullin Came here and Carrid Sally Cockss Chest away. Cyrus went to mill. Calld to See his Sist Lambart, finds her Some better. Ephm here ys morn, took Breakfast and took his Books to go to School.   at home.  
 

 

 February 4, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at home till Sun Sett, was then Calld to go and See mrs Dickman. Doct Williams there. I Sett up all night, mrs mc Master & mr Waid allso. the Patient is very Sick indeed.   at mr Dickmans, his wife Sick.  
 

 

 February 7, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Mr Rices Dagt and 5th Child. XX.

Clear and not very Cold. I Sett out to go to meeting, went to the road where mr Timy Foster met me and took me in to his Sleigh and Conductd me to mr Rices at Winthrop. his wife was Deld of a Dagt at 1h 30m pm and I returnd home at Evn, Conducted by mr Moses Wood. am informd that mrs Dickman Expird last Evn at 7h.   at mr Rices at Winthrp. Birth 8th. receivd 9/ for my Service.  
 

 

 February 9, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. Mr Ballard, my Self and Cyrus attended funeral of the wife of mr Dickman. the Corps were brot in to the hous of worship where the Revd mr Stone Delivered a Discorse Sutable to the ocation, and afterwards made a prayer well adapted. the remains were Conducted to the Burying Ground of mr Mc Master and intered. mr Dickman, Bell, Polly mc Master and her Parents followed as Moarners. the Pall Bearers were Wm Pitt, J. Black, Capt Smith, Peter Jones, J. Child and mr Parker. after they proseeded to the grave, I retird to my Son Lamberts, there Spent the Evng. my Dagt has been not So well ys Day as Shee was yesterday.   I attended at the house of worship an prayers at the interment of the remains of mrss Dickman. we Met at 12 O Clock. Dagt Pollard Spent Evn at mr Lambarts.  
 

 

 February 25, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 3h morn to See the wife of Shubal Hinkley who is unwell. I tarried there thro ye Day. Shee had her women Calld aftern and her illneSs Came on regular at 11h Evn. we were there all night.   at mr Shubal Hinkleys.  
 

 

 February 26, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Shubal Hinkleys Son. XX.

Cloudy. mrs Hinkley was Safe Deld at 3h morn of a Son, her 3d Son and 4 Child by mr Hinkley. I returnd home after Breakfast, left my Patient Cleverly. a mr Allin here from Monmoth.   at Ditoes. Birth 9th. augs 14t Recd one Dollar.  
 

 

 February 28, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was unwell foren, attended public worship aftern. Revd mr Cofin of Brunswick performd. I Calld at Son Pollards. Patty is returnd from Winthrop, informs me that mrs Rice has been very Sick Since I left her.   at meeting and at Son Pollards.  
 

 

 March 1, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at home till 8h Evng when I was Calld to go to See Barsheba Cowen, wrode on hors Back to mr Burtuns, then in his Sleigh. mrs Burtun and Craigg here ys aftern. I found the patient not very Sick.   at Jabis Cowens. paid Nabby 12/4d. 
 

 

 March 2, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Snowd aftern. I tarried at mr Cowens all night. the women, part of them, went home. Barsha is better but her mother thot it fit for me to tarrie this night.   at Ditoes this Day & night.  
 

 

 March 3, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I returnd home from Cowens, left them all well. I Calld at Son Jonas, he and his wife, Moses Pollard, Son Lambart and yr wives are gone to Son Towns; went yesterday. I Calld at mr Peter Joness & mr Burtons.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 March 4, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Jobe Springers Dagt.

 Clear. mr Waid helpt us get wood. I was Calld at 8h Evn to See the wife of mr Jobe Springer, who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 10h of a Dagt, her Eighth Child and 5th Dagt. I left her Comfortable and returnd home about midnight.   at Capt Jobe Springers. Birth 10th. Son Jon a 33 years old ys Day.  
 

 

 March 8, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. mr Ballard is gone to Colo Howards. mr Saml Norcross Calld me to See his wife who is not So well as Shee Could wish. I gave her a portion of Dgn Bd and lefe Some for her to take if necesary. Doct Ward and Jessy Bullin here. Sary Neall and Suky Kennady Spent Evng here. Son Ephm Sleeps here.   at mr Saml Norcrosss. 
 

 

 March 9, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Capt Neys Son. recd 6/ Sept 7, 1797.

Clear and Cold. I was Calld at 3h morn to See the Lady of Capt Ney who was Deld about 5 of a Son, which wd 13_ lb. it Measured round ye Breast (after being Drest in thin Cloaths) 18_ inches. I left her and infant Cleverly after breakfast. Calld to See mrs Norcross, find her but feeble. mr Ballard went to ye hook, Cyrus been to Pittstn. Nabby washt and went to See her Sister, Phillip is gone to his Fathers. I was at mr Morses.   at Capt Neys and mr Norc ss. Birth 11th. John Jonston & his wife Sleep here.  
 

 

 March 12, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy Part of ye Day. mr Ballard has had a turn of Collic pain. Esq Cony Calld here, he informs me that mrss Brooks is very unwell. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 March 14, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Deryus Ballard here, mr Ballard paid him all that was his Due for his Service here. mr Ballard and I went to mr Thwings on a visit, we Calld at Son Lambarts and Pollards.   at mr Thwings, Son Pollards & Lambarts.  
 

 

 March 16, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A rainy aftern and Evng. I was Calld to See the wife of mr Moses Sewall, Shee being in Labour tho not very ill. Old Lady Dilingham was Calld the first of ye Evng. my Patients illness increast and ther Calld young mrss Dilingham at 4h morn, mrs Greely allso.   at mr Moses Sewalls.  
 

 

 March 17, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Moses Sewalls 2nd Son. XX.

Snowd. mrss Sewall was Deld (at 4h 45m morn) of a fine son before mrss Greely Came into ye Chamber. Shee and infant are very Cleverly. I left them at 9h and returnd. Left Old Lady Dilingham with her. mr Sewall gave me 10/6 as a reward.   at Ditoes. Birth 12th. receivd 10/6.  
 

 

 March 20, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mrs Welches Dagt. XX. I recd May 3, 1799 by weaving 14 & _ yds all wool Cloath.

Clear and Cold. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to meeting at the fort, Nabby to the hook. thee remains of the widdow Petingail were interd at 5h pm. I was Calld by mr Garish to See mrs Welch who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 7h Evn of a Dagt which Shee declared Said Garish was the father of. I left her as well as Could be expected and returnd home at 10, find mr Allin of Monmoth here.   at mrs Welches. Birth 13th. Funeral of the widdow Petingl. 
 

 

 March 22, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Kenneys Son. XX.

Cloudy. Nabby went to the hook with her Cousin. I washt my kitchen, was Calld at 9h 30m Evn to See the wife of James Kenny who was in Labour, and was Deld of a Son at 11h 30m Evng. I left my patients Cleverly and returnd home at 3h morn. mr Kenny gave me 6/7 for my Service.   at James Kenneys. Birth 14th. receivd 6/7.  
 

 

 March 28, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Bizar Benjamins Son.

Cloudy. I was Calld at 5h morn to See mrs Benjamin who was in Labour. Shee was Deld between Seven and Eight of a Son. I left them Comfortable and returnd as far as Son Lambarts at noon, tarried there till near night. Dagt Pollard and Patty Easty there. Ephm Came home with me.   at mr Benjamins. Birth 15th. at Son Lambarts aftern.  
 

 

 April 5, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 11h fornn to Wilm Chamberlains wife who is unwell.   at Wilm Chamberlains.  
 

 

 April 6, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at Ditoes, my patient is not very well. we find Shee has an inclination to Drink rhum. Shee Drank about one quart, her illness increast.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 7, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wilm Chamberlins Son. XX.

Clear. mrs Chamberlin was Deld at 3h morn of a Son, her first Child, and is Cleverly. I was Calld to mr James Catons at 10, his wife is not very well.   at Ditoes. Birth 16th. receivd 6/.  
 

 

 April 10, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd all Day. mrs Caton Calld her women and was very unwell all night.   at mr Catons.
