Hutchin's Note
12 November 1794
[numeral 141 written in what appears to be pencil with a circle around it.) 
[Received 
from 
State Dep't 
Nov. 24.1894
R.P.O.401310]
Sam.l Hodgdon
Quarter Master 
[undecipherable]
Sir
I am directed by the Department of War to apply to you for the Means of conveying the Baggage of invalids of my detachment to Pittsburgh. It is necessary that I should be furnished with two Waggons for that purpose one being insufficient as the invalids are numerous--you will therefore inform me by the Bearer whether I can have the Waggons by six o'clock tomorrow morning_ as I must make my report by this evening.
J. Hutchins
Lieut. in the Corps of Artillerists & Engineers
Nov 13th 1794
Sam'l Hodgdon
Quarter Master
