Charles Hyde Lieut. & Paymaster 1st S.L. [undecipherable] 1st Sub Legion   
John [undecipherable] 2nd Lieut     
The Clothier General will account for Fourteen Dragoon caps, Thirty four sergeant's hats, Thirty seven coats, Thirty seven vests, Seventy Eight shirts, Seventy Eight pairs of shoes, Seventy Eight pair of socks, Seventy Eight pairs of woolen overalls, Thirty seven stocks and clasps, Thirty seven blankets, and Seventy Eight Epaulets __ and fifteen Music's hats, fifteen coats fifteen vests, thirty shirts, thirty Pair Shoes, thirty Pair sock, thirty Pair Woolen overalls, fifteen stocks, fifteen blankets, and [undecipherable] epaulets __ also for Rank and File, three hundred and seventy five hats, Three hundred and sixty nine coats, three hundred and sixty nine vests, Eight hundred [undecipherable] shirts, Eight hundred and twelve pair of Shoes, Eight hundred and twelve Pair of socks, Eight hundred and twelve Pair of woolen overalls, three hundred and sixty nine stocks and clasps, and three hundred and sixty nine blankets_ and forty one Epaulets, for the use of the First Sub Legion, agreeably to the above Return __ Greeneville November 16th 1795
Anthony Wayne
