Philada Jany 17 1800
Dr Sir 
I have recd your letter of the 1st Inst. enclosing one for your son which I immediately forwarded to him, I have not seen him for some weeks, Mr. Allison was at the office a few days a goe and said he was much better than he had been for many months before.  
I am with esteem your obt Servt.
Wm Simmons
Colo Henly
