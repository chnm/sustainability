August 9 1800
Godlove Heiskell, Contrr
Frederickburg Virginia
Sir
Your account (transmitted to this Office by E Carrington) of whiskey & vinegar, furnished to Captn Brock's Detachment between 1 Jany & 30 April 1799 amounting to Drs. 19.92 has been admitted agreeably to the Certificate of Edward Carrington Esqr which sum you will receive from him upon granting him the necessary receipts.
I am &c W. S.
