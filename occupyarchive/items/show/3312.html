Title

Pirates of Wall Street - 22-Oct-2011 Occupy Wall Street NYC, NY

Description

A shirt purchased from a vendor. By this point in time there were daily vendors selling 'occupy' merchandise, much of it were cheap knock offs and similar but this was a good investment.

If you see pictures with the street vendor that was always parked closest to the corner, we could never get a picture of it, but in his window in marker he wrote something to the effect 'I was in Tahrir. Now I am here to support you'.

It is amazing the echo effect that occurred around the world, for the first time in my life the

Occupy Movement
