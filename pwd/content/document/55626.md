Alexr Gibson's Rect to S. Lewis

Recd 9th Decr
Received, Staunton November 21st 1796 of Samuel Lewis Senior, two hundred Dollars for the purpose of defraying the necessary expence of supplying and marching my recruits from Staunton to Knoxville, for which sum I acknowlege my self accountable for the due application thereof
Alexr Gibson Capt
U. S. Army
