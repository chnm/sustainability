Since American Civilization History is team-taught in conjunction with American Civilization English, we are able to assign many joint writing projects. My colleagues in American Civilization English were introduced to The Crucible CD-ROM at recent New Media Classroom regional workshop. They were so favorably impressed with the content of the CD-ROM that we have devised a joint project which requires that the students read the play, examine selected portions of the CD-ROM (including video clips of Arthur Miller discussing the play) and write a paper analyzing both the literary merits and historical accuracy of the play.
