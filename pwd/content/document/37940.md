To Major R Fenner
June 15th 1786
Copied
30696
Pay Office New York
June 15 1786
Sir,
By Col. Blount I send your order drawn by by the
Treasury on the receiver of the Continental 
[taxes for your state, in favor of the Officers of the]
Line agreeably to the list inclosed to you in my
Letter of the 10th Instant amounting to 13,367 34/90
Dols, the Receipts for which on your payment I wish you to transmit me to this office.
I am
Mr. Robert Fenner
