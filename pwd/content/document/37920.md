New York June 8th 1786

Sir

           A mistake is made in the printing of my Registers

Volume 2d page 22d from Number 26950 to 2700 viz: 26958 is entered as 26258 and as on Interest from Jany 1st 1784 whereas it ought to be 26958 on Interest from Decemr 1st 1783 and so on to 27000 which I wish you would be pleased to alter in your sets.

                                       JP

John Cochran Esq
