Major Contant Freeman 
                                 21st April, 1800
                             The 5th May
                                 59
                                Received 
                                 from 
                                State Dep't,
                               Nov. 24 1894
                               R.P.O. 401310
Fort Johnson,
                                                                  21st April, 1800

Dear Sir

The clothing which was sent by Capt. Jameson was received on the 18th with care.  I have been so much occupied that I have not had time to compare it with the invoice, but I shall do it as soon as possible and you shall be informed of the result.

By the reg work of Capt. [undecipherable] I enclose you a note respecting his company’s clothing.
								I am 
								 Dear Sir
								Your humble servant
								Conl. Freeman

                                                                May 1st  Reg Ast.

James  Hodgdon Esq. 
Sup. Ord. + Military States
