directs that the allowance to Leiu. Carmichael of $16. permonth for extraServices at Fort Mifflon & paid by Col. Mentges be admitted tothe C.r of the latter.

The Accountant of the War Department.
War Department, 5th Sept.r 1800.
Sir,
Col. Mentges was authorised by my Predecessor to appoint a fit person to give receipts, for the materials delivered at Fort Mifflin, to superintend the workmen and to take charge of the articles, for which Services the person so appointed was to receive a proper & reasonable allowance.
Col. Mentges informs me that he accordingly appointed Lieut.t Carmichael & paid him Sixteen Dollars per month for his Services: this allowance I consider proper & reasonable and request you to admit it to the Credit of Col.l Mentges in your Settlement of his accounts.

I am SirYrobServ.Sam.l Dexter
The Accountant of War Dep.t
