Knoxville 20 April 1800
David Henley
need ---- 18th
Au ssame day
William Simmons esquire
Accounts
Department of War
Knoxville 2 April 1800
Sir
Agreeably to your letter of the 27 January. I have those men two hundred and eighty eight dollars for arreanges of May. 
Forward at this time Copy of the entry of my books for the month of February 1000-
and am with respect
Your [undecipherable] & Servt:
David Henley
William Simmons esquire [undecipherable] War
