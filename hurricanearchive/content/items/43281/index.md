I walked those few blocks in waist deep water, and carrying my one bag I had brought to the hotel with me, over my head. Later, I found out that my grandparents had lost their entire house, and everything I had in that bag was everything I had in the world.\r\nThe bus took everyone to Baton Rouge, and allowed us to sleep in the lobby of a hotel for the night.
