Treasury Department
8th. March 1790
Sir
I wish you to inform me when Samuel Armstrong Paymaster to the eighth Massachusetts regiment received the Monies upon which a balance remains due from him.
I am, Sir,
Your obedt. Servt.
A Hamilton
Joseph Howell Esquire
