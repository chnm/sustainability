Office Army Accounts

New York

December 18th 1786

Sir

I find that you stand charged by both Henry B Livington with the following sums received by you for recruiting which I cannot find have been acounted for.

Paid you February 15th 1777 100 Paid you February 28th 1777 or 78 110

                                       210 Dollars

I am (undecipherable) Pierce

Cap Leonard Blecher
