 December 17, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charls Gills Second Daugt. XX.

Clear, Pleasant day. mr Waid workt here. I did my washing, Sally Did the house work. Cyrus Conducted me to See mr Dingley and famely. I find him poorly, his wife better. Hannah unwell. mr Ballard Came there for me at 8h Evn. mr Gill Came in Soon after him for me to go and See his wife who is in Labour and was Safe Delivered of a Daugt between 10 and 11h Evn. Old Lady Brooks, mrss Capin and Dagt Lambard were assistants, they all returnd from there. I tarried all night.   at mr Dingleys and at mr Gills. Birth 34th. it Snowd at Evn. receivd 6/ and _ lb hyson Tea for my Service.  
 

 

 December 18, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Gill went for mrss Conry to Nurs his wife. I left my Patients Cleverly and Came home at 11h 30m foren. mr Capin and Lambard with their wivs Dind with us. Cyrus went to winthrop for our Cloath, but it was not finisht. I was Calld at 11h Evn to mr Asa Williamss wife who was in Labour. I Slept a little.   at mr Gills. mr Capin & Lambard & wives Dind here.  
 

 

 December 19, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Asa Williams Dagt. XX.

Cloudy and Some Snow. mrs Williams was Safe Deld at 3h pm of her 6th Dagt and 7th Child. I left her Cleverly and Came to Son Jonas where I tarried all night. he has been to our house with all his Childn, left Jack and brot the others home. this is ye Aniversary of my mariage, 44 yrs.   at mr Asa Williamss. Birth 35th. I Slept at my Sons.  
 

 

 December 20, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Cloudy part of the day. Son Jona Conducted me home. I Calld at Shubal Pills, find his wife washing. mr Waid Choping wood for us. Capt Jones Sleeps here.   at my Sons and mr Pittss. 
 

 

 December 23, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I was Calld at 6h ys morn to See the wife of Wilm Sprage who was not So well as Shee Could wish. I was there all day, Shee is some better.   at Wilm Sprages.  
 

 

 December 30, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Jonas 4th Son.

A Severe Snow Storm. I was at my Sons, his wife had her women Calld at 9h Evn, viz, mrss Titcomb, Andrews and Kimbal and was Safe Deld at the 11th hour of her 4th son and 5th Child. I took the Care of them thro the night.   at Ditoes. Birth 36th. 
 

 

 December 31, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the Day. Son Jona went for a Nurs to Jason Pierces. mr Ballard Came there and Conducted me home. Son Lambard & wife returnd from Son Pollards. Jack Ballard is at our house.   at Ditoes & at Son Lambards. Cyrus Deld me a Goun which Abel Chase left with him for me.  
 

 

 January 1, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, Snowd at Evn. Dagt Lambd and Childn and Becky Sewall Came here this morn, they made me a winter Goun. Sons Lambard and Ephm Came at Evn and Conducted them home. I was Calld at 11h 30m to See the wife of Shubael Pitts who was in Labour.   at home till after 11 O Clock at night. Dagt Lambd made me a goun, Becky here with her.  
 

 

 January 2, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Shubael Pittss 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear and Cold. mrss Pitts was Deld at 45m afler noon of her 2nd Dagt. I left her and infant Cleverly at Sun Sett and went home with Son Jona, his wife has had an ill turn and is Some more Comfortable. I tarried there all night. it is Cold.   at mr Shubael Pittses. Birth 1st. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 January 3, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd Some. I Came from my Sons with mr Ballard, L faett with us; left my Dagt better. we Calld at mr Hamlins Store, bot a pair Specktacles, price 4/6; _ yd Cambric 3/9; 3 yds quallity /6; thread /4_; Biskit /6; plates 3/6. we calld to see mrs Pitts, find her Cleverly. we Dind at Son Lambards on roast Turkey, his Brother Truman and wife there. Son Ephm Sleeps here.   at Son Jonas & others.  
 

 

 January 5, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. I was Calld in hast to Son Jonas, his oldest Son had drank Spirrit and was apparently dead. we immersed him in warm water and put down oil. Doct Cony was Calld and used Some means, and he recovered thro the Goodness of God. I tarried all night.   at Son Jon as. Jack was very Drunk.  
 

 

 January 6, 1799, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. mr Ballard Came to our Sons, Jack is better but Complained of his throat. I was Calld from there to Son Lambds, his Babe unwell but got better and they Conducted me home. Cyrus and Sally to Singing School.   at Son Jona & Lambds.  
 

 

 January 28, 1799, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Abm Devenports 2nd Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld by mr Abm Devenpt at 3h morn to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was very ill but thro Gods goodness was Safe Deld between the hours of 8 & 9 of her Second Son and Child. I left them Comfortable and arivd at home at 11h 30m.   at mr Devenports. Birth 2nd. receivd 9/ July 11th, 99.  
 

 

 January 29, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Andw Goodins Son. XX.

Clear part of the Day. I was Calld at 4h Pm to go and See the wife of mr Andw Goodin who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 9h Evn of a fine Son, her Second Child. widow Davis and I tarried all night and took Care of the patient.   at mr Andw Goodins. Birth 3d. recd 12/ as a rewd.  
 

 

 January 30, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd a little ys morn. I left mr Goodins at 11h morn, my Patient been up and had her bed made and Seemd Cleverly. Watson returnd from Sidney last night. Cyrus is Breaking flax, he began yesterday. mr Ballard is gone to mill. Allin Lambd Sleeps here, his Dad gn to Son Pollards.   at mr Goodins. mr Ballard receivd 5 Bushels Corn for Surveying a road in Sidney.  
 

 

 February 3, 1799, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd foren. the men and Sally went to meeting. I went aftern. windy and the road filld So that it was Some difucult giting thro. Cyrus went at Evng and brot Sally fm Singing. we Calld at Son Lambards, his Father there.   at Meeting & at Son Lambds. [Lain.]

 Clear and pleast. Doct Coney Came here and brot Elipt Robbins here to be taken Care of, he seems to have Some feaver at night. Sally unwell and I Sett up with Robbins till after midnight. Old mr [ Dear] Townsend here.   at home. Robbins Came here to be Nurst.

 Clear and windy. Sally & I tarried from worship. Shee went to Singing at Evn, returnd at 10h 30m. I was Calld at 11h Evn to mr Roland Smiths, his wife unwell. I went to bed and had Some rest.   at [Roland] Smiths. Son & Dagt Lambard, his Brother & [   ] here at Evn.  
 

 

 February 11, 1799, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Roland Smiths Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was at mr Smiths till Evn when I was Calld to Capt Littlefields, walkt and was much fatagud. was Calld from there to mrss Smith again, and Shee was Safe Deld at 10h of her first Dagt and 3d Child and is Cleverly. I tarried there all night, had the Cramp. mrss Cleark & Meloy tarried allso.   at mr Smiths & [Littlefields]. Birth [4th]. recd 15/ [of] mr Smith June 17, '99.  
 

 

 February 12, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I Came home at 7h morn, left my Patients Cleverly. mr Smith is gone to Varsalboro for Sally Brown to Nurs his wife. I find Eliphalet as well as Can be Expected.   at mr Smiths.  
 

 

 February 13, 1799, Wednesday

 Clear. I have been at home till Evn when Calld to Capt Littlefields again, find his Lady not very well. I was there all night, Slept with the patient. Shee had but little rest.   at Capt Littlefields. mrs [Dickman] Deld by [Doct] Cony, mrs Churchs by mrs Mosier.  
 

 

 February 14, 1799, Thursday

 Clear. I was with my Patient all Day and night. Shee had rest this night.   at [Ditoes].  
 

 

 February 15, 1799, Friday

 Clear. I took Breakfast and left the Patient at work, and Cleverly, and returnd home at 9h m. Doct Cony Came and drest Etiphalet foren. he and Lady and youngest Dagt Spent part of ye aftern & Evn with us.   at Ditoes. Doct Cony and Lady here, Sally to Singing [at] Evng.  
 

 

 February 19, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Rhubin Branards Son. XX.

Clear and Cool at 1h this morn when I was Calld by mr Wood to go and See the wife of Rhubin Branard who was in Labour. I arivd there at 3h m, found they had Calld Old Lady Whiting. there were Some obstructions to remove. when Performd the patient was Safe Deld of her 3d Son at 5h morn. I left her and inft as well as Could be Expected and arivd at home at 10h morn. I receivd 9/ as a reward. I find Eliphalet Cleverly. mr Ballard went to Son Jonas, Cyrus is Dresing flax.   at [Rhubin Branards] [   ]. [Birth 5th]. [recd] 9/ [as a rewd].
