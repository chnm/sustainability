John Roberts 2 Certifs.
Issued to Moses White
9 30
76 --
85..30
New York, June 14 1786 rd from Jno Peirce My two Certificates
of his dated March 1 1784 in favor of Jno Roberts
and signed by Jno Peirce Comiss - one of which is numbered
37 377 for seventy six Dols & on Interest from
Jan 1 1782. The other numbered 37 577 for nine Dols &
30/90 and on Interest from Jany 1 1783 - being the amount
of the arrears found due sd Roberts as a soldier in
Colo Hazens Regiment- rd by virtue of being appointed 
Executor in his last will & Testament,
an which was approved of and Letters of administration 
on which granted to me by B.
Greenleaf Judge of Probate for the County of
Essex in the state of Massachusetts, residence
of which I have this day produced to sd Pierce
Moses White Exectr
