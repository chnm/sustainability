  Captain Mercer, with their companies, for the Rapids.  Surveyors have requested military escorts. Reason for assembling troops on Rapids of Ohio is to prevent Indians from attacking settlements.  Western
  Indians difficult to reconcile because they are adverse to idea of their lands being forfeited from their adherence to the King of Great Britain.
