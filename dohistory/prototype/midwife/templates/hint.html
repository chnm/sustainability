HINT PAUSE SAMPLE


Hints have not been implemented for this sample.

Hints will be available at the start of tasks, as well as at dead ends. Different levels of hints will be given according to the user's experience level, so that, for instance, a novice would get more help than someone with research experience. (Users will be asked about their level of experience, among other things, when they first log on to play the game.)

Where appropriate, hints will come in the form of anecdotal stories and advice from Laurel Ulrich and the filmmakers.

In the final game, players will earn points by answering the supplementary questions that appear alongside primary documents. They will then trade those points for hints throughout the game. In this sample, submission of answers, points, and hints have not been implemented.
