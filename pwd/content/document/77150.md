Mr John Harris 
Present 
Intendants Office 
Nov 7 1800
12 Bayonets to be Deld
Jones & Kinsey 
Deld 
recd inside in the order
Philadelphia 7th November 1800
Sir - 
You will be pleased to deliver twelve Bayonets to Messrs Jones & Kinsey and the same number to Martin & Holmes to enable them to fit the Bayonet Scabbard contractor for exactly to the bayonet which they are intended to cover - It will be best to take a lone receipt for them impressing that they are to be returned at an early period in the same order as they are received.  
I am Sir, Your Most Obedient Servant, 
Samuel Hodgdon

Mr. John Harris
