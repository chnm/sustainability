War Department
John Harris
Sir   January 1 1801
Your letter of the 26th Ulto has been received in consequence of which I have directed the Treasurer of the United States to remit to you the sum of Two hundred and thirty Dollars and nine Cents being for your own and Clerk's Salary for Services rendered in the Indian Department from 1st Oct to 31st December 1800 inclusive and for portage of Letters from Georgia & Fellico Factories agreeably to your statement.
I am Sir etc
