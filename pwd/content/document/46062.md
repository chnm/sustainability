Pittsburgh 29th Feby 1794
Sir 
By this days post I received Your Packet, addressed to Maj Isaac Craig, encloseing letters for Major General Wayne and sundry Officers with the army &c.  
Capt Crawford is hourly expected, he arrived at Wheeling the 21st Inst. 
I am Sir Your most Obt Humbe Servant
Michl Jones
Honl 
Majr Genl H. Knox 
Secretary of War
