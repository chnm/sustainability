I have seen news in the media and newspaper about Katrina.  It was just amazing but it\'s sad how people have lost their homes and love ones during Katrina.  From where I came from, I haven\'t seen anything or hear anything like it before.  I was born in Loas but raise in American so I did hear about it and seen pictures of hurricane but never seen one.  When they show how Katrina had flooded people homes and streets, I thought how terrible it was and how the children going to survive such a disaster.  I somehow feels helpless that I couldn\'t help and seening children die because there wasn\'t enough food to eat, just made me think of all the food I have and wasted.  It is sad that a lot of people lost their life and love one and I pray for them everynight when I go to sleep.  I just pray that God will bless American and everyone else. God Bless.
