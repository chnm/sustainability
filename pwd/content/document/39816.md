West Point April 12, 1789
Sir
Agreeable to your letter of 25th Ultimo, I have delivered Messrs Jonathan and Abiathar Ingells Forty five Tons of damaged cannon and Shot, agreeable to the enclosed acct--
I am Sir [symbol for etc.]
The Honorable Major General Knox Secretary at War --
