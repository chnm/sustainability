Private / 
James Mc. Henry Esq
Camp Scotch Plains
May 23. 1800
Sir
The letter with the inclosed came to hand the day preceding my leaving the city for this place. The hurry of the first moments have prevented my Sending it Sooner.
Oh mad! mad! mad!
Yrs Affecty
AH
