 April 17, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Lambards 3d Son.

Clear and Cool. I was Calld at 3h morn by Son Lambard to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe deld at 5 of her 3d Son and 4 Child, and is as well as Could be Expected. Son Jona and his wife Calld there.   at Son Lambds. Birth 10th.  
 

 

 April 22, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have workt in my Gardin, Sowd Peas. mr Ballard went to Hallowell, Cyrus to Son Lambard s; they are Cleverly there. he Conducted Jonas wife home. mrs Welch been here to Swingle her flax, her Childn with her.   at home, I Sowd Peas.  
 

 

 April 24, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Dexters Dagt.

 Clear. I have been very unwell but went to See Son Jonas infant, it is unwell with a Cold. I was Calld at Evn to mr Dexters wife, Shee was Deld of a Dagt by Doct Cony before I arivd. I went to mr Shubal Pittss and Slept with Sister Barton.   at my Sons, mr Dexters & Pittss. 11th Case.  
 

 

 April 28, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, pleast foren, Cool aftern. mr Ballard and Betsy attended funeral of mr Rheads Child. Revd mr Stone Calld & took Tea with us.   at home. mr Georg Rheeds Child interd.  
 

 

 May 1, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was very ill with pain, Jonas wife Came to take Care of me before day. Dagt Pollard & Son Lambard and Sist Barton Came to See me. I got Eas from pain but feel very feeble, my Sist tarries all night.   at home, unwell.  
 

 

 May 16, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, done my hous work. Sister Barton assisted me. Shee mended a Coat, ristbanded a Shirt and mended Breaches for my husband. I made a Chees to day, Ephm returnd from weding. Saml Trask Sleeps here.   at home. I am informd of ye death of one of Bror Jonas sone at Sea.  
 

 

 May 17, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to meeting. Jona, his wife and Dagt went to the North Parrish to hear Jotm Sewall. Son & Dagt Lambard Calld here on their way home. Allin went home with them. Luke Barton Sleeps here. mr Ballard has Seen 76 years this day. Birth day.   at home. Son & Dagt Lambd went home.  
 

 

 May 18, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard Sett out at 11h morn to go and assist Wm Brooks & Epm Town aprise and [Divide] 2000 acrs of tand which lies between this [and] penobscott river to 7 heirs of the late Doct Geneson. Bror & Sister Barton and yr Son Luke left here after Dinner to go to hous keeping at mr Crages Shop. I wish them prosperity. Cyrus and Ephm have been makeing fence. I have been to Son Jonas with my Sister as Shee wint down.   at Son Jonas. Doct Barton, his wife & Luke went from here to hous keeping.  
 

 

 May 22, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Ballard relurnd, Son Lambd Calld here. I have been at home, did part of my hous work. Sally washt, Ephm workt at Ditoes.   at home.  
 

 

 May 24, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to meeting and was Calld out by mr Cottle to go and see his wife who aprehended her Self near the hour of Labour. I found mrss Tyler with her.   at the meeting hous and at mr Cottles.  
 

 

 June 4, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Cottles 15 Child and 6th Son. XX.

Clear. mrss Cottle was Safe Deld at 11h morn of her 15th Child & 6th Son. I left her Comfortable and returnd home. I Calld at Son Lambards, find my Dagt very unwell. [Dagt] Pollard was with her. mrs Chamberlain Calld me in to See her Child which Seems very low. I have spent from may 24th till this day at mr Cottles. I find my hous in desent order at my arival and that my famely are all well.   at mr Cottles. Birth 12th. recd 1 Busl Corn, _do rie, 1 Crown. Wilm Hewen Expired.  
 

 

 June 5, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. rainy. Son Lambd Came with News yt his wife was very ill, Sally went to Nurs her. I have spent the day alone, feel Gloomy. Son & Dagt Town Came here at Evng. I feel very much falagued indeed. Ezra Sleeps here allso. mr Cottle Calld and left me _ Bsl Corn, Says his wife is Comfortable.   at home, my friends here at Evn.  
 

 

 June 21, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and the longest day of ye year. I have again had the happyness to wait on God in his hous, and been Entertained with two agreable discoarses Deld by mr Parker from Dresden. mr Ballard and I spent intermition at Brother Bartons. Son & Dagt Ballard, with 5 of yr Childn, took Supper with us, Our Nephew EbenZ Moore allso.   at meeting. mr Parker Performd.  
 

 

 June 24, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Soals 2 Dagts. XX.

Cloudy foren, raind before night. I was Calld to see mrs Soal at 7h morn, Shee was Deld of a Daugl at 11h 10m, and 5m after of another daugt. Shee and infts likely to do well. I relurnd at 4h am.   at mr Soals. Birth 13th 14th. recd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 June 26, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. mrs Chamberlain went to mr Woodwards foren, to my Sons aftern. I have workt in my gardin, Sett Cabbages and Cucumbers. mr Dingley Calld at Evn.   at home. mrs Chamberln Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 30, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to my Sons this morn to See his Oldest Son who is very ill with pain like a Collic. we administred a Clister and gave him ye decoction of ye flowers of London pride, and he got releivd about noon; and I Came home at 2h pm. I workt in my Gardin and went again towards Evn, find him Comfortable. mr Forbes and wife Came home with me.   at my Sons, Jack is Sick. mr Forbes & wife Sleep here. Rhoda Pollard is 7 years old.  
 

 

 July 1, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Clear and warm Aftern. mr Forbes and wife Sett out for home. Hannah Coole, mr Burtun & A Daughtr of mgr Pains have Calld here. I have Cleand my Sellar, made a Chees, houghd Beens &C, and knit Some.   at home. Wm Stone had a Son Born, mrs Mosier assisted.  
 

 

 July 11, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I houghd in ye Gardn and Shelled peas till 11h morn when I was Sudendly alarmd with news that my Dagt Lambd, in attempting to Come and visit me, was thron from her hors with her dear infant, and much hurt. I imediately Sett out to go and See them, was overtaken by Doct Cony who was Calld to their relief. I found, on my arival, that my Dagt was not So much hurt as was feared; the infts head was very much Sweld. thee Doct Examened it and Bathd it with Camphr. he allso lett Blood and it revivd so that I left them and returnd home near night. find Doct Barton, Gill, Sons Pollard, Jona and their wivis and Childn, who with Son Lambard and Dolly Kymbal, took Tea with us. Blesed by the Great Author of Mercys that the lives of my Childn were Spared.   at Son Lambds. A mercyfull preservation took place when in Emenent Danger.  
 

 

 July 14, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till near night when we had a Shower. Sally Selvester left me. I feel very unfit to do without help. mrs Dingley Came to see me aftern. Shee made me a prest of A qt Spirit and Some hyson Tea. Caty Pitt, Nabby Page, Polly Cowen & Lucy Stone Calld in when the Shower.   at home. mrs Dingly here. Sally left me, we paid her all her wages.  
 

 

 July 15, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. Cyrus went to take the hors home that Sally wrode. I have been at home, done my work; feel very unwell. I went to See Jonas Son Ephm who is unwell. mrs Pierce Calld here aftern.   at my Sons. Ephm is unwell.  
 

 

 July 16, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, rain with thunder aftern. I have been very unwell ys day but have my work to do. mrs Farewell Calld here. Capt Sanford here on Business.   at home. Bror Barton Calld here.  
 

 

 July 19, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to hear mr Stone, Ephm to the N Parish. I have been at home. Hannah Braly Came at Evng to assist me about work.   at home. Hannah Braly Came here.  
 

 

 July 24, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. I have done the most of my hous work. Hannah Spun, pickt wool, ironed and helpt some about hous. mrs Farewel Calld here. mr Ballard returned from Surveying. Jona is better. Ephm helpt Cyrus rake hay this aflern.   At home. mr Ballard relurnd.  
 

 

 July 31, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Saml Jacksons wife Calld here. Son Lambd dind, he informs me his Sister Filbrook is very low. Phebe Runels & Polly Cypher took Tea here. Hannah Braly went away with them. I paid her 6/11_ for her Service here.   at home. Stutely Springers wife Deld of a Dagt by mrs Mosier yesterday  
 

 

 August 3, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, Cool morn. I have been at home, done my hous work tho very unable; was Calld to my Sons to See Wilm, he is very ill. mr Ballard down in Town, receivd the tax Bills. Saml Bralys wife here to Tea.   at my sons. Wm unwell.
