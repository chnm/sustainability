Estimate of Money wanting for furnishing the following Military Supplies Ordered to the State of Georgia
To pay for Repairing and Cleaning 1000 Stand of Arms - @ 2/9d } 137.10
40 Arm Chests for Ditto - @10/ - 20.0.0
5000 lbs: Lead - @500/$1000 - 125.-.-
25 Barrels for Powder - @3/9 - 4.13.9
25 flannel Bags for Ditto @ 5 - 6.5.-
750 Powder Horns - @5/ - 187.10
750 Straps for Powder Horns - @ 1/ - 37.10
[total] 518.8.9
Philada 9th March 1793 - Equal in Dollars to 1382.49/100
