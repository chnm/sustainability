 May 13, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard preparing for planting. mr James Page Dind here. old Lady Coutch Calld, Ephm helpt her over ye river. I have been at home. my Girls washt & went to mr Densmores. Cyrus went to Jon as.   at home. Dolly went to mr Densmors. 
 

 

 May 14, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Cumings Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard had his flax Sowd. I was Calld to Saml Cumings to See his wife. Shee was Deld at 2h pm of a Daugt, her 3d Child. I Calld to See mrs Child, find her Seting up. I arivd at home about Dusk, walkt from mr Childs.   at Saml Cumingss. Birth 25th. Dolly at mr Danys. receivd 6/ of mr Cumings July 13.  
 

 

 May 15, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Dolly went to mr Danys to work, mr Ballard to Survey for Colo North. I went to take my leave of mr Dany & wife who left ys town and are going to New town. I went to [Godlin] Pages & paid him 1:18:8 which mr Ballard owd him. I was at other Stores.   at mr Danys & at the Stores. Elisha Preskott paid me 6/ for assisting his wife Octr 27th. 
 

 

 May 21, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I finisht Cleaning my Door yard, went to See mrss Hamlin, find her much fatagud with her Joirney. I Calld at mr Baxters. find mr Ballard was returnd when I arivd at home. mrs Densmore here.   at mr Hamlins & mr Baxters.  
 

 

 June 1, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Bulah Dagtr. XX. X. Birth Joseph Browns Dagtr. XX.

Cloudy, Some lite Showers. I was Calld at 3h ys morn to See Bulah at mr Cumings. Shee was deld at 4 of a Dagt. from there I went to mr Joseph Browns to See his Lady who was Safe delivd at 2h pm of a Dagt. I tarried till 4h when his Son Conducted me to mr Shepard by water, from there I took passage with Josph Preskott to mr Daws. walkt from there home, feel very much fatagud. I receivd 6/ of mrs Brown. her Husband is gone Down ye river.   at mr Cumings. Birth 26th. Birth 27 th. 
 

 

 June 3, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Brother Davis left here this morn home ward bound. Dolly went to work for mr Densmore, Cyrus to Jon as. Ephm to mill to Pittstn. mr Ballard been to the hook. I have been at home. Son Town Calld here.   at home. Son Town Calld here.  
 

 

 June 4, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Mrs Barton Calld here on her way to Winslow. I paid her 6/ in part for her labour for me. John Stackpool with her. Patty Goodn, Fanny Cox & Dolly Dind. Sally & P. Densmr & P. Livermr Took Tea. I went to help mrs Norcros quillt. mr Savage Came there to See his wife who has ye Collic. I tarried there ys night.   at mr Norcross & Savages. my Dagt had Compy. mr Ballard at Coart. mrs Barton went home.  
 

 

 June 5, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I returnd home at 4h morn, left mrs Savage Easy. I walkt and am mutch fatagud. Son Town Calld here. Mehitl Peirce to work for me, Shee Came last Evng. Ephm Slept at Jon as.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 June 7, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Child Came here for me to go & See his wife. I walkt there, find her Confind to her Bed. was Calld from there to Saml Norcross, his wife in Labour.   at mr Childs & Norcros.  
 

 

 June 8, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Norcross Dagt. XX.

Clear Except a lite Shower. mrs Norcross was Safe Delivd at the 11th h foren of a Daugt. I tarried with her till 8h Evn whin I left her & infant Some what Comfortable. I receivd 6/ as a reward. this is mrs Norcrss 8th Child, 4 of Each Sex.   at mr Norcross. Birth 28th. Dolly Came home from mr Densmrs.  
 

 

 June 9, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Child, mrss Pollard there. we helpt her out of Bed & Shee Sett in her Chair 2 hours. I administred a Clistar which opperated kindly & gave great relief. I Calld at mr Westons, Pitts & Son Pollards, find them all well.   at mr Childs. 
 

 

 June 11, 1793, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. mrs Oliv Hamlin here to have a goun made. Dolly returnd at 12 O Clok. I have been at home. mrs Densmr & Sewall Calld here.   at home. Oliv Hamlin here. mr Moores Son Drownd.  
 

 

 June 12, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I was Calld at 9h to See the wife of Henery mc Causland who is in Labour. I was there all night.   at Henry mc Causlands. [Jery] moore interd.  
 

 

 June 13, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Henry mc Causlands Son. XX.

Clear & warm. I was at mr mc Causlands, his wifes illness increast about 4h morn. her women were Calld and Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son at 6th h. I relurnd home at noon. recevd 6/ as a reward. I went to See mr Parkers [inft].   at Ditoes. Birth 29th.  
 

 

 June 20, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Greelys Dagt. XX.

raind finely this morn. I was Calld at 4h morn to See mrs Greely. Shee was Safe Delivd at 8 of a fine Dagt, her Eighth Child. I tarried till 8h Evng. Left her & infant very Cleverly. receivd 6/ as a reward.   at mr Greelys. Birth 30th.  
 

 

 June 23, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth James Hinklys Son. XX.

Clear foren. Sally Densmore Brot their infant to have a burn drest. I was Calld to See James Hinkleys ys 3d wife at 12 O Clock. Shee was Delivd at 11h Evn of a Son, her first Born. I tarried all night, my Patients are Cleverly.   at James Hinklys. Birth 31st 
 

 

 June 24, 1793, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I receivd 6/ for my Service, returnd home at 9h morn. Calld to See mr Parkers infant. Shee thinks it is better. mrs Coutch Dind here. I went as far as mr Shepperds. Bot 1 pair Shoes @ 4/4, three yds Black ribbon @ /6; at Doct Colmans bt 1/2 oz Camphr /8, Do myrr /7_, Do of alloes /4_, Viall & Sallts /6_, 3 oz Spermaseta /4_. at mr Dummers bt 2 muslin hand kercfs, 1/2 yd lawn 7/9. at mr Greelys, Shee is not so well as Could be wisht. her infant had fitts last Evng. at Saml Norcorss, Cleard her inft breasts of milk. Esqr Lithgow here at Evng, he wishes to See me at his house about a fortnit hence.   at Dittoes. at the Stores. Cyrus Sleps here. a Child killd by the fall of a tree at [sic].  
 

 

 June 27, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Thos Stikneys Son. XX.

Clear & warm. I was Calld to Thomas Stickneys wife in Labour at 8h morn. Shee was Safe Delivd at 10 of a Son, her first Born. I receivd 6/ as reward, left her Cleverly at 3 pm & returnd. Cyrus Sleeps here. Borrowed 13-3/4 lb veal of mr Densmr.   at Thos Stickneys. Birth 32nt. 
 

 

 June 29, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. mr Ballard at the fort & ye hook. I went to See mr Blaks infant, it has taken a Cold, Seems a little revivd. I Calld at mr Hamlins, Shee is feeble. at mr Childs, his wife much as Shee was. mr Beadle is very unwell. at mr Pitts. Dolly returnd from mr Bullins. Cyrus Sleeps here.   at mr Blaks & other Neighs. a man wounded in the woods, very badly.  
 

 

 June 30, 1793, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm indeed. mr famely attended worship at the hook. I, my Self, Did aftern. Calld to See mrs Greely who remains very feeble, her infant has a Soar mouth. Calld at mr Hinkleys, his wife is Cleverly. Jona & his little Son here when I Came home. Dolly wrode to mr Pollards. I went to See mrs Densmore, shee is not so well as Could be wisht.   at meeting at thee hook. Calld at mr Greelys.  
 

 

 July 3, 1793, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Neys Son. XX.

Clear & very warm. I was Calld at 8h morn to See Capt Neys Lady who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 of a very fine Son. mrs Cox, Cleark, Hinkly, Pilsbiry & Sherburn were assistants. we tarried till near Sun Sett when we left my Patients Cleverly. I receivd 6/ as a reward. mr Ballard is returnd. I went to See mrs Densmore, find her Easier.   at Capt Neys & mr Densmrs. Birth 33d. mrs Neys 9th Child.  
 

 

 July 4, 1793, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to Sidny. I have been at home. mrss Hinkley Calld here. Cyrus Sleeps here. I am informd that Hannah is Sick.   at home.  
 

 

 July 5, 1793, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. I went to Son Pollards, find my Dagt very unwell. Shee was more Comfortable when I left her at Evn. Dolly workt at mr Dumers. mrs Livermr Calld in here at Evn.   at Son Pollards. Cyrus Sleeps here.  
 

 

 July 6, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I was Calld to See mrs mc Neall at mr Kenedys. mrs Fairbank & Lambrt here. I went aftern to See mrs Greelys infan, it is better than it was. Dolly went to Son Pollards, Hannah is much Better.   at mr mc Neels & Greelys. Cyrus Sleeps here.  
 

 

 July 13, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn. two Gentlemen here bound to Sandy river, a Lady & little mrs allso. Saml Cumings rewarded me for assisting his wife may 14. mrs Densmore here foren. we had String Beans for Dinner. mr Ballard returnd at Evng.   at home. mr Ballard returnd, informd yt ye Cause of Andrews & my Son is Continued.
