### Please use the following citation format to cite particular documents in your own research and writing.

> “From [Sender] to [Recipient], [Date],” *Papers of the War Department*, *1784-1800*, Roy Rosenzweig Center for History and New Media, accessed [Date], [URL to item page]. Original Source: [collection, name of archive].

### Example Citation for "[In Support of Hodgdon](/s/home/item/79270)"

> “From Samuel Colesworthy to Samuel Hodgdon, 12 December 1792,” *Papers of the War Department*, *1784-1800*, Roy Rosenzweig Center for History and New Media, accessed April 23, 2019, [http://wardepartmentpapers.org/s/home/item/79270](/s/home/item/79270). Original Source: Post Revolutionary War Papers, RG94, National Archives and Records Administration.
