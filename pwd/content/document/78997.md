212
Knoxville February 26, 1800
Sir,
You will receive by Mr. Herbert
A Box Marked in yyour name Cont=
+aining the Vouchers for Mr. Heller's pay-
+ments as Paymaster to the Troops in
Tennessee Amounting to fifty five
Thousand eight hundred Dollars and
Ninety five cents, Which you will 
find accurate
I am with respect
Your Humble Servt
David Hurley
Agent of War
William Simmons esq
