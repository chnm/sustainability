 March 11, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold, the wether moderated at Evng. I was Calld at 11 Evn to See the wife of Benn White who is in Labour.   at Benjamn Whites.  
 

 

 March 12, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Bmn Whites Son & Sixth Child. XX.

Clear and Pleasant. I was with mrss White who was Deld at 3h morn of a fine Son, I left her as well as could be Expected, and returnd home at 7. I reicivd 9/ as a reward. the men went to meeting, I went to bed and Slept a fine nap. Son Town Came here at Evn, Sleeps here, his famely well.   at Bmn Whites. Birth 17. recivd 9/. Son Town Sleeps here.  
 

 

 March 14, 1797, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld at 2h morn to Doct Colmans to See his Lady. mrss Cutler is there, allso mrss Emerson & her Child there. I tarried all night, went to Bed and Slept till after midnight when my patient Seemd more unwell.   at Doct Colmans.  
 

 

 March 15, 1797, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Doctr Colmans Dagt. XX.

Clear and pleasant. mrss Colman had mrss Pollard Calld at 3h pm, Shee was Safe Deld at 4h 30m of a fine Dagt, her 5th Child. I Sett up with her this night. Shee and infant are Cleverly. mrss Cutler tarries there this night.   at Ditoes. Birth 18 th. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 March 16, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Chilly. I left my patients Cleverly and returnd home at 7 hours this morn, feel so much fatagud that I have not attempted to do much work. mr Ballard is gone to Survey for Freeman. Hinkley, Brother Moore and his wife Dind with me, they have been to pittstn.   at Ditoes. Came home at 9h morn.  
 

 

 March 19, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. Cyrus only of my famely attended worship foren, mr Ballard and my Self aftern. Huse left here this morn bound for monmoth. Polly Buterfield to Capt Williamss. mr Ballard, my Self & Cyrus partook of a fine Turkey at Son Lambarts after meeting. Bulah Calld here. Son & Dagt Pollard with their Childn were at Son Lambats.   at meeting and at Son Lambarts. Caleb Huse left us.  
 

 

 March 22, 1797, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Georg Thomass Son. XX.

Clear Day, Snowd at Evn. I was Calld at 5h pm to See the wife of Georg Thomas, Shee was Safe Deld at 7 of a Son which wd More than the lite side of mr Densmores Stilyards wd weigh. I left my patients as well as Could be Expected and reacht home at 10h 10m. mr Ballard returnd from monmoth this Day. Rheuben Moore here.   at mr Densmores. Birth 19 th. recd 2/10 [   ] march 30th. Cash 3/2_d  
 

 

 March 26, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the ice on the river Breaking up. there was no preaching at the fort. I was Calld at 11h foren to see mrs Pilsbury who is Sick with the rash, her husband Sick allso, I watcht and performd Services that were necessary. mr Waid Set with me. the Lady was in a fine persperation the most of the night, but Seemd very ill.   at mr Pillsburys, his wife is very Sick, I watcht. the river opend.  
 

 

 March 28, 1797, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd and raind Some. I Came from mr Burtuns, left James mending. Came to mr Pillsburys find her very ill, in a kind of Deliriam. her raising had ceast and her mouth very dry, they informd me Shee had been much So thro the night Past, it is my opinion the use of the Bark was in Some measure the Cause.   at Ditoes & Ditoes. mrs Pillsbury is very Sick indeed. I walcht, was in at ms Eades.  
 

 

 March 29, 1797, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. I tarried with mrs Bilsbury till near night and returnd home, left her very ill. old mrs Kenny Came and advised to giv her a Sirrip of vinigar & oinions and a Decoction of Gold thread and Shumake Birries, it was done and Shee Seemd revivd.   at Pilsburies.  
 

 

 April 1, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 5h morn to See the wife of Wilm Briggs, who was Exercised with pains, I made use of means which Seemd to relieve her and by their request I tarried all night.   at Wm Briggss, his wife unwell.  
 

 

 April 2, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Briggs Seems more Comfortable, Son Cyrus Came there to Call me home as Son Town was after me to go and See my Dagt. I returnd home and Sett out for Son Towns between 9 & 10h morn, we reacht there about 5 pm, find his famely well as usual.   at Ditoes mr Capins & Son Towns. mrs Copeland Deld of a Dagt this Evng by Piohon.  
 

 

 April 4, 1797, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. Capt Stackpols Lady Came to Son Towns to visit. my Dagt is well. I feel Cleverly.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 6, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am at my Sons, my Dagt is not So well. mrss Haill, Spring and Stratin were Calld and tarried this night with us.   at Ditoes, my Dagt unwell at night.  
 

 

 April 7, 1797, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mrs Spring went to mr Fullers, Calld to See us aftern, mrs Cragen with her. my Dagt is better and they all returnd home. Son Jona took Breakfast there.   at Ditoes, my Dagt is better.  
 

 

 April 8, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy Snowd at night. my Dagt felt her Self so ill that Shee had mrss Cragen, Haild and Fuller Calld. we had a Severe Snow Storm before morn.   at Ditoes. my Dagt unwell again, women were Calld.  
 

 

 April 13, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Son Towns Dagt & 11th [Child]. XX.

Clear. Son Town Calld me out of bed at 1h 5m Morn, I find my Dagt very ill, not able to be removd from bed, Shee was Dild of a Dagt and all performd which her labour required at 1h 20m. no woman arivd till after all was performd but Dresing the infant. this her Eleventh Child, Shee is more Comfortable than I Ever Saw her.   at Ditoes. Birth 20 th. I have been Some unwell this Day.  
 

 

 April 15, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Pleasant. my Dagt had an ill turn this morn. I administrd a Clister which relievd her, Shee Seemd willing I Should leav her and I Sett out for home at 9h morn. we Calld at mr Thomass and at Capt Smiths. we Crost the ferry in this Town at 2h 30m. I was Calld in at mr Hamlins to See his youngest Dagt who has the rash, I hope it is not Dangerous. was informd there that mrss Briggs has been Deld of twins sinc I left her and that they are Dead, and that the wife of Shubal Pitts was Deld of a Dagt this Day. I Calld to See her, find her as well as Could be Expected.   at Ditoes & Capt Smiths, Hamlins & Shubal Pitses, Birth his Dagt at 9h morn. I reacht home and find my famely well.  
 

 

 April 16, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Town Sett out for home. I was at home foren. Cyrus Came up from Pitts tn. Augustus Ballard was here and Dind. I went to meeting aftern, was Calld to See two of mr Hamlins Childn who are Sick. Son Pollard has been unwell but is better. I was at mr Blacks, his youngest Child is unwell.   at meeting, mr Gittil Performd. I was at mr Hamlins & Blaks. Son Ephm & Ebenz Cole here.  
 

 

 April 20, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Atkinss Son & [3d] Child. XX.

rainy. I was Calld at 1h morn to go to mr Atkinss his wife being in Labour and was Deld at 4h morn of a fine Son which wd 9_ lb. I left her and inft Cleverly and returnd home at 11h foren. I receivd 12/ as a reward.   at mr Atkinss. Birth 21st. receivd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 May 1, 1797, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Phelpss Dagt. XX.

Cloudy foren, Clear part of ye aftern, raind at Evn. I was Calld at 5h morn to See the wife of mr Phelps, Shee was in Labour and was Deld at 10 of a fine Dagt. I Calld in to See mrss Guild, find her and famely well. I was at mr Lords. I returnd home aftern, Sowd Lettis, peper, S grass, radish & Savory Seeds.   at mr Phelpss. Birth 22nt. Septr [11] receivd 9/ of mr Phelps.  
 

 

 May 4, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn, Clear aftern. we all attended worship at the fort. the revd mr Stone Discoarst from Isaah 66 C 2nt vers. my Dagt Lambart had a faint turn in the time of Service. Shee did not attend the aftern. I was at Son Pollards at intermision. Rhoda Seemd very unwell. I Calld after Evng Service, find her better. at Son Lambarts, Shee is more Comfortable. Hannah Fletcher Dind here, a mr Harford allso.   at meeting and Sons Pollards & Lambarts.  
 

 

 May 14, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I attended worship afternoon. Calld after meeting at Shubal Pittss, Sons Pollards & Lambarts. Sally Williams Sleeps here, Cyrus allso. EbenZ Coal here.   at meeting and at my Sons Pollard and Lambart.

 May 21, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Capt Husseys 4th Son.

rainy morn. I was Calld to See the wife of Capt Saml Hussey, found her Deld of a fourth Son by the help of mrss Cocx. I tarried till about Sun rise when I was Conducted home by water. mr Ballard went and redeemd his watch for one Dollar. he and Hoiet & Polly went to meeting foren, Cyrus & Sally aflern. mr Ballard and I went to mr Dingleys to See mrss Haden who is not So well as Shee Could wish. Son Jona here while we were there.   at Capt Husseys. Birth 23d to which I have been Calld.
