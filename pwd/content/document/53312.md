New York April 5th. 1796

Samuel Hodgdon. Esqr  

               Sir.

                  We received yours of the
      19th.March_The ship Boxes have been

      West Point.

                   We are
                       Sir,
                       Your Obed. Servt.
                       James Manning & Co
