New York, January 9th 1787

Sir,

In the settlement made with you for the monies you received as Paymaster to Colonel Spencers Regiment there was omitted the following sums. I am therefore to request that you will be pleased to inform me of the manners you are to expect your discharge from them __
1777 March 	Paid you for recruiting 	800
1779 Decr 	Paid you for the pay of Formans Regiment for April and May 	1.20550
1777 	Paid you by Cols. Malcom for the recruiting service 	206
[blank] 	[blank] 	Dolls. 2.211 50/90

I am Sir

J. Pierce

Capt. Jn.o Santford
