Extract of a letter from the Secretary of War to Mr. Dayton 
July 25 1792
I have received your favor of the 23d inst: "Please to direct Mr. Williamson to ship the saddles for this place, & directed to Samuel Hodgdon Commissary of military stores; and upon the receipt thereof, and of his account, it shall be paid to her order in this city"
[Marginalia: 194 in pencil}
[Stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24, 1894. R.P.O. 401810
