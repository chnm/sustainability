 February 14, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 at Ditoes. mr Pollard Came home, left his Load at fort hallifax.   at Ditoes. we could not git any milk from my Dagt breast. 
 

 

 February 15, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was at Ditoes. Gidn Braley got yellow lilly roots to apply to mrs Pollard Breast, it Seemd to giv her more pain.   at Ditoes. my Dagt is no better.  
 

 

 February 16, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Hannah Seems Some Easier. I went after noon to Son Lambds and returnd at Evng. my Dagt had an ill night, the infant very wrestless.   at Sons Pollards and Lambds.  
 

 

 February 17, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 at Son Pollards. Doct Howard Calld, thought the Breast not fitt to open. my Dagt had an ill night.   at Son Pollards.  
 

 

 February 18, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Pollard Calld Doct Colman to open his wives breast, it had a Copeous discharg and Shee Seems more comfortable. the infant is Cleverly. mrss Black came up with her Brother.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 19, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard came to son Pollards and I Came home, left my Dagt Low but as well as could be Expected. there was a great discharg from her Breast this morn. we Calld at Son Lambards, they are well as usual. am informd that mr Allin is interd this aftern. we Calld at Son Jonas, his son wilm is Sick. the Revd mr Joseph Boman, who was the Pastor we Satt under at OXford, Came to see us this aftern.   at Sons Pollards & Lambds. Revd Joseph Boman here to See us. Funeral of mr Phinias Allin.  
 

 

 February 23, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See Dagt Pollard. her Breast is likely to Break again and Shee is very feeble. I tarried all night.   at son Pollards.  
 

 

 February 25, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. at Ditoes. my Dagt very ill, ye Doct sent for.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 February 26, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast. Doct Colman Came and opened my Dagts Breast, Shee is Easier. the Doct Conducted me home. I then went to Son Jonas, his infant very sick. we gave it medisin which gave relief. my son returnd from winslow and I Came home late at Evn. son Towns famely are well. Nabby hail marrid ys Evng.   at Ditoes. my Dagts Breast opend. was at Jonas allso  
 

 

 February 27, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I went to Shubal Pittss, find his Dagt very ill. I Calld at mr Thwings, mrs Kider there. I Calld at my sons, his Babe is better.   at mr Pittss, Thwings & Son Jonas.  
 

 

 March 1, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mr Dingleys inft, it is very Sick. I Spent ye night with them.   at mr Dingleys.  
 

 

 March 2, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I have spent ys day with mr Dingley. A mrs Wiman from Goshan there. the Child very ill, we gave it Senna which brot of much fleghm. it Seems more Comfortable this night.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 March 3, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. I left the Child some Easier and Came home at 10h morn. Son and Dt Lambd here, they have been to mr Capins. Daniel Piper of No 2 was here. mr Ballard deld a plan of his Survey there and Some Sartificates to Carrie to the Setlers, and one Plan for D. Levermore Esqr.   at Ditoes. Son & Dagt Lambard here.  
 

 

 March 4, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Betsy washt the rooms. Rufas Ballard and wife Dind here. I Churnd, had 4_ lb butter. Betsys Bror Wm dind here. I was Calld at 11h Evn to go and See the wife of Shubal Pitts.   at Leut Sbl Pittss. there was a Ball at mr Pittss. 
 

 

 March 5, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. [Birth Shubal Pittss 1st Son. X.]

Clear and Pleast, mrss Pitts was Safe Deld at 1h 50m ys morn of her first Son & 3d Child, it wd 11 lb. I tarried till after noon. my Compy were Old Lady Cox, Pitts, Sister Barton, Moody, Soal & Witherel.   at Ditoes. Birth 5th. recd 10/.  
 

 

 March 9, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld, after mr Ballard and Sons went to Town meeting, to go to Reed field and See the wife of Amos Lyon, find her Some unwell. I larried all night. mrs Noles and Swanton were Calld, we all had Some Sleep.   at Amos Lyons.  
 

 

 March 10, 1801, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Amos Lyons 1st Dagt. XX.

Clear and pleast. my Patient was more unwell ys aftern. two mrs Noless, her Marn & mrs Swanton there, and Shee was Safe Deld at 5h Pm of a Dagt, her Second Child; the first was a son. I tarried all night.   at mr Lyons. Birth 6th. recd 12/. mr Ballard to harlim & Varsalboro  
 

 

 March 18, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml Titcomb 2nd Dagt, dead born.

Cloudy. I Combd 4_ lb flax and was Calld to See the wife of Saml Titcomb who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 6h pm of her Second Daught, it was dead Born. the Patient has had a Severe Cough but her Case was Natural and not Severe, and I know not what to ascribe the death of ye inft to but the Cough the Mother had.   at Saml Titcombs Esqrs. Birth 7th. recd 13/2d as a rewd.  
 

 

 March 23, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I was Calld to mr Georg Reeds to See his wife and youngest Child who are unwell. Jabish Cowens wife and I Sett up all night, the Child wrestless.   at Georg Reeds.  
 

 

 March 27, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld by son Lambd to go and See his wife who is unwell, his Father there. Ephm workt here.   at son Lambds.  
 

 

 April 3, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I returnd home at 10h morn. Betsy Sett out to go to her dads. I was Calld to see the wife of Magr Bengm Stickney who is unwell, was there all night.   at Ditoes & at mgr Stickneys.  
 

 

 April 4, 1801, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mrs Slickney had her women Calld aftern and was unwell all night. we had Some Sleep.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 April 5, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mgr Benn Stickneys 5th Son. XX.

Clear. my Patients illness Came on Severe this morn and Shee was at 10h 30m Safe Deld of her 5th Son & 7th Child. I lefe her and inft Cleverly and reacht home at 3h 30m. we all Supt at Son Jonas. Betsy returnd at Evng.   at Ditoes & at Son Jonas. Birth 8th. recd 9/ as a rewd. 
 

 

 April 8, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I dug the ground and Sett 165 Cabbage Stumps and french Turnips. I was Calld, after I had done, to go and See the wife of Georg Rhead who was in a weak Situation. mrss Mott & Woodwd there all night. I Slept some.   at mr Rheads ys night. workt in my gardin all day.  
 

 

 April 9, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

at mr Rheads.

Snowd foren, Cloudy aftern, raind at Evng. my Patient was Deld at 2h pm, the Birth premature. I left her as well as Could be Expected and returnd at 5. it is Fast day. mr Ballard and Allin Lambard have been to meeting.   at Ditoes. Case 9th, recd 6/.
