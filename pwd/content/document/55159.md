Accountants Office Oct.5th.1796
John Chester Esq. Supervisor Weathersfield Connecticut
Sir The Treasurer of the United States will remit you the sum of One hundred five Dollars & 46/100 which you will please to pay to Capt. Edw.d Miller at Middletown; being for the pay of himself & men for September 1796 and take his duplicate receipts for the due application thereof.
I am &c Wm Simmons
