Philadelphia 20th May 1791
Sir
The bearer comes forward with three Chests of Medicines to your address in your receipts for him you will insert the Regiment and Battalion to which  they are annexed- this will be one means to prevent mistakes in their further destination- The keys the Waggoner has with him - Many of the Troop are now in motion, and will be with you soon
I am, Sir, your most Obedient Servant
Samuel Hodgdon
QMG
Major Isaac Craig
