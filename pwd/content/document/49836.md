Letter to Bennet

      Pemberton
       22 April
Department of War
Accounting Office
April 22d 1795
Sir
By virtue of your letter of attorney to John
S. Ganon I have placed in his hands the Pay
of the Scouts for the protection of Woodford County
State of Kentuckyween 1 April 1793 & 18 Septr 1794 - incl
amounting to Six hundred ninety seven Dollars
& 17/100 for which you are held accountable
for the due application agreeably to the inclosed
Receipt Roll which is to be signed and trans =
mitted for your information --
I am [undecipherable]
your ob Ser.
Major Bennet Pemberton
sig of Wm Simmons
Franklin County
Kentucky                         [undecipherable]
