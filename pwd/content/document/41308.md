War department,31st March 1791
Sir
Deliver to the order of Colonel Clement Biddle, Quarter master General for the State of Pennsylvania, two hundred muskets and bayonets, five hundred weight of musket powder and one thousand weight of lead.
I amSirYour humble servantHKnoxSecy of War
Major CraigFort Pitt
