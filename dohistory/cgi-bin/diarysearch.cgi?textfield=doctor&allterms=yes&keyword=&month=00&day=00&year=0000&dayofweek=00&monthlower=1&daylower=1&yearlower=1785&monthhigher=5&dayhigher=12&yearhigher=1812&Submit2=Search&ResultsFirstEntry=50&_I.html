 November 30, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I have been at home. Mr Ballard went to Doct Coneys. Dolly to mr Westons for needles. Patty Savage is married to mr Gordin of winthrop this day. Saml Brown Dind here.    
 

 

 December 11, 1786, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 A Clear & very Cold Day. Excesive Cold night. mr Ballard Spent Evinning at Capt Sualls, Doct Coney also. we had washing Done to day & killd 6 fowls.    
 

 

 December 14, 1786, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Thanksgiving Day. I went to meeting & was Calld to Colon Howards to ascist mrs Pollard Lay out his Son James. Mrs Betsy, Polly & Jack are very Sick. I watcht. Doct Colman unwel. Death of James Howard.    
 

 

 January 12, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 a snow Storm. I was Calld to mr Savages to See his Childn that were Sick. Doct Coney there. mr Bullin & wife went from here. I stayd at Savgs & watcht. Son Town Came here, his Childn Been unwell. at home.    
 

 

 January 22, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Cold Day, Clear at Eving. I Combd flax. Gillbrth went from here. mr Alin had 1/2 oz Salv. Salvina Hatch Sleeps here. mr Ballard been to Doct Coney, the Girls at mr Crages.    
 

 

 January 23, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear morn, Snowd aftern, & Eving. I have been at home kniting on mrs Fosters Stockin. mr Ballard at Doct Coneys. two of the Cragges here for keeping for yr Teem.    
 

 

 January 24, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mr Savages Child, tarried all night. mr Ballard Came from Doct Coneys, Carried me up on a Sled. I finisht my Stockins. a man from woolwich Sleept here ys night.    
 

 

 March 26, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Ephm had a fit.

Clear & Cool. Ephm had a Fitt. Doct Coney Calld to See him. mr Ballard been at mr Pollards with ye other Select gent men.    
 

 

 March 29, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. Danniel Robbins Came here from the westward. mr Voce Remoovd from mr Emersons to Doct Coalmans. mr Ballard at mr Pollards on public buisiness. I have been at home, finisht hannahs Stockins.    
 

 

 March 31, 1787, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard been to Doct Coneys. I have been at home. Hannah Cool Sleeps here, Danl Robbins also. Cyrus Bot 1 lb Tea. Lidia & Aaron Foster Brot Cyrus 15 Eggs.    
 

 

 April 26, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. Nathan Tylors Dafter. XX. Receivd nothing.

I was Calld at 2 O Clok to Nathan Taylors wife in Travil, found her Dilivrd of a Dafter. Doct Williams operator. very rainy. I tarried till morn then Sett out on foot, walkt to the Bridg Below mr Kenidays when Tylor Came up with a hors. I rode home, arivd at 7 much fatagud. Jono & Saml Dicky had a quarrel; what a Shame it is. CapT Nichols & his Son Jonathan here this morn going to CobessiContee.    
 

 

 April 30, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, a Shower at Evinng. mrs Bisbee & Polly here. the old Lady Dind. I went to Doct Colmans with her. mr Learned & mr Stephens Dind, old mr Hardin also, and informs he has Lost his Best Cow; a heavy loss. Pamela is gone to mr StephensS at winthrop.    
 

 

 May 1, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 a rain. I have been at home. mr Ballard to Doct Coneys. mr Ingraham had 1/2 Bushl flax Seed. mr Leighton Sleeps here, informs that Joseph Ben Fillbrokss Hous is Burnt & most of his goods.    
 

 

 May 8, 1787, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 I returnd at 11 O Clok morn. Left mrs Edson So well that Shee was quilling yarn for her Sister to weave. mr Learned & Sally White Dind here. mr Welman Sent me a piece of Salmon. Hannah & Dolly, Ephm & Patty attend School. Pamela went to mr Crags. our men turnd off Boards to Davis. Town meeting yesterday. Doct Coney Chosen to Represent this Town in Gen Coart. mr Carr Delegate for Convention at Falmoth or Portland.    
 

 

 May 21, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. the Girls Strikeing harnis. I went to See mrs Williams. mrs Voce & Betsy here. mr Smyle Sleeps here. mr Ballard surveying for Revd mr Foster & been to Doct Coneys. mrs Harris, Lidia Bisbee, Sally Peirce & others here ys Evinng. mr Savage made the irons for our Loome. I paid him 4 Shillings in Cash.    
 

 

 July 4, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. independence of America observd in this Town. Pamela & I went to mr Blacks, he was not at home. Seth & Joseph Williamss wives here. mr Dudly, Dr Barton, mr Leighton & Biah Town Sleep here. mrs Harris & other young people here. Hannah took her webb out of the Loom, there was 40 yards.    
 

 

 July 19, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to Deacon Coneys & ye Docters, mr Churches, & Capt Belchers. mr Ballard went to a vesel at ye hook & got some Corn. I Come home with him. Pamela returnd. Shee was joind in Wedlok with mr Porter yesterday. mrs Church was Dilivd of a son last Tusday morn at 1 O Clok & 20 m. Doct Coney operator. Jerymy Baddoks wife ye Same Night of a Dafter. old mrs Fletcher performd the ofice of a midwife for her.    
 

 

 July 25, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to Doct Coneys to See Pegg Cool who is near Expireing, from there was Calld to See mrs Brown who has a Dread full Lame hand. went Back to the Doctrs, Set up all night. Peggy Expird about 2 O Clok y s morn.    
 

 

 July 26, 1787, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 At Doct Coneys. Peggy Departed ys Life about 3 O Clok ys morn. Bitsy champny taken Sick. the Doct returnd from S. river at 2 PM. I was to see Old Lady Coney. Came up with mr mc cMasters as far as mr Westons.    
 

 

 July 30, 1787, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Hannah Came to mrs [Fosters]. I returnd, Calld to See mr Foster; find him mending. Left Lidia better also. Dolly is unwell. Coln North is returnd. Doct Colman Enforms yt Betsy Stackpool was Drouned 28 inst in ye river Sebastacuk. May God Sanctify ye instance to us all. I am informd yt mr Mc Masters Son Daniel is very Sick with the rash. mr Williams has Bled again & is very poorly. mr Ballard & Dolly unwell. I Batht their feet & gave them Some herb Tea. I feel much fatagud my Self. Ephm Cowen & Ezekl Leighton here ys night.    
 

 

 August 22, 1787, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 I atended funeral of mrs Claton & her infant. am Enformed that mrs Shaw has Doct Coney with her. I Calld to see James Howd, find him low. mrs North also is Sick. a thunder Shower ys Evinng.    
 

 

 August 24, 1787, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth. James Hinkleys Son. X.

Calld from Shaws to James Hinklys wife in travil, put her safe to Bed with a Son at 7 O Clok ys morn. Left her as well as is usual for her. Came to mr Shaws, receivd 6/8; received 6/8 of mr Hinkly also. Came to mr Cowens, find his dafters & Jedy ill. Claton & David Came inn from Sandy river, People well there. arivd at home at 5 aftrnn. Doct Coneys wife Delivrd of a Dafter Last Evng at 10 O Clok.    
 

 

 August 26, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 I was Calld to Joseph Fosters from mr Cowens to See Aaron, find him unwell. returnd to mr Cowens, tarried till near night. Left them poorly. Doct Coney been there & to Fosters also.    
 

 

 September 9, 1787, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 at mr mc Masters till Eving. left them poorly. find mrs Straten & Alice Ballard here. Cyrus returnd from Son Towns, left them all well. Doct Colman Published to mrs Sukey Atkins of Nubery.
