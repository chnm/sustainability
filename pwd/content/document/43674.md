War department
October 12th 1792
Sir,
I have received your letter of the 5th instant and the enclosure.
Mr. Hodgdon has forwarded to you by the last post (the Sixth Instant) a sum of money to enable you to discharge your enagements as they respect the former Quarter Master General. He writes you by this conveyance.
I am Sir your humble servant
H Knox Secy of War
Major Isaac Craig
