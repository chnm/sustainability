October 25, 1791
to Capt. Rob. Fenner

           2 NC


typed at bottom 
CCS12
Pay Office
                                      Philad octo 25th 1791


Sir

       I have received your letter of the 24th Ult

and Strike-through text

laid the same before the Comptroller of the U States for 

his opinion thereon  enclosed you will receive a Copy of his
Letter to me on the subject alluded to _ from the opinion of 
Mr. Wolcott that a compensation should be allowed you I conceive it would be best for you to prepare
& arrangeyour accounts & vouchersin order previous to coming here for settlement
Strike-through text
to prevent any delay in the settlement of your account
I mention this as I expect they will be examined  by the Auditor of the Treasury
whose Clerks are generally very much engaged.

                                     I am etc
                                         JHowell

Capt. Robert Fenner
Halifax, No. Carolina
typed number at very bottom CCS12
