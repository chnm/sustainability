During the Katrina Memorial\'s I watched several documentaries and programs.  I\'ve noticed each year rawer and heart wrenching footage is shown.  I guess their reasons are that enough time has passed and people are now mentally prepared to see this footage.  But I wonder what will next year hold in store for us if the Lord spares our life.  What other horrific stories are they waiting to show us?  On another note, while watching the memorials my family and friends started recalling their experiences and how different life was Pre-Katrina.  Than, it dawned on me, about a third of my conversations include the words Pre-Katrina.  No matter how much I tried to go on with my life something as simple as casual conversations will never let me forget the devastation that changed my being forever.  \r\n
