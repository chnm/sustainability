William Bass
19th Septmr 1799
Saml Hodgdon Esqr
[circled: 222] [stamped: Received from State Dep't. Nov. 24. 1894. R.P.O. 401310]
Sir
There has been only one Sete of drum heads Delivd Colo Moore on the Order. there is not above Eighty pairs of White Woolen Overalls in store, there is plent of Cold. 
Your most Obedt Servt
Wm Bass
19 Sept 1799
Saml Hodgdon Esq
