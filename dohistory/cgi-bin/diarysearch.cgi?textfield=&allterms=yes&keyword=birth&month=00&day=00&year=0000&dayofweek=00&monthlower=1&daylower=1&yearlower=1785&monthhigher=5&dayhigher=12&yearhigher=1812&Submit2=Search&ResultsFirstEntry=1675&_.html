 July 24, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Some heavy Showers this aftern. I have done a larg wash and am fatagud. I brewed, allso. Dagt Lambd Came before I had finisht, She assisted me. mr Ballard been to Jonas. a mr Sturges Calld here, informd me that Son & Dagt Town past by his Dadys yesterday a little Past noon.   at home. Dagt Lambd & Childn here this aftern.  
 

 

 July 25, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Printer Robinsons Son & Second Child.

Clear. I was Calld at 4h ys morn to See mrss Robinson, wife to the printer. Shee was Deld of a fine Son before I arivd. Shee was alone at the Birth, her Neighbour women had got her to bed. I Drest the infant, took Breakfast and returnd. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Jonas. the wives of James & Thos Hinkley Spent the aftern here. we are informd that Son & Dagt Town went Past Levy Robinsons this morn.   at mr Robinsons. Birth 21st. two mrsss Hinkleys here.  
 

 

 July 27, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home till Sun Sett , was then Calld to See the wife of Isaac Stone who is Extreamly ill with the Collic. I tarried and watcht. the red pepper was what Seemd to reliev her. mr Town Conducted Dagt Pollard home, Says his wife is Comfortable.   at mr Isaac Stones.  
 

 

 August 2, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. our men at Jonas to work. mr Josp Whites wife here. mr Page Calld as Shee was going to Lecture. I have been doing my house work and Some in My Gardin.   at home, mrss White here.  
 

 

 August 6, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. our men went to work at Son Jonas. Meriah Grifin Came and washt for me. I Did my house work and washt the Bed rooms & Kitchen. this day is the Anniversary of my Daght Pollards Birth.   at home. Meriah Grifin washt for me. I paid her 2/ for her Service. we are informd Dagt Town was very Low las t tuesday.  
 

 

 August 10, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till the aftern when we had a very heavy Shower with heavy thunder and Sharp lightning. my Dagt Lambard was here. mrs Pitts Calld here. mr Ballard returnd before it raind much. mr Martin here to work. I have had two informations this Day; that my Dear Dagt Town is thought to be very near the Close of life. may God prepare her and all of us for his will Concerning her.   at home.  
 

 

 August 12, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 a very rainy foren, it held up at noon and mr Ballard Sett out for home, and I tarried at my Sons. Capt Stackpool & wife, mr Fuller and his, there aftern. my Dagt was taken with fainting and Distress in her limbs. Shee and we thought her to be Expireing, but Shee revivd at Evn and gave good Exhortation to her Childn. mrss Stackpole and I watcht with her.   at my Son Towns. my Dagt had an ill turn.  
 

 

 August 14, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Sett out from Son Towns for home, left my Dagt Some revived. may the Great God Support her thro all he Sees fitt to lay upon her in ys tife and Croun her with glory here after. may he be a very present help to her ConSort and Childn and all Connections!!! I Calld at mrs Capins and Rufus Ballards and mr Benn Browns on my retn.   at Son Towns. Cyrus went to Son Towns.  
 

 

 August 18, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I have felt not So well as I Could wish. Patty Livermore and Polly Hodges Came and helpt me do my work. mrss Livermore Came in to See me. Jane Welch here, promest to Come and work for me next weak. I was Calld by mr Floid at 10h Evn to go to See the wife of a mr Wood, of winthrop, who was in Labour. I arivd about mid night, find the Patient very ill. I Spent the night without any Sleep.   at mr Woods of winthrop.  
 

 

 August 19, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth mr Woods Son & first Born. XX.

Clear and very warm. mrss Wood was Safe Deld at 6h morn of a fine Son. I left her and inft Comfortable, and reacht home at 10. I went onto the Bed and Slept till aftern. Son and Dagt Lambard and Childn, Becky Sewall, mrss Dingley and mrs Eunice Stackpole here after meeting. I receivd 9/ of mrss Wood Sept 12, 1798 for my Service there this day.   at mr Woods. Birth 22nt. mrs Dingley informs me that Dagt Town remains much as Shee was.  
 

 

 August 20, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Jane Welch Came here to work. mr Ballard, P. Bullin and Jona Brown Sett out for Poland to perform a Tour of Surveying by the apointment of the Genl Coart. they left our house at 4h pm. I was Calld at 5 to go and See mrss Brown. I took the keys. Bullin Came back for Papers and was oblidged to pry the hinges of ye Desk. mrs Brown Calld her women at 10h Evn. we had not much rest for the night.   at mr Benn Browns, Varsalboro. the wife of Moses Ingerham Deld of a Son by Doct Cony this night. mr Balld left home for Poland.  
 

 

 August 21, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Browns Dagt & 11 Child. XX.

Clear Except a Shower at Evn. mrss Brown was Deld of a Dagt, her 11th Child, at 11h 30m. I left her and inft as well as Could be Expected and reacht home before Sun Sett. mr Brown Gave me 6/ Cash and a Chees, wd 7 lb, for my Service. his Son Natl Came for the hors, he Sleeps here this night.   at mr Browns. Birth 23d. I heard, at mr Thoms, that Dagt Town is no better.  
 

 

 August 22, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been mending a Skirt and bed quillt. I went to See mrss Dingley aftern. mrs Page Calld here.   at mr Dingleys.  
 

 

 August 23, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Some Cooler. mrs Dingley Calld here going to ye hook. Shee Bot me Some pins and needles. I went to Son Lambds & Pollards, hear that Dagt Town is much as Shee was. I went to Hamlins and Carters, bot 1 pr Cards, 1 DZn Biskit, Sheet g Board, Cost 5s;2d.   at Sons Polles & Lambs, receivd a Letter from Bror Jon a, his famely are well.  
 

 

 August 25, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I gatherd musturd and did other matters in my Gardin, was Calld at 6h Pm to go and See the wife of Richd Foster of winthrop, find her Some more Comfortable than when She Sent for me. I tarried all night, mrs French allso.   at Richd Fosters.  
 

 

 September 1, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been helping do the house work. Dagt Ballard Came here, Shee thinks of going to Winslow tomorrow. I went to See Nancy Dingley who is unwell, her Dady & marm are gone to winslow. Jane is not very well this Evng. I gave her Cammomile Tea and Shee got Cleverly by morn.   at mr Dingleys. Nancy is unwell. A barn was Consumed by fire at Reedsfield last thursday, it belonged to A mr Smith.  
 

 

 September 2, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. we all attended meeting. the Ordinance of the Supper was Administred. I went to mr Dingleys, Nancy is better. they informd me that Dagt Town has been more Comfortable ys Two Days, for which favr I would Bless the Great Author of all our mercies. Son Ephm and Jery Heartford took Tea with us. I was Calld about Sun sett to See the wife of Zyba Petingail who was in Labour, but not very ill. I tarried all night. had no Sleep.   at meeting. the Lords Spr administrd. I was at mr Dingleys & informd yt Dagt Town is revivd a little. was at mr Petingals. Son Ephm here. this is the aneversarey of the Birth of Dagt Lambard, 26 years Since.  
 

 

 September 3, 1798, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at mr Petingals. my Patient Lingars. mrss Fletcher Calld there ys morn. Shee and I went to mr Allins. the women Calld to See mrs Petingl at midnight.   at mr Petingls & Allins. mr Hill and Rachel Savage were Married.  
 

 

 September 4, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Zeba Petingls Dagt & first Born.

Cloudy morn. mrss Petingail was Deliverd of A fine Dagt at 1h 40m this morn. I left her and infant Cleverly and arivd at home at 4h and 20m, went to bed and Slept till 8. have been Since Doing matters about house.   at mr Petingls. Birth 24th. I receivd 12/ as a reward for my Service.  
 

 

 September 7, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld before Day to See the wife of Jessy Cleark, tarried all day. I went to See Uriah Clearks wife who is very unwell. my Patient Seems to be more Comfortable.   at Jessy Clearks and at his dads.  
 

 

 September 8, 1798, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 raind this morn, Cloudy part of the day. I left mrs Cleark about house and returnd as far as Son Lambards. mrss Capins there. we went in at Son Pollards. Shee Deliverd me a Dollar which mr Swanton Sent there for me. Cyrus Came to Son Lambards and accompanied me home at Evn.   at Ditoes and at Sons Pollards & Lambards.  
 

 

 September 11, 1798, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy morn. I was Calld about the midle of the night to See the wife of Richd Foster who is in Labour. I arivd before Day, my patient is linguring.   at Richd Fosters. Son Cyrus is 42 years old ys day.  
 

 

 September 12, 1798, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Richd Fosters 2nd Son and 3d Child. XX.

Clear. mrss Foster was Safe Deld at the 12h of a fine Son her 3d Child, which wd 14 lb. I left her Cleverly at 3 and arivd at home at 7h Evng. I find that I have had 2 Calls Sinc I have ben gone, one by mr Robbins and one by mr Cleark. God Grant them Safe Deliverance.   at mr Fosters. Birth 25th. receivd 6/ Oct 21st of mr Fostr at Son Lambards.  
 

 

 September 27, 1798, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I workt about house and in my Gardin foren. Son Jon a Came here at 1h pm. we Sett out for Son Towns, we had a fall from the Shais by the hors Stumbling. we arivd there at 7h Evng, my Dagt very Sick.   at Son Towns, find my Dagt very ill. mrss Smyli watcht.  
 

 

 October 14, 1798, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleasant. Cyrus Came up and went to Meeting. I went, was at mr Hamlins at intermition. I Calld at Son Pollards, his youngest Child is unwell with a Soar mouth. I Sent Watson with Some honney and Safron for it. I went to mr Dingleys, his wife is not very well. I Calld at mr Livermores, all well there.   at Meeting and at mr Dinglys. it is 21 years this Day Since I and my famely landed on kenebek [Shore].
