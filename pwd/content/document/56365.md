Williams & FRancis
28th January
1797
Encloses Gen Knox's
account for Wine


To Major Hodgdon
DrSir/
We handed you last Summer an 
Account against Genl. Knox for the 
Business of which He denied us to 
call on you. We now encloses a Duplicate
with the addition of Duties [undecipherable] then
amounting to ₤345.17.3.. which we will 
thank [paper ripped word missing] to send us a Check for.
Should it be [undecipherable] to pay the
Amount [?]here, we have an Oppy of
passing a [undecipherable] Genl. Knox in Boston
You Reply will oblige.
I your obed servt.
Williams & Francis


Major Hodgdon
