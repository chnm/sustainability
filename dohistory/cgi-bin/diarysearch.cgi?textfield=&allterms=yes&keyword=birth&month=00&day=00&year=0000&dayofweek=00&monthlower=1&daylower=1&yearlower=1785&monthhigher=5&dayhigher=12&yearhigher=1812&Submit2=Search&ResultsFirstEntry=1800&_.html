 June 17, 1799, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear foren, Cloudy aftern. Dagt Lambard Came here, Shee and I walkt as far as J.O. Pages Store. we Calld at P. Norcross, Rold Smiths and mr Martins.   at Hallowl. 
 

 

 June 19, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard Sett out for mount Vernon. I went to work in my gardin, killing Buggs on my Plants and at 9h m. mr Benn White Calld me from that to go and See his wife who is unwell. I went aftern to See Old Lady White and mrss Burns and Slept at mr Whites. the Patient Seems better.   at mr Whites.  
 

 

 June 20, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Whites 5th Dagt. it was Born dead. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard Sett out for Colo Dutuns Directly after I reacht home at 8h m. I have been Doing my hous work and in my Gardin. mrss Densmore Came aftern for Beens to Plant. I was Calld in hast to go to mr Whites again. the Boys Landed me at Jackson Landing. I took of my Shoes & walkt in my Stockins, Steerd as Strait a Coars as I Could and reacht mr Whites very Soon, but was much fatagud. his wife was Delivd at 10h Evn of a Daughtr which was Dead. I tarried all night, Slept Some. ye patient as well as Can be Expected.   at mr Whites. Birth 24th. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 June 21, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mrss White as well as we Could Expect. Calld at mgr Stickneys and the Leuts whose infant had a very ill turn. we feard it was Expireing, but by the use of means it revivd and I left it. walkt to mr Pages Landing from whence mr Stickney Conducted me home by water. I arivd at 2h Pm. Did Some matters about house and went to mr Dingleys and took Tea with her, mrs Livermore, Moore, Mc Causland &C.   at Ditoes. Funeral of his infant Dagt.  
 

 

 June 22, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. mr Ballard went to mr Moodys and Bot 7yds Callico, 1yd Cottne, 2 lb Sugar; then he Conducted Dagt Lambd here to make my Gown, thin went up Streat. was informd yt Betsy welch was found in the river. he then went to Capt Williams, was Sumoned on the Jury. mrss Dingley here, mrss Livermore and her Dagt Heartfod Calld here. Rufus Ballard and Son Lambd took Tea with us.   at home. mrs Land made me a goun. Betsy Welch was found and Buried.  
 

 

 June 23, 1799, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till Evn when we had Thunder Showers. Cyrus Sett out to go to John Browns at winthrop, at 9h m, for Hepsy. I had the misfortune to put a Bone out in my foot at 9h 15m. Doct Cony was immediately Calld and Sett it, and I was Comfortable. mrss Dingley Came to my assistance, Shee, her husband and Patty Livermore Came in after meeting to See me, Son Ephm allso. Cyrus returnd without hepsy.   at home. Put a bone out of place in my foot. Doc t Cony Sett it, he wd not take any thing for his Service.  
 

 

 June 24, 1799, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Cains Dagt. XX.

Clear part of the Day, thunder Showers before noon. I have Done my hous work and Some in my Gardin. mr Ballard been to Son Jonas. I was Calld at 2h pm to go and See the wife of Capt Cain who was in Labour. I walkt, after Crosing the river, as far as mgr Stickneys, then wrode behind Willm Littlefield without Saddle or Pillion, and the Patient was Safe Deld at 4h pm of a very fine Dagt. I left them as Comfortable as Could be Expected and arivd at home at Sun Sett.   at Capt Cains. Birth 25. receivd 6/. 3/ remains Due to me.  
 

 

 July 9, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Abm Pages Son. XX.

Clear and very Pleasant. I was Calld at 6h m by Capt Abm Page to go and See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10h 40m of a fine Son which wd 12lb _. I left her and infant at 1h pm Cleverly. recd 9/. mrs Page has not had a Child for 11 years past till now. mr Ballard been to Son Jonas. I went Down to Hallowell after 4 O Clok and Bot at Pages Store 8 yds Black Lubstring @ 9/6 pr yd, 12 Dol & 4/; 1_ yds Lining & 1 Skein Silk, 1 Dolr; 1_ yds ribbond @ /10d 1:3; at Doct Pages, 1 oz Spermaseta /9.   at Capt Abm Pages. Birth 26th. recd 9/. I went to Hallowell aftern.  
 

 

 July 10, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard went to Son Jonas. I went to Son Lambds after houghing my Cucumbers, from there to mr Burtuns. my Dagt with me. Old Lady Hamlin, mrss Crage and Lidia Mc kekney there to Tea. mr Burtun Paid me in Crickery for my assisting his wife in her illness on 30 may last. 1 Boul @ 4/6, 1do 2/6, 1do 1/6, 1 pitcher 3/, and a Chamr Pot 1/6. we Calld at mr Pittss. Hepsy been picking wool and mending and Did the hous work.   at mr Burtuns & Son Lambds.  
 

 

 July 11, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Church Williamss Son & first Born. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard went to Son Jonas. I workt in my Gardin foren. mrss Dingley and her Childn and Elijah Moore Dind here. I was Calld at 2h pm to See the wife of Church Williams who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 10 Evn of a Son, her first Born. I tarried all night, the patient as well as Could be Expected.   at Church Williams. Birth 27. mrss Dingley here and Dind. I receivd 9/7 for my Service.  
 

 

 July 12, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I left my Patient Cleverly at 4h morn and relurnd home, went to bed and Slept, then went to Seting plants. mrss Densmr had plants of us.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 July 17, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Stones Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard to Coart. Dagt Lambd Came to make ma Black Silk Gown. I was Calld at 12 O Clok & 15 m to go and See the wife of Isaac Stone who was in Labour. Shee was Deld at 6h pm of her 3d Son. I left her and inft as well as Could be Expected after 8, and got Safe home. Dagt Lambd walkt home attended by her husband. Son Town Sleeps here.   at Isaac Stones. Birth 28th. recd 12/ as a reward.  
 

 

 July 22, 1799, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. I felt very unwell this morn, did not Sett up but very little till afler 9h m. mrss Capin Calld to See me. mr Ballard and Cyrus went to Son Jon as, Hepsy and I have been piking wool.   at home. mrss Capin here. Allin Lambd 3 years old this day.  
 

 

 July 25, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and hott. mr Ballard and Cyrus to Jonas. mrss Pollard went from here at 9h morn. I walkt with her as far as mr Densmores, Calld there and at mr Dingleys, pikt wool after I Came home. Polly Hodges and Allin Lambd here.   at mr Densmores & Dingleys. mrss Pollard went from here.  
 

 

 July 26, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Martins 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear and very warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus at Jon as. I was Calld to See mrss Martin at Hallowell at 10h morn. Shee was Safe Deld at 4h 30m pm of her Second Child and Dagt. I left her and inft Cleverly and arivd home at 7. mr Martin gave me 12/ for my Service, he is 33 years old this Day. my assistants were Capt Neys Lady and mrss Smith and Molloy. mrss Beeman did not Come in till after the Birth. the Phraim of mr Gillets hous raisd this aftern.   at mr Martins, Hallowell. Birth 29th. recd 12/ as a rewd. I gatherd Cucumbers, & Sent to Dagt Lambds.  
 

 

 July 27, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Gows Dagt and 4th Child. XX.

rainy. I was Calld at 1h 35m morn to go and See mrss Gow who was in Labour. I walkt in the rain and reacht mr Gows at 1h 50m, found the Patient Deld of a Dagt by the help of mrss Tucker and a little more Done by Doct Page. I got her into Bed and dresst the infant, and tarried and attended them till 7 then returnd home. found my famely at Breakfast. I receivd 6/ of mr Gow. Samuel Trask Came here for me to go and See Dagt Lambd. I went and Came home at Evng.   at mr Gows. Birth 30th. recd 6/.  
 

 

 July 30, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. mr Ballard & Cyrus to Jon as, Hepsy to mr Hinkleys. mr Densmore Came and Desired me to go and See mr Beadles Son who is Sick. mrss Thomas Came and Staid in my house and I walkt there, found ye patient very Sick. I fear its Complaint is of a putred kind, we administred a Clister, prepaired of Comphry, mallows and mellolott.   at mr Beadles to See his Sick Child.  
 

 

 August 1, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very warm. mr Ballard to Son Jon as, Cyrus to winthrop. Hepsy went to mr Hinkleys to carry filling yn. mr Edward Savages widdow Dind with me. Shee has been to Rousekilld. I went to mr Dingleys, mrss Livermore there. Cyrus informd me that mr Benn Porter of Goshan has buried his Son Benn, and that his wife bore him a Son a few weaks Since. we had a fine mess of cucumbers.   at mr Dingleys. wido Savage here. Cyrus brot news of a Death & Birth at mr Porters.  
 

 

 August 2, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Shower. our men to Jonas. mr Brown Came here and tarries all night. I was Calld at 11h Evn to See mrss Child who was in Labour and Pritty ill when I arivd. Old Lady Hamlin, mrss Duttun, mrss Haywood and Soal assisted me. I am informd that Capt Jerimy Learnd has buried his third wife lately. may his tryals be Sanctifyed to him and to us all.   at mr Childs.  
 

 

 August 3, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Childs 4th Son. XX.

Cloudy and Some rain. mrss Child was Deld at 3h morn of a Son who has a hair lip which Disfiguers it, it is otherwise a fine Child. may they take a Seutable notice of the Dealings of God towards them. mr Child went and brot mrs Croel to Nurs and I returnd home. I Calld at Son Lambds, they are as well as they have been lately. I went to bed, had been asleep and was Calld at 10h 45m Evn to go and See the wife of Capt Jobe Springer who is in Labour. Shee was not very ill thro the night and I Slept Some. mrss Jones and Fletcher went home. mrss Andrews Staid.   at Ditoes. Birth 31st. at Jobe Springer at night. Decembr 1802 recd of mr Child 10/6 as a reward for this Case.  
 

 

 August 4, 1799, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jobe Springers 7th Dagt. XX.

Clear and very warm. my Patient had her women Calld at 2h pm and was Deld of her 7th Dagt and 10 Child at 3. I left them Cleverly and relurnd to Son Lambds where Son Cyrus Came and Conducted me home.   at Jobe Springers. Birth 32nt, and at Son Lambds.  
 

 

 August 6, 1799, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. mr Ballard and Cyrus to Son Jonas. I was Calld at 11h morn to See mrss Rollands who Seemd to be in Labour but her Case was Linguring. I tarried all night. mrss Hinkley and Church there all night.   at mr Rollan.  
 

 

 August 7, 1799, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I am at mr Rollandss. mrss Hinkley and Church went home, and returnd. we went to bed and Slept Some. the Patient Calld us up.   at Ditos.  
 

 

 August 8, 1799, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Roland Son. XX.

Clear and warm. we were Calld up at 1h morn. mrss Swetland Calld and the Patient was Safe Deld at 2h 45m morn of a fine Son which wd 8 lb 11 oz. after Compleating my business I returnd home at 6h morn. I receivd 10/6 as a reward. mr Ballard to Son Jonas, Cyrus to ye hook and to his Brothers. Hepsy went to the hook.   at mr Rolands. Birth 33d. recd 10/6.  
 

 

 August 9, 1799, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Pickfords Son. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard and Cyrus to Jonas. Dagt Lambd Sent Saml for me. I went and Dind there. went to mr Childs, find the infant Sick with a Soar mouth. Shee as well as Can be Expected. I returnd to My Dagts, Drank Tea, mr Ballard allso. I was Calld in hast by mr Pickford to See his wife who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 9h Evn of a fine Son. we took Tea and I returnd to Son Lambds at 11h, went to bed and had Comfortable rest.   at Son Lambds, Childs & mr Pickfords. Birth 34th. recd 12/ as a reward.
