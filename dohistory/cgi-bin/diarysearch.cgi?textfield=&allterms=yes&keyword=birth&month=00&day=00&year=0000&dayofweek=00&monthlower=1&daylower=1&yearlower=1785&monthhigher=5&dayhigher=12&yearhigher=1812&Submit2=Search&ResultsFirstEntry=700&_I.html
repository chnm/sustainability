 June 14, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 2 h morn to See mr Blacks infant who is unwell, it was a little revivd before I arivd. I gave it a Syrrup of mullin which gave it relief. I went from there, Dind at mr Joness. Calld at mr Savages & mr Craggs, his little Dagt is not so well as usual. I receivd a letter of may 23 by Esqr Cony from Sist Waters, informing they are well.   at mr Blaks & other Neigbrs. receivd a letter from Sist Waters of may 23.  
 

 

 June 21, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mrs Livrmr & mrs White here. Esqr Coney Calld to See Dolly, recommends her taking Port Wine. Leut Hains Learned, Shuball Pitts & Juet Foster here all night. mr Ballard been to ye hook to a meeting of the Propriaters of Unity.   at home. mr Learned Sleeps here.  
 

 

 June 29, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I houghd my plants by the Door, went to mr Livermrs. Came home, wed onions. Son Town Came here, informs his family are well & that Thomas Sewalls wife was Delivd last Lords Day. mr Ballard been to runn ye lines round his land at the north End of ys town. Polly Livermore here on a visit. Dolly not So well as usual. Son Town Sleeps here.   at mr Livrmrs. Son Town Sleeps here. mr Ballard been runing lines for him Self.  
 

 

 July 19, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of ye Day. I went to mr Catons. Calld at mr Pollards, Jonses, Boltons, Voces & Densmores. Came home and went to mr Shuball Hinkleys. Capt Savages Lady Came home with me & Sleeps here. Shee informd me that Sally Peirce Swore a Child on my son Jon a & he was taken with a warrent. mr Abisha Cowen is his Bonds man for appearance at Coart.   a mr Catons & Hinkleys. mrs Savage here. Nathan l Norcross married to Eunice Hodskins. two [Blac ] allso.  
 

 

 July 24, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. mr Ballard & Ephm attended public worship. we had a Shower after meeting. John Gardner & Arther Lithgo Esqrs Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 July 31, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm morn. mr Ballard, Jona and Hannah attended public worship and I wrote to Brotr Collins, Elijah Barton & Sist Waters to Send by mr Burtun. I Sent him 7/11-2/1 by Hannah to purchace things in Boston for me. mr Pollard and his wife here. I was Calld at Sun Sett to Saml Badcocks wife, went by water. I was Seisd after I gott there with thee Collic. Did not Sleep any this night.   at Saml Badcoks. I wrote to my [friends] at Oxford. I had the Collic ys night.  
 

 

 August 1, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. I was at mr Badcocks, very ill with the Collic all day, had Ease at Evng.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 August 4, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left mrs Badcock, walkt home. Dolly is poorly. Foster to work here. finisht my [Shoes], I went to mr Pitts Store, Bot 1 quart W E Spirrit, 1 Skein of Black Silk and 2 knots off Thread, all cost 2/3. I Calld at mr Pollards & mr Densmores, went to See mrs Welch who is unwell. mrs Lucy Norcross & mrs Pheba Hinkly here. mr Ballard workt on mr Densmrs house.   at Ditoes. had Compy. 
 

 

 August 12, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Showers foren, Clear aftn, a Shower at Evn. I was Calld a little before night to See Asa Masons wife, got there at Day light down, had no Sleep ys night.   at Masons. Liddia [went] home.  
 

 

 August 13, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Asa Masons Son.

Clear. I am at Masons Still. went & gatherd Black berries. mrs Mason grew ill aftern. Calld her women at Evn & was Safe Deld at 10 h of a Son. we tarried thro the night. my patient is Cleverly. had no Sleep ys night.   at Ditoes. Birth 18th. 
 

 

 August 14, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I Sett out for home Soon after Day, walkt as far as mr James Savages then Rode out as far as mr Isaac Savages, his sons brot me Down by water. I Searcht my Cloaths immediately after I arivd, found a vast number of Flees on them. I Calld at mr Pittsis, Boltons & Savages. mrs Dorcas Pollard here for Parthenia.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 August 24, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Saml Badcks Son.

Clear. mr Ballard returnd. Capt Fillebrowns Lady here. Dolly is better. I was Calld at 5h 30m Evn to See Saml Badcoks wife, arivd at 7. Shee was Delivd of a fine Son at 10. I tarried all night.   at Saml Badcoks. Birth 19th. I receivd 3lb1/4 unwasht wool of mr Badcock.  
 

 

 August 25, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Capt Neys Son. XX.

Clear & very warm. I Came home at 6h morn. Son Town here. I was Calld at 8 to See mrs Ney. Shee was Safe Deld at 1h pm of a Son. I tarrid till near night. there was a Sprinkling of rain. I receivd 6/ as a reward. find mr John Jones, Ezekiel Chace and mrs Rockwood here. mr Jones Sleeps here.   at Capt Neys. Birth [20 th]. mr John Jones here.  
 

 

 August 27, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Isaac Hardings Dagt X. & X. James Savages Son X.

Clear. I was Calld by Isaac Hardin at 4 h morn, his wife Delivd of a Dagt at 1 h pm. I went Directly from there to James Savages, my hors mrd in a Swamp and I fell off. arivd at Savages at 3, find her Safely Deld of a fine Son by his marms assistance. were fresht and went back to Hardins by Colo Duttuns farm. I wrode Hardins hors home, arivd at 8 very much fatagud.   at mr Hardins & James Savages. Birth 2[1]th. Birth James Savages Son. Birth 22.  
 

 

 September 1, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Esqr Juetts Dagt. XX.

Clear & Pleast. Jona is gone to Winslow. I was Calld by Esqr Juett to See his Lady at 1h pm, arivd at 2. Shee was Safe Deld at 3 of a Dagt, her 8th Child; ye number of Sex are Equal. mrs Dearburn, Broadst, Shaw, Barry & Lang attendants. I wrode home alone, arivd at 9 Evn. the hors pestyrd me, but I got Safe thro.   at Esqr Juetts. Birth 23st. 
 

 

 September 2, 1791, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Except a Shower. mr Ballard & Cyrus went to pitts town. I was at mr Livermores & mr Densmores. Son Town Sleeps here. Esqr Juett Came for his hors, gave me 6/ as a reward for my assisting his Lady yesterday.   at mr Livermrs & mr Densmores. Son Town here.  
 

 

 September 10, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Town left here homewd Bound. Bror Town was here. mr Balld went up to mr Pollards. I went to mr Wicksoms to Carry 4 lb wool for her to Comb. I Calld at mr [Baxters], bot 1/2 lb Tea a 3/pr lb. Cyrus went to Pitts town, took posession of ye Grist mill & returnd here at Evn.   at mr Wicksoms. Bror Town here ys Day. Cyrus went to Pitts town to tend mill.  
 

 

 September 11, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Badcks Son & 5th Child. XX.

Cloudy & Some rain. I went last night before mid night to John Badcoks, his wife being in Labour. a message Came there at 10h ys morn for me to go & See mrs Sherburn, Shee being in Labour allso. I Could not leave my Patient. I Delivrd her of a fine Son at 1 h pm & returnd home. walkt 2 miles, Came by water to ye rock & walkt from there. find mr Jones here, mr Hamlin & wife & mrs Livermore here. Cyrus went to pitts town, Carried his Bed & Chest; he is 35 years old this Day. a Child of Seth Williamss wounded it Self in ye boddy ys Day. Jeremy White Calld me at 9 Evn to See his wife. I Sett up the most of thee night. old Lady White & Shuball Hinkleys wife there.   at mr Badcocks. Birth 24th. mr Jones & others here. Cyrus 35 years old ys Day. mrs Sherburn Deld of a Dagt. Williamss Child wounded. I have helpt 403 Childn into Existance in this Eastern Country Since the year 1777.  
 

 

 September 22, 1791, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Densmores Dagt.

Cloudy part of ye Day. mr Densmr Calld me Early ys morn to See his wife. Shee was Safe Deld of a Dagt at 3 h pm & I returnd home at Sun Sett. I have been very unwell.   at mr Densmores. Birth 25.  
 

 

 September 25, 1791, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Jery Whites Dagt. XX.

Cloudy & rain. Cyrus at home. I was Calld to See mrs White at 4h pm, find her in Labour. her women were Calld & Shee Safe Deld at the 7th h. I tarried all night. thee inft, a feemale, mother and Child Cleverly, receivd fee Octr 25th.   at mr Whites. Birth [26]th. 
 

 

 September 26, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. old Lady White went home & Rebeckah Came to Nurs. I returnd home at 11 h morn. I Spent the aftn & Evn at mr Westons. Slept at mr Hamlins. it raind in thee night.   at Dittoes, mr Westons & Hamlins.  
 

 

 September 27, 1791, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy foren. I took Breakfst with mr Hamlin, Dind with mr Burtun, Drank Coffee with mrs Childs. Calld at mr Pollards & Westons and arivd at home at 7 h Evng. mr Ballard been to Varsalboro. mrs Burtun made me a prest of 2dz Bisquit.   at mr Hamlins & other Neighrs. 
 

 

 October 5, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren. mrs Barton Left here, homeward Bound. Dolly went to mr Wicksoms. I was Calld at Evn to See Jeremy Whites inft, gave it manna, annis Seed & Safron; it revivd. I tarried all night. I Scolt my pickles ys day. Cyrus Came here at Evn.   at Jery Whites, his inft unwell.  
 

 

 October 10, 1791, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Benn Whites Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Walker Calld me at 2 h morn to Benn Whites wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at ye 7th of a Dagt, and I returnd home at 12 O Clok. Calld at mr Walkrs, JackSons & Devenports. I took a nap after I returnd. Cyrus returnd from Gillbreaths & went to Pitts town, his Daddy is there.   at mr Benn Whites. Birth [26]th. 
 

 

 October 15, 1791, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy foren. I went to mr Chamberlins to Borrow a read. Calld at mr Childss, his wife is very unwell. I Dind at mr Pollards. mr Ballard & Ephm been to git a mill Stone from mr Hardins to the river. mr Keneda & Savage Bolton helpt them.   at mr Chamberlins & other Neigrs.
