Sir Pay the wether[?] Sum of 2,000 dol
to Mo Carlisle but to no Other
Molutroll[?]
No. 355.                           Exchange for 20000 Dollars
S I R,                                     Cincinati April 12 1799.
AT Ten days sight of this frist____________________ of Exchange
the second_____________ of the same tenor and date being unpaid,
please to pay to Thomas Gibson             or order, the sum of
Two Thousand
Dollars which sum I have received of him in Cash: and charge the
same as per advice herewith to my Account.
The Scry. of War,      Pay of the army for C. Swan
of the United States         1799.
[note in the left margin]
presented 1 st May 1799

      P. Hayner
             Pretto

Accountant Officer 
May 8t 1799
