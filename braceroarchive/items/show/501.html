Social Security Card back

Description

Social Security card for Jose Parra.

Creator

Jose Parra L.
Social Security Administration

Date

2003-00-00

Subject

Identification card

Source

Chihuahua, Mexico

Publisher

UTEP

Contributor

Steve Velasquez

Rights

UTEP

Language

eng

Format

scan of id card

Rights Holder

Parra L., Jose

Text

KEEP this card. SIGN it immediately. SHOW it to your employer. Mention the number in all letters about your account. If you lose this card apply for a duplicate, not a new number.

Once a year you can get a statement of wages credited to your account. Get a form for this purpose from any Social Security Administration Field Office.

If you change your name notify the nearest Docial Security Administration Field Office immediately.

WHEN YOU REACH AGE 65 OR DIE THE NEAREST SOCIAL SECURITY ADMINISTRATION FIELD OFFICE SHOULD BE NOTIFIED AT ONCE.

Federal Security Agency
Social Security Administration

Form OA-702 Rev. I-46

paper
