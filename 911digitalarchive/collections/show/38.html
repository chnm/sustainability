Title

Peg McCloskey’s emails and PowerPoint Presentations

Description

This collection contains eight emails that describe various events surrounding the attacks on the World Trade Center and the Pentagon. Also included are two PowerPoint presentations; one containing only images, the second containing images accompanied by text from Leonard Pitts Jr.’s response in The Miami Herald on September 12, 2001.
