W.D. Accountants Office
                           April 4th 1794

Sirs

           The Treasurer of the U States 

is directed to remit to you the sum of One hundred and ninety eight dollars being for the services of Mr. John Blakely of Wilmington in safe keeping a trunk of Gold taken from 
                 74

on Board the Spanish Brigt StJoseph from 5th October 1793 to 14th February 1794 which sum the Secretary of War requests you will pay to him taking duplicate receipts for the same to be transmitted to this Office ~

                            I am Sir etc
                             Joseph Howell

Wm. Polk Esqr. Supervisor at Charlotte care of James Read Esqr. Collector _ Wilmington, No. Carolina
