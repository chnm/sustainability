I have been volunteering for the past four days with the Salvation Army and the Red Cross at the shelters and warehouses on Kelly USA.  I am in AFROTC at school along with the Arnold Air Society on campus.  Having worked each day twelve hours in the warehouse the items that we really need are Extra Large Clothing, especially underwear for men and women and we need Baby Clothing.  NO WINTER CLOTHES... a lot of people are just basically cleaning out their closets which makes the work harder on us.  Also boxes to crate items in we are short on.  You can volunteer from 8 am any day of the week until they kick us out of the warehouse.  The Warehouse is at 809 Davy Crocket Road on Kelly USA right off of highway 90 and 36th street.  Please if you can get this out to the student body.  There is sooo much volunteer work that can be done. \r\n\r\nRespectfully\r\n\r\nShane Kinsman\r\n\r\nOriginally collected by University of Texas, San Antonio, http://www.utsa.edu/today/hurricane/ShareKatrinaStories.htm
