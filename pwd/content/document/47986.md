Major Hodgson
Honored Sir
if there shold be more artificers wanted than you have already ingaged will you please to permit me to recommend John Langdon and John Barras who I know to be sober industryous active men and likely to be very usefull
from your humble Servant
Joseph Eastburn
To Major Hodgson
Septemr 17 1794
