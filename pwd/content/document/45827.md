No. 1 
War Department, January 4th, 1794
Sir,
I have received your letter of the 27th ultimo.  
The Secretary of the Treasury has signified to me that he is content that the post at Fort Franklin be supplied with whiskey, for the time mentioned in your letter, on the account of the contractors, and upon the terms mentioned.  
I am Sir, with esteem & respect, Your Obedt Servt
H Knox 
Major Isaac Craig
