Braintree Jany 2 1789
My dear Sir 
I have but this hour, received the Letter you did me the Honour to write me on the 30th Ult. - enclosed in haste are a Letter to Mr Jefferson and another to a Friend who will be equally glad to see Mr Jones.  I am much obliged to you for giving me this opportunity of doing this little service for your Friend and only wish it was of more importance, both for your sake and his, being with very great Regard dear Sir, your very humble Servant John Adams
Honour 

tenant Governor Lincoln.
