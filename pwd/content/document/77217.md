War Department, Nov 15 1800
Sir,
Please to issue your Warrant to the Treasurer of the
United States for the sum of One hundred thousand Dollars, on account
of the pay, Subsistence and Clothing of the Army.
I am Sir, etc.
Samuel Dexter
