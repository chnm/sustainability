No 9
Secy of War
Dated 4th Feb
Recd 11th
forms & sundry 
papers usd from 
Treasurer ---
War Department
7th February 1801
Sir
Enclosed herewith you will receive a Deposit Warrant receipted by the Treasurer of the United States for Ninety Six Dollars 51 Cents on Georgia Factory account, also sundry letters & accounts received from the Factors.
I am Sir
With respect
Your obdt Servt,
Sam Dexter
Mr John Harris
