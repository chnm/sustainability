Department of War Accountant's Office Jan.y 23d. 1796

Joshua Wentworth Esqr. Supr. New Hampshire

Sir

Your letter of the 1st. Inst. enclosing your account Current for commissions on receiving and payment Sundry Sums of Money from this Department have been rec which account shall be examined and the amount found due to you will be remitted by the next post.

I am etc. William Simmons
