Knoxville
22 October 1796
John McKee
500 [undecipherable]
No 35
Knoxville 22 October 17796
Received of David Henley agent of war five hundred dollars in advance for to pay expenses of seven Chickasaws [undecipherable] Mataha Mico-Mingo Mataha-Thomas Brown-John Brown-George Augusta-Halatatuska-Halatachie. New Interpreter Malcom McGhee and myself.  The above named indians being on their way to visit the President of the United States at Philadelphia.
John McKee
