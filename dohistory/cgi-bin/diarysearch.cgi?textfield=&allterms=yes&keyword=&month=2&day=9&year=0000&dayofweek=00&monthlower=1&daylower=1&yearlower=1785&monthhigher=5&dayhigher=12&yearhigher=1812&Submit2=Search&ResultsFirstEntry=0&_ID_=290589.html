 Clear. I Came home, Rode on a Slead. was Calld to see mr Porters Infant, it being unwell, gave it some Senna.    
 

 

 February 9, 1788, Saturday

 Clear Part of the Day. mr Emerson went and put Jabiz Cowen & his Dat, Rebekah, to mr Woodwards.   I was at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1789, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & pleast. Cyrus returnd, his Sister & Polly & Lucy with him. I have been at home.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Isaac Hardins Dagt. XX.

Very Cold. mrs Hardin Was Delivrd at ye 5th h of a Dagt. I Left her & Child Cleverly & returnd at noone. find Son Town was gone & Epm with him. mr Ballard went to Woods for Hay. Foster frose his feet.   at Ditoes. Birth 5th receivd this fee of Doct Colman Sept 11th 1795.  
 

 

 February 9, 1791, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 A Snow Storm. mr Ballard Carried Some wood to mrs Welch. I began a mitt for Cyrus.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1792, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & Snow. mr Bolton went to Docts & Bot Senan & Seeds; I gave the infant Some. they Calld mrs Savage there & I left the Child a little Easier & returnd home, was over Sett once. felt Exceedingly fatagud, went to bed before dark. was Calld out again at ye 11 h pm & went to Joseph Preskotts, his wife in Labour. mrs Hinkly & Preskot wrode part of the way with me.   at Ditoes & mr Preskots. receivd a letter from Sist Waters of Jany 20th 1792. Sent one to Doct Barton.  
 

 

 February 9, 1793, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & moderate. mr Ballard and Ephm went to mr Preskots for Hay. I have been at home. Cyrus went to Moses Sewalls, bot 8_ lb Chees, Cost 5/8, gingrbd /4. three of mr Densmrs Dagt s here.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1794, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd home & went to meeting, we all went but Cyrus. I went to mr Seth Williamss after Exercises, his Sick Dagt is very Low. I Calld to Sam Pollards, they are well. find Dolly very unwell, Balht her feet & gave her herb Tea.   at Butlers, Hamlins, Williamss, Son Pollards and at meeting. Doct Ward & Patty Bullins Cryd the first time.  
 

 

 February 9, 1795, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy part of the Day. mr Ballard, Silva Moore & Ephm been Surveying the river. Dolly wrode to Son Pollards, finds Jonas wife as well as Can be Expected. mrs Barton & Sally Cocks washt, I assisted to Do kitchen work.   at home. Dolly made a Gown for mrss Abbott.  
 

 

 February 9, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. Mr Ballard, my Self and Cyrus attended funeral of the wife of mr Dickman. the Corps were brot in to the hous of worship where the Revd mr Stone Delivered a Discorse Sutable to the ocation, and afterwards made a prayer well adapted. the remains were Conducted to the Burying Ground of mr Mc Master and intered. mr Dickman, Bell, Polly mc Master and her Parents followed as Moarners. the Pall Bearers were Wm Pitt, J. Black, Capt Smith, Peter Jones, J. Child and mr Parker. after they proseeded to the grave, I retird to my Son Lamberts, there Spent the Evng. my Dagt has been not So well ys Day as Shee was yesterday.   I attended at the house of worship an prayers at the interment of the remains of mrss Dickman. we Met at 12 O Clock. Dagt Pollard Spent Evn at mr Lambarts.  
 

 

 February 9, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been with mrs Pollard, tarrie this night, Shee Seems not So well as Could be wisht. old mr Pollard went and Conducted her mother and Sister there, and his wife and mrs Black went home.   at mr Pollards.  
 

 

 February 9, 1798, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. mr Ballard went to Son Jon as. Brr Lambart and his wife and Son past by here at 10h 30m on their way to Bath. I have been at home. our young men got home 2 Stacks wood and broke ye Sled.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1799, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 A very rainy day. I have finisht kniting the Seventh pair of mitts. I began the first Decr 24th 1798.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1800, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was Calld by Wm Chamberlin to go to his Brother Johns wife. I find her unwell but not So ill as Shee might be. tarried there all night, Sufered with ye Cold. mr Pilsbury Expired this aftern at 4 O Clock.   at John Chamberlins. Lambd & wife went past here at Evn.  
 

 

 February 9, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Pages 2nd Son. XX.

Snowd. I was Calld by Alpheus Lion to go and See the wife of John Page. we past by mr Hamlins 20m before 9 ys morn. the patient was Deld at 11 of her Second Son and 3d Child. I left all as well as could be Expected. past mr Hamlins 20m before 5h pm, reacht home before dark; was Cold and fatagued.   at John Pages. Birth 4th. recd 6/ as a reward.  
 

 

 February 9, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at Evn. Betsy washt and washt ye kitchin. Cyrus returnd, informs me Lucy is much as Shee has been; the rest of ye famely Cleverly.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1803, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home, boiled my Soap Grees in Ley, began to fut a pair of hoes for Cyrus. Nabby spun 1 Skein yn for my Dagt Ballard. mr Ballard wrote to A mr Peirce, Boston.   at home till the middle of the night.  
 

 

 February 9, 1804, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd and blowd. I have been kniting. Lydia ironed and did hous wk & [twisted] yarn. Jona Sett out yesterday for hay.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1805, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Jona had 4 swine Butcherd, he has been down below all day. mrs Pierce here to help his wife ys aftern. I have finisht Dagt Lambds Stockins and helpt some about house.   at home. mr Trask Sleeps here.  
 

 

 February 9, 1806, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, Cold & windy. my husband and I at home. Son Cyrus Came home. Jack Pierce & Jona B. Junr here. P. & S. Weman allso at Evng.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1808, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I have done the most of my hous work and knit Some.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & very Cold. I have mended Cloaths, done hous wk & knit Some.   at home.  
 

 

 February 9, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy foren, wind NW and very Cold. I have done hous wk, mended & knitt. Ephm and Wilm Sleep here. brot me 1 qt Spirit, 1 [lb] of Sugar which I Sent money by Jona for.   at home.
