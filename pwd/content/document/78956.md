description: "Knox relays to Governor Blount, President Washington's desire to invite John Watts, Little Turtle, and as many of the real chiefs of Cherokees  to pay a visit to Philadelphia where they would\
  \ be abundantly supplied for themselves and their nation. President Washington believes such a journey would help remove causes of hostility. Knox discusses expenses of presents and asks Blount to keep\
  \ the number of attendees and costs down. Asks that Blount accompany the entourage. \r\nReminds Blount to regularly muster the militia.\r\nAs for the meeting date, Knox proposes 17 April 1793 in order\
  \ to roughly coincide with peace talks with the Western Indians [Sandusky Conference]."
