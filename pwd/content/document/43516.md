War-department,
September 1st, 1792
Sir,
Yours of the 24th has been received.  Mr. Hodgdon will inquire into the case of Michael Stevor, and if possible he shall be punished if he deserves it.
I am
Sir,
with esteem,
Your humble Serv.,
Knox
Secy of War
Major Isaac Craig.
