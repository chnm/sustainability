 November 26, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mrss Byrnss Twins, both Dagts.

A great Snow fell last night & ys morn. I am at mrss Byrns as Shee was Siesd with her illness very Severe about noon. Calld her women a little before Sun Sett and Shee was Safe Deld of Two Daughters before 8h Evn. there was but a Short Space between the Births, they are fine Children. may God long preserv them. I Sett up with my Patients, mrs Canre and Benn Whites wife allso. it was a very Cold night. I was Calld to mrs Byrns yesterday. mr Ballard & Dingley broke thro the ice and got me over the river. I was fatagud in Climbing ye bank on the other Side. mr Dingley & Graves assisted me.   at mrss Byrnss. Births 52 & 53. I receivd 9/ in part of my fees. mrs Stickny gave me 6/ which mrss [Can ] Sent me. there is 3/ yet my Due.  
 

 

 November 28, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 river on foot Clear and Cold. I Crost the river on foot from Colo Howards to mr Burtuns, Came to mr Pollards, was Conducted from there home by mr Whiten in a Sleigh. arivd at home at 2h pm. Phillip Bullin here makeing a plan, he tarries all night. I was Calld at 8h Evn to See mrss Mory who is in Labour.   at Colo Sewalls. Crost the river on ye ice. at mr Morees.  
 

 

 November 29, 1796, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Morys Dagt. XX.

Clear and very Cold. mrss Mory was Safe Deld at 1h ys morn of a Dagt, her 6th Child, Eaqual number of sex. I tarried thro the night, my patients Cleverly. I had 1 hours Sleep, took Breakfast, receivd 6/7 in part of my fee, 2/6 remains Due. I arivd at home at 10h morn, find Cyrus is not So well as Could be wisht.   at mr Moress. Birth 54th. receivd 6/7. Due 2/5.  
 

 

 December 4, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. mr Ballard only, went to meeting from here. I have been unwell. Cyrus has a bile on his hand. mr Rice Calld on me to go & See his infant, but I am So unwell I Could not. mr Pitts Calld here at Evng.   at home.  
 

 

 December 8, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my Sons Jon a & Ephm Came and assisted us to Butcher a beef Cow, the wt of the quarters was 669 lb. they began the work at noon and I have Cleand the Tripe & feet and Tried the Tallow; Straind it to cool. I Closed my Evngs work at 11h 30m.   at home. it is one year this Day Since my Son Ephm went to liv with Son Lambart.  
 

 

 December 11, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. I was Calld out of Bed this morn to go & See my Dagt Lambart who is very unwell, her throat has Canker in it. I nurst her up, Shee Seems more Comfortable and I returnd home at Evng. mr Ballard, Cyrus, EbenZ went to meeting.   at Son Lambarts, my Dagt unwell.  
 

 

 December 15, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast for the Season. this day is observd as a Day of thanksgiving to our Great parent for his blesings bestowed the year past. we were favd with the Company of all our Children and the Children of those who reside in this Town. mr Gill and wife and his Sister, our friends (Except son and Dagt Town, Jack Ballard and Rhoda Pollard) retired home by 9h Evn. I was Calld at 10 to See mrss Graves who is in Labour. Shee remaind ill thro the night. my Self nor the women who attinded had no Sleep.   at home till 10h Evng. I was then Calld to See mrss Graves.  
 

 

 December 16, 1796, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr Graves Son. XX.

Clear and very pleast. mrss Graves was Safe Deld at 8h 30m this morn of a fine Son and is as Comfortable as Can be Expected. Shee was very ill in time of labour. I left her and arivd at home perciscly at 12, find Polly Wall very unwell with an ague in her face. I held up till Evn when I went on the bed and Slept 3 hours. receivd my fee 30 inst. is unwell, Polly Wall.   at mr Gravess. Birth 55th. my Childn Gone to Varsalboro, Some of them  
 

 

 December 17, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Charls Dingleys Dagt. XX.

Clear and pleasant. I was Calld about noon to See mrss Dingley who was in Labour and was Deld of a Dagt at 2h pm. I left her & infant Cleverly and returnd home in the Evng, find Son & Dagt Town here.   at mr Dingleys. Birth 56th. fee recd [Feb] 6th, 1797.  
 

 

 December 19, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Abbots Son. XX.

Snowd all Day. I have been washing. I was Calld at 10h 10m Evn to See the wife of John Abbot who was in Labour. Shee was delivd at 11h 30m of a fine Son. I tarried all night. mrss Felps & Dany attended us.   at John Abbots. Birth 57th. Since my marriage is 42 years.  
 

 

 December 21, 1796, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at 1h 10m morn to See the wife of Saml Norcros who is unwell. I Spent the Day there.   at Saml Norcross. 
 

 

 December 22, 1796, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Saml Norcroses Son. XX.

Snowd. I have been at mr Norcross, his wife had her women Calld and was Safe Deld of a Son at 9h 20m Evng. I watcht. I feel fatagud, but the women Could not Sett up with the patient, Shee was feeble.   at Ditoes. Birth 58th. fee recd Augst 1797.  
 

 

 December 24, 1796, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was Calld to mr Thwings, his wife unwell but was about her house all Day, very unwell at night.   at mr Thwings & Saml Norcroses.  
 

 

 December 25, 1796, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth [  ] Thwings Son. XX.

Clear and Cold. mr Thwing Sett out for mrs Eastman to take Care of his wife who Cept Chamber all day and was Deld at 5h pm of her Third Son. he arivd at 7 with her Nurs. I tarried all night, my patient Cleverly.   At mr Thwings. Birth 59th. Saml Norcss inft interd.  
 

 

 December 26, 1796, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not So Cold. I Came home at noon. I receivd 9/10_ for my Service. I was Calld Directly to See mrs Dingley who has taken Cold and is Exercised with various Complaints. I tarried there this night, made use of remidies which gave Some relief.   at Ditoes & at mr Dingleys. receivd of mr Thwing 9/10_. mr Waid workt here.  
 

 

 January 6, 1797, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld to See mrss Burtun at 1h 20m morn, Shee Seemd to get better. we went to bed at 3. I spent the forenoon with her and went to visit mrs Thwing. was Calld from there at 2h PM by reason of Polly Burtuns being Burnt in a most Shocking maner by her Cloaths takeing fire. Shee being in the Chamber with her Brother Billy, we used Every measure for her rilief but all in vain, Shee became a victim to Death.   at mr Burtuns & mr Thwings. Polly Burtun was Burnt. 
 

 

 January 7, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Cold. I was at mr Burtuns, his only Dagt Expired at 11h 30m morn, her death ocationed by her being burnt.a distresed famely they are, the mother expecting Soon to be Sick. I tarried this night, mrss Burtun is allmost over whelmed with Sorrow. God grant her his gracious aid in this visitalion.   at mr Burtuns his Dear Daughter Expired. mrs Cocks & Duttun assisted to lay it out.  
 

 

 January 9, 1797, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was Calld to See the wife of Isaac Hardin at 12h pm, tarried there all night. mrss Cumings and Briggs there. my Patient did not Seem very Sick.   at Isaac Hardins.  
 

 

 January 11, 1797, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not So Cold. Old mr Luke Lambart Calld here to See us. Patty Town went from here to Son Lambarts. Phillip Bullin planing here. I have been at home, did my hous work and Cleand my rooms.   at home.  
 

 

 January 12, 1797, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Fickys Dagt.

Clear and Pleasant. we had our Piggs Butchered by Sons Jona & Lambart, Son Town assisted. I was Calld about noon to See mrss Ficky who was in labour and was Dild of a Dagt at 3h pm, the wives of Shubal & James Hinkley attended. I Came home before Sun Sett.   at mr Fickys. Birth 1st Phillip Sleeps here.  
 

 

 January 14, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was Calld at Evn to See mrss Burtun who was unwell. old mrss Hamlin there, we went to bed together.   at mr Burtuns.  
 

 

 January 15, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Kimbals Son. X X

Clear and moderate. I was Calld at 6h morn from mr Burtuns to mr Kimbals to See his lady who was in labour and was Deld at 4h 30m Evn of a Son. I returnd to mr Burtuns at 7, was Calld back at 10, by reason of the infants bleeding, made use of means which preventd it. tarried there thro the night. it snowd.   at mr Kimbals. Birth 2 nt. receivd 9/ as a reward.  
 

 

 January 17, 1797, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth Genl Sewells Son. XX.

Clear. I was Calld from mr Burtuns at 2h morn to See the Lady of Genl Sewell who was in labour and was Deld at 11h 30m of a Son, Shee was faint after Delvy. I left her Comfortable at 4 and returnd home. Calld at mr Burtuns, find her as well as Shee has been. I Crakt a spair rib after I Came home. mr Plantain from Sharon Sleeps here.   at Genl Sewalls. Birth 3d. receivd 9/7  
 

 

 January 21, 1797, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Bakt & Brewed and Done my house work. mr Capin and Lady, Son & Dagt Lambart here at Evn, I was Calld by mr Linkorn to See his wife at 8h Evn, left my Company to wait on them Selves. my Patient had her women Calld and was very ill all night.   at mr Linkons. august 7 th recd my fee of mrss Linkorn . 
 

 

 January 22, 1797, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Linkons Dagt. X X.

Snowd. mrs Linkon was Deliverd of a Daught at 4h morn, the infant apeard Destitute of life for some time but revived and I drest it and left that and the mother as well as Could be Expected, and arived at home at 1h pm. Polly Town Came home with me from Son Lambarts. Ephm Sleeps here.   at Ditoes. Birth 4 th. fee recd augs t 7th of mrss Linkon.
