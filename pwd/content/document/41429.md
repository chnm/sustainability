57
Philadelphia 6th May 1791
Sir
The bearer comes forward with stores answering to the enclosed invoice  they come to gather and therefore both duplicates are inserted in this Letter--there are the two loads mentioned in my address of yesterday--you will please to state particularly the order the Pack saddles were in--the expence of [Boating?] them up would have been too great--
I amSir,Your Most Obedient ServantSamuel HodgdonQMG
Major Isaac Craig
