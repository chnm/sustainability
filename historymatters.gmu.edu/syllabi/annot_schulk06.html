I have created a series of four sophisticated social history simulations based on recent scholarship. Each simulation requires that a student assume the role of a true-to-life historical character and solve a problem faced by that character. The first of the four simulations attempts to represent a cross section of the society of the former English colonies and Spanish New Mexico circa 1786. Each student is presented with a problem involving courtship and marriage and must make a decision regarding potential marriage partners. Teachers interested in obtaining a copy of this simulation may do so by sending an e-mail message to schulkin@gvi.net
