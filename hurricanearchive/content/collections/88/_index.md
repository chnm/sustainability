This collection comprises approximately 250 images, video, and a log from Mark Robinson and his colleagues, Scott McPartland, George Kourounis, and Dave Lewinson while chasing Hurricanes Katrina and Rita.

Mark Robinson is a Canadian stormchaser based out of Southern Ontario. He's followed hurricanes, thunderstorms, tornadoes, an snowstorms for about 6 years.
