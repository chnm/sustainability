Sec. of War
Nov. 22. 1796
15 tons of lead to be deliver-
ed to the Purveyor, and 
charged to the Sec. of State.
War Office November 22 1796
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to the Purveyor of public supplies
Fifteen Tons of lead and charge the same to the Secretary of State.
I am Sir Your obed. Servant James W Henry
Samuel Higdon
W. John Harris
