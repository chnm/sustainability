War department August 21 1798
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to Samuel Hodgdon to be transported to Capt James Stille in addition to the articles directed yesterday two Musicians Suits and one drum and fife.  
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
