After a terrible disaster, generous people around the world decided to help the victims that lost everything from their homes to their jobs. These generous people mean so much to the victims because for the things they have done for them. I am satisfied with the rebuilding that they have done for these hurricane victims. For this, it will give the victims a place where they can call home. The people that have help these victims came from a distance to help rebuild the lives. Such celebrities such as Usher and Christina Aguilera have put their time in trying to help the victims that has lost everything. Although it will take awhile to rebuild the lives and basically everything, at least it will give them hope that anything is possible and that there are people that want to help. There are people that physically can\'t be there for the victims, but, there are foundations, products, and companies that are willing to help these victims no matter what. I feel that helping these victims in any ways is a blessing and that everyone can help no matter where they are.
