Title

Smithsonian National Museum of American History, Visitor Responses on the 10th Anniversary in 2011

Description

To remember the 10th anniversary of the September 11th attacks, the National Museum of American History opened a temporary exhibition of objects and photographs. Curators collected visitor response cards asking "How did you witness history on September 11, 2001? Tell us your story." This collection contains those cards.
