About this prototype:

As part of an application to the National Endowment for the Humanities, we have built a prototype for a small portion of the interactive game that lies at the heart of the proposed website.

This prototype has been developed for viewing on high-end computers. A detailed target platform description is available. Ultimately, the website will be accessible for viewers with a wide range of equipment and browsers.

If you are not familiar with A Midwife's Tale, you may want to read about Martha Ballard and her diary before beginning the prototype.

We have a created a "cheat sheet" for the part of the game implemented in this sample. We are providing it in the event that you do not have enough time to go through the game sample "for real." You may want to print it out before you begin so that you can use it while you go through the sample (note: there will be no further links to the cheat sheet).
