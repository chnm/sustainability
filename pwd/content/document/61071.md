War Office
Feby 7th 1798
Sundries to ded to Saml Hodgdon Esq. to be transported to the western waters for the use of the Gallies
War Office February 7th 1798
Sir,
The Purveyor has been directed to purchase a quantity of Articles for the equipment of some Gallies to be built on the Western Waters.  As soon as they shall be received by you please to deliver them to Samuel Hodgdon Esq. for transportation to Pittsburg----
James McHenry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr. John Harris
