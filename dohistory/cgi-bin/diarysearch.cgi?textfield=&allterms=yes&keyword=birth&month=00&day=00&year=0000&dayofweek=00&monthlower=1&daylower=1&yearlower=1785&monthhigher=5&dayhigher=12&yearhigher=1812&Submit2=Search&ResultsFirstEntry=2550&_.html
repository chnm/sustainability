 June 4, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day, Showers aftern. mr Ballard at meeting, I went aftern. Text fn 1 Cor X C 12 V, aftn John 7th C 7 vers. we Calld at Dagt Lambds. I Calld to See mrss Mosier, find her very Sick. Son Ephm Calld here, gave me 1 lb 3 oz Butter.   at meeting, Dagt Lambds & mr Mosiers; Shee is yet living.  
 

 

 June 8, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I am very Lambe but wrode to Isaac Savages hopeing to receiv my Due of him, but did not receiv a Cent. I Calld at Jack Shaws, receivd nothing there. I bathed my Lame knee with Vinagar, went to bed but could not rest. got up and lay before fire all night. Jona Junr Sleeps [here].   at Isaac Savages & Jack Shaws.  
 

 

 June 10, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I have done my hous wk but feel feeble. mr Katons Oldest Daugt dind here, her Mother is Cleverly. mr Ballard to Son Ephms, Carried Some greens. he gave us 12_ lb pork; it is a Leg, & 5 lb Beef; a great favour. Dagt Lambd Lent us 4_ lb rie & 5 lb indien meal. 19th inst I Sent the meal home.   at home. mr Balld bot me 1 oz Snuff. they had a great day down at ye Settlement on ye removal of the imbargo.  
 

 

 June 11, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & misty. mr Ballard to meeting. Revd mr Stone discoarst fm Luke X C 41 & 42nt verses. Son Jona and wife Calld in to See me. Barny Lambd, Wm, Martha & James here after Meeting. Son Eph, his wife & Dagt & Dagt Lambd went to Son Pollards.   at home. Son Jona & wife Calld in, they Sent me 10 oz Butter ys morn.  
 

 

 June 20, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Anty Brackets 8th Child 6th Son. XXX.

Clear forenn. I was Calld by mr Anty Bracket his wife being in Labour at 0h 30m. Shee was Safe Deld at 7h 30m Evng of her 6th Son & 8th Child. I Came home, arivd at 12 O Clock [&] 30m. it raind aftern.   at Anty Brackets. Birth 6th. receivd 9s by an order.  
 

 

 June 24, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous wk and Some in my gardin, was Calld to See Hannah Ballard, find her very unwel. mr Ballard been to Survey for Nathl Cumings and Salmon Rockwoods, brot home 6 lb Old Chees. mrs Smith brot my meal home.   at Son Jonas to See Hannah. Shee is very unwel.  
 

 

 June 25, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Son Lambards 8th Child and 3d Dagt. [Case] [performd] by Doct [Elliu].

Cloudy all day. I was Calld by Son Ephm at 3h 30m in ye morn to go and See my Dear Daugt Lambard who was Delivd of her 8th Child and 3d Daught before he Came for me. no one Present at ye Birth of the Child but mrs Piper. Doct [Elliu] performd the remainder of her Labour and through the great Goodnes of God I found her as wel as Could be Expected. Son Ephm Shew a brotherly kindness, went and brot necessaries and then went to Son Pollards and brot Pally Town to Nurs her aunt. I returnd home before night and did my hous hold business. Martha [  ].   at Dagt Lambards. mr Ballard to meting. Text [ ] VIth 21. mr [ arker] Performd.  
 

 

 June 26, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I have done hous wk, houghd Beens & potatoes. Sett F. Turnips & Squash plants. mr Ballard had a Swine of Son Jona which wd 135 lb. he houghd Corn forenn, went to parrish meeting aftern. Calld to see Dagt Lambd, finds her Comfortable.   at home. mr Ballard to Parish Meeting.  
 

 

 June 28, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Burdens 6th Son & 12th Child.

Clear and warm. I was Calld by mr Burden at 7h morn, his wife in Labour. Shee was [fe] Deld at 6h 30m pm of her 6th Son & 12th Child. I left her Comfortable and returnd home, did my work up which took me till Eleven O Clock Evng. i was quite falagued.   at mr Burdens. Birth 7th. Wm Sleeps here.  
 

 

 July 4, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. Son Town left here after Breakfast. mr Ballard went to ye meeting hous to hear an oration Deld by mr [Alidin], he Dind at Son Ephms & returnd. mrss Town and I went to Son Jonas and Spent the aftern. my Dagt B. let me have 10 oz Butter.   at Son Jonas, his wife has the ague in her face.  
 

 

 July 12, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Sarah Waids Dagt. John Alby father. XX.

a rainy Day. I was So unwel lhat I Sett up but little, but was Calld about 1h pm to go and See Sarah Waid who was in Labour. Shee was Deld about 4 of a Daugt which Shee Declarad John Alby to be the Father of. I was Conducted home Just at night, had my work to do. Ezra Town and Jona B. Junr Sleep here.   at mrs Waids. Birth [8]th. receivd my fee & 3/ of what mrs Waid owd me.  
 

 

 July 16, 1809, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. mr Ballard to Meeting. Revd mr Stones text 2nd Peter 3 C 14th vers. Sons Town, Jona and their wives Calld foren to See me. Hannah helpt me part of ye day. Son Town & wife went to Son Pollards aftern, go home tomorrow if providence permit.   at home. Son Town & his wife here [foren].  
 

 

 July 17, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 the Sun rose Clear and it immediately became Cloudy and So remaind all day. I have done a little washing, Some Wk in my Gardin &C, but feel feeble. Son Jonas wife Came in to See me, took Tea; James with her.   at home. Dagt Balld Calld here a little while. Wm Sleeps here.  
 

 

 July 21, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy and Some rain. I did my ironing, was Calld at 6h pm to go to mr Mc farlings, reacht there at Sunsett.   at mr mc farlings.  
 

 

 July 22, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at mr Mc farlings, my patient more Comfortable.   at Ditoes.  
 

 

 August 7, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Butlers 2nd dagt.

Clear Part of ye day. I was Calld to See mrs Butler who livs in the hous which mr Livermore formally did. Shee was in Labour and was Safe Deld of her 2nd Dagt & 3d Child, about mid night. I tarried till morn.   at mr Butlers. Birth 8th. recd 6/ [ ].  
 

 

 August 8, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. I left my patient as wel as Could be Expected & reacht home at 7h am. mrs Emry, Sist Barton, mrs Smart, Dagt Pollard, Ballard, Dolly, Lucy, Wm & [   ] Lambd. [ ] Warrin & Georg Stone helpt mr Ballard hough potatoes.   at [Ditoes].  
 

 

 August 10, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cool. Sist Barton and I went to Son Jonas and mr Wimans. Son Jonas wife took Tea there, James Purington allso. I was Calld at 11h Evng to Phineas Pains wife in Labour.   at Son Jonas & Wiman & Phineas Pains.  
 

 

 August 11, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Phineas Pains 3d Dagt. recd 1 bsl wheat [Jany] 26, 1811.

Clear part of the day. mrss Pain was Deld at 1h pm of her 3d Dagt & 6th Child. I left her as wel as Could be Expected and reacht home at 4. Sist Barton mended mr Ballards Cloaths. Capt Solomn Voce Expired Sudently at 1 h pm.   at Phins Pains. Birth 9th. Death Capt Solo Voce, very Sudint.  
 

 

 August 12, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth mr mc Farlins 2nd Dagt & 3d Child. XX.

Clear. I was Calld by mr mc Farling at 4h ys morn to Go and See his wife. We arivd at his hous at 8h ys morn, his wife was Safe Delivd of her 2nd dagt & 3d Child at 9. [ by his present wife ] I Left there.   at mr mcFarlins. Birth 10 th. receivd 10/.  
 

 

 August 14, 1809, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done my hous work and attended Funeral (or at ye meeting hous to hear a Discoars on the Death of Solomon Voce Esqr and Capt of an independant Company. Revd mr Stone Deld ye Discoars from Leviticus X C 3d vers, and Aaron held his peace. there were a great Concoarse of people attended. I did not go to ye grave.   at ye meeting hous. Funeral of Solo Voce Esqr.  
 

 

 August 16, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done hous wk, Scolt the Bed room & bed stead. Dagt Ballard Calld in here, informd me her husband Setled his Debt and did not go to Jail. Shee Sent Martha with 3_ [lb rice] & 3_ do of Chees.   at home. Son Jonas wife [Calld] here.  
 

 

 August 17, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till after 4h pm. I have gatherd Beens to Sell. dagt Ballard Calld in here. I am informd Dagt Lambd is going to remmove again.   at home, gatherd some Currents.  
 

 

 August 31, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Son Lambard Calld in to See me. mr Ballard Sett out to go to Esqr Locks at 11h 3 [  ] foren. I have pulld my String Pea vines and gatherd [pees] and Sheled, gathered Mustard Seed, Shelld Some that was dried. I went to Son Jonas, took Tea there. Linda, Hannah, Ephm and Saml Pierce Sleep here.   at Son Jonas. Son Lambard Calld in to See me.  
 

 

 September 1, 1809, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and warm. Lucy Lambd & Mata Ballard been here this day. mr Ballard returnd from Esqr Locks, brot a Bushl of rie meal. he went to the Settlement, brot me 2 qts Spirit & 1 qt Vinigar, _ lb Snuff. Ezra Town Came her tarries.   at home. Ezra Town Sleeps here. this is Son Lambards Birth day, 37 years.
