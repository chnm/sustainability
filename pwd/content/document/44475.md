Accountants Office
April 29 1798
I Certify that there is due to George Claghorn Constructor
of the frigate Constitution the sum of five hundred dollars being amount
of his salary account from 1 January to 21 March 1798 admitted by the
Secretary of War which sum is to be transmitted to Jonathan Jackson, supervisor
at Boston to be by him paid to the said George Claghorn.
Dollars 500 W Simmons
The Sec of War Acct.
Warrt. 2712
