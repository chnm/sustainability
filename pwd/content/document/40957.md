Estimate of Monies required for the payment of Rents in the Ordnance department at Philadelphia
Carpenters Hall from January 1st 1789 to June 30th 1790 is 1 1/2 Years @ £83 124.10.-
Capt. Henry Lisles Store on the dock for same time 56.3 84. 7. 6
Pickering & Hodgdon's Stores for same time £68 102.--.-
Saml. Emlen for the Magazine for same time £30 45.--.-
Colo. T. Pickering for Ground Rent for 1 3/4 Years £25 113.15.-
William Lawrence 3 Months £48 12.--.-
Mr. G Habacker 3 Months £20 5.--.-
William Bradford 4 Mo. & 10 days 24 8.13.4
Philada Sepr 6 1790 £425.5.10

Griffith 1 [Load wt.] at 2692 tt [@45/] £60.11.4 3/4
Kenneth 1[Load wt.] at 2232 [@45/] £50.4.4 3/4
Cr £358.11.2
By my draught of 500 dollars in favor of J Burrell Esqr. 187.10.-
