~War-department, August 17th 1792.
No. 75
Sir,
I have received your letter of the 10th inst, covering a return of stores received since the last return.
The clothing has generally gone forward for the old troops, and all others, excepting a deficiency of about one company of the old 2d regiment, and a deficiency of blankets and shoes for the old troops, which will be forwarded immediately upon being furnished. It will require a perfect arrangement on your part in forwarding the clothing.
I am Sir, with esteem, your very humble servt:
Major Isaac Craig.
