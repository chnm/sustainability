War Office March 18 1790
Sir
The Secretary of War requests you to
give him what information you can on the merits
of the petition of Lt. Col. Samuel Carleton and the
other papers enclosed.
I am Sir,
Your most Ob. Servt.
H.Knox
Joseph Howell Esq.
