Fort Pitt 16 June 1791
Sir
I have Received your favor of the 9th Instant also your Order in favor of Coll Leane for Arms and Accoutrements to which I shall pay Immediate attention to
I intended to have inclosed an Abstract of my disbursements, but as Coll Hodgdon who is now here, has your Instructions to discharge my Engagements shall Render my Acct to him, And by next Post forward a duplicate. I have inclosed abstracts of my Receipts and deliveries at this Post up to the first Instant - 
I intended also to have forwarded Muster Rolls of Major Clarks Battalion of Levies, but as Recruits are coming in almost every day, I shall wait till next Post. some of the Companies are nearly full, & a Prospect of the Battallion being Compleated in a short time - 
I am sir
your Obedt
Humb Serv.
Isaac Craig
Honbe Maj. Gen. Henry Knox
