War Department May 31 1800
Sir,
Be pleased to deliver to Robert McCormick Nine hundred and seven Musket Stocks to complete the number of Muskets he has contracted to make for the Secretary of the Treasury -- to be charged to him and paid for on heretofore.
James W Henry
Samuel Hodgdon
Mr John Harris
