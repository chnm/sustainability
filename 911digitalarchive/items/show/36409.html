Title

384.jpeg

Description

Photograph of an original painting entitled E Pluribus Unum, which was planned a few weeks after 9/11. From the bottom up: The Hudson River and the WTC; New York's new skyline; the National Cemetery; the Pentagon at night after the attack; the Afganistan flag; a dove (for peace); a hawk (for war); and between them, the American Eagle landing on the Afganistan flag. Surrounding are the countries in the alliance so far, with two white squares to make room for anyone else who wants to join in.
