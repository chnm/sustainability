Martha Ballard's Diary, July 1 - January 5, 1809

1 7
Clear. I have Bakt, wkt some in the gardin. Sett Mellon plants South of ye hous &C. Dolly & Barny Lambard Dind here then Sett out for home. Lucy and Wm at Son Jonas. mr Ballard been houghing Corn. at [home]. Dagt Lambds Childn went home.
