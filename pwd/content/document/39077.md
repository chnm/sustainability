318 Joseph Howell Esq Boston Decemb 15th 1787

Sie

Your favor of 29th November is come to hand, where of you request of us a retrn of the Officers. None Commissioned Officers and Privates of the Invaled Regt commanded by Col Nichola Genl Hazens Regiment Armands and Lees Leigion. The Corps of (undecipherable) and Miners artificers and such independent Corps as were considered as part of the quotas of this state.

We now enclose you a return of that part of Col Nicolas Regt of Invalids that were stationed in Crton. Commnanded by Capt W Farland, also of the Sappers and Miners, and if you will look into the box of papers forwarded to John Pierce Esq by Royal (undecipherable) Esq Commisioner, you will find all the different Corps which this state, which this state considered as their quota of the continental army. We have had no return of Armands or Lee's Leigion. What returns we now enclose you, with what information you may obtain by opening the box mentioned, will afford you all the information you request.

We are Sir W Hon Servt

John Deming

Peter Boyer
