Letter from War Office
June 13th 1792
John Stagg Jr. 
[Received from State Dept. Nov. 24. 1884, R.P>O. 401310.]
[Encircled: 154]
War Office June 13th 1792
Sir,
The Secretary of War requests that you will furnish to the order of Col. Thomas Procter, for the celebration of the 4th of July next, forty seven flannel cartridges, for twelve pounders - one hundred and eighty eight pounds of powder - six portfires - and a proportion of tubes.  
Your humble Servt: 
Jno Stagg Junr.
