83
Pittsburgh 24th January 1794
Sir,
I am instructed by Major Isaac Craig (who is now at Wheeling) to acknowledge the Receipt of your letter of the eighteenth instant, together with the Enclosures, and to Inform you that Major Cass, together with Captain Cooks Detachment, embarked and set off for Fort Washington on the Twentieth Instant.  Captain Crawford is not yet Returned
I am Sir, 
With great Respect
Archd Read
Honble
Major General Henry Knox 
Secretary of War
Philadelphia
