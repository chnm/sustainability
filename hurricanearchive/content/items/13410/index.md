I\'m not one to go endorsing or promoting particular companies unless I can personally vouch that what I am posting is legit. Even so, I got an e-mail this afternoon about Pfizer\'s emergency relief to evacuees who are running/have run out of their Pfizer meds &amp; it seems to check out. \r\n\r\nBasically, the prescription drug company will issue an emergency supply to evacuees:\r\nFrom now until September 16th, Pfizer and these pharmacies are helping survivors obtain their Pfizer medicines. No matter where patients may be residing, if they are from the affected areas, they can go to any Walgreens, Rite Aid, Wal-Mart, Sam\'s Club or CVS pharmacy and ask the pharmacist for help. Many independent community pharmacies will also be participating.\r\n\r\nPatients without prescription drug coverage will get their medicines for free.
