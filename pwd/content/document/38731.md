202

Hartford May 22nd 1787

Sir

Major Elisha Painter was in Col Baldwins Regiment of artificers - and I believe held other rank in the continental army, did at New Haven in January 1781 his widow and he haveing no children, applies to the state for half pay, she wants the Documents which your office only can give, and if she dont obtain them this week or early next she will fail in her application. Will you be so good as to send a certificate of all that concerns this business, the widow will be thankful as well as your Hble sevt

Jou Wadsworth

John Pierce Esq
