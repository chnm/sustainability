description: The Secretary at War dispatches Captain Aupaumut, Chief of the Stockbridge Indians, to the Great Council of Indians at Lake Erie.  Aupaumut is instructed to convey to the Indians that the desire
  for peace, on the part of the United States, is rooted in a concern for humanity, not fear.  At the end of the document, Aupaumut signs a receipt for silver ear and nose jewels to be distributed to the
  Indians.  This receipt is dated 9 May 1792.
