 August 4, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Reeds 2nd Son. XX.

Clear. mrs Reed was Safe Delivd of a Son and her Second Child, both Sons, at Sun rise. I left her & inft Cleverly & returnd home at 11h foren. went to Bed, Slept till 2 pm then went out & pulld flax.   at Ditos. mrs Reed Sent Ephm half a Chees, wd. 2-1/2 lb. Birth 21.  
 

 

 August 7, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I pulld flax foren, went to See mrs [Reed] aft, who has had an ill turn & is gott Better. I receivd a Letter from Broth Davis of June 14th.   at mr Reeds. recd a Letter.  
 

 

 August 8, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & very warm. Parthena went to mr Foots, Hannah went as far as mr Bisbes with her. Calld to See mrs Woodwd who is Sick with the measels. I was Calld to See mr Isaac Clearks Lady who was Seisd with Pukeing & a Disintary attended with Severe pain in her Stomach. I gave her Some annis Seed Decoction with Manna Dissolvd there in and after wards 4 Soap pills which Expelld thee wind, & gave relief.   at mr Clearks and watcht with his Lady. mrs Jackson tarried there allso.  
 

 

 August 9, 1790, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & warm. I returnd home, Left my Patient much more Comfortable. find Son Town here on my return, his famely are well. Doct Williams and Son & Collins Moore Sleep here.   at Dittoes. receivd 1/2_ for ye medesin & time Spent.  
 

 

 August 20, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mrs Woodwd Calld here; her famely have got thee Measels. mrs Benjamin here at Evnn.   at home.  
 

 

 August 25, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I harvested my Gardin Pees. Hannah went to mr Hamlins. I was Calld this Evng to See mr Saml Norcrosss wife who is in Labour; had her women with her all night.   at Saml Norcrosss. 
 

 

 August 26, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. Saml Norcrosss Son. XX.

Cloudy part of ye day & Some rain. my patient ill all day. Shee was Safe Delivd of a Son at Six O Clok Evn. I tarried all night. Andw Goodwins wife was there.   at Dittoes. Birth 22.  
 

 

 August 27, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd by water with mr Andrews. the Canoe leekt, I wet my feet. I was Calld to See mrs Welches Children who were ill with a Cold. Danl Robbins & a mr Gleason Sleep here. mr Norcross gave me some [refuse ].   at Dittoes and at mrs Welches. a very rainy night.  
 

 

 September 2, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear & hott. I have been Doing work in my gardin, hilld Cabbages. Willm Haywood here, brot a Letter from Dolly this Day. shee is 18 years old. Son Towns famely are all well.   at home. Dollys Birth Day; She is 18 years old.  
 

 

 September 7, 1790, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Benjamins Dagt. it is ill with a Cold & worms. the Girls Spinning Yarn for mr Ballard Some Stockins.   at mr Savages.  
 

 

 September 10, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear aftn I was Calld to Capt Husseys. mr Greely, his Lady & mrs Dummer there.   at Husseys.  
 

 

 September 15, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. mr Hainses Dagt. XX.

Clear. the Doct left here before I rose. I was Calld to See mrs Hains Early this morn. Shee was Safe Deld at ye 10 hour Evn of a fine Dagt. I tarried all night. my Patients Cleverly. this is her 11th Child, 6th Dagt. mrs Cumings went home with mrs Harris. mrs Page & Briggs tarried at mr Hainses.   at mr Hainses. Birth 23d. I receivd 6/ of mr Hains. receivd Letters from friends.  
 

 

 September 16, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I returnd from mr Hainses by the way of ye hook; dismist the Lad which acompanied me at mr Wades. Calld to See her Child. lift it Some tinchture. Calld at mr Hodges, Jose unwell; at mr Westons; all well at mr Savages, Polly Better. Cyrus Delivrd me Letters, one from Broth Elijah of Sept 4th / 90, 1 from Sist Walers of ye 5th and 1 from Doct Barton, same date, informing my friends ar well Except Colo [Jewns] wife.   at mr Hainses, Wades, Hodges, Westons & Savages.  
 

 

 September 17, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear Day, rain at night. I was Calld to Capt Husseys at 1 H pm, had an ill turn there. mrs Goodin there this afternoon.   at Capt Husseys.  
 

 

 September 23, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth. James Burtuns Son & first Child.

Clear. I went from mr Burtuns aftn. Calld to see the School. went to See mrs Weston, from there to see Fanny Hodges who is Sick; returnd home in ye Evn. was Calld back to Burtuns, his wife in travil. Shee was Safe Delivrd of a Son at 11 O Clock and is Cleverly. I tarried & watcht.   at Dittoes. Birth 24th. first born.  
 

 

 September 24, 1790, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I was at mr Burtuns all Day & night. Dolly Came there to nurs his wife.   at Dittoes. Dolly allso.  
 

 

 September 25, 1790, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I tarried at mr Burtuns till 2 h pm, then Left her & infant Cleverly. I helpt her up yesterday & this day and made her Bed. I find mrs Polly Stackpool here at my return.   at Dittoes.  
 

 

 September 26, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. my Girls went to meeting and to See mrs Burtun. Shee is Cleverly. Elizabeth Taylor here; Dind. Moses Pollard brot me a Letter which E. Robbins brot from Broth Elijah Moore.   at home. receivd a Letter from Bror Elijah of 18th inst. 
 

 

 September 27, 1790, Monday

 Cloudy morn, Clear at noon. I left home at noon one h pm. went to James Hinkleys, receivd 6/ as a reward for attending his Lady in her illness with her Last Child. was then Calld to Capt Husseys, his wife unwell. I tarried all night.   at James Hinkleys & Capt Husseys.

 Cloudy & rain. I Came from Detoes. Calld at mr Hodges, his Dagt Fanny very Sick but revivd and seems Better. arivd at home at 5 h pm.   at Ditoes & mr Hodges [  ].  
 

 

 October 3, 1790, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Warrin left here aftr Breakfust. mr Gillman returnd from Surveying, left mr Ballard well yesterday. Hannah Rockwood & Dolly here, inform me mrs Burtun is very Cleverly.   at home.  
 

 

 October 6, 1790, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I went to See mrs Burtun, find her Cleverly; Dind there. was Calld from there to Jery Whites wife who is unwell. Set with her till 2 h m, was then Calld to See Capt Husseys Lady in Labour; her attenDants were mrs Cox, Hinkly, White, Goodwin & Sally, his Sister, & widdo Norcross.   at Jere Whites & mr Burtuns.  
 

 

 October 7, 1790, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

XX Birth. Capt Husseys Son. XX.

Clear. Capt Husseys Lady was Safe Deld of a Son at 11 h morn. I returnd to mr Whites at 4 Pm, tarried all night. Left my Patients Cleverly. mrs NorCross Left here yesterday for Boston. the new Born inft wd 10 lb.   at Capt Husseys. Birth 25th. first Born. receivd 6/ May 10, 1791 by Sally Hussey.  
 

 

 October 8, 1790, Friday

 Cloudy, rain at night. mrs White Sent for her women. they were with her all night Vis old mrss White, Norcss, moses & Benn Whites wives; Jackson, Stickney, Coburn & Lidia, his Sister. I Slept not this night. my [patient] very ill.   at Whites.
