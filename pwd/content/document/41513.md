Genl Knox Order in favor 
Of Col [Bean?] 
Major Isaac Craig 
Pittsburg 
N 11
Secretary of War 
has to send Arms & Ammunition
to Ohio County
27th May 1791
War department 
May 27th 1791
Sir 
I request that you will embrace the first opportunity, to forward to Colonel Lane at Wheeling, under escort of any troops going that way one hundred stands of arms & bayonets, two barrels of musket powder, and four hundred weight of lead.  
I am Sir, your humble Serv
H Knox
Secy of War 
Major Isaac Craig
