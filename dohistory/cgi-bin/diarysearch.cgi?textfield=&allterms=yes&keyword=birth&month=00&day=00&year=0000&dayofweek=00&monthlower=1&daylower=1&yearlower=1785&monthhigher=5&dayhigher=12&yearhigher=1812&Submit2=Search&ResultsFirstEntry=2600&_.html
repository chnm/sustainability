 November 26, 1809, Sunday

 Clear part of ye day & more moderate. Son Jona Calld in to see us.   at home.  
 

 

 December 21, 1809, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

Birth mr Thwings 3d Dagt.

Cloudy. I was Calld to mr Thwings at 1h ys morn. Shee was Deld of her 3d Dagt & 7th Child at Sun rise. I was alone with her when it was born. mrs Smile Came in Soon after.   at mr Thwings. Birth 21st. receivd 6s June 6th, 1810. 1_ Sugar.  
 

 

 December 26, 1809, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy [&] Some rain. I was Calld at 7h ys morn to See mrs Beck who was unwel, tarried all night. Shee is Some better.   at mr Becks.  
 

 

 December 27, 1809, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 raind. I left mrs Beck more Comfortable, Calld at mr Livermores and at Old mr Hamlins. he is he is [sic] giting better. I reacht home at 1h Pm, did my hous wk and wound 2 Skeins yn to Double.   at Ditoes & others.  
 

 

 December 30, 1809, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear till after Sun Sett. 3 of Son Lambds Sons & Dolly Sleep here. I have done various kinds work. Wilm Ballard is 11 years old this day.   at home. Grand Childn here. Wm Bald birth day.  
 

 

 January 8, 1810, Monday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Wm Babcocks 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear. I was Calld after 1 hour Pm to See the wife of Wilm Babcock. Shee was Delivd at 3 of her Second Dagt. I tarried all night and next day till Nurs Came.   at Wilm Babcocks. Birth 1st. receivd 9s as a reward.  
 

 

 January 11, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I made Sausages. Patty doing my gound. I was Calld at Evng to See mrs Babcock, performd Servises necessary. tarried all night.   at mr Babcocks, his wife is not so [   ].  
 

 

 January 14, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. Son Ephm & wife and Childn Came here. I was Seisd with a Collic pain ys morn, am very ill at night. Son Jona, his wife and part of his Childn, Son Lambard allso, Spent Evng here.   at home, Sick [with] ye Collic. Child n here, Patty returnd.  
 

 

 January 18, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Purkinss 1st Dagt, 2nd Child. XX.

Clear. we had men giting wood. I was Calld to See mrs Babcock, find find [sic] her very ill. was Calld at Evng by mr Purkins to see his wife in Labour. Shee was delivd before 10h Evng of her 2nd Child & first Dagt. I tarried all night.   at mr [Babcocks] & Purkins. Birth 2nd. recd 9/ as a rewd.  
 

 

 January 19, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 A very Severe Storm of Snow. I tarried at mr Purkinses all day, was Calld back to mr Babcocks at Evng. his wife is very ill. I Sett up part of this night.   at ditoes & ditoes.  
 

 

 January 20, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I was at mr Babcocks all day. Doct Cony Came to See the patient, ordered Senna to be given. I Sett up all night. mrs [ ] was very ill all night.   at mr Babcoks.  
 

 

 January 23, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 moderate. mr Beck Calld me at Evng to See his wife, her women were Calld. Shee was not very ill this night. Funeral of mrs Babcock ys day.   at mr Becks. Funeral mrs Babcock.  
 

 

 January 25, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X Birth mr Becks 4th Dagt. XX.

Cloudy. mrs Beck had her women Calld after 1h morn, was Deld at 2 of a fine Dagt. I tarried till 9 and returnd home. receivd an order on mr Nason for 9/. Brother Ebenz Moore Came here, Dind. he Sleeps here, has been to Portland.   at Ditoes. Birth 3d. receivd an order on mr Nason for 9/, had it answerd [25] inst.  
 

 

 January 26, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. my Brother left hear Early, home ward bound. I was Calld at 10h morn to See mrs Purkins who has had an ill turn. her Mother & mrss Pitts there. we got her up and made her bed, Changd her Linning. I left her more Comfortable and returnd home.   at mr Purkinss. his wife had an ill turn. I had Becks ordered answered at Nasons at 9 h Evng.  
 

 

 January 31, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and not So Cold. Old mr Hamlin and wife Calld here. I have done hous wk and knit.   at [home]. mr Hamlin and wife here.  
 

 

 February 2, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. Pally Town here, took Some Sewing to do for me. Ephm Cutt wood for us, his Grand daddy not able to do any thing.   at home. Patty Calld to See us.  
 

 

 February 14, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. I have Brewd, done hous wk & knit. mr Ballard to Son Ephms. Son Jona Calld in here, Says they are wel at Sons Lambards & Towns.   at [home].  
 

 

 March 4, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and pleast. Wm Trask took Breakfast. Son Jona & his Son Jona Dind. this is the anniversary of Son Jonas Birth, 47 years. we were informd that an infant Child was found under a wharf at Hallowell this day, Dead.   at home. Son Jona has Seen 47 years ys day.  
 

 

 March 17, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and Cold. Wm Farewel Esqr and wife Came to my Sons, Sister Barton allso. Luke brot me home. the Bell was toled for ye Death of Old mr Babcock, he was 89 years. I Calld at mr Hamlins, his wife not at home. Allin Lambard gave me 20 Cents. Son Ephms wife gave 2 lb Candles.   at [Ditoes]. Death Old mr Babcock.  
 

 

 March 25, 1810, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth John Pierces Son, first born. XX.

Clear part of ye day. I was Calld after 1h Pm to See the wife of John Pierce who was in Labour. Shee was delivd about 2 of a Son, her first Born; her husband absent. Sister Barton & Son Jonas wife there but Shee was Delivd before they arivd. Sist & I got home at dusk.   at Capt Smilh. Birth 4th. Jany 19 recvd the [last] of my Due.  
 

 

 March 27, 1810, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have done hous wk, began to brew. was Calld to Son Jonas, Elisabeth is Sick. EbenZ Trask and wife there, he Conducted Sist Barton & I home in his Sleigh.   at Son Jonas, the babe is Sick.  
 

 

 March 29, 1810, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of the day. I have done hous wk & knit. was Calld to Son Jonas at 9h 30m Evng to See James and Elisabeth who are unwel. I tarried all night.   at Son Jonas.  
 

 

 March 30, 1810, Friday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth B. Shaws 2nd Dagt. XX.

Clear. mr Ballard Came to Son Jonas, took Breakfast and we Came home. left ye Childn better. I was Calld at 3h 30m pm to See B. Shaws wife East of ye Bridg. Shee was Deld of a Dagt, her Second Child, about 11 Evng. I tarried till morn.   at Ditoes & B. Shaws. Birth 5th. recd 9/ Cash as reward.  
 

 

 March 31, 1810, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I left patients Cleverly and reacht home at 11h 30m Am, feel fatagued. Son Jona and Edmond Warrin here. Son Epm 31 years old yesterday.   at mr Shaws, fee recevd.  
 

 

 April 11, 1810, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Danl Woodwards 4th Son.

Clear and Spring like weather. I was Calld at 11h Am to go and See the wife of Daniel Woodward who was in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 1h Pm of 4th Son & 5th Child. I left her and infant Comfortable and reacht home about 3. I receivd 1s 6d in part of fee. Dagt Ballard and James here to Tea.   at Daniel Woodward. Birth 6th. recd 1/6d May 1st 1 Dolr & 20 cents.
