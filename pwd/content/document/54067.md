War office 27 June 1796
Sir
Captn Lewis arrived this evening from Quebec with Lord Dorchesters orders (Which I here inclose) to the officers commanding at the posts of Fort Miami, Detroit and Michilimackinac for their evacuation.  
You will be pleased to transmit to the Secretary of war a duplicate statement of the nature and condition of the works and buildings to be delivered us signed by the officers who may receive them, with such [word crossed out] additional remarks as he may make respecting them.
With great respect I have the honor to be Sir your most obdt
James McHenry
Secy. of war.
