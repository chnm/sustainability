 November 4, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I helpt do work about hous and pulld 1 Bushel F. Turnips foren, was not able to Sett up aftern. mr Waid Sloneing our well. mr Bullin and wife Calld here. mr Ballard returnd from Boudingham.   at home. mr Ballard returnd.  
 

 

 November 6, 1801, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 rainy. mr Ballard returnd fm Read field. I have pickt Some wool. Jona Butchered two Stears, Cyrus & Bill assisteid him.   at home. mr Ballard returned.  
 

 

 November 8, 1801, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy morn. mr Ballard, Pally & Cyrus to meeting at the meeting hous. mr Parker Performd. mr Gill, Lambd & Ephm and Dagt Ballard to ye uper Parish. mr Cibby perfmd. I was Calld to See the wife of John Chamberlin at 2h pm, her women Calld at 9h Evn. Shee lingured till after midnight.   at John Chambrlns. 
 

 

 November 9, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

Birth John Chamberlins Son.

Clear. my patients illness Came on and Shee was safe deld at 3h 30m morn of a son, 7th Child by her last mariage. I left them Cleverly and arivd at home at 10h, took some refreshment.   at Ditoes. Birth 18/th.  
 

 

 November 11, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd & raind. mr Ballard Collecting Taxes. mrs Chamberlin Calld here foren. I have Cut some Apples.   at home. mrss Chamberlin here.  
 

 

 December 14, 1801, Monday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. mr Ballard Sett out to Varsalboro to do Surveying for mr C. Vauhn. a woman by the name of [       ] Calld here. Shee requested me to give her Some buds of Balm Gilead and Some Suthern wood. Shee took what Shee wisht for and went on for Capt Stackpoles at winslow.   at home. mr Ballard to Varsalboro, Pally to Son Lambds; I Paid her 4 Dols.  
 

 

 December 17, 1801, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear, Cold & very windy. I have been at home kniting mitts. mrs Pollard Sleeps here. Son Pollard Calld here.   at home.  
 

 

 December 30, 1801, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very Pleast for Decembr. I have been at home, been able to knit Some, finisht futing a pair of hoes for Son Ephm. mr Ballards hand is better. Old Lady Butler and mrs Farewell Calld to See us. Ephm been to work at Jonas new hous. Cyrus after Corn and to mill. Patty Bakt.   at home. mrs Butler & Farewell here. Some Snow this night.  
 

 

 January 14, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear part of ye day. mr Ballard giting wood. Son Lambard Conducted his wife & 3 of his Childn here, he Came for them at 10h Evng. Dagt Ballard & part of her Childn Spent aftern. Old Lady Cypher and James Cyphers wife Calld here. Dolly Lambd tarries at my Sons ys night.   at home, finisht a pair mits for Hannah & began her another pr.  
 

 

 January 16, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 raind Some foren, Clear at Evng. I was Calld by mr Moody to See his wife at 7h Evng, her women were Calld in at 11h Same Evng.   at mr Moodys. mr Balld returnd. Ezra Hodges had 1 Son & 2 Dagts Born.  
 

 

 January 17, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Daniel Moodys 3d Son.

Clear. my Patient was safe Deld about 1h ys morn of her 3d Son. I got some Sleep and returnd home at 9h morn, my famely were all at meeting Except my self. Son Jona & famely dind with us.   at mr Moodys. Birth 1st. recd 9/.  
 

 

 January 30, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have Done my work, Bakt, &C, feel fatagued. mrs Parker Calld here. Ephms Mare is Lambe. I put honney & Campeva on to ye wound.   at home.  
 

 

 January 31, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear. I have been at home. mr Ballard & Ephm at meeting. I went to Bed at 9 and was Calld at 10 to See mrss Black, was there all night.   at mr Blacks.  
 

 

 February 1, 1802, Monday

view full Diary page

 

XX. Birth James Blacks 3d Son & 7th Child. XX.

Clear till Evn, Snowd a little then. my Patient s women were Calld in at dusk and Shee was Safe Deld at 6h Evn of a fine Son which wd 11 lb _, Her 3d son & 7th Child. Shee is as Comfortable as Can be Expected but has had a Severe Labour. I tarried att night. wido Thomas watcht.   at Ditoes. Case 2nd. receivd 7/6 by Dagt Pollard March 20th 1804.  
 

 

 February 2, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Clear and very pleast. I left my Patient after Breakfast. Calld to See mrs Moody, find Shee has taken Cold but likely to get over it. her inft is as well as Can be Expected. I had a Severe fit of Cramp ys night.   at Ditoes & mr Moodys.  
 

 

 February 3, 1802, Wednesday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. I was Calld at 1h m to go and See mrs Trip, found a number of women with her but Shee was not very Sick. I tarried this day with her, mrss Wiekson allso. we Slept there.   at mr Trips.  
 

 

 February 4, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I left ye patient as well as I found her. Calld to See mrs Black, find her not so well as I Could wish. Came home, laid on ye Bed and got Some Sleep before night. son Jonas wife and 2 youngist Childn here. Son Pollard and Rhoda Calld here at Evng.   at Ditoes & at mr Blacks.  
 

 

 February 9, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, Snowd at Evn. Betsy washt and washt ye kitchin. Cyrus returnd, informs me Lucy is much as Shee has been; the rest of ye famely Cleverly.   at home.  
 

 

 February 12, 1802, Friday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy, snowd a little at Evng. I have been at home overhalling raggs for Coverlid &C. Sally Cypher here, informds yt Saml Smith Expired yesterday.   at home. mrs Bridg Deld of her first Dagt & 3d Child. mrs Mosier performd.  
 

 

 February 18, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

X. Birth Pegy Whealors Dagt. X.

Cloudy & windy. I was Calld at 4h morn to See Peggy Whealor, Shee was Deld of a Dagt at 9 which Shee declared [ ] Gates was the Father of. I came home at 12 O Clock, tried the lard. A mr Meril of N. Gloster, his wife, Robd Forbes and Fanny Sleep here. his marm made me a prest of a pair of mitts.   at Saunders. Case 3d. receivd 9/ of L. Hamlin. had Compy.  
 

 

 February 20, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I have been at home, have Seen 67 years this day. O how many visicitudes have I pased thro; may I make a wise improvment of all. mr Eliab Lyon & wife Calld here. Friend Echobad Pitts dind with us. Dagt Ballard Calld here going to mr Butlers.   at home. mr Lyon & wife here.  
 

 

 February 21, 1802, Sunday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy. I am unwell but was Calld to See Dagt Lambd who is very unwell.   at son Lambds.  
 

 

 February 23, 1802, Tuesday

view full Diary page

 

 Cloudy & very windy. my Dagt very ill, her throat very Soar. I bathed it and Put on Flanel.   at Ditoes. my Dagt no better.  
 

 

 February 25, 1802, Thursday

view full Diary page

 

 Snowd. my sons returnd fm Bath and I returnd home, feel falagued. left my Dagt Cleverly to what Shee was.   at Ditoes. my sons returnd.  
 

 

 February 27, 1802, Saturday

view full Diary page

 

Birth Nathan Tylors Son.

Cloudy & some Snow. we made my Sausages. I was Calld to See Nathan Tylors wife, Shee was Deld at 8h Evn of her 6th Son & 10th Child. I tarrie all night. Hannah Bolton Expired at 3h pm. Dagt Pollard was gone to put on her grave Cloaths.   at mr Tylers. Birth 4th. Death of Hannah Bolton.
