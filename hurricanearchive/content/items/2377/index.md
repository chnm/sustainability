CAMERON, La. (Sept. 28, 2005)-Coast Guard crewmen, Texas Marine Mammal Stranding Network members and a Master Sergeant with the Louisiana Army National Guard carry a 7-foot, 400-pound dolphin to the Gulf of Mexico. Igor, the dolphin, was rescued from a ditch near South Cameron High School after Hurricane Rita swept through. He was transported on a Coast Guard HH-60 helicopter to the beach to be set free. U.S. Coast Guard photograph by Petty Officer 2nd Class Andrew Kendrick \r\n
