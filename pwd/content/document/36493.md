Note from Mr. Faesch dated 23 Jan 1784
Saml. Hodgdon
Mr. Faesches Compliment to Mr. Hodgdon, he would be much obliged to him, if he could procure the Table of Depreciation, made by Congress for him, or let him know, where it is likely to be got, as he would be glad to take it with him
Bad weather for us to go home, however bad or good we shall set of this afternoon, or tomorrow morning at furthest

Friday morning.
